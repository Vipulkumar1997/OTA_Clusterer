Hacienda at Warm Sands
586 Warm Sands Dr (at Parocela), Palm Springs, California
760/327–8111, 800/359–2007
The Hacienda at Warm Sands is the longstanding favorite Palm Springs gay resort selected by discerning, ‘in the know’, experienced travelers, from all over the world.  The Hacienda has been consistently recognized in prominent mainstream and gay travel publications as the ‘best-of-the-best’, characterized by its distinguished facilities, beautifully lush tropical landscaped grounds, impressive physical setting with ever-changing 180 degree unobstructed views of the San Jacinto Mountains, and its market-leading reputation for uncompromised legendary personal services.

The Hacienda at Warm Sands has a staff to guest room ratio of one to one – matched only by upscale, luxury, 5-star plus leading hotels of the world. The Hacienda is well known for consistently delivering unequaled guest pampering and exceeding guest expectations – for every guest, every time and on every day.

By many, The Hacienda at Warm Sands is considered one of the best gay resorts / hotels they have ever stayed in, anywhere in the world.  Please stay with us to experience our secret pampering ways, and in the process, discover someone special – YOU!
