Welcome to
Olivia’s 
Group Leader
Program!

Group travel on an Olivia vacation is exciting, fun, and rewarding! Picture yourself dining al fresco at a fabulous, all-inclusive resort with all of your closest friends. Or hiking, kayaking, or sightseeing together on an excursion on a 7-day Caribbean, Mexican Riviera, or Pacific Coast cruise. What a blast!

Traveling with a group adds an extra dimension to your vacation. It also reduces your cost! Best of all, forming your own Olivia group is easy. Our expert travel consultants are here to help you every step of the way, from showing you how to recruit and market your group to handling all of the logistics and finances.

Here’s to seeing the world the way it should be seen! 
With friends—on an Olivia vacation!

It’s as easy as
1-2-3!

1. Select your trip with the assistance of your Olivia travel consultant.

2. Recruit a group of 8 (including yourself - the Group Leader).

3. Enjoy the benefits of 
seeing the world with your group.
