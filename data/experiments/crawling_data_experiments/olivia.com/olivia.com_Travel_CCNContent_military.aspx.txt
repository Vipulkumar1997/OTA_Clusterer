At Olivia, we appreciate the women of our community who are serving or who have served in the armed forces.
We are proud to offer a military discount of $100 for any military guests
(active and/or retired) on our Cruise and Resort vacations.

1) Choose an Olivia Cruise or Resort vacation. (Riverboats and Adventures are not eligible for this program.)

2) Call Olivia at (800) 631-6277 to book your cabin or room.  You must let us know at the time of booking that you are active or retired military.  Proof of military service may be required.

Please note that category restrictions may apply and that this discount is not combinable with any other offers.
