Winter in Graubünden is a varied and multi-faceted affair. Discover the interplay between pristine wilderness and top notch, modern tourism infrastructure – from quiet cross country tracks in the Safiental valley to the sleek and sophisticated hotels in Engadine St. Moritz. And now you can discover your very own perfect holiday in the midst of the majestic Swiss alpine world. It’s a place of unforgettable memories, where you’ll find a home away from home – a place that touches the soul and opens your mind. Our tailor-made holiday itineraries will guide you through the destination and make sure that your stay in Graubünden stays with you forever. Get inspired!

Discover our topics










Skiing and snowboarding

Fly down beautifully prepared pistes and snow-laden slopes. With its 2,200-kilometres of ski runs, Graubünden is a genuine Eldorado for all lovers of winter sports. Soak up the mountain scenery. Pure pleasure!

Discover skiing & snowboarding




Ski resort Arosa Lenzerheide










Cross-country skiing

Cross country skiing is a much loved endurance sport.  With its extensive mountain valleys, Graubünden is an ideal region in the Alps for cross country skiing.

Discover cross-country skiing













Culinary arts

Delicious Capuns from Surselva or tasty Pizzoccheri from the Valposchiavo. Graubünden provides culinary diversity from three language regions. Eating well is part of the Graubünden way of life!

Discover culinary arts













Switch off in a mountain village

Do you need some peace and quiet to relax? Then the small and pretty holiday places in Graubünden are just the thing. Come to us and completely switch off far from the hustle and bustle.

Discover places to switch off




Mountain village of Tenna










Rail travel with the Rhaetian Railway (RhB)

A trip on the Glacier Express or a ride on the UNESCO World Heritage listed Albula and Bernina line is a must for any visitor to Graubünden.

Discover rail travel




Alp Grüm










Wellness, spas and recreation

Do you want to recover from a stressful week? The effect of the alpine landscape coupled with the various spa and wellness facilities ensure a relaxing spa holiday.

Discover wellness




Valbella Inn Resort










Family vacations

A large adventure playground, spreading over 7´106 square kilometers, for small and big children – this is Graubünden.

Discover family vacations




Ski resort Savognin

15 Things you should do in Graubünden

In Graubünden there is a lot to see and experience: 1,000 peaks, 615 lakes, 150 valleys, 3 language regions. There are some things you should definitely try out during your holiday in Graubünden. Like for example pizzoccheri and maluns. Does this appeal to you? Then it is high time to discover the 15 things you should do in Graubünden.

Discover our regions










Arosa Lenzerheide

Book your winter holiday in Arosa Lenzerheide now: The connection of the ski areas Arosa and Lenzerheide allows you to ski 225 kilometres of slopes. In addition, there are wellness and family holiday options.

Discover Arosa Lenzerheide




Ski resort Arosa Lenzerheide










Bergün Filisur

Tobogganing paradise of Switzerland and home to the UNESCO World Heritage section of the Rhaetian Railway: Bergün Filisur adds momentum to your winter holidays with the 6km long toboggan run.

Discover Bergün Filisur




Preda–Bergün sledging slope










Bregaglia Engadin

Graubünden and Italy – the Val Bregaglia connects them for an original holiday experience with southern flair. The «Valley of the Artist» is also suitable for families, just like the ski resort Maloja.

Discover Bregaglia Engadin




Mountain village of Soglio










Chur / Rheintal

In Chur, the capital of Graubünden, bishopric history and winter sports are connected for an intense holiday experience. Five ski resorts are nearby, as is the snowshoe arena «Dreibündenstein».

Discover Chur / Rheintal




Historic center of Chur










Davos Klosters

Five ski resorts of all ski levels, Europe's largest natural ice rink and 100 km of cross-country trails: The offer for winter holidays in Davos Klosters is diverse, and the same goes for après ski and events.

Discover Davos Klosters




Ski resort Parsenn










Disentis Sedrun

Famous for the Rhine Spring and Benedictine Abbey, Disentis Sedrun also brings a lot of variety to your winter holidays. Approximately 180 km of slopes, freeride pleasure and also extensive snowshoe tours.

Discover Disentis Sedrun













Engadin St. Moritz

Iridescent, spectacular and inspiring: Winter holidays in Engadine St. Moritz means top events, wild bob runs, promising peaks and a number of charming ski resorts from Corvatsch to Diavolezza.

Discover Engadin St. Moritz













Flims Laax Falera

In Flims Laax Falera families feel at home just as much as boarders and freestylers. A snow wonderland, four snow parks, the freestyle academy and many top events make sure there is variety and fun.

Discover Flims Laax Falera













Heidiland

The legend lives: follow Heidi's footsteps and enjoy different type of holiday in Heidiland – from ski slopes to wellness in the Tamina Thermal Spring, to shopping and fine dining.

Discover Heidiland




Ski resort Pizol










Prättigau

12km of pure adventure: There is no toboggan run in Switzerland that is longer than the run of Fideris Heuberge. The Prättigau valley is also a top snowshoe region.

Discover Prättigau




Ski resort Grüsch-Danusa










San Bernardino Mesolcina Calanca

Skiers have a choice in San Bernardino Mesolcina Calanca: The ski areas Splügen Tambo and Rossa are suitable for young and old. One experience: you can travel with the cableway to the car-free village Braggio.

Discover San Bernardino













Savognin Bivio Albula

The little ones playfully learn how to ski during the winter holidays in Savognin Bivio Albula. For variety, try the ski touring in Bivio and the rapid ice skating experience on the Albula Skateline.

Discover Savognin Bivio Albula




Family ski resort Savognin










Scuol Samnaun Val Müstair

Chalandamarz, Schellen Ursli, the Convent of St. John and the only national park in Switzerland: In Scuol Samnaun Val Müstair, Romansh culture and nature make sure for a sustainable recovery during the holiday.

Scuol Samnaun Val Müstair




Mountain village of Ftan










Surselva

Whether award-winning ski resort or a gastronomic winter hike: The Surselva can make your winter holidays quite a custom experience. This also applies to the ski tours through the Safien Valley.

Discover Surselva




Safien Valley










Valposchiavo

The trip with the Rhaetian Railway on the Albula and Bernina line offers charming views in Valposchiavo just like a snowshoe tour, which shows the original high valley of Cavaglia.

Discover Valposchiavo













Vals

The award-winning Therme Vals spa complex uniquely combines a nature experience, architecture and wellness offerings. A perfect complement to the ski resort Vals 3000 with 25 km of slopes.

Discover Vals













Viamala

Six ski resorts 100 km of runs, ice skating and curling on the natural ice of Alp Raguta, and additionally the mineral bath Andeer: The Viamala allows for an extremely varied winter holiday.

Viamala entdecken




Mountain village of Feldis
