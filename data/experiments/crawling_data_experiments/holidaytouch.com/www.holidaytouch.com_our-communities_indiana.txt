The Hoosier State offers rich heritage and cultural surroundings best experienced at Holiday Retirement’s four Indiana independent senior living communities. Farmland surrounds Indiana, which is part of the Great Lakes Region, and the state is home to Amish Country, the Hoosier National Forest, the Indianapolis Motor Speedway, the Indianapolis Colts and the Indiana Pacers. The Midwestern state is loyal to its collegiates at the University of Notre Dame, Indiana University and Purdue University. Residents enjoy four changing seasons.

We understand that each individual is unique and that needs may change over time. Holiday Retirement's Independent Living communities do not provide any health care services. Residents are welcome to use outside home health care options that may enable them to continue enjoying life at our community.

Holiday's Assisted Living and Memory Care communities provide care based on individual resident needs and desires. Care levels are derived from a unique and personalized care plan assessment to create a customized care plan.
