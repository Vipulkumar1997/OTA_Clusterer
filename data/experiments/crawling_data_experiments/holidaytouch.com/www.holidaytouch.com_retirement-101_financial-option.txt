Financial Options

Everyone has unique circumstances that help determine how they can best fund senior living for themselves or their loved ones. When reviewing finances, it’s important to determine how your income compares to the expenses at a senior living community.  But never fear, if income falls short of expenses, you may be able to make up the difference by tapping into savings and investments, including the current value of your existing home. But your options don’t end there. As with many financial decisions, it is always best to talk with your personal financial adviser to get all the details and customize an approach best for you.

Here are some additional resources to consider*:
