Experience southern comfort in the Peach State, home to Holiday Retirement’s eight Georgia independent senior living communities. From Atlanta to Augusta, the region offers farmland and beaches along the Atlantic, blending metropolitan and rural Southern culture. Georgia is the birthplace of Martin Luther King Jr., and boasts notable attractions like the Savannah Historic District and Augusta National Golf Club. Georgia is also known for being home to the Masters Tournament and the Atlanta Braves baseball team. Residents enjoy long warm summers and mild winters. Social Security and various retirement incomes are exempt from state taxes.

We understand that each individual is unique and that needs may change over time. Holiday Retirement's Independent Living communities do not provide any health care services. Residents are welcome to use outside home health care options that may enable them to continue enjoying life at our community.

Holiday's Assisted Living and Memory Care communities provide care based on individual resident needs and desires. Care levels are derived from a unique and personalized care plan assessment to create a customized care plan.
