Corporate Giving

Holiday Retirement is proud to partner with five distinguished and worthy nonprofit organizations that align with Holiday’s philanthropic dedication to advancements in health and human services, veterans affairs, and education.

We passionately believe in the power of giving back to others, and offer residents regular volunteer opportunities through our award-winning Seniors Serving Seniors and Society program to help residents get involved and make a difference in their community.

To learn more about these organizations, simply click on the photos below. Each organization page features a Donate button directly linked to the organization’s website.

Thank you for your support of these deserving charities.








American Cancer Society's Relay for Life


Holiday Retirement has been an active supporter of ACS' Relay for Life since 2008. We participate in events lasting 24 hours because cancer never sleeps.



Learn more and get involved.











Lines for Life


Holiday Retirement is honored to partner with Lines for Life and pioneer a service specifically dedicated to stopping the growing epidemic of suicide in seniors.



See how we are helping.











OHSU - Support the Knight Cancer Challenge


Holiday Retirement is proud to support Oregon Health and Science University’s Knight Cancer Institute.



Learn about the Knight Cancer Challenge.
