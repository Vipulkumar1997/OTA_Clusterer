Itinerary




Classic Travel now provides a honeymoon specialist to work directly with couples in order to create their ideal honeymoon.The wedding planning is difficult enough – leave the honeymoon to us!

Our agents have years of experience and expertise in customizing unforgettable trips that cater to their clients’ wants and needs and flawlessly match their personalities. Just as choosing the perfect dress is a crucial part of the wedding process, each couple needs a honeymoon that matches their style and personality.

We have personal relationships with the top hoteliers in the world, and can guarantee our clients impeccable service and personal attention when they travel to the hotels we recommend.

Benefits

Expert knowledge
and access to every destination in the world through the exclusive Virtuoso network.
Dedicated travel consultant
to research, plan and book your honeymoon.
Personalized extras
- we will work on every detail from a private dinner on the beach, couples massage, hot air balloon ride, etc.
Added Perks
– you will have access to all of the special Virtuoso amenities, such as upgrades when available, free breakfast, late check out, spa credits, etc.)
Personal Attention
– we provide you with a toll free number and email for easy communication – where you will reach the same person every time who knows you and your trip.
