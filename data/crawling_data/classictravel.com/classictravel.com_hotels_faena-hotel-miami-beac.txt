Faena Hotel Miami Beach
Faena Hotel Miami Beach is part of Virtuoso’s exclusive Preview Program. Virtuoso Preview was created especially for an extremely limited number of qualified pre-opening (or re-opening) properties to position them among the world';s best. Member Advisors can look forward to pre-opening series of information and updates, member training, on-property contacts and, preferred rates and exclusive benefits and amenities.
Faena Hotel Miami Beach is scheduled for opening November 1, 2015. Debuting in November 1, 2015, Faena Hotel Miami Beach defines a new benchmark for hospitality and South America';s warmth of spirit. Thoughtfully curated amenities, paired with genuine, graceful and attentive service, including dedicated butler service on each floor, enhances the guest’s experience on every level.
Located in the heart of Miami Beach, the six block Faena District balances art, architecture, nature and technology, with all elements coalescing to generate a neighborhood that redefines how to live. Surrounded by exquisite residences, innovative and immersive entertainment, retail and a 50’000 square feet art and cultural center, Faena Forum, Faena District remains true to its mission of creating an inspiring cultural renaissance.
