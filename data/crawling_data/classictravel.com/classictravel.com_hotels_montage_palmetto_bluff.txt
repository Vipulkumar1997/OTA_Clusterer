Montage Palmetto Bluff
The Inn at Palmetto Bluff, a Montage Resort, is nestled along the May River in the Lowcountry of South Carolina between Hilton Head Island and Savannah. Set within an award winning 22,000 acre development, the resort encompasses an extensive nature preserve, walking trails, a vibrant village, marina, restaurants, and a Jack Nicklaus Signature Golf Course. Accommodations include 50 spacious guest cottages and 30 Village Homes that all pay homage to the region’s rich heritage.
