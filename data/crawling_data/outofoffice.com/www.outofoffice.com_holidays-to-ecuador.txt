IN A NUTSHELL

A clash of different cultures, Ecuador covers the dazzling Amazon jungle and wildlife haven of the Galápagos Islands. Away from the natural beauty of the environment lies the capital city of Quito, a magnificent city filled to the brim with 16th and 17th century palaces and churches.
The foremost destination for wildlife-watching, the Galápagos Islands are like no other when it comes to unspoiled beauty. With many of the plant and animal species only to be found on these Islands, an unforgettable experience is to be had to journey around their isolated and stunning habitat.

VISA INFORMATION

When going on any holiday it is important to ensure your trip is as successful as possible. From beach holidays to city breaks, you need to check the visa requirements for the holiday you are going on in order to make sure you enjoy your trip without the stress of worrying about visas. The entry requirements for Ecuador vary depending on where you are visiting from. UK travellers are advised to check the Foreign and Commonwealth Office website for the most up-to-date visa information. It is travellers' responsibility to ensure they are able to travel to Ecuador and that they obtain the correct permissions for doing so. If you are not a British citizen please do check with your country's relevant embassy for the most reliable and up-to-date information.
