Accommodation In Victoria
We have great deals on Victoria accommodation to allow you to get more for your holiday dollar. Stay in our
Melbourne hotels

and experience Victoria's colourful capital, renowned for its culture diversity, award winning restaurants and great shopping.
If you have a passion for wine and relaxation, stay in our
Yarra Valley accommodation
, and explore the area's 55 wineries and 300 km of bush walking track in the Dandenong Ranges National Park.
If beautiful coastlines are your idea of perfect holiday, stay in our Mornington Peninsula accommodation and enjoy the area's historic seaside villages, vineyards and orchards. Or stay in our
Great Ocean Road accommodation

and enjoy what is arguably Australia's most spectacular scenic drives which features the 12 Apostles, the Shipwreck Coast and Australia's most famous surf beach – Bells Beach. While driving the Great Ocean Road, take time out and relax in our
Apollo Bay
,
Torquay

or
Lorne accommodation
. Our
Geelong accommodation

makes a great base for exploring the Great Ocean Road and southern Victoria.
For ultimate relaxation, stay in our
Daylesford accommodation
, and enjoy the area's 70 natural mineral springs. Or take it easy in our
Mt Dandenong
,
Port Fairy
, or
Lakes Entrance accommodation
.
Learn about Australia's gold rush history by using our
Bendigo

and
Ballarat accommodation

as a base. Learn about Australia's only armed rebellion at The Eureka Centre, or wander around the area's towns and see relics of the gold rush era, the grand hotels and public buildings, historic gardens and homesteads, and quaint shopfronts and miners’ cottages. See the Penguin Parade and one of the country's largest fur seal colonies using our
Phillip Island accommodation

as a base.
Get back to nature by staying in our Grampians accommodation and exploring the area's national parks which have many walking tracks and are great for rock climbing and abseiling, and boating and fishing and are abundant in native flora and fauna. Or enjoy scenic alpine landscapes, stories of bushrangers and historic towns using our
Bright accommodation

as a base.
In winter the area comes alive with some of the country's best skiing and our
Victorian ski fields accommodation

has you covered. Our
Gippsland accommodation

also acts as a great base for skiing and the area is renowned for its gourmet produce and the walking tracks and scenery of Wilsons Promontory. Or explore one of Australia's major rivers – The Murray, using our
Echuca accommodation

as a base.
