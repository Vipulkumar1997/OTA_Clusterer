About Mosaic House

The brand new MOSAIC HOUSE reopened on November 21, 2013, after an extensive renovation and refurbishment. The Design & Music Hostel is located in the center of Prague and is very easy to reach. The hostel offers a 100% non-smoking environment and has many award-winning eco features. All of the beautiful 55 shared and 41 private rooms have private bathrooms. We look forward to welcoming you in our hostel!

1.       Design & Music Hostel Prague

2.       Central location – downtown Prague

3.       Brand new - renovated & newly refurbished

4.       Opened November 21, 2013

5.       Non-smoking

6.       La Loca MUSIC BAR & LOUNGE located on ground floor

7.       Delicious tapas, burgers and cocktails in La Loca MUSIC BAR & LOUNGE

8.       First carbon neutral hostel in the Czech Republic

9.       Award-winning eco features

Accommodation Information

It offers 100% non-smoking environment and has many eco features.
All 55 shared and 41 private rooms at MOSAIC HOUSE have private bathrooms.

Location Information

Centrally located, in the neighborhood, numerous cafes, grocery stores, green spaces, local shops and clubs can be found with the Vltava River just a few blocks away. Public transportation lines (Karlovo námestí, Myslíkova) are located around the corner making all of Prague easily accessible.
4 minutes to the Vltava River
7 minutes to National Theatre
10 minutes to Wenceslas Square
15 minutes to Charles Bridge
15 minutes to Old Town Square

Cancellation Policy

Customers can cancel their reservation free of charge up to 1 day before arrival. After this date, charges will be applied by the property up to the first nights cost of the reservation, unless otherwise stated in the property’s conditions. Deposits paid are non refundable.
