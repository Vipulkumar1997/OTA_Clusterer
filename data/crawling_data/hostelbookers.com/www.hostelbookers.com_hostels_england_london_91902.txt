About Saint James Backpackers

Stay with us and enjoy:
Central London location (Zone 1)
A refurbished hostel with new beds, mattresses, furniture, bathrooms etc.
Free 100Mb WiFi
Loads of communal space, including:
Massive patio garden
Large lounge with 75'' HD TV
Two dining zones
A new modern communal kitchen

A Continental Breakfast is GBP 3 per day for all the guests: Fried eggs, scrambled eggs, boiled eggs, fresh fruit juices, tea and coffee, 4 types of breakfast cereals, toast with spreads (jam and peanut butter).

You can also use our kitchen to cook meals outside of breakfast time and can help yourself to complementary tea and coffee any time.

A building combining original Victorian architecture with modern amenities
An exclusive ladies-only dorm room and showers

Please note that we now have 24 hour reception.  Credit cards result in a 3% surcharge (£0.50 charge for debit cards). A £10 key deposit is payable at check-in, which is returned on return of the key at check-out.


Upon check-in guests are required to present valid photo ID (i.e. they MUST have government issued official photo ID such as passport or driving licence). Failure to provide ID will mean that you will be refused entry to the hostel and you will be charged for the first night.
