Hotel Description:

52- Storey Pullman Shanghai Skyway is a landmark building in downtown Da Pu Qiao area, Huang Pu district. It provides rooms which are no less than 48sqm, and is located close to Xin Tian Di and many other shopping and sightseeing areas. Take in the panoramic views from 49/F Zaffraan restaurant and enjoy skyline city bird's-eye-view and garden views. It overlooks the Bund, Shanghai Oriental Sports Center, Shanghai Mercedes Benz Arena and Exhibition Centers.
