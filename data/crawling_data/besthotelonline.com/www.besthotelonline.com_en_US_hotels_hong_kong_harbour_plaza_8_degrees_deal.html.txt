Address:

Hotel Description:


Award-winning Harbour Plaza 8 Degrees is located near the Kai Tak Cruise Terminal and Mongkok. Minutes from Tsimshatsui shopping, entertainment and sightseeing destinations, MTR Hunghom Station, MTR Prince Edward Station, Kowloon Bay & Kwun Tong Commercial area , which are conveniently reachable by complimentary hotel shuttle bus. The ultra-creative design lobby has a unique twist, designed with utmost innovation; utilized optical illusions where it feels as though you’re inside Italy’s Leaning Tower of Pisa. Features 704 guestrooms and suites equipped with modern amenities for your comfort. Recreational facilities include a full service business center, a well-equipped fitness center, a relaxing sauna, and a distinctively shaped outdoor swimming pool. Free use of handy Smartphone service for all in-house guests with unlimited local calls and international phone calls for 5 countries including China, South Korea, India, USA & Australia, unlimited mobile Internet data, an indispensable city guide, interactive maps and more fabulous offers.
