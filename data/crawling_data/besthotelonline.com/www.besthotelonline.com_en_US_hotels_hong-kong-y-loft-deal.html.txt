Address:
12/F, No. 238 Chai Wan Road, Chai Wan, Hong Kong


Hotel Description:


Affordable and modern, Y-Loft has direct access to Chai Wan MTR subway station via a footbridge. In addition to a 200-yard jogging track and gym, guests can also enjoy free local calls. Free Wi-Fi is available in all guestrooms.

Y-Loft is a 20 minute train ride to shopping and dining options at Central or Causeway Bay.

The spacious rooms at Y-Loft feature stylish hardwood floors and large windows, giving rooms a bright and airy feel. Each comes with a flat-screen TV, electric kettle and fridge. Selected rooms have balconies.

The hotel provides helpful service at the 24-hours front desk, as well as basic business services. Guests may also make use of the self-service laundromat.

A shared kitchenette area is available, complete with microwaves and dining tables.
