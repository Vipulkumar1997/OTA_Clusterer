Address:
353 Castle Peak Road, Tsuen Wan, Hong Kong


Hotel Description:


Royal View Hotel is located at the waterfront between Lido & Ting Kau Beaches. To ensure a pleasurable and comfortable stay for business and leisure travelers, the 688 guestrooms and designed in modern Chinese motif, and fully utilize available space. Most guestrooms have terraces and panoramic views of the bridges and the South China Sea beyond.Recreation and health facilities include an outdoor swimming pool, gymnasium, game zone, kid's room, Karaoke room, Jacuzzi, beauty and spa massage service. (Swimming Pool will be scheduled temporarily closed from 1st December, 2012 onwards and re-open on 01st April, 2013.) Blending Asian specialties with popular International items, Cafe Lido provides all day dining and serves a fusion buffet and has an a la carte menu. The Cafe@tingKau is an ideal place to unwind after a day of meeting or sightseeing, while Poolside Lounge is an ideal place for a private barbeque party or special wedding party.
