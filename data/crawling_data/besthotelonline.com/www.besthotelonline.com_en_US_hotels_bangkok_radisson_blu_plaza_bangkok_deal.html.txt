How to get there

From Suvarnabhumi Airport take the Srinakarin Expressway at Pattanakarn to the Rama 9 Expressway then Sukhumvit road (exit at north Ploenchit). Turn left to Sukhumvit road until passing Asoke junction. Keep left and continue straight ahead for 200 m. The hotel is located on the left side at Sukhumvit 27.
