Hotel Description:

Property Location

With a stay at Capella Singapore, you&apos;ll be centrally located in Singapore, convenient to Palawan Beach and
Universal Studios Singapore
®.  This 5-star hotel is within close proximity of Wings of Time and Madame Tussauds.
Rooms

Make yourself at home in one of the 112 air-conditioned rooms featuring iPod docking stations and plasma televisions. Rooms have private balconies or patios. Wired and wireless Internet access is complimentary, while DVD players and digital programming provide entertainment. Private bathrooms with separate bathtubs and showers feature complimentary toiletries and hair dryers.
Amenities

Relax at the full-service spa, where you can enjoy massages, body treatments, and facials. You&apos;re sure to appreciate the recreational amenities, which include 3 outdoor swimming pools, an outdoor tennis court, and a sauna. Additional amenities at this hotel include complimentary wireless Internet access, concierge services, and babysitting/childcare (surcharge). Getting to nearby attractions is a breeze with the complimentary area shuttle.
Dining

Enjoy a meal at one of the hotel&apos;s dining establishments, which include 2 restaurants and a coffee shop/café. From your room, you can also access 24-hour room service. Quench your thirst with your favorite drink at a bar/lounge.
Business, Other Amenities

Featured amenities include complimentary wired Internet access, a business center, and express check-in. Event facilities at this hotel consist of conference space and meeting rooms. A roundtrip airport shuttle is provided for a surcharge (available on request), and free self parking is available onsite.
