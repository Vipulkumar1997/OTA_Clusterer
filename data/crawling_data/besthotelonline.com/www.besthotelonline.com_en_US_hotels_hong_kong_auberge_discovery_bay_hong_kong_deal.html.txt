Address:

Hotel Description:


Located on the picturesque Lantau Island, Auberge Discovery Bay Hong Kong invites visitors to explore another side to Asia’s most dynamic city. Although part of Hong Kong, Auberge Discovery Bay Hong Kong is worlds apart. Comprising 325 superbly appointed rooms and suites, guests are surrounded by ocean views, lush greenery and natural beauty. With its unique location, Auberge Discovery Bay Hong Kong offers the best of both worlds. Guests can retreat to the beauty of an island oasis via a high-speed ferry from Central or by regular buses from Hong Kong International Airport in only 25 minutes. Close to popular tourist attractions, including Hong Kong Disneyland, Ngong Ping 360 Cable Car and the Big Buddha, Auberge Discovery Bay Hong Kong is perfect for getaway.

In order to provide a more relaxing environment, Auberge Discovery Bay Hong Kong will become a non-smoking hotel starting from 24 September 2016.
