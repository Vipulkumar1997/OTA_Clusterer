Hotel Description:

Ibis Tokyo Shinjuku is located in the heart of Tokyo's cosmopolitan and popular Shinjuku district. Famous as Tokyo's vibrant entertainment area, it also is a major commercial hub of Tokyo. The hotel is just a 3 minute walk to the Tokyo Metro Shinjuku train station and JR Shinjuku Station. Ibis Tokyo Shinjuku is a perfect base for exploring Tokyo for Business or Leisure with access to all parts of Tokyo via Subway and Train. This 206 room hotel offers free internet in all guest rooms.
