Hotel Description:

Sofitel Shanghai Hyland is located in the heart of the city's commercial hub on China's most famous pedestrian shopping street, Nanjing Road. 401 impeccably designed guest rooms and suites including 189 exclusive Club Sofitel rooms located on the uppermost 19 to 29 floors. Other facilities include 2 restaurants, 1 bar, spa and 9 conference rooms. The hotel is within walking distance of People's Square, the Bund and Grand Theatre, and adjacent metro stations - Nanjing Road East and People's Square.
