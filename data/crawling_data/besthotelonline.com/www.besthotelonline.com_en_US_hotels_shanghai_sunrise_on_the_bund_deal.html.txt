Hotel Description:

Property Location

With a stay at Sunrise on the Bund, you&apos;ll be centrally located in Shanghai, convenient to Shanghai Jewish Refugees Museum and The Bund.  This 4.5-star hotel is within close proximity of Shanghai Ocean Aquarium and Shanghai Natural Wild Insect Kingdom.
Rooms

Make yourself at home in one of the 151 individually furnished guestrooms, featuring refrigerators and LCD televisions. Amenities

Relax at the full-service spa, where you can enjoy massages, body treatments, and facials. If you&apos;re looking for recreational opportunities, you&apos;ll find an indoor pool, a spa tub, and a sauna. Additional amenities at this Art Deco hotel include complimentary wireless Internet access, concierge services, and gift shops/newsstands.
Dining

Enjoy a meal at one of the hotel&apos;s dining establishments, which include 5 restaurants and a coffee shop/café. From your room, you can also access 24-hour room service. Breakfast is available for a fee.
Business, Other Amenities

Featured amenities include a business center, express check-in, and express check-out. Event facilities at this hotel consist of a conference center and meeting rooms. A ferry terminal shuttle is available for a surcharge.
