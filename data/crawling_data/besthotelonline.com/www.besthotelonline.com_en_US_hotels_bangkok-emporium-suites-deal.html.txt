Hotel Description:


Emporium Suites by Chatrium presents elegant luxury living and unparalleled convenience in one of Bangkok’s most prestigious areas.

Situated above Emporium Shopping Complex, our 5 star hotel in Bangkok offers everything from designer shopping to easy access to the business district. Each guestroom provides exceptionally spacious comfort, with floor-to-ceiling windows, separated kitchen and the high quality furnishings. Dining at The Emporia combines delicious international cuisine with stunning city views, whilst cocktails can be sipped by the poolside at the Pool Bar. Stay fit in our modern Fitness Centre, with both male and female steam and sauna rooms before getting down to business in one of our five meeting rooms.

New feature with online newspaper available at Emporium Suites for all guests to enjoy reading complete environmentally friendly digital copies of your favorit publications.

How to get there

From Suvarnabhumi International Airport

By Car
From Suvarnabhumi International Airport
. Take the motorway and continue to take the expressway towards Bang Na and get off at Ardnarong Exit
. Turn left at traffic lights into Kasemrad Road and continue to Rama IV intersection
. Turn right into Rama IV Road and make the first left into Soi Sukhumvit 26
. Continue straight until you are facing Sukhumvit Road, then turn left
. You’ll find Emporium Suites by Chatrium on the left, at the corner of Soi Sukhumvit 24

From Don Mueang International Airport
. Take the motorway and continue to take the expressway towards Bang Na and get off at Ardnarong Exit
. Turn left at traffic lights into Kasemrad Road and continue to Rama IV intersection
. Turn right into Rama IV Road and make the first left into Soi Sukhumvit 26
. Continue straight until you are facing Sukhumvit Road, then turn left
. You’ll find Emporium Suites by Chatrium on the left, at the corner of Soi Sukhumvit 24


By Airport Rail Link/BTS Skytrain

.
From Suvanarbhumi Airport, take the Airport Rail Link to Phyathai Station, which will connect you to the BTS Skytrain where you can make the transfer to BTS Phrom Phong Station. Alternatively, you
can take the MRT to MRT Sukhumvit Station/BTS Asoke Station to make the transfer
From BTS Phrom Phong Station, use the direct covered link and walk through the adjoining
Emporium Shopping Complex to get to Emporium Suites by Chatrium
