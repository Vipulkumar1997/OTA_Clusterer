Address:
18 Merlin Street, North Point, Hong Kong


Hotel Description:


iclub Fortress Hill Hotel offers 338 chic and trendy guestrooms. It is a contemporary select-service hotel centrally located amidst Hong Kong's famed Fortress Hill and North Point districts, a prime centre for business and entertainment, access to Causeway Bay in FIVE minutes.

Convenient access to both WORK and PLAY

Centrally located in Fortress Hill, iclub Fortress Hill Hotel is at the crossroads of convenient shopping, dining and business in Hong Kong.

Nearby attractions

Access to the major transport hub in ONE minute

iclub Fortress Hill Hotel offers easy access to the main entertainment and financial district. It takes only 1-minute walk to the nearby tram station or 4-minute walk to arrive the Fortress Hill MTR station.

Access to Causeway Bay in FIVE minutes
The tram and subway can get you to Causeway Bay from our hotel in less than FIVE minutes, where the place is all the tourists and Hong Kong youngster will go for shopping and dining; Famous shopping malls such as Times Square and SOGO department store are all situated in Causeway Bay. Walking distance to the famous Causeway Bay Typhoon Shelter for the delicious local seafood and to experience the historical Sampan boat. Causeway Bay is also the location of Hong Kong Central Library, the largest public library in Hong Kong and Victoria Park, where is the place for series of Hong Kong MEGA events such as Hong Kong flower show and Hong Kong Brands and Products EXPO. Fire Dragon Dance at Tai Hang is also highlighted by Hong Kong Tourism Board. The Hotel is only 10 minutes’ drive to the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Center.

Access to the famous International cuisine restaurants in TEN minutes
"Tai Hang Eat Street" includes two long streets with lots of International cuisine and specialized local restaurants. In Tai Hang, there are many popular restaurants and bars along Tung Lo Wan Road and Wun Sha Street, you can enjoy your wonderful meal and spend your wonderful night time there. Lin Fa Temple is also located in Tai Hang, which was probably constructed in 1863 for the worship of Kwun Yam and it is an important historic landmark to the local community.

Access to the heritage construction and recreation facilitates in NO time
"Oi!" is located at 12 Oil Street in Fortress Hill which is a Grade II historic building that was built in 1908 as the clubhouse of the Royal Hong Kong Yacht Club. Nowadays, It is a new platform for the nurture young artists to realise their dreams in art. Come to know more about "Oi!" and join the activities.

If you like playing for fun, "Ryze Ultimate Trampoline Park" in North Point is one of your options. The venue divided into fews areas with variety of sport games for you to dissipate your energy in different ways. Let's move with your friends!
