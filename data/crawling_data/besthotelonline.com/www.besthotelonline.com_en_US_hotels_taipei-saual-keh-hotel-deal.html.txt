Hotel Description:

Property Location

A stay at Saual Keh Hotel places you in the heart of Taipei, convenient to Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall and Taipei 101.  This hotel is within close proximity of National Theater and National Concert Hall.
Rooms

Make yourself at home in one of the 43 individually decorated guestrooms, featuring refrigerators and DVD players. Wired and wireless Internet access is complimentary, and flat-screen televisions with satellite programming provide entertainment. Private bathrooms with showers feature rainfall showerheads and complimentary toiletries. Conveniences include safes and desks, as well as phones with free international calls.
Amenities

Take advantage of recreation opportunities such as a fitness center, or other amenities including complimentary wireless Internet access and concierge services.
Dining

Enjoy a satisfying meal at a restaurant serving guests of Saual Keh Hotel.
Business, Other Amenities

Featured amenities include a business center, limo/town car service, and complimentary newspapers in the lobby.
