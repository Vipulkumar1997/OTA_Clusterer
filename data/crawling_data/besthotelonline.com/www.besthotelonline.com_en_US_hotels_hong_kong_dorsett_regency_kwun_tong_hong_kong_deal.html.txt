Hotel Description:


Nestled in the heart of Kowloon East, Dorsett Kwun Tong - Hong Kong is an ideal spot from which to discover the city. The hotel is merely an eight-minutes walk to the MTR station and surrounded by APM, Telford Plaza, Emax, and other shopping venues. This contemporary haven is the perfect place for rest and relaxation for both business and leisure travelers. A stay here offers impeccable service and all the essential amenities needed to invigorate travelers. The hotel provides meeting facilities, a business center, Wi-Fi in public areas, and airport transfers to ensure guests the greatest comfort. When you are looking for comfortable and convenient accommodations in the city, make Dorsett Kwun Tong - Hong Kong your home away from home.
