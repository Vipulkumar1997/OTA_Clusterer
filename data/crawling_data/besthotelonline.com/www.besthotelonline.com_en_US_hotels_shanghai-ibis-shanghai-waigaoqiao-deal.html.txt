Hotel Description:

The hotel is located a close to the Gate 3 of Waigaoqiao Free Trade Zone in Pudong New Area, with walking distance to restaurants, bars and massage. The hotel has easy access to Waigaoqiao Port, Power Station and New International Expo Center. The hotel is 8 minutes walking distance from Metro line 6 station.
