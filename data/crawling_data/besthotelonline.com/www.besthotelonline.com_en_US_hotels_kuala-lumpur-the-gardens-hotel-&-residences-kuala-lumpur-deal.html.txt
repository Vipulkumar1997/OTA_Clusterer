Address:

Hotel Description:

Strategically located in between Kuala Lumpur and Petaling Jaya, well connected to light & commuter rails and with convenient access to major expressways, it offers a total of 627 premier accommodation with 448 elegantly furnished guestrooms at St Giles The Gardens - Grand Hotel & Residences and 179 luxuriously spacious full-services residences at St Giles The Gardens - Grand Hotel & Residences designed for executives on extended stay. Privacy is assured with separate entrance and lobby yet access to facilities are seamlessly linked on the 5th floor.
