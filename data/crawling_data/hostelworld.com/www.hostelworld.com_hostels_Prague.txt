About Prague

Prague, “the City of a Hundred Spires” as it is nicknamed, has more to offer than its pointy roofs. However, taking pictures of the city from a high view point should definitely be on your list.

At ground level, there are plenty of remarkable places to be visited. The Old Town (also known as Stare Mesto), is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Some of the most popular sites of the city include Prague Castle, Charles Bridge, a meeting point for painters and musicians during the summer and St Vitus Cathedral, the biggest church of Prague. Our hostels are ideally located, so you will be sure to be at a walking distance from the attractions of your choice as well as the train station and several metro stops.

If you want to optimise your stay, we suggest you book a room in a 16th century building or look for a hostel with a fitness center. Also keep an eye for the hostels which offer pub crawls!
