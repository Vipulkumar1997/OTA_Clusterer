About Amsterdam

Amsterdam is not only a must-visit destination for backpackers in Europe, but the perfect place to find a cheap hostel for a long weekend of fun with friends.
Being one of Europe’s most popular places to party, you won’t have any trouble finding hostels with their own bar. Get ready for a night out with a few drinks in the hostel bar,
Hostels in Amsterdam are super clean, convenient and comfortable, which means you’ve got the perfect place to crash after a long night.
There are a few options for where to stay in Amsterdam. You can opt for the city centre itself,
Or, you can stay somewhere in the canal loop for a bit more breathing space. Everything is still walkable but you can escape at the end of the day.
The most popular attractions for lovers of art and history are the Rijksmuseum,
Some cool areas to check out are De Pijp, which is home to Amsterdam’s most popular market, Albert Cuyp Markt. It’s also packed with great places for brunch.
