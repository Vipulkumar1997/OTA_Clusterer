About Budapest

Budapest is a hidden gem in Europe. You might not have heard a lot about the capital city of Hungary before, but once you set a foot there, you will definitely want to go back.
low-priced restaurants in Budapest
free things to do in the Hungarian capital
Not only are hostels cheap in Budapest, but you definitely get your money’s worth and more! From free breakfast to free walking tours as well as yoga and cooking classes,
Where to party in Budapest: ruin bars, spa parties and the Sziget Festival
Budapest is not only about parties, it is also about architecture, history and art. Our hostels are centrally located so you will easily find your way to the main city attractions
