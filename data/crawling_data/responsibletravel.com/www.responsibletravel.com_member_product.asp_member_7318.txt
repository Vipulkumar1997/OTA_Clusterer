Svalbard and Northern Lights holiday


Did you know that Spitsbergen is one of the few places on Earth you can see the northern lights during day time? From mid November to mid February you can catch the aurora all day, but already late September we see the green light dancing on the sky in the evenings. 
Green Sky is a program tailored to give you as a guest the best chance to see the northern lights, but also experience the magic of the arctic in a small city close to the North Pole. During the 4 days you will walk with snowshoes and headlamp, try dogsledding and learn more about the local trappers and Svalbard history.
