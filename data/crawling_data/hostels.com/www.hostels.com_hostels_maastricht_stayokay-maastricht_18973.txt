Public transport
The hostel is a 15-minute walk from Maastricht station. You could take bus numbers 1, 9, 11, 56, 57 or 58 until the Avenue Céramique stop. It’s a 10-minute walk from the bus stop to the hostel. To plan the train trip we recommend to use www.ns.nl.

Private transport
From the north: Follow the A2 southwards in the direction of Maastricht. Turn right into the Viaductweg. After 1.7 kilometres take the exit to the city centre (Centrum). Turn right after 300 metres into the Boscherweg heading towards the centre. Take a left into the Maasboulevard after 350 metres. From the south: Follow the A2 northwards in the direction of Maastricht. Take the Europaplein exit heading towards Tongeren (N287). After 1.8 kilometres take the exit to the city centre west (Centrum-West). Take a right into the Maasboulevard after crossing John F. Kennedy bridge.

Located Near


Stayokay Maastricht is ideally located along the river the Maas in the heart of Maastricht. Visit the casemates, enjoy a canal tour through the city or walk along the grape fields and try some delicious wine in the vineyards. Relax in the Thermae 2000 in Valkenburg, or go to Vaals where the borders of three countries come together.

Popular surroundings:
- Casemates - 3 km
- Fort St. Pieter - 6 km
- Thermae 2000 - 12 km
- Amusement Park De Valkenier - 13 km
- National Park Hoge Kempen - 22 km
