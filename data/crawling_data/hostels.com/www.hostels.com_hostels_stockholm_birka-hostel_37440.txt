Facilities






The perfect location in the heart of Stockholm

Mordern hostel in the Capital of Scandinavia for those who seeks for a good rate and a comfortable stay.

We offer private rooms and dormitories. Vi also offer a dormitory for only women.

We offer free Wi-Fi in the common areas. We have a common and a fully equipped kitchen and a friendly social room were you can cook, meet new people or prepare for your adventurous in the city.

All of us att Birka Hostel value the security and safety of our customers and as such all the common areas are protected by cameras and also all rooms and common areas are equipped with the latest sprinkler systems which are connected to Stockholms emergency services.

Coffe, tea and pasta is always free and available in the kitchen

Laundry service is offered at a fee.

Breakfast is served daily for an additional fee.
Monday – Friday 7:30 – 10:00
Saturday – Sunday 8:00 – 10:00

Directions

You can walk to our hostel from the central station, it takes about 15 minutes.
The easiest is to take the metro, it is just one stop, the station is called Hötorget and is located on the green line. Choose direction towards: Alvik, Åkeshov or Hässelby Strand.
Take the exit Tunnelgatan and walk to the right when you get up from the stairs.
