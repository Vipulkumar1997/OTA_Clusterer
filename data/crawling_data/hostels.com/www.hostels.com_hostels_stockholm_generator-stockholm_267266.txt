Facilities






Generator Stockholm is here, complete with 24 hr reception & with a duty manager always around and happy to help. It has flexible and spacious social areas, filled with beautiful design touches. Property features impressive ground floor chill-out lounge, restaurant and bar, where we've put a Nordic twist on the cocktail menu.

Stay connected with free Wi-Fi and share those holiday snaps. In need of some clean clothes? The laundry is open 24 hours for washing, drying and ironing. Come to our travel shop for city tour tickets and more. You forgot your toothbrush or want a travel journal to keep track of your experiences? Visit our GenStore for exclusive Generator products. Let us help you make the most of your stay! Our hostel staff are incredibly friendly, multilingual and know the city inside out. They’re on hand to help you with whatever info you need, just ask.

Just a 5 minute walk from the central station, Generator Stockholm is located in the chic neighbourhood of Torsgatan. Renowned for its architecture, contemporary art and alternative fashion, Stockholm adds some Nordic nuances to the Generator family. The Vasa Museum and the Royal Palace is just a short walk. Enjoy the local delicacies and fall in love with the city with a spot of sightseeing by boat. Take a stroll and enjoy Nordic style coffee or head down to one of the many vintage stores.

All room types are en suite and include bed linen. Towels included in Double/Twin & Premium categories. Classic Generator style brand design throughout the building.

From the Airport:
Fastest journey is to take the Arlanda Express directly to Stockholm Central Station.

From the Station:
Exit the station and turn left onto Vasaplan,  turn right onto Östra Järnvägsgatan
Continue straight to stay on Östra Järnvägsgatan until you reachNorra Bantorget
Norra Bantorget turns slightly left and becomes Torsgatan. You will find us at number 10.
