Facilities






Bru Bar and Hostel is Cork City's 2 minute walk from the bus and train stations right in the city centre.

All rooms feature private bathrooms, most are en-suite. The hostel as a whole is finished to a very high level. Bru also features free wi fi & internet access as well as a full bar where you can relax and enjoy a pint of the black stuff with fellow travellers.  Hostel rules. No smoking anywhere inside hostel.

NB: Our 3 bed private rooms have a double bed and a single bunk.

The Hostel is a quite zone at ALL times. Respect the staff and other guests. We will end the stay of persons/groups who smoke, create disturbance, are offensive or have severe lack of common sense. No refunds will be given.

***LARGE BOOKINGS BOOKED IN 1'S 2'S 3'S 4'S ETC IN AN EFFORT TO COME IN UNDER THE RADAR WILL BE IDENTIFIED AND CANCELLED OR REMOVED FROM THE HOSTEL UPON IDENTIFICATION. NO REFUNDS WILL BE GIVEN.
ANY PERSON INTOXICATED OR UNDER THE INFLUENCE OF DRUGS  UPON ARRIVAL WILL BE REFUSED ENTRY.

Directions
Show Map
Hide Map


From the train station, turn left and continue walking until you see the LeisurePlex in front of you. Bru Bar & Hostel is across the street.

From the bus station, cross the bridge and walk towards an old church up on the hill in front of you. When you pass the LeisurePlex on your left, walk around the corner and you will see Bru Bar & Hostel across the road.

From Air Coach, City Link drop off. Cross the street and walk up the small one one lane by the Metropol Hotel to MacCurtain street. Turn right and you will see the flags of the Hostel 100meters away on the other side of the street.

From the airport hop on a bus and get off at the bus station, follow the above directions.
