Facilities






DREAM Hostel Warsaw is the largest hostel in Warsaw, which occupies 4 floors in a separate 5-storey building, which is recognized as an architectural monument. The building was made overhaul and installed modern systems of ventilation, fire protection, soundproofing and lift, due to this building fully equipped for comfortable accommodation of guests from around the world. All this is complemented by a stylish design that we have done thinking about the comfort and safety of every guest.
There is a bar in common room where you can order breakfast or delicious cocktail and just have a good time. You will be surprised by the quality and unsurpassed taste.

Must-Read Hostel Info

This property has specific Terms & Conditions.

Dream Hostel Warsaw Policies & Conditions:

Check in from 14:00.
Check out before 11:00.
Please note that early check-in and late check-out are available at surcharge.

Cancellation policy: 48 h before arrival date.
Payment upon arrival by cash, credit cards, debit cards.

Taxes included.
Breakfast not included - 15 PLN (~3,5 EUR) per person per day.

General:
Breakfast from 7:30 to 10:30.
No curfew.
24/7 reception.

Please note that guests are required to show a valid passport  or ID card upon check-in.
Please note that guests under the age of 18 years are not allowed to stay in shared rooms. Guests under 18 years are allowed to be accommodated only in private rooms with parents or siblings over 18 years. Children 7 years and younger stay for free using existing beds. We do not have age limit, but we advise you to book private room if you are over 65.

Group reservations:
Please note that for the group bookings for more than 6 people, different prepayment policies may apply. Please note that if the reservations are made for 6 guests, the hostel has a right to charge the prepayment from the card. Large groups (more than 30 people) is a subject to availability. The Hostel Manager reserves the right not to accept large groups, depending on the individual situation (availability, number of nights etc) or to charge the prepayment. Please contact the property directly for the group reservation details.

When you pass the roundabout with the fake palmtree that means you are close to us, bus will turn left to the Nowy Swiat street. Get off the bus at the final station Pl. Located Near


Dream Hostel Warsaw is located in Warsaw in the center of Old Town on the Krakivske Predmistie, 55 (Krakowskie Przedmieście).
This street is one of the most prestigious streets in Warsaw and the best places for walking. The Old Town Market Square (Rynek Starego Miasta) is 2 minutes from the hostel.There are many historical monuments in this street that are worth to look: Presidential Palace, Potocki Palace, Warsaw University, Church of St. Anne, monument of Adam Mytskevych etc.
