Directions

We're right next to Porto's main monument the Clerigos Tower which can be seen from most parts of the city. Just ask for directions to the Clerigos Tower and you will find us!

From Campanha train station:
If you're coming to Porto by train, this is where you'll arrive to. From there take another train to Sao Bento train station, using the same ticket. Go out via the Almeida Garrett exit and once you're outside the station, walk to the main avenue, Aliados. Then go straight ahead up Clerigos Street to the church on the top. You will find us there, on the left corner.

From the airport:
Take the metro E line (purple) to Trindade station. Change there to the D line (yellow), going in the direction of Santo Ovidio, and get off two stops down the line at Sao Bento Station. Go out via the Almeida Garrett exit and once you're outside the station, walk to the main avenue, Aliados. Then go straight ahead up Clerigos Street to the church on the top. You will find us there, on the left corner.

Arriving in Porto by domestic bus:
The Rede Expresso buses stop at Campo 24 De Agosto, Take any line until Trindade station, change to D line (yellow), going in the direction of Santo Ovidio, and get off at Sao Bento Station. Walk to the main avenue Aliados. Then go straight ahead up Clerigos Street to the church on thetop. You will find us there, on the left corner.
Otherwise, the Renex buses stop at Joao Chagas (Cordoaria) garden. You have to cross the garden towards the big Clerigos tower, then go around it and you'll find us there, on the corner; when you're going down it's on your right.

Arriving in Porto by international bus:
You'll be at Casa da Musica interface, so get the metro to come to us. Take the metro E line (purple)in the direction of Campanha / Estadio Dragao / Fanzeres to Trindade station. Change there to the D line (yellow), going in the direction of Santo Ovidio, and get off two stops down the line at Sao Bento Station. Go out via the Almeida Garrett exit and once you're outside the station, walk to the main avenue, Aliados. Then go straight ahead up Clerigos Street to the church on the top. You will find us there, on the left corner.

From the airport:
Take the metro E line (purple) to Trindade station. Change there to the D line (yellow), going in the direction of Santo Ovidio, and get off two stops down the line at Sao Bento Station. Go out via the Almeida Garrett exit and once you're outside the station, walk to the main avenue, Aliados. Then go straight ahead up Clerigos Street to the church on the top. You will find us there, on the left corner.
