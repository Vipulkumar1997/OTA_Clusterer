Welcome to the heart of Austin!  We are the only hostel in the city center!  Located at the epicenter of downtown, the Firehouse Hostel & Lounge is within walking distance to everything this extraordinary city has to offer.  The building is tucked away just around the corner from historic 6th Street with its legendary nightlife and live music scene.  The Firehouse is your perfect oasis amid all the action.

Featured in Lonely Planet, the Guardian, the Washington Post, and on the Travel Channel!

The Firehouse Hostel is located in the oldest standing fire station in Austin, originally built in 1885.  A real, historic firehouse!  Locally owned and operated.  Started by travelers for travelers, the hostel first opened in 2013 after an extensive renovation project that updated the building’s interior with modern amenities.  Today, the Firehouse is the largest hostel in Texas with capacity for over 70 guests.

We provide…
•	clean, secure, and comfortable accommodation
•	24-hour reception
•	secure luggage storage
•	free linens and towels
•	free Wi-Fi
•	free healthy breakfast

The attached Firehouse Lounge is a great place to meet travelers and locals, sip on classically prepared cocktails or locally brewed beers, and enjoy live music.  Enter this speakeasy-style craft cocktail bar through a hidden bookshelf in the hostel lobby.

Guests love…
•	our fun, friendly, and helpful staff
•	the character and old world feel of the property
•	our amazing breakfast with fresh fruit, cereal, pastries, bagels, and more
•	our pub crawls (three times per week) exploring off the beaten path bars and venues
•	our unbeatable location, close to all the sightseeing, attractions, venues, and nightlife

Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are here to help make your stay exciting and unforgettable.  Austin’s new bike-share program has several stations only a few blocks away.  We are conveniently close to bus stops for all the major bus routes.  You can even take the bus to/from the airport for only $1.50!

If you’re looking for a fun and unique way to explore and experience Austin, the Firehouse is THE place to stay!

Taxes not included (additional 15%)
Guests must be 18 and up unless traveling with a parent
The Firehouse Lounge is limited to guests 21 and up only
Parking is not included but is available at several nearby garages and lots.

Located in the oldest standing firehouse in Austin, built in 1886, the Firehouse Hostel is opening this fall to provide Austin area travelers with an affordable and friendly option in Downtown Austin.  We offer clean and secure accommodation, 24 hr reception, laundry facilities, secure luggage storage, free wifi and a complimentary, healthy breakfast.

The attached Firehouse Lounge is a great place to meet travelers and locals, sip on a classically prepared cocktail or locally brewed beer and enjoy live, local music.

The Firehouse Hostel and Lounge is truly a one of a kind establishment in downtown Austin.  Come experience what the Firehouse has to offer.

From the Airport

Public Bus - $1
The bus stops only a few blocks from our front door!
Walk to the lower level of the ABIA and board bus 100 Inbound. Get off at 4th and Congress. Cross the street heading east, walk 1 block to Brazos Street and turn left. Walk north 2 blocks and you have arrived. Look for the red awnings

Driving:
Upon exiting the airport turn left onto State Hwy 71 heading west. Stay on Hwy 71 exit I-35 N then take exit 234C. Turn left onto E 6th St Turn right onto Brazos St. There are two parking garages and two lots within two blocks of the Hostel .

Supper Shuttle
Supper Shuttle offers door to door drop off and pick up 24 hours a day 7 days a week
Cost is $15 per person. Pre book your pick up at http://www.supershuttle.com/

From the Greyhound Station
Take the number 10 bus Southbound to the 6th and Congress St stop
Walk one block east to Brazos St.
Walk half a block North to 605 Brazos St




Located Near


The Firehouse Hostel is located at 605 Brazos St, in the heart of Austin's downtown district.  We are just steps away from an enormous selection of restaurants, bars, shops and attractions.  Austin's historic 6th Street, the epicenter of Austin's nightlife and events, is only a block away.  Local area attractions such as Barton Springs, Zilker Park, the State Capital, and The University of Texas are a short bike or bus ride away.
