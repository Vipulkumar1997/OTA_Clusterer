Explore the UK by Rail

For a unique and exciting way to explore all that the UK has to offer, our rail holidays are a great value choice whether travelling alone, with friends or with the whole family. What’s more, our Explore the UK by Rail packages encompass all corners of the country, meaning that nowhere is off limits when discovering the UK by rail this year.
Read more


With your chosen train holiday, you simply choose the city in which you want to be based, select your hotel and then use the rail network to explore the surrounding areas and points of interest. All of the hotels are also located within a one mile radius of the rail station, meaning that travel within your chosen region couldn’t be easier.
Read more


What makes our Explore the UK rail holidays unique, is that there is no tour-guide structure to your trip, instead your flexible itinerary is chosen by you so you can enjoy and explore your chosen destinations and attractions at your own leisure. On a scenic rail break you can travel on almost any train within a region with very few restrictions.
