Whatever you want from your next short break, our hotels in England page is the perfect starting point when planning a getaway. Whether you want a city break, a visit to an attraction or theme park with the family, a country escape or a mini-holiday by the sea, England has something to offer everyone.
Our England hotels range from budget accommodation to luxury retreats. Wonderfully diverse, you can travel North or South and discover somewhere brilliant, fascinating and exciting! For example, you could choose to visit the rolling countryside and historic cities of
Yorkshire
- recently voted one of the world's top visitor destinations - or head to the sunny
South-West
and enjoy a cream tea on the English Riviera.

Mad about theatre? We're famous for our
theatre breaks
, and whether you stay in a
central London hotel
for a trip to the glittering West End or venture to Shakespeare's home town and see the
RSC in Stratford-upon-Avon
, it's sure to add something special to your getaway.

Perhaps you'd like to sample sumptuous cuisine in one of the country's cultural and cosmopolitan hubs? Check out our
hotels in Birmingham
and Manchester hotels
Alternatively, our
two night dinner deal packages
? with dinner included on the first night of your stay offer great value, and these breaks are located right across the country.
And, if you're looking to escape, unwind, relax or reconnect with nature, then look no further than our
Lake District hotels
- England's premier destination for outdoor excursions!
