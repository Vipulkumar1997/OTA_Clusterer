Prague, Czech Republic








Situated in the north west of the Czech Republic, the capital city of Prague offers a captivating backdrop of historical attractions, striking architecture and an excellent choice of hotels on both sides of the River Vltava. Many of our hotels in Prague are located in the city's compact centre which is embellished in rich history.
Prague is one of Europe's most popular city-break destinations and is therefore very easy to travel to. Most UK airport offer flights to Prague with up to five flights a day from London Heathrow alone. Choose from our excellent range of
Prague flight breaks
when planning your trip to the "city of a thousand spires"
Prague Old Town Square
is the centre of the Medieval city and now houses many fantastic eateries as well as a host of trendy bars. From here you can explore the Jewish Quarter, take in the splendor of the astronomical clock and stroll across the 15th century Charles Bridge.

Prague Castle
is one of the world's largest and oldest castles and is one of Prague's most popular tourist destinations. The castle contains the Crown Jewels of the Kings of Bohemia although they are only exhibited on special occasions. St Vitus Cathedral is also located within the castle. Spectacular Gothic columns, soaring vaults and stained glass windows make the cathedral a must-see during your break in Prague.

Prague City Museum
explores the history of Prague since prehistoric times to the present day. The museum houses millions of items and several exhibitions devoted to natural history, archaeology, science and the arts.
