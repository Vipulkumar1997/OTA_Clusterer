About Our North Iceland Holidays


Discover the beautiful Lake Myvatn geothermal area.

Visit the famous Godafoss waterfall and the Dimmuborgir lava formations.

Relax in the Lake Myvatn Nature Baths (optional entry).

Incredible Iceland breaks also include a Search for the Northern Lights tour - Northern Iceland is one of the best places to witness the beautiful Aurora Borealis

There are also a range of optional excursions to add to your Incredible Iceland break including Whale Watching, Superjeep tours and even a trip to a beer spa!

















Discover the stunning North Iceland landscape with our exclusive Incredible Iceland holidays. Flying from a range regional airports, these are the only direct flights to Akureyri from the UK and all come with a generous baggage allowance of 20kgs per person. Guests also benefit from the services of a Super Break representative in Akureyri and at the airports in the UK as well as transfers from Aukureyri Airport to the hotels. All flight departures from the UK are at 08:30am with arrival back to the UK at 15:30. With flight times of around three hours, you'll be able to make the most of your time on your Northern Iceland holiday.


Our North Iceland Holidays include a choice of five hotels, from the 3* Hotel Nordurland to the 4* Saeluhus Akureyri. Base yourself in the centre of town or the winter wonderland setting of Kjarnaskogur, just outside Akureyri. The Saeluhus Akureyri even offers private balconies with hot tubs, perfect for relaxing under the stars or even the Northern Lights.
