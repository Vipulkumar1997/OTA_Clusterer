What's Included on a Walking Break?
















It's true that there's nothing quite like escaping to the peaceful realms of the British countryside for a recharge of those tired-out batteries, and with a Walking Break you can enjoy two nights away exploring the idyllic spot of your choice.

Each Walking Break includes:


Two-night hotel stay

Breakfast each morning

Dinner on the first evening

Local map & walking guidebook

Packed lunch


Uncover the hidden gems of the scenic UK countryside. Explore the picturesque Peak District, the idyllic Cotswolds or the wilds of the Northumberland coastline.
