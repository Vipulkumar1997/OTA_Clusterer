KidZania London Breaks

The UK’s first educational entertainment experience, KidZania is a great attraction for children to experience what it’s like working in different careers in a fun-filled, inspiring setting.

About KidZania London

KidZania is a real-life role-play experience for 4-14 year olds which inspires children to explore the world of opportunities, and features over 60 unique professional environments for children to choose from, including police stations, hospitals, TV studios and even an aviation academy. Each role-play experience is designed to teach children essential life skills including financial literacy, team work and independence, along with inspiring their future careers.

KidZania London Highlights


Children can take part in a play at the Acting Academy and perform in front of friends and family

Bring your favourite Pokémon characters to life in the Animation Studio

There are 60 zones which combine learning and reality with fun and entertainment



What Makes it Super

Kids get to be in charge at KidZania, it’s a great concept as children can act out their dream job and be inspired by each of the experiences. KidZania is designed to empower and inspire children while teaching them useful life skills.
