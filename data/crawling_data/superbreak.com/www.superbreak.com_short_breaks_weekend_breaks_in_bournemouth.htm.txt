Bournemouth Top Tip

A great spot to visit in Bournemouth is the Lower Gardens park, not far from the seafront. On a Sunday afternoon you will find shows on at the park's bandstand, which is the perfect place to relax and enjoy the sunshine on a summer weekend break in Bournemouth.
