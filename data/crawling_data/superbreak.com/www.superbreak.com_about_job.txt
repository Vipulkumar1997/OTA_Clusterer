Super Break Jobs

We currently have over 150 members of staff working for us. From the sales team who work closely with Travel Agents to the contracts team that secure our fantastic hotels and offers, the contact centre team who are on hand to advise customers with their booking or the web team who help make it all work; there are plenty of career opportunities within Super Break.

Super Break are a well-established travel company having been in operation for over 30 years. During that time Super Break has gone from selling hotel beds only to selling a variety of products and breaks including West End theatre tickets, P&O; mini cruises, Eurostar breaks, flights, O2 concerts and rail packages.

At our modern offices in central York we offer a variety of staff benefits including charity days, familiarisation trips, free fruit and refreshments and generous staff discounts on our products.

If you wish to apply for any of the jobs listed below, please send a current CV with a covering letter to careers@SuperBreak.com

Department: Trade

Maintain regular communication with sales calls, performance updates and annual business reviews across the agency networks and at head office level.

Oversee the travel trade element of the company’s public relations activity, ensuring Super Break’s high profile as a much-loved supply partner is maintained.

Actively source and feedback market, competitor, product and service feedback via the sales team which can assist the development of the business.

Lead the team to champion social media with Trade

Ensure agent problems and complaints are resolved quickly and efficiently, in line with the customer experience programme

Collapse Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable Supervisor

Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable Supervisor

Department:
Finance

Reporting to:
Ledger Manager

Location:
York

Key Objectives/Primary Job Purpose:

Super Break have a brand-new position for an Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable Supervisor to take responsibly of the functions across Super Break and the wider group, ensuring that key processes are closely followed, and deadlines are met. This is a great opportunity to focus on making improvements in processes across both brands and support the development of the team.

Key Areas of Responsibility:


Day to day management of AP and AR functions across Super Break and the wider group

Identify and drive process improvements across the team to position the business for future scalability and growth

Management of outsourced functions to develop and simplify processes and ensure outsourced functions are utilised to full potential

Daily management of relevant inboxes to ensure any escalations are flagged quickly and effectively

Ensure any self-bill invoices are reconciled to reservations database and any differences investigated

Management of sales invoicing processes, ensuring timely and accurate invoicing

Oversee timely and accurate posting and allocation of all incoming receipts - BACS, Direct Debits, Credit Cards, Payment Agencies, Vouchers

Ensuring that all supplier purchases and employee expenses are appropriately authorised and recorded in line with the business Delegation of Authority

Ownership of the SICON purchase order processing system, providing the wider business with assistance with this process

Ensuring accurate processing of Corporate credit cards & employee expenses, including ensuring adherence to the company’s Travel & Entertaining Policy

Responsibility for all supplier payments, ensuring payment terms are adhered to

Management of Virtual Credit Card processes

Oversee timely and accurate posting and allocation of all outgoing payments - BACS, Direct Debits, Virtual Credit Cards

To understand and provide support with daily bank reconciliations ensuring any reconciling items are investigated and resolved

Assist the group treasury function in the production of weekly / monthly / annual cashflow budgets & forecasts

Manage escalated queries from outsourced functions, suppliers, customers and travel agents

Ensuring that all processes owned by the AP and AR teams are adequately controlled

Experience in a similar role or exposure to an AP & AR function

Strong people management and development skills

Advanced Excel skills (pivot tables, Vlookup)

Excellent analytical skills and attention to detail

Experience of dealing in different currencies

Ability to work collaboratively across different departments
