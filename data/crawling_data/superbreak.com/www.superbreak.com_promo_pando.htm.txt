P & O Mini Cruises







Looking for a special way to celebrate the festive season. 
				Our
Christmas and New Year mini-cruise breaks
are are a great way to celebrate. 
				We especially love this amazing
Christmas shopping mini-cruise
which visits 3 different cities - perfect for exploring the Christmas markets.

Our P&O Mini Cruises offer a great way to combine a relaxing overnight cruise with a day out in some of the most popular city break destinations in Europe. 
				Travelling with P&O Mini Cruises you can relax and travel in comfort as you enjoy some of the great on-board entertainment and facilities available with P&O Mini Cruises. 
				From dining in the buffet restaurant or the Brasserie, to watching a film in the onboard cinema, trying your chance at the roulette wheel or simply sitting back and listing to the 
				resident band there is plenty to keep you entertained on board a P&O Mini Cruise to Amsterdam, Rotterdam or Bruges.

You can also extend your stay, and combine both 2 nights on board, plus a couple of nights in a great hotel in your chosen city.

With P&O Mini Cruises you can enjoy a great value
3 or 4 night break to Amsterdam or Bruges from just £95pp
. 
				Simply follow the links below to check prices and availability.

We have also teamed up with P&O; to bring you some superb Themed Mini Cruises – 
				please see our
events pages
.

Alternatively, create your own break sailing from Hull to Zeebrugge or Rotterdam and travel on to enjoy a day in Ostend, Ghent, Antwerp, the Hague or Brussels (transfers to these 
				cities is not included in the price). Call
01904 717 362
for more information on the build-your-own breaks.

Important Information


Cruise breaks to Amsterdam and Bruges include coach transfers.

Brussels, Ghent and Ostend can be reached by train from Bruges (rail travel is not included in the price).

Car parking is available at Hull. Charges apply at all car parks.

All cabins are bunks and will require one passenger to use a ladder.
