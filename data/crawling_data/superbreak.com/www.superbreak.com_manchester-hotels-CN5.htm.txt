Booking a
hotel in Manchester
is a must. The cosmopolitan city is known for being the lively capital of the North West, with a superb range of
Manchester
hotels perfect for discovering the city's unique vibrant atmosphere. The city offers ground-breaking architecture such as
Beetham Tower
, world class shopping at the
Trafford Centre
, and amazing nightlife comprising of everything from Real Ale pubs to cutting-edge clubs.
There is something for everyone staying at our
hotels in Manchester
to discover. From an exciting music scene and world-class sporting events to family days out and attractions to suit all tastes, Manchester has so much to offer on a short break.
Head to
Manchester Arena
for a choice of live concerts and entertainment performing year round, or for a more intimate gig
The Manchester Academy
offers the perfect setting appealing to all musical tastes.

Sports fans visiting the city can take a trip to the home of
Manchester United
for a game at Old Trafford, or head to the Etihad Stadium to the see
Manchester City F.C.
in action. You can also add
Manchester United Museum and Tour tickets
or
Manchester City FC Stadium and Club Tour tickets
to any hotel booking.

Need something for the kids? Then why not visit the
LEGOLAND? Discovery centre
and stay in a nearby Manchester hotel. Based at the Trafford centre, the attraction is easily accessible and great for the kids.

For those willing to venture a bit further out, make sure you visit the world famous
Chester Zoo
. As the UK's most popular Zoo with 1.4 million visitors a year, this is your chance to explore 110 acres of award-winning zoological gardens.

Manchester is easily accessible, with a nearby airport and great rail connections, plus a great selection of hotels spread throughout the city, making planning your short break easy and hassle free. For more information on the city, why not visit our
Manchester Travel Guide
. Alternatively,
explore more UK weekend breaks
.
