City Cruises London

An excellent way to see the best of the capital, take a trip on the Thames with City Cruises.

About London City Cruises

There’s no more magical way to see some of London's best sights than enjoying a cruise on the River Thames. Sail through the very heart of the capital and enjoy the gorgeous sights and sounds of the vibrant city. Soak up the life of the busy city from the tranquil calm of the water and enjoy the very best views of London’s most strikingly beautiful monuments; see the O2 Arena and Canary Wharf, the impressive St Paul’s Cathedral and the pretty Southbank, glide underneath London’s famous bridges and be over-awed by the London Eye and the Houses of Parliament.

City Cruises London Highlights


Enjoy tasty sandwiches, scrumptious scones and refreshing tea on the Afternoon Tea Cruise

The London Showboat Cruise includes a 4-course meal and entertainment by professional singer

A delicious two-course lunch is included in the Classic Lunch Cruise

Breathtaking views of London at night on the River Lights Cruise

Booking includes hotel



What Makes it Super

The combination of delicious food, first class entertainment and stunning views offers an unbeatable London experience. The view of iconic London monuments by day or night truly makes for a wonderful experience.
