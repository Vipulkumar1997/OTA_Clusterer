Inspiration From The Blog

The Lake District is the perfect place to visit for long country walks, stunning views and, yep, food! And with all that exercise, you’re bound to get hungry pretty quickly! Take a peek at our top picks for places to eat in The Lake District, from dawn ’til dusk. Try the cute Copper Pot Cafe in Ambleside for breakfast, Chocobar in Bowness for a mid-walk lunch before heading to the iconic Wasdale Head Inn for dinner and drinks.
