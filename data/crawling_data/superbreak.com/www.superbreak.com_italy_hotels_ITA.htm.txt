Hotels in Italy





Adriatic Riviera

Set against a spectacular backdrop of rolling countryside peppered with ancient castles and elegant towns, the Adriatic Riviera is a popular beach destination. Visit the chic boutiques of Riccione, experience exhilarating water sports along the coast or simply relax on one of the many golden beaches. Be sure to visit Pesaro, rich in historical buildings and stunning architecture.

Bologna, Modena & Northern Italy

This picturesque region is brimming with a serene landscape, fairy-tale castles, historic cities and medieval cathedrals. Visit Bologna for fine architecture, lush parks and shopping or discover Modena’s automobile heritage. From Ravenna’s golden beaches, Rimini’s fabulous nightlife and Parma’s delicious cuisine, some might say this beautiful region has it all.

Florence

Florence (Firenze), the epicentre of the Italian Renaissance, is the art-lovers favourite destination. Influenced by Leonardo, Dante, Machiavelli and Michelangelo, Florence is filled with fabulous architecture, museums and galleries. This compact city is the
capital of Tuscany
, a stunning province of hills, mountains and, of course, Chianti.

Italian Lakes

Northern Italy includes the regions of Aosta, Emilia-Romagna, Friuli, Liguria, Lombardy, Piedmont, Trentino and Veneto. A prosperous industrial and agricultural area, it is enclosed in the north by the Alps and contains the magnificent Italian Lakes.

Milan

Stylish and fast-paced Milan is a major trailblazer for fashion and design. Enjoy shopping in one of the capitals of style and absorb the city’s unique culture. Alternatively Milan has plenty of historical sites and world class venues for sport, opera and trade fairs, making it a perfect destination for any discerning tourist.

Neapolitan Riviera

The Neapolitan Riviera is the rugged and beautiful coastline of the Campania region in Italy. A walker’s paradise, it includes lively Naples, the ruins of Herculaneum and Pompeii, the active
Mount Vesuvius
and a multitude of picturesque towns.

Pisa

The gorgeous city of Pisa, located in Tuscany, Central Italy, is the perfect location for an absorbing and cultured short break. Sporting a wealth of art and history, Pisa is renowned for its breath-taking landmarks and tourist hotspots. Visit the famous
Leaning Tower of Pisa
, the star of the stunning Campo dei Miracoli. The Field of Miracles also includes the Duomo, Baptistry and Camposanto. These and many more architectural gems are a legacy of Pisa’s Golden Age as a maritime power from the 11th to 13th century.

Rome

By booking a hotel in Rome, you are opening yourself up to one of the most culturally intoxicating cities in the world. From ancient Rome to the thoroughly modern-day fashion stores, the Eternal City boasts an endless list of attractions and an abundance of Rome hotels. Rome’s renowned historic attractions include the Vatican with Saint Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel, the Colosseum, the Roman Forum, and the Pantheon. But don’t leave without throwing a coin in the Trevi Fountain and climbing the Spanish Steps. Rome is also home to two airports, making travel that little bit easier, you can book
Rome flights
alongside your hotel with SuperBreak. Make the most of your short break in Rome by pre-booking a sightseeing tour with us along with one of our many
hotels in Rome
.

Tuscany

This picturesque region is blessed with rolling hillsides, beautiful beaches and plenty of local charm!  A treasure trove of culture and history, Tuscany is renowned for its famous wines and delicious cuisine. Be sure to explore the wealthy Arezzo with its Gothic Cathedral and magnificent Fortress or head to the exclusive coastal resort of Forte dei Marmi, home to warm powdery shores, designer shops and fashionable restaurants.

Venice

Known as ‘The Floating City’, Venice is a truly unique and beautiful destination off the north east coast of Italy, with a fantastic range of
Venice hotels
to choose from. Laced with canals, the whole city is built around the curving Grand Canal and is full of historic buildings. Our Venice hotels are within walking distance of the many galleries, museums and grand cathedrals replete with breathtaking artworks from the world’s masters.

Verona

Verona is one of Italy's loveliest towns with pink-hued medieval buildings. Nearby peaceful Trento is located in a spectacular mountainous region. Sleek Vicenza is blessed with outstanding 16th century buildings by the architect Andrea Palladio.
