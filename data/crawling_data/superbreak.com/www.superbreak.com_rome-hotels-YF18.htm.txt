Colosseum, Rome








By booking a hotel in Rome, you are opening yourself up to one of the most culturally intoxicating cities in the world. From ancient Rome to the thoroughly modern-day fashion stores, the Eternal City boasts an endless list of attractions and an abundance of Rome hotels. Rome’s renowned historic attractions include the Vatican with Saint Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel, the Colosseum, the Roman Forum, and the Pantheon. But don’t leave without throwing a coin in the Trevi Fountain and climbing the Spanish Steps. Rome is also home to two airports, making travel that little bit easier, you can book
Rome flights
alongside your hotel with SuperBreak. Make the most of your short break in Rome by pre-booking a sightseeing tour with us along with one of our many
hotels in Rome
.
Perfect for a cultural getaway our hotels in Rome are located throughout the city and range from budget 2* accommodation to 5* luxurious hotels. Most of the historic sites are within walking distance of one another, so by booking any of our Rome hotels you can set off on foot and see all that this ancient city has to offer.
Famous for its local delicacies, a range of cafés, bistros and restaurants can be found close to most hotels in Rome, with a great choice in the popular districts of
Piazza Navona
,
Centro Storico
, along
Via Cavour
and around
Termini Station
. The
Borgo
district near the Vatican offers the cheapest options and for more authentic cuisine head to
Trastevere
.
Some of the must see destinations in Rome include:
The Vatican, Saint Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel
– A short metro ride takes you to the Vatican City. Containing Saint Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel, visiting the centre of the Catholic world is a must

Ancient Rome and the Colosseum
– Sitting across from each other, the ruins of Ancient Rome are a sight to behold, especially the archways and the law courts which give you an idea of the grandeur of ancient Rome. Awe inspiring Colosseum sits just across the road to the ruins and helps you to understand just what it must have felt like to be a Roman!

The Trevi Fountain
– The beautiful fountain sits in the centre of Rome and is at its best lit up at night. Take a trip here and get a picture with your loved one
There is so much more of Rome to see than we could ever write down, although our
Rome travel guide
does contain a little more information. So why not book your Rome hotel with us now and experience the magic of the Eternal City.
Italy is a popular tourist destination due to its rich history and beautiful surroundings. Take a look at some other
hotels in Italy
we have across other cities and towns.
