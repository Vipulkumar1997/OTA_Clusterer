Albert Dock, Liverpool








Our extensive range of
Liverpool hotels
can be found in all corners of this diverse city, and its attractions are no exception. Crowned the cultural capital of the North West, Liverpool offers a rich tapestry of fine art galleries, museums and a great choice of hotels in Liverpool city centre.
Soak up the culture at Liverpool's UNESCO-listed Albert Dock comprising of the Tate Liverpool,
The Beatles Story
and Merseyside Maritime Museum or explore Hope Street home to the Liverpool Philharmonic Hall, Everyman Theatre and majestic cathedrals. Come and discover a city proud of its cultural heritage and take your pick from the best hotels in Liverpool online with us.
With plenty of things to do and see, make the most of your short break by combining one of our hotels in Liverpool with our popular attraction tickets and rail travel. Most Liverpool hotels provide an ideal base for exploring the area, within walking distance of all the shops, sights and sounds of the city. View some of the finest art in Europe at Liverpool's Walker Art Gallery, and for those who love a spot of shopping, you'll find the greatest designer brands at Cavern Walks and everything under one roof at Liverpool One.
A short distance from many Liverpool hotels you'll find a thriving bar, restaurant and cafe scene. Dine out in Liverpool's Chinatown, and for some great night life head to Concert Square and Matthew Street.
