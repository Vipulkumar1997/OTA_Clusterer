What's Included
















Relax and enjoy a great Friday night out with friends by booking one of our Friday Night Out packages. All packages include:


One-night stay in a comfortable city centre hotel

Welcome drink

Tasty 3-course meal in a popular restaurant

Exclusive nightlife voucher which can be used at a popular local venue


Nightlife voucher venues vary depending on location booked. For those looking to dance the night away, the Tiger Tiger, Level or Pryzm packages include guestlist entry to the nightclub, while the Slug & Lettuce and Revolution packages offer discount drinks vouchers – perfect if you’re looking for a relaxing evening of cocktails with friends. Don’t fancy a Friday Night Out?
Try one of our Lazy Sundays
, relaxation guaranteed!
