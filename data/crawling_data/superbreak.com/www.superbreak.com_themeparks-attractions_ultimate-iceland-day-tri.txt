About our Ultimate Iceland Trips

Take the ultimate short break and discover one of the most exciting destinations on Earth. Visit in winter and head on a search for one of the world's most impressive natural phenomenons - the Northern Lights. If you're planning to stay in summer, then your search will be for whales in their natural habitat! Reykjavik has become one of the most popular places to spend a winter break, and with its stunning scenery, breathtaking landscapes and alternative attractions it's no wonder. Book your Iceland package holiday with SuperBreak and see for yourself all that this beautiful country has to offer.

Ultimate Iceland Highlights

Our trips to Iceland packages are created to get you the most out of your trip, some of the highlights include:


Search for the Northern Lights (winter) or go on a whale watching expedition (summer)

Visit the Golden Circle

Relax in the Blue Lagoon hot springs

Includes return Flybus and airport transfers

Includes hotel stay - we would recommend at least 2 nights hotel stay

Add flights by visiting our Reykjavik page and adding your Ultimate Iceland ticket at the end of your booking



What Makes it Super

With so much to see and do in Iceland the Ultimate Iceland package makes it super easy (and great value) to experience all of the top attractions and activities, from the Northern Lights to the Blue Lagoon – meaning all you have to do is look forward to your break.

Just launched…Discover Iceland’s Arctic North only with Super Break

Explore Northern Iceland with our brand new and exclusive Incredible Iceland breaks. Northern Iceland holidays include 3 or 4 night hotel stays, direct flights to Akureyri from eight regional UK airports, two amazing excursions with £200 per person and overseas transfers.

Discover the beautiful Lake Myvatn geothermal area, visit the famous Godafoss waterfall and Dimmuborgir lava formations on a Land of Fire and Ice Tour or search for the Northern Lights as you venture close to the Arctic Circle.

Experience stunning Arctic scenery, amazing food & drink and incredible excursions from just £699pp
