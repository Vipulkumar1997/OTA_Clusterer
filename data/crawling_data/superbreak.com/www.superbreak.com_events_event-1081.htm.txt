Strictly Come Dancing - The Live Tour 2018 - Nottingham - Evening - 2nd Price - 6th & 7th February 2018

Nottingham









Get ready to be part of the 2018 Strictly Come Dancing Live Tour experience at The Motorpoint Arena, Nottingham
Feel the excitement, experience the emotion, see the stars and enjoy pure dancing pleasure as this year’s contestants brave the fearsome judges; Craig Revel Horwood and Bruno Tonioli.
Many of the much-loved celebrity dancers and their professional partners from the fifteenth series will reunite at The Motorpoint Arena, Nottingham, competing for your votes in the hope of winning the iconic Glitterball Trophy.
With stunning performances and incredible costumes, cha-cha and rumba your way through the show with a little help from the amazing live Strictly band. Make your winter magical with a trip to see this hotly-anticipated event from one of the most successful TV formats in the world. But be quick, as the Strictly extravaganza is set to be extremely popular.
*Details of celebrities and professional dancers appearing on tour will be announced over the next few months.*
Performance Times:
6th February 2018 at 19:30
7th February 2018 at 19:30
