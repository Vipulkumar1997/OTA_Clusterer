Hotels in Germany





Berlin

As Germany's largest city there are plenty of hotels in Berlin for you to choose from when visiting this cosmopolitan city with a turbulent past. Located in north eastern Germany, the capital city of Berlin boasts a rich cultural heritage. Many of our Berlin hotels are near the city's multiple galleries and museums. You will also find more than 50 theatres and around 300 cinemas, making it a great location for entertainment, to find out more visit our
Berlin travel guide
. Whatever your interests there's a great selection of hotels in Berlin to suit all budgets that make an excellent base for exploring this historic city.

Cologne/Bonn

Twin cities that couldn't be more different, there's much to see and do when you choose a break in one of our hotels in Cologne or Bonn. Cologne is a cosmopolitan city, with a big population, a lively nightlife and towering modern architecture. Historic Bonn is one of Germany's oldest cities, with a prestigious University and Federal City status.

Hamburg

Hamburg is Germany's second largest city. The imposing and impressive Rathaus dominates the city centre and the large lake in the heart of the green Hanseatic city is a paradise for all in summer.

Hanover

Set on the River Leine, Hanover is one of Northern Germany’s most important cities. A fantastic base from which to explore the country, Bremen, Hamburg and Berlin are all within easy reach. A terrific choice for your short break in Germany, our hotels in Hanover are comfy and well located. There’s much to see and do here.

Heidelberg

Surrounded by picturesque scenery, Heidelberg is situated on the river Neckar and is home to one of Europe’s oldest Universities. Heidelberg castle dominates the old town, with its’ long narrow streets. Must sees include the Castle garden, Karl-Theodor Bridge and the late Gothic style Church of the Holy Spirit.

Munich

The south of Germany's biggest, most vibrant and exciting city, Munich is a fantastic choice for a European short break. From high end shopping, to unique Bavarian Cuisine, to the world-famous Oktoberfest Beer Festival held here annually, there are loads of great reasons to select one of our popular hotels in Munich.
