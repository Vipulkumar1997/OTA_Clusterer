Victoria Quarter, Leeds








With award-winning attractions, exciting events, a fantastic nightlife and fabulous restaurants all at hand,
Leeds hotels
are just the right choice for an exciting short break. The city is one of the UK's largest, most contemporary and most vibrant - perfect for shopping, a theatre break, soaking up the culture or sumptuous dining, Leeds is not to be overlooked.
Whether you're on a budget break or a glamorous getaway, we've got the hotel in Leeds for you. With a compact centre, the city is incredibly easy to explore. With so much to see and do, it can be hard knowing where to begin!
Treat yourself at one of the finest shopping centres in the country,
The Trinity Centre
, or find a bargain at the
Kirkgate Market
or go high-end shopping in the
Victoria Quarter

When evening rolls around Leeds has a plethora of amazing bars and restaurants in which to unwind, before heading for a night at the theatre at the terrific
West Yorkshire Playhouse
or enjoying the latest blockbuster in style at the swish Everyman Cinema

Embrace culture in Leeds and find yourself at the famous
Royal Armouries Museum
or the popular Leeds Art Gallery

For a fantastic day trip, explore the fabulous rolling Yorkshire countryside just outside Leeds, and see some of the region's top attractions;
Kirkstall Abbey
and
Harewood House
to name just a couple.
Start planning your short break, and discover the city's countless museums and galleries, its spectacular Victorian architecture and excellent shopping - it all starts when you book your break! Check out our fab range of hotels in Leeds in the list below. Alternatively,
explore more UK weekend breaks
.
