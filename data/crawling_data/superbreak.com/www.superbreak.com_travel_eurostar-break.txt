Eurostar City Break Deals

Eurostar City Breaks are the ideal way to explore some of the best Eurostar city break destinations including Paris, Disneyland Paris, Lille, Brussels, Bruges, Ghent, Antwerp and Ostend. Travelling from London on any one of our city breaks is simple, convenient and great value for money, plus there is complete flexibility to combine with rail travel from your local station.
Read more


On our Eurostar Breaks you can travel in style and comfort, enjoy a quick and easy check in, reserved and comfortable seats and a bar buffet serving drinks and light refreshments. Upgrades can be made to our Eurostar Breaks for a small supplement to Standard Premier and you will enjoy spacious and comfortable seating, a selection of complimentary magazines on board, a complimentary light meal and drinks served at your seat. On our Eurostar Breaks you could be shopping in Lille in just 1 hour 30 minutes, exploring the historic capital of Brussels in under 2 hours and soaking up the atmosphere of Paris in 2 hours and 20 minutes. With a wide range of hotels available throughout Europe from 5 star luxury and boutique hotels to 2 star value hotels there will be something for everyone on our Eurostar City Breaks.
