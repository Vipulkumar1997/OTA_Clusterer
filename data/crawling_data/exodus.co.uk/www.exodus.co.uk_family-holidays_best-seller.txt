Bestselling Family Holidays

We offer 44 family holidays in 35 countries across the globe so whether you dream of travelling to lush jungles to see orang-utans in the wild or cycling the Loire Valley with your own little monkeys, we promise to satisfy your longing for adventure. Our Family Adventures will take you the world over, from surfing on the the wild windswept coastline of Portugal, to exploring Sri Lanka’s cultural treasures and wildlife wonders. You may find yourself – and your little ones – dogsledding in Finland in February half term or zip lining in Costa Rica during the Easter break. Wherever you go, our Family Holidays are out of the ordinary. But if you’re in need of a little inspiration on where to go next holiday, here are some of our post popular family trips to spark your kids’ imaginations.

If you do not receive an ATOL Certificate then the booking will not be ATOL protected. If you do receive an ATOL Certificate but all the parts of your trip are not listed on it, those parts will not be ATOL protected. Please see our booking conditions for information, or for more information about financial protection and the ATOL Certificate go to:
www.atol.org.uk/ATOLCertificate
If you book arrangements other than an ATOL protected flight or a package holiday, the financial protection referred to above does not apply. Our ATOL number is 2582 and our ABTA number is Y0751.
