Looking for a quick city break?
Discover the World Rainbow Hotels collection
of gay and lesbian friendly city centre hotels







Looking for a quick city break? World Rainbow Hotels have a global   selection of hotels in the top destinations for gay and lesbian travel.  New York, Paris, London, Paris, Sydney and Hong Kong are just  some  destinations with hotels that offer ideal city breaks.

Amble  through historic café filled streets whist staying in a city  centre hotel in Budapest or stay up and party all night in a city centre  hotel in Barcelona or Las Vegas. Our collection of gay and lesbian  friendly city centre hotels offer something for everyone, from  luxurious, boutique hotels to budget friendly options for the more cost  conscious traveler.
All World Rainbow Hotels members are approved  into the program based on our strict acceptance criteria, which  analyses the hotel's location and characteristics. Hotels also promise  to offer a genuine welcome for everyone and have a proven knowledge of  the local gay and lesbian scene.

World Rainbow Hotels is a global collection of gay and lesbian welcoming hotels.  To be approved into World Rainbow Hotels, hotels must meet a strict  selection criteria which analyzes the hotels location, characteristics  and category, knowledge of the local gay scene, current involvement with  the LGBT community and the hotel's compliance with non-discrimination  policies and same sex benefits for their staff. Hotels range from city centre hotels to resorts as well as a collection of small luxury and boutique properties.
