Out and About Gay Travel Guides - World Rainbow Hotels


Out and About Gay Travel Guides are available at each and every World Rainbow Hotel. They are prepared by the concierge and staff at the hotel and aim to provide you with up to date information about the local gay and lesbian scene.

Out and About Gay Travel Guides should help you to find your way in a different city. The Out and About Gay Travel Guides will provide you with information about the local lesbian and gay friendly restuarants and gay and lesbian nightlife near to your chosen hotel.

Out and About Gay Travel Guides can be downloaded from the hotel's page on the World Rainbow Hotels website or are available when you check in at a World Rainbow Hotel member.

Examples of some of the hotel's Out and About Gay Travel Guides are shown below.

Please note that the information contained in the Out and About Gay Travel Guides is provided by our hotel members. If you have recommendations for a hotel to include in their guide, or information that you feel may be helpful for the hotel to update, please contact the hotel.

Out and About





















World Rainbow Hotels is a global collection of gay and lesbian welcoming hotels.  To be approved into World Rainbow Hotels, hotels must meet a strict  selection criteria which analyzes the hotels location, characteristics  and category, knowledge of the local gay scene, current involvement with  the LGBT community and the hotel's compliance with non-discrimination  policies and same sex benefits for their staff. Hotels range from city centre hotels to resorts as well as a collection of small luxury and boutique properties.
