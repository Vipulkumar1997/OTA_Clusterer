A London landmark, the hotel features 160 contemporary guestrooms, including 54 suites and 7 signature suites. Each is designed with careful detail and features complimentary WiFi, Bang & Olufson entertainment systems, media hubs, Frette bed linen and Floris amenities.

After a busy day, guests can relax in the Akasha Holistic Wellbeing Centre, which features a state-of-the-art-gym, a large lap pool, a sauna, a Hammam/steam room and a wide range of spa treatments.

Hotel Café Royal also offers a selection of restaurants and bars for guests to enjoy. At the heart of the hotel, the Ten Room offers informal all-day dining, while the Green Bar serves a selection of cocktails. Restored to revive the ornate Louis XVI décor and detailing, the Oscar Wilde Bar (formerly the iconic Grill Room), offers afternoon tea, while The Café features an espresso bar and serves a selection of pastries and cakes.

Out and About





















World Rainbow Hotels is a global collection of gay and lesbian welcoming hotels.  To be approved into World Rainbow Hotels, hotels must meet a strict  selection criteria which analyzes the hotels location, characteristics  and category, knowledge of the local gay scene, current involvement with  the LGBT community and the hotel's compliance with non-discrimination  policies and same sex benefits for their staff. Hotels range from city centre hotels to resorts as well as a collection of small luxury and boutique properties.
