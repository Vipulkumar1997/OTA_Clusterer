Descripción del Hospedería del Atalia

La Hospedería del Atalia está situada en el barrio judío de Córdoba y alberga una terraza en la azotea con vistas a la mezquita. Este hotel boutique ofrece conexión Wi-Fi gratuita en las zonas comunes y habitaciones con aire acondicionado.
El Atalia se encuentra a sólo 50 metros de la Mezquita de Córdoba y a corta distancia a pie de la sinagoga, del puente romano y del moderno centro de la ciudad.
La Hospedería Atalia presenta una decoración de estilo clásico y dispone de un salón elegante, decorado con una pequeña fuente. Las bebidas están disponibles en el bar o en la terraza del establecimiento.
La recepción del Atalia atiende las 24 horas e incluye un mostrador de información turística.

06/05/2015

We stayed here the first night on our trip to Spain in April 2015. The hotel is conveniently located, 10 minutes or $6 Euros by taxi from the station and right in the center of the old town. The rooms are small as you would expect in a medieval neighborhood. The breakfast for Euros 6 was worth it and filling. 
The Mesquita is less than a 5 min walk and you can use the terrace at night for a great view. Great location and friendly staff!
Highly recommended, but pricey if you are expecting a US style accommodation for the price.
Room Tip: Ask for a room on the higher floors.

It is centrally located being only 5 minutes away from the Mezquita by foot, and surrounded by a great collection of restaurants of shops. The narrow, winding and confusing streets that border it really gives on an authentic experience of the Juderia neighborhood of Cordoba. Yet the hotel itself is tucked away and quiet, with a nice courtyard out front. The rooms are clean, well equipped and beautifully furnished, even with an elevator. Breakfast is better than average and has good service. Front desk staff is helpful with suggestions. Memorable stay.
