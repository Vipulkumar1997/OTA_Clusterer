In the foothills of the breathtaking Karwendel Alpine Park you find the wonderful "Karlwirt"!

It's main building is more than 200 years old, ranking among the most historical buildings within the Achensee region. It has always been one of top spots for jovial days among friends.

Highlights in abundance...

... culinary delights prepared by our excellent kitchen team. Fresh farm products and premium quality are of utmost importance for a wonderful wining and dining experience.

... relax and unwind after en eventful day in our swimming bath (140 m² water surface) offering an indoor pool, an outdoor pool and a rustic outdoor sweating parlor.

... comfortably equipped rooms with all modern amenities and great views of the Alpine scenery, the Karwendel Alpine Park, the golf course or the cross-country skiing tracks.

... the Achensee regions has a lot to offer in summer and winter alike: mountain biking, surfing, sailing, paragliding, diving, swimming, golfing, horseback riding in summer; cross-country skiing, Alpine skiing, tobogganing, ice curling, ice skating, plus a lot more in winter!

We look forward to welcoming you soon!
Simply contact us for more detailed information.

Payment options: in cash or with EC card (AUT, GER, NED). Sorry, no credit cards!



In the foothills of the breathtaking Karwendel Alpine Park you find the wonderful "Karlwirt"!

It's main building is more than 200 years old, ranking among the most historical buildings within the Achensee region. It has always been one of top spots for jovial days among friends.

Deeply rooted tradition and genuine hospitality are of utmost importance for us. We try our best to make you forget the hustle and bustle of everyday life:

TIME for a nice talk among friends in a leisurely atmosphere, cuddly rooms with all modern conveniences and excellent dining - this is what we offer!

We still preserve old family traditions at our hotel which dates back to 1794.

We look forward to welcoming you soon!
Simply contact us for more detailed information.

Discover the lovely Karlwirt ~

* Comfortably equipped rooms with all modern amenities and great views of the Alpine scenery, the Karwendel Alpine Park, the golf course or the cross-country skiing tracks.

Spa & Wellness


QUITE BIG...

Relax and unwind after an eventful day in our swimming bath (140 m² water surface) offering an indoor pool (+29 °C), an outdoor pool, massage jets, power shower jets, a large relaxation area with many loungers and a spacious sunbathing lawn with sunshades.
