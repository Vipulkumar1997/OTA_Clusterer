Location & Getting There
















Arrival by car



Arrival by train



Arriving by Plane






Toll-free road: From Munich on the motorway to exit 'Holzkirchen' - Tegernsee - Achenpass (941 m) - border - along Lake Achensee to Maurach

Or take the Inntalautobahn to exit Wiesing-ACHENSEE - B181 road to Maurach (10 km)






Calculate Route Now





from:






to:







Calculate Route Now











Jenbach station (located between Innsbruck and Kufstein); then take the bus*) or a taxi to get to Maurach (4 km). From the Tegernsee station take a taxi or bus no. 9550 via Kreuth to Achenkirch and Maurach.

*) Free bus ride from Jenbach to Achenkirch, Maurach, Pertisau or Steinberg - just show the driver your booking reservation. Free transportation is also offered during your stay - ask your host for your free bus ticket. Guests staying in Wiesing also enjoy free transportation in the Achensee area.




From Innsbruck airport continue to Jenbach by train, then take the bus*) or a taxi to Maurach.

or:
From Munich airport or Salzburg airport take the train to Jenbach, then take the bus*) or a taxi to Maurach.
