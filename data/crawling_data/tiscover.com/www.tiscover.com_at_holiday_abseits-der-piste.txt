There is no better way to appreciate the peace and quiet of the winter landscape than through a backcountry ski trip. The reward for the effort of climbing a mountain, are dazzling panorama views and exhilarating downhills through virgin powder snow!

A myriad of wonderful of activities, varied sports and plenty of enthusiasm are the prerequisites of a wonderful active winter holiday for the whole family. Grab your caps and gloves – get ready for ultimate winter fun! Try snowshoe hiking through the white winter wonderland, ice skating on the most scenic natural ice rinks or action-filled tobogganing down to the valley. Here you will find the best locations and loads of tips on unlimited winter activities amid unspoilt nature!
