Welcome to Hotel Pension Central and "Landhaus Central"!

Sports lovers will find plenty of opportunities to pursue their favourite sports. Skiing and snowboarding, whether you´re 8 or 88 -there will be suitable slopes! The bus stop for the free ski bus to the Spieljoch, Hochfügen and Hochzillertal ski areas is just 70m from our house. Our hotel has a ski boot dryer.

Our apartments are completely equipped with bath or shower, WC, cable TV, hairdryer, radio and balcony. Our house features a lift.
Our apartments are also available with room service. In our annex building we offer nice rooms with shower/WC, balcony, cable TV and hair dryer.

A tunnel connects the two houses. So you can access the games room, the sunbathing lawn, the wellness oasis, the table tennis room, and the common and breakfast room from there easily.



Welcome to "Hotel Pension Central" and "Landhaus Central"!

Take some time out to relax and unwind!

Enjoy long days of sunbathing (large lawn with deck-chairs, swing and slide, table tennis and shower)
The Zillertal Valley lends itself to nature exploration, hiking and cycling. Or how about a game of crazy golf? Do whatever you feel like doing!

We offer comfortably appointed apartments and rooms (Pension Central), each with bath or shower, WC, cable TV, hairdryer, radio and balcony. An elevator is available. Apartments with shower, separate WC, sitting area. Landhaus Central accommodates cozy rooms with shower/WC, hairdryer, cable TV, radio and balcony.

A tunnel connects both buildings, thus allowing our guests access to all amenities, such as children's play room, sunbathing area, spa facilities, table tennis, breakfast room and lounge, lockable bicycle storage.
