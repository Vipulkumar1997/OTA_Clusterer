Delightful winter holidays in the “sunny south”

Enjoy an unparalleled winter break in the mountains of South Tyrol and Trentino! Where else can you get heaps of snow, sunshine and mild temperatures at the same time? The popular Dolomiti Superski region, for instance, comprises the renowned ski resorts of Val Gardena, Alta Badia and Cortina d'Ampezzo and offers immaculate ski runs and challenges even for the most discerning skier. Visitors from all over the world have come to appreciate the holiday resorts of Northern Italy for their beauty and largely unspoiled landscapes.

Information on the region










Welcome to Italy – Holidays in South Tyrol, Trentino, etc.

Marvel at the sheer beauty of bizarre mountains and rock formations, enjoy the mild climate at Lake Garda, or be part of bustling city life… Northern Italy harmoniously combines alpine and Mediterranean influences. Lakeside or walking holidays for couples or families in spring, summer or autumn… The lakes and mountains of Northern Italy are always worth a visit. What else could you possibly dream of?
