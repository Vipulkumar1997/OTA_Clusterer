Style of hotel and ambience


There is hardly any hotel that is as Bavarian as the characteristic Hotel Schreiberhof in Aschheim. It offers you individual furnished and comfortable rooms and harmoniously unites tradition, business and relaxation. A wellness area with sauna and steam bath invites to moments of calm and well-being. In the restaurant, traditional Bavarian delights are served. Business travellers benefit from the modern conference facilities.

The Deal Price includes following free services:


Double room for 1–2 persons

Free Wi-Fi access in the room

Free parking facilities directly by the hotel

Free use of the sauna

Free use of the fitness room

One bottle of mineral water in the room

Free use of the wellness area



Breakfast can be booked on location at a special deal rate of EUR 10.00 instead of EUR 15.00 per person.
