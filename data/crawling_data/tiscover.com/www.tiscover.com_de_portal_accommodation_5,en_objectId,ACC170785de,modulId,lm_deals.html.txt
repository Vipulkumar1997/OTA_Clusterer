Style of hotel and ambience


The Yachthotel Chiemsee in Prien at lake Chiemsee offers modern equipped rooms in a quiet area. Some of the suites provide a beautiful view on the lake. The hotel’s gym and spa promise relaxation and regeneration. The conference rooms are modern and professionally equipped, earning the Yachthotel Chiemsee several awards.
Please note: Rooms with lake view are available on request for an extra charge of EUR 20 per night / room.
Dogs are allowed for an extra charge of 13 EUR / day.
** Please note also, that the standard price in the calendar is excluding half board service. **
