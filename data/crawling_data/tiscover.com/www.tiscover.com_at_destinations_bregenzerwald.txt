Holidays in Bregenzerwald

Scenic Bregenzerwald in Vorarlberg ranks among Austria's most popular and beautiful holiday regions, comprising the lovely villages of
Bezau
,
Egg
,
Alberschwende
and
Andelsbuch
. Here rest, relaxation and culinary pleasures are guaranteed. The entire Bregenzerwald landscape lends itself to outstanding walking and hiking to the region's places of strength. Vorarlberg – the center of the Alps – also attracts passionate winter sportsmen. Besides romantic winter walking and snowshoe hiking, the fascinating Bregenzerwald holiday region offers top
modern ski lifts
and
immaculate slopes
reaching up to 2000 meters above sea level.



Bregenzerwald – Ultimate fun on the slopes

The Bregenzerwald is a real winter dorado for all those in search of activities. Some 200 well-groomed ski slopes for all skill levels are waiting for both skiers and snowboarders. The biggest ski area between Lake Constance and Arlberg –
Mellau Damüls

– offers immaculate runs until spring, a fantastic Burton Snowpark and a unique ski tunnel. Great skiing for families, kids and real experts! Quaint ski huts or mountain restaurants promise leisurely sunshine hours or
après-ski fun
.
Cross-country skiers
appreciate Bregenzerwald because of the gently rolling tracks and secluded side valleys while
ski touring
fans can choose from a wide range of picture-book tours to Vorarlberg's vantage peaks.
Tobogganing
makes a fun-filled activity for all ages and
winter walking trails
take nature explorers to the area's most scenic spots. Especially
snowshoe hiking
amid crisp and clean mountain air has become a new trend. What about a romantic horse carriage ride? Followed by some relaxing hours at your homey hotel, and a wellness session to restore your energies after a sporty day in the mountains.

Activities








Winterwandern Bregenzerwald










Also in winter, lovely Bregenzerwald makes a wonderful hiking paradise where you can choose from
400 kilometers of cleared winter walking routes
taking you right through Vorarlberg's winter wonderland.

Scenic
winter walking in Bregenzerwald
offers loads of opportunities for both leisurely explorers or ambitious peak conquerors. All routes within the region are perfectly signposted with
pink trail markers
.

A fabulous winter walking tour travels from
Au-Argenau through the forest to scenic Bergkristallhütte
and further on to the idyllic Bodenvorsäß farmers' settlement at 1200 meters above sea level. After a nice rest stop you can enjoy a speedy toboggan adventure down to the valley.

Very recommendable nature exploration:
ancient Walserwege trails in Damüls
and
panorama walk
from Bödele via Lustenauer Hütte to Schwarzenberg.











Walking Bregenzerwald










Experienced mountain aficionados will find plenty of hikeable terrain in the Bregenzerwald area, such as the
Horizon Hike
near Schwarzenberg, or the
Damülser Rundtour
loop trail, which winds its way past Uga-Alpe and across Sünser Joch.

9 lifts and cable cars
are the gateway to the mountains. The educational flower trail and the trailhead for tours to Glatthorn, for instance, are easily accessible by Stafelalpe chair lift.

The
Sagenwanderweg Schwarzenberg
tells an ancient local tale, while the
Themenweg Au
meanders through the Argenschlucht gorge and the Auer Ried Nature Reserve.

Many routes are easily manageable for families, even with strollers: Among the most popular trails are the
Holdamoos nature exploration
trail at Au/Schoppernau, and the
lake-to-lake tour
at Schröcken.











Mountainbiking Bregenzerwald










Bregenzerwald boasts myriad mountain bike trails for beginners, advanced and real pros. A perfect way to explore the
scenic landscape
of awesome natural beauty. Guided tours and technique courses at certified Bike Schools are on the program as well.

Beginners should head for
Hochhäderich loop trail
in Hittisau, offering also great rest stops and refreshing moments at the swimming pool after an adventurous day in the mountains.

Among the
highlights for mountain bikers
ranks the Bregenzerwald Holiday Region incl. the picture-book
"Rund um die Kanisfluh"
MTB trail. With a bit of luck you can ibexes along the trail.

The
high alpine Tannbergrunde route
in Schröcken is also suitable for bikers in search of pleasant nature exploration as they can visit the unrivaled „uf m Tannberg“ alpine museum.

Information on the region










Bregenzerwald – Walking pleasures and activities

In the summer, lovely Bregenzerwald is a real hot spot for nature fans. Walking and hiking enthusiasts will find a 1000 kilometer network of perfectly signposted routes within the entire region, comprising easy walks on the valley floor, demanding hikes or challenging peak conquests – everything is possible in the Bregenzerwald holiday region. Superb vantage points are spread all over the area, some of the highlights are accessible by mountain lifts for free for holders of the
Bregenzerwald guest card
. Hearty snacks and mouth-watering local delights are served in the numerous
Alpine inns
and serviced mountain huts.
Climbers get together in the fantastic
Schwarzenberg climbing garden
. Golf fans have fun on the
18-hole Golfpark Bregenzerwald in Riefensberg
. What's more, Bregenzerwald has everything a road cyclist and mountain biker could possibly desire since the 30 kilometer long Bregenzerwald cycle path leads from the northern village of Egg to Schoppernau in the south. Real daredevils are as well spoilt for choice in Bregenzerwald: canyoning, rafting, canoeing, kayaking, cave trekking, paragliding or hang-gliding rank among the most thrilling adrenalin sports.



More detailed information provided by the Tourist Board


A true pleasure!
Skiing and snowboarding

Loud party music is more the exception than the rule in the Bregenzerwald region. Leisurly skiing or boarding through the impressive countryside are more important here. The Bregenzerwald ski areas were expanded with deliberation. Today, 94 ski lifts in 22 communities make 259km of slopes accessible.

Comes handy: the 3-valley ski pass is valid for all ski areas of the Bregenzerwald region and neighbouring valleys. Take a ski bus to commute between the various villages, owners of ski passes are free of charge.

Beginners and experts are professionally taken care of by the numerous ski schools. Laughter and fun are most important in the kids' ski schools.

Choose between holiday apartment or hotel - the Bregenzerwald has so much to offer!

Absolutely magic! Winter for sensualists.

Cross-country skiing, Nordic Walking, snow hiking with our without snowshoes - this is the perfect place to be active!

Replenish your vital energy in the peace and quiet of the magic winter wonderland. The Bregenzerwald region features 300 km of cross-country trails and 200 km of perfectly groomed and marked hiking paths. Most villages offer guided tours.

Absolutely delicious: The top-quality cuisine and the many outstanding culinary locations in the Bregenzerwald Cheese Street.

Absolutely beautiful: The new, light-flooded spa areas which have been constructed in many hotels in the recent years.



The Bregenzerwald region
It's especially the outstanding scenery which our guests appreciate.

The green foothills smoothly merge into impressive mountain peaks of an altitude of up to 2,300 meters. Shady woods alternate with romantic river valleys. A myriad of paths lead to hidden mountain lakes and up to peaks which provide fantastic views.

One card - much variety
This is the best way to explore the most beautiful spots - and all this for free

Summer guests who stay in the Bregenzerwald region for three nights or longer between May and September, fully benefit from the Bregenzerwald Guest Card. The card entitles you to free rides on busses and mountain railways. Furthermore, it gives free access to a great number of swimming pools and baths.

Hiking
Walking in the nature is the best way to let one's thoughts wander.

Around 2,000 km of perfectly marked hiking paths lead to the most picturesque spots and vantage points. On the many themed hiking trails, you can expand your knowledge on beauties of nature or the lives and works of renowned celebrities.

Comfortable atmosphere and hospitality
There is a lot to do. On request, we offer tours and excursions guided by experts.

Throughout the summer, many villages offer programs for families and kids, ranging from visits to local farms to hiking tours and other physical activities.

From holiday apartment to hotel, the Bregenzerwald offers a great variety of accommodation!

Architecture & Cheese

This are two of the main treasures which the Bregenzerwald region has to offer.

The Bregenzerwald region is quite well-known for its perfect blend of traditional and modern architecture. Furthermore, the spicy cheeses are appreciated all-around. The Bregenzerwald Cheese Street gives insight into the regional cheese culture. Sightseeing tours, tasting sessions and other events are offered throughout the year.

Tradition & Modernity
Music, theater, cabaret. There is always an event on the agenda.

The cultural program comprises both classical and modern events. One of the annual highlights is the Schubertiade Festival in Schwarzenberg in the Angelika-Kauffmann-Saal hall with is around 70 concerts and recitals. But the Bregenz Festival is also a very special highlight!
