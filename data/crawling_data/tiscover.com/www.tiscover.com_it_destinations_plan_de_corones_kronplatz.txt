Information on the region










Plan de Corones/Kronplatz

Plan de Corones/Kronplatz is not only one of the most modern linked skiing areas in Europe, but also the ideal place to spend relaxing summer vacations. Indulge into this unspoilt and charming nature and enjoy the peace and quiet. Plan de Corones/Kronplatz can boast numerous possibilities for relaxing walks or adventurous hikes.
