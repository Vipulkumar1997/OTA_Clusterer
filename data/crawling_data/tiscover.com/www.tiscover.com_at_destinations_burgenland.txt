Austria’s sunniest province enjoys a balmy climate, idyllic landscapes and soils that lend themselves to wine growing.



The Burgenland offers a great
mix of culture, nature, wine, health
and
sports
. Watersports lovers will enjoy the many activities at
Neusiedler See
, Europe’s second largest steppe lake. Steady winds draw sailors, windsurfers and kite surfers to the pristine lake.
Austria’s smallest province boasts
2500 kilometers of cycling trails
and scenic landscapes at every turn. Burgenland is also a
great destination for
horseback riders
.
In the winter Burgenland charms visitors with its idyllic landscapes.
Holidays in Burgenland
are ideal for those in search of
peaceful relaxation
. The province boasts some
beautiful thermal spas
with extensive wellness areas and water worlds as well as quiet zones with fantastic nature views.
Whether you are looking for a spa hotel, a room or a campsite for your Burgenland Holidays – chances are Tiscover has it for you.

Burgenland at its finest!

Winter in lovely Burgenland... means picture-book Pannonian landscapes covered all in white. gently rolling hills and wide open basins. Walking in the snow, enjoying horse carriage rides through the Puzta region, ice skating or sailing on the giant Neusiedler See lake. Snow permitting, skiers can choose from varied ski slopes in central and southern Burgenland while cross-country skiers have unlimited fun on endless Nordic tracks.

All those in search of relaxing wellness holidays have as well found the right spot as the spa areas and thermal springs spread all over the Burgenland offer everything their heart could possibly desire.

BURGENLAND – That's my way of life!

Burgenland at its finest!

Welcome to lovely Burgenland, Austria's easternmost and youngest province! The entire area boasts an infinite number of highlights and activities for thousands of guests coming from all over the world. Culture, nature, wine, health, sports, and a lot more – The choice is all yours!

Discover the unique fauna and flora of the breathtaking Neusiedler See/Seewinkel National Park, a UNESCO world culture heritage area. Among the varied sports highlights: cycling, water sports, golf.

All those in search of relaxing wellness holidays have as well found the right spot as the spa areas and thermal springs spread all over the Burgenland offer everything their heart could possibly desire.
