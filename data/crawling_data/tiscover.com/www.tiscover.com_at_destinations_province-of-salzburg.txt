Salzburg is synonymous with fun-filled holidays, whether you are looking for lakes, a glacier, a national park or the famous Salzburg Festival.



Feel the power of nature with all your senses on a hike through the
Hohe Tauern National Park. Almdorf Großarltal
is a paradise for hikers and families. The Salzburger Almenweg hiking route is 350 kilometers long and dotted with
120 serviced mountain inns.
Relax in the sun and enjoy the scenic beauty of one of the
Salzkammergut's many lakes
or take a refreshing plunge in a pool. With 6000 kilometers of cycling and mountain biking trails, Salzburg is also one of
Europe’s most popular cycling destinations.
The Salzburger Land province is one of the Alps’ most
famous
ski destinations
and offers everything skiers and snowboarders need for a first-class winter experience, including 2000 kilometers of groomed downhill slopes, 565 ski lifts and 2200 kilometers of prepared
Nordic ski trails
, 36 of which run at over 1000 meters above sea level.
The
100 winter sports resorts
and
22 ski areas
constantly invest in new ski lifts and infrastructure for the best possible experience.
Whether you are looking for a hotel, guesthouse, apartment or campsite – Tiscover is sure to have it for you.

Winter in Salzburg equals extensive slopes, a dream landscape and cultural highlights in one of the world´s most beautiful towns.


Winter is in the air and Salzburg boasts a wide range of sports and activities right on its doorstep: Enjoy Sportwelt Amadé ski area featuring more than 350km of immaculate slopes accessible with only one ski pass, Kaprun-Zell am See European Sports Region offering the year-round skiing and glacier area, genuine hospitality at Hochkönig's mountain realm, sun-flooded ski trails in Saalbach-Hinterglemm where World Cup Races are held, or simply relax and unwind amid Salzkammergut's lovely winter wonderlands and lakes. Come and see for yourself! Join on a virtual Tiscover tour ....



Summer in Salzburg has plenty to offer: lakes, national parks, unlimited recreational facilities and the Salzburg Festival. You are spoilt for choice!


Summer in the Province of Salzburg means choosing from a wide variety of offers. Bathing in the romantic lakes of the Salzkammergut region, a boat ride on Lake Wolfgangsee, hiking in the Hohe Tauern National Park, exploring Salzburg, Mozart´s hometown, with the Getreidegasse lane and the cathedral. In spring, the world-famous "Sound of Music" musical is shown on the original scenes, and the Pentecost Festival offers special cultural experiences. Explore Salzburg with the insider tips provided by Tiscover!
