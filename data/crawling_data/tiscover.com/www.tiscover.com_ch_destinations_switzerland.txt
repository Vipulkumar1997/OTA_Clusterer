Winter at its finest… Welcome to Switzerland!

Switzerland is the top destination in terms of winter sports, thanks to its well-balanced offering and legendary hospitality. With thousands of kilometers of ski runs from Grisons to Valais and breathtaking views of mountains rising higher than 4,000 meters Switzerland will make the hearts of skiers and snowboarding enthusiasts beat faster. No matter if you go for posh resorts such as Davos or rather for cozy villages in the style of Adelboden – Swiss ski resorts make dreams come true for visitors of all ages and ambitions. Relax and unwind in a place of outstanding natural charm, covered by a thick layer of crisp snow shining and sparkling in the sun, and forget about the daily grind…

Information on the region










Switzerland stands for an unparalleled holiday experience: Mountains higher than 4,000 meters and picturesque lakes with a touch of the Mediterranean!

As a matter of fact Switzerland offers all you need for your perfect holiday experience: There are impressive mountains, charming landscapes, and lovely lakes. Cozy and friendly towns and villages and a varied offering in terms of activities and attractions for the old and young await you from Lake Constance to Lake Geneva, from the Jungfraujoch to Ticino. When walking, hiking or mountain biking in the Swiss mountains you’ll be in not only for action and adventures, but also for breathtaking views of numerous glaciers and peaks higher than 4,000 meters! The Mittelland region, Ticino and Switzerland’s lovely lakes offer recreation and relaxation in abundance, along with perfect conditions for swimming and cycling.
