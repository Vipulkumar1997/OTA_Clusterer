Varied holidays and activities

A scenic holiday in Allgäu/Bavarian Swabia promises unlimited sports and recreation throughout the year. The unique area, stretching between Bavaria's south-west and Upper Swabia, has many famous towns like
Augsburg
and
Ulm
plus a huge number of historical places worth visiting, among them the world-famous
Neuschwanstein Castle
,
Legoland
and
Augsburg's Puppet Center
. Additionally, the wide open landscapes of exceptional beauty make a
real paradise for skiing, walking, hiking, cycling and swimming fans
. The region's traditional inns serve outstanding culinary delights: a plate of "spaetzle" are an absolute must for all visitors to Allgäu/Bavarian Swabia. A great blend of authentic Bavarian flair and Swabian zest for life creates this very special atmosphere just perfect for memorable holidays.



Great ski holidays and winter sports

A winter vacation amid lovely Allgäu/Bavarian Swabia has everything a snow fan's heart could possibly desire. Ambitious skiers can choose from
eight ski areas in the Allgäu
, ideal for all ages and skill levels. In addition, cross-country skiers are spoilt for choice in the Allgäu/Bavarian Swabia holiday region since the area offers some
1800 kilometers of well-groomed Nordic tracks and snowshoeing routes
right across the picture-book winter wonderland. Superb tobogganing awaits you on great runs and varied ice skating rinks promise endless fun among family and friends. Enjoy a fairytale winter holiday in beautiful Allgäu/Bavarian Swabia.

Cycling Allgäu/Bavarian-Swabia










Leisurely cyclists and nature explorers are warmly welcome to the scenic Allgäu­Bavarian Swabia holiday region. Following the tracks of Mozart's ancestors or discovering ancient volcano landscapes characterized by deeply rooted legends: cycling promises true relaxation amid breathtaking valleys and hills.

Attractive
theme tours and adventure worlds
for young and old are spread all over the scenic Allgäu­Bavarian Swabia region. The two splendid asteroid craters of Nördlinger Ries and Steinheimer Becken offer the best opportunity to explore the fascinating culture landscape on a wonderful
"Crater to Crater Route"
.

A great highlight for families:
"Trail of Legends"
leading right through the fabulous Allgäu nature paradise and its legendary spots. The sports-minded are recommended to follow the
"Allgäu Panorama Tour"
with breathtaking views of the surrounding scenery.

Information on the region










Summer holiday fun in Allgäu/Bavarian Swabia

The summer season boasts heaps of rewarding excursion sites and splendid activities in the Allgäu/Bavarian Swabia holiday region. Historical sights are
Neuschwanstein Castle
, the ancient center of the
Fugger town
of
Augsburg
or the
Unterwittelsbach Sissy Castle
. The ultimate Geopark Ries gives an insight into the consequences of a meteorite impact dating back some 14 million years. Undisputed highlights for the youngest guests are the fantastic
Legoland near Günzburg
or Augsburg's Puppet Center and museum. Action fans are recommended to discover the
Allgäu Skyline Park
featuring more than 50 attractions – among them the Sky Wheel, Europe's highest overhead roller coaster.
Nature explorers will find a huge
600 km network of walking trails and hiking paths
in the immediate surroundings. Cycling enthusiasts can enjoy the
scenic Danube cycleway
, the
Allgäu loop track
, varied theme trails and wonderful nature paths leading right across the
Neu-Ulm culture land
. Further attractions are the Geopark Ries, the Augsburg-Western Forests nature park and the highly idyllic landscapes along the river Danube and other splendid brooks. No less than
30 lakes offer endless water fun
on hot summer days – for example marvelous
lake Constance
.
