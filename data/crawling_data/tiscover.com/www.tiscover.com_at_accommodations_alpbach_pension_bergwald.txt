A warm welcome to the Bergwald!
Relaxed atmosphere and typical Tyrolean hospitality await you when staying at the Moser`s.

The B&B; Bergwald is the address in the Alpbachtal to spend your holiday at:
unique, authentic and traditional situated 1100m above sea level.
The guesthouse is located in a wonderful setting next to the forest, looking down over the village, offering absolute tranquillity and fantastic views.

During your stay with us discover and enjoy all the many things the Alpbachtal has to offer Winter or Summer:
Hiking, swimming, climbing, mountain biking, sightseeing or simply relaxing in midst nature.
Skiing, horse-drawn sleigh rides, tobogganing, Winter or snow shoe hikes and a multitude of other activities to choose from depending on the time of year.
How about lying in a meadow letting your thoughts flow?
We can give you numerous tips and information to help you spend a perfect holiday.

A Winter highlight:
our new "Bad`Stub" with Finnish sauna made of aromatic pine wood, infrared cabin and relaxation areas provide for an ideal setting to relax and feel energetic again; how about a relaxing massage?
SKI PASS: we can organise your ski passes for you so they are waiting for you on your arrival
Horse-drawn sleigh rides, tobogganing, Winter or snow shoe hikes and more… approx. 8 minute drive to the SKI JUWEL with 109 (fall line) kilometres pistes

In Summer you are in the middle of the holiday region that offers 900km total hiking area and a particular perk is the free services of the Alpbachtal Seenland Card, which includes free lift rides up the mountains, use of the Regionalbus, admission to the open air Museum of Tyrolean Farmhouses and many other things.

Our guesthouse offers:
Breakfast & recreation room, free Wi-Fi, selection of beverages, parking, boot dryer etc., 24 hour coffee and tea service, rooms are cleaned daily, fire detection system and a large terrace for sunbathing.
Pets by prior arrangement.
7 double or multi-bed rooms and 2 apartments (total 26 pers.). All units are furnished in the typical Tyrolean style (shower or bath, WC, hairdryer),
flat screen SAT TV, safe, balcony. Rooms – buffet breakfast included
Apartments – self-catering or can be booked with breakfast buffet. Half board available in the nearby Roßmoos inn.

BE OUR GUEST – COME STAY WITH US!
We look forward to welcoming you
***** your Moser family, Bergwald*****
We speak: German, English, Français, and Italiano. The Austrian general terms and conditions for the hotel industry are applicable.

Motorway exit Kramsach > Alpbachtal (Brixlegg, Reith, Alpbach)

In the center of Alpbach turn left before you reach the church - past Romantikhotel Böglerhof - past Congress Centrum Alpbach - past Hotel Alphof and past several old farmhouses - just follow the road across a small bridge and up the hill (through the forest) - 2nd house to the left: welcome!

Information:
Approx. 2km from the village center (St. Oswald church)

On Oberer Höhenweg, just before Gasthof Rossmoos.

Walking time into the village: approx. 20min.

Alpbachtal - WILDSCHÖNAU ski area JUWEL
To the Alpbach ski lifts:
Wiedersbergerhorn gondola and
Pöglbahn in Inneralpbach are about an 8-minute drive away.

Arrivals an check in only between 13.00 - 18.00h - no more late arrivals.

Payment only in cash.
