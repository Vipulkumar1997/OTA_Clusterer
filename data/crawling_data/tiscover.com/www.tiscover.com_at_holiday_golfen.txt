Putting, chipping, enjoying:
golf sports
have become the
ultimate trend
in the mountains! An infinite number of Golf Hotels und golf destinations in the Alps – especially Burgenland's Bad Tatzmannsdorf, the Schladming-Dachstein Region and the scenic Kitzbühel Alps - offer
made to measure holidays
for passionate golfers: be it an intensive golf course in order to receive a
handicap certificate
within a few days or a more leisurely golfer's package including a revitalizing massage after an exciting day on the green! No matter if you are an absolute rookie or already a pro golfer, the outstanding Alps boast more than
200 immaculate golf courses
amid the stunning Alpine scenery - right at the heart of gently rolling hills or romantic Alpine lakes. A wonderful Alpine vacation is also a perfect way to combine golf sports with exploring
nature treasures
. We will show you the most scenic places!
