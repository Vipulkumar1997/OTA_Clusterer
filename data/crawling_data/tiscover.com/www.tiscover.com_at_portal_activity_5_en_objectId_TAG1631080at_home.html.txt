The Glacier Ski Areas - also known as the eternal ice - are just perfect for
skiing pleasures
and always worth a visit! Dreamlike slopes with absolute snow guarantee await you in Austria and Switzerland throughout all seasons. Fun-filled sports days in the snow! The
unique Matterhorn Skiers Paradise, splendid Sölden, the Stubai Glacier and Saas Fee
are only a few of the countless glacier ski areas in the Alps. Breathtaking panoramic views plus perfectly groomed slopes with plenty of snow amid picture-book high Alpine landscapes! Skiing up to 3000 meters above sea level makes a very special experience. Treat yourself to a wonderful skiing holiday
with absolute snow guarantee
in the magnificent Alpine glacier ski areas. Don't miss out on this highlight!
