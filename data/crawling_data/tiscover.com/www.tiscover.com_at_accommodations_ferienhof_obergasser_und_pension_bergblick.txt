Description










Winter vacation




Summer vacation




A Picture-Postcard Winter Fantasy for every taste!

Mountains and snow-covered meadows and trees as far as your eyes can see.
Dream away in our apartments and houses and our romantic comfort rooms offering every conceivable amenity.
Enjoy a couple of days in a peaceful winter scenery -- perfect for children and adults alike. Cozy evenings by the fireside.

Relax in our generous sauna section featuring jaccuzzi, table tennis room, children's games room, and winter garden.

Great package deals for families, ice skaters, and cross-country skiers.
Child discounts available on request.
Free curling and tobboggan rental!



Welcome to the nature paradise!

Mountains and blooming meadows as far as your eyes can see.
Direct access to the lake. Relax and unwind in beautiful apartments and romantic comfort rooms providing all conceivable amenities.
Ample space for youngsters and oldsters alike: 25,000m² of meadows, gardens, bathing shores, and forests -- for our guests exclusively.
Cozy evenings at the open fireplace or at the barbecue site in our old fruit tree garden.
Relaxation in our generous sauna section featuring jaccuzzi, table tennis room, children's games room, and winter garden.
Great package deals for fishermen, families, and winter sportsmen.

2 private bathing shores -- admission free of charge!

Free loungers, free rowboat, bicycle and mountain bike rental.
Child discounts available on request.

*Please call us for further information -- we will gladly send you your individual offer*
