Freedom and adventures start where the road ends: discover and explore the Alps’ most beautiful spots on two wheels! Thrilling mountain bike routes to gentle pasture trails and scenic cycleways along rivers - ambitious mountain bikers as well as leisure cyclists will find plenty to keep them entertained in the Alps!
Browse our Tiscover tips for fun-filled cycling and mountain biking holidays in the Alps: signposted cycleways and mountain bike routes, licensed cycle sports schools and bicycle rental shops, bike-friendly hotels offering bike guides and guided tours, bicycle transport on mountain lifts, trains and boats ...
