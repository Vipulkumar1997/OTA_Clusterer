Style of hotel and ambience


The Hotel am Mirabellplatz offers an excellent accommodation for an unforgettable stay in Salzburg. Built in 1653 as a palace for the Archbishop Paris of Lodron, you find a fascinating mixture of historical charm and modern ambience. Make yourself comfortable in your attractively furnished room and spend relaxing moments at the Chill out Lounge. Austrian and regional specialties await you for breakfast at the comprehensive buffet.

The Deal Price includes following free services:


Double room for 1–2 persons

Free Wi-Fi access in the room

One bottle of mineral water in the room



Breakfast can be booked on location for EUR 15.00 per person.
