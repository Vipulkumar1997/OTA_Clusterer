WINTER HOLIDAY ON THE FARM
Here in the open countryside surrounded by nature, woods and meadows you will be able to RELAX and enjoy a deep SILENCE.

Do you feel like spending an exciting winter holiday on the farm or going on a skiing holiday?
Then our farm will be the perfect place.

A winter holiday on our farm, situated at a height of 800 metres above sea level, is characterized by a close relationship to nature, cosiness, pleasure, excitement, a lot of activities and skiing adventures.

Families do profit from our special package offers that make their winter holidays an unforgettable experience. On St Nicolas´ Day, for example, when the patron saint of sailors and children pays a visit with his "sinister" companions, not only the kids will be excited!!

The skiing areas of Wurzeralm and Hinterstoder-Höss can be reached by car within 20 minutes. There is however also a shuttle that takes our guests directly to the ski resort. You are still not exhausted after a long day on the ski-runs?

Then you should take your sledge for a moonlight ride down to the valley! Kids have a lot of fun building snowmen and snow castles and sliding down the hills in safe surroundings offered by our farm.

One of the most enlightening experiences is due to the spectacular display of fireworks on New Year´s Eve that can be seen right from the balcony of our farm.

Those who wish to promote good health should spend some time sweating and relaxing in our "Duftgrotte" - some kind of aromatic steam room with adjacent relaxing area.



SPEND YOUR HOLIDAYS ON OUR MOUNTAIN FARM ESPECIALLY EQUIPPED FOR CHILDREN OF ANY AGE IN THE "KALKALPEN" NATIONAL PARK.

The fine views of the Windischgarstner valley and its surrounding mountain ranges will create lasting impressions on our guests.
We offer a lot of space for large families and adventurous packages. The family holiday on our farm allows parents to relax lying in a deckchair or pass some time in our sauna with aromatic herbs while the children are enjoying themselves on the playground or having fun in the area ZWERGERLWALD especially designed for kids.
Children are also welcome to feed and take care of the various animals living on the farm such as cows, rabbits, guinea pigs, dwarf goats, cats and the pony called "Nico". Everyone who is ready for meeting some adventures can go for activities such as milking, riding or baking bread.

Once a week parents and children are invited to take part in the barbecue "STOCKHATZ´N" at which they can grill their own sausages.
Hikers will be pleased by the wonderful hiking tracks leading through the "Kalkalpen" national park. However, before taking off for mountain tours they will be served a nutritious breakfast with regional products in a congenial atmosphere.

The advantage card "NATURERLEBNISCARD PYHRN PRIEL" offers you a lot of free services.

Enjoy your family and hiking holiday on the farm in the holiday resort "Pyhrn-Priel" and a unique taste of country living at its best!

Location & Getting There
















Arrival by car



Arrival by train






West autobahn; exit Voralpenkreuz, direction Kirchdorf/Kr and Graz until Winsdischgarsten. Exit Roßleithen, just before Windischgarsten at a small place (storeroom and station on the left) turn right. After about 3 km in front of the Renault garage there is a sign "FERIENHOF CHRISTA" with a teddybear (logo for children friendly farms). There you turn right onto the Christa haul trail. After 2,4 km you will reach the farm at the end of the street.
