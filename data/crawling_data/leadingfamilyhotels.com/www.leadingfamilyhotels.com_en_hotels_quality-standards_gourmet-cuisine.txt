Top in cuisine and service

The exclusive "Leading Family" children’s hotels distinguish themselves by a high quality gourmet cuisine. The aim is to guarantee the utmost in quality concerning the products selected and the preparation of the dishes.




































































































































































































































We offer various buffets and menus included in the room price: Guests of the ‘Leading Family’ children hotels have the choice between rich breakfast and lunch buffets. In addition we serve changing dishes-of-the-day as well as snacks and cakes in the afternoons. In the evenings we offer an exclusive dinner menu with choices possible for each course.
