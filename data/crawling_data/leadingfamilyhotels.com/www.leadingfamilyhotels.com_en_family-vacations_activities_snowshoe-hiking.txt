Snowshoe hiking

Discover the beauty of the nature surrounding your 4-star children’s hotel during a snowshoe hike. Have a walk through the untouched wintery landscape with your snowshoes and experience action and pure nature.








































































There are also many
winter hiking trails
nearby the family hotels which run through the beautiful countryside. The winter hiking trails are all well prepared and therefore perfect for a walk with the family. Have a walk through the dream-like snow covered landscape and enjoy a stunning view to the surrounding mountains.

The ski schools and ski rentals near your family hotel all offer snow shoes for rent. Some of the finest powder snow lies right by your children’s hotel. Experience a relaxing trip into an idyllic wintery landscape and enjoy the silence of the nature.

Many ski schools also offer
guided snow shoe hiking
in groups. Discover the fascinating snow landscape of the Alps with a guide on your family vacation. Some of the 4-star family hotels also offer guided snow shoe hikes into fascinating natural landscapes.

The children’s hotels offer easy hikes suitable for children up to the age of 12. Take advantage of the hotel’s own offers and experience untouched wintery landscapes and the beauty of the surrounding snow-covered mountains!
