Fantastic skiing holiday with your family

Our exclusive children’s hotels are the perfect locations for all winter sports fans, because they are situated in top skiing destinations. Great skiing areas situated in famous winter sports regions await you and your family!








































































Exciting family vacation in top skiing resorts

Two of our family hotels are situated in the famous skiing regions
Tyrol Zugspitze Arena
and
Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis
. Another children’s hotel is located in South Tyrol (Italy) in the top skiing region
Val Gardena/Gröden
. Guests of our child-friendly hotels enjoy perfectly shaped slopes and the skiing resorts provide great snow conditions.

These skiing resorts are among the most snow-sure of the region. Skiing in the Alps means not only a great snow experience, but skiing is also possible in spring! Spend an amazing
skiing family vacation at Easter
at the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts". The finest snow and pleasant temperatures offer great conditions for skiing on your family vacation. Enjoy lots of sun and the charming view on the Alps mountains.

On your skiing vacation at the luxury hotels of "Leading Family" you enjoy a
top infrastructure
in the
attractive skiing resorts
. Great snow parks with lots of obstacles and jumps offer maximum action. The
skiing areas
are all
very family-friendly
and provide ski slopes for beginners as well as speed courses for pros. Ski schools and several snow parks for children provide many offers and specials for the smallest guests.

Snow pleasure abroad the ski slopes

A lot of winter activities for families are offered abroad the ski slopes.
Perfectly shaped cross-country tracks
await you in every of the ski resorts. Next to the family hotels are
many well prepared winter hiking trails
as well as toboggan runs that cross the winter landscape.
