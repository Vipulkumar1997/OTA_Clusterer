FAMILY VACATION IN VAL GARDENA/GRÖDEN AT THE HEART OF THE FAMOUS DOLOMITES

Val Gardena/Gröden is a top holiday resort at the very heart of the
world famous Dolomites
, a
UNESCO world heritage
! Discover the beauty of the fascinating landscape of Val Gardena in South Tyrol, Italy. The village of St. Ulrich, where the luxury children’s hotel Cavallino Bianco is situated and also spreads a fashionable charm. Surrounded by the impressive Dolomites, the family hotel Cavallino Bianco is situated right at the center of the mountain village, next to the pedestrian zone. It is therefore the perfect place to have a stroll through the village and to make a shopping tour.

FASCINATION OF THE MOUNTAINS – DOLOMITES OF VAL GARDENA/GRÖDEN

The
family hotel Cavallino Bianco
offers its guests a luxury atmosphere as well as a
breathtaking view on the Dolomites of Val Gardena
. On your family vacation in Val Gardena numerous 3,000 m high mountains are on your doorstep.

Explore the beauty of these wonderful mountains on your family vacation in summer.
A large number of hiking and mountain bike trails
run through the Dolomites of Val Gardena. They all guarantee exciting mountain adventures in front of a unique mountain panorama.

A SKIER’S PARADISE - VAL GARDENA/GRÖDEN

On your winter family vacation in Val Gardena/Gröden you will be impressed by the numerous winter sport attractions. 175 km of perfectly prepared slopes and cross-country tracks with an overall length of around 98 km, Val Gardena/Gröden is one of the
most attractive and largest skiing areas in the Alps
. With the ski pass
DOLOMITI SUPERSKI
you can use all the ski lifts in the 12 skiing regions.
1,200 km of snow guaranteed slopes
await you and your family.

A wonderful spectacle of nature awaits you on your family vacation in the Dolomites of Val Gardena!
