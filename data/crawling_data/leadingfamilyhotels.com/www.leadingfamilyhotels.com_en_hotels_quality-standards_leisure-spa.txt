Many sports and leisure offerings

The "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" are all situated in beautiful natural landscapes and famous vacation resorts which offer great leisure facilities. Therefore a number of different sports and leisure activities can be done on your family vacation.











































































































































































































































































Three children’s hotels are situated right in the
top ski resorts
Gröden/Val Gardena (Italy), Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis and Zugspitz Arena (both of them in Austria). Families find a lot more to experience away from the ski slopes: Cross-country skiing, snowshoe-hiking, sledging or ice skating. One luxury family hotel is situated right in the famous
thermal spa of "Bad Tatzmannsdorf"
and even provides its own thermal spa.

Not only in wintertime but also in summer and autumn the family hotels offer a variety
of leisure activities
in the beautiful landscapes that surround them. Discover the nature‘s beauty by hiking, climbing, nordic walking, swimming, riding, mountain biking, golfing or tennis. All the children’s hotels provide their
own ski and hiking guides
and some family hotels also offer golf lessons with
golf pros
.

Our family hotels offer a wide range of sports programs.
Modern fitness rooms
with special cardio and fitness equipment are available for guests. On your family vacation you can do your daily workout with a personal trainer by your side. Improve your personal fitness level at the
various gymnastics classes
offered at your exclusive children’s hotel. Enjoy an active family vacation at the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" together with your children.










Simply the best for families!
