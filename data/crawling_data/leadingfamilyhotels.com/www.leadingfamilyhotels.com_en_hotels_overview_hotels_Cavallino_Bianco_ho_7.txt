CAVALLINO BIANCO – THE FAMILY SPA GRAND HOTEL IN VAL GARDENA/ITALY

The ‚Family Spa Grand Hotel Cavallino Bianco‘ is the perfect place to spend your amazing family vacation in the heart of the beautiful Dolomites. The baby hotel offers family activities and wellness and has been awarded as "Best Family Hotel of the World" for 5 years in a row: 2013-2017. Both, you and your family will be captivated!

FAMILY VACATION AT THE GRAND HOTEL CAVALLINO BIANCO

The "Family Spa Grand Hotel Cavallino Bianco" in Val Gardena is the top destination for families and single parents with children or babies. A
spacious indoor play area
and daily leisure programs guarantee a fun, action packed holiday for your kids. The family hotel provides
age-based childcare programs
for babies and children - 7 days a week. For its child friendly services the Cavallino Bianco has been awarded with 5 smileys and the "Best Family Hotel of the world" from 2013 to 2017







FIRST CLASS SPA HOTEL IN THE DOLOMITES

The Cavallino Bianco is your exclusive spa hotel in Val Gardena/ South Tyrol, situated in the middle of the incredible Dolomites. The stylish
spa area "AQVA SANA"
offers its guests more than 2.900 m² of space for relaxation. Parents can enjoy relaxing hours in one of the 6 saunas or in the exclusive relaxation room equipped with water beds and vitamin buffets. The spa area of the Cavallino Bianco also offers its guests three indoor pools and one outdoor heated pool. Let yourself be pampered by a relaxing massage or a cosmetic treatment in the beauty center
"THERESIA’S BEAUTY & SPA"
!

ACTIVE HOLIDAYS IN THE WORLD FAMOUS DOLOMITES OF SOUTH TYROL

During your family vacation at the "Family Spa Grand Hotel Cavallino Bianco" in Val Gardena/Gröden you are surrounded by the
fascinating mountains of the Dolomites
. The Dolomites were designated as a UNESCO Natural Heritage due to their variety and outstanding natural beauty. Explore the amazing natural landscape of the mountains by a
guided hike or ski tour.
Discover the wintery dream-like landscape of
DOLOMITI SUPERSKI
, the world’s biggest ski association: 1.200 kilometers of ski slopes across 12 ski areas are waiting for you. Alternatively you can round off your active holidays in the modern and well equipped Cavallino Bianco-fitness studio.
Daily fitness programs
are also offered for guests of the family hotel.

CAVALLINO BIANCO - HIGHLIGHTS



Exclusive atmosphere:
stylish interior with luxury family suites (up to 140 m²) equipped with all comfort a family could ask for


Professional baby and childcare
(5 smiley-hotel) in age-based groups 7 days a week and daily exciting programs with varied indoor and outdoor activities


Sensational location
in the heart of the Dolomites: situated right next to the ski area, ideal starting point for hiking trails and bike tours


Unique facilities for children:
spacious indoor play area ‘Lino Land’ (over 1.250 m²), safe outdoor play area, kids-pool with adventure slides and separate baby pool, beauty offers for babies and kids


Spa area "AQVA SANA":
6 saunas, tepidarium-terrace, stylish relaxation room, 3 indoor pools and one outdoor pool (> 2.900 m²)


Gourmet cuisine:
gourmet breakfast buffet, rich lunch buffet and changing dishes-of-the-day and a dinner menu in the evenings with choices possible for each course- in the afternoon snacks and cakes from the buffet


Sports program:
Daily trips with ski and hiking guides from the hotel, daily animation and fitness programs for the whole family, well equipped fitness studio for adults and children

Ski, mountain bikes, segways, buggies for rent
