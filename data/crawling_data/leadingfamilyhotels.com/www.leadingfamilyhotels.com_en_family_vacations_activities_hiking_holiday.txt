Hiking in Austria and South Tyrol (Italy)

The child-friendly luxury hotels are all situated in very famous hiking regions, in the heart of the Alps!








































































Fascinating mountains and lovely hills await the guests of our children’s hotels. Many attractive hiking trails are at your disposal: On your family vacation you can choose between easy hikes along alpine pastures to demanding walks to the summits of the surrounding mountain peaks.

All "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" holiday destinations provide
many hiking trails
. Whether you spend your family vacation in the
charming Tyrolean mountains
of Serfaus or in Lermoos – a dream-like mountain scenery is guaranteed. On your family vacation in
Serfaus
you can even enjoy a breathtaking view over three countries (Italy, Switzerland and Germany) in some places. On your family vacation in
Lermoos (Tyrol)
you will hike along the sunny side of the highest mountain of Germany,
the "Zugspitze"
, and enjoy a stunning view on it.

If you spend your family holidays in the exclusive children’s hotel in
Val Gardena
, the fascinating Dolomites of Val Gardena are right on your doorstep.
The Dolomites
are world famous because of their unique beauty and they have also been designated as
UNESCO world heritage
.

The 4-star children’s hotels
will gladly advice you regarding hiking trails
and the most attractive excursions in the surroundings. Your family hotel also provides detailed information and
local hiking guides
are at your disposal. Benefit from our offers for guided hikes with our local experts and experience the most attractive places of the surroundings.
