Dreamlike family vacation at the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts"

Spend a pleasant family vacation at the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts". The children’s hotels distinguish themselves by their first-class international hotel standards and their focus on sophisticated family’s requirements.





















































































The 4-star luxury hotels guarantee the utmost in quality. Each and every hotel selected to be part of the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" has to pass the strong criteria of an excellent
high level service
, adjusted to the
special needs of families with children
.

The "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" provide a
child-friendly infrastructure
. The suites are equipped with
spacious bed and living rooms
and separate children’s bedrooms. Guests of the 4-star family hotels also await a wide range of baby equipment for free use. The children’s hotels also provide
many indoor and outdoor leisure facilities
: spacious water parks with water slides, soft playgrounds and outdoor playgrounds as well as indoor and outdoor pools.

Excellent spa offers and children supervision

Each of the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" has its own charm due to their
spacious and stylish spa landscapes
. Many sauna facilities, relaxation rooms and several rooms for spa treatments await the guest families. The child-friendly member hotels have quickly realized that children have a different understanding of wellness than adults have. Therefore they provide spa areas for the entire family with
family saunas
as well as special
spa areas and offers for children and toddlers
. Parents feel free to relax in the spa landscapes for adults, while they know their children are well taken care of in the children’s clubs.

The "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" provide a
unique supervision program for children
.
Children from 0 to 13 years
are supervised in different age groups by our
professional staff
. The supervision service is guaranteed at least 6 days a week and up to 12 hours daily. The exclusive family hotels offer a varied indoor and outdoor program for children adhering to their age. Many indoor programs are provided for your children in the
baby’s and children’s clubs
as well as varied sports and outdoor activities for kids and teens.
