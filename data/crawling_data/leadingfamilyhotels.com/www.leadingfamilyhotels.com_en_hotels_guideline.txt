LEADING FAMILY HOTELS & RESORTS GUIDELINES

The "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" have been selected and evaluated according to the strictest criteria. Spend an amazing family vacation at the leading hotels for sophisticated family holidays in Europe. The hotels guarantee the highest standards and are perfectly adapted to the wishes and desires of even the most demanding families.

LEADING FAMILY SERVICE

Our four-stars plus ‘Leading Family Service’ stands for
first-class international hotel standards
. The child friendly hotels guarantee a sophisticated service, perfectly shaped to the individual wishes and needs of families. The amenities are also family-friendly and spread a relaxing and charming atmosphere. The exclusive four-star service is available for a package price and provides many luxurious all-inclusive family offers.

LEADING FAMILY LEISURE

The exclusive children’s hotels provide
a wide range of leisure facilities
for an active and unique family vacation. The family hotels offer all the facilities, little children are dreaming of; indoor and outdoor pools, water slides and indoor soft playgrounds as well as outdoor playgrounds. Supervised

LEADING CHILDCARE

The "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" provide a unique program of childcare:
professional and age-based supervision
awaits small guests. In each of the family hotels, at least 12 supervisors take care of the children 6 days a week. Your children are well supervised and are provided in age specific baby or children’s clubs.
Daily altering adventure programs
provide the children with all different activities.

LEADING FAMILY SPA

The exclusive "Leading Family" children’s hotels are distinguished by
various wellness offerings for adults and children
. The stylish spa areas provide generous and modern facilities for a unique relaxing atmosphere. The perfect place to leave your everyday routine behind you! Several saunas, laconium and relaxation areas await you.
High level beauty treatments
(cosmetics, massage & bath treatments) round off the range of wellness offers at the luxury hotels for children.
