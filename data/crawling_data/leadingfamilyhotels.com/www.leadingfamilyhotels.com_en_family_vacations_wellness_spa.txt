VARIOUS SPA OFFERINGS FOR CHILDREN

Children have a different understanding of wellness and relaxation. That’s why the 4-star family hotels provide
special spa offerings for children
. Spacious water parks and several indoor and outdoor pools are available to the children. Water & fun, that’s exactly what children dream of!

The pools are heated all year round and provide many extras for children:
water slides, water playgrounds and special pools for toddlers
. Some hotels even provide special family spas and saunas for children as well as beauty specials for children and babies. At the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" your dream of
relaxing in a luxury spa hotel with your children
will come true!

The luxury spa landscapes of the 4-star children’s hotels are created observing the wishes of sophisticated families on vacation. Come in and be persuaded by the various spa facilities of the ‘Leading Family Hotels & Resorts’.
