Fun and games in the children’s clubs

Children often have different ideas of a perfect family vacation than their parents have. The concept of the exclusive "Leading Family" children’s hotels therefore aims to provide various offerings for children.















































































































Professional supervision in the children’s clubs

In the
children’s clubs
of our family hotels we supervise
children aged 3 to 6 years
at least 6 days a week. Each day we offer at least 12 hours of
varied program for children
. Our professional supervisors take your children on an adventure full of fun and games every day of your family vacation!

The exclusive 4-star family hotels offer a
varied program
for little guests in their
children’s clubs
(tinker, ball games etc.) as well as a
rich outdoor program for kids
(swimming courses, skiing courses, golf courses, climbing, riding, go-cart etc.). On their family vacation at our child hotels kids can also demonstrate their talent for the big stage on fashion or playback shows.

At the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" you know your children are well taken care of. Spend a relaxing family vacation and take some
time off as parents.
Enjoy some precious time with your partner without having a bad conscience. Regain new strength on your family vacation in our beautiful spa landscapes, during a romantic walk through the surrounding area or while having a candle-light dinner. Leave your daily worries behind you and feel totally at ease surrounded by the relaxing atmosphere of our luxury child hotels.










Simply the best for families!
