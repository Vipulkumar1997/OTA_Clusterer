Leading Family Hotels & Resorts - Ski-Guiding

Experience the most attractive slopes and the most impressive natural wonders of your holiday destination. Most of the 4-star children’s hotels provide knowledgeable and local ski and hiking guides.








































































More information about the various offers you can acquire at the reception and through the newsletter of your family hotel.

The "Leading Family" ski guides accompany you into unknown areas in the surroundings of your family hotel. Experience dream-like wintery landscapes in the heart of the Alps and learn more about the regional mountains. All local ski schools also provide their own ski guides and many other guiding offers.

At the child-friendly hotels of "Leading Family", hotel’s own guides are at your disposal all year around, in winter as well as in summer. Discover the most charming places of every season with our local hiking and ski experts. The guides are also at your disposal in the children’s hotels for more information about your holiday region and the most beautiful hiking trails and ski routes.
