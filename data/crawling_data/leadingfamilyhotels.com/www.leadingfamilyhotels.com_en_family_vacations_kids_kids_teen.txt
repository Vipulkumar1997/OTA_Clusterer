Varied adventure program for kids and teens

At our child friendly hotels we offer a varied program for kids aged 7 and over. We know that kids of this age ask for action and fun and need a lot of entertainment. Therefore our exclusive family hotels offer a wide range of activities for kids and teens aged 7 and over.


































































































In different age groups we organize an extensive and interesting program like
trips into the nature
. Other outdoor activities offered: rafting, canyoning, rapelling over a canyon. The top highlight for your family vacation is an overnight stay on a high alpine pasture. The kids are free to explore the surrounding nature in groups.

Furthermore we offer
various talent shows for children
on family vacation: fashion shows, playback shows, theatre shows etc. At the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" children will not be bored during their family holidays thanks to the many activities like excursions and show highlights offered.

Great wellness sensations on your family vacation

The 4-star children’s hotels focus exclusively on families or singles with children. We make every effort to realize your demands of a
perfect wellness vacation with your family
. The wellness landscapes of the stylish children’s hotels are equipped regarding the needs of families. In our family hotels we provide everything children may wish: special pools for toddlers (heated all year round), water games areas, water parks.

There are a lot of things for your kids to explore so we guarantee fun and adventure. The luxury indoor and outdoor pools and large family saunas are the right places for you and your children to spend relaxing family moments at our exclusive children’s hotels.
