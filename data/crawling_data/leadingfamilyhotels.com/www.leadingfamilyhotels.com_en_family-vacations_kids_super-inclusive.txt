Super inclusive for Children and Babies

The "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" offer a unique all-inclusive service that provides many facilities for children and babies. At our 4-star child hotels we offer a special children’s buffet in the evenings.





























































































































































































Children don’t have to wait for their meals and can choose between their favourite dishes. All the family suites of the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" provide separate children’s rooms. They are perfectly adjusted to the needs and wishes of parents and their children on family vacation.

Unique program of supervision

Furthermore the all-inclusive model of the children’s hotels provides a
unique supervision program
. Your children and babies are being supervised at 6 days per week and up to 10 hours per day. In each and every family hotel at least 12 supervisors take care of the toddlers up to 2.5 years in baby clubs.

Our professional staff supervises children up to 13 years. In the children clubs are offered various leisure activities for children between 3 and 6 years: tinker, magic school, playback shows, riding, climbing and ski courses. For kids and teens aged from 7 to 13 years we offer
many outdoor activities
under the slogan: "adventure, fun & action".

Leisure facilities for children & accessories for toddlers

Guests of the exclusive child hotels also benefit from the
numerous indoor and outdoor leisure facilities
. Spacious indoor water parks with big water slides, heated indoor pools and spacious softplay facilities await your children at the luxury hotels of "Leading Family". The spacious outdoor areas of our family resorts provide outdoor pools and different playgrounds.

Our
all-inclusive service
surplies all important accessories for babies and toddlers. Guests of the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" don’t need to bring their own baby equipment. Available at our member hotels, free of charge are; strollers, bouncers, baby bath, high-chairs, changing mats, baby phones, bottle warmers, cots etc.










Simply the best for families!
