Relax & Spa

The “Leading Family Hotels & Resorts” provide spacious and luxury wellness areas. Guests will find every imaginable comfort and a wide range of spa offers for total relaxation.





































































































































































































































































































Let yourself be pampered by the numerous spa offerings of the four-star children’s hotels.
Saunas, aromatic steam baths and luxury relaxation rooms
await you and your family. Parents can feel free to enjoy some time off while their kids are busy in the children’s clubs of the family hotels. The
beauty and vitality sectors
of the child friendly hotels
guarantee high quality standards
too. Lay back and relax while enjoying a massage, facial or a bath treatment on your family vacation. In each and every exclusive family hotel you can select from several high-quality product lines.

WELLNESS FOR CHILDREN AND ADULTS

Children have a different understanding of wellness than their parents. The "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" therefore provide separate
spas for children
and some also offer special
family saunas
. Kids have at their disposal spacious indoor and outdoor pools, heated all year round. Huge water slides and other water play features as well as separate
pools for toddlers
, guaranteeing lots of action and fun for your kids on your family vacation.

After a visit to the sauna the
stylish relaxation areas
offer the perfect, calming atmosphere to sit back and unwind. Thanks to their amazing wellness landscapes the family hotels create perfect conditions for a
unique wellness vacation with children
. Simply soak up the relaxing atmosphere and enjoy the refreshment of your soul!
