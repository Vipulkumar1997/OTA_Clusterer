Spend your family vacation at the exclusive child hotel Alpenrose and all your dreams of a wonderful holiday will come true. The family hotel and member of the ‘Leading Family Hotels & Resorts’ is situated in Tyrol, surrounded by the beautiful Alps. Enjoy the delights of the many family and wellness offerings of the kid’s hotel.

FAMILY VACATION HOTEL ALPENROSE

The
4-star superior child hotel Alpenrose
offers all the facilities to make your
family vacation
an unforgettable experience. Like all the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts", the hotel offers its guests a top hotel standard. The suites of the kids hotel Alpenrose provide luxurious living space with separate children’s bedrooms.







At the child hotel
spacious indoor and outdoor play areas
await your children. The family hotel, awarded with 5 smileys, provides a
unique supervision program for children and babies
. More than 2,000 m² are exclusively at the kid’s disposal: cinema and theatre, spacious soft playground, huge slide (5 floors), indoor go-karting. The
"Pirate Land"
with its real sandy beach and a
spacious water park
with several indoor and outdoor pools round off the spa’s offerings for children. All this ensures the parents can lay back and spend a relaxing holiday!

EXCELLENT FAMILY VACATION AT THE "HolidayCheck Award"

The family hotel Alpenrose is a member of the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts". The
leading hotels for family vacation in Europe
have been selected and evaluated according to the strictest possible criteria. The child hotel Alpenrose provides 90 luxury suites - among them 15 wellness-family suites (up to 195 m²). Our friendly staff provide a first class service, aiming to fulfill the needs and wishes of a family on holiday.

Spend an amazing family vacation -
a wide range of leisure offerings and professional childcare
are at your disposal at the child friendly hotel Alpenrose! No less than 24 professional supervisors take care of your children 7 days weekly and create exciting daily programs for them. A
spacious spa area (750 m²)
with 6 saunas, a modern fitness room with varied fitness equipment and an indoor golf course, provide the basis for a relaxing family holiday.

CUISINE & SPORTS HIGHLIGHTS

The child hotel Alpenrose is well known for its exclusive five-star service and its
gourmet kitchen
. Every day we offer a dinner menu in the evenings with choices for each course. In addition we offer rich breakfast and lunch buffets and in the afternoon we serve snacks and a cake buffet. The Alpenrose is also known for its sophisticated wine service. We recommend a visit to the
wine lounge
, where more than 38 000 bottles of exquisite wine are stored!

Take advantage of the
numerous sport offerings
at the family hotel Alpenrose in Tyrol. The children’s hotel is worth a visit, both in summer and in winter. Visit a golf course or take part in a guided bike tour to the mountains. As a member of the bikehotels, the Alpenrose offers guided bike tours for cyclists of all levels, three times a week. The famous
"Tyrol Zugspitze Arena"
has been selected as Austria’s best bike region and provides great conditions for hiking, skiing, snowboarding and cross-country skiing. Take advantage of the hotel’s own ski rental service on your winter holiday. Unique throughout the world:
skiing on the hotel grounds
- young beginners will find
optimal slope conditions
from mid December until mid April.

HIGHLIGHTS FOR FAMILIES


More than 120 qualified employees guarantee for an
individual and five-star service
to make sure, that your family holiday will be a unique experience


Top facilities for children:
2.000 m² indoor playground including rooms for childcare, children dining room ‘knights’; 5.000 m² outdoor adventure area


Spacious spa landscape:
750 m² first class spa (incl. private spa), 6 saunas, indoor and outdoor pools and water park with tube slide and brine bath


Marvellous landscape:
Impressive mountain scenery on the sunny side of the famous "Zugspitze"


Numerous sport offerings:
golf courses, tennis, fitness, bike tours, rafting, canyoning, bungee jumping, hiking


Top skiing area
right next to the hotel: 100 m to the gondola lift "Grubigstein" and 15 minutes to the glacial area ‘Zugspitze’!

Bicycles for rent, ski rental, car rental, motorcycle, inline skates and baby buggies for rent
