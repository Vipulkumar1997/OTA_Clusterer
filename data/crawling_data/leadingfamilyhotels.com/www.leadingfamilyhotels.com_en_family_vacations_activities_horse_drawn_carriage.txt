Horse-drawn carriage ride through the idyllic surroundings

Round off your vacation day at the exclusive children’s hotels of ‚Leading Family‘ with a romantic horse-drawn carriage ride.















































Discover the surroundings of your family hotel during a carriage ride. Enjoy the stunning view over the beautiful landscape! Explore the fascinating countryside of your vacation region while doing a horse-drawn carriage ride with your family.

We offer
horse-drawn carriage rides all year round
, in summer as well as in winter. Discover the beauty of the surrounding landscapes of your children’s hotel during all seasons: Idyllic wintery landscapes with snow-covered trees, green meadows and fields in summer or coloured deciduous forests in autumn. At the 4-star superior family hotels you will get more information on timetables and routes of the carriage rides offered.

On your family vacation at the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" Löwe & Bär in Serfaus (Tyrol) you can make a
romantic carriage ride through the village of Serfaus
or to the nearby village Madatschen. The
child-friendly family hotel Cavallino Bianco in Val Gardena (Italy)
offers daily carriage rides for its guests starting right at the entrance of the hotel. Discover the charming small town of St. Ulrich and the fascinating ‘Dolomites’ mountains that surround it.

Try something new on your family vacation at the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" and make a
romantic carriage ride
. Explore the
fascinating natural landscape
surrounding your family hotel together with your children or just in two.
