Serfaus – A family’s paradise in the beautiful Tyrolean Mountains.

The exclusive children’s hotels Löwe & Bär are in the midst of the most family-friendly region in the Alps! In winter Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis impresses with its spacious skiing area with almost 200 km of pistes up to 2,800 m and guaranteed snow.




























































































































































Top family skiing area in the Tyrolean mountains

The exclusive children’s hotels Löwe & Bär are in the midst of the most family-friendly region in the Alps! In winter
Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis
impresses with its spacious skiing area with 214 skiable kilometres of slopes up to 2,800 m and guaranteed snow. The region provides a wide range of attractions for children and therefore is amongst the best family skiing areas in the Alps. The
ski school "Kinderschneealm"
and the
"Murmlipark"
(right in front of the hotel Bär) offer their little guests several specials and an extraordinary supervision program. Different snow parks, a speed slope and action highlights like the tight rope
"Serfauser Sauser"
, the
"Fisser Flieger"
(hang glider) or the
"Skyswing" (giant swing for 8 persons)
round off the many attractions for guests.

Snow & fun abroad the ski slopes

The exclusive family skiing resort of Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis offers a wide range of winter activities on the ski slopes. On your family vacation at the child hotels Löwe & Bär no less than
30 km of perfectly prepared cross-country tracks
and
toboggan runs
await you.
100 km of attractive winter hiking trails
invite you to have a walk through the wintery dream-like landscape. Take the opportunity to do a horse drawn sleigh ride on your family vacation through the village of Serfaus or into the surrounding area.

Hiking paradise for families

Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis is the
top destination for your family vacation
in the mountains. Because of its numerous sports offerings for families, the region is unique in the Alps! Take advantage of the
"Super.Sommer.Card"
in summer and autumn that enables you free travel with the cableways and hiking buses. The "Adventure Mountains" offer many attractions, such as
adventure hikes for children
(witch trail, "Murmli"-trail), the
summer fun park
with its summer toboggan run, a giant swing as well as a hang glider and the "Murmliwasser", an
amazing water fun park
for children. There are lots to discover on your family vacation in the "Adventure Mountains" of Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis.
