Four stars plus

The "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" aim to distinguish themselves from the numerous hotels offering typical family vacations. High quality family hotels still are, and remain, a rarity.





































































































































































































































































































The exclusive children’s hotels therefore aim to offer top hotel standards and an
exclusive four-star plus service
adjusted to the special needs and wishes of families.
The four-star child hotels have to fulfill very strict criteria of approval, therefore guaranteeing
top hotel standards
. The four exclusive family hotels in Austria and Italy offer their guests amazing and luxurious family vacations. The kid’s hotels provide
top international quality standards
and an exquisite four-stars plus service for their guests. Luxurious amenities and products of high quality await you on your luxury family vacation.

LEADING FAMILY HOTELS & RESORTS - ALL INCLUSIVE SERVICE OFFERS

The children’s hotels offer a professional and
unique supervision program
for kids aged 0 to 13 years. At least 12 professional supervisors are at the children’s disposal 6 days a week and take care of them in age-based groups. Various leisure programs are offered for children to make sure, that the family vacation becomes an adventure for both parents and kids. At the baby hotels of "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts", parents always know their children are well taken care of and therefore can lay back and relax knowing their children are safe and well cared for.

The
wellness offerings
of the children hotels are
numerous and adjusted to the needs of families
: Separate spa areas for parents and kids and special family spas await the guests of the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts". Besides the wellness landscapes, the child hotels provide exclusive and superbly-designed facilities and offer several saunas and relaxation rooms. Top beauty and spa treatments with high-quality product lines complete the wellness offerings of the exclusive family hotels.

Furthermore the four-stars plus service of the exclusive children’s hotels includes the
"Leading Family Concierge Service"
. The staff of your family hotel will take care of your every need, ski ticket reservation, change flight bookings or order transportation to name just a few examples.










Simply the best for families!
