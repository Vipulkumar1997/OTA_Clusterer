Quality standards & philosophy

The “Leading Family Hotels & Resorts”  aim to be the top destinations for family holidays in Europe.





































































































































































































































































































The "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" strive to stand out in the “family hotel” field providing the highest quality standards in terms of service and accomodation. Our hotels have been selected and evaluated according to the strictest possible criteria. The following
4-star child hotels
have been able to fulfill the strict criteria for approval: the
Alpenrose
in Lermoos and the hotels
Löwe & Bär
in Serfaus (both Tyrol/Austria) and the
Cavallino Bianco Family Spa Grand Hotel
in Val Gardena (South Tyrol/Italy).

FIRST-CLASS SERVICE FOR ALL INCLUSIVE PRICE

The
exclusive "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts"
ensure a first-class service as well as international top hotel standards. Guests of the child hotels enjoy
a four-stars plus service
, fine tuned to the particular needs of families with children. A variety of modern leisure facilities and an excellent supervision program for your kids guarantee, that your holiday will be a unique experience! Guest families benefit from the many services our
all-inclusive package
is providing. However they get a first-class service and products of high quality, customized to the special needs and wishes of the guests.

TOP IN CUISINE AND WELLNESS

The children hotels of "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" are focused on wellness. The spacious and
stylish wellness areas
invite you to experience total relaxation. Thanks to the pleasant atmosphere of the exclusive wellness landscapes, parents can leave their everyday routines behind. The
family suites
all provide spacious bedrooms and living rooms and separate children bedrooms. The luxury family suites even provide private saunas and whirlpools.

The
gourmet cuisine
of the child hotels also provides dining of the highest quality, with only the best ingredients used. Guests of the kid’s hotels can choose between dishes-of-the-day or the dinner menu with choices for each course. The all-inclusive service also includes rich buffets as well as snacks and cakes in the afternoons and complimentary non-alcoholic drinks.










Simply the best for families!
