Löwe & Bär in Serfaus Tirol ****S

Take some time out in the fascinating landscape of the Tyrolean Mountains. The child hotels Löwe & Bär offer all the amenities, a family with children could ask for: a 4-star plus hotel standard, professional childcare, various leisure facilities, numerous sport, entertainment and spa offers, along with first class gourmet cuisine.

AMAZING ALL INCLUSIVE OFFERS FOR FAMILIES

At the exclusive family hotels Löwe & Bär you will spend an unforgettable family vacation. Serfaus is the number one family-destination for winter and summer sports. Experience the excellent 4-star superior service, customized to fit the individual needs of every family!

Our guests expect the best: In our luxury child hotels we use only the highest quality products in our restaurants. Our family suites are coupled with modern amenities. Guests of our child hotels enjoy an individual and outstanding service. Inclusive in the room price: a dinner menu in the evenings with choices for each course, in addition to splendid breakfast buffets, lunch buffets and changing dishes-of-the-day. Last but not least the family hotels Löwe & Bär provide a unique and professional childcare service 6 days a week.






FOUNDING MEMBERS OF LEADING FAMILY HOTELS & RESORTS

The family hotels Löwe & Bär are founding members of the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts". The child hotels are renouned for
high quality in child care
, having been selected and evaluated according to strict criteria. They guarantee
first-class hotel standard
combined with age-specific childcare and
leisure facilities for the entire family
. The spacious indoor pools and soft playgrounds of the Löwe & Bär offer several water slides, crawl tunnels etc. and therefore guarantee action and fun for your kids!

WELLNESS IN THE ALPS

Löwe & Bär are the exclusive
wellness hotels
for families in the Alps. The spa areas of Löwe & Bär are real oases of wellbeing - offering several saunas, aromatic steam baths and laconium. The numerous spa offerings are rounded off by a
family spa area
including a family sauna. Round off a perfect day on your family vacation by hanging out in the
beauty centre of Löwe & Bär
. Let yourself be pampered by a relaxing massage or by one of the many bath and cosmetic treatments on offer.

HIGHLIGHTS CHILD HOTELS LÖWE & BÄR


First-class international hotel standard and individual all inclusive
4-star superior service
for demanding families


Professional childcare
of babies and children (0 - 13 years) in age-based groups, 6 days a week.
Varied program of leisure activities
: plays, several shows, 3d-cinema for children and magic school


Varied leisure facilities:
unique indoor and outdoor pools, family spa and big water slide (100 m). The "Löwen- und Bärenparadies" for children provide spacious soft playgrounds on 2 floors including a big slide and a crawl tunnel.

The
family suites
(35 to 100 m²) have luxury and comfortable living and bedrooms perfectly adapted to the needs of both, parents and children

The
stylish spa areas
of the Löwe & Bär create a pleasant and relaxing atmosphere: several saunas, steam baths, whirlpools and a generous relaxation room

The
beauty resorts
offer various cosmetic and bath treatments and massages

The child hotels Löwe & Bär are
situated right at the center of Serfaus
and only 80 meters from the bus stop of the subterranean village train in Serfaus. It takes only a few minutes to the amazing ski area
"Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis"
, a ski paradise for families with 204 snow-sure kilometers of slopes. In summer and autumn the area becomes a truly hiking paradise!

"Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis" has been awarded "Top Ski Resort" and "family ski area of the year". Therefore Serfaus is the
top family destination for wintersports in the Alps
! The ‘Kinderschneealm’ (<45.000 m²) and the "Murmli-Park" (in front of the hotel Bär) provide a lot of attractions for kids, several practice areas with obstacles and more than 70 child care specialists for your children

Unique in the Alps! In
summer and autumn
(from July to October) guests of Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis
travel for free
with all ski lifts and the hiking bus
