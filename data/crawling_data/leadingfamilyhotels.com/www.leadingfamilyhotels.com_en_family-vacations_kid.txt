Holidays with children

There is a saying: "Children are precious gifts". Like all parents know, children also have special wishes and they often have very precise ideas of a happy family vacation. Therefore the "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" spare no efforts to fulfill all wishes of their little guests.








































































Baby-perfect equipment & unique model of supervision

The "Leading Family Hotels & Resorts" provide equipment that fulfills all needs that families with children have. The luxury 4-star children’s hotels have focused exclusively on families and singles with children and babies. They guarantee
high hotel standards
and amenities perfectly
adjusted to the needs of children and babies
.

Quality is our primary concern - our exclusive child friendly hotels have been selected according to very strict criteria. The leading hotels for sophisticated family holidays in Europe unite
first-class international hotel standards
and an excellent service, custom designed to the wishes of sophisticated families on vacation.

Our exclusive family hotels also offer a
unique supervision service.
Children are supervised 6 days a week for up to 12 hours per day. At least 12 professional supervisors take care of the guest children in different age groups from 0 to 13 years.
A diverse program
with lots of fun and action awaits your children: ball games, fashion shows, magic school, theatre and playback shows, etc. For older kids and teens we offer numerous outdoor programs such as rafting, canyoning or excursions to mountain pastures.

Offerings for babies and toddlers

Auch für die Kleinsten bieten die Leading Family Hotels & Resorts ein intensives Betreuungsangebot und eine
babyperfekte Ausstattung
. An sechs Tagen in der Woche werden Ihre Babys und Kleinkinder bis 2,5 Jahre mindestens neun Stunden pro Tag in eigenen Babyclubs betreut. Das
Konzept des Leading Childcare
der Kinderhotels sieht außerdem eine großzügige Ausstattung mit Baby-Utensilien vor.

Von Babywagen über Babywippen, Badewannen, Wickeltisch über Fläschchenwärmer, Babyfon sowie Babynahrung bleiben in den Luxushotels für Kinder keine Wünsche offen. Die Hektik beim Kofferpacken und ein überfüllter Kofferraum bleiben Ihnen daher erspart. Mit den Leading Family Hotels & Resorts können Sie bereits entspannt und mit einem guten Gefühl in den verdienten Familienurlaub starten!










Simply the best for families!
