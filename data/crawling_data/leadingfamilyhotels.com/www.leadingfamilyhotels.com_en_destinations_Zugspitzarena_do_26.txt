TYROL ZUGSPITZE ARENA - TOP HOLIDAY REGION FOR A DREAM-LIKE FAMILY VACATION

The exclusive children’s hotel Alpenrose is situated in a breathtaking landscape, on the sunny side of the "Zugspitze" mountain. The sunny holiday region "Tyrol Zugspitze Arena" offers a wide range of leisure activities for families and for athletes as well. Beautiful mountains, lakes and numerous bike trails are available to guests of the luxury family hotel Alpenrose. For passionate mountain biker the area provides an extensive route as well as a free ride route. The region has been voted two times as "best bike region of Austria". We also recommend to explore the beautiful landscape by feet - lovely hiking trails run through the amazing Tyrolean countryside. Spend an amazing family vacation at the child hotel Alpenrose on the sunny side of the "Zugspitze"!

Winter wonderland for skiers, snowboarder and …

The "Tyrol Zugspitze Arena" offers its guests
148 kilometres of pistes
and 55 mountain railways on
7 diverse skiing areas
. From the children’s hotel Alpenrose it takes only 200 m to the gondola chairlift that takes you to the local mountain, the "Grubigstein". It takes only 15 minutes by car to get from your family hotel to the
glacier skiing area ‘Zugspitze’
. The snow-sure ski resort offers other 87 kilometres of snow-covered pistes.
20 well prepared cross-country trails
run through the beautiful countryside of the area. The ‘Tyrol Zugspitze Arena’ provides various skiing offerings from beginners to experts and therefore is your perfect holiday region for an amazing family vacation. Alternatively the area around the 4-star family hotel offers you many other leisure activities:
winter hiking, tobogganing and snowshoe hiking
. Action junkies should take the opportunity for
paragliding all year round
, including the stunning view on the Zugspitze!

Paradise for mountain biking and hiking

In summer the "Tyrol Zugspitze Arena" is known as an attractive destination for mountain bikers. More than 100 mountain bike trails run through the beautiful mountain landscape of the ‘Zugspitze’. The region has been voted twice as
Austria’s best bike region
. Alternatively there are many fantastic bike trails running through the valley, like the "water trails" round amazing lakes, such as "Heitwanger Lake", "Plan Lake" and "Eib Lake".
Advanced mountain bikers have at their disposal several cable ways to take advantage of the many mountain trails. The highlight for all mountain bike pros: the
freeride trail
which starts at the middle station of the cable way to the "Grubigstein" and provides several jumps, bridges and natural obstacles!

The "Tyrol Zugspitze Arena" is also very famous for its
marvellous hiking trails
and is the perfect destination for a family vacation with children. You can hike along the many lakes in the valley or to one of the beautiful mountain lakes. On your family vacation you can choose between hikes along alpine pastures and pleasant walks to the summits of the surrounding mountain peaks. Take advantage of the many offers in the close surroundings of your children’s hotel; climbing tours, paragliding, canyoning, rafting, bungee-jumping, tennis and golfing.
