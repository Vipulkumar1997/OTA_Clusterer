Package Deals
For carefree holidays, the tourist destinations of Aigle, Leysin and Col des Mosses have some great package deals on offer.
Throughout the season, whether you want to recharge your batteries or enjoy a multi-activity family holiday, the local tourist offices have put together some truly special deals with a large variety of different experiences to savour.
Combined with accommodation, these offers guarantee competitive prices throughout the year. Booking is easy, either online or by simply calling the tourist office. Offers can also be adapted to suit individual needs of holidaymakers.
