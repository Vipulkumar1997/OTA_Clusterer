Group accomodation
There’s a whole host of wonderful places for groups to stay in Leysin and Col des Mosses.
The vast range of both summer and winter activities in the resorts make them ideal for school camps, club outings and even weekend trips with friends. Lodgings are in scenic locations often close to walking trails or to the ski pistes.
Depending on the needs and desires of each particular group, the accommodation provides all you need whether on a self-catering basis or with an in-house catering service for extra comfort.  Residences are specially adapted to welcome groups and usually offer outdoor play areas, relaxing areas and large reception rooms for games nights.
