Families
Families are especially welcome both in summer and winter in the resorts of Leysin, Col des Mosses and Aigle.
Every attempt is made to make sure that each and everyone experiences memorable moments of adventure. In summer there’s a huge number of easily accessible hikes where walkers can enjoy being out in nature whether its lakeside or in the mountains. In winter the ski schools and their snow gardens offer children an ideal introduction to skiing.
Throughout the year a whole calender of events and entertainments brings all generations together for some engaging activities to share and enjoy. Play areas and picnic spots also play their part in essential outdoor living.
