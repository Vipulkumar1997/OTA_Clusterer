Lake Geneva Region Tourist Office
The Canton of Vaud, a place to visit in 2016, according to the New York Times !
Sport and leasure
State of the art infrastructures and exciting new experiences ensure a holiday rich in discovery when you visit the region of Aigle, Leysin and Col des Mosses.
For training sessions, to improve your fitness or quite simply to spend some relaxing time with family and friends, these destinations offer sporting and leisure activities adapted to all shapes and sizes as well as to all ages.
Summer is the time for hiking and in so doing you can explore the valley and its vineyards or the majestic mountain peaks.
