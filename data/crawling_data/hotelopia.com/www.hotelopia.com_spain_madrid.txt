Information about Madrid



The city of Madrid is the capital of Spain and of the Community of Madrid, previously the province of Madrid.     We highly
                        recommend visits to the following places of interest: the Reina Sofía Centre of Art, where you can see Picasso’s famous “Gernika”,
                        the National Archeological Museum, the Wax Museum, the Museum of Natural Science, the San Fernando Museum of the Royal Academy
                        of Fine Arts, the Prado Museum (1785), and many other museums.    We also advise you to take the time to go and see the following
                        sights: the Madrid Walls (9th century) the Church of San Nicolás de los Servitas, Casa de los Lujanes (15th century), the
                        Capilla del Obispo chapel (16th century), Segovia Bridge, el Monasterio de la Encarnación (17thcentury), Plaza Mayor (17th
                        century), which was built in 1617 in Renaissance style and with a statue of Philip II in its centre. Another important monument
                        is the Palacio Real, which was inaugurated in 1714 by Charles III and is considered one of the most beautiful palaces in Europe.
                        Come and discover this impressive city and its many cultural attractions, visit Madrid!
