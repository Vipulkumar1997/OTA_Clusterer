                        beachesâ€¦these are just some of the attractions of this handsome city.    Among the most popular sights and activities in Cape

                        different gardens and myriad plant varieties; and Table Mountain, which has eleven different vantage points for looking out
                        over this lovely city, the peninsula and the coast.    Cape Townâ€™s beaches are quite varied, with different things to see
