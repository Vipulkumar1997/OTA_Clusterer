Information about Detroit - MI



The city of Detroit is the largest in the state of Michigan, United States. It is the main port in the River Detroit area.
                        This city is known for its connections with the world of cars, and it is for this reason that it is sometimes called Motor
                        City. For car fans, the Automotive Hall of Fame is a fascinating museum that tells the story of the industry's pioneers. 
                        Detroit is also a very musical city, where you can enjoy some great night clubs, and music in all styles, from jazz to rock.
                        There are many concerts and events for the visitor to enjoy. Not only is there great nightlife here, but you can also experience
                        some of the place's historical spots, its museums, its casinos and amusement parks.     There are also some excellent restaurants,
                        and the good food and drink here includes some really tasty local desserts.     Come to Detroit and you'll find a city where
                        you are guaranteed to have fun!
