Information about Sofia



The city of Sofia is the capital of the Republic of Bulgaria, and is also the biggest city in the country. It is located in
                        the western part of Bulgaria, at the foot of the Vitosha Mountain. It is the administrative, cultural, commercial, economic,
                        social and educational centre of the country.     We recommend that you visit the following points of interest: the Alexander
                        Nevsky Cathedral, which dates back to the year 1912, being declared a cultural monument years alter, the Ivan Vazoff National
                        Theatre, the Russian Orthodox Church – built in 1913 –  the round church of St George (4th century), the Bania Balli Mosque
                        and the National Assembly, among other beautiful buildings.     We would also urge you to see the numerous thermal water fountains
                        that the city has to offer. One of the most important of these is in the centre of Sofia, and there are many other fountains
                        throughout the city that are claimed to have healing and therapeutic properties.     Sofia is a lovely city that offers a
                        broad range of both recreational and cultural attractions, while also having excellent hotels and fine cuisine. Discover Sofia,
                        come and visit it!
