Information about Miami - FL



Miami is a major US city located in southeastern Florida, between the Everglades National Park and the Atlantic Ocean.   
                        It is one of the main tourist destinations in the country, and it is known as the capital of sun because of its pleasant climate
                        and beautiful beaches. Those who like to have an active holiday will enjoy the wide range of water activities and sports available
                        on its beaches.     Apart from its nice white-sand beaches, Miami has many other things such as museums, historical sites,
                        parks and gardens. The city is close to two national parks of stunning beauty: the Everglades, rich in fauna and flora, and
                        the Biscayne, with a tropical reef that is unique in the area.     Miami also boasts lively nightlife where there is no shortage
                        of bars and quality international restaurants, many of which specialise in fish and seafood dishes. Excitement and fun are
                        at home in Miami, where you can enjoy high-technology entertainment and games as well as extreme experiences at the Richard
                        Petty Driving Experience.     Come and discover everything Miami has to offer!
