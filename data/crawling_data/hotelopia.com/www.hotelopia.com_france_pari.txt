Information about Paris



Paris is the capital of France and the world’s most popular tourist destination. It is the most glamorous city in the world
                        and the one with the most historical monuments and landmarks. Explore the beautiful Champs Elysees from the Concorde to the
                        Arc de Triomphe. You will find all of the top brands on Avenue Montaigne.    Visit Place de la Concorde and Place Vendôme’s
                        luxury hotels and jewellers.    A stroll along the banks of the Seine is a good way to see the city’s landmarks. The Eiffel
                        Tower, the Louvre, la Défense, the Cathedral of Notre Dame and the 35 beautiful bridges along the river are highlights of
                        any visit.    Don’t forget to go and see Disneyland, Jardin du Luxembourg, Parc Astérix, Ile Saint-Louis, the Catacombs, the
                        Latin Quarter, Montmartre and Quartier du Marais, the Sacré-Coeur, (the oldest church in Paris), the Opera, Les Invalides,
                        la Place de la Bastilla, the Palace of Versalles and the Lafayette Galleries, where you can buy excellent perfumes.    The
                        best nightlife is in Place Pigalle and Blance, the Champs Elysees, in Saint-German-des-Prés, Montparnasse and around the Sorbonne
                        and the Pantheon.    Paris awaits you in its pomp and glory.
