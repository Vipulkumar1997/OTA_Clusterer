Information about Orlando - FL



The city of Orlando is to be found in the state of Florida, in the southeast of the United States.     This urban area is
                        known for its tourist attractions and theme parks, including the world-famous Walt Disney World, and others such as SeaWorld
                        and the Universal Orlando Resort. Orlando is a city for having fun and indulging fantasies as it offers attractions for visitors
                        of all ages. Here you can enjoy the many shows, museums and theatres that the city offers, as well as do sports or enjoy the
                        nightlife.     Orlando is also a major business centre, with all kinds of shops with the latest trends and the most important
                        brands for a shopping trip. Disney World combines magic, technology, cinema and nature for endless fun in the different parks,
                        which are the Magic Kingdom, Epcot, Disney-MGM Studios and the Animal Kingdom. Universal Orlando Resort has two different
                        amusement parks which will dazzle the visitor, bringing cinematic experiences to life. SeaWorld reveals some of the mysteries
                        of the sea, making for real adventures.     You can't help having fun in Orlando, come and visit!
