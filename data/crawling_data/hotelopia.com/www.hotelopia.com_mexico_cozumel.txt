Information about Cozumel



Cozumel is a Caribbean island off the Yucatan peninsula, in the southeast of Mexico. It belongs to the municipality of Quintana
                        Roo and it is one of the largest and most populated of Mexican islands.     It has become a major tourist destination because
                        of its beaches, its people and its many different attractions. Nature has gifted Cozumel beautiful coral reefs where diving
                        and snorkelling are unique experiences. There are many different possibilities for all levels of diver, from beginners to
                        professionals. The beaches are extremely beautiful, with their clear waters and their waves which are ideal for surfing. 
                        The food and drink on offer is excellent, and the seafront restaurants are very enjoyable places to spend a while. Furthermore,
                        Cozumel has an important cultural element, as there are Mayan archaeological sites here. One of the largest is located in
                        San Gervasio, while the Punta Sur Park houses the El Caracol ruins. Cozumel awaits you, come and enjoy holidays in paradise!
