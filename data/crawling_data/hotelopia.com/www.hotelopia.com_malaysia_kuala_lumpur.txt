Information about Kuala Lumpur



Kuala Lumpur is the capital of Malaysia. The city is now a popular tourist destination with an endless number of attractions
                        such as shopping, recreational activities and visits to cultural sites. The city itself is of great beauty and its scenery
                        is truly breathtaking. Some of the most important tourist places here are the House of Parliament, Kuala Lumpur Tower,  the
                        Eye on Malaysia and the Golden Triangle, which contains the Petronas Twin Towers.     The city also boasts some residual forest
                        areas which include national parks, botanical gardens and the like.     As well as tourist attractions, you can also enjoy
                        the distinctive nightlife here thanks to the variety of fashionable nightclubs, bars and lounges that you can find in the
                        city. Whatever you are looking for, you'll find it in Kuala Lumpur. Come and see for yourself.
