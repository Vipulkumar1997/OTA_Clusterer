Information about Niagara Falls - Canada



The Niagara Falls are a set of large waterfalls on the river Niagara, between the United States and Canada. The Canadian falls
                        are part of Queen Victoria Niagara Falls Park, in Ontario.     The park's gardens offer spectacular views of the falls, there
                        are also paths leading to observatories that will make you feel as though you are right under the falling water.     From
                        the Skylon tower observatory, you will get a panoramic view from a height of 160 metres, in the backdrop behind you is a view
                        of Toronto, and you can dine in a revolving restaurant.    You can pass under Niagara Falls through tunnels and on platforms,
                        or you can board a pleasure boat that will give you an unforgettable experience. Another interesting trip is going from Fort
                        Erie to Fort George, sailing along the Niagara River.    The Canadian side has a significant amount of tourist infrastructure.
                        Clifton Hill street is es muy comercial, con distintas atracciones temáticas, especial para la diversión y la vida nocturna.
                        In the Fallsview area, there is excellent hotel accommodation available to suit every budget.    Each night, Niagara falls
                        are let up using different coloured lights; it is an impressive show that you should not forget to see.
