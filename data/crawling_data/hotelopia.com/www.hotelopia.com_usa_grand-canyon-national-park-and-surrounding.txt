Information about Grand Canyon National Park and surroundings



Grand Canyon National Park is one of the oldest national parks in the United States. It is in the southwestern state of Arizona.
                        The park's main attraction is, obviously, the world famous Grand Canyon, a natural valley carved out of the rock by the Colorado
                        River. It is considered one of the natural wonders of the world, and was named a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1979).    
                        Discover one of the wonders that nature and time sculpted in this part of the world, enjoy landscapes without equal, lush
                        forests full of pine and poplar trees, see for yourself local wildlife that includes deer, pumas and antelopes.     The park
                        also offers visitors all kinds of outdoor activities, among the most popular of which are rafting and hiking.     Savour the
                        spectrum of colours that is the Grand Canyon, enjoy its vast expanse and a natural beauty that will leave you breathless.
                        Visit the spectacular Grand Canyon National Park!
