Information about Las Vegas - NV



The biggest city in the US state of Nevada is the world famous Las Vegas.     Considered by many to be the ideal holiday destination
                        for those looking for a good time and some excellent boutiques, Las Vegas was founded in 1905. It is the World Entertainment
                        Capital and has many mega casinos, like the Luxor, Mandalay Bay, Rio, The Excalibur and the Flamingo, as well as many other
                        leisure options for adults, a diversity that gained it the nickname Sin City.     Besides the casinos and luxurious hotels,
                        the city’s main draws are the many shows that it puts on every night, some of them, like the Masquerade Show in the Sky, are
                        free, while others, like the well known Cirque du Soleil set standards for excellence. In Vegas you will be able to find something
                        for everybody, from magic shows and theatre to music and other acts. Some fascinating places to visit are: the Bellagio Fountains,
                        which are synchronised with music to make a fabulous show; the Flamingo Garden, which is full of exotic birds; the MGM Grand
                        Lion Habitat, where you can see several excellent examples of this wild animal; and many other points of interest that are
                        sure to amaze you.     Magic and good old fashioned fun are waiting for you in Las Vegas.
