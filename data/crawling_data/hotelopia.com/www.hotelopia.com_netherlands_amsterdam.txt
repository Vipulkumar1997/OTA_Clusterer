Information about Amsterdam



Amsterdam, located in the province of North Holland, is the capital of the Netherlands. It is the country's largest city and
                        it is of great importance economically and culturally. The city is the most populous area of the great Dutch conurbation known
                        as Randstad.    Amsterdam is known for its splendid canals and its wonderful 16th and 17th century architecture. The city
                        also has some fine museums where you can see works by great artists such as Rembrandt and van Gogh. And so if you like cultural
                        activities then you shouldn't miss the Rijksmuseum, which has a major collection of artworks, including many by important
                        local artists from the 17th century. Another very popular museum is dedicated to one of the world's most famous artists, Vincent
                        van Gogh. Another must is the Rembrandthuis Museum, located in the house where Rembrandt lived and where he painted some of
                        his most celebrated paintings.    Amsterdam has a lively nightlife and a wealth of night clubs, bars and restaurants for all
                        tastes. It is a very liberal, tolerant city and a major social and cultural centre.
