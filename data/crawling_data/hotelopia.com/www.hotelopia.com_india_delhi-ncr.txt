Information about Delhi NCR



New Delhi is the capital city of the Republic of India and is located within the larger metropolis of Delhi.     If you like
                        shopping, this is the perfect place for you. Here you can find all kinds of crafts and traditional objects from every corner
                        of India, a large range of fabrics, metals, and precious stones, different types of woods and an endless number of spices
                        and perfumes. New Delhi is also a base for exploring further afield, like climbing in the Himalayas, doing extreme sports
                        such as rafting in some of the country's rivers that come down from the world's highest peak: Mount Everest. If you like snow,
                        you can ski in the different ski resorts in this area.    200 kilometres away from New Delhi, do not miss the Sariska Tiger
                        Reserve, where you will find interesting wildlife, with a large variety of Indian plants and animals.     New Delhi, one of
                        the world's great metropolises is waiting for you!
