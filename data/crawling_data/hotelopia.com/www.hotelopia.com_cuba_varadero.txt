Information about Varadero



Varadero is a Cuban town located on the Hicacos Peninsula.    The main attraction in Varadero is its beaches, which run along
                        22 kilometres of the peninsula. They have fine white sand, crystal-clear waters and a warm breeze. There are also caves and
                        scarps that can be easily reached.    The place offers the perfect conditions for scuba diving, fishing and doing all kinds
                        of water activities.  Apart from the traditional sun and beach option, there is also an amphitheatre that hosts many international
                        events and the Plaza América Convention Center, which strengthens Varadero's potential as a destination for holding congresses.
                        Varadero offers different services and comforts, like restaurants near the sand with amazing views of the sea, so that visitors
                        enjoy their stay to the most.    If you want to enjoy the Caribbean climate, peace and some excellent beaches, then Varadero
                        is the destination for you!
