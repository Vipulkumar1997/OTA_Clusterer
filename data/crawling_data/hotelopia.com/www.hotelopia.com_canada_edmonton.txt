Information about Edmonton



Edmonton is a modern, attractive and clean Canadian city and the capital of the province of Alberta.    Its shopping centre
                        West Edmonton Mall is well-known as it is North America's largest mall. It includes over 800 stores; an amusement park, Galaxyland;
                        and the World Waterpark, with seal shows, mini golf and an ice skating rink. There are also many restaurants where you can
                        have the tastiest dishes, there is free Internet access, a theatre and a casino. For accommodation, you can stay in one of
                        the nearby hotels.    The city boasts an excellent array of parks that are at their best in spring and summer. Different festivals,
                        musicals and plays are held here. Don't miss the Alberta Legislature Building, a landmark in the city. A few minutes from
                        Edmonton is the Ukranian Village theme park, which explains the life of the first immigrants to Alberta. Edmonton is well-communicated
                        and so is also a good base for visiting other interesting places in the province such as Calgary.    Come and visit Edmonton.
