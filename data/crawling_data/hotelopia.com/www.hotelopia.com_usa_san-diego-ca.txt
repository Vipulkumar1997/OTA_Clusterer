Information about San Diego - CA



San Diego is a major city in the southwest of the United States.    Its pleasant climate and white sand beaches, along with
                        all the attractions and the impressive infrastructure it offers, make this city one of the most popular destinations in North
                        America. Balboa Park is home to a large number of museums, gardens and the city's famous Zoo, while Gaslamp Quarter houses
                        many events and festivals.    Ocean Beach is on the west side of Point Loma, while Mission Beach and Pacific Beach lie between
                        the ocean and Mission Bay, a man-made aquatic park. Seaport Village is a shopping and dining complex overlooking the bay.
                        There are impressive amusement parks such as SeaWorld or the San Diego Wild Animal Park. Visit PETCO Park, the famous open-air
                        stadium, and the Qualcomm Stadium.    Go to the Air & Space Museum in Balboa Park. Other attractions in the city include Little
                        Italy, the Berkeley steam ferryboat, the Mission San Diego de Alcalá, Mount Soledad in la Jolla, the Old Town, the Star of
                        India (the world's oldest seafaring ship), the USS Midway museum, the Torrey Pines State Reserve and many others.    San Diego
                        offers something for everybody. The only thing you will need here is time to see all the interesting things this amazing city
                        offers.
