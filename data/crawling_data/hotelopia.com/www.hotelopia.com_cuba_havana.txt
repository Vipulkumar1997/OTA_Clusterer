Information about Havana



Havana, or La Villa de San Cristóbal de La Habana to give it its full Spanish name, is the capital of the Republic of Cuba.
                        If what you are looking for is the authentic flavour of the Caribbean, Havana is the place for you, one that is famous the
                        world over for its music, its food and its people.    The architecture of the city is colonial, and some of its most famous
                        districts are La Habana Vieja, Vedado and Miramar.    The eastern beaches are famous for their beauty and are very popular
                        with people who want to dive and practice other water sports there.    Havana’s night life is truly something to be experienced,
                        full of myriad rhythms, ranging from salsa to jazz, while its restaurants offer typical local dishes and its night clubs promise
                        endless fun.    These are just some of the many entertaining things to do in Havana.    All the flavour of the Caribbean in
                        one city, the art and culture of a wonderful people…there’s so much to discover in Havana!
