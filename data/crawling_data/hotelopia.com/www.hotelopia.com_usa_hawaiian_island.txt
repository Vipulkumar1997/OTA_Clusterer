Information about Hawaiian Islands



Hawaii is an archipelago made up of nineteen islands and reefs. Located in the Northern Pacific, it a US state. The archipelago
                        is known by the name of its largest island: Hawaii.    The most visited islands are: Oahu, Maui, Hawaii, Kauai, Molokai and
                        Lanai. These boast wonderful tropical forests, beaches of white sand and crystal clear waters, varied flora and important
                        monuments and historical sites.     There is no shortage of things to do in Hawaii, from engaging in water sports to going
                        for pleasant walks.     Besides Hawaii itself, there is another island that is especially popular: Oahu, home to the naval
                        base of Pearl Harbor and the famous Waikiki beach, which lies at the foot of the extinct Diamond Head volcano. It is also
                        on Oahu that you will find the biggest city and capital of Hawaii: Honolulu, where you will find a wide range of museums,
                        shops, and fantastic restaurants.    Maui, in turn, offers whale sightings, diving, surfing, and many other exciting activities
                        that attract thousands of tourists every year.    The islands of Hawaii are a dream come true, a paradise that is just waiting
                        for you.
