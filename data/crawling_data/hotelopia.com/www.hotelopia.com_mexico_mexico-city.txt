Information about Mexico City



Mexico City, also known as Mexico D.F, is the capital of the Federal Republic of Mexico. It is also the political, commercial,
                        and cultural centre of the country, and the most populous city. UNESCO has declared the historical centre of the city as a
                        world heritage site. It possesses stunning buildings that have great historical and architectural value. Highlights include
                        the Metropolitan Cathedral, the National Palace, where you can view works by the celebrated Diego Rivera and the Palacio de
                        las Bellas Artes (palace of fine arts). We also recommend that you get to know the Xochimilco ecological park, a picturesque
                        area full of canals along which you can take a ride in a typical trajinera, a wooden canoe decorated with flowers. This area
                        was also declared a UNESCO world heritage site.     Mexico city also offers a large variety of museums. The following stand
                        out: the National Anthropology Museum, the Museo del Templo Mayor (Museum of the high temple), the National Art Museum, the
                        San Carlos museum and the Natural History Museum, among many others.     Other interesting sites include the Paseo de la Reforma
                        promenade, where you can see examples of local architecture and find numerous shops, art galleries, monuments, hotels and
                        endless opportunities. Discover this marvellous American metropolis; visit Mexico City!
