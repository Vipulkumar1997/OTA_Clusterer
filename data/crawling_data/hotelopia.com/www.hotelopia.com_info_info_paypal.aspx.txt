Book your hotel with PayPal




PayPal allows businesses and consumers that use email to send and receive
online payments safely, easily and quickly
.
                     The PayPal network is based on the existing financial infrastructure of bank accounts and credit cards to create a real time
                     global payment solution.



1)  Select the method of paying online
The Hotelopia booking process is the same as always without any extra charge.
During the booking process you should
                     select the pay online box to make payment via PayPal
. Remember that you also have the option to pay at the hotel.




2)  Booking form

Once you get to the booking form, fill in all the fields and don't forget to select the PayPal button at the bottom where
                     the payment methods are shown, and accept the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.


3)  Payment
Upon reaching the payment you have two options:
- Use your PayPal account if you are already registered.
- If you do not have a PayPal account, you can enter your details and pay by credit card through the PayPal system.
                     By doing this, the transaction is done through PayPal using your credit card or debit card without having an account.
