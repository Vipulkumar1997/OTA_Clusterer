Information about Bangkok



Bangkok is the capital city of Thailand, on the banks of the River Chao Phraya, near its mouth on the Gulf of Thailand. It
                        is a dynamic, exotic, affordable city full of skyscrapers, temples, sophisticated shopping centres, interesting nightlife
                        as well as quality hotels and restaurants.    You will be amazed by the Grand Palace, a complex of buildings and temples with
                        spectacular architecture.     Here you can see the Reclining Buddha; ask him for a wish, as this is the tradition. You can
                        have a traditional massage at the Wat Pho temple, the largest and oldest in Bangkok. The city is also home to the National
                        Museum, the largest in Asia.    The Wat Traimit temple houses the Golden Buddha, a figure three metres high and over 5 tons
                        in weight.      Don't miss the elegant Wimanmek Golden Teak Palace and the Suan Pakkad Palace and its beautiful gardens. 
                        On Siam Square, near the World Trade Centre, visit the exotic Erawan Shrine, go shopping in the traditional market of Chatuchak
                        (open on Saturdays and Sundays only), or visit Chinatown.    Other attractions and activities to do in the city are Dusit
                        Zoo, the Planetarium, various fun fairs, safaris, golf courses, horse riding and much more... Bangkok will delight you with
                        its past and its present.
