Information about Lisbon



Lisbon, the capital of Portugal, is the country's largest city.     It has a warm, sunny climate, perfect to spend time in
                        the outdoors and discover this city's many attractions. Lisbon is a city with a rich cultural life and its historical neighbourhoods
                        are a must for all visitors. The names of the main districts of the city are Baixa, Alfama, Bairro Alto and Belém. The charm
                        of its streets, its alleys and its architecture makes any walk through Lisbon memorable.     In these neighbourhoods you can
                        find a number of monuments and places such as the D. Maria II National Theatre, the Praça do Comércio (or Commerce Square)
                        and the Rossio, in Baixa; the Cathedral and the Castle of Saint George in Alfama; and the striking Hieronymite Monastery and
                        the Belém Tower, in Belém, both UNESCO World Heritage. The Bairro Alto, Lisbon's central area, is a shopping and entertainment
                        district that comes alive at night.     Lisbon is a city that, with its history and its vibrant cultural and nightlife, will
                        thrill your senses. Come and discover it!
