Information about Chicago - IL



Chicago is a US city located on the southwestern shores of Lake Michigan, in the state of Illinois. The largest city in the
                        region.     Chicago has an active cultural life and offers thousands of entertaining possibilities. It is possible to see
                        exhibitions, events and shows every day of the week, while shopping trips and the city's nightlife and restaurants are certain
                        to appeal.     Chicago's culture can be found in its many famous museums, covering a wide range of subjects: art, history,
                        photography etc. If what you need is to relax, Chicago has Urban Spas that will let you leave your stress behind. The restaurants
                        offer some of the best national and international cuisine, and some delicious desserts and chocolates.     In Chicago, some
                        of the best sights are free, including the Cultural Center with its spectacular stained glass, such as the dome of Preston
                        Bradley Hall; Lincoln Park Zoo, which houses a wide variety of species, as well having some lovely gardens; and Garfield Park,
                        ideal for kids and those who love flowers.     Chicago has everything you could want, come and discover it for yourself!
