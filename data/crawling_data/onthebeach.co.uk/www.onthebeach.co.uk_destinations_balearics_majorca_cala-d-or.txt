Read more about Cala D Or



Cala D’or is situated on the east coast of Majorca about 45 minutes from the airport. Originally modelled on a white washed Iberian village, all buildings had to be no more than 1 storey to help create the desired effect. However in the latter years the laws have been relaxed allowing slightly taller buildings in the resort. You will find beautiful little coves of golden sandy beaches with calm Mediterranean waters which makes it a very popular destination with families.

If you want to see what the resort has to offer then jump on the big yellow fun train which takes you all around Cala D’or and into the stunning harbour where you can marvel at the millionaire yachts or people watch at one of the many bars and restaurants . All around Cala D’or you will notice plenty of things to keep the whole family entertained from the crazy golf courses, go carts, bouncy castles and mini funfairs.
