Overview

With just a promenade separating it from a sweeping bay, the Hotel Troya is in a perfect location for beach lovers. Popular with couples, families and friends alike, the hotel is also super-handy for exploring all the sights and sounds of the resort, as it’s just across from the iconic Veronicas Strip.

How far is the nearest beach?

The hotel overlooks the beach.

Distance from Airport

The hotel is 19km from Tenerife South Airport.

Rooms

Each of the bright and contemporary standard rooms feature an en-suite bathroom, air conditioning, 32” satellite TV, safe and a balcony or terrace. Further in-room amenities include Wi-Fi access, direct-dial telephone and a fridge. Extra-spacious family rooms are also available, along with luxurious Junior Suites, and exclusive Club Alexandre rooms which offer perks such as spa access (ages 16+ only), bathrobe/slippers, Nespresso machine and USB charging facility.

Food & Drink

In the main restaurant, there’s a wide selection of buffet cuisine served with show cooking, including fresh pizzas, pastas, salads and delicious desserts. Plus, for the little ones, there’s a dedicated children’s menu. For light bites and Mediterranean specialities, there’s also the Palapa restaurant located in a shady spot right by the pool – and a range of delicious beverages are served at the poolside Cocktail Lounge, including refreshing juices and long drinks.

All Inclusive Food & Drink

The Hotel Troya’s All Inclusive programme includes breakfast (07:30-10:30), lunch (12:30-15:30) and dinner (18:30-21:45), all served buffet-style in the main restaurant. A variety of snacks and locally-produced alcoholic/non-alcoholic drinks are served at the pool bar and lobby bar from 10:30-12:30, 15:30-18:30 and 21:45-23:30. Please note that premium branded drinks, reserve beverages and some wines are not included in the All Inclusive programme.

Sports & Entertainment

Right by the promenade, the hotel boasts a spacious outdoor swimming pool, along with a separate pool for little ones. To soak up that incredible Tenerife sun, there’s also a chilled-out sun terrace, furnished with sun loungers and parasols throughout – and to continue the relaxation, you can head to the beautiful wellness centre (ages 16+ only), which features a hydrotherapy circuit, sauna, steam bath and Jacuzzi.

If keeping active is more your thing, there’s also loads to get stuck into, including tennis, table tennis, mini-golf and a well-equipped gym. Plus, the hotel animation team are on hand with a fabulous daytime and evening entertainment programme, including professional shows – and for the little ones, there’s a playground and colourful mini-club for ages 4-12. Basic Wi-Fi access is also available in public areas and guest rooms free of charge.

Chargeable Extras

In-room fridges and safes, room service, phone calls, picnics, premium Wi-Fi access, laundry services, beauty salon services, car/bicycle hire, use of games machines and access to the wellness centre are payable locally.
