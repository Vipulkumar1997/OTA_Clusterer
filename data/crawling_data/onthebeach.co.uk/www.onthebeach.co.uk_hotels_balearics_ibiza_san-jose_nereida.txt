Overview

Close to the seafront and the bay of San Antonio, the three-star Nereida Aparthotel is a great pick for a holiday in Ibiza. This property features swimming pools and entertainment for the whole family in a popular resort on the west of the island.

How far is the nearest beach?

The hotel is about 300m from Xinxo Beach.

Distance from Airport

It is 18km from Ibiza Airport to the hotel.

Rooms

Studios that can accommodate up to three guests and Apartments for up to four guests are available at this hotel. Each guest unit features a telephone, satellite TV, hairdryer, bathroom, air conditioning, balcony and a kitchenette.

Food & Drink

Guests can visit the main buffet restaurant for breakfast, lunch and dinner. There is also a snack bar for light bites and refreshments.

All-inclusive Schedule (subject to change):

Breakfast: 08:00-11:00
Lunch: 13:00-14:00
Dinner: 19:00-21:00
Snacks: 11:00-13:00 and 15:00-19:00
Drinks: 10:30-23:00

## Please note that the All-inclusive programme is for a minimum stay of five nights in the summer season. ##

Sports & Entertainment

The hotel offers a large swimming pool and a children’s pool, as well as sun umbrellas and loungers on the sun terrace. Wellness services include massages and treatments, and FREE Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. Children will love the mini club (ages 4-11), and there is also an entertainment programme for adults that runs through the day and into the evening. Activities and sports available nearby include a water park, snorkelling, scuba diving, cycling, hiking and tennis.
