Book this hotel before 20.11.2017 for travel between 25.11.17 - 22.12.17, 06.01.2018 – 30.06.2018 and 27.08.2018 – 31.10.2018. Offer applicable to all room types on any board basis. Prices displayed include discount.

Overview

This family-friendly resort is ideal for sun-seekers and fun-seekers of all ages, and has a great choice of facilities, including a splashtastic children's aqua park.

How far is the nearest beach?

The hotel is approximately 2km from the beach.

Distance from Airport

The hotel is located 28km from Arrecife Airport.

Rooms

The THB Tropical Island offers 414 standard and premium apartments, each with an en-suite bathroom, living area, separate bedroom and kitchenette. Further in-room amenities include air conditioning (May-October), tea/coffee making facilities, a hairdryer and a balcony or terrace.

Food & Drink

There’s an Italian restaurant and a Tapas restaurant on-site, along with two relaxing pool bars (one exclusive to Premium Apartment guests), and a traditional Irish pub.

Sports & Entertainment

The hotel features two huge swimming pools, (one exclusive to Premium apartment guests) - along with two outdoor Jacuzzis, two tennis courts, and two multi-sports courts. For little ones, there's a choice of children's pools, a splash park and a playground - and for grown-ups, there's a spa area including a Jacuzzi, steam room, sauna and a range of treatments. Further activities and facilities include table tennis, basketball, volleyball, handball and a gym, plus a fantastic seasonal entertainment programme. Please note that some services may incur a local charge.

Accessibility

This hotel offers eight adapted accommodation units with wheel-in showers.

** Please contact our dedicated sales team prior to booking to discuss your requirements and specify any necessary facilities or equipment. Adapted rooms are available only upon request. **
