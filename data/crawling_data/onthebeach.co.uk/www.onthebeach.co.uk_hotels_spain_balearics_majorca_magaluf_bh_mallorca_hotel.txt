Overview

BH Mallorca Hotel combines a party atmosphere with a holiday resort. Included in the room rate is entrance to an 18+ waterpark with slides and a wave pool, entrance to Island Beach’s ultimate party zone and access to daytime DJ sets and poolside parties. Guests will also get tickets to Open Air Gigs every Tuesday and Thursday, with David Guetta, Martin Garrix, Rudimental, Skrillex, Duke Dumont and more on the line-up (15/06/2017 – 24/08/2017).

## Adults Only 18+. 17 year olds may stay subject to additional terms and conditions. Please contact a sales agent for further information.##

How far is the nearest beach?

This hotel is about 200m from the beach.

Distance from Airport

It is about 29km from Majorca Airport to the hotel.

Rooms

Each of BH Mallorca Hotel’s 656 Suites has Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a balcony with one of five views, twin beds, two sofa beds, a bathroom with a shower, a TV with USB ports, two full-length mirrors, a hairdryer, a safe (charges apply), a telephone and magnetic door keys. Entrance to the waterpark, Island Beach Club and gigs is included in the room rate.

Accessibility

Ground floor wheelchair adapted rooms with wider door frames, a walk-in shower and low light switches, as well as all the amenities listed above, are available on request.

Food & Drink

From 09:00 – 21:00, All-inclusive guests receive complimentary food from the buffet restaurant and drinks from the pool bar. Drinks include soft drinks, and local wines, beers and spirits.

Sports & Entertainment

The water park and Beach Club are open from 11:00 – 20:00. The hotel also features a Live Gig Arena with parties until late, an on-site gym, world-class DJs and a festival atmosphere.

There’s also a wave pool which comes alive with great sounds from our resident DJ and plenty of sun beds to chill out on; the perfect place to have fun while catching some rays.

## Please note that there is a 30.00 euro per person deposit payable on arrival and refundable on check out. ##
