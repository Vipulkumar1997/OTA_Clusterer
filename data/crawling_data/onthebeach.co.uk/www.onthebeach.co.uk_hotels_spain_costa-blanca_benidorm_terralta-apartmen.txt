Overview

The Terralta Apartments are situated just about a kilometre from the seafront, close to shops, restaurants and resort amenities. The property offers a high standard of modern accommodation. Its location in a fairly quiet residential area makes it an ideal choice if you're looking for a relaxing holiday away from the crowds, but within easy reach of the beach and all of Benidormâ€™s entertainment.

## For bookings of three rooms or more, please contact our sales team prior to booking to discuss your requirements, as reservations of this size are only available upon request. Stag and hen parties are not permitted at the hotel. ##

How far is the nearest beach?

The nearest beach is located 1km from the hotel.

Distance from Airport

It is 56km from Alicante Airport to the hotel.

Rooms

All Terralta Apartments are spacious, clean, and very well equipped with a lounge with a sofa bed. They also feature a satellite TV, internet access, air conditioning, private terrace or balcony and a kitchenette with a microwave, fridge, electric hob, kettle and toaster. Baby cots are available at an extra charge. Larger apartments have a second double bedroom and can sleep up to six guests.

Food & Drink

The main bar offers a very good range of drinks, both national and imported, as well as a daily menu and a varied Ã  la carte menu. During the summer months (June-September) there is also a pool bar offering alcoholic drinks, refreshing soft drinks, ice cream and snacks.

Sports & Entertainment

There are plenty of things to do at Terralta Apartments for both adults and children, including a swimming pool, separate childrenâ€™s pool, childrenâ€™s playground, kids club and regular entertainment for all ages. A fitness suite with a steam bath and Jacuzzi is available at a charge. Benidorm is a short walk or ride away and has a countless shops, bars, restaurants and entertainment options, should you fancy getting out of the hotel for a bit.

Accessibility

Overall the hotel and resort offers good access, though some assistance may be required for some of the ramps and streets to the town centre. The swimming pool features a sloped entrance. Adapted bathrooms feature sliding doors, non-slip tiles and a pull-down shower seat with grab rails at the shower, bath and toilet.
