Overview

The Sunset Beach Club offers fantastic summer holidays in Spain to suit the whole family, whatever the time of year. The hotel’s professional, friendly staff will introduce guests to the fantastic facilities, high-quality services and great entertainment available at the hotel and along the vibrant Costa del Sol.

How far is the nearest beach?

This hotel is located on the beachfront at Benalmadena Costa.

Distance from Airport

The hotel is located 26km from Malaga Airport.

Rooms

The Sunset Beach Club offers One Bedroom Apartments, One Bedroom Apartments with Sea Views, Deluxe Junior Suites and rooms tailored to honeymooners or apartments for groups of five to six guests. Each accommodation type provides a comfortable base for both short and long stays, and features a lounge with a sofa bed, a dining area, a fully-equipped kitchen, a bathroom and a furnished balcony with Mediterranean or mountain views. All apartments also include air conditioning, a telephone, a hire safe and a satellite TV.

Food & Drink

The Oasis Restaurant & Terrace is the main dining venue at the hotel. It serves breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as hot and cold drinks all day. A take-away service is available during mealtimes. Live evening entertainment continues here throughout the year and there is a daily happy hour in the Panorama Bar. Other bars include the Luna Beach Club, the Tikki Pool Bar, the Salitos Bar and The Moonlight Bar. Some bars are open during high season only.

Sports & Entertainment

Within the complex, sporting and leisure activities include two large outdoor swimming pools set within landscaped terraces, a children’s paddling pool, a children’s play area, a children’s adventure climbing area, health and beauty services, an archery and pistol range, table tennis, billiards, a colourful kids club, mini bowling lanes and a games room. The hotel also features a 24-hour reception and FREE Wi-Fi in public areas. At the beach, guests will find the Padi Scuba Centre, a variety of water sports and plenty of opportunities for relaxation.
