Read more about this Hotel



Overview

Located in the north of Costa Teguise, this super-friendly property has family fun at its heart. Little ones are sure to give the splash park a huge thumbs up - and thanks to its superb recent refurbishment, grown-ups can relax in truly stylish surroundings.

How far is the nearest beach?

The hotel is approximately 900m from the beach.

Distance from Airport

The hotel is 15km from Arrecife Airport.

Rooms

The property’s One Bedroom Apartments sleep up to two adults and two children, and lead on to a roomy private balcony or terrace. An en-suite bathroom, kitchenette and air conditioning are also included, along with a satellite TV, safe, hairdryer and free Wi-Fi. Plus, for larger families, there’s a small number of Two Bedroom Apartments available, each of which sleep up to four adults and two children.

Food & Drink

The main buffet restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner, and offers regular themed evenings showcasing delicious world cuisines. For daytime snacks and drinks, there’s also a handy pool bar, together with an indoor bar perfect for an after-dinner tipple.

All Inclusive Food & Drink

The hotel's All Inclusive programme includes breakfast (07:00-10:00), lunch (13:00-15:00) and dinner (18:00-21:00), all served buffet style in the main restaurant. A choice of snacks are also offered from 10:00-23:00 (outside of lunch and dinner service hours) - along with a wide range of locally-produced alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks from 10:00-23:30. Please note that premium/imported drink brands and bottled water are not included in this service.

Sports & Entertainment

At the centre of the complex, you’ll find a huge free-form pool complete with islands and bridges, accompanied by an colourful splash park for children. There’s also a great entertainment programme offering daytime activities and evening shows – including a hugely popular mini disco for your little dancers.
