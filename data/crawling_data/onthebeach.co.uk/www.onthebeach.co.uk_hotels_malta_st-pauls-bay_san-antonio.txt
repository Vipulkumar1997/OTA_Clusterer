Overview

The db San Antonio Hotel + Spa is a family-friendly hotel located in the north of Malta, in the popular resort of Qawra/St Paul’s Bay. Its architecture has a typically Mediterranean style, and it is one of the most impressive hotels in Malta with over 500 rooms and suites, and a variety of restaurants, bars, swimming pools and fitness and spa facilities.

How far is the nearest beach?

The hotel is located approximately 100m from the beach.

Distance from Airport

The hotel is located 20km from Malta Airport.

Rooms

The Hotel offers Twin rooms, Double Rooms, Family rooms and Suites. All rooms have a balcony with the option of a sea view, pool view or inland view. All rooms are equipped with walk-in showers or bathtub facilities, a hairdryer, a ceiling fan, central heating and air conditioning, FREE Wi-Fi access, a direct dial telephone, a mini bar, complimentary tea and coffee making facilities, a complimentary safety deposit box, a 32” LCD flat screen satellite TV, a wake-up call service, an iron and ironing board (upon request), a smoke detection system and an outlet adaptor with voltage 220/240.

Food & Drink

Guéliz Restaurant is the main hotel restaurant, serving breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Tagine Restaurant offers an All-inclusive buffet of Moroccan and Indian food. A variety of meats and seafood dishes are served with a blend of spices and herbs. It requires a booking to be made in-house.

D-Bistro Restaurant is the dine-out venue offering an à la carte menu with a selection of grills, pasta and pizza dishes. Healthy options and kids menus are also available here.

Yushan Restaurant is the All-inclusive buffet venue, offering an array of Chinese and Asian food on the top floor of the hotel, with breath-taking views of St. Paul's Bay. All main dishes are prepared and served directly by our Asian Chefs from the live cooking stations.

Q-Zins Restaurant offers All-inclusive themed dinners on the top floor of the db San Antonio Hotel + Spa. The buffet dinner is served directly from our chefs' live cooking stations with the following cuisines: Italian, Mexican and American. Open in summer only, when weather permits.

KosyBar is the hotel’s main bar serving a variety of cocktails, spirits and coffees. An evening entertainment programme is available every night throughout the year. Alternatively, Café Maroc is open during the day serving teas and coffees, alcoholic drinks and a selection of snacks such as sandwiches, wraps and pasta. Guests are advised to book in advance to ensure a table at the restaurants.

Sports & Entertainment

The hotel is close to a small sandy beach, and the grounds feature two interconnecting outdoor swimming pools and a kid’s pool which can be accessed by all hotel’s guests throughout the summer months.

The hotel also offers wonderful facilities for children in the summer and winter months, including a pirate boat with slides, two shallow children’s pools with sun loungers for parents, a colourful indoor space for games and activities and a fully-equipped interactive room for children and adults with PS4s, Nintendo Wii, tablets and smart TVs.

Guests can also enjoy a variety of outdoor sport facilities which include a fitness centre, beach volleyball and mini golf. An extensive animation programme in English, French, German and Italian language is available throughout the year. A kids club (ages 4 – 11) is open daily with various activities organised by the caring animation staff. There is also Le Marquis Spa with a heated indoor pool, five treatment rooms, Hammam, a Jacuzzi, saunas, a steam bath and a relaxation area.

Accessibility

This hotel offers wheel-in showers and grab rails.

** Please contact our dedicated sales team prior to booking to discuss your requirements and specify any necessary facilities or equipment. Adapted rooms are available only upon request. **
