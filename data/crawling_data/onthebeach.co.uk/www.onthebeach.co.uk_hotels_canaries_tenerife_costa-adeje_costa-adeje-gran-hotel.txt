Overview

If you're looking for something special for your holiday to Tenerife, the five-star Costa Adeje Gran Hotel could be the perfect choice. It's an impressive hotel in the resort of Costa Adeje, with brilliant views over the Playa del Duque beach. The area offers relaxation, nightlife and a lovely selection of shops and restaurants. The hotel itself is a deluxe property, surrounded by countryside, with amazing views of the Atlantic Ocean. This stylish hotel is a great match for couples, families and friends who are looking for a tranquil holiday with beautiful beaches and lots of things to see and do.

How far is the nearest beach?

The beach is about 500m from the hotel.

Distance from Airport

Tenerife South Airport is located approximately 34km from the hotel.

Rooms

Double Rooms sleep three, and are air conditioned and decorated in warm colours. Facilities include queen or twin beds, an additional sofa bed, a satellite TV, a telephone, a hairdryer, piped music, a safety deposit box (at extra charge), a minibar (at extra charge), a private bathroom and a private balcony or terrace with outstanding views. Interconnecting rooms are available on request.

Senior Suites have the same facilities as the double rooms, with the added luxury of a hydro massage bath in the bathroom, a separate living room with an extra TV and a balcony. If you choose a suite, you're also able to dine at the exclusive La Laja restaurant.

Food & Drink

Foodies will love the choice of eateries and bars at Costa Adeje Gran Hotel. La Finca offers three daily buffet meals, with vegetarian, vegan and celiac-friendly menus available, and an à la carte restaurant called La Laja specialises in Canarian fine dining and international dishes. There is also the Snack Bar by the pool, the Bar Hall, La Zumeria and Bar Atico that serve both alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks from scenic locations throughout the resort. If you like your food and drink, you won’t be disappointed with the choice and quality.

All-inclusive Schedule (subject to change):

Breakfast: 07:30 – 10:30
Lunch: 13:30 – 15:30
Dinner: 18:30 – 21:30
Snacks: 10:00 – 18:30

Sports & Entertainment

Do everything or do nothing at all – it's up to you, but you'll never be short of options at Costa Adeje Gran Hotel. There are two swimming pools (one heated in winter) with spacious sun terraces and sun loungers, and an additional rooftop swimming pool with stunning views out over the Atlantic. Sports and spa facilities include a tennis court, a squash court, a gymnasium, a day spa, a wellness area, two saunas, a Turkish bath, a Jacuzzi, a kids club and a nightly mini disco for children. Nearby is the popular Adeje Golf Course which is great for golf enthusiasts.

The Costa Adeje Gran Hotel offers all the facilities and services you'd expect from a five-star hotel: 24 hour reception, room service, laundry service, currency exchange, internet access, all day entertainment, quiet public areas, reading room, bridge room and TV room.

Accessibility

Ramps are available throughout the hotel, offering easy access to restaurants, pool areas, reception and rooms.
