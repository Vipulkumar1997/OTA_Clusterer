Overview

H10 Lanzarote Princess is situated in the traditional fishing town of Playa Blanca on the south coast of Lanzarote. This four-star hotel has excellent facilities and a great location, with public transport links and diverse shopping and entertainment venues only 500m away. A kids club, an outdoor swimming pool and an entertainment programme makes this hotel the ideal choice for the whole family as well as for couples or for groups of friends.

How far is the nearest beach?

The hotel is located just 250m away from the sea, and the sandy beach is separated from the hotel by a pleasant street.

Distance from Airport

It is about 30km from Lanzarote Airport to the hotel.

Rooms

The resort offers a total of 407 rooms. The spacious accommodations include an en-suite bathroom with a hairdryer and a balcony or terrace. Additional furnishings include a direct dial telephone, a satellite TV, a minibar (local charge) and air conditioning.

Food & Drink

Guests can eat at the Yaiza Restaurant, where buffet-style meals, show cooking and boulangerie can be found. There is also the choice of Dolce Vita, an Italian à la carte restaurant, La Choza Snack Bar or Mike’s Coffee for hot drinks and homemade pastries. Bars include Coco Loco, the in-pool drinks venue, the Vintage Bar that has live music in the evening and Papagayo, the theatre bar with entertainment at night.

All-inclusive Information:

Meals, snacks, local drinks, selected international drinks and cocktails are included. One free dinner at the Dolce Vita Italian restaurant is available for guests staying longer than seven nights (subject to availability).

Sports & Entertainment

In the spacious garden area guests will find an outdoor swimming pool, which is heated in winter. Sun loungers and parasols are also available for use, and sports enthusiasts may choose from a wide range of options in the daily entertainment programme such as water aerobics, tennis, table tennis, archery, snooker, bocce and mini golf. The complex features a gym that is perfect for fitness fans, as well as aerobics classes. For younger guests, there is a kids club (ages 4 – 12), a children’s pool and a playground.

Additional facilities include a foyer with a 24-hour reception desk, lifts, a currency exchange, a hairdresser's salon, a boutique, a TV room, a conference room, a nightclub and a bar. FREE Wi-Fi is available in most public spaces.

Accessibility

** Please contact our dedicated sales team prior to booking to discuss your requirements and specify any necessary facilities or equipment. Adapted rooms are available only upon request. **
