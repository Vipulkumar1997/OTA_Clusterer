Overview

BelleVue Club is an established resort hotel in Alcudia, which is suitable for all ages. The hotel enjoys a fantastic location close to Alcudia's long, sandy beach and all the entertainment venues that the area has to offer. This is a great value for money property which offers large spacious rooms and a host of on-site facilities that are ideal for families.

How far is the nearest beach?

This hotel is located 800m from the beach.

Distance from Airport

The airport is situated 65Km from the hotel.

Rooms

Guests can choose from Studios (up to three people), One Room Apartments (up to four people) or Two Room Apartments (up to six people). All apartments are spacious and comfortably furnished. They each have a fully-equipped kitchen with a fridge, a bathroom, a private balcony, a satellite TV, a telephone and a safety deposit box (extra charge).

Food & Drink

Eating and drinking opportunities at Bellevue Club are varied and delicious. There is a cafeteria serving international food, two buffet restaurants with local and international cuisine and a bar and café. For Italian cuisine the Dolce Vita pizzeria is the place to go, and a Mediterranean à la carte restaurant, a brasserie restaurant and an ice-cream parlour are also available. Guests can also discover the Morocco Bowling Bar, The Tavern Irish pub, The Crown English bar, eight pool bars serving snacks and refreshments and a Show Garden with entertainment and shows every night.

Sports & Entertainment

The Bellevue Club is a trusted family resort with excellent amenities for all the family, including swimming pools, mini golf and tennis. A three-minute boat trip takes you from the hotel to the beach. On-site entertainment is complemented by the extensive range of activities in the local area, such as restaurants, bars, go-karting, water parks, sailing and an area of outstanding natural beauty.
