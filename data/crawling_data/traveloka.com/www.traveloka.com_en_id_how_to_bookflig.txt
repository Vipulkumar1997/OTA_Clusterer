How to Book Flights with Traveloka

Complete your booking in 5 minutes or less!


How to Book Flights
How to Book Hotels

How to Pay








Search Your Flight

Start your search by filling in the flight details.









Choose and Book Flight

Airline name, flight schedule, and ticket price will be shown on the search results page. Choose and book the best flight for you.















Fill In Contact Information and Passenger Details

After choosing your flight, fill in the contact information and passenger details.
You can add travel insurance to your booking as well.










Complete Your Payment

From the many payment methods available, choose one that best suits you.















Receive Your E-ticket

Within 60 minutes
after your payment is confirmed, we will send your e-ticket to your email.




Should you need further assistance, or if you haven't received your e-ticket/hotel voucher after 60 minutes, please contact our 24-hour Customer Service.
