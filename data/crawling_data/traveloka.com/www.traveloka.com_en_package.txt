How to book Flight + Hotel Packages with Traveloka?













Search package

Start your search by filling in your destination, travel dates and your duration of stay.










Select package

On search results page, select the package you prefer. Click Change Room or Change Flight to customize your selections.










Book package

See trip summary and click Select Package to continue. Fill in contact information and traveler details. Make sure all details are correct, then proceed with payment.









Proceed with payment

Select your preferred payment method. Once we have received your payment, your trip voucher will be sent to your email within 60 minutes.
