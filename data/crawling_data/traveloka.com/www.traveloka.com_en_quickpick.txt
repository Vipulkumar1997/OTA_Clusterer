Passenger Quick Pick

Fill in passenger details now, pick passengers quickly later. Passenger Quick Pick saves you the hassle of entering the same passenger details every time you book.

Check It Out!



Benefits of Passenger Quick Pick
How to Use Passenger Quick Pick






Faster booking

Save your time by having all passenger details filled out in a single click!






Easier future bookings

By adding your details into Passenger Quick Pick, you no longer need to memorize your details or input them manually during booking.












1. Log in

To use Passenger Quick Pick,
log in
to your Traveloka account.






2. Select Passenger Quick Pick

Go to
My Account
and select Passenger Quick Pick.












3. Add passenger

Click
Add Passenger
and enter passenger details. You can add your travel companions' details too! Don't worry, Passenger Quick Pick can store up to 20 names.






4. Add more people to your Passengers Quick Pick

You may also fill in your and your travel companions’ details directly in the booking form and have them added automatically to Passenger Quick Pick for your booking convenience next time.












5. Choose from the list of passengers

Simply pick the traveling passengers from the provided list of passengers, and have their details added automatically in the booking form.
