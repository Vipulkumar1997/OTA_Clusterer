Best Day Travel, Mexico

. . . Excellence in Personalized Service


Best  Day Travel (Viajes Beda, S.A. de C.V.) is a 100% Mexican  enterprise established in Cancun since 1984, with renowned prestige in the  tourist industry all over the country, perfecting day after day the PERSONALIZED SERVICE concept that has  distinguished us since the very beginning.
Honoring our mission statement  that reads:
'
To become  the best in the creation, commercialization and operation of travel services'
,  we are glad to express to you the satisfaction experienced throughout our more than 19  long and successful years of providing online services; we have initiated and cultivated excellent  relationships with some of the most famous wholesale travel service agencies  all over the world, including countries like the USA, Canada, Sweden, Norway,  Iceland, France, Brazil, Chile, Argentina, Peru, Uruguay, and Mexico, among  many other countries in America, Europe and Asia.
Although considered a top-class agency from the very  beginning, since 1996 Best Day Travel has used the most advance electronic  devices to book tours and activities. Our very useful
"
touch screen panel stands
"
that  include videos of all the activities and tours offered in Cancun and the  Riviera Maya (all of them displayed in up to four different languages) are a  perfect example of these great technological advances. Our clients are able to  place the reservations in the most advanced, interactive and simplified way  possible thanks to this and other fabulous technological solutions.
We own a vast  fleet of comfortable modern vans dedicated to transporting our clients  to and from any point within the city, as well as conducting group tours and  ground transportation services in our beautiful first-class busses to any  important tourist spots within Cancun and the  surrounding areas.
Best Day Travel relies on the assistance of more

than 350 professionals  distributed all over Cancun and the Riviera Maya. They are all impeccably uniformed,  constantly being trained, and have extensive experience in the tourism field,  something that will surely be felt through their kind treatment and when  suggesting and orienting anyone, with the sole purpose of helping you live the  most wonderful travel experience you've ever had.
Headquartered in the hotel zone in Cancun,  Best Day Travel also expanded its offices to the heart of Playa del Carmen,  where exclusive attention to all the Riviera Maya is offered on a daily basis
,
thus assuring excellent service and  the customer's satisfaction. Many are  the reasons why those on

our long  list of clients keep choosing Best Day Travel as their agent of choice but it  mainly is due to our vast array of products with the best prices on the market  as well as the unsurpassed personalized services we offer.
There are  definitively many reasons why our long list of clients might choose Best Day  Travel as their ideal company to place their reservations, but it mainly is to  the vast array of products offered, with the best prices on the market, as well  as the unequalled personalized service.
