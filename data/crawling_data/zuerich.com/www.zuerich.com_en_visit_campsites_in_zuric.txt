Campsites in Zurich and Around Lake Zurich





On these campsites around Lake Zurich, campers can enjoy peaceful nights in a tent or mobile home close to the water’s edge.


Whether they are on Zurich’s city limits, the legendary
Lützelau Island
, or the banks of Lake Walen, these campsites in Zurich and the rural area around Lake Zurich attract adventurers and nature-lovers alike with their variety of tent and mobile home pitches. Many of them have a direct lakeside location, inviting campers to take a refreshing dip after an exciting day full of discovery.
