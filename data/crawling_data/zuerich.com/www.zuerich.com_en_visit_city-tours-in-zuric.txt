Popular City Tours in Zurich





The easiest and best way to get to know Zurich is to take part in one of the many interesting walks or tours of the city.


The easiest and best way to get to know Zurich is to take part in one of the many interesting walks or tours of the city. Whether on foot, with a Segway or with a bicycle rickshaw – the experienced guides know Zurich like the back of their hand. They will relate stories about the most famous sights and take guests to hidden-away narrow, winding streets.

Private tours for groups or individuals, as well as public guided tours, can be booked at the Tourist Information at Zurich Main Train Station.

What’s more, holders of a
Zürich Card
benefit from 50% discount on various public city walks and tours.

The city tour offers can be booked direct at Zürich Tourism.
