Events – Exhibitions, Concerts, Stages, Partys and Much More




The city on the River Limmat pulsates all year round. The highlights of Zurich’s event calendar include the Street Parade, the Zurich Festival, open-air concerts and cinemas in summer, the Weltklasse Zurich (track & field meeting) and New Year’s Eve Magic organized by Zurich’s hoteliers.
