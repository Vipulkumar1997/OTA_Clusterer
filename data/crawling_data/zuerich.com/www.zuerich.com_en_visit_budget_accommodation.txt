Budget Accommodation in Zurich

In Zurich, there are a number of hostels, guesthouses and B&Bs that are suitable for visitors on a tight budget.












Home
Budget Accommodation


Share this















Stay in Zurich for Little Money





Guesthouses, Youth Hostels, Budget Hotels and Camping sites


Whether in café-guesthouses in the heart of the city’s trendy quarters, at a campsite directly by the lake, or in a budget hotel – in Zurich, you can also stay the night without having to spend much money.












Cheap Alternative




Dormitory, double room or single room – the youth hostel in Zurich offers a good, cheap alternative to a hotel.





More





















Youth Hostel Zurich












Typical Zurich




The popular café-bar in Zurich’s Wipkingen quarter has five attractive and favorably priced guestrooms with a shared bathroom.





More





















Kafischnaps












In the Trendy Quarter




The family café in Zurich’s vibrant Kreis 4 quarter offers 18 comfortable rooms with free Wi-Fi. There is one shared bathroom to every three rooms.





More





















Pension für Dich












Great Breakfast




The 10 rooms at this guesthouse are all lovingly furnished in an individual style, equipped with Wi-Fi, and feature a shared bathroom on each floor.





More





















Zum Guten Glück












Budget Hotel




Just half-an-hour from downtown Zurich, the Hotel Ibis Zürich-Adliswil offers low-price overnight stays and a modern grill restaurant.





More





















Ibis Zürich-Adliswil












Directly by the Airport




This family hotel near to Zurich Airport features comfortable rooms, as well as a restaurant with a steak room and a cozy ambiance.





More





















Hotel Welcome Inn












In Zürich-Nord




This hotel is within walking distance of the Messe Zürich and the Hallenstadion, and provides guests with well-priced and comfortable rooms.





More





















Ibis Messe Airport












Directly by the Lake




A campsite by the lake for tents and camper vans. Guests can also rent a hanging tent, a camp bed in a safari tent, or a cottage for 4 persons.





More





















Camping Fischers Fritz












In the entertainment district




Awaiting guests at the easy Hotel are simple, comfortable rooms with a private bathroom. There are numerous bars, clubs and restaurants close by.





More





















easy Hotel Zürich












Family friendly




This hostel is very popular with families and offers modern rooms in a rural area of Jona.





More





















Youth Hostel Rapperswil-Jona












On the Island




Those who are not reliant on a central location in the city and enjoy being outdoors will find a camping paradise on the tiny island of Lützelau.





More























Camping Insel Lützelau












Right by the Lake




This hostel is located in a beautiful park setting right by the lake, only half an hour from Zurich.
