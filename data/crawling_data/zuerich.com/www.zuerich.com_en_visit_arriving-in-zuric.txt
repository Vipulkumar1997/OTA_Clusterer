Arriving in Zurich

Zurich is located in the center of Switzerland and at the heart of Europe.  Whether you are coming from Madrid or Stockholm, most European capitals are reached in less than 2.5 hours from Zurich by plane. With its Main Railway Station, Zurich also has the largest railway station in Switzerland  – with direct connections to other Swiss cities and the surrounding countries. Find out more about arriving in Zurich by bus, train, car or plane.
