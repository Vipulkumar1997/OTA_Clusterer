Knights and princesses





Little princesses, brave safari adventurers and inquisitive investigators will experience days in Zurich they will never forget. Choose a topic above that is of the most interest to your child and discover great ideas for excursions the entire family will enjoy.












Those in the know purchase the best toys and nicest children’s clothing at the traditional stores Franz Carl Weber and Tigerfink.








Countless cafes and restaurants welcome children; for example, Kafi für Dich with its painting wall just for the little ones.

meters (23ft): that is the length of the Allosaurus that can be viewed at Aathal Dinosaur Museum

300

years of childhood can be experienced at the Toy Museum in Zurich





Experiment, explore, make new discoveries: no matter whether in the great outdoors or at the natural science museum – your little discoverers can explore the Zurich region and discover the mysteries of the world.










The Technorama exhibit is an exciting demonstration of physical laws and chemical reactions.
















Fossils, skeletons and mummies offer an impressive account of the history of humans and animals at the Kulturama.
















The environment in focus: at the "Umwelt Aarena Spreitenbach" children learn about topics such as sustainability, energy and technology through playful activities and exciting experiments.























Princesses and knights






xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?














853

BC is when Laufen Castle first featured in a document and is thus among the oldest in the region






xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?









1

The Kyburg is the first castle museum to be opened in Switzerland






xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?









1315

1315 is when the Battle of Morgarten took place. Nowadays a theme trail leads through the site





Your little medieval enthusiasts can visit the magnificent castles in Zurich and the surrounding region, explore deserted fortress ruins and marvel at the exciting stories of noble kings and brave knights.










The impressive Kyburg Castle tells the stories of yesteryear: armor, halberds and a medieval kitchen whisks visitors back to the Middle Ages.
















During the guided tour of the castle grounds of the Stein ruin, children can listen to the exciting story of King Albrecht von Habsburg.
















Children and their parents can explore a trail rich in variety and learn about the history of the Battle of Morgarten.























Safari travelers






xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?















386

Animal species from six continents live at Zurich Zoo






xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?












2

Obstacle courses at the Atzmännig rope park are specially designed for children






xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?









6

kilometers (4 miles) is the approximate length of the Planet Trail on the Uetliberg





Giraffes, elephants and other exotic giants await brave safari adventurers in Zurich and the Lake Zurich region. Native wildlife, gorgeous plants and exciting excursion ideas transform leisure time in Zurich into a true safari.










Elephants, lions and giant tortoises – children encounter their favorite animals at the Zurich Zoo.
















Llama trekking through Zurich’s wine country gives children and adults an opportunity to experience animals up close and personal.
















Animal lovers can observe past and present native wild animals in their natural habitat at the Goldau Nature Park.




























Public transportation in Zurich is child-friendly: SBB trains have sections for children, and the newer trams are easy to board and exit even with strollers and buggies.








Parks and playgrounds in the heart of the city offer a great place for picnics, playing ball, swings and slides.
