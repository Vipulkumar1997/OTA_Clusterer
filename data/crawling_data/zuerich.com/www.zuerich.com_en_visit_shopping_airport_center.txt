Airport Center at Zurich Airport


The stores and restaurants in the public areas at Zurich Airport are open every day from early in the morning to late at night.



Description

Prices

Opening Hours

Video & 360°

Map




With 80 shops, bars and restaurants, there's no time to get bored at Zurich Airport. Vicotria's Secret, H&M, Grieder, Herren Globus, Esprit, Navyboot, Ochsner Shoes... the Airport Center houses a huge selection of brand names under one roof. Here there is something for everyone, no matter how big your purse or how extravagant your taste. And after your shopping spree, the upperdeck restaurant is the perfect place to relax over an invigorating coffee or refreshing cocktail. Here, you can immerse yourself in the aeronautical ambience and let your travel dreams take flight. The Airport Center in the public zone of Zurich Airport is open 365 days a year from early in the morning until late at night.
