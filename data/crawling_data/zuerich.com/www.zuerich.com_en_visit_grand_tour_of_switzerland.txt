Grand Tour of Switzerland





The Grand Tour of Switzerland leads past cultural and scenic gems. On the 1,000 mile (1,600km) long route through Switzerland, tourists and locals alike experience many unforgettable moments. From palm-lined lakeshores to sparkling glaciers, from medieval villages to buzzing cities – the Grand Tour of Switzerland packs in an incredible number of sights. The concentration of attractions is unrivalled worldwide.
