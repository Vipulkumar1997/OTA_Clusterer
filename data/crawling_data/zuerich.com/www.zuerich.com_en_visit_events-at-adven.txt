Events in Zurich During Christmas Season





Spectacular events create a magical Advent mood in Zurich.


The Lichterschwimmen floating candles event has delighted spectators for over half a century, while the Circus Conelli on the Bauschänzli and the official switching on of the Christmas lights along the Bahnhofstrasse are just two of a whole host of highlights during the Advent period.

More christmas events
























November 10 – December 2017


Candle Making








Candle making has been among Zurich's Advent traditions since 1969...








More

























November 16 – December 31, 2017


Illuminarium








From November 16 to December 31, 2017, the National Museum is transformed into a winter wonderland of lights, illumination, illusion and cuisine.








More

























November 17 – December 31, 2017


Circus Conelli








Conelli’s Christmas Circus has been a firm part of the festive season in Zurich since 1992.








More


























November 17, 2017 – January 2, 2018




Salto Natale









The circus with a difference sweetens the cold winter months with artistry and music.








More

















November 23, 2017


Christmas lighting & Night Shopping








The switch-on of the Christmas lighting in Zurich is celebrated with a night shopping event.








More



























November 23 – December 23, 2017


The Singing Christmas Tree








This Christmas market enchants with its unique daily concerts.








More

























November 23 – December 23, 2017


Christmas Markets








Allow yourself to be captivated by the enchanting atmosphere at Zurich’s Christmas markets.








More



























November 24 – December 24, 2017


Stories of the Old Town – Christmas Special








This guided walking tour of the Old Town is a journey of discovery for both visitors and locals. At Advent, the Christmas lights along the Bahnhofstrasse and the various Christmas markets give rise to a magical ambiance in Zurich.








More

















November 26, 2017


Christmas procession








Santa Claus and his «Schmutzli» assistants hand out delicious treats during the parade along Bahnhofstrasse.








More



























November 28 – December 23, 2018


Märlitram








For children, the best way to enjoy Advent is on a trip in the "Märlitram". Angels read their young passengers Christmas stories while the festively decorated streetcar trundles through the city...








More

















November 29, 2017


Badener Liechterwecke








At the “Liechterwecke” ceremony, the contemplative light installation is switched on in the streets of Baden.








More

























1 Dec – 23 Dec, 2017


Musical Advent Calendar








During the Advent period, daily short concerts are held in the foyer of the Opera House – admission is free.








More


























December 3, 2017




“Samichlausschwimmen”









Not for the fainthearted – the “Samichlausschwimmen” swimming event takes place in Zurich in winter.








More


























December 10, 2017




Silvesterlauf









This race always has a great atmosphere. Whether wearing fancy dress or regular sports gear, competitors of all ages run through the festively lit Old Town.








More


























December 21, 2017




Lichterschwimmen









The atmospheric spectacle of lights at Christmas time – with greetings from Zürich Tourism








More

























December 31, 2017


New Year’s Eve








In Zurich, the New Year is greeted in style with a spectacular fireworks display over Lake Zurich.








More
