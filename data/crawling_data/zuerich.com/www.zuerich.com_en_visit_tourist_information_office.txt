Tourist Information at Zurich Main Train Station





Anyone who has questions about Zurich, requires information about city tours and excursions, or would like some insider tips is at the right address at the Tourist Information office, located in the main concourse of Zurich Main Train Station.


The well qualified and knowledgeable staff at the Tourist Information office are pleased to provide visitors with competent advice.

“Very helpful! On arrival, we bought the Zürich Card here. The agents were very helpful and friendly. They spoke perfect English and many more languages. They took their time with each client.”


Our services

Besides valuable insider tips, visitors can find out all kinds of useful information about Zurich. The services offered by the Tourist Information office include:

City tours
in Zurich

Excursions
all over Switzerland

Tickets for public transportation: ZVV, Zürich Card, train tickets, Swiss Travel Pass

Hotel reservations

Various museum tickets

Traveler’s WIFI
sales outlet

Souvenirs

Starticket & Ticketino
ticket sales

Gift vouchers

MeinFernbus coach tickets

Various
brochures
and city maps

In addition to this wide range of services, at the Tourist Information office urban explorers can benefit from free WiFi and use the tablets available displaying the most important information and apps. Incidentally, if you want to be constantly up to date on what is going on in Zurich, be sure to sign up for the Zürich Tourism Newsletter. The Tourist Information office can also assist with individual wishes and requests, compile a tailor-made itinerary, and make group bookings and hotel reservations.
