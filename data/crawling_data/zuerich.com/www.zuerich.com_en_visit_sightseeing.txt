Sights and Attractions in Zurich and Region





Whether on a guided walk through the Old Town, on a visit to the Grossmünster church, or strolling under your own steam through the trendy Zürich-West quarter: Zurich’s sights can be explored in all kinds of different ways.


Those who would like to find out historical facts and insider information about the city should join one of the highly popular city walks led by an enthusiastic and knowledgeable tour guide. However, you can of course also explore the city under your own steam. The itineraries outlined below will help you find the most suitable route for your tour.

Whichever you choose, it is well worth purchasing a
Zürich Card
. With this city pass, visitors benefit from a reduction in the price of the popular “
Stroll Through the Old Town
” city walk, and also enjoy free travel by tram, bus, rail and cable car.
