Swissôtel Zurich is a luxury business hotel ideally located close to Zurich's business, cultural, shopping and entertainment areas and within easy reach of both Zurich Airport and Zurich's city centre. The 347 renovated luxury rooms and suites reflect a contemporary & sophisticated style, featuring panoramic views of Zurich and the superb Swiss Alps. All our rooms are non-smoking. You will enjoy a full range of leisure facilities such as the Spa & Sport and its treatments, cocktails at Le Muh Bar, fine dining at Le Muh privé, and creative food prepared with love to detail at Le Muh léger.
