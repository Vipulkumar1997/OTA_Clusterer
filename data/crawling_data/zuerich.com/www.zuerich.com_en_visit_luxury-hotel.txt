Luxury Hotels in Zurich





Whether by the lake, in the heart of the city, or high above Zurich’s rooftops – the city’s most prestigious hotels treat their guests to five-star luxury and outstanding service.


Whether in elegant fashion boutiques and jeweler’s stores on the Bahnhofstrasse or in exquisite gourmet temples – in Zurich, there are countless opportunities to treat yourself and your loved ones to something very special.

Anyone seeking to wallow in luxury and spend an unforgettable stay in Zurich is at the right address at the prestigious 5-star hotels in Switzerland’s largest city. Here, guests are well and truly pampered with breathtaking views over the lake, first-class
spa facilities
, and exquisite
fine dining
at the in-house restaurants.












Right by the Lake




This deluxe hotel lies directly next to Lake Zurich and features an idyllic garden and an award-winning restaurant, the Pavillon.





More





















Hotel Baur au Lac












High Above the Rooftops




The hotel, The Dolder Grand, is distinguished by its magnificent views, exclusive wellness oasis, two Michelin stars and impressive art collection.





More





















The Dolder Grand












Right by the Lake




The Eden flaunts its elegant facade directly on the promenade along the lake shore. It boasts a stylish restaurant and a spectacular roof terrace.





More





















Eden au Lac












Between Nature and City




This exceptional, luxury designer hotel lies at the foot of the Uetliberg. Guests are treated to two excellent restaurants and an elegant spa.





More





















Atlantis by Giardino












Middle Ages & Modern Times




Situated in one of the oldest streets in Zurich, the Widder Hotel impresses with classic design, creative cuisine and a fantastic cocktail bar.





More





















Widder Hotel












In the Business District




Located between the Bahnhofstrasse and Lake Zurich, this modern hotel features a health club, gourmet restaurant and bar with an open fireplace.





More





















Park Hyatt Zurich












By the Limmat




The Marriott Hotel rises up over the River Limmat in a very central location. Its restaurants serve exquisite Thai cuisine or Swiss specialties.





More





















Zurich Marriott Hotel












Tradition and Luxury




Visitors have enjoyed the hospitality of the Storchen for over 650 years. Both Paracelsus and Wagner have stayed at this hotel directly on the Limmat.





More





















Storchen Zürich












Boutique Hotel




Housed in a protected building, not far from Lake Zurich, this exclusive boutique hotel pampers guests with luxurious suites and delectable cuisine.





More





















Alden Hotel Splügenschloss












Directly on Paradeplatz




Enjoying a top location, the Savoy Hotel Baur en Ville attracts guests with 5-star luxury. Two restaurants serve haute cuisine and Tuscan specialties.





More





















Savoy Hotel Baur en Ville












In the Trendy Quarter




The luxurious Renaissance Tower in the urban Zurich-West quarter offers a superb panorama, a sauna and a first-class steak restaurant.





More





















Renaissance Zurich Tower Hotel












Extravagant Design




The lifestyle hotel surprises with its unusual design, and treats guests to Japanese and Italian cuisine, as well as a spa for relaxation.
