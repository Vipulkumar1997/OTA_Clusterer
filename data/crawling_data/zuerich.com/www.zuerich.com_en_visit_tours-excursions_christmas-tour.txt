Christmas Tour with the Night Watchman


Accompany the night watchman on his rounds during the Advent period and see Zurich from a new perspective.



Description

Prices

On this guided walk, you can find out who the “Wienachtsfrau“ was, where the best “Tirggel” (traditional honey-flavored cookies) are made, what the tasks of the lamp lighters were, and the relevance of the mysterious Muskazinen cookies.
