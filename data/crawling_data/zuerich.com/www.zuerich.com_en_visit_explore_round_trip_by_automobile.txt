The Grand Tour of Switzerland is a self-drive route for exploring Switzerland by automobile. Part of the Grand Tour leads right through the center of Zurich. This section impresses with its huge diversity of cultural, natural and culinary highlights.




























City Highlights



The Zurich section of the GToS leads from Einsiedeln via the picturesque town of Rapperswil to urban Zurich. From here, the route continues to cultural Winterthur and on to the Rhine Falls near Schaffhausen.









Zurich



More





The city by the water emanates a vacation feeling in the heart of the metropolis. With Lake Zurich and the Rivers Limmat and Sihl, visitors feel as if they are in the Venice of Switzerland.















Rapperswil



More





This small, picturesque town enchants with its countless rose bushes, impressive castle, and attractive Old Town with its own riviera.















Schaffhausen



More





This small medieval town impresses as a result of its picturesque and historical façades, the Munot fortification, and of course the imposing Rhine Falls.















Winterthur



More





The former industrial city is now a cultural hotspot: music festivals, comedy and theater, a photographic museum, and its own film festival are just some of the annual highlights.

Covering an area of 16,000 sq. m (172,000 sq. ft), Sechseläuten is a favorite meeting place among Zurich’s inhabitants. It is here that the traditional Sechseläuten spring festival and the city’s most beautiful Christmas markets take place. On 180 days a year, the square is given over completely to the public, while on the other 185 days it is the site of various events and festivals.

Cultural Highlights



Modern architecture, contemporary art and historical treasures: along the Grand Tour route, there are a host of cultural highlights to be discovered in Zurich and region.









Grossmünster



Sightseeing in and around Zurich





Zurich’s skyline would be unrecognizable without the distinctive twin towers of the Grossmünster. One of the towers, the Karlsturm, can even be climbed.















Rapperswil Castle



Sightseeing in and around Zurich





The castle – which has towered over the Town of Roses, Rapperswil, since the 12th century – can be seen from a long distance away.















Kunsthaus



Sightseeing in and around Zurich





At the Kunsthaus Zürich, art lovers can admire the city’s most important collection of modern art, as well as fascinating temporary exhibitions.















Fotomuseum Winterthur



Sightseeing in and around Zurich





The museum of photography in Winterthur hosts impressive exhibitions from international photographers all year round.























Familiy Activities



Relaxing on a boat trip on Lake Zurich, practicing your soccer skills at the FIFA Football Museum, or having an elephant ride at Knie’s Children‘s Zoo – in Zurich, fun and adventure for all the family is guaranteed.









Top Spots for Families

The official Photo Spots on the Grand Tour of Switzerland perfectly frame each and every snapshot. Einsiedeln Abbey is just one of the many motifs.








In Rapperswil-Jona, the photo frame captures a breathtaking view of the picturesque Old Town and Rapperswil Castle.





Photo Spots along the route of Zurich









Rhine Falls



Read more























Europe’s largest waterfall, the magnificent Rhine Falls, are just an hour from Zurich.









Culinary Highlights



Traditional Swiss cuisine, innovative top restaurants or trendy locales: in the Grand Tour section along Lake Zurich, gourmets are in for a treat.









Villa Sunneschy



Restaurant Highlights Along the Grand Tour of Switzerland





This magnificent art nouveau villa is home to a first-class restaurant and a beautiful lounge by the lake.















Blaue Ente



Restaurant Highlights Along the Grand Tour of Switzerland





A visit to the Blaue Ente restaurant is a very special experience. Here, in what used to be a flour mill, guests are pampered with outstanding cuisine.















Boilerroom



Restaurant Highlights Along the Grand Tour of Switzerland





With its high ceilings and steel constructions, the Boilerroom restaurant in Winterthur is characterized by industrial charm.















Romantik Seehotel Sonne



Restaurant Highlights Along the Grand Tour of Switzerland





Guests at the Restaurant Sonnengalerie, housed in the Romantik Seehotel Sonne, can take a short break by Lake Zurich.























Hotel recommendations



Sit back and relax, put your feet up, and look forward to the forthcoming touring days: the best place to do this is in the top hotels along the route of the Grand Tour of Switzerland.









The Dolder Grand



The Most Beautiful Hotels Along the Grand Tour of Switzerland





The luxury hotel high above Zurich is distinguished by its fabulous view, its spa and its gourmet restaurant.















Ambassador à l’Opera



The Most Beautiful Hotels Along the Grand Tour of Switzerland





Directly behind the Opera House and next to the lake, this hotel treats guests to first-class service, beautiful rooms and a prime location.















Marina Lachen



The Most Beautiful Hotels Along the Grand Tour of Switzerland





Wake up in the morning and look out over Lake Zurich from your bed: that is possible at the Hotel Marina Lachen, near Rapperswil.















Hirschen am See



The Most Beautiful Hotels Along the Grand Tour of Switzerland





Guests feel as if they are on vacation by the sea at this guesthouse, located just a short distance outside the city.




























Those who fancy getting a real taste of Switzerland on the Grand Tour of Switzerland can treat themselves to a Grand Tour Snack Box. This picnic basket contains a variety of delicious regional specialties.








The Snack Box can be purchased at 46 different outlets, and later refilled as required en route. One of these sales outlets is the quaint Restaurant Adlisberg – which is in itself well worth a visit.





The Grand Tour Snack Box









E-Grand Tour



Charging Stations




















The Grand Tour of Switzerland can also be done in an electric vehicle. An extensive network of charging stations enables you to travel comfortably, reliably and ecologically.
