The Event Highlights in Zurich and Surrounding Region

Whether related to sports, music or local customs – a host of first-class events are held throughout the year in the city of Zurich and the region around Lake Zurich.












Home

Top Events in Zurich and in the Region





These are the event highlights for 2018. Be sure not to miss them!


In Zurich, a host of events for all tastes and ages are held throughout the year. Allow yourself to be inspired by our Event Highlights 2017:

























December 10, 2017




Silvesterlauf









This race always has a great atmosphere. Whether wearing fancy dress or regular sports gear, competitors of all ages run through the festively lit Old Town.








More


























January 26–28, 2018




Mercedes-Benz CSI









Horse-riding of the highest standard is guaranteed in Zurich during the Mercedes-Benz CSI
...








More


























March 1–4, 2018




Art on Ice









The world-renowned ice skating gala impresses with international music stars, who provide the music for the ice skaters’ performances.








More


























March 8–11, 2018




Man's World









Man’s World is an exhibition especially for men: excavators, motorcycles, barber shops, BBQ utensils, cigars, whisky-tasting, and much more besides.








More


























April 16, 2018




Sechseläuten









Everything you need to know about Zurich’s spring festival.








More


























April 22, 2018




Zurich Marathon









The Zurich Marathon leads through particularly pretty scenery: through the city and along the lake.








More


























May 24 – June 3, 2018




FOOD ZURICH









The Zurich food festival sure to tickle your taste buds.








More


























June 1–24, 2018




Zurich Festival









Over four weeks, Zurich’s venues show off their variety, focusing on a joint theme.








More


























June 14– 17, 2018




blues’n’jazz Rapperswil









The open-air festival by Lake Zurich thrills with its great concerts and spectacular backdrop.








More


























June 15–17, 2018




Zurich Pride Festival









Colorful, loud and joyful – at Zurich Pride, the LGBT community demonstrate for their rights, with the widespread support of the people of Zurich.








More


























July 5–7, 2019




Züri Fäscht









Anyone who misses Switzerland’s largest festival, the "Züri Fäscht", from July 5–7, 2019 will have to wait another three whole years before they have the chance again...








More


























July 6 – 8, 2018




Caliente!









Things heat up at Caliente! Europe’s largest Latin festival creates a lively ambiance, with its South American music, cuisine and market stalls.








More


























July 29, 2018




Ironman Zurich









The Ironman event in Zurich brings together athletes from over 70 countries.








More


























August 11, 2018




Street Parade









At the largest techno parade in the world, hundreds of thousands of ravers dance around Zurich’s lake basin.








More


























August 16 – September 2, 2018




Zürcher Theater Spektakel









13 stages and over 40 current theater productions from all over the world in 18 days – the "Zürcher Theater Spektakel" is a highlight in Zurich's cultural calendar...








More


























August 22 – 25, 2018




Zürich Openair









Every year, the only open-air concert with its own airport thrills electro, rock and pop music fans with international acts.








More


























August 30, 2018




Weltklasse Zurich









Olympic gold medalists and world record holders meet at the summit of track and field in Zurich.








More


























September 23, 2018




SlowUp Zürichsee









One day a year, the area along Lake Zurich is traditionally given over to non-motorized traffic.








More


























September 27 – October 7, 2018




Zurich Film Festival









The Zurich Film Festival offers a platform for the most promising filmmakers from around the globe.
...








More


























October 31 – November 3, 2018




Jazznojazz









Jazznojazz – the music festival for jazz, funk, and soul








More


























November 10, 2018




Räbechilbi Richterswil









For the “Räbechilbi”, around 30 tons of turnips are transformed into artistically carved lanterns.








More


























November 6–11, 2018




International Short Film Festival Winterthur









At this festival, the main focus is on the short film. And the movie that wins here is automatically nominated for an Oscar in the Short Film category.








More
