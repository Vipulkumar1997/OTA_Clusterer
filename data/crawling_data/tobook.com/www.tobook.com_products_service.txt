Hotel Web-Booking Engine service (Install Direct-bookings) on your own website


Easily add more functionality to your own website with the toBook.com 
		Instant Reservations Service, Direct Booking Engine. The room availability and price 
		information that you enter on toBook.com can be used on your own 
		(or other) website as well. By adding our
Instant Reservations 
		Service
to your website, you will be providing your potential 
		customers the possibility to see 'real time' how much the requested 
		room will cost them. For a small fee, you can offer them the opportunity 
		to make instant reservations on your site.


Advantages

- The
Instant Reservations Service @ Your Website
offers 
		visitors on your site the possibility to make on-line reservations 
		24 hours a day, and get an immediate confirmation. This option 
		will boost your sales because your reception will be open at all 
		times and because of the far reaching simplification of the communications 
		process.

- Visitors can automatically see if you have available allotment 
		for the requested period and therefore they will be more eager 
		to make an
Instant Reservation
.

- With this system, no more obsolete reservation requests emails 
		will reach you and therefore it will prove to save much of your 
		valued time.

- You will be able to be up-to-date and to answer to more of your 
		potential customers demands.

- toBook.com will actively promote your property's site and accompany 
		you in promoting your website.
