Mona Lisa Medical Wellness & Spa in detail


Your Resort

☀ The historic
Mona Lisa Butik Hotel
(non-smoking) is located on the outskirts of Kolberg, one of the most beautiful seaside resorts on the Amber Coast, in the Podczele district, about 500 metres from the beach and 5km from the city centre. Nearby there is also a bus stop (50 km from hotel). The spacious gardens of the spa hotel border on the protected natural area 'Ekopark', so you can have a beautiful view of the park and the unique bird reserve.

How You Reside

The quietly located hotel is full of cosy charm and warm colours to make you feel truly at home. 38 moderns rooms (14-21sqm) with bath, shower, toilet, bath robe (€1 per day), hairdryer, telephone, satellite TV, most with balcony or terrace. 8 apartments spread over 2 floors (stairs), WIFI-throughout hotel. Safe at the front desk.

Description of available rooms
Dining

Restaurant with fireplace. For half board, breakfast and dinner is a buffet. With all inclusive there is also a light lunch buffet, afternoon coffee, tea & cakes! Mealtime drinks (juices, water, house wine, draft beer) also included in halb board. Diabetic and light diet meals, reduced diet meals and vegetarian food on advance request. Cafe bar with sun terrace, children's corner. Large garden with barbecue facilities.

Wellness, Spa Health


Spa and wellness area
(850 m²) with swimming pool & whirlpool corner (7-11am / 2-10 pm), small sauna & steam room. Beauty Farm (Mon-Sat 10am-6pm) with REPECHAGE products.

Spa area
(Mon-Fri 7am-10pm) for treatment and rehabilitation treatments with qualified therapists. Doctor in house. Treatments such as various baths (carbonic, mud & brine bath), hydro-massage, partial massage, mud packs, laser, cryogenic, electrical, magnetic field and galvantherapy, ultrasound, iontophoresis, solux lamp, inhalation, individual and group exercises, water aerobics, exercise bike. Full body massage and lymphatic drainage for a fee.

Symptons:
Diseases of the musculoskeletal system, rheumatism, respiratory diseases, metabolic and circulatory diseases & diabetes mellitus.

Sport & Leisure

Explore the scenic countryside on the well-developed cycle paths & walking trails that run nicely across the beach directly to the Kolberger centre. Bicycle rental (approx, €2.50). Discover the beautiful Spa town of Kolber, which offers various cultural activities throughout the year, such as concerts, exhibitions and theatre performances.

Additional information

TRAIN to
Kolobrzeg
(Kolberg), about 6 km from the hotel. Transfer Station. SpaDreams recommends a taxi about PLN 60/€15 per car (1-8 pers.). FLIGHT to
Szczecin-Golenlów (Szczecin SZZ)
, about 98 km away.
Transfer (Roundtrip):
from SZZ airport, about € 150 per car (1-4 pers.). Reserve through SpaDreams, pay on arrival.

INFO Tourist tax:
PLN4/day, about €0.95/day, to pay on site.Parking: CCTV surveillance, about €5 / day.
Child discount
:. 2 adults + 1 child (0-3.9 years) 100% (from 4 to 12.9 years) 50% (from 13 to 15.9 years) 20%.
Pets
: not allowed. On Saturdays and Sundays there is reduced room service.

The hotel will be closed between 02.12 - 19.12. 2017

Good to know

The Unique Microclimate
Kolberg is known for its clean, iodine and iron-rich sea air, ideal for respiratory diseases and improving overall well being.
