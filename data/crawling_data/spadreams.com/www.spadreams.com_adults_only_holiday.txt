Stress Free Holidays for Adults Only
Are you looking for a place of pure peace, relaxation, romance or luxury for your next holiday? Let yourself be inspired by our wide range of
adults-only hotels
!  With SpaDreams Adults Only Holidays it is easy to book a holiday where relaxation is guaranteed.
Whether newly married, extremely stressed or just looking for relaxing rest and relaxation, An Adults Only Holiday is sure key to blissful relaxation. In today's fast-paced world it is becoming increasingly important to seriously relax in order to refill ourselves with new energy for everyday life. According to the motto "We love children, but the grown-ups need a holiday too", our wellness hotels offer first-class services to guests with a minimum age of 16 or 18 years. Our top-rated adults-only hotels are perfect for couples, groups, and solo travellers.
Enjoy your holidays at the spa, meet like-minded people in the morning's fitness program or lay out in peaceful solitude near the beach or pool. Rounding out these already amazing resorts offerings are culinary highlights,
spa wellness
programs and an entertainment program with adults especially in mind. For the active adults-only holiday, some hotels offer numerous water sports activities - such as sailing, windsurfing or diving.
You love an undisturbed night's rest, an open breakfast buffet or
romantic
dinners with a good glass of wine on the terrace? Then discover our adults only holidays and treat yourself to an escape from the real world.  An escape that is for grown-ups only.
Whether in Europe, Asia or elsewhere, with SpaDreams you will find the perfect place to take a time-out from the everyday life for an unforgettable Adults-Only Getaway.
