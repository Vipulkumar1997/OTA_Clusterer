The Leela Kovalam in detail


Your hotel

☀ The luxury hotel
The Leela Kovalam
is located on a beautiful cliff with spectacular panoramic view over the Kovalam beach in the southern state of Kerala.

How You Reside

183 spacious and elegant guest
rooms
with bath / shower / WC, hairdryer, safe, satellite TV, telephone, mini bar, coffee and tea making facilities and Wi-Fi. 41 Garden View rooms (about 44 sq.m.) are quite a bit away from the main house near the beach, with sun deck and view of the beautiful tropical garden. 84 rooms with sea view (approx. 46 sq.m.) with balcony on the cliffs overlooking the beach. 58 Club rooms and suites on the cliff overlooking the sea in the exclusive club wing, with butler service, separate lounge, club spa and pool, The Cafe and library.
Single rooms are double rooms
for single occupancy.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

Buffet
restaurant
"The Terrace" (open 24 hours) offers the best of the Asian, European or Indian cooking and a superb view over the sea. In the open beach restaurant "The Tides" fresh seafood specialties are served directly from the grill. Skybar, bar, The Café.

2 infinity pools overlooking the sea.

Health, Ayurveda & Spa

Divya Spa
(approx. 700 sq.m.), led by 3 doctors and 20 therapists, offers a variety of treatments with a special focus on Ayurveda. Herbs from the hotels own organic greenhouse. Gym, yoga and meditation centre. Beauty Salon.

Leisure activities:
archery, badminton, tennis, table tennis and billiard.

Additional information

ARRIVAL to
Trivandrum
(TRV).

TRASNFER, about 15 km,
included with Ayurveda programmes
, with 'luxurious relaxation' programme, there is a taxi transfer of €85 per car (there and back) FIT 729T1,

Please note:

In garden view rooms, due to the close proximity to a Temple and a Mosque, in the early mornings and/or late evenings loud prayers should be expected. Therefore, SpaDreams recommends Sea View or Club Room.
