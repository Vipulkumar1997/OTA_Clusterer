Malteser Klinik von Weckbecker in detail


Your Hotel

☀ This specialist clinic for naturopathic medicine is stituated in
Bad Brückenau
. This scenic environment at the Bavarian Rhön is ideal for hiking and recharging your batteries.

The modern clinic has all the comforts of a hotel and combines best naturopathic therapies such as fasting with all the latest modern research. The new centre for osteopathic pain management is leading the way for a holistic approach to curing and offers attractive and tailored treatments. Dedicated and skilled therapists support the improvement of physical, mental and emotional well-being with gentle medicine that does not just cure the symptoms, but starts at the cause of complaints.

In times of stress, this hotel can provide a haven of wellbeing. Surrounded by a beautiful natural landscape, it provides the ideal combination of enjoyment and recovery.

How You Reside

105 comfortable rooms for your well-being. Mostly single rooms approx. 16-25 sqm, some double rooms 20-45 sqm and apartments with bath or shower, WC, telephone, satellite TV, safe. Comfort rooms with balcony, hairdryer, internet connection. Allergy-free, handicapped accessible rooms. Bathrobe (about € 10), lift.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

Tasty and organic cuisine
! Cooked meals are vegetarian with 100% organic foods, from a healthy fitness breakfast to a vital menu (organic vital full board €35 per day), fasting, alkaline food diet full board (€25 per day) or individual diets such as diabetic cuisine, lactose or gluten free food (also available for other food intolerances such as histamine or fructose intolerance, full board €45 per day).

The hotel offers lawns, a meditation room, a chapel, a library, a teaching kitchen and cosmetic treatments.

Wellness, Spa and Health

Naturopathy Therapy:

The clinic has a large Kneipp area, beautiful fitness rooms, skilled physiotherapists and a wide variety of massages (classic, according to Breuss and Thorn, acupoint meridians, etc.). Other therapies available include craniofacial therapy, traditional Chinese medicine, osteopathy, biomechanical muscle stimulation and colontherapy. Other therapies are homeopathy, phytotherapy (medicinal plants), orthomolecular medicine, sports, exercise, relaxation therapy, meditation. Current blood values are required for all programmes, which can be brought with you or taken by the doctor at the hotel (laboratory cost from about € 30).

Indications:

Obesity, diabetes, metabolic, gastrointestinal, cardiovascular, joint and spinal diseases. Other indicators are allergies, susceptibility to infection, high blood pressure, headache, migraine, fibromyalgia and burnout.

Golf available off-site: 30 km away Bad Kissingen, Fulda and Maria Bildhausen. A driving range and a tennis court/hall are located in the village.

Good to know

Osteopathy at the Malteser Klinik von Weckbecker

Due to the effectiveness of osteopathy for pain relief there has been founded a separate osteopathy department.

Determination of causes of pain as well as pain relief are the key objectives of the therapy. The osteopathic treatment is suitable for those who suffer from pain caused by muscle tension (e.g. in shoulder or neck) followed by symptoms such as joint pain, head aches, jaw pain intestinal problems, chronic sinusitis, tinnitus or dizzines.
