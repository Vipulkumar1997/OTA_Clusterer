Devaaya Ayurveda & Nature Cure Center in detail



The Location

Goa
is a small state in the west coast of India beloved by visitors. Thanks to its unique history and location Goa offers a harmonious blend of Indian and Portuguese cultural influences, which results in relaxed ambience with no need to rush.

The Retreat

The home of
Devaaya Ayurveda & Nature Cure Center
is a dream-like, green
Divar Island
located on Mandovi River, about 20 km away from Panaji – the capital of Goa shaped by Mediterranean influences. Away from the mass tourism, the retreat is a paradise for everyone, who is interested in Ayurveda. The best-known beaches of Goa are 30 min. away by car, the mainland (e.g Panaji ) is 10 min. away by car.

How You Reside

24 comfortable
Superior Rooms
and 32
Villa Rooms
in traditional rustic style of Goa with bathroom/WC, TV, telephone, kettle, fridge, air-conditioning, ceiling fan, hair dryer (on request), balcony/terrace with field view, garden and the river. Villa Rooms in free-standing bungalows. 1 suite with separate living room and bedroom. Single rooms are double rooms for single use.

Description of available rooms
Dining and Leisure

Restaurants with vegetarian and ayurvedic cuisine only. Meals as a buffet or a menu. Ayurvedic meals advised by the Ayurveda doctor available on the menu. Lactose- and gluten-free meals available. Alcohol consumption and smoking are not permitted at the resort. No caffeinated drinks are served. The restaurant “Malar” is air-conditioned. Terrace restaurant “Manos” (dependent on the weather conditions).

24h room service, PC with internet, Wi-Fi and safe at the reception, boutique with Indian craft and Ayurvedic herbal products, conference room, seasonal evening entertainment. Swimming pool (20 x 8 m, approx. 28 - 30°C), hard tennis court.

Health, Spa and Beauty

Devaaya Ayurveda Centre
with specialised team of Ayurveda doctors, female and male masseurs and therapists following the Devaaya Ayurveda Philosphy (Devaaya = divine healing). Yoga and meditation (private sessions on request) are a part of the therapy. Specialised naturopathy doctors and therapist team. The ayurvedic medicines are not an essential part of the treatments.

The therapy at Devaaya can help get gid of toxins, which contribute to development of various illnesses and it strengthens the natural healing powers of the body. The naturopathy at Devaaya is based on massages, hydrotherapy, ayurvedic diet and activities. Some treatments take place in new open huts with screens.

Activities

Hard tennis court, fitness room, paths for walking, jogging and cycling, trekking, angling (as on offer, no fee). Help with organisation of: trips to spice plantations, elephant rides, bathing with elephants, trips to the beaches of south Goa, crocodile and dolphin watching etc.

Tourist attractions nearby
: bird retreat on Chorao Island, historic parts of Goa (“Velha Goa”) with cathedrals, churches and monasteries – UNESCO World Heritage Site, beautiful Keri beach and Tiracol fortress.

Additional information

FLIGHT to Goa (GOI), approx. 35km.

TRANSFER (return: included with Ayurveda cure / Wellness & Rejuvenation € 62 pp
