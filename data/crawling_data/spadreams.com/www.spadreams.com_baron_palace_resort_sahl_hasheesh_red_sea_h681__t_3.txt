Baron Palace Resort in detail


Your Hotel

☀ In the heart of Sahl Hasheesh Bay on the Red Sea, the Baron Palace Resort Sahl Hasheesh is located on the 650 m long private sandy beach, surrounded by a tropical garden. The architecture of the property blends Arab and Mediterranean elements. The centre of Hurghada with bars, restaurants and many shopping opportunities is about 20 km away. Shuttle service several times a day (for a fee).

How You Reside

650 elegant and comfortable rooms with balcony or terrace, bath and shower /toilet, hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers, individually controlled air conditioning, LCD satellite TV, telephone, coffee/ tea making facilities, safe and WLAN. Classic Standard (45 sqm) with sea or garden views, Baron Club (about 45 square meters) and many with sea views and exclusive access to Club Lounge with Baron Club. Swim-Up Suites (approximately 52 sqm) with shared private pool (Adults only) and a private beach area, family room (about 52 sqm) with 2 sofa beds, One-Bedroom Suites and Three-Bedroom Suites (approximately 56 sq m), Panorama suites (approximately 83 sq m), Senior Suites (ca.123-142 sqm), Royal suites with private swimming pool. Baron Club rooms and suites with DVD and CD player, suites with rain shower, sep. WC. EZ are double room single use. Smoking rooms and 2 for disabled guests. Lifts.

Description of available rooms
Dining

Main restaurant Red Sea with indoor and outdoor areas. Morning, noon and evening buffet. Vegetarian, vegan, lactose-free and gluten-free and dietary requirements. 7 additional à la carte restaurants serving Italian, Egyptian, Mexican, Asian- and fish / grilled specialties and a sushi bar and a poolside restaurant with BBQ, each with indoor and outdoor areas. For dinner, appropriate dress is required. Café, 7 bars.

Wellness & Spa

Spacious outdoor pool area (about 5000 square meters) with 3 swimming pools, sun terraces, sun loungers and parasols. On the beach chairs and umbrellas. Adults only area. Pool bar and lounge. Spa (2300 m², daily. 9-19 clock) with an indoor pool area (daily approx. 7-18 clock), saunas (including a Finish and samarium), steam bath, whirlpool, steam and brine steam bath, hammam (for women and Men separated), experience showers, aromatherapy and hydrotherapy, relaxation areas.

A trained and professional team of therapists offering a wide range of massages, wellness and beauty applications such as Hot stone, Thai, Balinese and shiatsu. Thalasso treatments and Thalassotherapy pool. Massage the area with 8 treatment rooms, among others for sludge applications. Medical Spa with treatment rooms. Vitamin and Health Bars. Health club with meditation room overlooking the indoor pool. VIP treatment suites, inter alia, with private Räumen- and relaxation area, sauna, steam room and Jacuzzi. Massage pavilion by the beach. Beauty parlour. Hairdresser.

Leisure

Fitness Centre (daily approx. 9-21 clock), and Personal Training. Jogging track. Many possibilities for excursions in the surrounding area. Bicycle Rental. 3 tennis courts (floodlit). Children's area (about 1300 square meters) with Baby Club (from 1 to 3.9 years), Kids Club (4 to 7.9 years), Juniors Club (8 to 12.9 years) and Teen Club (13-17, 9 J.). Babysitting GEG. Fee. PADI diving u. Water sports centre on the beach among others with kayaking, pedal boating, beach volleyball, beach aerobics.
In hotel German is spoken.
