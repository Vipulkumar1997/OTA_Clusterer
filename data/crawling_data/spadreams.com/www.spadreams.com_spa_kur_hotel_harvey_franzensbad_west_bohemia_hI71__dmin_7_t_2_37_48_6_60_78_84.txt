Spa & Kur Hotel Harvey in detail


Your Hotel

☀ The hotel
Spa & Kur Hotel Harvey
lies by the forest park of Franzensbad.

How You Reside

107
rooms
with bath/shower/WC, hairdryer, safe satellite TV, telephone, Wi-Fi, coffee and tea equipment and mini bar. Doubleroom
comfort
(approx. 35 sqm) including terrace with view over the street. Doubleroom
superior
(approx. 35 sqm) including balcony with view over the park.

Description of available rooms
Dining

Restaurant with local and international specialties. Breakfast buffet in the morning, in the noon and evening menus. Also vegetarian, gluten free and diet food available. Bar.
Summer terrace.

Wellness, Spa & Health

Modern spa section (Monday – Saturday, 9a.m. – 9p.m.) with qualified doctors and therapists, 24-hour-nurse service. Moor wraps and baths, CO2, pearl, soda and full body baths, fango packs, underwater, aromatic and classic massages, lymph drainage, group gymnastics, paraffin hand-packs, inhalation, gas injection, electro therapy. Additional: oxygen therapy, dry carbondioxide bath, manual lymph drainage, vitamin injection, hot stone, chocolate and honey massage.

Leisure

Biking, hiking, Nordic walking, concerts, cultural events, dance nights. Trips to the spa destinations Marienbad and Karlsbad.

GOLF 18-holes Golfclub Franzensbad in Hozlov, approx. 10 km far away.

Additional information

TRAIN to
Franzensbad Station
, approx. 1 km. Rail fares on request at SpaDreams.

TRANSFER (return):
Karlsbad airport
approx. € 60/Person (car 1-2 persons), approx. € 40/person (car 3-8 persons). Reservation with SpaDreams, payment on arrival.

INFOS Tourist tax
: included.
Surveilled parking lot
: €3/day.
Child discount:
2 adults + 1 child (0-1, 9 yrs) 100%.
Pets:
not allowed.

Good to know

Franzensbad (Františkovy Lázně)

is located in the West Bohemian tri-border area near the historical town of Eger (Cheb). The healing effect of the springs in Franzensbad has been known since the 15th century. They are used, among others, for inhalations, gas injections, dry carbondioxide baths, pearl bath, mineral baths and bubble baths.

Indications:
Diseases of the locomotor system and cardiovascular system.
