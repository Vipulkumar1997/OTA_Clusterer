Siddhalepa Ayurveda Resort in detail


Your Holiday Destination

☀ The town of Wadduwa is located on the south-west coast of Sri Lanka and with its beautiful, palm-fringed beaches, is appreciated by both tourists and locals alike.

Your Ayurvedic Resort

The Siddhalepa Ayurveda Resort is proud of its long tradition. For almost 20 years the guests have been pampered with superior ayurvedic treatments. The superb location amidst tropical gardens directly by a long beach make the resort the perfect place for an ayurvedic treatment. Apart from an open lobby and a restaurant the resort offers a large, fresh water pool with a waterfall ideal for relaxation. The paths of the garden rich in herbs lead to a relaxation area by mangroves on the beach. The resort belongs to the Siddhalepa Group, which is a family business which has cultivated the ayurvedic traditions for over 200 years. Today the company is known for its products even in Europe.

How You Reside

Multiple 1-3-storey buildings are spread over the area of the resort offering in total 50 rooms. The amenities are up to the western standards with rain shower or bathtub, WC, hair dryer, telephone, satellite TV, tea/coffee set, fan and air-conditioning, safe and mini fridge. Every room has its own Singhalese theme and a unique charm. Superior Rooms with terrace or balcony (approx. 42 s qm) and pool or garden view. Deluxe Rooms (approx. 49 s qm) with terrace and sea view. Deluxe Suites (approx. 65 s qm) with recently renovated large terrace and lateral sea view. Wi-Fi in the lobby (for a fee). Single rooms are offered as double rooms for single use.

Description of available rooms
Restaurant

The meals served in the restaurant are prepared in consultation with the ayurvedic doctors. Depending on the occupancy the guests enjoy the meals from a menu or from a buffet. Fish is served occasionally. International dishes are available on request.

Ayurveda Centre

The highly-qualified 5 doctors and 20 therapists were educated at the Siddhalepa Ayurveda Hospital.
The reputation of the Ayurveda treatments at Siddhalepa Ayurveda Resort has been built on the long experience in treating international guests. Using one of the 22 treatment rooms the guests can be sure they are treated with greatest care. The offer of the resort is completed with yoga and meditation. The resort is especially appealing to those who seek an authentic aurvedic treatment.

Leisure

Apart from relaxing time by the pool or beach walks you will enjoy lectures on Ayurveda. The team of the resort are happy to help planning excursions. Fitness centre.

Additional information

ARRIVAL
Colombo Airport (CMB)
, approx. 80 km.

INFOS: Young adults starting at the age of 13 are welcome at the resort.

Good to know

The Siddhalepha Group
Approx. 200 years ago the Hettigoda family started to produce and sell ayurvedic products under the brand Siddhalepa. The group runs ayurvedic centres in Germany, Slovakia and Czech Republic as well as a hospital and a university in Sri Lanka. In the factory in Sri Lanka there are produced lotions, toothpaste, care products and massage oils as well as ayurvedic herbal medicines.
