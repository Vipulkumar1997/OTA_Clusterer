Holidays with Disabled Access
At SpaDreams we place at high importance the ability for persons with physical disabilities or limited mobility to joyfully visit our hotels.
We want every person to enjoy an equal and exciting
holiday without complications
.  To make finding and booking an amazing Holiday even easier, we have selected a variety of accessible hotels that all of our guests can enjoy, regardless of mobility.
The rooms at our
barrier-free hotels
are wheelchair accessible with ramps, wide doors and generally spacious design. To provide guests with even more comfort, there is also a lift,
wheelchair accessible
bathrooms and additional wall brackets.
Our main concern is that guests feel welcome and enjoy a friendly atmosphere.  With handicap accessible resorts far and wide, it’s easy to find a destination to love.
European Holidays
that are easy to get to and have high standards are an excellent choice for anyone with mobility issues.  Looking for a
sundrenched beach
? SpaDreams has sunny
accessible holidays
in
Spain
,
Italy
, C
roatia
, and
Portugal
.  Ready to enjoy world-class mountain views? Then book a barrier free holiday in
Austria
,
Germany
, or
Slovenia
.
Too many amazing handicap accessible holidays to choose from? Just contact one of our
experts
who will be pleased to advise you individually and help you book a holiday with
disabled access
.
