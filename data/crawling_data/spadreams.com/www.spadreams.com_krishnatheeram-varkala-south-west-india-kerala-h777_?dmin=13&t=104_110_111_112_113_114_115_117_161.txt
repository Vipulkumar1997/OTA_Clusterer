Krishnatheeram in detail


Your Holiday Destination

☀
Kerala
is the most southwestern state of India and known for its moderate and healthy climate and its Ayurveda resorts.
Verkala
, a small picturesque seaside resort with a beautiful beach, about 50 km north of Trivandrum and 190 km south of Cochin, is also popular among western tourists for its temples and its romantic location along a steep coast as well as for its Yoga centres.

Your Ayurveda Resort

The Ayurveda resort
in Keralan style is located on top a rock formation which surrounds the beach, with a unique view on the Malabar coast in tropical garden location. It is only a short walk to the narrow, silver-black sand beach. The main beach is more convenient for bathing and is located about 300 m away and is accessible by steep stairs.

How You Reside

30
rooms
/
cottages,
simple but appealing with shower/WC, sat-TV, telephone, fan, some are separated from the main area by a small street.
Vasantham
with large bathroom, separate shower, mini bar, balcony/patio, mostly with sea view, some with air conditioning. Laundry service. Guest safe at the reception.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

Open
reaturant
with international Ayurvedic kitchen. Ayurveda guests get their meals prepared according to their Dosha type. Sunset lounge.

Spa & Health

Ayurveda centre
(about 50 m away) under the management of a professional Ayurveda team. Ayurvedic treatments, e.g. herb oil massage, Shirdhara, Marma massage. Yoga and meditation are also part of the Ayurveda cure.

Free time:
Relax in the hammocks in the garden or on the sunbeds or walk along the beachfront that offers many shops and restaurants. Boat trip to the famous Backwaters, visit of local markets and temples. Bicycle rental.
