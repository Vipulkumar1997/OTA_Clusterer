Paradise KohYao in detail


Your Hotel

☀ Surrounded by tropical rainforest and numerous cliffs, the
Paradise KohYao
is located in the northeast of the Thai island of Koh Yao Noi, and secluded in a small bay with a 400m long private beach. The gorgeous island covers about 120 sq.km and is part of the national park that lies between the provinces of Phuket and Krabi, which offers an interesting flora and fauna, coral reefs and countless fish species. A free shuttle service runs daily to the
National Park
. On excursions in the surrounding region, abandoned rice fields and caoutchouc farms can be visited. Particularly popular are activities such as snorkeling and scuba diving, rock climbing, elephant trekking, boat trips and mountain bike tours (partly to be booked by local providers, range of water sports depends on the weather). Hotel own boat shuttle to the island of Phuket or Krabi, each approx. 1 hour. This popular resort has an open lobby with a reception desk, an Internet terminal (free of charge), a small shop, library (free rental of books, magazines and DVDs).

How You Reside

70 studios and villas
are designed for ecological construction and are in 1-2-storey buildings with 4 residential units that fit harmoniously into the surrounding landscape, the many existing golf carts overcome the steep slopes and transport guests and luggage across the entire hotel complex easily.
Superior Studio
(54 sq.m) is spacious and decorated with Thai natural materials and includes bath or shower / WC (partly unroofed bathroom with outdoor shower), satellite TV, telephone, CD and DVD player, hair dryer, bathrobe, slippers, safe, minibar, tea and coffee maker, air conditioning, balcony or terrace.
Jacuzzi Studio
(56 sq.m) is located on the sea side with a view of the entire resort, with an open living area, jacuzzi tub in the bathroom and a spacious terrace. Plunge pool Deluxe Studio (approx. 62 sq.m.) with jacuzzi on the terrace and view of the bay. Pool Villa (approx. 91 s.qm.) directly at the beach with private pool.
Single rooms are double rooms
for single occupancy.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

If you love fresh Italian and Thai dishes, then you are exactly right.
2 beach restaurants and 2 bars
offer freshly caught seafood and fish from the region or from Canada, as well as high-quality meats (especially beef and lamb) from Australia. The main restaurant "
Seafood Grill
" surprises guests with Thai and Western dishes, snacks, salads, seafood buffet and a daily theme night with a barbecue or an authentic Thai night. In the mornings you can enjoy a rich American breakfast buffet. When booking HB / FB you get a lunch and dinner menu in the Seafood Grill or choose your meals from the extensive buffet (some meat and fish specialties, seafood and Japanese buffet at an additional cost). The restaurant "
Al Fresco
" serves light Mediterranean dishes, pasta and pizza (made on a real stone oven), local seafood and authentic Italian ice cream with seating directly on the beach.
On request, vegetarian, lactose- and gluten-free diet possible
.

For a refreshing dip in between, a
pool bar
offers fruit shakes, cocktails, soft drinks, fresh sliced fruit and snacks. You can experience a spectacular evening at the
sunset bar
on the beach with delicious coktails and unwind after a busy day. Candlelight dinners in the romantic torchlight at the beach are offered as well (reservation required).

Wellness, Spa & Health

A total peace returns, if you forget the daily routine in the
350 square metre
swimming pool, lovingly surrounded by shady palms and trees or on the sun terrace, where you can enjoy the view of the strange rock formations of the landscape. Jacuzzi, separate children's pool, sunbeds and umbrellas at the pool and beach included. At low tide, swimming may be limited in the ocean (bathing shoes are recommended).

A touch of paradise surrounds you in the tropical
Paradise Spa
(built directly into the rock, with a plunge pool) with herbal steam sauna, massages using natural herbal ingredients, Anti-Aging-/Anti-Stress- treatments, facials, aromatherapy, Thai and Balinese massage, Indian head massage, classic back massage, sea salt scrub, exotic Thai herbal scrub, sesame and honey scrub, coconut & turmeric scrub, mud and mud body wrap, mint and cucumber wrap and much more. Individual treatments can also be booked in the package.

Focused on
yoga
in all its facets, the resort combines the advantages of this philosophy and way of life to restore a balance between mind and body through a combination of physical exercises (asanas), breathing exercises and meditation. Highly-qualified, experienced and longtime practicing yoga therapists supervise attractive yoga programs for beginners and intermediates, as a à la carte service or multi-day programme available.

Leisure & Sports

If you are looking for tranquility but also for
activity during your holiday stay
, you will find a varied offer of cooking classes, Thai boxing shows, beach volleyball, badminton, diving, snorkeling, climbing, jungle trekking, boat trips, speed boats, canoe and mountain bike tours (partly for a fee, depending on weather) at the hotel.

Additional information

FLIGHT to
Phuket (HKT)
. All flight fares on request with SpaDreams.The
boat transfer
to Koh Yao Noi Island (approx. 75 minutes) is only carried out during daytime. Guests scheduled to arrive after 4 pm, should plan an extra night in Phuket. In addition, due to safety reasons it is not possible to carry out the transfer in high winds or strong waves. In these rare cases, the on-site staff will provide further guidance.
