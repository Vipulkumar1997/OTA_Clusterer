Holiway Garden Resort & Spa in detail


Your Retreat

☀
Holiway Garden Resort & Spa
is located by the northern coast of Bali. Situated by the sea and surrounded by beautiful tropical gardens, coconut palms and mango and cashew trees which ensure pleasant shade the resorts is a paradisiacal retreat with stunning views and unique ambience.

"At home in paradise" is here more than just a motto
, it is the way of life. Friendliness, warm welcome, hospitality and individual approach to each guest are the essence of the resort. The guests visiting the resort are mostly solo travellers, couples or groups of friends creating casual, open, relaxed enviroment where you can put on ease, relax, set your soul free and gather energy and inspiration for your daily life.

How You Reside

18 villas (approx. 135 s qm) in Balinese style with western amenities ensuring ultimate comfort and relaxing ambience. The construction materials were selected from ecologically sustainable sources to offer pleasant, healthy atmosphere. The spacious, comfortable, airy interiors on 2 floors give room for free thoughts and dreams.With 2 bedrooms, living and dining area, kitchenette, outdoor bathroom with shower and WC, desk, terrace furniture, mosquito nets, air-conditioning and roofed balcony.

Description of available rooms
Dining

At "Baruna" restaurant you can taste far eastern specialities with a hint of the Mediterranean influences or enjoy a sundowner by the sunset. Healthy and fresh ingredients such as home made bread, freshly caught fish, meat from free-range chicken, fruit and vegetables from the loca market and rice of bio quality. Vegetarian meals available on request.

Spa & Wellness

"Lotus SPA" offers various massages and beauty and wellness treatments performed by experienced therapists. You can choose for example from Balinese massage, four-hand synchronous massage, Lomi-lomi massage, ayurvedic massages, foot and full-body reflexology massages, hot stone massage, chakra-balancing massages, flower and herbal baths, facials, manicure, pedicure and different scrubs. The treatmetns are performed with use of natural products and oils only.

Daily meditation and yoga sessions available for a fee.

Leisure

Swimming pool with sun terrace and loungers. Bicycle and e-bike rental for a fee (approx. € 3-5/day). Excursions to all tourist destinations in Bali can be organised by the hotel on request. Participation in local celebrations and temple ceremonies as on offer. Snorkelling and diving offered by local suppliers, reef directly by the resort.
