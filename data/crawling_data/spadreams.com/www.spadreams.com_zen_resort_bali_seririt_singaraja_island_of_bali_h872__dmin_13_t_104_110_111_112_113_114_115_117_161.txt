Zen Resort Bali in detail


Your Hotel

☀ The luxurious
Zen Resort Bali
lies in the unspoilt north of Bali and is hidden amidst a tropical landscape of rice terraces and vineyards. The fantastic beach is only about 300m away.

How You Reside

14 comfortable
villas
with sea or garden views in traditional Balinese style. Open-air bathroom with outdoor shower, partially Jacuzzi, separate WC, wood flooring, walk-in closet, terrace. TV and radio were ommited intentionally.

Description of available rooms
Dining

The
restaurant
offers Asian and international cuisine à la carte with stunning views of the sea. For
Ayurveda guests
the meals are made individually after consultation with the Ayurvedic specialists.

Wellness, Spa & Health

Infinity pool
with Jacuzzi. Pool bar. Yoga hall and open-air meditation area surrounded by lush flora, ideal for morning exercises.

Zen Spa
with a qualified therapist under the direction of an Indian Ayurvedic doctor. Choose from a selection of 28 specific
Ayurvedic treatments
such as Ayurvedic full or partial body massage as well as traditional Balinese and Javanese massages and beauty treatments. Various
yoga, meditation and relaxation practices
for expanding consciousness and achieving a state of inner peace.
Individual yoga classes in groups. Certified yoga instructor. International guests describe the resort as a
spiritual place of power.

In-house and certified
PADI diving school
for beginners and advanced.
Excursions and cultural activities
, such as swimming in the hot springs of Banjar, meditating in the Vihara Buddhist temple or a visit to the waterfalls of Munduk and much more.

Additional information

FLIGHT ARRIVALS:
airport: Denpasar
(DPS), ca. 3 hours away.

TRANSFER
airport DPS
included for Ayurveda cure, for Zen Wellness Programme on request.

INFO
Visa/ health regulations
on request.
