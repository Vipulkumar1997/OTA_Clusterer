Schwarzwald Panorama in detail


Your Hotel

☀ Situated on a steep slope, the Schwarzwald Panorama overlooks the climatic health resort of Bad Herrenalb.

How You Reside

Rooms, suites and apartments, all 97 with bath / shower, toilet, hairdryer, satellite TV, telephone, safe, mini-bar, work desk and balcony. Classic 1-4th 1st floor of the forest, Superior / 2. Floor u. Deluxe (upgrade possible) 3rd / 4th Floor, to the valley with stunning panoramic views. SGL are double room.

Description of available rooms
Dining

As organic Partner Hotel uses up to 70% of organic certified seasonal and regional products. Breakfast buffet, Lunch à la carte, in the evening 3-course menu. Vegetarian, lactose-free and gluten-free, vegan diet with reservation. Lobby lounge, bar, café, restaurant with panoramic terrace.

Wellness & Spa

On 1,000 square meters. Mineral-thermal indoor pool (10x15 m, 30 °), whirlpool, Finnish wet sauna, bio sauna with colour lights, steam room. Relaxation room with tea bar, fitness suite. Yoga, solarium (fees apply). Selfless Lifestyle consultations for the careful management of yourself. Beauty with CLARINS products.

Spa Area

TCM Praxis "Of course Weber" Chinese Traditional Medicine. The hotel is the ACURA Clinic Falkenburg connected for cardiology and orthopaedics (connected). Specialists. Sports orthopaedic, cardiac and spinal checkups. Ask your health insurance for a Health Cure.

Leisure

4 tennis courts, cycling, biking and hiking directly from the hotel.

Additional information

HOW TO GET Station
: Karlsruhe, from there take the S-Bahn to Bad Herrenalb (about 500m), rail fares to Karlsruhe on request.
Airport:
Stuttgart about 86 km.
Transfer
from Bad Herrenalb station included (by appointment).

INFO Tourist tax
: € 2,60 / day, payable on site. Parking: included. Underground parking: €8/day.
Children:
on request.
Pets
: € 20 / day.
