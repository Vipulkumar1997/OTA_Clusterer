Home to some of Europe's best spas
Discover pure wellness on the shores of the stunning Adriatic Coast! Combing a rich cultural legacy with striking scenery and a wealth of natural healing resources, the Adriatic Coast is the perfect location for a wellness escape. Two of our favourite destinations on the Adriatic Coast are
Croatia
and
Slovenia
.
The island-studded Dalmatian coastline and tiny fishing villages make Croatia one of the most charming places for a holiday break. Endless sandy beaches, green islands and a rich cultural heritance are not the only reasons to add Croatia to your bucket list. Croatia is best-known as one of the most exciting sailing destinations in the world and is also a perfect spot for
spa and wellness
. Visit the
Isle of Lopud
or St. Andrea Island and indulge yourself at one of our high-class spa hotels. Relaxing spa treatments, exquisite Croatian wine, delicious fresh seafood and fish and views of the Adriatic Sea will make your spa holiday in Croatia an unforgettable experience.
Winding turquoise rivers, snow-dusted mountains and enchanting architecture make
Slovenia
a beautiful destination for a
spa wellness holiday
. Our high quality spas tempt guests in with first-class, natural treatments and a warm, cosy atmosphere.
