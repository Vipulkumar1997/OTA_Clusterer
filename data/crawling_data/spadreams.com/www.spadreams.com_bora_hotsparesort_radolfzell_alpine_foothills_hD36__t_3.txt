bora HotSpaResort in detail


Your Holiday Destination

☀ Lake Constance is one of the most beautiful holiday regions in all of Germany. Radolfzell is a city on the western coast of Lake Constance, approximately 25 km from Konstanz, 10km from Singen, 86 km from the Zurich greater area, and 160 km from Stuttgart. The health resort town has a history stretching back almost 1200 years.

Your Holiday Resort

Bora HotSpaResort is the first four-star superior design hotel based off the unique concept of the so-called nudist hot spa, which arose from the original bora sauna world. A world full of warmth with an open view, promising peace and a chance to slow down.

How You Reside

84 rooms and suites with air-conditioning, flat screen TV, phone, Wi-Fi, minibar, safe, hairdryer and make-up mirror, bathrobe and towels, and balcony. Double Room Comfort (25m²), double room Panorama (24m²) and Double Room Exquisite (31m²). Double Room Panorama Exquisite (29m²) with lateral lake view. Disabled access rooms also available. Suites on request. Single rooms are double rooms for single use.

Description of available rooms
Dining

Varied breakfast buffet available and three-course meal in the evening for those on half-board. Light, modern Euro-Asiatic dishes, snacks and refreshing drinks can be found at “Rubin” restaurant and at the bar. The constantly changing menu offers seasonal and regional products. Specialities can be enjoyed at the lakeside beer garden. The hotel also has a glass covered sky lounge with a fascinating 180-degree view over Lake Constance.

Spa and Wellness

The open and completely naturist HotSpa (approx. 8000m²) with direct access to Lake Constance and comfortable sunbathing lawn includes an earth sauna, smoke sauna, bamboo sauna, kelo pine sauna, samarium, steam bath and salt grotto. The special Onsen bath with a water temperature of 38-40°C is another unique feature of the HotSpa.

The complete offer of massages, treatments, thalasso cosmetics, ayurvedic and Balinese treatments centres around the theme of “warmth”.

Beauty

Exclusive and individual cosmetic and beauty treatments such as mother of pearl manicures/pedicures, purification treatments, body packs, whole body peeling treatments and much more are on offer.

Leisure

The cycling and hiking trails around Lake Constance offer varied options for free time activities, water sports (swimming, surfing, sailing) and a round of golf. Radofzell old town has many cultural highlights. Small shops and a shopping centre in the centre provide excellent shopping opportunities. Recommended day trip destinations include Mainau, the “flowering island”, Sea Life in Konstanz, and the Rhine Falls, the largest waterfall in Europe. Four 18-hole golf courses are just 15-30 minutes away from the resort by car.

Additional information

ARRIVAL Airport: Friedrichshafen (FDH) approx. 50 km away or Zurich (ZRH) approx. 80 km away. Train station: Radolfel approx. 2km away.

TRANSFER SpaDreams recommends a taxi.

INFO Touris tax: approx. € 2/day payable on site (incl. guest card with discounts on the local attractions and free use of public transport). Garage: aprox. € 5-10/day, payable on site. Child discount: 2 adults + 1 child (0-2.9 yrs) € 10, (3-5.9 yrs) € 20, (6-16.9 yrs) € 35, from 17 yrs € 65. No pets.
