Hotel am Schlosspark in detail


Your Hotel

☀ The Hotel am Schlosspark, in
Gotha
, in the middle of the green heart of Thuringia, offers 4 star superior comfort and pampers all of your senses. Separated from the hotel by trees, meadows and bushes, lies the oldest baroque castle of Germany, the castle Friedenstein.

How You Reside

95 stylish
rooms
with a good feeling ambiance, 58 double rooms, 20 single rooms and 16 junior suites, with bath/ shower, WC, hair dryer, telephone, satellite TV, Wi-Fi, mini bar and seating corner. Smoker rooms are available on request. Double rooms may be used mainly as single rooms. There are 2 handicapped accessible rooms available. 2 lifts.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

Restaurants
„Orangerie“ and „Lucas-Cranach-Stube“ – are bright and pleasant, with a sun terrace. A fitness breakfast buffet, for half board guests, a 3 course menu, vegetarian dishes, and a seasonal balanced menu. Lactose and gluten free are also available. English hotel bar, lobby, 24 h reception, Wi-Fi, Internet-PC.

Wellness & Spa

Wellness residence Thuringia
: A team of a healing practitioner, a physio and a wellness therapists as well as cosmeticians offers a modern medical wellness concept, which can improve your mental and physical well-being in the long run, using medical expert knowledge in combination with the most modern cosmetic treatment equipment and high class care products. The offer contains homoeopathic advice, Bach flowers therapy, acupuncture, flex and kinesic taping; cupping and as an extraordinary area of expertise burn-out advice and therapy. Various massage techniques from relaxing to exotic and cosmetic, and from classic to high-care anti-aging are also on offer as well as body molding with the wrapping technique, lymph drainage and ultrasonic sound. In the ambiance flooded with light you can optimally relax and let your soul dangle. We are certified member of Dialia®, the health network for diabetics.

Sauna area
: Finnish sauna, steam sauna, infrared warmth cabin, ice shower, salt grotto, soft pack loungers, and Rasul in the room of the four elements. Daily sports programme (on request), Nordic walking, stretching, PMR progressive muscle relaxation according to Jacobson.

Leisure & Sports

Bike riding and hiking through the diversified nature of the Thuringia forest, tennis. In the winter skiing and luging. Explore the cultural side of Thuringia with its palace Friedenstein, the Maria glass cave Friedrichroda, classic cities such as Erfurt, Weimar or Eisenach.

GOLF 18-hole golf course Gut Ringhofen ca. 15 km away. Greenfee discount.

Additional information

ARRIVAL by
train
, approx. 800 m. Train prices on request. Transfer from the station we recommend a taxi, about € 7 per trip, to be paid on spot.

ARRIVAL by
plane
to Erfurt, approx. 25 km, on request.

Parking: included. Garage: about € 7 / day. Child discount: 2 adults + 1-2 children (0-6, 9 years) 100%, (7-14, 9 years) 50%. Dogs: none.

Mandatory supplement Silvestergala (31.12.17) with bed/breakfast € 99, with half-board € 78.
