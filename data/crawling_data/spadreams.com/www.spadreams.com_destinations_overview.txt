Huge choice and endless possibilities
Do you want to recharge your batteries and leave your everyday life behind? In numerous destinations you'll find fantastic offers for a refreshing wellness holiday or a revitalising beauty break. Let yourself be pampered and bring your body, mind and soul into harmony. Take a glance at our varied selection of romantic wellness weekends or short holidays. No matter where you decide to go, SpaDreams can help you find your dream break!
Are you interested in travelling to India or Sri Lanka? Then a holistic Ayurveda cure is perfect for you! India and Sri Lanka are regarded as the motherlands of traditional far eastern healing. During an Ayurveda holiday you can experience authentic treatments, bring your body into harmony and sustain your health. SpaDreams hotels in Germany, Poland and Portugal also specialise in Ayurveda cures and offer first class programmes.
Would you like to improve your health with a cure in a recognised spa resort? We offer classic therapeutic cures in the most popular regions in Europe! Undertake a cure in the popular regions of the Czech Republic Marienbad, Karlsbad and Franzensbad, or enjoy a healing holiday by the Polish Baltic sea. Germany is the home of many of our best cure hotels - with their healthy climates, Bad Füssing, Bad Kissingen and Bad Gögging are amongst the most famous spa regions in Germany.
Are you looking to lose weight and start a sustainable, healthy lifestyle? With SpaDreams and our wide range of fasting and slimming offers, you can achieve your goals! Choose from detox or slimming in our Schroth cure hotels. We offer a diverse array of fasting programmes worldwide, from fasting according F.X Mayr or Dr. Buchinger juice fasting, to drinking cures and metabolic diet programmes.
