Surya Lanka Ayurveda Beach Resort in detail


Your Ayurveda Resort

☀ In the south of Sri Lanka, close to Tallala near Matara, far away from the main tourist crowds.
Surya Lanka Ayurveda Beach Resort
, right at the beautiful sand beach.

How You Reside

35 spacious rooms in 4 separate two-storey buildings with shower / WC, blow-dryer, mosquito net, terrace /balcony, garden and / or ocean view, simple but cosy with natural materials and wood furnishings, airy and at a pleasant temperature thanks to fans. Air conditioning, telephone and TV were deliberately omitted. Safe at the reception. Wi-Fi and Internet computer.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

Ayurvedic buffet meals
are mostly eaten outdoors. Non-vegetarian dishes on request and after consultation with the doctor. Alcohol and smoking are unwanted in the living area.
Big swimming pool in the nice garden.

Wellness, Spa & Health

The Surya Lanka is one of the few spas in Sri Lanka that perform Ayurveda according to the classic original sources - as recommended by the "Ashtanga Samhita", the oldest Ayurvedic scripture from the 6th Century BC.

The qualified Ayurveda doctors and therapists are specially trained to deal with European guests. 12 treatment rooms for treatments of the highest quality such as head, face, Abhyanga, synchronous and thermal massages, herbal and flower baths.

A German speaking staff member or an interpreter is usually on site.

Good to know
