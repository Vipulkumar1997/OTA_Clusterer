Treat yourself to a short escape!
Do you desperately need to escape but can't get the time off work for a long holiday?
SpaDreams offers a huge range of short escapes in breathtaking environments, perfect for regaining energy and recharging your batteries. Be pampered with massages, peelings or medical wellness treatments . Indulge in sensational spa treatments in luxury hotels and enjoy delicious, healthy cuisine prepared for you by world-class chefs for surprisingly low prices.
Aside from classic spa breaks, we have a wide variety of themed short escapes for you to choose from. Get active with one of our fitness weekends - with yoga, sports classes and gyms with top-quality equipment available, you'll be able to release plenty of pent up energy and return home feeling refreshed and revitalised.
Our romantic weekends are the perfect short escape for any couple searching for some time to themselves. With sensual  spa treatments, stunning locations, champagne bubble baths, our romantic short escapes are a wonderful way to treat your partner.
Solo travellers will also find their ideal short escape amongst our offers. Many of our resorts offer group activities and fantastic ways to get to know other solo travellers, so that you won't be alone during your wellness break.
