Coco Lagoon by Great Mount Resort in detail


Your Holiday Destination

☀
Pollachi
is located in the south of India, near Coimbatore and due to its proximity to the Western Ghats mountain range it offers a pleasant, tropical climate. And due to the southwest and the northeast Monun it also offers a lush natural landscape.

Your Hotel

Built in a fusion of classic and modern architecture (opened in the September 2013) the
Coco Lagoon by Great Mount Resort
is peacefully located in a nearly 3-acre coconut plantation. The beautiful, park-like garden with a lagoon offers retreat and recreation.

How You Reside

All 32 spacious
rooms
at the resort are furnished comfortably and elegantly and have a bath or shower / WC, hairdryer, air conditioning, safe, mini bar, coffee and tea making facilities, flat screen satellite TV, overlooking the lagoon and the water fountain. 20 Executive Deluxe Room (approx. 23 sq. m.) which are located in five bungalows, spread out in the western part of the resort. Rooms are on the 1st floor with balcony. 5 Royal Suites (1st floor, about 51 sq. m.) and 5 Presidential Suites with separate living room (ground floor, about 51 sq. m.) and 1 Premier Garden Villa with Jacuzzi and private garden, as well as an Emperor Pool Villa (approx. 84 sq. m.) with additional private pool located on the eastern side of the resort. Rooms for disabled guests are available on request.
Single rooms are double rooms
for single occupancy.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

For Ayurveda guests there is a separate Ayurveda
Restaurant
which serves individual ayurvedic and vegetarian menus according to the dosha type as the Ayurveda specialists recommend. The restaurant "The Palm" offers international cuisine and a coffee shop in the evening.
Beautiful "Blue Bay" infinity swimming pool overlooking the lagoon. Amphitheatre for theatrical acts or musicals, games room and modern fitness centre.

Ayurveda & Health

Vaidya Sutras,
the International
Ayurveda & Wellness Centre
of the Lagoon Coco, orientates itself by the well-known family of Ayurveda Vaidyaratnam (Ayurvedic physicians) and invites the guest to an authentic and pleasurable Ayurveda combined with an upscale atmosphere. Ayurvedic medicines are manufactured by the Vaidyaratnam group itself. In six therapy rooms an experienced Ayurvedic physician and his team are taking care of you. They are well-trained by the Vaidyaratnam family therapists how to care about the welfare of the guest. Yoga and meditation are also available.

Leisure time:
The hotel organizes excursions to the temple of contemplation at the foothills of the Western Ghats to the Vaidyaratnam Ayurvedic medicine factory or to the Valparai, a hill station in the Anamalai mountains around 1.000 meters high and a paradise for nature lovers. Also worth a visit is a trip to the Temple Dhyanalinga Yoga or the Hindu Temple Perur Pateeswarar.

Additional information

ARRIVAL
Cochin airport / Kochi (COK)
, about 130 km or
Coimbatore
(CJB), about 45 km.
