Hod Hamidbar Resort & Spa Hotel in detail


Your Hotel

☀ This fully air-conditioned hotel with its cultivated beach and a pleasant atmosphere is located to the north end of Ein Bokek. This is about 100 km away from Jerusalem. The hotel is very popular amongst German guests and frequently visited by them.

Just the pure view on the bizarre landscape of the Dead Sea with its extraordinary lighting conditions and colours is pure recreation and wellness. Here you can let go your everyday concerns and grind and do something really well and healthy for your body and soul alike with the unique natural salvation means of this region. A German speaking contact person is available in the hotel. Knowledge of the English language is recommended and beneficial.

There is a special animation programme (depending on the season) with for example music or dance evenings. Conference rooms are ready for our guests and so is the lobby with its Wi-Fi and Internet PCs.

How You Reside

210
rooms
are modernly and comfortably designed and each have a bath/ a shower/ a WC, a hair dryer, a safe, a radio, a TV, a telephone, Wi-Fi, an air conditioning, either a coffee or a tea maker, a mini bar with a fridge and a splendid view on the Dead Sea from the side. Superior rooms have been designed as family rooms. A bathing robe is also available after an advance announcement. Non-smoker rooms are also on site.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

Restaurant
(buffet) with international and typical native cuisine and a view on the Dead Sea. Lobby with stay areas and a terrace. Here you will enjoy the splendid sunsets in the evenings with its extraordinary color games and in front of a unique landscape setting with mountains and the sea. A bar, a coffee- shop and a juice bar, a disco, a snack bar by the pool are also available.

Wellness, Spa, & Health

There is s fresh water swimming pool with a panorama view and a beautiful beach, both with sunbeds, umbrellas and a drinking water spring. There is also a pool for children and a nature solarium on the roof. A bike rental outlet is also on site.

In the
Mayan Spa
you can relax and be pampered as long as you wish. The indoor pool is filled with water from the Dead Sea, which is rich in minerals(heated). There is also a brimstone pool, a whirl pool and a sauna. Multisided treatments for health, wellness and beauty such as for example massages (also Ayurveda, Thai, Shiatsu), reflex zone treatments, packages with mud from the Dead Sea, algae and Aloe Vera, aroma oil baths, hydro therapy, peelings, facials, fitness equipment and fitness coach await you. Entrance to the Spa is allowed from an age of 16 years.

Additional information

ARRIVAL
by plane to Tel Aviv
(TLV)

INFOS

Good to know

Climate cure in the German Medical Centre GMC

The hospital of the
German Medical Centre GMC
(opened from Sun. to Fri.) is specialised on high class, effective therapies for the salvation of s
kin diseases, respiratory system diseases
and
rheumatism.
The GMC is located in your hotel and is as a
stationary rehabilitation
facility after § 107 SGB (Code of Social Law in Germany) article V with a utility supply contract and a refund arrangement after § 111 SGB (Code of Social Law in Germany) article V approved. With
natural healing methods
under application of regional specific resources (such as water from the sea, salt, sea mud and air) there are
German speaking medics
and specialists in the specialist fields of dermatology, rheumatology as well as pneumology, who will care for you and your well being for the sake of your enduring healing process. In addition, there is all day a
medical emergency rescue
service
placed at the disposal of our guests.

Recommendations

After consulting your doctor you should stop taking any medicaments in sufficient time before the start of your cure, if they are not required at all costs. Cream with cortisone should only be used up to 4 weeks before the beginning of the cure. Pills with cortisone should be taken steadily up to the consultation and examination by our medical staff at the beginning of the cure. Please do bring a detailed report of your medical record from your doctor with you to the initial examination and consultation by our medical staff at the beginning of your cure. Furthermore, please do bring any medication you use and, in case you have them, any diet plans with you to the consultation as well.
