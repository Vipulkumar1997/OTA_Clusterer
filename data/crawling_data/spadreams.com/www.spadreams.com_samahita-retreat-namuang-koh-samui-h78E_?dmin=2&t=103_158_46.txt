Samahita Retreat in detail


Your Hotel

☀ A beautiful, authentic, eco-friendly Yoga retreat, lying directly on the quiet, fine sand beach in the south of the island of Koh Samui, just 18km from the airport. Samahita means ‘balance’ in Sanskrit. Cosy atmosphere, environmentally friendly design, a place of calm and relaxation, non-smoking and alcohol free.

How You Reside

40 modern rooms (Queensize beds), most with a sea view , balcony or terrace, air conditioning, fan, fridge, safe, kettle and hairdryer. 20 superior rooms (around 35qm), 6 semi rooms (two rooms, each 25qm, which share a bath), 2 share two-bedroom lofts with two sleeping areas and a bath (around 50 qm)

Description of available rooms
Dining & Entertainment

Sattva Dining Samahita Yoha full board, with brunch and dinner from the buffet, vegetarian, Sattvik, fish, eggs (no meat), vegan, raw food, international and Thai cuisine. Juicebar and boutique.


Yoga & Spa Centre

With Yoga experts for Pranayama, classic Hatha and Ashtanga Yoga. Ideal for both beginners and advanced Yoga goers. Ayurveda therapies, soothing massages, hydro-colon therapy, individual coaching, infrared sauna, wraps and peelings. Yoga Shala, meditation garden with Bodhi tree, lotus pond, open lounge, saltwater pool, herbal steam bath, children’s play area and shops.

Leisure

Attractions such as Laem Sor Pagoda 2km away, Na Mueang waterfall, 6.5km

Additional information

Flight Arrivals: to Koh Samui (USM). Private transfer (around 18km). Info: Yoga training and child discount on request.
