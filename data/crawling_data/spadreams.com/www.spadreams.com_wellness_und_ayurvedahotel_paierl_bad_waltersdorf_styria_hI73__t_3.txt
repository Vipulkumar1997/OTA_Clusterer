Wellness- und Ayurvedahotel Paierl in detail


Your Hotel

☀ Enjoy
original Ayurveda in Bad Waltersdorf
. The Hotel Thermenhof Paierl is surrounded by an enchanting landscape of vineyards, sparkling rivers and villages. In the wine country and spa you can leave all your cares behind and go cycling, hiking and golfing. The hotel's healing thermal water is used to relieve symptoms and for health care and regeneration. The healing effect of the thermal water complements the Ayurvedic treatments which are offered in the
Shiva Ayurvedic Centre
.

How You Reside

66 modern, ecologically-built rooms with high-quality wood parquet - overlooking the golf course, spa or vineyard, with shower or bath / WC, hairdryer, satellite TV, telephone, Wi-Fi. "
Isabell
" (25 sqm) without a balcony. "
Welsch
" partly with connecting door, balcony. "
Tramin
" (31 sq.m.) separate WC, walk- in closet. "
Wabi
" access to the Asian garden on the room terrace. Single rooms are double rooms with single occupancy. "
Sabi
" (45 sqm) with living area, whirlpool tub. Internet terminal in the lobby. Lift.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

Styrian
restaurant
with culinary delights from the local region and Asian - Ayurvedic cooking. Paierl's gourmet full board. Breakfast buffet, lunch buffet, soup, salad bar, 2 main dishes, dish from the wok, dessert, (also on departure day) dinner or buffet in the evening - products are from the farmers of the region, some organically grown.
Ayurvedic
, vegetarian, lactose and gluten free meals are also offered. Special diets are available on request. Comfortable hotel lobby with bar, wine cellar, Zen garden with a tea house, on Tuesdays and Fridays live music in the bar.

The remedy

Sodium bicarbonate-chloride thermal bath (28 to 36°C) is beneficial and good against rheumatism, for stress reduction and burnout prevention. Relaxes and strengthens muscles, tightens skin and connective tissues and activates the self-healing powers and the body's defences.

Wellness, Spa & Health

Thermal & sauna world
on 1980 sqm, with indoor thermal water pool and panoramic pool (32°C), thermal hydrotherapy pool in the sauna area (36°C) - Therme only for hotel guests (bathing area: 7 a.m. - 10 p.m., sauna bereich: 9 a.m. - 10 a.m., on departure day the bathing area is available until 5 p.m.), Outdoor swimming pool (28°C year-round) with lawn, minerals, steam bath, infrared cabin, Styrian sweating cabin, Celtic sauna with nudist garden, ice grotto, fountain with Grander water, solarium, aqua relaxation meditation oasis, cardio fitness room.

Realm of the senses
- the home of massages and beauty treatments. Classical and Oriental massages such as partial or full body massages, special massages (hot stone, aromatherapy massage, couples massage, reflexology, lymphatic drainage, energy - balancing massage, cranio sacral treatments, Breuss massage and much more), as well as wraps and baths. Cosmetic treatments using Sothys, Maria Galland and Cosmeceutics .

The
Shiva Ayurvedic Centre
includes a team of Ayurvedic doctors and therapists from South India. For the treatments only Ayurvedic products of highest quality are used which are produced according to centuries-old traditions. The following Ayurvedic treatments are offered:
back & spine
from 5 nights,
anti-stress and Swastha
(intensive detoxifying) cure from 7 nights,
Panchakarma
from 14 nights and
Langhana cure
(for weight reduction) for 21 nights.

Ayurvedic treatments
: scalp massage (for problems like stress and migraine), facial massage (for stress and eyestrain, reduces dark circles and counteracts the aging process of the skin), foot massage (for foot pain, cold feet), relaxation massage (regulates blood circulation and relaxes the muscles), intensive massage (for arthritis and joint pain), inhalation (clearing of the respiratory tract and sinuses), steam bath (regulates blood circulation and detoxifies the body), herbal bath (effective against skin diseases and irritations, detoxifies the body and relaxes the muscles), Nasya (intensive inhalation for clearing the respiratory tract, effective against headaches).
The Ayurvedic philosophy in the Thermenhof Paierl is to make Ayurveda accessible to all people to provide new vitality and energy for their body, mind and soul.
"Man is the focus here."
Active & leisure programme: Yoga, Tai Chi, Qi Gong, pilates, water aerobics (especially for the spine) in the beautiful natural scenery of Eastern Styria round out the experience.

Good to know

Colourful and authentic Ayurveda in Styria

Ayurveda is a holistic natural healing method with the aim to rid the body of toxins and waste materials in order to strengthen the immune system, to activate self-healing powers and to ensure greater mobility as well as peace and stability. You feel healthy and rejuvenated.

The Ayurvedic offers include everything from single treatments through intensive purifications up to several weeks of Ayurveda Panchakarma and Langhana cure for intense weight loss.

Ayurveda doctors with a university degree, a spa physician, professional Ayurvedic therapists with years of experience and Ayurvedic cooks take care of your physical well-being.

In addition to the soothing Ayurvedic treatments, you can enjoy and let yourself be pampered by the Styrian cordiality.
