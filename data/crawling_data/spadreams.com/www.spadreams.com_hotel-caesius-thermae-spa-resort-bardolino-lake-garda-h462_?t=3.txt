Hotel Caesius Thermae & Spa Resort in detail



☀ In the North of Italy, in the region of Veneto, you will find Bardolino, a place famous for its wine regions. Not even 3km south lies the tranquil holiday destination of Cisano. This impressive location lies directly on Lake Garda with good bathing opportunities, tremendous local sights and countless olive groves.

Your Wellness Hotel

Embedded in a magnificent 40,000qm park and garden area, you will find the Hotel Caesius Thermae & Spa Resort (fully air conditioned). The elegant hotel consists of five buildings and is separated from Lake Garda by only a street. To the centre of Bardolino, it is just 1.8km away- a 10 minute walk along the Lake promenade or with the hotel shuttle.


How You Reside

185 rooms and suites. Air conditioning, bath or shower, toilet, TV, telephone, safe, minibar, tea and coffee making facilities, balcony or terrace. Comfort (around 21qm) with a view of the mountains or of Lake Garda. Family suites (around 45qm, not in the main building) with a separate living and sleeping area. Single rooms are double rooms for single use. Lift. (Building with suites has no lift).


Description of available rooms
Restaurant

Main Restaurant ‚Benacus‘ with inernational and regional specialities. Breakfast buffet, lunchtime and evening meals à la carte. Ayurvedic, dieting, vegetarian and gluten free food, also available. Light menus. Gourmet restaurant ‘Le Vele’ (summer). Bar, pool bar, spacious common areas and terraces.


Wellness & Spa

3000qm area (daily 9am-7pm, sauna area 2pm-8pm, from 16 years). Inside pool (around 200qm), whirlpool, saunas, steam bath, Tepidarium, sensorary shower, plunge pool, ice bath and quiet area with juices. For a fee: Solarium and private wellness area for 2-4 people (amongst other things with a Hamam). Massages, manicures, pedicures, anti-aging treatments, Shiatsu, Rasul, lymph drainage, aesthetic medicine (laser, peelings, hyaluronic acid) and much more. Fitness area (around 100qm), personal trainer, morning aerobics.


Cure Area

Thermal centre with its own mineral sources, medical baths, 3 heatable thermal baths, Kneipp path, mud packs, inhalation (also for children), climate cures, physiotherapy, kinetic therapy, 3 doctor’s practices, nutritional advice.


Ayurveda Medicine Centre

An Ayurveda centre equipped to a high quality in Italy. Ayurveda doctor and therapists with training in India, medical Ayurveda examination with a pulse diagnosis, treatments depending on the Dosha for example, oil massages, forehead oil flow (Shirodara), dry massage (Udvartana), herbal steam baths (Swedana) etc.


Free Time

In the hotel area there are five pools, including two children’s pools and 1 whirlpool, loungers and parasols, snackbar. Guided walks through the wine areas and olive groves, guided bike tours, aqua aerobics. Music evenings. Boat trips (middle May-end of September, depending on availability). Tennis courts (from 29/5, depending on availability) in the Residence Arca (around 3km). For a fee: bike hire and excursions. 9 and 18 hole golf courses (around 7-52km), sometimes with a reduced green fee.
