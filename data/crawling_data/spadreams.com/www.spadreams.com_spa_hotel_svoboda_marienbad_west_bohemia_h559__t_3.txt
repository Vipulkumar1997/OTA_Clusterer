Spa Hotel Svoboda in detail


Your Hotel

☀ The Danubius Spa Hotel Svoboda consists of 3 buildings: Svoboda, Margareta and Palladio in a quiet park not far from the Singing Fountain. Houses are interconnected via a corridor.

How You Reside

99 non-smoking rooms with bath or shower, WC, Satellite-TV, minibar, telephone. Simple Standard Rooms come with a shower in Margareta. Renovated Comfort Rooms in Svoboda with bath, hairdryer, safe and park view. Suites on request. Wi-Fi in the Lobby-Bar, reception and in the lounge. Internet Corner.

Description of available rooms
Dining

Carlton Restaurant with stylish ambience. Breakfast and dinner buffets. Regional and international Cuisine, also dietary meals. Common Room with TV, Library, Billiards and Children’s Corner. Lobby Bar.

Wellness, Spa and Health

Palladio Health Centre & Spa: mineral baths from the hotel’s own forest-source, dry CO2 gas-baths and injections, paraffin- and mud packs, various massages, electro, magnetic field, and cryotherapy, Lavatherm and rehabilitation, tai-chi, drinking cures, 24 h medical stand-by.

GOLF 18-hole Marienbad Royal Golf Club approx. 5 km away.c
