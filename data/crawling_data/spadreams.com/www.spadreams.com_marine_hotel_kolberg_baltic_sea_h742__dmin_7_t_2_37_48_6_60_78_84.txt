Marine Hotel in detail


Your Location

☀ Ever since the 19th century, the Polish seaside resort of Kolobrzeg on the Baltic Sea has been attracting masses of spa guests due to not only the fresh, iodine and iron rich sea air but also due to the salt mineral springs and the healing power of the region’s moor. Today, the seaside resort ranks as one of the best-known spa locations in Poland, boasting six kilometres of fine sand beaches and a large spa garden.

Your Hotel

Located in a quiet area right by the sea with extraordinary architecture, the luxurious hotel complex belongs to the most modern and comfortable spa hotels in Poland. The whole hotel is non-smoking and is connected to the neighbouring Hotel Ultra Marine. The resort centre is located roughly 3 km from the hotel.

How You Reside

231 exclusive rooms and apartments with lateral sea view and equipped with shower/WC, hair dryer, bathing robe, air conditioning, telephone, satellite TV (flat screen), mini-bar, safe and Wi-Fi. Standard (ca 24-35 sqm), Apt. Luxury with either lounge and bedroom and a French balcony on one floor, or on two floors with no balcony (ca. 31-44 sqm), Apt. Luxury with terrace on two floors (ca. 50-53 sqm., terrace ca. 10 sqm.) There is also a lift to all floors. Game room.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

In the PerMare Restaurant you can enjoy traditional Polish and international cuisine. Breakfast buffet, lunch or dinner as buffet, for dinner also à la carte possible (for a fee). Vegetarian fare is available. In addition there is a terrace with sea view, roof café with its own panoramic terrace, lobby bar, bowling alley and night club. Play ground.

Wellness, Spa & Health

Vital World Spa with swimming pool and underwater music, relaxation pool, outdoor whirlpool, sauna, steam and aroma bath, tepidarium, ice and water grotto. INE Wellness & Medical Spa with diverse offers of wellness and classic spa treatments such as standard and hydro massages, cryotherapy (cold therapy), ultra-sound and inhalations. There are also a hair dresser, fitness and gymnastics rooms as well as a full spa programme including Pilates and yoga available. The hotel also has bicycles and Nordic walking sticks for rent.
