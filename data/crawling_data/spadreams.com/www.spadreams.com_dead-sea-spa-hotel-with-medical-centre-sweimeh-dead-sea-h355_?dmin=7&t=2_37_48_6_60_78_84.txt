Dead Sea Spa Hotel with Medical Centre in detail


Your Hotel

☀ The
Dead Sea Hotel
lies at the lowest point of the Earth together with the
Dead Sea Medical Center,
a specialised hospital for skin and joint pains. It is located on the north eastern bank of the Dead Sea, surrounded by sublime mountain ranges and untouched nature. The neighbouring city of Amman is also a popular day trip destination and weekend relaxation resort for Jordanians and round trip groups.

How You Reside

The site is vast and open with a sea view. In the main building and in 1-3 floor bunglows there are 277
rooms
(ca. 18 sq.m.), mostly refurbished, with bath or shower/ WC, a hair dryer, a mini bar, a water boiler, a safe, a satellite TV (German channels), a telephone, an air conditioner/ radiator (from Dec. to Feb.) and a balcony or terrace. Bigger
deluxe rooms
(ca. 35 sq.m.) in the new wing. Also rooms with connecting doors. 1 disabled-friendly deluxe room, the site has limited access for disabled guests. Bathrobe (reservation needed when booking). Lift.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

In the main building there is a reception area and lobby,
restaurant
(buffet) with a coffe shop. Pub/Sportsbar with international and Arabic à la carte cuisine. Occasional evening entertainment such as belly dancing, live music. WiFi in the hotel.

Wellness, Spa & Health

Own
beach
as well as fresh water pool, in the aqua fun centre with water slide and water mushroom (daily open). Beach bar, pool bar. Shuttle from the hotel to Amman (depending on the season, approx. 20 JD/ approx. € 20 per person and way). Big fitness room.

The Dead Sea Medical Centre
(open daily) is integrated into the hotel complex, with experienced dermatologists. A doctor or a nurse are available for emergencies at any time – even when the centre is closed.

Dermatological treatment rooms, rooms for medical, physical and psychological care, massages, under water massages, mud packages, ultrasonic sound, short wave, micro wave, cryo therapy (freeze).
Heated indoor therapy pool with water from the Dead Sea, fitness room, cosmetic section, emergency room and pharmacy.

A natural solarium (separate areas for ladies and gentlemen) directly on the beach with a sea water pool, sunbeds, showers, fans, sun umbrellas and fridges as well as daily fresh mud from the Dead Sea. Nude bathing is not permitted in areas other than the solarium area.

Good to know

Desire for sun and light?
Light therapy at the Black Sea, the natural health Spa.
The special light circumstances on the Dead Sea improve the well-being of our guests.

Dive in into a sea full of light, colours and warmth.

Experience Jordan

Explore the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, from the "rosa red“ Petra to the mysterious "desert castles“. Booking of day trips and cars via the hotel.

Individual round trip to the rock city Petra & Wadi Rum
- ideal subsequently to your stay on the Dead Sea. Details on request via SpaDreams.

Climatic treatments on the Dead Sea

Book a climatic cure stay in the Dead Sea Medical Centre for 1-4 weeks. Treatment programmes include:


Initial medical consultation with measurement of blood pressure and weight. Anamnesis by a GP and/or a dermatologist

Creation of an individual therapy plan

Medical assistance and round (2x weekly, if requested daily by a GP and/or dermatologist)

If requested a psychological consultation

Medicine and health care products by doctor's prescription and according to your cure therapy

Therapeutic treatment (if necessary, depending on your cure programme) such as aqua or medical gymnastics, mud wrap, massage, cryotherapy (cold therapy) and more

Final report

Use of the nature solarium and fresh mud from the Dead Sea, in the Medical Centre use of pool with water from the Dead Sea and fitness centre

Emergency service (24 hours) by a doctor or nurse available


Available treatment programmes
on request (bookable for 1, 2, 3 or 4 weeks): Psoriasis, vitiligo & acne, nerodermatitis, psoriasis arthropatica, rheumatism, uveitis, uveitis & Bekhterev syndrome.
