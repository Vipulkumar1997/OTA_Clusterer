Welcome to Germany!
A number of charm
ing hotels await you in Germany, our best destination for
therapeutic fasting
.
Germany is a
conglomerate of cultures
, from the rural charms of Bavaria to the dynamic streets of Berlin: d
iscover enchanting, traditional spa towns in the south or
vibrant, modern cities
scattered throughout the country in between stretches of
bewitching forests
and snow dusted mountains. Whether you would like to indulge yourself with
spa treatments
, improve your
fitness in the mountains
or detoxify your body with therapeutic fasting, you'll find your ideal hotel amongst our offers in Germany.
Home to some of the
most romantic spots in the world
, you'll find an abundance of well-preserved, quaint, historical towns and villages nestled in between the
magnificent mountains
of Bavaria and the green hills of Hessen. Many
spa hotels
in Germany offer traditional treatments that utilise natural remedies such as alpine herbals, peat, sea salt or mineral waters for an essential wellness experience.
Our spa hotels by the North Sea or by the Baltic Sea in the north of Germany are ideal for lovers of fresh sea air. If you would like to combine your spa stay with interesting excursions, take a trip to the islands of Juist or Rügen. More easily accessible destinations are scattered along the coast.
Health spas in Germany offer not only effective treatments but also delicious healthy cuisine. If you are thinking of a taking
detox holiday
there is no better destination than Germany. Choose from fasting according to
O. Buchinger
,
F.X. Mayr
,
J. Schroth
or
S. Kneipp
to take the first steps on your journey to
a new, healthier you.
Germany is also the perfect choice for
active holidays
. Numerous
hiking
and walking trails, as well as
scenic natural landscapes
await you! Re-connect with nature and relieve yourself of stress with an active leisure break in Germany.
Visit the region of
Oberstaufen
for a refreshing holiday in the heart of the
Bavarian Alps
, with exciting outdoor activities year-round. The area offers natural beauty as well as numerous
cultural and historical attractions
to entice guests - visit the tourist highlights of
Neuschwanstein Castle
, or take a day trip to the beautiful
Lake Constance
.
Contact our customer service team today and find your perfect break in Germany!
