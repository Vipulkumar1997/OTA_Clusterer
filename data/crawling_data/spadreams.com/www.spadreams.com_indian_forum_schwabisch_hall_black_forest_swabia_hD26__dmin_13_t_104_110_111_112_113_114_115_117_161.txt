Indian Forum in detail


Your Ayurvedic House

☀ The Indian Forum in Schwäbisch Hall sees itself as an Indian cultural center, as an ambassador of Indian culture and hospitality in the midst of Germany.

In the art and culture Schwäbisch Hall located and housed in a lovingly restored century villa at the entrance of the pedestrian area, the Indian Forum invites to experience authentic Indian way of life and inspiration. Visitors will find original Indian food, and a varied spa, massage and courses offered by the Ayurveda institute. In addition to the Ayurvedic treatments, yoga classes and the wide range of natural medicine practice also throughout the year a multi-faceted artistic and cultural program will be offered.

How You Reside

2 Ayurvedic just and functional rooms with shower / toilet, Wi-Fi, hair dryer and cooler.

Description of available rooms
Food & Beverages

Restaurant Gandhi with balanced meals from mostly local ingredients on the ground floor. All meals individually by regulation. The vegetarian breakfast is served, vegetarian lunch buffet, a small hot dish like a soup and some bread. All day tea is offered.

Ayurveda & Health

In the two upper floors of the Indian Forum is an Ayurvedic teaching and training institute with an attached clinic. There are 3 practice rooms, consultation room, steam room and relaxation room.
Authentic, Indian Ayurveda is offered by 2 experienced Ayurvedic physicians and 4 trained experienced specialists.

Treatments including massage (Klass. Whole body, partial body massage, Shirodhara forehead oil, Punnagadi Kizhi Herbal Pouch Massage, Pinda Sweda, Abhyanga, body wraps, peelings and facials. Ayurvedic treatments are ideal for the following indications: migraine, back pain, skin diseases, allergies, rheumatic diseases, arthritis, burnout, stress, fatigue, diabetes, eczema, inflammation.

Yoga and meditation
for relaxation and activation of self-healing powers of the body.
