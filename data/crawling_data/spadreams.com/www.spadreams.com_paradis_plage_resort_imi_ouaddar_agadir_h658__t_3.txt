Paradis Plage Resort in detail


Your Hotel

☀ The Paradis Plage Resort stretches over 3 hectares directly on a 5 km stretch of sandy beach. Morocco's first surf yoga & spa resort, about 27 km north of Agadir (shuttle transfer twice a day, pre-booked one day in advance), in picturesque surroundings. The location right on the Atlantic offers ideal conditions for action and wellness lovers. The Fischerdorf Taghazout, also known as a surfspot, is about 7.5 km away.

How You Reside

95 stylish furnished suites with shower/WC, hairdryer, radio, satellite TV, WiFi, air conditioning, safe, minibar and kitchenette. Junior Suites (about 62 sqm) with balcony or terrace, some of them disabled-friendly. Premium suites (about 72 sqm) with separate living- Bedroom, terrace with pool and sea view or garden and sea view. Prestige Suites (about 90 square meters, for 4 full paying guests) each with 2 bedrooms and bathrooms, living room and terrace. 4 two-storey bungalows (about 90 sqm) with separate living- Bedroom, terrace with sea view, 5 villas (approx. 150 sqm, for 6 persons) with 3 bedrooms, two of them with bathroom, terrace and garden.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Entertainment

"The Ocean" main restaurant offers buffet (half-board) in the morning and in the evening. Also vegetarian and diet meals. "Le Lounge" with a beach view serves regional pastries, smoothies and cocktails. Sea specialties and gourmet chefs in the "Chiringuito Beach Restaurant", cocktails in the accompanying bar with camp fire. À-la-carte restaurant "Le 27" with Mediterranean cuisine and seafood.

Wellness & Spa

Solar heated outdoor pool with sunbeds and umbrellas. Let yourself be pampered in the Paradise Spa (about 300 square meters) with local products with natural ingredients such as eucalyptus, argan oil and honey. Indoor and outdoor jacuzzi, traditional and modern hammam, zen area, light therapy, several treatment rooms and massage pavilion directly on the beach. Wellness and beauty treatments such as whole body and partial body, shiatsu, chocolate and hydromassage, body peeling and wrap, facial treatments, manicure and pedicure. Indoor and outdoor fitness area. Yoga Shala with yoga room on the beach and certified yoga teachers, various yoga styles (including Hatha, Vinyasa Flow, Yin, Manipura). Private yoga lessons (about € 36 per hour).

Leisure

Surfhouse (about 300 sqm) directly on the beach with bar, lounge, large terrace and integrated surf school. Surf lessons for beginners and advanced. Surfboard rental (about € 27 per day). On request half-day trips with surfguide. Rental of bicycles and stand-up paddles upon availability. Beach volleyball, slackline, beach football, open-air cinema. Excursion possibilities e.g. to the Sahara, to the national park in Souss Massa or to Agadir and to the Kasbah fortress with fantastic views. Children's club (4-11,9 years, seasonal), baby sitter on request (approx. € 10 / hour, nightly transport surcharge). Golf: Two 9-hole golf courses and an 18-hole golf course, about 7 km away. Golf packages on request.
