Hotel Botánico & The Oriental Spa Garden in detail


Your Hotel

☀ The beautiful hotel Botánico lies peacefully on the edge of Puerto de la Cruz in the La Paz villa quarter at the foot of Mt Teide and surrounded by a spacious,subtropical park area (ca 25,000 sqm). The hotel is located in the immediate vicinity of the Botanical Garden and is also a mere 1.5 km from the center of town, the seawater pools and the “Playa Martiánez” with its famous, black lava beach. The hotel provides a complementary shuttle bus running all day.

How You Reside

252
rooms
and suites located on 5 storeys with either a sea view, view of the garden or a view of the volcano Teide, with a bath or shower/WC, hair dryer, bath robe, individually controllable air conditioning, mini-bar, satellite TV, telephone, Wi-Fi, safe and either a terrace or balcony. The Deluxe Rooms are located on floors 1 to 4 (ca 35 sqm) with accommodation for 2 people as are the Ambassador Suites (ca 50 sqm) with living area for up to 3 people. Also available are non-smoking rooms and interconnected rooms for 2 adults and 2 children.
Single rooms are double rooms
for single occupancy. The hotel has 3 lifts.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

A breakfast buffet and an à la carte 3-course menu in the evenings is included with half board accommodation with the option available to enjoy an evening in one of the
speciality restaurants
, which include Asian, Spanish/Mediterranean and Italian cuisine (all non-smoking). A kids' menu is available as is the possibility of an individualised diet programme which you can request in advance. Ayurvedic cuisine upon request. After 6pm, you are discouraged from wearing shorts, sports- and bathingwear within public areas and in the restaurants. In addition, gentlemen are asked to wear a collared shirt in the Oriental Restaurant, with a jacket also to be worn between the months of October and April.

The hotel also has a foyer, bar, live music performances, pool bar, luxury boutique, business center and car rental. A babysitter is available on request.

Wellness, Spa & Health

Heated outdoor and kids' pools as well as 2 Astro Turf tennis courts with quartz sand and equipped with flood lights. A diving school is located nearby.

Botánico Adventure Academy for Children
(4 - 15 years)
, offering a special education and games progamme in cooperation with the
Loro Parque.
Available daily between 1.7. and 31.8 (Mon. - Sun.). Further details including prices available on request.

“The Oriental Spa Garden”
measuring approx. 3500 sqm, €35 (£30) per day (over 16yrs only). The spa area has 20 treatment rooms, indoor and climatised outdoor pool with hydromassage, outdoor whirlpool, professionally equipped gym, fitness classes, Japanese sauna, Turkish bath, Laconium, ice grotto, experience showers, aroma therapy area and quiet zones with water beds. For a surcharge you can also have your own personal trainer or an exclusive spa experience for couples, beauty treatments, Oriental open-air massages in the pagoda such as Shiatsu, Ayurveda, Thai or Reiki, hydromassages and use of the baths. There is also a hairdresser additionally offering manicures and pedicures. The products used come from Thalgo.

GOLF The hotel has its own putting green measuring approx. 5000 sqm complete with bunker. El Peñon Golf Club is ca 15 minutes from the hotel. Buenavista 18 hole Golf Course is about 40 minutes. Green Fee reductions available.

Additional information

AIRCRAFT To Tenerife North (TFN). Transfer (back and forth): Taxi transfer (approx. 20 km) on request.

To Tenerife South (TFS). Transfer (back and forth): transfer (approx. 100 km) or private transfer on request. All flight prices daily via CRS or on request at FIT.

INFO Parking: incl. Child reduction: 2 adults + 1 or 2 children (0-1,9 years) 100%, (2-15,9 years) 50%. Dogs: approx. € 15 / day plus final cleaning approx. € 50 (small to max. 8 kg).

Good to know

Hiking Paradise Tenerife

About 10 bookable guided hikes per week with various levels of difficulty, which could include treks up Mount Teide, through the Masca Gorge or through the Erica and Lorbeer Forests, all with wonderful views. As an example, a 3 day hiking package is available for ca €120 (£104) and bookable through the hotel.
