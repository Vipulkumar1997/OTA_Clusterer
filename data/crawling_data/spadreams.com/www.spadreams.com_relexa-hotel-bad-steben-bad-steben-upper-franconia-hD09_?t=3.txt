relexa hotel Bad Steben in detail


Your Hotel

☀ Located in the beautiful Frankenwald Nature Park, the hotel welcomes you with its homelike comforts and numerous beauty, wellness, fitness and health facilities that provide year-round recreation. With a large wellness area, beauty farm, doctor's office and a medical bath and massage department, the hotel offers an attractive variety for all ages. If you would like to supplement your health program with sports activities, then you are in the right place: Bad Steben and the surrounding area offer a 26km sign-posted Nordic Walking network, five mountain bike tours, over 100km of hiking trails, clay tennis courts, an 18-hole golf course (in about 28 km) and a shooting range. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel (about 100m) is the spa Bad Steben with 900 square meters of water, sauna, steam room, Kneipp pool, lagoon pool and more.

How You Reside

122 comfortable and tastefully furnished rooms (non-smoking) in the main building or Parkschlösschen. Double room Basic (about 20sqm) with bath or shower, toilet, hairdryer, minibar, radio, cable TV, telephone, WiFi (about €5/day), some with balcony. Single room Basic (about 16 sqm) without balcony with the same equipment. Lift.

Description of available rooms
Dining

3 Restaurants serve fresh seasonal regional specialties and popular international dishes. In the morning a rich breakfast buffet, in the evenings, Sundays to Thursdays, an exquisite 4-course menu, on Fridays a cold-hot specialty buffet and on Saturdays a festive candlelight dinner. At lunchtime optional lunch or dinner. Specialty diet cooks are happy to prepare calorie- and carbohydrate-reduced foods for you. On request also separation, vegetarian, lactose and gluten free food (surcharge) possible. Bar.

Wellness, Spa & Health

1,200 sqm
wellness area
(open daily) with indoor pool (9m x 13m), Finnish sauna, color light / bio sauna, aroma steam bath, waterfall showers, Kneipp basin, relaxation oasis with water beds and saunas.
Whole, partial body, aroma oil massages, Ayurasan Body (gentle full body oil massage after Ayurvedic healing teachings), herbal, algae, Cleopatra baths, peelings and packs with bog, sea salt or aloe vera, Thalgo treatments. Facials after Gertraud Gruber for a fee

Medical practice
(specialist for general and sports medicine, spa doctor, urologist) and medical bath and massage department in the building. Prescriptions from the doctor can be brought. Applications such as lymphatic drainage, heat therapy, mineral and radon baths, Bad Stebener natural peat pack, light, magnetic field, oxygen and ASLAN therapy.

Indications
: Musculoskeletal disorders, rheumatism, osteoporosis, cardiovascular and vascular diseases, diabetes, obesity, fatigue.

New
: e-bike rental incl. Equipment for individual tours through the Franconian Forest (for a fee).

Additional information

TRAIN ARRIVALS: Bad Steben, approx. 1km.
Transfer (there & back): Train Station: Bad Steben included (with pre-reservation).

INFOS Tourist tax: approx. €2.90/day, payable on arrival. Parking: included. Child Discount: by request. Pets: approx. €10/day (without Food), with pre-reservation.

Good to know

Therme Bad Steben - Bavarian Thermal Baths

About 150m from the hotel is the spa Bad Steben (entrance depending on the area about €15 - €29/day, payable locally). Here you can expect the three areas;
Water world
,
sauna area
and
wellness cathedra
l. The water world includes. a large bathing hall, brine outdoor pool, aqua classes. In the sauna area you will find 5 different saunas with hourly infusions, a salt cave and a cozy relaxation area. In the wellness-dome is put on the salutary effect of brine-with brine-floating bath, -Inhalationsgrottte and -Lagune.
