Escape to an exotic paradise
Are you searching for the holiday of a lifetime? Our exotic holidays are your ticket to discovering a new, healthier you in the most unexpected of places. From the shimmering shores of Sri Lanka to mystic Thai temples, the bustling markets of India to the awe-inspiring volcanoes of Indonesia, you can satisfy your craving for adventure from the comfort of a luxury hotel. Elephant rides, jeep safaris, shipwreck snorkelling and much more are available from our stunning hotels world-wide.
Dive into the sparkling, clear waters of the Maldives with a one of a kind scuba and spa experience, relax with a donkey milk bath in the Moroccan mountains, re-energise with exotic therapy in Turkey, be pampered like Cleopatra in Egypt, make a splash in the salty waters of the Dead Se, explore the breath-taking landscapes of Nepal  or simply sun yourself on a beach in Mauritius.
Whether you're looking for an action packed adventure in a distant corner of the earth or a blissful holiday in an oriental paradise, our exotic holidays will fulfil your every desire. Let our expert team advise you and make the exotic holiday of your dreams a reality.
