Indulge in a romantic weekend
Whisk your partner away to a fairytale world of candle-lit dinners, blossoming flower gardens and aromatic oils with a romantic weekend away at one of our luxury spas.
Awaken your senses with couple's massages, treatments for two, champagne bubble baths and much more - take the chance to escape your daily lives and spend quality time together. Whether you're looking for 5 star luxury or a cosy retreat and alone time, you will be sure to find your romantic paradise amongst our vast variety of hotels.
With exclusive adult areas, crystal clear water and pristine white beaches, the 5* Princesa Yaiza Suite Hotel Resort is an enchanting location for couples to enjoy some alone time. The amethyst sauna, blossom and herbs grotto, ice fountain and many sensual spa experiences on offer at the Romantik Seehotel Ahlbecker Hotel make it an ideal destination for couples looking to explore their passion for one another.
We offer a huge variety of alternate destinations for couples who want are more into active leisure than candles and roses, or want to lose weight, detox or fast together. All of our packages including fitness, yoga, walking, nordic walking, golf and much more can be booked for two. Call our customer service team today and plan your romantic weekend!
