Spa Hotel Thermal in detail


Your Spa Hotel

☀ Karlovy Vary is picturesquely situated in a valley at the confluence of the Tepla and the Ohre. The historical place is especially famous for the Karlovy Vary drinking treatment using mineral spring water with temperatures between 30 and 72 °C. The Spa Hotel Thermal (Non-smoking) is located in the center, near the colonnades.

How You Reside

273 modern rooms and suites with bath/WC, telephone, satellite TV, minibar, safe, hairdryer, air conditioning, WiFi, bathrobe, slippers. Comfort rooms with park view, french Balcony. Comfort & City View with city view, balcony. Superior rooms with park view, french Balcony and Superior City View with City View, Balcony Renovated 3.-9. Floor. Comfort Suite with 2 bedrooms and separate living room, french. Balcony. 3 rooms for the disabled. Lift.

Description of available rooms
Dining

National and international cuisine. Breakfast buffet, lunch and dinner buffet. Diet, Reduction and Vegetarian Food. Lobby Bar Movie Café with drinks, desserts and light snacks. Smoker's lounge.

Wellness & Spa

Modern wellness center with indoor pool (16x6m), whirlpool, Kneipp treading pool, Finnish sauna, infrared cabin, steam room and fitness room. Manicure, pedicure. Wellness products ROBEUS. Hair stylist.

Spa Area

Modern spa department (Mon-Sat 7am-7pm). Doctor (Mon.-Fri.) And therapist team. Moor mud and paraffin wrap, salt cave (about €6/45 min.), Chocolate, honey, underwater, reflexology or hot stone massages, whirling or bubble bath, facial masks, laser, scanner, magnetotherapy , Biolamp, solux, quartz lamp, electrotherapy (Myostimulator, Endo-Vaco, BTL-06, Phyaction), ultrasound, lymphatic drainage, dry carbonic acid bath, oxygenotherapy, pneumopuncture, hydrocolon and much more

Indications
: Diseases of the digestive tract (chronic gastric dyspepsia, peptic ulcer of the stomach and duodenum, conditions after gastric surgery, duodenal ulcer and esophagus, functional disease of the intestine, especially chronic constipation, irritable bowel syndrome, chronic inflammatory bowel disease/Crohn's disease, chronic perctocolitis, conditions after small-and large bowel resections, gallbladder and biliary tract disorders, cholelithiasis, biliary dyspepsia, gallbladder and biliary tract conditions, post-acute hepatitis conditions, chronic liver inflammations, fatty liver, compensated cirrhosis, pancreatic disease, post-acute pancreatitis, chronic pancreatitis, conditions after operations and transplants of the salivary gland), disorders in metabolic disorders (obesity, diabetes mellitus/diabetes of the I. and II. type, including diabetic complications, disorders of lipid metabolism/dyslipidemia, disorders of uric acid metabolism/gout), disorders of the musculoskeletal system (chronic pain of the spine functional and degenerative, conditions after spinal surgery, joint pain, arthritis, metabolic diseases of joints/arthritis urica, post-accident conditions) or joint surgery and total joint endoprostheses)

Leisure

Tennis, ice skating, skiing, snowboarding, rock climbing, pétanque and Nordic walking. Numerous cycling routes and hiking trails in the immediate vicinity (SOOS nature reserve). The famous Karlovy Vary colonnades and the famous Grand Hotel Pupp, known by the James Bond movie Casino Royale, are just a few minutes' walk away. Church of St. Mary Magdalene, Russian Orthodox Church of St. Peter and Paul, Diana Lookout Tower, Hirschsprung View, Castle Tower, Carlsbad City Theater, MOSER Glass Museum, Becherovka Museum.
