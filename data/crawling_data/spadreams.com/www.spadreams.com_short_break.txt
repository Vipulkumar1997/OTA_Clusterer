Take a short break
When everything gets too much, sometimes all you need is to take a short break. Unfortunately, our busy schedules do not always give us the chance to take time off when we need it most. SpaDreams short breaks are here to help. Regardless of whether you're staying for a weekend or a week, our partner hotels will give you the wellness experience you deserve!
From luxury spas to nordic walking, yoga retreats to get-fit weekends, SpaDreams can find the perfect short break for you. We have short breaks tailored to suit senior travelers, couples, families and many of our programmes are suitable for those travelling alone. Can't stand the thought of leaving your cat or dog behind for a weekend? We also offer a selection of pet friendly hotels. From city breaks and spa weekends, to island escapes and mountain getaways, browse through an array of destinations and find the perfect place to take a short break without wasting too much time travelling long distances.
