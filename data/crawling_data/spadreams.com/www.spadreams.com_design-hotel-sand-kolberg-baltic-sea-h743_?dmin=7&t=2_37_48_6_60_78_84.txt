Design-Hotel Sand in detail


Your Hotel

☀ The
new design hotel
(for non-smokers) is located in the
Baltic Sea coastal resort Kolberg
(Kolobrzeg), the well known health resort with the unique climate, ca. 150 meters away from the Baltic Sea . The hotel captivates its guests through the extraordinary architecture and its indoor accommodation. It takes about 15 minutes on foot to make your way to the old city centre from the hotel, to the beach it takes about 10 minutes.

How You Reside

148
rooms
with a shower/ a WC, a satellite TV, a telephone, a hair dryer, a bathing robe, a mini bar, a safe, most of them with a terrace or a loggia. Economy rooms (ca. 25- 30 sq.m.), Standard rooms (ca. 30-50 sq.m.), Apartment Plus (ca. 30- 50 sq.m.), Apartment Lux (ca. 50-70 sqm) with a separated living area. Lift.

Description of available rooms
Dining & Leisure

Traditional national and international dishes and also vegetarian cuisine and dishes for diabetic with advance notice are offered by the
restaurant
„Sandacz“, which also has a terrace. Buffet for breakfast and dinner, for dinner à la arte is possible as well (for a fee). Bar, sand club, sun terrace on the roof.

Wellness, Spa & Health

The
Medical SPA & Wellness area
(ca. 600 sq.m.) offers various treatments for cures and wellness such as massages, Ayurveda, Thai massages, inhalations, marsh packages, cold therapies, laser therapies and magnetic field therapies (open from Monday to Saturday). Indoor pool, whirl pool, sauna, steam bath, ice fountain and cardio fitness equippment (daily). For a fee: cure programmes, solarium, rental of sun beds, bike rental and Nordic walking sticks rental.

Beauty studio
with products from, for example, Dermalogica. Hair dresser.

Additional information

ARRIVAL: Stettin-Goleniów (SZZ) Transfer about 95 km, on request.

INFOS

Children (0-5,9 y.) Approx. € 0,50 / day, children from 6 yrs and adults approx. € 0,95 / day, to be paid locally.

up to 5 kg on request.
