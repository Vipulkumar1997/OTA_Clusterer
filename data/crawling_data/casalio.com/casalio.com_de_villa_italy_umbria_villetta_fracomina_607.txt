Villetta Fracomina, Italy, Umbria, Perugia, 1 bedroom, Private Pool, air-conditioner

Villetta Fracomina is a light filled Guest Apartment available for rental when the main house is unoccupied. It is open plan with a mezzanine floor and has a covered loggia with panoramic views over the surrounding countryside. It is located within easy strolling distance of the Centre of the Estate where the Osteria, stables and tennis court are located. Guests staying at Villetta have exclusive use of the 18 x 4.5 m swimming pool, outdoor kitchen and custom built spa bath.

Guest House:
- Open plan sitting room area with doors to covered loggia
- Bedroom zone with spacious shower room and separate w.c. cloakroom
- Small well-equipped kitchen
- Study area with sofa bed on mezzanine floor, TV, DVD
- Guest shower room on mezzanine floor
- Available for 2 guests with possibility of third occasional guest

Private Garden:
- An 18 x 4.5 metre infinity swimming pool
- Irrigated lawn area around pool and spa bath
- Custom built spa bath with variety of seating and beds
- Outdoor kitchen and dining table
- Private entrance and car port
- Gazebo
