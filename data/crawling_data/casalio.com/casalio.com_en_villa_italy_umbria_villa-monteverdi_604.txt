Villa Monteverdi, Italy, Umbria, 3 bedrooms, private pool, air-conditioning
This fascinating 3 bedrooms villa set on the top of the Mount Elceto is a great example of a glorious restoration in the perfect site. From the outstanding position of the Villa is clearly visible. The Villas stands on the ruins of a medieval church.
Finest interior design and a long infinity pool that seems to float on the huge valley make the Villa a very special place for a unforgettable holiday.
The villa is composed of 3 spacious and comfortable en suite bedrooms, one with a romantic poster bed, richly and finely decorated to grant the highest level of elegance!

All the interiors have been taken care of down to the finest details in order to offer the guests a perfect holiday.
