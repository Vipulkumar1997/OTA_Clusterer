Villa Sangallo, Italy, Umria, Pierantonio - 10-18 persons

Villa Sangallo in Umbria in Italy is an elegant luxury villa, set up in the Umbrian hills, offering a peaceful atmosphere, breathtaking views and surely one of the most relaxing atmospheres in Umbria. With its 9 rooms, each individually decorated in warm rich colors, the Villa is an authentic Umbrian jewel offering peace and tranquility. Extensive private grounds surrounding the luxury villa provide privacy, as well as making it ideal for walkers and nature lovers, wishing to explore the surrounding woodland and nature reserve.

The villa sleeps up to 18 people and can be rented as a unique villa or separately in 3 apartments and one luxury romantic suite in the cottage.

Each apartment is furnished and decorated in a luxury style and has its own fully equipped kitchen, dining room, living room and laundry. All the bedrooms have their own individual color schemes and are of a good size with excellent facilities and magnificent views.

Tramonti - 180 sq m - has two king bedrooms, one twin bedroom and 3 bathrooms; sleeps 6 and comfy seating for 12.

Alba - 180 sq m - has 3 twin bedrooms and 2 bathrooms; sleeps 6 and comfy seating for 8.

Olivo- 130 sq m - has 2 king bedrooms and 2 bathrooms; sleeps 4 and comfy seating for 6.

Tiglio - 35 sq m - is an independent luxury suite with ensuite bathroom and all comforts of a modern 5 stars hotel room including frigo-bar.

Each apartment has washing machine / dishwasher / fridge/ lcd TV / DVD player / CD player / ipod speakers/ wireless internet broadband / air conditioning / hairdryer / baby cot and child's high chair / heating.

Amenities: private infinity pool 7mx15m with separate jacuzzi seating 8 people; private fenced luxuriant garden; barbecue; outdoor dining table with pergola; panoramic terraces with outdoor seating facilities;

Cooking classes are available for the guests of the villa.
