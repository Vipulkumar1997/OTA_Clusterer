Villa Laurana, Italy, Umbria, Perugia, 10-14 people, private pool, air-conditioner

Villa Laurana consists of three buildings: Main House, Guest Annex and Pool House. The buildings together with two old mulberry trees form a large courtyard. Villa Laurana is a converted 17th century farmhouse completed in 2002 to the highest standard of renovation and design. Extensive private grounds and generous 360 degree views, a large overflow pool and several outdoor sitting areas is what makes the villa truly unique.

Available as 5 Bedroom (sleeping 10) or as 7 Bedrooms (sleeping 14):

Main House:
- 1 master suite with en-suite bathroom and private terrace on first floor
- 1 large double bedroom on ground floor with en-suite bathroom and direct access to the garden
- 1 double bedroom with en-suite bathroom and access to garden
- 1 double bedroom with bathroom
- 1 large twin bedroom with en-suite bathroom
Annex:
2 individual double bedrooms each with en-suite bathrooms, direct access to garden and large pergola

Other Rooms:
- Large kitchen with separate larder, fully equipped including 6 burner gas cooker, dishwasher and icemaker fridge
- Dining room with fireplace seats 14
- Living room with fireplace and stereo sound system
- Summer room with three French windows and large arched window with direct access to garden, with plasma TV, satellite video, and multiregional DVD player
- Study with fireplace, satellite TV and multiregional DVD player, stereo sound system
- Small TV room with plasma TV, satellite video, and multiregional DVD player
- Laundry room fully equipped with washing machine, dryer and ironing machine

Garden:
- Large outdoor dining table seats 14
- Secluded breakfast table seats 8
- Traditional bread oven (for roasts, grills and pizza)
- Pool house with shower and WC, changing facilities, towel storage
- Heated swimming pool 7.5 x 14 metres with overflow towards valley, underwater lighting and outdoor shower
- Ping-pong table
- Carport for 4 cars and automatic illumination
- Private 800 meter access road with gate and automatic illumination
