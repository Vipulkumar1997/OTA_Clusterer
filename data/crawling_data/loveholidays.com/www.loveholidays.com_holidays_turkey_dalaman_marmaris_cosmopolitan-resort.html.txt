Set in the vibrant resort of Marmaris, the 3-star Cosmopolitan Resort Apartments are the ideal stay for families and couples seeking an action-packed holiday with plenty of sunshine and sea in the mix. Located just 850m from the beach, you won’t find yourself too far away from the sand, giving you plenty of opportunities to engage in some water sports or top up your tan. What’s more, the town is just a short walk away – perfect for holidaymakers seeking time at some of Marmaris’ top attractions.

The hotel has 160 apartments, with accommodation comfortably decorated. There are a number of options to suit guest’s needs, including one and two bedroom apartments as well as larger suites. Each room has a living area and is equipped with their own fridge, TV, Air conditioning and balcony. Some rooms have a kitchenette for your catering needs. The hotel also provides rooms with disabled access.

The complex boasts two swimming pools, giving guests a chance to kick back and unwind in the comfort of the hotel area. Alongside the pools, there are Jacuzzi pools for your relaxation as well as sun loungers and parasols for your comfort. Those taking a trip with little ones will enjoy the water slides, as well as the designated kid’s area and mini club to keep them occupied.

Seeking something a little more laid-back? The resort’s on-site fitness centre boasts a sauna, traditional Turkish bath and Jacuzzi as well as massage treatments. Those wanting entertainment can make use of the games room complete with video games and billiards, or relax in the TV lounge.

The hotel’s on-site Mediterranean restaurant makes for a great place to enjoy local cuisine, with enough on offer for even the most discerning guest. Served buffet style, there are regular theme nights to give guests the opportunity to try a range of delicious delicacies. Viking’s Terrace Restaurant is a specialised seafood restaurant overlooking the pool and serves up unique dishes inspired by the Aegean area.

Accommodation

Room facilities

The hotel is based around 160 apartments. In addition to the basic one- and two-bedroom apartments, there are a number of suites which offer larger floor space. All rooms have a living area and are equipped with their own fridge, TV, AC unit and balcony. Some rooms also have a kitchenette. The hotel provides two rooms designed for disabled visitors.
