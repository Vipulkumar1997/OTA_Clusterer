Lush Lloret de Mar Holidays 2018 / 2019

Lloret de Mar is a popular holiday destination located along the shoreline of the
Costa Brava
in
Spain
and full of stunning scenery. Lloret de Mar is a popular alternative to other European holiday destinations, and boasts just as much allure as Tenerife or
Benidorm
. With cheap flights, some great all inclusive hotels and deals on packages, Lloret de Mar is a top destination for 2018 / 2019 holidays!

Chill out, relax in an
all inclusive
, and tan yourself on the gorgeous golden beaches during the day - with a cocktail or two! Immerse yourself in the vibrant and exciting nightlife and dance the night away in some of the liveliest clubs in the Costa Brava. Lloret de Mar is packed with a variety of activities – such as water sports, theme parks and the markets - so you will never get bored!

Lloret de Mar is suitable, fun and enjoyable for all types of holidaymakers too – including couples and families – so it is the ideal place to stay if you are looking to book your holiday away in the Spanish sun. If you're keen on the Costa Brava, but want to see what else is out there, another attractive destination on this Spanish costa is the wonderful
Tossa de Mar
, or the magnificent
Barcelona
.

Top Attractions






Lloret de Mar Holidays has a number of beautiful sandy beaches and a captivating clubbing scene. However, for those of you who have families and/or are seeking the authentic Spanish experience, Lloret de Mar has that to offer to you. The area has both historical and contemporary elements for you to explore.

Visit
Sant Joan Castle
for picturesque views over pretty landscapes and visit
Castell d’en Plaja
, which overlooks the stunning
Balearic
Sea. Head further afield to Blanes to see the
Marimurtra Botanical Gardens
. For avid historians, you can visit the
Ruins of Empuries
in order to take a peek into ancient Spain.

For more heritage and quintessential sites, the streets located near the Lloret de Mar are full of quaint cafes and bustling restaurants, allowing you to indulge in traditional Spanish cuisine.
Tossa de Mar
, just up the coast of the Costa Brava, is a popular day trip for those staying in Lloret de Mar.


Read More about Top Attractions





Wine and Dine






Lloret de Mar is full of a number of cafes, bars, restaurants and clubs. Wine, dine and taste authentic and traditional Spanish cuisines, full of flavoursome and delicious dishes. Relax and chill out in a bar with a wine or a cocktail. There are some wonderful all inclusive hotels with restaurants and catering facilities for you and your family’s needs. Lloret de Mar is perfect for a
cheap holiday
to
Spain
with the whole family.
Tossa de Mar
, with a choice of over 300 restaurants, a fantastic beachfront promenade and a warm atmosphere, can make a nice alternative for an evening out.


Read More about Wine and Dine





For Families






For families staying at the resort there are numerous activities suitable for kids and families to get involved in. Due to the high number of activities to enjoy in Lloret de Mar, it is an ideal place to holiday with a family.
The Adventure Park
makes for an excellent day out for the whole family, with something for everyone to try out. There are also water parks and a Mini Club on the beach where little ones can play with professional supervision to give you peace of mind. Throughout the year, Lloret de Mar holds various events that children will love such as the carnival. As this is such a popular family destination, lots of restaurants cater to children as well so you can be sure there will be something for the fussy eaters and everyone will be happy!


Read More about For Families





Action and Adventure






There are a variety of activities to engage in inside or outside of Lloret de Mar. Action is centred around the sea with plenty of water sports to try out and professional courses or instructors to get you on your way. Kayaking, scuba or deep sea diving are all activities available or for fun above the waterm jet or water skiing is a possibility. Away from the water you can get your thrills by bungee jumping or at the adventure park with zip wires and climbing in the forest. There are lots of beautiful hikes in the area and Lloret de Mar is a promotor of Nordic Walking, a particular type of walking that is a great way to get fit and in the spectacular surroundings of the
Costa Brava
.


Read More about Action and Adventure







Life's a Beach






Life is definitely a beach if you stay in Lloret de Mar, as it located along the shoreline of
Costa Brava
, so you are certain guaranteed to enjoy the beautiful sandy beaches that are located near the resort.

Also, during your holiday to Lloret de Mar you can get yourself involved in a number of sports and activities, including: scuba-diving, kayaking, wind-surfing, and volleyball. So get involved – by keeping yourself, your loved one and your family occupied with various activities and sports you are certain to enjoy your time in Lloret de Mar.

Just down the Costa Brava is the magnificent city of
Barcelona
, whose beaches are comparable with the best in
Benidorm
or
Tossa de Mar
. A day trip to Barcelona shouldn't be out of your equation.


Read More about Life's a Beach





Nightlife






If you really want to let your hair down and go completely wild, then Lloret de Mar has a number of clubs and nightlife hotspots to go to, so you can go crazy on a night out and dance the night away on your 2018 / 2019 holidays! Although not quite
Benidorm
, Lloret de Mar has a party feel to it, and you are spoiled for choice for clubs with every kind of music and evening you could wish for. Clubs stay open late and some are bars in the afternoon that become clubs at night. There are also a number of beachside bars so that you can sip cocktails as you watch the sun go down over the sea. If you fancy taking your chances, head to the casino and play a few games of roulette, or for a quieter night there are plenty of small bars and pubs to sit and drink wine in.


Read More about Nightlife





For Couples






For couples, Lloret de Mar is a romantic hotspot for those looking to spend time away. There are romantic, peaceful corners to seek out, in beautiful surroundings. Take your partner out for an adventurous ride in a kayak for two and spend a pleasant day out on the ocean. Or try diving and look at the amazing marine life.

There are a number of spas in Lloret de Mar where you can head for some relaxation, couples massage or just spending the day being pampered. Lloret de Mar is chock full of romantic places to dine out with your partner or bars to have a wine and listen to local music.


Read More about For Couples





Culture






Even though Lloret de Mar is a popular tourist destination, you can still immerse yourself in authentic Spanish culture: the food, the language, the literature and the rich history. Lloret is also very fond of its festival from religious to historic or recognition of old traditions. The carnival, held every year is a fantastic festival to witness, with costumes and music  and parades, giving you a feeling of the flair of the Costa Brava. There is also a medieval fair with lots of shows, magic, markets and music all in period costume. On July 24
th
Lloret de Mar celebrates its patron saint Santa Cristina with a procession to the Saint’s chapel followed by events and celebrating. If you are visiting in winter months, it is worth being around to witness the trawling festival on the first Sunday of February and December. Trawl fishing is the traditional method of fishing in Lloret and to pay tribute to fisherman people gather at dawn to throw the first net. The participants then feast on the fish caught for breakfast.
