Cheap flights on holidays from London Heathrow Airport

London Heathrow Airport is the major central hub of all UK air traffic, carrying 72 million passengers and 1.4 million tonnes of cargo each year. The sheer number of flights that go through London Heathrow Airport might seem a little daunting at first, but you definitely won’t struggle to find the perfect flight for you; whether you’re flying on business to
Thailand
, jetting off to
Las Vegas
for the weekend, or starting out on an adventure to
Florida
. Every day is the busiest day at London Heathrow Airport, but with the help of cutting edge technology, fantastic customer service, and hardworking staff, you can guarantee a smooth start to your trip.

How to get to London Heathrow Airport

London Heathrow Airport is just 15 miles west of central London and easily reached by road or public transport. With a journey time of 15 minutes between the airport and London Paddington, the Heathrow Express train offers the quickest and easiest route from the city centre. Alternatively, the London Underground’s Piccadilly Line has three London Heathrow Airport stops: one for terminals two and three, and one each for terminals four and five. If you’re coming from further away, National Express and other coach operators run services to Heathrow from more than 500 destinations. There is an extremely broad range of car parking facilities to suit your needs and regular shuttle buses that connect to whichever terminal you’re flying from.
