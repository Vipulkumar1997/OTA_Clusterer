Kiotari beach holidays
The long stretch of sand and shingle beach awaits you on a beach holiday in Kiotari. The blue flag awarded beach offers a perfect setting for a family day in the sun. The sand gently shelves in to the water, which is great for kids to play around in and runs the whole length of the resort so there are plenty of places to relax in and soak up the sunshine. There is also a good range of water sports on offer including: jet skiing, windsurfing and parasailing - so much choice on your beach holiday in Kiotari!

Explore Kiotari

The increasingly popular resort provides all the amenities of a modern resort with a range of shops for all those essential bits of shopping on your holiday in Kiotari. Head to the nearby village of Asklipion where you will find a beautiful Byzantine church and mediaeval castle ruins – great for a day trip on your holiday to Kiotari. Why not hire a bike on and head to the picturesque resort of Gennadi or Lardos. You could hire a car and follow the coastline or head inland and explore some of the surrounding traditional Greek villages.

Why not venture a bit further on your holiday; Kiotari is within easy reach of the pretty beach resort of
Lindos
where the streets are scattered with pretty white buildings overlooking attractive bays. Not forgetting the beautiful Acropolis, which is well worth a visit. For a great day out with the kids on your family holiday to Kiotari, visit the fantastic
Faliraki
Water Park or head over to the walled medieval city in
Rhodes Town
and walk along the winding cobbled streets with shops, tavernas and hidden churches. After a long day out, you may want to relax in a local taverna and watch the world go by – what better way to spend your holiday in Kiotari.
