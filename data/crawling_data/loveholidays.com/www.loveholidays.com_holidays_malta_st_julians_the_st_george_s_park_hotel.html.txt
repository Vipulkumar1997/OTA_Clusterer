Boasting a stunning location just a short walk from a golden sandy beach, the  St. George’s Park Hotel is situated in the quaint and picturesque resort of St Julian’s in Malta. Offering holidaymakers a lively holiday just a few minutes’ walk from vibrant nightlife, you can’t go wrong with a stay here.

Choose from family rooms, sea view rooms or rooms to catch some "zzzzs" between partying. Security is priority and you will have access to a safety deposit box too. If you think of anything else then the front desk are on hand 24/7 to assist with your every need including luggage storage and laundry. You can also stay connected to friends and family at home with the hotel’s WiFi which is available at a small charge (free in public areas.)

The 3-star St. George’s Park Hotel boasts extensive in-house facilities suitable for rest and play. There are a variety of swimming pools to try out, including one just for children. If you can’t sit still then how about a game of squash or a circuit around the fitness centre? You really will be spoilt for choice, and if you can’t decide what to do, let the on-site entertainment team take you by surprise!

When you start feeling peckish, you can head out and explore the bars and restaurants of St Julian’s, or you have are two restaurants right on your doorstep. Start with an aperitif in the bar, then choose between buffet-style dining or Italian a la carte. You've got the flexibility of room only, bed and breakfast, half-board or all inclusive - meaning you can tailor your holiday at St. George's Park to suit you.
