Nestled in beautifully lush gardens in the popular resort of Lara Beach, the 5-star Limak Lara Deluxe Hotel & Resort is great for couples and families looking for an unforgettable holiday in the popular resort of Antalya. The five-star hotel offers luxury facilities and countless activities for you to get involved in, all just steps from the beautiful beach.

The design and interiors of the Limak Lara Deluxe Hotel have a Far Eastern theme, and rooms are beautifully decorated. All have satellite TV, air-conditioning, tea & coffee-making facilities, en-suite bathrooms and balconies, so you can relax in comfort.

Guests looking for active pursuits will love the hotel’s great range of activities including water sports, tennis, mini golf, games and more. For some serious relaxation, indulge in a pampering treatment at the hotel’s onsite spa with a steam bath, indoor pools and a hot tub. This all-inclusive resort has a range of international cuisines on offer at its onsite restaurants, including Indian, Italian, Japanese, Mexican, Thai and Turkish - so you'll find something to satisfy even the most particular of palates! You've also got 8 bars to choose from, including a pool bar and disco, so you're never far from a cocktail or a cold beer.

Watersports, plenty of onsite games, fabulous restaurants and bars, an indulgent spa - all this combined with warm and friendly staff ready to help in any way makes for a memorable holiday in Turkey.

Accommodation

Room facilities

The Limak Lara Deluxe Hotel & Resort features 441 guestrooms and suites including 323 standard rooms, 92 family rooms, 4 rooms for disabled, 1 deluxe suites and 26 elegantly furnished Jacuzzi suites. All standard rooms have central air conditioning, sea view, IP telephone, safe box, LCD TV with 40 international channels, balcony, internet, laminate floor, music channel, fire alarm, electronic keylock system, and hair dryer. Late checkout is available (charged locally). Tea and coffee facilities are available in all rooms.



Other Information

From 1st November to 30th April the hotel's all inclusive programme only serves local drinks. Imported drinks are at an extra charge.

Rates and conditions are valid for UK residents only.
