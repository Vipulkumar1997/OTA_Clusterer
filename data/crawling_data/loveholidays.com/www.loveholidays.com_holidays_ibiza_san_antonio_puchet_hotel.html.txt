The 3 star Puchet Hotel is in San Antonio, one of the most renowned resort areas on Ibiza. Just a short walk from a sandy beach, where there are loungers and beds for hire, the Puchet Hotel has outdoor pools and a pool area suitable for children.

Rooms at the Puchet Hotel are spacious and light and come equipped with a private bathroom, a telephone, safe and either a balcony or terrace where applicable. There’s also a small refrigerator, Free WiFi, flat screen TV and air conditioning.

Serving up a tasty breakfast to get your day started right the hotel restaurant also serves dinner between 8pm and 10pm. If you want to enjoy some drinks or snacks by the pool, the snack bar is just right, during the summer months it’s open almost all day (11am til 11pm). Along with the culinary delights there’s also a swimming pool area with adjoining solarium, adapted rooms for the disabled, meeting rooms and transfers arrangeable through the reception. There’s also a gym, supermarket and parking – awesome!

Within walking distance of a sandy beach, the Puchet Hotel is just perfect for a family or couple looking for a relaxing stay in this idyllic part of Ibiza. Remember, you are on Ibiza, the party capital of the Mediterranean, and you’re never far from the action! If you’re looking for a spot to balance fun and relaxation, the Puchet Hotel is for you.

Food and Drink

Snack Bar by the pool

Available during the summer season, open from 11am til 11pm serving up drinks, snacks, cocktails and other beverages.

Trigo Trago

The onsite restaurant Trigo Trago serves up breakfast and other delights all day long, with a huge, varied menu with culinary treats suitable for everybody - including veggies!

Buffet

The buffet at the Puchet Hotel consists of 12 cold dishes and 12 hot dishes, and there's also some show cooking. Located in the big dining room by the pool, the breakfast buffet is served from 8am til 10am, and dinner between 8pm and 10pm. If you fancy enjoying the buffet, get in touch with the staff on reception! Note, this is only available during peak season.
