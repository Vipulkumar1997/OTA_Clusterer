The 4-star Gran Hotel Bali Benidorm is one of the tallest hotels in Europe. Located in the heart of the action and with luxurious facilities, you’re guaranteed a stylish, comfortable holiday in Benidorm that’s packed with activities.

With spectacular views from the higher floors, the Gran Hotel Bali offers spacious, airy rooms that all come fully-equipped with satellite TV, mini-fridges and air-conditioning. Some rooms have balconies or terraces too, for maximum enjoyment of those breath-taking vistas. WiFi is available for an extra charge.

There's a choice of swimming pools at the Gran Hotel Bali Benidorm, including one especially for children to play in safely. The others include a lake-style pool, a semi-olympic swimming pool and an outdoor Jacuzzi area. A hot tub, fitness room, sauna, spa and wellness centre are among the modern facilities available to the grown-ups for relaxation, while for the kids, there's a playground and an entertainment programme during high season. The whole family can enjoy the games and activities on offer within the hotel, including the games room, table tennis and billiards.

Located close to the central shopping and leisure area of Costa Blanca, you’ll have immediate access to plenty of exciting attractions. During the day you can visit nearby theme parks including Terra Mitica and Terra Natura, as well as wandering the shops and cafes or playing golf, all within walking distance of the hotel. By night, you’re close to the best bars and clubs for a lively evening out. A ticket desk at the hotel itself can also help you decide on the best places to visit during your stay.

Food and Drink

The hotel has 2 restaurants, these are buffet-style restaurants with show cooking.
There's also two hotel bars (one poolside, one disco-style) and cafeterias.
During high season the hotel operates a fancy poolside bar.
At the Cotton Bali Club there's a late-night disco that operates in peak times.

Other Information

The Gran Hotel Bali is situated in the area "La Cala de Benidorm" just 350 metres from the beach and a short drive from the popular theme park, Terra Mitica which is well worth a visit.

Please note the official star rating for this property is 4*.

Reception service, housekeeping, hair and beauty salon, Wi-Fi (with extra charge), conference facilities available for all types of events, including the biggest conference room in Benidorm.
