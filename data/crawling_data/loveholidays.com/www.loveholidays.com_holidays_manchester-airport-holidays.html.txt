How to get to Manchester Airport

Manchester Airport is easy to get to. It is serviced by a number of transport links which arrive from all over Britain. If you're travelling by car then be sure to put the postcode M90 1QX into your Sat Nav system and follow the directions up to the departures part of the building. When you arrive, there is a short stay car park where you can stop for free and unload your luggage if you have been given a lift to the airport. All three terminals also have their own multi-storey car parks. Alternatively, public transport users can make full use of the train facilities which run regularly to the airport. There is a train service which runs from Manchester Piccadilly station roughly every 10 minutes. Trains from most other major cities including Birmingham, Edinburgh, Glasgow and Leeds also service the airport. Other options for reaching Manchester Airport include travelling by coach or by local bus services.
