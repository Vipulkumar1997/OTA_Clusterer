Situated in the lively centre of the Playa de las Americas, the Sol Tenerife Hotel offers a relaxing 4-star holiday right on the beach. With beautiful views of the sun-kissed Mediterranean, yet close to the exciting nightlife the island has to offer, the hotel is the perfect getaway for young couples. Plus, the great range of facilities on-site and the beachfront setting also means families are just as welcome...Especially the ones seeking to enjoy a soothing time on the sunny Canary Islands.

As the Sol Tenerife Hotel is a part of the Melia Hotels International Chain, this guarantees both the highest quality of customer service and a friendly atmosphere. Offering a great mix of evening entertainment and plenty of relaxing options for your leisure, this is an ideal choice for anyone looking to spend some quality time on the beach during the day, and a vibrant city atmosphere in the evenings. The Sol Tenerife Hotel is also conveniently located close to the commercial centre full of bars and pubs ready to satisfy any taste.

Back in the hotel enjoy a spacious dining area, free WiFi and two stages for shows and performances, hosting regular entertainment. As for the guestrooms, they are all spacious and fully equipped with TV, air-conditioning, safe, hairdryer and an en-suite bathroom with bath or shower.

Privileged beachfront location aside, the Sol Tenerife Hotel boasts three outdoor pools (one just for children) and a poolside bar, too! As for activities, there is plenty to choose from both on-site and in the local area. There are cycling and hiking opportunities as well as three golf courses located nearby, while back in the hotel, there's a fab tennis court.

Accommodation

Room facilities

The Sol Tenerife Hotel offers a total of 522 double rooms distributed in two connected buildings. The majority of the rooms offer sea views. All of them have a fully equipped bathroom with hair-dryer, air-conditioning, satellite TV, direct dial telephone, fridge and safety deposit box (payable locally).



Other Information

Hairdresser, Supermarket/Shops, Doctor on Call. WIFI access is also available in common areas (payable locally)

Please be advised that WiFi in rooms and common areas will be free of charge from Summer 2016.
