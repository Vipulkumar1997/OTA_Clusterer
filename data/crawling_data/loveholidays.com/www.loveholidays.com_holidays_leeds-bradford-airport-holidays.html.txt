How to get to Leeds Bradford Airport

Leeds Bradford Airport is signposted on all major roads, including the M1 and M62 motorways and the Yorkshire section of the A1. The airport is within easy driving distance of Leeds, Bradford, York, Sheffield, Huddersfield and Doncaster, and there are excellent short, mid, and long stay car parking facilities available to suit the length of your holiday. There are flying tiger bus connections from Leeds and Bradford train stations and Harrogate bus station. A wide range of bus services make it quick and convenient to get to the airport, running daily from early morning to late evening, including weekend and bank holidays.
