30 airlines

100 destinations

Alicante

Palma

Canary Islands

Our favourite hotels from Glasgow Airport

Just 400 metres from the popular resort of
Lloret de Mar
in
Costa Brava
, Spain, loveholidays.com is excited to offer the
Bolero Apartments
for you and the family to stay in complete comfort. These apartments overlook the wonderful beaches this area is proud to offer, and the hotel itself is set within gorgeous tropical gardens, making sure you get the most out of the glorious Spanish sunshine. This is a great spot for family holidays as they offer both kids' and adult swimming pools. This is also the perfect location from which to explore many other great destinations in Spain, at only 30 and kilometres from Girona and 50 kilometres from one of Europe’s most exciting cities,
Barcelona
. Also located in Lloret del Mar is the beautiful
Moremar Hotel
. This hotel offers its guests a laidback, Caribbean atmosphere and is a great choice for families and couples. There is plenty of entertainment on offer through the day and night, so there will certainly be something to suit everyone’s tastes. There is a fantastic restaurant on site with a wide range of dishes available as well as a welcoming terrace bar with theme nights on almost every evening! A car hire facility is also available to ensure you can enjoy all the great beaches and attractions this region has to offer.

Explore

Situated in the lively resort of
St Julian’s
in
Malta
, the
Burlington Apartments
sit within the vibrant town of Paceville. This area is packed with great restaurants and bars and is known in particular for its nightclubs. These self catered apartments are the perfect spot to explore the island, there are buses from town to the nearby attractions of
Sliema
,
Valletta
and
Bugibba
. You’re in complete control of your holiday from the Burlington Apartments and can take things at your own pace. These apartments are the perfect choice for young couples and friends, as the nightlife is so vibrant in this region, however it is less suited to young families and elderly couples.
