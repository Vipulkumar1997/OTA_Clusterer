With its’ fantastic location in the heart of Puerto de la Cruz, a large outdoor pool and close proximity to the beach, the 3-star DC Xibana Park is a favourite for holiday makers of every type.

The DC Xibana Park hotel is ideal for sun-splashed holidays on the gorgeous Canary Island of Tenerife. The nearest beach is just around 600m away. If you’d prefer to stay onsite while topping up your tan, the inviting and spacious sun terrace which surrounds the hotel's private outdoor pool is a fantastic place to lie back and enjoy the sun. Grab a cocktail from the poolside bar and take full of advantage of that glorious, year-round, Canary Island weather! Travelling with children? There is a kids' pool too!

Every guestroom is fully equipped with an en-suite bathroom with hair-dryer, telephone with outside line, a hire safe and television, providing a comfortable home away from home. Everything you need for a fantastic holiday can be found onsite. In addition to the outdoor pool, sun terrace and children's’ pool, the hotel benefits from a games room, table tennis, bar games like darts and billiards, and tennis for those looking to stay active. Relaxation enhanced through the provision of massage chairs, while the free WiFi in the lobby area makes it easy to stay in touch with the outside world. There is a hotel restaurant and you can top up your drink at either the pool or lobby bar. The DC Xibana Parks' multilingual and helpful staff are on hand to answer any questions you have about the hotel and surrounds.

If you want to do a bit of exploring, the DC Xibana Park hotel is ideally located. You'll find plenty of bars, restaurants and attractions just 150m away in Puerto de la Cruz resort's lively centre. The famous El Lagos Martianez, Europe’s largest open-air art museum with stunning swimming pools, is just a few meters from the hotel, making it easy to skip any summer queues. Those looking to adventure further afield will not be disappointed with Tenerife’s beautiful beaches, vibrant towns, and the stunning Teide National park providing attractions for every holiday taste.

After a relaxing stay on the sunny island of Tenerife? With its fantastic facilities and stunning location in Puerto de la Cruz, the DC Xibana Park might just be the hotel for you!
