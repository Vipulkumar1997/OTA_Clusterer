Housed in a Renaissance-inspired palace, just a few Kilometres from the centre of Rome and the Vatican, The Church Palace is a 4 star hotel which welcomes guests in a relaxed and cosmopolitan atmosphere. The hotel is surrounded by more than 28,000 m2 of private garden, overlooking Villa Carpegna and Villa Pamphili, two of the most beautiful parks in Rome. The property includes excellent and useful facilities such as two elegant restaurants, a cosy bar and complimentary wireless high speed Internet access for those who want to keep updated during their stay. Moreover, the spacious and air-conditioned guest rooms and suites are ideally appointed with everything necessary for a comfortable stay such as an electric safe and a minibar.
