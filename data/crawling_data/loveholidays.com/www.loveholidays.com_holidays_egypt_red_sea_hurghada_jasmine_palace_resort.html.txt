The Jasmine Palace Resort, Hurghada is a year-round 5-star retreat boasting a good location and amenities. Ideal for an escape to Egypt, the hotel will provide for all your holiday needs from the moment you step on its grounds.

The Jasmine Palace Resort invites you to fully relax throughout your stay. Whether you are a couple in need of some relaxation in the sun, or a family after a fun trip abroad, all your needs will be well catered for. Experience the top-notch Egyptian hospitality as you take full advantage of your all inclusive stay. There are five restaurants and six bars on-site, not to mention the daily animation programme and evening entertainment.

Complete with a private sandy beach on the gorgeous Red Sea, it is here where you can admire the breath-taking sunsets over the desert and the turquoise waters of the sea. Make the most out of the multiple outdoor pools, some of them heated through winter, or head to the hotel's spa for a soothing massage. Plus, there're loads of fun water sport activities on the beach. If you're travelling with kids, there is a separate children's pool, kids club located at the kids pool area, and a kids cinema.

The guestrooms at the Jasmine Palace are spacious and comfortable, fully equipped with all the comforts you might need, including en-suite bathroom, air conditioning, satellite TV, safety deposit box, minibar and balcony or terrace.

Local Tips

The resort is 16 km from New Marina and 18 km from Giftun Island – ask reception about excursions that are available for booking.

Food and Drink

The hotel has 6 restaurants with a choice of cuisines ranging from Egyptian, Italian, Chinese, Seafood and Indian cuisine.
Evening drinks can be enjoyed at the lobby bar or any of the 6 bars on site.

Rooms
