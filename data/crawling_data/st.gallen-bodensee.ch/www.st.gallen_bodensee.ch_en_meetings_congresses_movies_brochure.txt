Lake event ideas

Not only is Lake Constance europes largest inland lake, but it offers a great variety of special venues along side the lake or floting in the water. Staging an event close to the water is always an added value and motivates your team even more. Let you inspire by Lake Constance!


Lake event ideas











City event ideas

Organise your event in a UNESCO World Heritage City. St.Gallen is proud of its old town with cosy restaurants and many special venues. All locations are within walking distance or can be easily reached by public transport. This enables a stress free meeting for your delegates. You will be surprised what the city of St.Gallen has to offer.


City event ideas











Mountain event ideas

Your next meeting can be held with breathtaking views on top of a mountain. the backdrop of Eastern Switzerland offers majestic peaks in the middle of the alps. Many high altitude venues can be easely reached from St.Gallen, which offers you a great opportunity to spice up your event. The area is also well know for its living swiss traditions.


Mountain event ideas











The destination St.Gallen-Bodensee

The region of St.Gallen-Bodensee will be introduced in this movie. Famous for the rich history and cultural activities, this region is a must seen in Switzerland. Here you can still discover hidden gems which will surprise you.
