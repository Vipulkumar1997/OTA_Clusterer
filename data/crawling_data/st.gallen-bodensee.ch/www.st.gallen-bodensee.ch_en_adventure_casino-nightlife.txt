Casino & Nightlife








St. Gallen and its surrounding region also offer a multitude of options late at night

You can enjoy yourself in one of the many bars and clubs in St. Gallen or on the shores of Lake Constance. Or you can enter an exciting atmosphere full of glitz and glamour at the Swiss Casinos St. Gallen. A large selection of extremely modern slot machines and table games awaits you right beside the Olma-Areal grounds.
