The tourist destination St.Gallen-Lake Constance contains 32 different municipalities reaching from St.Gallen’s Lake Constance shoreline to the beginning of the rolling foothills of the Alps and the St.Gallen Rhine Valley.







Let yourself be soothed by the idyllic villages surrounded by verdant fields or experience an exciting day in one of the dynamic cities.
