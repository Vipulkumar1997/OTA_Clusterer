Excursions & Tours








«Textilland Ostschweiz» offers interesting local excursions and trips for families, car lovers and also those interested in history, culture and architecture. Whether on foot along the “Textilweg St. Gallen”, by car on the “Textilland Explorer Tour” or on a guided city tour: you can experience the formative influence of the textile industry on Eastern Switzerland in a simple and engaging way.
