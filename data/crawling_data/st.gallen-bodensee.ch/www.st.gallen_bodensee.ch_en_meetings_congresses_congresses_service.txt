TAILOR-MADE SERVICES
St.Gallen-Bodensee Convention supports you during the organization of your congress.
CONSULTING
We assist you during the selection of suitable venues for congresses, exhibitions, seminars and evening events.
SITE INSPECTION
We organize viewings of possible hotels and congress venues and accompany you upon request.
BIDDING
We are happy to prepare a tailor-made bidding document for you.
RESERVATIONS
We take care of the congress and event venues, organize the right hotel rooms for you, deal with contingency issues and take over the entire handling process (with direct reservation confirmations sent to participants).
PARTNERS
We have an excellent network of selected partners whom we can recommend to you, e.g. for giveaways or transfers.
PRESENCE
We are onsite during the congress and can take care of your delegates. We are also happy to provide you with a variety of informational material, such as city maps.
