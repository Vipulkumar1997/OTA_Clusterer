Be guided

Let be GPS be your guide with the free app «TextileStGallen» through the textile city of St.Gallen. Experience the rich influence of the textile industry in St.Gallen and its region in an easy and exciting way, being on the spot or at home on the sofa.







The free app
«TextileStGallen» is ideal for anyone being interested in cultures or architecture, for students or even people familiar with St.Gallen, but wanting to see the city through new eyes.
The app is your personal and individual guide through the textile city of St.Gallen. It guides you through GPS and, once installed, works offline. You won’t need any WLAN.
Functions:

Two GPS guided
walking tours along the architectonic witnesses of the textile era in the city
of St.Gallen
More than 30 sights
Every description
contains pictures and summaries (as text and narrated versions) about its
history,
architecture and present-day use

Culinary and
cultural tips
Shopping and
touring tips

Event-Highlights
Setting: Choose
between the online and offline version whenever you use the app.
The bilingual app (german/english) is the digital development of the two textile trails
«St.Gallen downtown»
and
«St.Gallen West»
. «TextileStGallen» is available for free at the
«App Store»
and at the
«Google Play Store»
