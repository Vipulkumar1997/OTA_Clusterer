We are happy to assist you in finding the right hotel and organizing a suitable activity programme. We can offer you existing package deals or arrange an individual program just for you.

Are you interested in a study trip?

Our study trips are intended for tour operators, travel agents, business clients and further key figures in the tourism industry who are interested in including St.Gallen to their
scope of services. All participants have the opportunity to get to know St.Gallen (more) and than be able to sell it.

We are happy to offer you a suitable program and are looking forward to your approach!
