Bühne 66



Schwyz



Bühne 66 is an amateur theatre club which was founded in 1966. The name of the theatre club is derived from its first year.

Chärnehus



Einsiedeln



The Chärnehus theatre group stages its productions at various venues in Einsiedeln – both indoors and out.
»
More









Chupferturm



Schwyz



Music, theatre, comedy and children’s productions are all part of the Chupferturm repertoire. It fosters local talent as well as Swiss and international artists.
»
More









Duo Fischbach Theater



Küssnacht am Rigi



Many do not realise that Duo Fischbach has its own theatre. The two comedians perform here and also open the stage to famous artists.
»
More









Einsiedeln World Theater



Einsiedeln



The «Einsiedeln World Theater» is a grand open-air theatre, inspired by Pedro Calderón de la Barca’s «El gran teatro del mundo.» The production is performed outdoors on the abbey square.
»
More









Popular theatre



Schwyz



The inhabitants of the canton of Schwyz love performing on stage. This is reflected in the myriad of popular theatres. As amateur actors they are able to delight audiences and carry them away from the daily routine.
