Places of interest









Town Hall



Schwyz



The village square in Schwyz ranks among the most beautiful throughout all of Switzerland. It is in part thanks to the Town Hall.
»
More









Manor houses



Schwyz



The manor houses can be found in town and on the outskirts. One of them, the Ital Reding House, is a museum open from 1st may to 31st october.
»
More









Guided Tours of the Abbey



Einsiedeln



On several tours you will receive broad insight into the 1000 year old culture of the Benedictine abbey.
»
More









Jumps



Einsiedeln



The Einsiedeln jumps are used for training, competitions and for many different kinds of events. The jumps are always worth a visit.
»
More









Wooden footbridge



Hurden–Rapperswil



You can walk in the middle of the lake and still have solid ground under your feet. Enjoy Lake Zurich from a completely different perspective.
»
More









Hohle Gasse



Küssnacht am Rigi



The Hohle Gasse (Hollow Lane) is an important historic site situated in the heart of Switzerland.
»
More









Suworow Bridge



Muota Valley



General Suworow attempted to reach the lowlands in Schwyz via the Kinzig Pass and through the Muota valley. But the French stopped his army at the end of the valley (what is now the Suworow Bridge).
»
More









Devil's Bridge



Egg



The Devil’s Bridge spans the Sihl and connects the Etzel Pass with Einsiedeln and is therefore a part of the Way of St James.
»
More









Morgarten House



Sattel



The historic battleground of Morgarten lies just north of the village of Sattel in the hamlet of Schornen between Lake Ägeri and Sattel.
»
More
