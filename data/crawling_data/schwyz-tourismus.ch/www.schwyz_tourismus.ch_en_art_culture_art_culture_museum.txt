Museums & exhibitions









Museum of Swiss Charters



Schwyz



Swiss history between myth and reality. The Federal Charter of 1291 played a major role in Swiss history.
»
More









Exhibition at Forum Schwyz



«Alois Carigiet. Kunst, Art, graphic & Schellen-Ursli»



The exhibition is devoted to this creative man from the Grisons and illuminates the many facets of his work in a way that will captivate adults and children alike; 04.11.2017 - 11.03.2018
»
More









Forum of Swiss History



Schwyz



The Forum of Swiss History Schwyz is one of the most important museums of cultural history in the Alpine region. It is a modern historical museum where culture and history come together offering visitors a special experience.
»
More









Ital Reding Estate



Schwyz



The Ital Reding Estate is one of the most treasured groups of buildings in the canton of Schwyz. The manor house, the Bethlehem House and the former farm building are located inside the estate walls.
»
More









Victorinox Brand Store and Museum



Brunnen



Discover knives from all eras and learn more about the unique success story of the Swiss Army Knife.
»
More









Vögele Kultur Zentrum



Pfäffikon



Every year the Vögele Kultur Zentrum presents two special exhibitions that address contemporary issues from different perspectives.
»
More









Reopen - Rock Avalanche Museum



Goldau



The rock avalanche museum is reopen to the public and is free of charge.
»
More









Abbey library



Einsiedeln



The abbey library (established in 934) bears witness to its rich tradition and culture.
»
More









Museum Fram



Einsiedeln



The historical museum was established by the Einsiedeln Cultural Heritage, a private foundation. The museum exhibits the diversity and history of the monastery village of Einsiedeln, with its cultural and pilgrimage tradition.
»
More









Werner Oechslin Library



Einsiedeln



The library, designed by Mario Botta, chiefly houses primary sources on architectural theory and its related fields in original editions. The stock was considerably increased after 1985 by Dr. Werner Oechslin.
»
More









Art in the park



Gersau



Every year the GersauART Culture Club stages an art exhibition in the Kurpark (spa garden). The themes change from year to year and offer cantonal, national and international artists a breathtaking platform.
»
More









Tree sculptures



Steinen



Alfons Bürgler turns trees upside down – literally. The artist transforms them into goblins, women, men, lovers or dancers.
»
More









Mineral Museum



Einsiedeln



The Mineral Museum houses over 1000 Minerals from all over the world – the minerals range from a quartz gem to huge and gorgeous crystals from South America and China.
»
More









Carnival and mask museum



Pfäffikon



In 2003 Markus Kläger, artist and wood carver, established the first carnival and mask museum in Switzerland.
