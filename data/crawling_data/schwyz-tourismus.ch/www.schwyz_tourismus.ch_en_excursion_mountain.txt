200 Years of Mountain Peak Experiences



Rigi



The first Summit Hotel in Switzerland was opened in 1816 by the Mt. Rigi Director, Joseph Martin Bürgi on the Rigi Kulm. This summer, Mt. Rigi, the queen of the mountains, is celebrating the 200 year anniversary.
»

Stoos mountain railway and cable car



Stoos



Stoos is a car-free recreation, sport and vacation resort with a complete infrastructure and highly diverse offers for winter and summer.
»

Hoch-Ybrig



Hoch-Ybrig



The aerial cable car takes guests from the Weglosen (Unteriberg) valley station up to the Hoch-Ybrig hiking and recreation paradise. Public transportation stops and free parking are located near the valley station.

Bödmeren Forest Reserve



Muotatal



Hidden, right at the very back of Muotatal, lies one of the most ancient surviving Alpine forests - the Bödmerenwald. The forest is around 550 ha in size, with some parts of it still untouched today. You can discover it for yourself, with the the themed trail "Urwaldspur" and the hiking trail "Urwaldweg".
