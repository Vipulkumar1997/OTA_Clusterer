Visitors Card










Lake Lucerne Region

Discover the Lake Lucerne Region in a convenient way and benefit from various discounts with the free visitors card.

The card is valid throughout the whole Lake Lucerne Region and offers over 70 excursions and museum entries with special conditions to all overnight guests.
The visitor card is available in the region's hotels, holiday-apartments, campings and tourist information offices.
