Specialties from Schwyz









Alp Cheese



Region of Schwyz



Those who travel along the footpaths of the Schwyz Region know to value the countless cheese dairies. Many workshops also have nearby restaurants, where you can build up your strength for the next leg of the journey with fresh specialities.
»
More









Cream kirsch torte from Muota Valley



Muotathal



Cream kirsch torte from Muota Valley – a dream in white. The light biscuit base is soaked in high-quality kirsch from Schwyz and topped with cream and white chocolate shavings.
»
More









Edel Kirsch



Brunnen



Vintage kirsch brandies, single-variety kirsch brandies aged in wooden vats, and a variety of wild cherry distillates: Edel Kirsch brandies for the discerning palate.
»
More









Exclusive wine



Seewen



The world of wine is characterised by a variety, and the most wonderful thing about it is its diversity. Those who discover the slumbering potential in wine derive a great deal of pleasure from being able to surprise others.
»
More









High-quality distillates from Schwyz



Oberarth



The signature features of the fruit brandies from Oberarth are purity and authenticity. This applies just as much today as it did over 160 years ago.
»
More









Kirschstrasse



Arth – Steinen – Schwyz – Brunnen



Schwyz and the area surrounding it is famous for its cherry blossom and numerous cherry specialities. Discover the sensual world of cherries, Kirsch brandies and Kirsch chocolates.
»
More









Sausages from Schwyz - Swiss Tell



Seewen-Schwyz



Homemade bratwurst from Central Switzerland, saucisson from Schwyz or specialty bacon, just to name a few. The vast selection of Swiss Tell specialties pleases even the most refined palate.
»
More









Schafböcke from Einsiedeln



Einsiedeln



Originally, the biscuits (Schafböcke) were intended for people who had made the pilgrimage to Einsiedeln to visit the monastery. The pilgrimage biscuits are made from a batter consisting of honey and eggs.
»
More









Specialty beers from Schwyz



Einsiedeln



The pale Maisgold beer, natural Alpine beer (unfiltered), fruity spelt beer and popular lager beer from Einsiedeln all render the beer world of Schwyz diverse. And it continues to develop.

Freienbach



With their expertise, the winemakers ensure that the juice of the sweet grapes is transformed into precious wine during the winter months.
