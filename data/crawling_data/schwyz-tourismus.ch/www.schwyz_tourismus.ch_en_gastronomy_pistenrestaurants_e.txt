Mountain restaurants








Berggasthaus Herrenboden


Sattel-Hochstuckli



Mountain guesthouse set in the tranquillity of nature, featuring a sun terrace, children’s playground and a park with small animals. It is situated along one of the lovely hiking trails in a ski resort that is ideal for families.



Berggasthaus Herrenboden
6417 Sattel
Tel. +41 (0)41 835 12 88
Fax. +41 (0)41 835 12 38

www.herrenboden.ch











Berggasthaus Mostelberg


Sattel-Hochstuckli



Located next to the Mostelberg mountain station with simple, yet delicious Swiss cuisine. Round off an action-packed day of fun and sun in the snow by enjoying a beautiful winter sunset and coffee schnapps. Families are welcome!



Berggasthaus Mostelberg
Mostelbergstrasse 170
6417 Sattel
Tel. +41 (0)41 835 11 78
Fax. +41 (0)41 835 11 60

info@mostelberg.ch

www.mostelberg.ch











Alpwirtschaft Laui


Stoos



After a fabulous ski run from Fronalpstock, it’s worth heading off to Laui Alp and treating yourself to traditional Alpine cuisine. Open in winter from Christmas to Easter.

Gasthaus Balmberg


Stoos
