Dialysis during your holidays










At Spital Lachen and Schwyz

Spend a wonderful holiday in the beautiful region of Schwyz despite needing regular dialysis treatment. Our hospitals will take very good care of you.

At Spital Lachen and Schwyz there is always the possibility of a holiday dialysis. The doctors and dialysis professionals teams speak English, German, French, Italian and Dutch, and have many years of experience in dialysis, nephrology and internal medicine. Excellent medical care from many specialists is available.
