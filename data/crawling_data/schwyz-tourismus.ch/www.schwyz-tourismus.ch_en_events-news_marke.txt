Spring & summer markets



Goods, produce and a cheery atmosphere



The market stands begin reappearing with the first sunny days of spring. Shopping as you stroll through the market with its broad range of goods is an inspiration. The markets in the canton of Schwyz are a treat for the eye.

Autumn & winter markets



Tradition and festive meeting point



Villages in the canton of Schwyz have treasured their autumn markets for generations. This is where tradition, local crafts and guests from afar all come together. Culinary highlights round off the vast assortment of goods.
