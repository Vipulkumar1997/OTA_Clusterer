Villages









Fire of Freedom



Region of Schwyz



The authentic Schwyz experience world at the heart of Switzerland presents the very best of nature, adventure, culture and history: from the primitive Muotathal valley to the monastery of Einsiedeln and the Swiss Knife Valley Visitor’s Center.
»
More









Alpthal



Sunshine Valley



The commune of Alpthal is a typical fore-alpine village that lies in the quiet valley of the same name, between the Mythen and Einsiedeln. The village is surrounded by magnificent green meadows with fore-alpine flower fields and dark pine tree forests.
»
More









Altendorf



Central and close to nature



Altendorf is a lake commune in a gentle hilly region, that rises up to 1200m, where several farms lie scattered across the slope.
»
More









Arth



Three villages – one commune



The commune of Arth consists of the villages of Arth, Oberarth and Goldau, and lies in a stretched valley between Lake Zug and Lake Lauerz. It spreads from the top of the Rossberg (1568m) all the way to the Rigi Kulm (1797m) on the other side.
»
More









Brunnen-Ingenbohl



Vacation spot with tradition



Due to its ideal location by Lake Lucerne, the village of Brunnen has been a privileged place of encounter of the important men of Uri, Schwyz and Unterwald since the Middle Ages.
»
More









Einsiedeln



The monastery village



Located by the famous Benedictine abbey, and surrounded by a wonderful scenery made of soft hills, green meadows and quiet moors.
»
More









Feusisberg



Panoramic view guaranteed



The commune of Feusisberg benefits from an exceptional location at the heart of green meadows and forests.
»
More









Freienbach



Vibrant and attractive



Discover the authenticity and the diversity in one of the most beautiful regions of Switzerland.
»
More









Galgenen



Rural yet modern



Galgenen lies on the border of the fore Alps, on the shore of Lake Zurich. Its village profile is dominated by the parish built in 1822-25 in the memory of Saint Martin.
»
More









Gersau



Mediterranean atmosphere



The beautiful village of Gersau is located directly by Lake Lucerne on the south slope of the Rigi. Due to its mild climate, it is also known as the «Riviera of Central Switzerland».
»
More









Illgau



A village of authentic character



The charming mountain village lies on an idyllic and sunny terrace protected from the wind, around 200m above the valley of Muota.
»
More









Immensee



A small paradise



Immensee benefits of a privileged location by Lake Zug.
»
More









Küssnacht



At the foot of the Rigi



Located just by the foot of the Rigi, on the picturesque shores of Lake Lucerne, Küssnacht am Rigi is a popular holiday resort.
»
More









Lachen



The most beautiful sunsets



The commune of Lachen lies on the shore of Lake Zurich and offers one of the longest public lakeside walkways where you can enjoy magnificent sunsets.
»
More









Lauerz



The dreamy holiday resort



The village is located on the picturesque Lake Lauerz on the north slope of the Rigi and was able to maintain its authentic character.
»
More









Merlischachen



Flowery village by the lake



The flowery houses in the village centre are Merlischachen’s symbol.
»
More









Morschach



The panoramic sundeck



Due to its central location, this picturesque mountain village is the perfect starting point for excursions, boat trips, walks and hikes in Switzerland.
»
More









Muotathal



The commune with unwavering determination



The name Muotathal is known well outside the country’s boarders, evoking nature, authenticity and tradition.
»
More









Oberiberg



The highest commune of the canton of Schwyz



Oberiberg is a mountain village and a sunny leisure paradise, located at 1125m.
»
More









Reichenburg



Ideal for families



The village located on the foot of the Austock offers a magnificent view of the lush Linth plain.
»
More









Rothenthurm



The high valley of beavers



Rothenthurm is located in a high valley at approx. 900m, surrounded by a protected marshy landscape.

More









Sattel-Morgarten



The place of the first freedom battle of the Confederat …



Since the Middle Age important transit ways cross at Sattel. Not far from the village, the hamlet of Schornen was the stage to the memorable battle of Morgarten.
»
More









Schübelbach



Attractive recreational facilities



The commune of Schübelbach disposes of a varied club life and a considerable amount of recreation opportunities.
»
More









Schwyz



The capital of the canton



The majestic décor of the two Mythen, the tranquillity, tradition and innovation: those are the village of Schwyz’s main characteristics.
»
More









Steinen



Cherry village



Steinen, the «village of cherries», is located in an idyllic frame between the Rossberg and Lake Lauerz.
»
More









Steinerberg



A wonderful view over the lake of Lauerz



Steinerberg is a commune located on a stunning terrace on the sunny slope of the Rossberg, offering an exceptional panoramic view of Lake Lauerz, the valley of Schwyz and the mountains.
»
More









Stoos



Picturesque mountain village



Stoos is a family-friendly destination for holidays and excursions, located in Schwyz, in the heart of Central Switzerland, high above Lake Lucerne. This small, car-free winter and summer resort is an attractive place for all kinds of sporting activities or just relaxing away from the noise of traffic and hustle and bustle.
»
More









Tuggen



The Linth plain Riviera



Tuggen bestows views over the panorama of the Glarus and St. Gallen Alps. It stands out thanks to its largely intact settlement profile, dominated by its archeologically interesting parish.
»
More









Unteriberg



Place of sports and leisure



A dreamy snowy landscape in the winter, a hiking paradise in the summer.
»
More









Wägital



A matchless idyll



With its magnificent Lake Wägital, the region of Wägital is one of the most beautiful corners of Central Switzerland.
»
More









Wangen



Lives and revitalizes



The commune of Wangen is located in the middle March area at the foot of the Buchberg and borders Lake Zurich on the north side. The political commune encloses the municipalities of Wangen and Nuolen, as well as a part of Siebnen.
»
More









Wollerau
