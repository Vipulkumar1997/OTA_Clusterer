The Deal

A Thanksgiving cruise surrounded by Hawaii's breathtaking landscapes sounds like a good idea to us. Sail roundtrip from Honololu (Oahu) and explore the Big Island, Maui and Kauai during your 7-night, four-island vacation.

What's included and per-person prices:


$1399 ... Nov. 24, Dec. 1, Dec. 8, 2018

Receive $50 in onboard credit per cabin

Choose one perk: one night pre-cruise hotel stay, specialty dining, free gratuities or $50 in shore excursion credit


Ports of call include Kahului, Hilo, Kailua-Kona, Nawiliwili and Na Pali Coast.

Travelzoo Tip:
Take a
helicopter tour excursion for the best way to see the Na Pali Coast. Ride over the stunning coastline to take in Waimea Canyon and Waialeale Crater, where waterfalls, mountains and the ocean collide.

Ship details:
The recently renovated
Pride of America
boasts updated decor, private cabanas, new restaurants and bars, an upgraded spa and fresh interiors. The Hollywood Theater offers Broadway-style performances while Club Mardi Gras provides a lounge atmosphere for dancing into the evening. The spacious ship also features an art gallery and onboard cultural center, which explores the history and traditions of Hawaii, as well as the fleet's only luau-inspired show, Rock-a-Hula.


Book by Dec. 14.

*        Prices are per person, based on double occupancy, and valid for U.S. or Canada residents only. Rates are cruise only and do not include applicable government taxes and fees. Prices, stateroom availability and promotional offers are capacity-controlled and subject to change without notice. Cancellation penalties, blackout dates and additional restrictions may apply. Cruise lines reserve the right to charge a fuel supplement without prior notice should the closing price of West Texas Intermediate Fuel increase above a set amount, determined by each cruise line, on the NYMEX. Cruise lines will have sole discretion to apply the supplementary fuel charge to both existing and new bookings. Minimum age requirements vary by cruise line and destination.
