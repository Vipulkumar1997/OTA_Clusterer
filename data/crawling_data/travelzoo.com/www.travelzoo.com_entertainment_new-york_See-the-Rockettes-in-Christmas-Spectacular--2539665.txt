The Deal

Get started on holiday shopping with tickets to New York City's quintessential holiday show, the "Christmas Spectacular Starring the Radio City Rockettes." Travelzoo members can save up to 50% on select shows through New Year's Day at Radio City Music Hall.

Use the booking calendar to see showtimes. Seats are available in the following levels; prices vary within the ranges by performance date and time:


$24.50-$54 (reg. $39-$60) ... Third Mezzanine

$40-$60 (reg. $50-$80) ... Side Orchestra and Second Mezzanine

$45-$72 (reg. $55-$110) ... Orchestra and First Mezzanine


The cheapest tickets can be found for Monday-Wednesday shows in early December and some 10 p.m. shows throughout the season.

The one-of-a-kind holiday family celebration features dance numbers from the Rockettes with special effects and even Santa himself.

Travelzoo Tip:
Spend the night in New York City and
save more than 40% on stays
at nearby hotels including The Gregory and Iberostar 70 Park Avenue.

Note:
Extra fees apply and are up to $18.35 per ticket, but these are less than on full-price tickets.
