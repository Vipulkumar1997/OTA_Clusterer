The Deal

This Travelzoo-exclusive deal launching today is the best we've seen recently for an Azores getaway. Escape for 7 nights to Terceira Island, with nonstop, roundtrip flights from Boston, for $499 per person -- that's more than $700 off booking on your own.

Just a four-hour flight from Boston, the Portuguese island boasts a UNESCO World Heritage Site Renaissance town, natural lava-rock swimming pools and one of the few volcanoes you can walk inside.

Stay at the 4-star Hotel do Caracol and receive an essential oils and Azorean volcanic stone massage, plus airport transfers and daily breakfast.
