Please note that there may be a visa requirement for your tour and it is the responsibility of each traveller to ensure that they have the correct visa in place prior to departure.
Please do not leave it for the last moment as appointments are busy during the holiday seasons despite been an agent
. If you are not in possession of the proper documentation you may be prevented from boarding your flight or crossing the border.

We strongly advise that you contact our recommended visa agent, CIBT Visas, for the most up to date advice and to purchase any visas required for your trip.

Their online service contains up-to-the-minute information on specific visa requirements for all nationalities to all destinations, and handles over a million possible visa requirements. Travellers do not have the expense of travelling to an embassy or the hassle of standing in a queue. To make sure you know about the visa requirements for your destination, please use the link below.

Please note that CIBT Visas is an independent company from Sona Tours Ltd . Sona Tours Ltd will not accept any liability incurred by yourself whilst using CIBT Visas.

Please contact Sona Tours via email info@sonatours.co.uk with your passport copies for us to prepare the confirmation letter for your visa application.
