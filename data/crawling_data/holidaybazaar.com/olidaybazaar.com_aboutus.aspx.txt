WHY BOOK WITH US

What makes a travel company expert on the destinations it builds trips to? Some might say spending a few weeks a year in those destinations. The international traveler has come a long way with regard to levels of service expectations; that translates into us satisfying our customers with increasingly nuanced levels of service. At Holiday Bazaar, we’re on the ground year-round, with our fingers always on the pulse of what’s new and what’s happening.



But that’s not all that sets us apart. With over 25 years of travel-industry experience in East Africa and combined with expertise you get an agency that can guarantee reliability, safety and peace of mind in the form of:

•The finest in-country services, including same-time-zone access to the very consultant you booked your trip through.

•The most knowledgeable and well-rounded guides, who’ve done their homework on best routes, experiences and programs so they can lead you on a tour that’s all the more interesting and special.

•The strongest support from tried-and-tested suppliers.



Holiday Bazaar Limited has a strong competitive advantage over others in the market not only because of our strategic affiliations with complementing local and international companies but also because of our unrivaled innovative ideals, not to mention our high credibility for delivering on our promise.

Lastly, our itineraries are merely suggestions. We’re just as happy to tailor one of our tours to meet your specific wants and needs, so you can experience the destinations offered on your terms.



Our People

The management teams at Holiday Bazaar are experienced travelers and travel professionals who are knowledgeable, capable and passionate about the services HBL promote and offer. They are confident of their ability to meet the meets of their customers. Holiday Bazaar Travel Consultants are highly trained and experienced having destination knowledge of all the key destinations on offer. This ensures a high standard of performance and productivity.

Ours is personalized, courteous service looking after our clients’ before and after departure. With a staff of dedicated travel professionals, there is always someone to attend to every client’s need as our main priority is the satisfaction of our clients.
