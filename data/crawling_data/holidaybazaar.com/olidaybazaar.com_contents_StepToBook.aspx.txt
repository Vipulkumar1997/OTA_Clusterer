Step to Book














Tour Search





























Sona Tours offer very simple steps to book your holidays online.
Or Click on the link to download the
Booking Form




Reservations:

You can make your reservations by either






Call:



Call in the office to check the availability or request a brochure +44 208 951 0111
                                        & ask for bookings department.






Online:


Visit our website to check the availability and book online -
www.sonatours.co.uk






Email:


Scan the completed booking form together with the payment method.





Post:


To Bookings dept: Sona Tours Ltd, 718 Kenton Road, Kingsbury Circle, Harrow, Middlesex.
                                    HA3 9QX
Nearest station: Kingsbury tube station on Jubilee line, 320 meters walking distance.
                                    Next to VB & Sons Kingsbury branch.





Face to Face:


Walk into our office and process the reservation face to face


Office Address:
Sona Tours Ltd, 718 Kenton Road, Kingsbury Circle, Harrow, Middlesex. HA3 9QX
Nearest station: Kingsbury tube station on Jubilee line, 320 meters walking distance.
                                    Next to VB & Sons Kingsbury branch.


Open:
Monday to Friday 9.00am - 6pm and Saturday (seasonal) 10am - 4pm







Payment Methods:

You can make your reservations by either





Cash:


Only accepted if you visit our office and not by post





Deposit into
Bank:


Visit any Barclays branch (details below) after faxing the booking form and receiving
                                    a reference from the office.





Cheque:


Posting the booking form along with your cheque (payable to Sona Tours Ltd). Cheques
                                    acceptable only if paid 14 days prior to departure.





Debit/Credit card
book online:


Visit www.sonatours.co.uk to book. OR Write your debit or credit card details on
                                    the booking form and forward the form to our office address Please note that UK
                                    debit card incurs a surcharge of £1 and credit card incurs a 3% for total amount.
                                    International credit cards 7%.





Bank Transfer:


a) Email/post completed booking form.
b) Obtain a reference number from our office which you require to process online
                                    bank transfer.
c) Email us a screen shot of the payment confirmation.
Please note that there are no charges when transferring within UK and normally takes
                                    5 working days. If transferring from overseas then, there will be a charge of £25
                                    per transfer which is to be borne by the remitter. Else request for account details
                                    for $ or € and the rates at the time of booking.
