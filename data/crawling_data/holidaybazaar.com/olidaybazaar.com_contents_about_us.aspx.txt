Tour Search





































For Individual Travellers or Elite VIP

Customised for 2 – 16 Passengers


Europe:

We tailor make and specialized on European tour packages with driver cum guide in
                                our 8 and 16 seater vehicles. So no matter if you are a group of 2 to 16 or more
                                we can arrange your entire request. All vehicles will be equipped with air conditioning
                                and CD player with driver cum guide (English, Hindi and Gujurati speaking).

For groups from 9 to 16 passengers we can provide you with a 16 seater Mercedes
                                sprinter or similar with fully panoramic sun proof tinted windows; air conditioning,
                                reclining seats with seatbelts, on board music and DVD player( this vehicle depends
                                on availability).


Elite VIP services in Europe:

If you are looking for Elite VIP treatment, we can tailor make your private tour
                                offering various luxuries such as Private Chauffeur with a Limousine to any luxurious
                                car available at the time of bookings.



International / Worldwide:

We can customise all our international tours for individual or group travel. From
                                    a family of 2 or more to groups, we can arrange for all your requirements. The itineraries
                                    in this brochure are for our group departures. We can, however, tailor holidays
                                    just for you according to your requests for individuals or smaller groups.


Community & Social Groups:


We understand the needs of community spirit and the effort required
                                    to keep it live and active. Thus we
can customise tours for all community
                                groups and cater the dietary requirements where available. It can be with luxury
                                coaches or private limousines for any pilgrimage, pleasure, social events or sports
                                related.

Please call our office to get more details on these packages.
