Find yours within our Himalaya brochure filled with inspiring itineraries, each uniquely different.

Community Project Travel

For those looking to ‘give back’ when they travel, our Community Project Travel range provides a combination of an active holiday in an adventurous destination and an environmental or humanitarian component. Our projects are completely sustainable, aiming to provide assistance to underprivileged communities that receive little to no government support. Operating as a not-for-profit cornerstone of World Expeditions’ travel philosophy, Community Project Travel offers sustainable opportunities for you to get involved and make a difference in Nepal, Tanzania, Kenya, Peru, Cambodia and Australia.

Asia

Cycle Vietnam North to South, pedal along the backroads from Luang Prabang to Hanoi or discover the Mekong Delta on a ride to Ho Chi Minh City. Trek amongst hill tribe villages in Thailand, Vietnam and China, kayak in Halong Bay or take a quiet boat trip down Myanmar’s Irrawaddy River. While history buffs will find plenty of intrigues to discover along the mystical Silk Road from China to Uzbekistan and on the Trans Siberian journey from Beijing to St Petersburg, keen volunteers can get in touch with their altruistic side during our community projects in Vietnam. From the best of Indochina to the myriad of ancient temples and pilgrim trails in Japan or the striking steppe of the Mongolian grasslands, our Asia brochure features family adventures, trekking expeditions, multi-activity itineraries and cultural journeys in the region’s most remote and spectacular places.

South America, Central & North America

Travel in the Americas evokes different things for different people, and our range of active itineraries provides ample opportunity for unforgettable adventures. While trekkers will delight in our fantastic self guided W Trek in Patagonia’s Paine National Park and a journey to the mysterious ‘Lost City’ of northern Colombia, cyclists will find inspiration discovering Cuba or Hawaii’s Oahu island by bike. While wildlife abounds in Guyana and the Galapagos Islands, our Great Canadian Trail adventures immerse you in the incredible wilderness for which Canada is renowned. Whether you’re keen to experience a starry night camping in the grandeur of the Grand Canyon or to climb volcanoes in Chile, travellers including families will find inspiration within the pages of our South, Central and North America brochure.

Africa & Middle East

From north to south, mountain to sea, culture to wildlife, Africa and the Middle East are simply spell bounding destinations. Teeming with nature, magnificent landscapes and diverse cultures, our innovative itineraries continue to satisfy the bucket list and more year after year. While Africa is home to many unique environments and cultures including the Serengeti, Okavango Delta and Kalahari Desert, the fascinating Masai people in Tanzania, the idyllic beaches of Zanzibar, and the Mountain Gorillas of Rwanda and Uganda, the Middle East is crammed with intriguing history and ancient architecture that inspires every visitor in its presence. Order our Africa and Middle East brochure and discover which adventure is waiting for you on one of Earth’s most diverse and incredible continents.

Adventure South



Adventure South NZ is unrivalled in their geographic coverage, originality and overall quality of adventures holidays in New Zealand.  Choose from walking, cycling, winter or multi-activity holidays, on either the North or South Island.






Australian Walking Holidays



Australian Walking Holidays specialises in small group guided walking adventures. Since 1982 we have been guiding small groups of travellers on Australia&apos;s finest walks across our tropical, coastal or red centre landscapes.






Great Canadian Trails



Great Canadian Trails specialises in active holidays in Canada. With both guided and supported self-guided options available, our unique itineraries draw upon some of Canada&apos;s most inspiring parks, trails and landscapes from coast to coast.






Huma Charity Challenge



Huma enables those with adventurous spirits to challenge themselves and make a difference for a cause close to their heart. Travel, fundraise and meet life-long friends on one of Huma&apos;s meaningful and unique challenges around the world.






Sherpa Expeditions



Sherpa Expeditions is a specialist in self guided and guided walking and cycling holidays in the United Kingdom and Europe. Detailed route notes provide a definite guide to places in Europe for active walkers and cyclists.






Tasmanian Expeditions



Tasmanian Expeditions is the most experienced operator of treks and adventure travel holidays in Tasmania. We own and operate the most comprehensive range of adventure holidays available across Tasmania&apos;s varied landscapes.






UTracks



UTracks are the active European holiday specialists. Whether you prefer cycling or walking, 2-star or 4-star, small groups or self guided, land, river or sea – UTracks can help you to explore Europe exactly the way you want.






World Youth Adventures



World Youth Adventures is our specialist division dedicated to organising tailor made overseas school group adventures. Specialists in Service Learning projects, choose from more destinations than any other school group provider.






Yomads



Yomads offers adventures for the 20s and 30s on six continents. Designed as a way to bring young and likeminded travellers together, Yomads caters to those interested in lightly structured and active trips that allow freedom to roam and explore.
