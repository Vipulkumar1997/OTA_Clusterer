Save 10% - when booking your 10th and all subsequent group trips

Terms & Conditions - Adult Loyalty

1) You are entitled to a 5% discount when you book your fifth to ninth Explore group trip and a 10% discount when you book your 10th and all subsequent group trips.
2) Loyalty discounts are available on all Explore small group trips and private trips, also on Self-Guided holidays and Polar Voyages. Bookings with Explore Tailormade are excluded.
3) Discounts will be applied against the base price of the trip including any trip extensions but excluding extras such as flight upgrades and pre- or post- trip accommodation.
4) Discounts will be applied per person, not per booking, depending on each individual’s previous bookings with Explore.
5) Explore loyalty discounts can normally be combined with other promotions, including the Explore group discount, up to a maximum per person discount of 15%. Your loyalty discount will be calculated after deducting any other discount.
6) The discounted sum will be taken from your final invoice, not your deposit payment.
7) A different loyalty scheme applies on Explore Family Adventures, however if you have previously travelled on Family Adventures these bookings may be counted towards this adult loyalty discount offer.
8) Explore reserve the right to change or withdraw this loyalty discount offer at any time and without notice; discounts are offered at our discretion based on our records of each individual’s previous bookings with Explore.


Terms and Conditions – Explore Group Discount

1) You are entitled to a 5% per person discount when you book an Explore trip with a group of six to nine people; if you have ten or more in your group you are entitled to a 10% per person discount.

2) Group discounts are available on all Explore trips with 6 passengers or more, including small group trips and selected private trips.  Bookings on Polar Voyages and Tailormade itineraries are excluded.

3) Group discounts will be applied against the base price of the trip, whether your booking is flight inclusive, trip only or single occupancy.

4) The group discount will include any trip extensions but excludes extras such as flight upgrades and pre- or post- trip accommodation.

5) Group discounts will be applied at the time the booking is made; passengers added to the booking subsequently will not be entitled to the discount.

6) Group bookings will be administered through a single contact address, which will be used for all communications between Explore and your group.

7) If individual cancellations result in the group size falling below the qualifying threshold we reserve the right to reduce or withdraw the discount.

8) Explore group discounts can normally be combined with other promotions, including the Explore loyalty discount, up to a maximum per person discount of 15%. Your loyalty discount will be calculated after deducting any other discount.

9) The discounted sum will be taken from your final invoice, not your deposit payment.

10) Explore reserve the right to change or withdraw this group discount offer at any time and without notice.


Terms and Conditions - Family Loyalty

1) You are entitled to a 5% discount when you book your second and all subsequent Explore Family Adventures trips.
2) Loyalty discounts are available on all Explore Family Adventures group trips and private trips. Bookings with Explore Tailormade are excluded.

6) The discounted sum will be taken from your final invoice, not your deposit payment.
7) A different loyalty scheme applies on adult Explore group trips, whereby the 5% discount is available only on the fifth and subsequent trips; your Family Adventures loyalty discount cannot be applied on adult trips, however your previous bookings on Explore Family Adventures may be counted towards the adult discount offer.
8) Explore reserve the right to change or withdraw this loyalty discount offer at any time and without notice; discounts are offered at our discretion based on our records of each individual’s previous bookings with Explore.


Terms and Conditions - Family Group Discount

2) Group discounts are available on all Explore Family trips with six passengers or more, including small group trips, private trips and tailor-made trips. Bookings on Polar Voyages are excluded.

10) Explore reserve the right to change or withdraw this group discount offer at any time and without notice.
