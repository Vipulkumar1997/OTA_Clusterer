The adventure travel experts


At Explore, we specialise in small group adventure holidays and travel tours for every kind of customer. Whether you're looking for a solo travel experience, an active family adventure or a self-guided walking or cycling holiday, Explore offer over 550 worldwide exploring holidays in over 120 countries.

Our variety of adventure travel holidays take our customers to all corners of the globe. From group walking and trekking tours in Africa and Asia to cycling trips and polar voyages in South America and Europe, we also offer family adventure holidays, trips for solo travellers and much more. Passion and experience are at the heart of our ethos and we pride ourselves on over 36 years of expertise as one of the most trusted adventure travel companies in the UK. We create exciting itineraries that will take you off the beaten track to fully experience the culture of each destination. From the remote tribes of Ethiopia’s Rift Valley to the local people of the high Andean altiplano in Peru, our cultural discovery trips allow you immerse yourself in these rich and alluring ways of life. Our fully trained Explore Leaders will also always be on hand to add a unique 'Explore twist' to your trip while ensuring that responsible tourism remains at the very core of each adventure.

Discover our Explore worldwide adventure tours and travel the Explore way for a fun and unique adventure holiday experience...
