Exclusive discounts just for Erasmus students - Discover Europe!

Enjoy more than 300 special offers across Europe, and reserve the cheapest accommodation!
















Erasmus is probably the best time of your life, now it gets even better! Pay less, Travel longer and Party more with our Special Erasmus Offers! Enjoy over 300 great discounts all around Europe and book the cheapest accommodation available.



We provide
a network of well-rated, reliable and safe and cheap accommodations all over Europe
. All participating properties will be marked with a Special Erasmus symbol. In order to ensure the high quality of the program, we just selected few chosen properties, which proved their high standards already. Whether you just want to travel around Europe or need a place to stay after your arrival while looking for a flat, our offers are tailor-made for you!

Use the form below to search the
discounts for Erasmus Students
in the best hostels in Europe… and beyond!



The best is that the offers doesn’t stop with the discounts, but you also get many cool extra services depending on the property! It varies from free breakfast, free laundry service, free internet, free coffee and tea or a Welcome Drink to more outstanding examples like Free City Tours, Free Bike Rental, Free Concert Tickets or Free Pub Crawl even Free Yoga Lessons! Be ready to be surprised!

How you can take the Special Erasmus Offers?

1. Create your free Lifetime
HostelsClub Membership Card
and immediately save the 2 € Booking Fee. Sign up for free! 100% for free and no hidden costs.


If you already have a HostelsClub account, please contact us.

2. Use the search form in the top of the page, filter the results using the special category 'Erasmus' (on the left side of the page) and start enjoying your Special Erasmus Offers.

Pay less, Travel longer and Party more with our Special Erasmus Offers!



Terms and Conditions:

- The Special Erasmus Offers are exclusively reserved to students participating in the ERASMUS program or another linked exchange program.


- Sign-Up or Log-In to your Lifetime HostelsClub Membership Card Account to save the 2 € Booking Fee.

- You need to identify yourself at Check-In to prove you take part in the ERASMUS program. Please take with you a valid ESN Card, your Student-Id or the Letter of Confirmation of your host university and show it to the responsible person in the hostel upon arrival.

- The discount is granted at Check-In.
