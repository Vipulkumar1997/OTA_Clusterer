About the property

Casa Petronila in El Vedado area is a unique and inviting accommodation perfect for enjoying your stay in Havana, offering visitors over 15 years of experience receiving guests. The hostel is located near 23 St. a main street in Havana and from where is easy to get for all places in the city.



Accommodation

The hostel features 2 charming and spacious bedrooms, with double or twin beds. Each has mini-bar, private bathroom (24-hour hot shower), air-conditioning and more. The master room has a private terrace overlooking the garden.
Each room has capacity for a third traveler (personal bed) at $CUC 5 per night.
High season /Groups: 2 extra triple ensuite rooms are available. Availability up to 12 people. On request.
Free linen and towels are regularly changed and daily room clean up is guaranteed.



Common areas, services and activities

The house is spacious, as well as the common areas: living and dining room, balcony overlooking the garden. You can also enjoy a delicious breakfast, lunch or dinner and have a taste of good Cuban food.
Other services included are:
Free domestic phone calls & private parking.
Free tourism-related information, assistance to exchange your money safely & home stay reservations in other Cuban cities.
Laundry.



Surroundings

Casa Petronila is located centrally in El Vedado, a main area in Havana and from where it is easy to get to all places.
Nearby attractions, many at walking distance:
•	Seafront Boulevard;
•	Fifth Avenue in Miramar;
•	La Rampa St., Coppelia ice cream parlor & Havana University;
•	Revolution Square;
•	Viazul bus station;
•	Cabaret Tropicana, restaurants, nightclubs…
•	Many other interesting sites & tourist services.
Old Havana is 5 minutes in car. East Havana beaches are 20 minutes in car.



Why you should stay here

Casa Petronila offers excellent accommodation all year round for short & long-term stays in Havana. With more than 15 years’ experience and thanks to our expertise, kindness and flexibility in receiving guests, you will experience a nice but real Cuba.
