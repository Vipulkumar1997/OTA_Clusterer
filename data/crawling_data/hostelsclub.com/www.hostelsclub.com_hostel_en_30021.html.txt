About the property

Now bookable! The A&O; München Laim enriches the Bavarian hotel landscape with the beginning of autumn. With its fantastic location directly at the train station “Laim” – just 4 S-Bahn stops from central station – and the brand new furnished rooms in various categories, the house is perfect for all Munich-discoverer, Bavarian lover and budget travelers.



Accommodation

The shared rooms are like the rest of the entire house brand new and offer beside comfortable mattresses always a separate shower and toilet in the room. The single, double and family rooms which constitute the hotel area of the A&O; München Laim are spacious and modern designed and offer 2** comfort.



Common areas, services and activities

Cheap accommodation in the city of traditions will be possible in the latest A&O; München Laim in the hostel- , as well as in the hotel area.



Surroundings

The A&O; München Laim is located in the eponymous district directly at the S-Bahn station Laim, just four stops from central station. The house is located next to the castle grounds with the Nymphenburg Palace and the Royal Deer Garden. Also the vicinity to the city center and the famous Theresienwiese is great!



Why you should stay here

The A&O; München Laim enriches the Bavarian hotel landscape with the beginning of autumn. With its fantastic location directly at the train station “Laim” – just 4 S-Bahn stops from central station – and the about 160 brand new furnished rooms in various categories.
