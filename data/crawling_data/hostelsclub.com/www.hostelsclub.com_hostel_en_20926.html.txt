Accommodation

Villa Makassar has ten non smoking rooms and suites, which spread over 1300 sqm, inspired by the works of influential Art Deco artists from the 1920-1930 decade.

All rooms have been carefully designed and tastefully decorated to the highest standards for your comfort and relaxation, combining the timeless elegance of Art Deco with modern high-tech facilities.

Bathroom or shower, air conditioning, ventilation, smoke detector, flat screen satellite TV/DVD, free internet access, hair dryer...



Common areas, services and activities

Hammam
Massage rooms
Hair and beauty area
Large heated counter-current indoor jacuzzi and outdoor swimming pool
Well-being area with chromotherapy lighting.
24 hour reception
Free Wifi
Reading room with music and board games
Art library
Solarium and sunbeds
Outdoor swimming pool
Restaurant
Roof terrace
Bar with views on Atlas mountains
Laundry and dry cleaning service



Surroundings

A short journey from the airport, villa Makassar is situated in the World-Heritage Medina of Marrakech.

Close to the main attractions of the old city, the Moroccan Royal Palace and Bahia Palace, villa Makassar offers a unique setting and a tranquil space away from the hustle and bustle of the colourful souks of Djema el-Fna square.
