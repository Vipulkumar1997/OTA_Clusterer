About the property

The Scream House was born in September 2010, the property is housed in a liberty style building near to the center of Milan ( 10 minutes by metro), ideal for students, groups or families since its convenience, attractive to business travelers is nice and clean with accurately to provide you with the most comfortable stay. Scream House offers to all of the guests a youthful, friendly and quiet accommodation.



Accommodation

Composed of private rooms ( 2 room, 1 for 5 person and 1 for 6 person) with free internet access and internet point, possibility to deposit you luggage at the front desk all day free! The mixed rooms host from 6 to 10 in bunk beds with the possibility of an extra bed. Included in the price of accommodation: free wireless high speed internet, iron and iron board, free access Kitchen and free lockers. There are two bathrooms not ensuite, showers and hairdryers at disposal.



Common areas, services and activities

The reception is open 24hrs, staff is waiting to welcome you warmly into the hostel and help to the best of their abilities. If you have problems getting orientated in the city they can help you by providing information on public transport, places and maps of the city.
For anyone who wants to drink coffee in the morning near to the reception is coffee machine and there are several wooden tables and chairs where you can comfortably sit.



Surroundings

Excellent location for those coming from the central station because we are 5 minutes from it. From Central Station take the subway line 2 (green) in the direction of Cascina Gobba and get off at Loreto. Take metro 1 (red line) towards Sesto FS and get off after two stops at Rovereto. Una Once outside you have to look after the two bridges, our hostel is in the middle of their, 1 min by walking. When you arrive at the main gate, call at the intercom 2024 and the receptionist will open for you.
