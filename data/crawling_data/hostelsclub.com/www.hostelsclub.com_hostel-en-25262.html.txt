About the property

Licet and Pepe welcomes you to Cuba to Casa particular open 24 hours in La Havana.



Accommodation

Our guest house has 2 rooms with private bathroom, each one with living room.



Common areas, services and activities

We will provide you with Internet access, personal refrigerator, spacious balcony, dining. We offer hot water, air conditioner, tv, refrigeration, and cuban happy family. We will give you information of everything about our city!



Surroundings

You will be in walking distance of Calle 23rd a local hot spot, and a short distance from Habana Vieja (Old Havana), University of Havana, Amejeiras Brother’s Hospital, etc. Late night snacks can be obtained at two restaurants located virtually next door. Other local attractions within walking distance include Hotel National and Hotel Habana Libre, Museums, Theatres and other interesting places.



Why you should stay here

You will have the pleasure of experiencing the Havana sunset drinking Mojito´s, coffee and other Cuban drinks while enjoying this lively location.
