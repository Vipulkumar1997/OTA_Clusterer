About the property

The Hotel Vip Zurique is strategically located in the new financial center of Lisbon, just a few minutes from the historic center of Lisbon and the international airport.



Accommodation

It has 252 rooms, 4 suites and 2 rooms for the disabled.



Common areas, services and activities

The hotel has free internet access (wireless), car park, 09 fully equipped meeting rooms, with capacity for up to 300 people; most of the rooms have natural light.
The restaurant "O Infante" offers a variety of Portuguese and international cuisine, the self-service cafeteria is a good choice to have a lighter meal at a lowest rate. You can also enjoy a drink at the bar or by the pool (seasonal).



Surroundings

The Hotel Vip Zurique is located in the heart of the new financial area in Lisbon. Near the Praça de Touros, and the Fundação Calouste Gulbenkian gardens and museums.



Why you should stay here

The Hotel Vip Zürich it is only 15 minutes from the International Airport and 5 minutes from the city center.

Terms and conditions

The property accepts the following credit card brands for balance payments: Visa, Mastercard, Visa Electron, American Express, Diners, Debit Cards.
The property reserves the right to pre-authorise credit cards prior to arrival.
Taxes not included
: Please note that from the 1st of January 2016 a city tax of EUR 1 per person, per night is not included in the total price and should be paid on site. This tax is charged to guests aged 13 and older. It is subject to a maximum amount of EUR 7 per guest..
Free cancellation at least 24 hours before arrival date, first night of stay fee for late cancellation or no show
