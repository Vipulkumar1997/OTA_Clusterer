About the property

The Hostel of Rene y Mary is a large colonial house, located in the oldest most preserved zone of Trinidad, located in the central Cuban province of Sancti Spíritus an UNESCOs World Heritage sites since 1988.



Accommodation

The house is decorated with old fashioned furniture and family treasures. It has a spacious patio that offer guests with a quiet and relaxing vibe, alongside exotics plants and a refreshing pool.



Common areas, services and activities

Adjacent to the garden is an independent romantic terrace that provide privacy to the visitors. Friendly hosts and family, accommodate each tourist with a warming welcome and exquisite service.



Surroundings

Only 5-8 minutes from the city's Bus station walking distance, and just 5 minutes from all the tourist sites of Trinidad.
