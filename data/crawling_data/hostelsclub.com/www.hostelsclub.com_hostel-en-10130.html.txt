About the property

To book Backpackers Paradise Los Angeles means to enjoy several additional free services like LAX Airport Shuttles which can help travellers save on their travel budget.



Accommodation

Accommodation is provided in budget dorms and private rooms. All dormitories have private bathrooms, they can fit from 4 to 20 beds and can be male, female and mixed.



Common areas, services and activities

This hostel boasts of an outside heated pool and complimentary services including 24-hour airport shuttle service, free WiFi, free continental breakfast, (Free Champagne, Free Cookies, Tea, Coffee, Free Cake) and free ride to the beach.



Surroundings

Backpackers Paradise Los Angeles is located only few minutes from LAX airport: booked guests can use the free 24hr shuttle to and from the airport.



Why you should stay here

Backpackers Paradise Los Angeles is the ideal hostel accommodation when backpacking to Los Angeles and the ideal place where to meet other international travellers and backpackers.
