About the property

Piero Rotta Milan Hostel is well positioned for guests intending to visit sights of artistic and cultural interest such as Milan’s Cathedral and Castle, the Santa Maria delle Grazie Church with Leonardo da Vinci’s Last Supper, as well as night-life around the Brera and Navigli areas, not to mention the ethnic restaurants, top-name fashion stores, sport facilities and San Siro Stadium.



Accommodation

Hostel Piero Rotta offers different accommodations according to your needs: for those who want to save money and have fun we suggest our 6 beds male or female dorms with individual lockers (padlock provided).

If you're looking for a comfortable stay you can book a 'deluxe 2 beds' room, which includes bath set, daily changed towels and you'll find your BEDS DONE as in a hotel.

This hostel in Milan is a welcoming place specially for young people, families, school trips and sport organizations.



Common areas, services and activities

Milan hostel guests will find many good facilities, like: free Internet Wi-Fi, Satellite TV in the lounge, microwave ovens, vending machines, towels for hire, coin laundry, bike parking and car-sharing.

There's also a big garden surrounding the hostel, for a relaxing stay (not easy to find such a green area in Milan!).



Surroundings

Easy to reach for those travelling by train or airplane, but also for those arriving by car or coach. The Milan hostel is not far from the motorway and provides a large car park to pay (€0.80 per hour).



Why you should stay here

HI Hostel Milan is officially recognized by Hostelsclub as Eco-friendly! This hostel in Milan is becoming LEED certified and getting renovated with a sustainable philosophy. HI Milan believes in eco-tourism all the way!

Terms and conditions

The property accepts the following credit card brands for balance payments: Visa, Mastercard, Visa Electron, American Express, JCB, Aura, Diners, Debit Cards.
The property reserves the right to pre-authorise credit cards prior to arrival.
Taxes not included
: City tax 2€ per person per night (for guests older than 30).
This property charges 1 night/s if canceled less than 2 days before the arrival date.
The hostel is affiliated to the circuit AIG and Hostelling International.
All our guests must be members of either one of these associations.
Guests can register on arrival, the cost of the membership varies depending on the length of stay and on the card profile, it starts from the €2 welcome stamp per night per guest.
All guests that will collect six stamps, will be granted an yearly membership.
