Information Hub New Lisbon Hostel

Want to experience something completely
new and awesome
in Lisbon? Hub New Lisbon is in the
best location
near Bairro Alto and Príncipe Real and has 162 brand new beds waiting for your arrival!
You are in the
heart of the city
, within walking distance to streets without car traffic noise, full of locals restaurants, the coolest neighborhoods, fun bars, clubs, eclectic shops, rare gardens and a plethora of museums and sites for exploring. Our amazing, professional and passionate crew-all great travelers as well- will help you get around and explore all the best places in Lisbon.
Inside the hostel we have several options for having fun or just hanging around to relax. Who said kids should get to have all the fun? Imagine a therapeutic
“swim” in our “pool”
filled with soft plastic balls? Adults highly encouraged to take the plunge!
For addicts we have games like
foosball tables
and
Playstaytion
. If you are more in the mood to relax, you can spend time soaking in the sun on the
lounge terrace
with outdoor showers or enjoy an artisinal cocktail at the
bar on-site
. Our fireplace will warm your sore feet on a cool night after touring all day. The bar is open to the public providing a chance to mix and mingle with guests, other travelers and locals.
Security
is a priority with a
24 hour desk
and
card key access
only to rooms. Bright and colorful
all-female dorms
are also available.
Several options of restaurants nearby and we also have a kitchen where you can prepare your own meals.
At Hub New Lisbon Hostel you will feel
like a 'alfacinha', a true Lisbon native!

The hostel has:
-Free breakfast
-Movie/ Cable TV/Music
-Outdoor Shower with sun lounge
-New beds, pillows, linens-Power plugs (3 per bed)
-Private reading lights-Plenty of bathrooms and showers (some dorm rooms have their own)
-Doubles with city and river views
-Lockers for international size travel bags (bring a lock or buy one in the machine)
-All female dorm wing
-Security
-On-site Bar
-Fireplace
-Games
-Vending machine
Hub New Lisbon Hostel Policies and Conditions:
-Check in from 15:00 to 22:00 -Check out at 11:00 a.m.
Cancellation policy
:
-24 hours before arrival, based on 3 p.m. check-in time
-Payment upon arrival by cash, credit cards, debit cards
-This property may pre-authorize your credit card before arrival
General:
-Taxes included
-No curfew
-Non-smoking
-Towels rental available for 2 euro

Directions Hub New Lisbon Hostel

Airport - 8 km | Belém - 8 km | Caparica Beaches - 20 km | Estoril & Cascais Beaches - 25 km | Sintra - 30 km
From airport:
Transfer/Shuttle
(around 40 minutes) - 1 person 10€ | 2 to 7 5€ per person | 8 4,5 € per person. Shuttle receives people from other hostels.
Subway
(30min - 1,40€)) red line to Saldanha > yellow line to Rato > walk through Rua da Escola Politécnica to Jardim do Príncipe Real (garden), at the end turn to right and go down Rua de O Século nº 150.
Aerobus line 1
(40 min - 3,15€) to Cais do Sodré (last stop) > bus line 758 and stop at Praça do Príncipe Real > go down Rua de O Século nº 150. Tip: iwalk from Cais do Sodré. Take Alecrim Street to Príncipe Real Garden. Turn left to Rua de O Século nº 150. Around 20/25 min.
Taxi
(20min - around 15€). Tip, take the taxi at departures
