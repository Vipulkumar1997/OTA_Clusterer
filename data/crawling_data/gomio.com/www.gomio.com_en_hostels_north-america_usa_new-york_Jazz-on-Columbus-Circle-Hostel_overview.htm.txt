Information Jazz on Columbus Circle Hostel

Welcome to Jazz on Columbus Circle! Experience NYC when you walk through our doors, immerse yourself in the history and culture of the city through our themed floors and rooms. Nestled next to Central Park, Jazz on Columbus Circle is a stone’s throw away from the world famous Columbus Circle traffic circle and minutes from Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts.
* At check-in, all guests must present a valid passport or valid out-of-state ID.
* Please note Residents of New York cannot be accommodated at this property.

Directions Jazz on Columbus Circle Hostel

Getting There Information:

Driving Directions

From JFK Airport:
Get on I-678 N from 130th Pl and Bergen Rd. Follow I-678 N and I-495 W to E 37th St in Manhattan. Exit from I-495 W. Take 3rd Ave and E 47th St to 8th Ave
From Laguardia:
Get on Grand Central Pkwy Take Brooklyn Queens Expy E, I-278 W and I-495 W to E 37th St in Manhattan. Exit from I-495 W Take E 42nd St to 8th Ave.
Public Transport From JFK:
Take the Airtrain to Jamaica Station Take the World Trade Center bound E train Get off at 7th Avenue Station and walk to the hostel.
From LaGuardia:
Take the Q70 bus  Get off at Roosevelt Av/ 74th Street Transfer to the World Trade Center bound E train at Jackson Heights- Roosevelt Avenue Station Get off at 7th Avenue Station Walk to the hostel
