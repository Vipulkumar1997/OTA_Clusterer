What to See in Cusco, Peru


Cusco
is the capital of the Inca Empire and one of the most visited cities in
Peru
. You can see the fascinating history of the Incas through the various museums and galleries or you can visit some of the ancient ruins. Cusco is a popular starting point for those wanting to make the trek to Machu Picchu, an old, abandoned Incan site that is shrouded in mystery.
Gomio
has
budget hostels
in Cusco that allow you to access many of the best tourist attractions such as the markets and the Plaza de Armas.
