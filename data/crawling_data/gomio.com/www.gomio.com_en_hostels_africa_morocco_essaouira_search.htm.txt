Essaouira
is a relaxed and artsy fishing town in
Morocco
with renowned woodwork and lacquerware. The beaches are beautiful for windsurfing and kitesurfing as well as camel and horseback rides.
Gomio
offers Moroccan style
hostels
in Essaouira that are cheap, clean and fun! The
hostels
are conveniently located in the middle of the medina but the rest of Essaouira is also easily accessible by nearby public transportation.
