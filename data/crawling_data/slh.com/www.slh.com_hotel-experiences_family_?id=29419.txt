Luxury for all ages


There’s never a bad time to get the whole clan together for a holiday, and SLH offers luxurious family-friendly hotels that span both seasons and borders.

You can experience the bliss of an all-inclusive beach break, explore the African wilderness on a magical safari or let loose in the concrete jungle of New York with your family, from tots and toddlers to tweens and teens. SLH provides a number of properties suitable for families with young children, with babysitting services and a range of children’s activities available.

So pack up the sun cream, the safari gear and comfy shoes for city walking, and take a look at the many possibilities SLH offers to young families.
