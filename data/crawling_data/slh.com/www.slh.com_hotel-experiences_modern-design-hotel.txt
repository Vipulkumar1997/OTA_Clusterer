LUXURY BY DESIGN


Sleek architecture. Sublime interiors. Experience the ultimate in 21st-century luxury in our modern design retreats. We’ve carefully chosen the most striking and elegant destinations, assembling a selection of small hotels that make a big style statement.

Discover avant-garde masterpieces, Art Deco boutiques and thoroughly modern mansion houses. Escape inside centuries-old palaces turned über-cool havens, or futurist landmarks breaking new ground. At these independent hotels you’ll find gravity-defying restaurants, underground spas and pools that seem to hover in mid air.

Created by world-famous architects and trend-setting designers, every luxury hotel in our collection is brimming with character – and wrapped up with exceptional service.
