An Adrenalin Rush Laced with Luxury


Looking for an independently-minded, action-packed getaway to please even the most daring thrill-seeker? You’ve come to the right place.

Exploring the world wouldn’t be the same without an injection of the extreme to really get that adrenalin pumping. With over 520 independently-spirited hotels in more than 80 countries, the opportunities to explore the world are endless with SLH.

From jungle treks in Thailand to adventure sailing in the Greece to extreme sports in New Zealand, there’s no experience that can’t be discovered with our activity-laden hotels. And the best news?
