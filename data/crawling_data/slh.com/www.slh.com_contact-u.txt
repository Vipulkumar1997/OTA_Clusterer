Reservation enquiries via email

If you would prefer to email us, please send your enquiry to
reservation@slh.com
. All emails are monitored Monday to Friday only. If you require urgent assistance, especially during weekends and public holidays when the office is closed, we suggest you to contact our reservation team using our toll free numbers for an immediate response.

Ammending or cancelling an existing reservation

If you have made your reservation on slh.com you can
amend or cancel your reservation online
(cancellation charges may apply, please check your booking cancellation policy before cancelling). Please ensure that if you cancel your reservation, a cancellation number needs to be generated to confirm your booking is fully cancelled, without a cancellation number you may be charged for a no-show fee.
