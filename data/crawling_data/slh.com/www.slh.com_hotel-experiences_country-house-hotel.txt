Escape to the Country


Sink into a more leisurely pace of life with our exclusive country retreats. Time seems to stand still at these out-of-town havens, where days and nights are given over to total indulgence.

A country house escape could see you sipping fine wines, surrounded by rolling vineyards. Or living your very own fairytale in an enchanting, hilltop castle. Our collection spans the quintessential to the quirky – from classic manor houses to forest lodges, lakeside villas to luxury suites in cliffs and caves.

With over 100 small hotels in gorgeous locations, you’ll be spoilt for choice. Whether you want to explore the great outdoors, or simply curl up by the fire and relax.
