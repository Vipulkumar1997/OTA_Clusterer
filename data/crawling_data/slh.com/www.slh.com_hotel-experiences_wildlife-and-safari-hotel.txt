WALK ON THE WILD SIDE


Brave the far flung plains for a luxury escape that promises the thrill of adventure with the comfort of luxury. Our exclusive game lodges are perfectly placed to encounter elusive wildlife and immerse yourself in a rustic wilderness experience in some of the world's most incredible savannahs.

Spend your days traversing these amazing landscapes on exhilarating game drives, watch the spectacle of the wildebeest migration, or even a balloon safari over the African plains. In the evening, relax with an indulgent spa treatment, dine on authentic and exotic cuisine, and retire to the elegance of your luxury tent.

Each of our exclusive game lodges has been designed to remain authentic to their impressive surroundings whilst retaining all the touches of modern luxury. Choose from one of the properties below and embark on an adventure like no other.
