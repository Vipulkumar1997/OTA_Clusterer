TAKE THE PLUNGE


Perched on rooftops, carved from rocks or in dense tropical forest – at our boutique retreats you’ll take a dip in some of the world’s most beautiful settings.

Get a taste of paradise at a swim-up cocktail bar. Or sink into a steaming saltwater lagoon, surrounded by snowy mountains. Watch the sun set from the very edge of an infinity pool. Or look up at the stars from your own, private plunge pool.

Whether it’s a city break, beach retreat or countryside escape, these small, luxury hotels give you the perfect opportunity to stretch out in luxury.
