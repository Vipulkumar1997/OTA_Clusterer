A TASTE OF LUXURY


Seeking out new flavours and creative cuisine is one of the best things about any holiday. With our collection of exclusive hotels boasting celebrated chefs and irresistible menus, you won’t have to go far to find them.

Pairing luxury surroundings with the finest culinary experiences, these boutique retreats satisfy all the senses.You could be sampling fresh fare from the surrounding grounds, or contemporary takes on regional specialities.
Each of the hotels below holds at least one of the following awards: Michelin Star, AAA 4 Diamond, James Beard Award, Miele, Foodie, Gault et Millau, Good Food Guide, Der Feinschmecker, Forbes, Gambero Rosso, L’Espresso.

You’ll find our fine dining escapes in buzzing cities and the peaceful countryside, beside beaches and up in the mountains. The only question is, where will you try first?
