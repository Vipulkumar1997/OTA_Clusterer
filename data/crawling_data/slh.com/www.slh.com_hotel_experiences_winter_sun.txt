SOAK UP THE SUN THIS WINTER


The gloom and doom of winter looms over many, but fear not – the sun will come up tomorrow if you head to the summer hotspots this winter.

No matter where you are, you’re only a hop, skip and a jump away from some sizzling hot weather. That’s the beauty of having over 500 independently minded hotels in more than 80 destinations – it’s always summer (and happy hour!) somewhere.

So where will you escape to? There are so many options. There’s the vibrancy of Mexico, the untouched beaches of New Zealand, the chilled out vibe of the Caribbean, the Tanzanian touch in Zanzibar…the list goes on..

At the end of the day, the choice is yours. Choose sun!
