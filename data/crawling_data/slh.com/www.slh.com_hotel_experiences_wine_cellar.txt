AN EXPERIENCE TO SAVOUR


Cool caverns of ancient stone lined with bottles of some of the world’s finest vintages – our hotels with wine cellars are the perfect treat for the true gourmet.

Savour cool, crisp whites and rich, vibrant reds as you relax on your luxury getaway. Our collection of wine-lovers’ escapes can be found by stunning mountain ranges, tucked away in vibrant city centres and dotting some of the world’s most renowned wine regions. Where will your taste take you?

Each boutique hotel offers the chance to indulge in fine dining, and many boast the finest wines their regions have to offer. Let us help you find your perfect pairing.
