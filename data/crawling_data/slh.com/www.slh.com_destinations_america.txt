INTRODUCING The AMERICAS


Modern cities, ancient cultures, iconic landmarks and incredible landscapes – the Americas are as vibrant and varied as life itself. From the Empire State Building to the Golden Gate Bridge, America is big on must-see sights and must-have luxury. Discover the continent’s wild side in tropical Costa Rica and inspiring Argentina. Or get off the beaten track and climb volcanoes, watch whales, or laze on picture-perfect beaches in Mexico. The Americas may be vast, but from the luxury of your small, boutique hotel everything’s within easy reach.






Welcome to Mother Nature’s adventure playground – thrilling cities and mysterious ruins, pristine beaches and pure wilderness.

Highlights


CITY BREAK
The Americas are home to many of the world’s greatest cities. Soak up the trend-setting style and movie star looks of New York, LA, San Francisco, Miami and Vancouver. Delve into the curious mash-up of ultra-ancient and super-modern in Mexico City. Or discover South America’s sultry side in beautiful Buenos Aires. For the inside track on what’s happening – and what’s not – just ask at your boutique hotel.

COUNTRY RETREAT
Explore every imaginable landscape – from the end-of-the-world beauty of Patagonia’s Ushuaia to the natural majesty of Yellowstone National Park. In the Americas you could escape to a tropical beach in Costa Rica or lose yourself in the Galapagos Islands. For a fresh perspective on your destination, try horse riding, trekking, scuba diving or wine tasting.

BEACH
Whether you want the world’s finest surf or most stunning snorkelling, the Americas has it all. You could follow the in crowd to world-famous hang outs along the California coast or find your private paradise in Nicaragua. Or, for mysterious ruins, swaying palms and crystal-clear waters, head for the Caribbean coast of Mexico and Belize. Like the local tequila, it’s an intoxicating mix.
