THE CITY AT YOUR FEET


Arts and culture? Festive winter breaks? Designer boutiques and buzzing nightlife? Historic sites and fine dining? Whatever you’re looking for, our small, city centre hotels put you at the heart of where it happens.

You could live like royalty in grand, former palaces, or cosy up in welcoming townhouses set in quiet squares. Experience cutting-edge cosmopolitan style at chic design hotels. Or take a break from the hustle and bustle with private resorts wrapped in urban gardens.

Each boutique hotel is as unique as the city around it. And behind every door is a world of luxury, just waiting to be discovered.
