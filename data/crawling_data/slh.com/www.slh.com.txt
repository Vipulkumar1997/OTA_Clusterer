Boutique Hotels & Resorts | Small Luxury Hotels of the World




Be Inspired

Can't decide between a relaxing beach holiday or a cultural city break?







Spa Resorts



Balance mind and body, book an indulgent treat or simply seek out a bit of ‘me time’. With our collection of small, luxury spa resorts you’ll find the space to truly unwind.








BEACH RESORTS



Hidden bays, pure white sands, swaying palms – there are times when only a beach holiday will do. At our selection of small but perfectly formed hotels, you’ll find those special spots that seem truly out of this world.








Fine Dining Hotels



Seeking out new flavours and creative cuisine is one of the best things about any holiday. With our collection of exclusive hotels boasting celebrated chefs and irresistible menus, you won’t have to go far to find them.








Ski Resorts



Imagine racing down a thrilling ski run, mountain views opening out before you. Or perhaps gazing out across snow-covered scenery while you float in a private indoor pool.








Adult Only Hotels



Boutique boltholes. Heavenly hideaways. When it’s a trip away for just the two of you, or a weekend with close friends, an exclusive adults-only escape can be just the ticket.








CITY CENTRE HOTELS



Arts and culture? Festive winter breaks? Designer boutiques and buzzing nightlife? Historic sites and fine dining? Whatever you’re looking for, our small, city centre hotels put you at the heart of where it happens.










Private Island Hotels



Be the first to leave footprints in pure, white sands, or to dive beneath turquoise waters. On a private island you’ll experience the life of luxury as a 21st-century castaway – and could even have your very own butler.








Modern Design Hotels



Sleek architecture. Sublime interiors. Experience the ultimate in 21st-century luxury in our modern design retreats. We’ve carefully chosen the most striking and elegant destinations, assembling a selection of small hotels that make a big style statement.








Family Hotels



There’s never a bad time to get the whole clan together for a holiday, and SLH offers luxurious family-friendly hotels that span both seasons and borders.








Country House Hotels



Sink into a more leisurely pace of life with our exclusive country retreats. Time seems to stand still at these out-of-town havens, where days and nights are given over to total indulgence.








All-Inclusive Hotels



Embrace the life of leisure, with small hotels that offer an all-encompassing, luxury experience. Where, from the moment you arrive, you can relax knowing that absolutely everything will be taken care of.








Hotels with Pools



Perched on rooftops, carved from rocks or in dense tropical forest – at our boutique retreats you’ll take a dip in some of the world’s most beautiful settings.










Golf Resorts



Discover some of the world’s finest courses in our selection of luxury golf resorts. From a relaxing day on the green, to practicing your swing in a world-class training centre, these stylish hotels will inspire novices to pros.








Hotels with Rooftops



Imagine taking a dip in a moonlit infinity pool. Savouring a world-famous skyline from a rooftop bar. Or enjoying an evening of fine dining accompanied by panoramic vistas. With our selection of small, luxury hotels, the sky’s the limit.








Michelin® Hotels



Wake your taste buds up with delectable dishes that floor you with their intricate flavours, calculated preparation and impeccable presentation.








Hotels with Wine Experiences



Enjoy a leisurely getaway at laid-back farmhouses and elegant châteaux, surrounded by sun-soaked vineyards. Unwind in expansive, lakeside ranches in renowned wine regions, or delve into treasure-filled cellars beneath chic, city centre retreats.








Honeymoon Hotels



For some, the perfect honeymoon is about giving in to utter luxury. For others, it’s the chance to plan the adventure of a lifetime. With our carefully selected boutique hotels, you can have the best of both worlds.








Lake and Waterfront Hotels



From mystical pools deep in the jungle to shimmering mountain lakes, you needn’t head to the sea to find a waterside paradise. Our collection of lake and waterfront hotels are perched on some of the world’s most beautiful shores.
