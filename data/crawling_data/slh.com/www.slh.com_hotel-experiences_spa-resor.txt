TIME TO UNWIND


Balance mind and body, book an indulgent treat or simply seek out a bit of ‘me time’. With our collection of small, luxury spa resorts you’ll find the space to truly unwind.

Step into bubbling thermal springs or cliff-side Jacuzzi pools. Enjoy a traditional massage in a rainforest pavilion, or experience the very latest therapies amid snow-capped mountains. These exclusive spas can be found dotted across the countryside, on secluded islands and tucked away in the heart of cosmopolitan cities.

Each one is unique. All are set within beautiful, boutique hotels – and wrapped up with exceptional service. Which will you escape to?
