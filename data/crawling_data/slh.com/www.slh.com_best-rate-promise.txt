Best Rate Promise










OUR PROMISE


We believe you won’t find a better publicly available rate for our hotels anywhere else. If you make a reservation on SLH.com and then find a lower rate elsewhere with the same booking conditions, we will revise your booking to the lower rate.









Making a Claim


If you find a lower published rate after booking your stay at any Small Luxury Hotels of the World property, simply contact us to make a claim. Please email us at
promise@slh.com
. We will match the lower rate if the below conditions are met:


The claim is made for an existing SLH reservation within 24 hours of booking

The SLH booking confirmation reference is provided with the hotel name, arrival and departure dates and room type booked

The SLH booking must be with a fully flexible rate and within the cancellation deadline

The two rates must be still available when we process the claim

The conditions of the lower rate must be the same:

It is for the same room type and dates

It is in the same currency used by the hotel

It is with the same meal plan, cancellation policy, deposit policy



The lower rate must be a published rate available not restricted to members only

A screenshot and URL of the website showing the lower rate with the total amount (tax included) in the same currency










The Best Rate Promise does not apply if:



The above information cannot be provided or the details are incorrect

The booking is made within 3 days of arrival date (weekends excluded)

The lower rate is for ‘secret’ hotels revealed after the booking is confirmed

The claims are made comparing different currencies

The lower rate is available through special memberships or loyalty points
