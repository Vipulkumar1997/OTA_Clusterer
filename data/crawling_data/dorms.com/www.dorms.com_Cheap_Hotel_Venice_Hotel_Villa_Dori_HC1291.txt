Just a 10 minutes ride by shuttle service from the historic heart of Venice and the Riviera del Brenta, 3 stars hotel Venice Hotel Villa Dori offers elegant rooms, inspired by the sumptuous Venetian Villas and palazzos, enhanced by superior comfort and services. Links between the hotel, Venice, the airport and train station are pleasantly assured by the Venice Hotel Villa Dori transfer service.
-We only accept credit cards. Debit cards or any card with the writing 'ELECTRONIC USE ONLY' will be not accepted by us to guarantee the reservation.

-We reserve the right to pre-authorise bookers' credit cards at time of reservation and anytime before check-in: if the amount will be not pre-authorisable by our POS, we will e-mail the booker, requiring to send us new credit card details within 24 hours. After this limit the reservation will be automatically cancelled.

LOCATION:
On the Riviera del Brenta, in the peaceful Venetian countryside, just several minutes from the historic center of Venice, 7 km from Mestre and 25 km from Padua, Venice Hotel Villa Dori is one of the 3-star hotels ideally located between the Riviera del Brenta and Venice, for a vacation surrounded by the splendid Venetian Villas, discovering the magic of Venice. The hotel is also ideally located for business travellers since the Venice Hotel Villa Dori is easy to reach from the Mestre ring road, the A4 and A27 highways and from the large corporations in Marghera and the many small and medium-sized industries scattered between Mestre and Padua.
The Riviera del Brenta (State road 11) is a one minute car ride from the Venice Hotel Villa Dori. Hugging the coast of the Brenta river channel that reaches Padua (25 km) reveals a series of extraordinary Venetian Villas and delightful towns (Oriago, Mira, Dolo, Stra) that create one of the most alluring scenarios in the Veneto Region. The Riviera is also serviced by ACTV bus line 53 (stop 200 meters from Villa Dori) which runs from Venice to Padua, a must on your stay at a hotel on the Riviera de Brenta.
To visit Venice, guests can use the hotel shuttle to/from Venice - Piazzale Roma or take bus 53 (stop 200 meters from the hotel). Both stop in Piazzale Roma, the car terminal and starting point for your walking or ferry tour of the most beautiful and acclaimed sites of Venice: St. Mark's square, the Rialto bridge, the Gallerie dell'Accademia, etc.
Downtown Mestre is easy to reach from the Hotel Villa Dori on bus 10 CA' SABBIONI that stops in front of Villa Dori. A pleasant stroll along the Piazza Feretto with its unusual elongated form leads to the characteristic Torre dell'Orologio, the only one left standing of the ancient Medieval castle. Cinemas, theaters, boutiques, restaurants and coffee houses enliven the square and environs both day and night.

OUR SERVICES:
Resplendently sophisticated and sumptuous, the rooms at the Venice Hotel Villa Dori stand out from those in 3-star hotels on the Riviera del Brenta and in Venice for their elegance and comfort.
The interior decor inspired by Venetian Villas and historic Venetian palazzos delightfully combines lush fabrics, precious marble and stylish furnishings with authentic antiques.
Amenities include a reception (24h), hall, TV room,hotel between Venice and the Riviera del Brenta.
A splendid American style breakfast buffet (not included in room price)of hot and cold pastries and baked goods is served in the breakfast room accompanied by traditional Italian coffees (espresso, cappuccino etc.). Breakfast may be served in your room upon request.
Services are the pride of the Venice Hotel Villa Dori: transfer shuttles accompany hotel guests to Venice, the Venice airport and Mestre train station; internet, fax service and daily newspapers are also offered; tourist information is provided at the reception where guests can book trips and guided tours of the Villas in the Riviera del Brenta and Venice. The hotel provides free parking for its guests.
Venice Hotel Villa Dori is also an ideal location for small meetings and events or to celebrate achievements or occasions. 2 meeting rooms with modern audio and video equipment are available for guests who wish to hold an event in a hotel between Venice and the Rivera del Brenta. Coffee breaks, buffets, business lunches and dinners are assured by the Tantra Cafe, the hotel's acclaimed restaurant.
At the reception, hotel staff provides tourist information, suggestions and bookings for guided tours and restaurants in Venice (even in gondola) and to the Venetian Villas in the Riviera del Brenta as well as theater and concert tickets.

ROOMS:A wide range of rooms and suites decorated with charm & fashion await you at the Venice Hotel Villa Dori, an outstanding 3-star hotel in Venice and the Riviera del Brenta for its superior individuality and comfort.
Each room has a private ensuite bathroom,a direct phone line, alarm clock, safe, satellite TV, hair dryer, adjustable air conditioning and heating. The quadruple room has 1 double bed + 1 bunk bed.Please contact us via e-mail to arrange the bed configuration (queen size, twin...)

TANTRA CAFE RESTAURANT & BAR:The intriguing and romantic Tantra Cafe is the illustrious, exclusive and cordial restaurant at the Venice Hotel Villa Dori, open to both hotel guests and the public. Surrounded by the latest fashion decor, an original mix of classic Venetian, ethnic and design, impeccable service and typical Venetian and Italian cuisine with hints of ethnic and international flavor welcome diners for an unforgettable sophisticated and authentic dining experience. Venetian specialties like 'sarde in saor' (fried sardines on a vinaigrette bed) and spaghetti with clams are alternated with Argentinean angus steak, sushi, grilled fish and the traditional Italian pizza baked in wood-burning ovens. The wine selection is astonishing: 800 Italian and international labels with more than 150 types of champagne to accompany any dish and satisfy even the most refined taste.
The Tantra cafe is not just a restaurant but also a meeting place where you can try over 130 types of cocktails and liquors while enjoying good music. This is an extremely versatile and unique location for any type of event, even during the day: debates, conventions, meetings, conferences, shows, private parties, business dinners or banquets, show cases, private viewing, expos, fashion shows, photographic or cinematographic sets.
