The hotel is located near (400 m) a metro station “Hloubětin” (15 min from the centre of the city). Everywhere in the hotel is free access Wi-Fi. Public parking is on the street in front of the hotel. On the hotel’s ground floor is a restaurant with a big TV-screen were you can anjoy czech and international cuisine. Over there you can order a special menu that will be prepared for you, by our chef. The Hotel has 14 different rooms. Every room has bath, WC, fan, Satellit-TV (czech, english, german channels). You can pay both cash and cards (Visa, Master-Card). Our friendly staff answer you with pleasure for all your questions. Nearby is situated a swimming pool, tennis court and Arena O2.
