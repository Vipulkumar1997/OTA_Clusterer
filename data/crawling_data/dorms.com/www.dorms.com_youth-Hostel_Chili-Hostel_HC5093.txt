Chili hostel rocks. Explore and enjoy Prague, People, Parties from our Hostel the Hot Spot in the heart of Prague 1 – the centre of prague.

It's a centrally located, larger hostel and by reason of our location surrounded by historical monuments, restaurants, pubs, clubs and shops. Enjoy the beautiful view of the stunning Prague architecture along the Vltava which is a stone´s throw away from our place. Generally all the major sightseeing attractions of Prague are in walking distance including the National Theatre, Charles Bridge or Old Town & Wenceslas Square.

They provide accommodation for individual backpackers, schools, groups, couples or families. This hostel offers a different selection of different mixed dormitories and private rooms all with shared bathroom hosted in a modernized old building of the 19th century. Female dorms are also available as private rooms. They have created their dorms for your convenience to experience a valuable time in Prague. Next to the normal features their dorms/windows are equipped with black-outs allowing you to rest at any time of the day either after a long cultural sightseeing tour in Prague or a long night out.

The common area exists of two levels, the ground floor and the cellar. On the ground floor you will find its fully equipped kitchen (Tesco just around the corner), smoking corner, its 8 PCs with free internet and of course a lot of possibilities to meet other travelers from all over the world. Its basement is the perfect place to celebrate your time in this wonderful city without disturbing anybody as the next dorms are up on the first floor. You will find in the basement also Pool table, Table soccer and a Juke Box with Karaoke function. Give them your personal touch and leave a message on our doors at the ground floor:).

Security is important to them. They have additional lockers at the reception and a safe in their Office. Please feel free to use them at any time. Furthermore they have restricted the access to their house, letting only those in who belong here. For your own safety they do take a deposit of 200 czk for the key and the linen provided.

Travelers staying longer than 5 nights are allowed to use their washing facilities once free to wash their belongings. Furthermore a new set of linen/bed sheets and towel will be provided extending the stay over 5 days.

Its 24/7 multilingual reception is always happy to assist you with sightseeing tours, pup crawls and further information. Its information board holds many information for you to explore Prague and its neighborhood including all the secret spots of Prague. At the reception you can also buy drinks, beer, call cards as different tickets to different events like pup crawls (VIP tickets), Czech beer tasting tour or other events.

The breakfast (on request, 80czk) is served between 8.30am and 11am and includes ham, cheese, jam, honey, butter, Czech bread rolls, bread, cornflakes, cereals, hot sausage and coffee, tea, milk or juice.

Parking is only permitted with permission in Prague 1. The nearest Parking Space is the National Theatre which inccurs a daily charge. Parking at Karlovo Namesti which is 2 min nearby is possible for free at Saturday and Sunday.

They invested in their hostel during the winter season and are still working on it. Please use their feedback form to leave any suggestions to make this place a better place. THANKS:)

Generally they only accept Czech Koruna for final payments for your bed at their hostel. By all means you may pay your initial deposit in the currency of your choice i.e. Euros but final payment must be made in Czech Koruna. While they are happy to change Euro into Czech Koruna, please note that this will be at a rate that is set by the hostel and will differ from the market rate. They do also accept credit card payment. City tax is excluded of the price.

They are looking forward to seeing you in their place and have a safe trip to wherever your paths my lead you.
