Hostel Colombo is a nice and cozy hostel with many facilities.
Just outside the center 100 meters from Venice Mestre, you will enjoy delicious cuisine and even a Neapolitan pizza in this cozy hostel! Hostel COLOMBO offers private rooms and common rooms that cater to all needs and for people of all ages, genres, nationalities: globetrotters, couples and families, groups. Free wi-fi, board games, books and travel information parking are available by confirmation from us and cost 5 euro per day.
Furthermore, bars, lunches and dinners at the hostel's restaurant, laundry,hire of towels, books and travel guides are available as an extra.


Hostel Colombo's Policies and Conditions:

Check in from 14:00 to 02:00.
Check out from 02:00 to 10:00.

Cancellation policy: 48 hours before arrival.
Payment upon arrival by cash, credit cards, debit cards (This property may pre-authorise your card before arrival).
Linen and towels are not included in dorms (5€/stay)
Taxes not included - occupancy tax 1.40 EUR per person per night for dorms.
Taxes not included - occupancy tax 2.40 EUR per person per stay for private rooms.

Breakfast not included (EUR 4.00 per person).

General:

No curfew.
Non smoking.

Important Information



SPECIAL BOOKING POLICIES

The property accepts the following credit card brands for balance payments: Visa, Mastercard, Visa Electron, JCB, Aura, Diners, Debit Cards.

The property reserves the right to pre-authorise credit cards prior to arrival.

Taxes not included: CITY TAX, to be paid in cash on arrival:



High season: 2.40€ per person per night (private room)

High season: 1.40€ per person per night (shared room)

Low season: 1.70€ per person per night (private room)

Low season : 1.00€ per person per night (shared room)



(kids under 10 years old are tax free)

(boys/girls between 11 and 16 years old only pay 50% of the tax).



CANCELLATION POLICY

Free cancellation at least 24 hours before arrival date, first night of stay fee for late cancellation or no show



IMPORTANT TO KNOW

No wheelchair access

Children accepted. Children can stay for free in parents' bed/s if younger than 3 years.

Private rooms age restriction: no restrictions

Dorms age restriction: no restrictions

Smoking policy: All rooms and the common areas are NON smoking

Animals/pets accepted



TERMS AND CONDITIONS

- - - NON REFUNDABLE ROOMS (PREPAID RATES) - - -



Please note that if you book a 'non refundable' room you must provide a valid credit card with enough money to cover the full amount of the booking! When the booking is made you'll be charged of a 10% deposit and right after you'll be charged of the 90% balance. If the provided credit card is not valid or if is not possible to charge the full amount the hotel will ask you to provide an alternative credit card within 24 hours. Otherwise the booking won't be guaranteed and the deposit lost. Cancellation not acceptable and money non refundable.

The difference must be paid at least 14 days (2 weeks) before the group arrival.
