Tucked into the turn of the tumbling Vltava river lies the humble neighbourhood of Holešovice. Its' industrial past has since passed into a residential and business quarter whose calm nature belies the nearby centre (10mins tram ride away). It is here that Sir Toby's is located.

Sir Toby's upholds the finest hostel tradition, with delightful dormitories for sleep satisfaction, and private rooms of all possible sizes and descriptions. Tea is for free in our comfortable kitchen, where cooking combines with camaraderie. We've internet access, Prague maps, and laundry, and our young friendly staff can help with your quandaries. Continental breakfast for an additional fee and there's a bar in the evenings for gossip and glee. Transport tickets are available, as are lockers and you can borrow from our library while a Sir Toby’s guest. Our historical art-nouveau building is ready to host your European euphoria or your meandering melancholia.

A calm neighbourhood, it should be noted, does not mean devoid of life... Within a five-minute walk of the hostel, one can find supermarkets, photo-processing stores, a teahouse, a post-office, hairdressers, second-hand clothing stores, the Prague Markets (for fresh fruits and vegetables, clothes, and much more), a paper shop, copy shop, health food shop, butcher's, baker's, restaurants, clubs, and whatever else one's daily life requires.

Amongst other things, we offer: Late check out until 12 midday! Internet, FREE wifi, complimentary tea, International public pay phone, laundry service, BBQ, and extensive information about Prague. Linens are included included in the price and towels are available for a small fee.

Event nights almost every night of the week, come join the fun!

Booking multiple beds in dorms does not guarantee you to stay together in the same room. If you want to be sure to stay together, please book a private room.

IMPORTANT TO KNOW

No wheelchair access

Children accepted. Children can stay for free in parents' bed/s if younger than 6 years.1 child can stay free in existing bedding. If more than 1 child or a separate bed is required, the full rate will be charged. No children are allowed in dormitory rooms.

Private rooms age restriction: no restrictions

Dorms age restriction: from 18 years to 39 years

Smoking policy: All rooms and the common areas are NON smoking

Animals NOT accepted



TERMS AND CONDITIONS

**We accept a MAXIMUM of 7 nights stay in every 3 month period in dormitory style rooms.

**No check out and check in on same date allowed, minimum 2 nights between check out and check in.

** We accept payment in CZK and EUR (25 CZK to 1 EUR). If paying with a card, there is a small service fee of 2%.

**Groups of 9 or more people cannot make an online booking; group bookings can be made by request only.

**Booking multiple beds in dorm rooms does not guarantee you to all stay in one room; to ensure you all stay together, book private rooms.

**Age restrictions apply for dorm rooms: only guests aged 18-39 will be accepted into shared dormitory rooms.

**We are a 100% non-smoking property; smoking is not allowed anywhere inside the building.

MINIMUM STAY: All groups of 10+ people must stay a minimum of 2 nights.

PAYMENTS: For a group we require 100% of the reservation total to be paid in advance to secure the rooms, within 5 days of booking.

CANCELLATIONS: If for some reason you need to cancel your reservation after paying the total cost of the reservation, we cannot guarantee any refund. All alternations to the room setup and number of guests will be accepted at our discretion.

CHECK IN & CHECK OUT: Our front desk is open 24 hours. Check in is available from 15h. Check-out time is 12h.

BREAKFAST: All groups are required to include breakfast for each person and each day. It is available 8-12h at a rate of 150 CZK per person per day.

NOISE & DAMAGE DEPOSIT: a noise and damage deposit of 500 CZK (20 EUR) per person may be required to be paid in cash on arrival. This deposit is held to ensure no disturbance is made to other guests, neighbours, or staff, and that no damage is caused. The deposit will be refunded after check out once the rooms have been inspected and will be based on the performance of your group. We are entitled to withhold the deposit for disturbance caused. Any costs incurred are to be paid for in full and are not limited to the amount of the deposit. We do not anticipate keeping any of the deposit. In fact, we wish to return the full deposit back to each group without any problems.
