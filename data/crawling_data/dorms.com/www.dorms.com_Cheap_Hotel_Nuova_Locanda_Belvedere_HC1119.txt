Welcome to Nuova Locanda Belvedere ..New Managment ,New Style ,New Staff..Nuova Locanda Belvedere is a budget Hotel and Hostel.
We are pleased to welcome you in Venice.
Nuova Locanda Belvedere is located in Marghera(VE). We are just a five minute walk from the city's main train station, Mestre - exit Marghera. There is also a bus to Venice city center every 10 minutes and it takes just 7 minutes to get there. All our rooms are completely renovated and they have,wc,frigo bar,tv,Air conditioning,...etc.. all the comforts to make your stay unforgettable and affordable in magical Venice. Our hotel has reasonable rates and very clean, comfortable rooms with Air Conditioning, private WC, refrigerator, and TV. Free Car parking is also provided. For those who are looking for night life during the holidays we are just 2 minutes walk from the bigest Discothèque of all north-east of Italy. Staying with us you can have 1 caffè for 0.55 cents, 1 Cappuccino for 0.55 cents, 1 hot chocolate for 0.55 cents and many other drinks, all at the same low price 0.55 cents!!!
