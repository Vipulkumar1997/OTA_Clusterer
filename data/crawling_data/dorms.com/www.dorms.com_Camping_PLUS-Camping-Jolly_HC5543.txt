Welcome to PLUS Camping Jolly. If you've been searching for affordable accommodation in Venice then look no further. Venice has a limited choice of budget accommodation but PLUS Camping Jolly offers you a great alternative to a hostel or hotel in Venice without losing the atmosphere. With clean comfortable bungalows, dorms, mobile homes and house tents that can accommodate 2 to 5 people all only 10 minutes from the centre!

The bungalows, dorms and mobile homes all come with their own private bathroom and shower. If you want to treat yourselves or enjoy more privacy, private twin and double room ensuites are available.
!!!NEW!!! Book the TWIN SUPERIOR and the TRIPLE SUPERIOR with BREAKFAST INCLUDED!!
You won't find many places where you can sunbathe, go swimming, have free use of a fitness centre, play table tennis and more. You'll also find a supermarket, internet café, washing machines and dryers, luggage room, and lots of good times, music, and drinks at the 'Ciao Bella Pub'.

Taste the local Italian cuisine -  our 'Ciao Bella' restaurant and bar is open all day serving great Italian style breakfasts, pizzas, salads and other snacks. It's the perfect opportunity to have good food for a great price in a cool atmosphere'. All guests will receive a welcome dinner  voucher upon check-in that entitles you to a Pizza or Pasta + drink for only € 7.50!


Take advantage of our regular shuttle bus to and from Santa Lucia train station (Main station)
10 minutes from from Santa Lucia train station in Venice go down the steps directly outside the train station and turn right; cross the new glass bridge which goes over the Grand Canal.
At Piazzale Roma,  take the PEOPLE MOVER (the journaey takes about a minute) to reach Tronchetto Island, where you can take our  private shuttle to the campsite.
