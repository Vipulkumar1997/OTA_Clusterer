Our Overseas Offices


In addition to our headquarters in the historic city of Chester, we have offices and representatives in Barbados, Dubai, Mauritius and Antigua, where our highly professional teams are on-hand to look after you from the moment you arrive in resort, through to the time you leave for home. Not only will they provide a ‘meet and greet’ service to assist you at the airport and be available to help with any problems that you may have in resort, but they are also there to assist with arranging private transfers, restaurant bookings, excursions and car rental – not to mention share their first-hand knowledge of the local area, which means they can make personal recommendations for activities to suit your interests.
