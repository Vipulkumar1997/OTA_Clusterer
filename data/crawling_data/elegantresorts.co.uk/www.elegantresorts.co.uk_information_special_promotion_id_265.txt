A gorgeous boutique sanctuary on the world-famous Palm Island in Dubai, One&Only The Palm exudes the sophistication, contemporary style and exemplary service that One&Only Resorts properties around the world have become famous for. Set on a stretch of pristine, white-sand beach and offering fabulous views of the modern Dubai skyline, the resort is the perfect place in which to enjoy a peaceful luxury holiday or long weekend break, whilst having the bright lights, endless entertainment and thrilling attractions of the city close by, including the amazing new Dubai Parks and Resorts, comprising five theme parks and a water park.


ONE&ONLY THE PALM
, DUBAI – THE RESORT

Enjoy the stunning interior design during a stay in The Manor House, boasting a combination of Moorish and Andalusian influences and chic, generously sized, Palm Manor Premiere Rooms featuring a private balcony or garden patio. Dining is superb here, including at the French restaurant of three-Michelin-starred chef, Yannick Alleno. Drinks can be enjoyed at the hip 101 Bar, offering incredible views of the Dubai skyline across the water, while indulgent treatments are offered at Guerlain Spa.







Complimentary Upgrade Offer

Stay 4 nights in a Palm Manor Premiere Room, including a complimentary upgrade to half board, plus up to a 35% rate reduction and one day ticket per person to Dubai Parks and Resorts. Includes economy flights, private transfers and UK lounge passes.


From £1,995 per person – saving £1,750 per couple.


Valid from 17-22 December 17; 8 January to 23 March 18 and 8-30 April 18. Book by 15 December 17.
