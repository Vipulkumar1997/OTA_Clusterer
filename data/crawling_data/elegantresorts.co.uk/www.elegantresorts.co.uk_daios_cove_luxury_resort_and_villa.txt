Luxury Holidays  At  Daios Cove Luxury Resort & Villas

Daios Cove Luxury Resort & Villas is perfect for beach-lovers as it is located on the seafront and overlooks its own private beach – just a ten-minute drive from the heart of Aghios Nikolaos. 
The resort's collection of buildings are spread out along a hillside, surrounded by lush gardens and have a contemporary style with elements of Cretan architecture. Accommodation ranges from Luxury Sea View Rooms and Junior Suites, to One Bedroom Suites and One, Two and Three-bedroom Villas – most with a private pool and spectacular sea views. Guests of the villas will benefit from ‘Cove Club’ enhanced services including breakfast in the Ocean Restaurant and a villas-only section on the beach. The resort has wide-ranging appeal and is a fabulous option for families.

Property Highlights:
+






Family

Daios Cove Luxury Resort & Villa is geared up for families - complete with a child-friendly beach, delicious children's menus, babysitting services and a fabulous Kids' Club - guaranteeing an unforgettable holiday for everyone. Available for the very youngest guests, aged 4 months to 4 years, the Crèche ensures that they are supervised, cared for and entertained by experts, allowing mums and dads some well-deserved alone time. Older children can check-in for fun at the fully equipped Kids' Club, where enthusiastic, friendly and highly trained staff will oversee fabulous games and activities to ensure that your little one's stay is the very best it can be.





The Beach

On an island fringed with golden sand and surrounded by turquoise-coloured waters, the private beach of Daios Cove Luxury Resort & Villa might just be the most beautiful spot. Unwind in complete tranquillity and exclusivity as the beach service - including plush sun beds, shading umbrellas and luxurious towels- ensures your complete comfort. Embrace the gloriously clear Aegean Sea with a fabulous range of watersports, including catamarans and water-skis, alongside expert tuition from the friendly experts. After your day of beach fun, refresh with exotic cocktails and freshly made snacks at the convenient and laid-back Beach Bar, or why not enjoy them from the comfort of your sun lounger?





Romance

Daios Cove Luxury Resort & Villas' idyllic location, overlooking the sparkling Aegean Sea, is the ultimate destination for a romantic escape. For unrivalled intimacy and uninterrupted quality time together, book a luxurious villa complete with a private, heated seawater swimming pool and a spacious terrace. Luxuriate with a romantic spa treatment in a harmonising and invigorating sanctuary of calm, before dining under the dazzling Cretan stars with a bottle of chilled Champagne. For the ultimate declaration of love, say "I do" in this enchanting retreat, or embrace one of the luxurious honeymoon packages for an intimate and unforgettable start to married life.





Scuba Diving

Whatever your diving level, experience an unforgettable undersea adventure at Daios Cove Luxury Resort & Villa, with help from the cutting-edge diving centre and approved coaching from PADI diving professionals. If this is your very first attempt, the tideless, clear water around the coast ensures calm conditions and great visibility for you to get to grips with this captivating sport. More experienced divers can embark on a thrilling underwater excursion to a fascinating shipwreck, passing a vibrant kingdom of marine life - including Moray Eels, Giant Clams, Octopus and Red Snappers - as they go!





Spa & Wellness

For rejuvenation of your mind, body and spirit, head to Daios Cove Spa, a haven of calm enhanced by soothing scents and sounds. With 'a healthy mind in a healthy body' philosophy, the spa combines natural products with therapeutic and movement treatments for the ultimate wellbeing experience. After indulging in one of the many unique therapies - including Cretan-inspired Signature Journeys - in your luxurious treatment room or exclusive spa suite, continue your journey of wellness in the indoor infinity pool, the cutting-edge hydro massage facility and the invigorating experience showers.
