Luxury Holidays to Thailand with Elegant Resorts



Thailand’s strikingly diverse landscape and the Thai people - renowned for being gentle-natured, welcoming and friendly - has made this country a long time favourite of Elegant Resorts clients.
Bangkok
is a great introduction to Thailand’s treasures, whilst
Phuket
and
Koh Samui
offer some of the world’s best diving sites alongside their gorgeous palm-lined beaches and limestone karsts, not forgetting the
scenic
green paddy fields to the north. Visitors on an Elegant Resorts luxury holiday to Thailand can look forward to a smorgasbord of experiences including riverboat cruises, visits to exquisitely crafted temples, delicious Thai cuisine, elephant-trekking and fabulous spa treatments. The luxury hotels featured in our Thailand portfolio regularly reel in global awards and are unrivalled in their facilities whilst the Thai’s unparalleled approach to service is legendary.
