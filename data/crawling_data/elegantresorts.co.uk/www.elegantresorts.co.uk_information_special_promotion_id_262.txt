Explore the Middle East and Beyond with Oman Air


Experience the stunning landscapes and fascinating culture of Oman, flying with Oman Air from Manchester or London. With daily direct flights to Muscat from Manchester Airport and twice-daily flights from London Heathrow Airport, you could be enjoying the beauty of this magical city in just seven-and-a-half hours. Oman Air will ensure your journey is a comfortable one, whether you travel in Economy or Business Class.



Business Class

Enjoy a spacious and well-appointed personal space-in-the-sky. You can relax during your flight on a comfortable flat bed, or take in a wide range of entertainment on your personal screen while choosing from a tasty selection of modern or traditional fine dining options.


Key Features

30-seat configuration with direct aisle access

Fully flat, 6ft 4in  beds

Baggage allowance of 50kg

Fine dining options and complementary selection of wines and Champagnes

One-piece meal table and cocktail table

17-inch monitor in the front partition and reading light
Ethernet port and PC power outlet

82-inch seat pitch and electronically-controlled backrest, leg rest and seat-depth





Economy  Class

Enjoy an award-winning Economy Class with outstanding leg room, comfortable seats that are ideal for relaxing and a great selection of in-flight entertainment. Dining options include international and Arabic dishes, accompanied by complimentary hot and cold drinks.



Key Features

Seat pitch of 34 inches

Amenity kit, pillow and soft fleece blankets

A selection of meal choices

Four-way adjustable headrest

Individual 10.6-inch LCD screen and USB ports

One PC power outlet and Ethernet port per double seat




The Oman Air Network

With daily flights from Manchester and twice-daily flights from London Heathrow, conveniently arriving at the same time, you can connect to many destinations from the hub at Muscat.
