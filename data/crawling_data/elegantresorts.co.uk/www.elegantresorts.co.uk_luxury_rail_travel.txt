Luxury Rail Travel  with Elegant Resorts

Luxurious train journeys are such an elegant choice of travel, offering the romance and opulence of a bygone era. Elegant Resorts' portfolio features the world's grandest trains, which journey along some incredible routes, passing through stunning terrain. Each one offers excellent dining experiences and personal service, relaxed sophistication and glamour and the thrill of an ever-changing view. There are also options to enjoy superb guided excursions en route and stop-overs in hotels as part of a more varied itinerary. For more information about routings and travel dates, please call our Travel Consultants to discuss your personal arrangements.
