Caribbean
coast. Beneath this coastline lies one of the world’s largest reefs – the Great Mayan Reef. In total, the reef covers more than one-thousand kilometres and its, unusually transparent, waters are home to hundreds of different marine species making it a divers’ paradise.Outdoor or watersports enthusiasts should head to Los Cabos, situated on Mexico's Baja Peninsula.
