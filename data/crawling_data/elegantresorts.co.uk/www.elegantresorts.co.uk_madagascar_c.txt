Luxury Holidays to Madagascar with Elegant Resorts


A truly one-of-a-kind destination, Madagascar is an unspoilt paradise of amazing animals and incredible nature. Vast regions of the island are virtually uninhabited and scarcely explored meaning those who desire to venture into the unknown are sure to fall in love with this island’s sense of adventure.
Landscapes are diverse – with dry deserts to tropical rainforests within just 300km of each other – presenting the ideal environment for all kinds of unique activities and a home to vast array of native animal species. Undeveloped white sand beaches line the coasts as they meet the crystal-clear turquoise waters, making this a perfect destination for beach lovers too. Activities here stretch from hiking and rock-climbing to kite surfing and diving - whatever your interest, you’re sure to make unforgettable memories here.
