Luxury Private Islands with Elegant Resorts

There’s something magical about staying on a private island. A private island offers a level of exclusivity and privacy that’s unsurpassed. It’s the perfect escape option for true hedonists and true romantics. Our private islands are available to hire in their entirety, so you and your group have the perfect venue to do what you want when, making it the ultimate way to celebrate a family reunion, a special occasion or to host a private party for you and your friends to revel in almost unimaginable style. This is what dreams are made of: a paradise location and wonderful staff to cater to your every whim – whether that’s a delicious beach barbecue or a relaxing massage. All you have to do is enjoy all the wonderful facilities, the exquisite cuisine, your beach environment and your sea views – pure pampering without interruption.
