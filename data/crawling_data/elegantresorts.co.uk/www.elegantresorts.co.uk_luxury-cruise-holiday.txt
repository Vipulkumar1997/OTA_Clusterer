Luxury Cruise Holidays with Elegant Resorts



A luxury cruise with Elegant Resorts not only offers you the chance to relax and unwind in beautiful surroundings, enjoying attentive service and superlative food, but it allows you to visit many countries, beautiful hotels and unique locations in one memorable holiday. With the recent democratisation of cruise clientele and the advent of new ships that are more intimate than the conventional cruise ship, you can now choose between a contemporary, relaxed and informal cruise styles, as well as the more traditional styles of cruising.



Our collection of luxury cruise itineraries, literally, span the globe and offer you a choice of classic cruises to the Mediterranean, northern
Europe
and the
Caribbean
, as well as transatlantic journeys and cruises to places off-the-beaten-track, such as the
Galapagos Islands
and even Antarctica. In most cases, you’ll find each cruise company provides a range of complimentary and enriching activities, designed to give you a fuller flavour of the destinations you are travelling to.
Please feel free to call our cruise Travel Consultants with any enquiries you may have, for a tailor-made price or to make your reservation.
Bon voyage!
