Luxury Skiing In Switzerland with Elegant Resorts


Switzerland is invariably skiing at its most stylish, with its slice of the Alps boasting some of the most glamorous luxury ski resorts in the world. A luxury Switzerland ski holiday with Elegant Resorts, means you'll find chocolate-box villages twinkling with the lights of designer shops and world-class hotels and restaurants, all set against a backdrop of towering peaks and rolling mountainsides - while out on the slopes, glamour meets glühwein as the super-stylish clientèle gather for lunch at the hot spot mountain-top hideaways.
