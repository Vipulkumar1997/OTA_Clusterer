Luxury Holidays to India with Elegant Resorts


With a luxury India holiday you are always welcomed with a smile by one of the most hospitable and friendly nations on earth. True, they may stare at you too, but they're curious and delighted to know that you are interested in their homeland and willing to discover more. We suggest that you put India right on top of your list of places to visit. Its credentials as a cultural destination are well known, but it has so much more to offer the luxury traveller, including some of the most luxurious hotels in the world, a friendly welcome, excellent service and a unique atmosphere. India is not just for backpackers, gap years and those trying to 'find themselves'. We guarantee that after just one visit you will be hooked.
