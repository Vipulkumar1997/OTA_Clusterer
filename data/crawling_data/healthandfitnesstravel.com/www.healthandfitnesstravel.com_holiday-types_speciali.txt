Specialist Holidays

For holiday-goers with specific time constraints, wellness goals, and travel preferences, we offer a diverse range of
healthy specialist holidays
, including breaks, retreats, all-inclusive trips, and our Ultimate Collection. Whether you are looking to improve your health with a quick visit to Croatia, lose weight alongside like-minded travellers in Spain, have fun in Mexico without worrying about the bill, or soak up the luxury of a seven-star spa in South Africa, our specialist getaways offer the ideal way to re-energise and enhance your well-being. On our recommended holidays, you will enjoy exciting activities, soothing treatments, super facilities, and more.
