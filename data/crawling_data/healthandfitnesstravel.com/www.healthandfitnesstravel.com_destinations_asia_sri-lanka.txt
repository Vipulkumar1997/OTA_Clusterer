Sri Lanka

Often described as the Pearl of the Indian Ocean,
Sri Lanka
is a hidden gem located to the south of India with tropical temperatures all year round. Dotted with rising mountains, carpeted with lush forests and surrounded by golden beaches, it offers the perfect setting to enhance your fitness, rejuvenate and connect with your inner self.

Explore the views behind the postcard pictures as you explore the vibrant jungles before you simply unwind at one of our luxury wellness destinations. Whether it’s trekking, detoxing or relaxing that takes your fancy, Sri Lanka has something for everyone.

Our luxury retreats in Sri Lanka are guaranteed to leave you feeling self-nourished and glowing inside and out. Discover a haven of natural beauty as you embark on a spiritual wellness adventure and see what this magical island can offer you.
