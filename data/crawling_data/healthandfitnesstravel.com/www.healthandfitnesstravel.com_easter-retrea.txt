Easter Retreats

Take a break over the Easter holidays with our luxury well-being escapes in idyllic destinations worldwide. From family fitness holidays in Europe, to couples beach spa breaks in the Caribbean and singles yoga retreats in Asia; there is a health and wellness holiday for everyone. With a variety of options, tailor-make your Easter well-being retreat with a combination of healthy cuisine, luxury spa treatments, fitness classes, sports and activities galore. From hiking to mountain biking, tennis to golf, meditation to detox and scuba diving to boot camps, our Easter wellness breaks will return you home healthier and happier.
