Christmas & New Year Retreats

Swap the festive season frenzy for a healthy holiday with our luxury Christmas & New Year retreats in idyllic destinations worldwide. From family fitness holidays in Europe, to couples beach spa breaks in the Caribbean and singles yoga retreats in Asia, there is a winter sun solution for every holiday maker. Find time to relax over the Christmas holidays with our rejuvenating Ayurveda spa, de-stress and healing wellness holidays, or get a head start on your New Year’s resolution with our active bootcamp, weight loss, and detox retreats. Tailor-make your festive season holiday with a combination of healthy cuisine, luxury spa treatments, fitness classes, sports and activities galore.
