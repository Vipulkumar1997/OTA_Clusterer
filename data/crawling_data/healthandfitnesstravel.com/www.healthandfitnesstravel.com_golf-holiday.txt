Golf Holidays

Take to the fairway on one of our
golfing holidays
with world-renowned courses located everywhere from Cyprus to Spain, Mexico to Mauritius and Barbados to Thailand. Golf is one of the most played sports in the world and we have a range of holiday packages that offer golfing opportunities for both the beginner and mid-low handicaps. Enjoy the fresh air and stunning views while you tee off around a Championship golf course or take a lesson from an pro and touch up on all aspects of your game in luxury.
