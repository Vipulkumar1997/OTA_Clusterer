Singles Holidays







Untitled Document










When travelling solo the world is your oyster. Embrace the opportunity to tailor-make your travel escape to your specific wellness needs with our
single friendly holidays
, whether yoga in Bali, weight loss in Spain or a choice of worldwide group retreats. Meet like-minded singles with group activities, group tables at meal times and dedicated holiday months for solo travellers.


























Yoga Holidays for Singles



Spa Holidays for Singles



Detox Holidays for Singles



Weight Loss for Singles







Retreat on a
singles yoga holiday
and relax on your own terms as you rejuvenate your mind, body and soul. From exotic India to sunny Ibiza, meet fellow yogis as you practice different disciplines from Hatha to Bikram.





For the ultimate luxury getaway, escape on a
singles spa holiday
and work your way through an indulgent menu of spa treatments and therapies. Complement with daily group or one-on-one fitness classes.





Revitalise your system on a
singles detox holiday
through a combination of juice, macrobiotic & raw food diets. Meet like-minded individuals when you enhance your body cleanse with low intensity fitness classes.





Embark on a
singles weight loss holiday
for a welcoming atmosphere where fitness and nutrition experts teach you a healthier lifestyle through tailored diet-plans and a choice of fitness classes.

Group Retreats for Singles






Meet new friends as you bond over an exhilarating group class on a
singles fitness holiday
. Focus on your fitness goals with a personal training session or join in with social group events like hiking or Zumba.





Singles tennis holidays
are a great way to boost fitness and connect with other singles over your shared love for the racquet wielding sport. Shape-up with private lessons, group coaching sessions and tournaments.





Kick-start weight loss on a
singles bootcamp holiday
, where close knit groups will provide an instant friendship group. Have fun whilst pushing your boundaries with interval, cardio and resistance training.





For social butterflies,
singles group retreats
ensure travelling solo no longer means travelling alone. Hit the water with stand-up paddle boarding in Ibiza, or shape-up with a bootcamp in the Algarve.
