Meditation Retreats

Meditation is a stillness of body and mind; discover the depth of your self and take a spiritual wellness holiday to find insight, bliss and peace. Our
meditation holidays
and silent retreats offer a mind-body approach to well-being that can help you change the way you think about experiences, reduce stress and anxiety.  Our mindfulness retreats combine group classes and personal meditation lessons led by experts in beautiful and relaxing destinations from India to Thailand, allowing you to dedicate time and attention so you learn how to meditate and unwind. Complement your practice with mind therapies from Shirodhara to Reiki.
