Twin & Multi Centre Holidays












With our
Twin & Multi Centre Holidays
the world is your oyster!
Tailor-make your perfect escape by combining multiple resorts and destinations in one healthy holiday. Whether you dream of island hopping in South East Asia, or discovering different corners of Africa before jetting off to Mauritius, we have a destination combination to suit you. Make the most of your worldwide adventure by experiencing numerous regions, cultures and cuisine as you go from city to beach, or mountains to jungle. With endless possibilities available you can mix-up your perfect holiday cocktail of relaxation, culture and adventure – all supplied with a healthy dose of fitness.

India



Africa & Indian Ocean



Oman & Dubai






Discover enchanting India and immerse yourselves in every corner of this incredible country. Indulge in authentic Ayurvedic treatments in the beachside destination of Goa; practice yoga amongst the awe-inspiring mountains of the Himalayas, and absorb the vibrant culture of dynamic Delhi.





Spot the big 5 on an unforgettable safari spa break in South Africa, or reach your fitness goals with a bootcamp under the African sun in Kenya. Combine with a fun surf and yoga break in Morocco, or unwind with your toes in the sand on a healthy holiday in Mauritius.





Escape to the majestic Middle East and experience the best of both worlds. Appeal to your adventurous side by enjoying activities such as mountain climbing and paragliding in a divinely secluded retreat in Oman. Move on to Dubai for some bustling culture, and enjoy a healthy beach wellness break.

Thailand



Vietnam, The Philippines & Malaysia



Bali & Java






Experience every inch of Thailand's diverse landscape and culture with these healthy breaks; bike around the bustling city of Bangkok on a cultural holiday, before retreating to an exquisite beach destination. Island hop from idyllic Koh Samui to phenomenal Phuket, whilst relaxing with wellness treatments and holistic therapies.





Visit Southeast Asia for an explosion of colourful culture, beautiful beaches and rich rainforests. Soak up the unique traditions and healthy cuisine of the Philippines; immerse yourself in an organic wellness retreat in the Malaysian jungle, and experience all-inclusive spa treatments on the beaches of Vietnam, for an unforgettable healthy getaway.





Explore the renowned islands of Indonesia as you jet off to tropical Java and Bali. Discover diverse marine life on a scuba-diving holiday in Bali, before relaxing at a specialist Thalassotherapy retreat. Skip over to Java and relax on a wellness holiday nestled amongst spectacular volcanoes and tropical jungles.

Experience the Americas and their rich culture and natural beauty with a healthy and active break. Visit Utah and hike the breath-taking Red Mountains, before cooling-off with water sports on the blissful beaches of Cancun and appeasing your adventurous side with surfing and horse riding in Costa Rica.





Enjoy the bliss of fresh air like never before with a luxury hiking and wellness getaway nestled amongst New Zealand's majestic mountains and lakes, before experiencing Australia's golden beaches with a wellness break on Hamilton Island or a fitness retreat in Noosa National Park.
