Malaysia

Discover a multicultural destination blessed with a vibrant mix of Indian, Chinese and Malay influences, as well as the history of its indigenous tribes in
Malaysia
. It boasts the world's oldest rainforest, one of the greatest cave systems and the tallest mountain pinnacle between the Himalayas and New Guinea.

Enhance your well-being in this unspoilt paradise with yoga; boost your vitality with herbal treatments and personalised holistic therapies or improve your swing on an award-winning golf course.

The organic spas in Malaysia will ensure that you return home in a renewed state of health and with a re-energised mind and body.
