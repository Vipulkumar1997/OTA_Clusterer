Discover Recover™ Holidays

Cultural and wellness holidays for the active traveller

Created exclusively by Health and Fitness Travel, our
Discover Recover™
holidays offer a unique opportunity to unite your perfect travel experience of wellness with culture for a life changing journey. Combine the benefits of exploring differing cultures whilst staying healthy and active in the great outdoors and choose from a variety of tailor-made excursions where your senses will be enlightened and enriched to give you a taste of the destination. Experience the country, traditions, mix with the local people and combine your perfect health and fitness holiday with the very best in local culture whilst getting to know the country. Then recover on one of our wellness holidays with indulgent spa treatments, exercise, relaxation and healthy food so you end your trip with a learned travel experience whilst feeling totally rejuvenated in mind and body.
