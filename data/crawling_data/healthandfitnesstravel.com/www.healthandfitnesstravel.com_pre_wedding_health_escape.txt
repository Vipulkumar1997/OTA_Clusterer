Pre-Wedding Health Escapes

Prepare for your big day the healthy way with our pre-wedding health escapes. Get in to shape and look your best with our specialist fitness, bootcamp and weight loss breaks or unwind and de-stress on our luxury spa retreats. From group yoga classes on the beaches of the Caribbean, to holistic Ayurveda spa therapies in Asia and detox holidays in Europe, tailor-make your ideal wellness escape with a variety of activities and treatments. Whether you are planning on travelling solo, as a couple with your groom-to-be, or as part of a group with your bridal party, enhance your well-being in luxurious worldwide destinations and return home wedding ready.
