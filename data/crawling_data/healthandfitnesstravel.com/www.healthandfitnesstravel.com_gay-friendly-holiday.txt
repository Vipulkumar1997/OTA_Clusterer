Gay Friendly Holidays







Untitled Document











With our exciting range of gay friendly holidays, the world is your oyster. From fitness holidays in the Caribbean or yoga retreats in Asia, to luxury spa breaks in the Maldives or cultural wellness holidays in Europe, our hand-selected destinations from around the globe welcome gay and lesbian couples into a friendly and accepting environment.














Fitness Holidays



Luxury Spa Holidays



Cultural Wellness Trips






Have the flexibility to choose your favourite fitness activities with our tailor-made Fusion Fitness Holidays™. Choose 4, 7 or 10 personalised fitness activities and wellness spa treatments, from a blend of four fusion elements including; Cardio, Strength & Sport, Mind & Body and Renewal.





From beach retreats to detox breaks, our luxury spa holidays offer something for everyone with a choice of wellness retreats in serene destinations worldwide. Enhance your well-being with our specialist spa holidays including Ayurvedic, weight-loss, fitness and ultimate luxury.





Our Discover Recover Holidays™ offer a unique opportunity to combine cultural discovery with wellness recovery. Unite the benefits of exploring local culture with tours and excursions, before rejuvenating with luxury spa treatments and wellness activities.

Yoga Retreats



Healthy Honeymoons






Kick start your well-being as you cleanse your body of toxins on our popular detox holidays. Through healthy nutrition, detoxifying spa treatments and well-being activities such as yoga, discover a range of health benefits, including stress reduction and enhanced mental clarity.





Discover this ancient way of life with our specialist yoga holidays from SUP to Ayurveda and meditation to culture. Improve your strength and flexibility with group classes and one-on-one sessions, as you experience different disciplines from Ashtanga to Pranayama and Yin to Bikram.





Begin your happily ever after on one of our luxury Healthy Honeymoons™. From adventure to cultural and beach activity honeymoons, there is something for every newlywed couple.
