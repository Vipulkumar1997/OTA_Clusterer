Maldives

Enjoy the unspoilt beauty and tranquillity of this small, but vibrant archipelago. Experience true island life on the
Maldives
' white sandy beaches, and look out onto the tropical cobalt ocean which boasts over three hundred different species of fish.

Embrace nature and relax with soothing Ayurvedic treatments, feel rejuvenated with indulgent spa therapies and find inner peace with yoga sessions. For the more adventurous types, snorkelling and scuba-diving are great for exploring the splendour of the ocean.

You will return home with a feeling of serenity, wellness and utmost calm.
