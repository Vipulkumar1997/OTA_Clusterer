How We Work

Guests’ Testimonials

Expertise

We are experts in creating and designing healthy holidays and we strive for excellence.

Best Value

Our healthy holidays offer the best rates and added value; we negotiate on your behalf to secure the best possible deals and bring you exclusive offers, ensuring that booking with Health and Fitness Travel provides added value over direct bookings. We design our holidays with you in mind and we deliver what you want most; from special prices for single travellers to room upgrades and free spa treatments to name a few. We offer a high quality of service and expertise at the best value for money.

Holiday Support

We have excellent relationships with all our suppliers and if anything goes wrong on your holiday you have our support and assistance to help liaise with the hotel to ensure that whatever needs resolving can happen more smoothly and more quickly.

Advice

We will look after you every step of the way and offer impartial advice on the best healthy holidays and destinations that suit your interests, preferences and budget. We will listen and recommend.

Bespoke Service

We tailor-make all our holidays to suit individual needs. From your initial enquiry to when you return home, we will take care of you so your holiday goes smoothly. We can create your programmes and pre-book all your activities, treatments and arrange everything so your holiday is stress-free.

Peace of Mind

We are committed to safe and responsible travel. Your holiday with Health and Fitness Travel is ATOL protected. All the flights and inclusive holidays are financially protected by the ATOL scheme.

Resources

We are the place to go to for all your health and fitness travel needs. With insider interviews, expert articles, social media platforms and an authoritative blog, our website offers a vast amount of detailed information and guidance.

Trend-Setters

We are always creating trend-setting and cutting edge tailor-made holidays, forecasting the next big trends in health and wellness travel. Our exclusive Fusion Fitness™ and Discover Recover™ holidays designed and created by us offer a totally bespoke travel experience.

Reputation

We are proud to be known as the leaders in wellness holidays and our reputation in the luxury health and well-being travel industry has been achieved by being a trusted authority for health and fitness holidays.

Time

We will do all the work so you don’t have to. We will take care of your requirements the moment you get in touch, offering you a stress-free and high quality service.

Highly Selective

Our holidays are carefully researched and handpicked, making sure they deliver on expectations. We have done the hard work for you and our team of experienced travellers and specialists in health and fitness have spent time sourcing our holidays around the world, which are hard to find. If we don’t want to holiday there ourselves, we won’t want you to. We choose who’s featured and our highly selective approach ensures we can offer the very best in healthy holidays.
