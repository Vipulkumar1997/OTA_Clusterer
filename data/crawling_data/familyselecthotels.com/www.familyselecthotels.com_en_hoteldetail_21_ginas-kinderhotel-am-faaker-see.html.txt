A „quacking“ warm welcome to your holiday at Gina‘s Families Only Kinderhotel beside crystal-clear Lake

Faak, on the Austrian Riviera, in Carinthia. The waters of Lake Faak, Austria‘s most southerly bathing lake, situated in the triangle of land known as the Adriatic Alps, where three countries meet, is pure enough to drink. Gina the
DUCK
is
DUCK
lighted by the 60,000 m2 of 5-Smiley quality awaiting just 30 families, all year

round! There‘s lots to enjoy here in summer and winter alike. Quack! The little ones are Gina‘s most important guests. They have separate childcare groups run by 3-5 trained nannies and nursery nurses, extremely popular with children and their parents alike.

Room



















































Country Suite


For 2-4 (2 adults) persons. Approx. 40 sq.m.
With bath or shower and WC, radio, telephone, refrigerator, microwave, safe, SAT-TV, balcony and children's loggia with view of the lake, baby alarm via mobile phone directly to the parents.
1 parents' bedroom with seating corner and 1 children's bedroom with bunk bed and cot.


































Mountain Suite


For 2-5 (2 adults) persons. With 2 separate bedrooms and living area or loggia,
balcony on the same side as the lake, bath or shower, WC, radio, microwave, bottle sterilizer, phone, refrigerator, safe, sat-TV, baby alarm via GSM mobile phone directly to the parents as well as all provisions for babies and children.






















































Penthouse Suite


For 2-5 (2 adults) persons. Approx. 42-60 sq.m.
balcony included. With bathtup and shower and WC, radio, telephone, refrigerator, microwave, safe, SAT-TV, balcony and children's loggia with view of the lake, baby alarm via mobile phone directly to the parents.
1 parents' bedroom with seating corner and 1 children's bedroom or children's loggia with bunk bed and cot.






















































Pool Suite


For 2-5 (2 adults) persons
.
With bath or shower and WC,
radio, phone, refrigerator, microwave, safe, sat-TV, balcony with view of the lake, baby alarm via GSM mobile phone directly to the parents.
2 separate bedrooms and a living room or living area
as well as all provisions for babies and children.










































Vipo Suite


For 2-5 (2 adults) Persons.

Approx. 65 square meters, 1 adult bed room with king size bed, 2 bed rooms for the children, large balcony (20m2) with view to the lake, shower, bathtub, wc, microwave, telephone, fridge, sat-tv, and baby and kids equipment, baby monitor, internet etc.


















































Big Family Suite


For 2-6 (2 adults) persons. With bath (double washbasin), shower and WC,
radio, phone, refrigerator, safe, sat-TV, balcony, baby alarm via GSM mobile phone directly to the parents.
3 separate bedrooms one with bunk bed, 1 combined living and bedroom
as well as all provisions for babies and children.










































Captain Suite


For 2-7 (2 adults) Persons.
With 4 separate bedrooms,
one living room, one reading corner, family bathroom with double washbasin, family shower, separate WC, radio, CD, SAT-TV, refrigerator, hotplate for baby food, safe, balcony ("upper deck") with view of the lake, baby alarm via mobile phone to the parents as well as all provisions for babies and children. The furniture and decor echo a nautical theme.
