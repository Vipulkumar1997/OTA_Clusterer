Room



















Doppelzimmer Talblick


ca. 20 m², für 2 Personen, mit Bad oder Dusche/WC, Sitzecke, Balkon, Direktwahltelefon, Satelliten-TV mit integriertem Radio, Fön und Safe


























Valley view royal double room


Our valley view royal double rooms offer every comfort for 2 people and up to 2 children on approx. 30 - 35 m².

The rustic rooms have a double sofa bed or an additional bed while the modern rooms offer a small children sleeping area with bunk bed with bed guard to prevent falling.

The rooms with comfortable furniture offer a seating corner, a telephone, a satellite TV with built-in radio as well as a safe. In your private bathroom with bathtub (modern rooms) or shower (rustic rooms) and WC, you will find as well a hairdryer and a small set of our Acquapura SPA product line. Furthermore, all valley view royal double rooms have a ca. 4 sqm balcony with valley view.


























Lake view royal double room


Our lake view royal double rooms are approx. 30 - 35 m² and offer every comfort and enough space for 2 people and up to 2 children.

The room has the possibility of an additional bed for one child or a double sofa bed for 2 children. These rooms are perfect for families with small kids and babies.

The rustic rooms with comfortable furniture offer a  seating corner, a telephone, a satellite TV with built-in radio as well as a safe. In your private bathroom with bathtub or shower and WC, you will find as well a hairdryer and a small set of our Acquapura SPA product line. Furthermore, all lake view royal double rooms have a ca. 10 sqm balcony.


























Lake view family room


Our lake view family rooms are approx. 35 m² and offer every comfort and enough space for 2 people and 2 children.

The room has a separate kids room with a bunk bed with bed guard to prevent falling for 2 children. We will be pleased to prepare a baby-cot at the side of the parents bed.

The modern rooms with comfortable furniture offer a seating corner, a telephone, a satellite TV with built-in radio as well as a safe. In your private bathroom with bathtub and separate WC, you will find as well a hairdryer and a small set of our Acquapura SPA product line. Furthermore, all lake view family rooms have a ca. 10 sqm balcony.


























Lake view royal family room


Our lake view royal family rooms are approx. 45 m² and offer every comfort and enough space for 2 people and 4 children.

The rustic rooms have a separate kid’s room with a bunk bed and a sofa-bed close to the parents bed, while the modern rooms offer a separate sleeping area with two bunk beds with bed guard to prevent falling.

The modern or partially rustic rooms with comfortable furniture offer a seating corner, a telephone, a satellite TV with built-in radio as well as a safe. In your private bathroom with bathtub and separate WC, you will find as well a hairdryer and a small set of our Acquapura SPA product line. Furthermore, all lake view royal family rooms have a balcony up to approx. 14 sqm.
