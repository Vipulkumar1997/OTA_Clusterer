Lots of things are waiting for you as a family - we offer a wide range of family suites with separate children's bedrooms and equipment for kids (softplay area, children's playing room etc). Everyone of our staff will help you in case of any concern.

For our smalles guest we offer child care (from 6 months and up). Action is waiting for you on our hotel exclusive playing yard (open in summertime) - pony riding, go-carts and lots more! Also our animals are waiting for your visit (ponys, rabbits, goats...).

Take a break in our 450 m² wellness area with indoor pool, water chute, indoor splash pool for kids, outdoor pool and outdoor splash pool for kids (open in summer). Furgli's herbal sauna is a highlight for the whole family - parents can relax in our sauna area!
