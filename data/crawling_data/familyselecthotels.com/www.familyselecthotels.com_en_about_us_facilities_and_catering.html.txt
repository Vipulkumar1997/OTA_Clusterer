Facilities and catering


FamilySelect hotels are specifically geared to the particular needs of families with children. The three weasel categories are your guarantee of verified and confirmed quality.



















As standard all FamilySelect hotels (3 weasels and above) provide spacious rooms and apartments with separate children’s rooms, children’s beds and a child’s toilet seat. 4-star hotels and above include a complete baby pack, with thermometer, nappy bin, baby bath, changing table and baby monitor, making your family holiday complete!

Our FamilySelect hotels not only cater for parents, grandparents and friends, our young guests can also expect a tailor-made service, including family-friendly tables, high chairs, children’s cutlery and crockery, and a separate children’s menu with child-friendly dishes. This is a guaranteed part of the service from 3-weasel category up. 5-weasel hotels offer full board for children, which includes a children’s lunch, a children’s menu at dinner and soft drinks all day long!
