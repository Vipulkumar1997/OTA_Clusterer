bookmark









Skiing and winter fun in the Schladming 4 Mountain Ski Area

Hauser Kaibling Planai, Hochwurzen, and Reiteralm are the skiing mountains of the Schladming 4-Mountain Ski Swing. Each ski mountain shows its best side and they all have one thing in common: They offer 123 fine slopes, 44 modern cable cars and lifts, culinary delights at 56 ski huts, offers for kids and families, athletes and connoisseurs.


Children program - great programs to join


Children from 3 years to 14 years will be entertained by three trained children entertainers. Cooking, baking, studying yoga for children, bouncy castle, creative workshop, shows ... are just a few examples.


Baby and toddler care
The little ones from 12 months up are at our toddler caregivers in the best hands. Wrap feed, go to sleep, daily walks and fun in the snow is one of our checkpoints. Our child care hours for the little ones are on Sunday 9:00 to 15:00 and Monday to Friday 9:00 to 6:00 p.m.
