Whether relaxing or active holiday, the Bregenzerwald offers you a stunning natural backdrop ... you will be amazed!

Our hotel is situated in the village center of Au at Bregenzerwald, despite the away from any street noise, so the Alepn Hotel Post is ideally suited for the most wonderful time of the year.

Our unique location is your advantage:
Next to the hotel you'll find the skilift for beginners and a cross-country slope. The hotel is an ideal starting point for hikes and mountain bike trails.
