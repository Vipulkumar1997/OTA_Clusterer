Guaranteed quality


We have a mark of quality, called Willi Weasel. The three strict categories (3, 4 and 5 weasels), which are regularly checked, guarantee comprehensive, family-friendly services, from child care 7 days a week, via child-friendly dining facilities, to a varied range of games and children's activities.

















3-weasel category



Hotel Location


The children’s play area is a safe distance away from road traffic


Family rooms – furnishings:


Spacious rooms and apartments

Child beds

Child-safe equipment and power sockets

Step-stool in bathroom

Child toilet seat


Dining area:


Family-friendly tables (bigger, with lots of space)

Non-smoking area

Various heights of high chairs and booster cushions, children’s crockery and cutlery

Toys to entertain children whilst waiting (such as colouring books and crayons, or story books)

Children’s menu, child-sized portions, children’s drinks


Play area:


Separate playroom or playhouse (easy to find, bright and friendly)

Safe power sockets, movable furnishings with toys and drawing equipment, etc.

Outdoor play area with swing, sand pit, slide, pedal cars and tricycles


Additional services:


Babysitting service can be organised on request (for an additional charge)
