The ****
Alpine Balance Hotel Weisses Lamm
is located in the heart of See Paznaun with magnificent panorama. Quiet on the mountainside near by the waterfall, you will find the holiday hotel in the
valley Paznaun (See, Kappl, Ischgl and Galtür)
in the beautiful Tyrol - a top house with all the classic qualities, much sense of tradition at the highest level and a luxurious ambience - for your ultimate vacation.

Arrive and feel good immediately in the wellness holiday - enjoy moments high above everyday life. The most wonderful natural spectacles reveal themselves directly in front of the
Hotel Weisses Lamm.
In summer and winter the sporting epicure discovers that a holiday in the Tyrolean valley Paznaun is unbeatable!



The new beautiful wellness and spar area is balsam and fountain of youth for body with an indoor pool, saunas, steam bath, brine steam bath, infrared cabin & heated loungers by
Physiotherm
, gym and relaxation area. Enjoy in addition massages (for a fee) and in addition other wellness offers.



These wonderful holiday facts added with the first-class creations of our kitchen staff - valley Paznaun -“here we come!”. Who needs "sea" when the village See makes the holiday in Tyrol already intoxicating.

winter informaton:
spoiling half-board: various breakfast buffet with a lot of freshness and 4-5 course dinner. The cable car station See is just 700 meters away, the ski bus stops right outside the hotel and takes you to the valley station in one minute. From there you can use the connecting bus to Kappl, Ischgl and Galtür.

summer
informaton
:

- 3/4 board:
various
breakfast buffet with plenty of fresh, vital afternoon buffet from 2 - 4 pm and 4-5 course dinner.

with natural swimming lake (300 meters), outdoor swimming pool in Ischgl, indoor pool in Galtür and an exciting outdoor weekly program (with kids- & youth club also days without parents or adventure for the whole family)
