Smileys Kinderhotel




bookmarked

bookmark












Our feel-good Kinderhotel is furnished in an attractive mix of Mediterranean flair and Carinthian country house style. We are located in Austria’s most family-friendly region, the Lieser-Maltatal valley, not far from Lake Millstatt.

Get your day off to a great start with a hearty breakfast using regional produce, then enjoy the beautiful scenery and diverse range of sporting activities on offer. Your children are in good hands, with child care seven days per week and a great range of children’s activities, including 10 unique children-only indoor areas, whilst you abandon your cares in our spa.

You can also relax in our “Alpen Steil” sauna complex, family indoor pool (open 07.00
-22.00), outdoor pool, or the hotel’s own beach at the loveliest location on Lake Millstatt.
In the evening there is a choice of menus, non-alcoholic drinks are included all day, and you can enjoy a buffet evening at which we serve regional and international specialities.
