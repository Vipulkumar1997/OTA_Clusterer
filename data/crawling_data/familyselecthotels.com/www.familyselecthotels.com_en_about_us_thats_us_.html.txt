The epitome of family holidays That’s us!


We specialise in family holidays. FamilySelectHotels focus on the particular needs of families and our selected hotels feature first-class, child-friendly facilities in rooms, play areas and restaurants, whilst simultaneously providing parents and grandparents with a wide range of opportunities to relax or play sport.

FamilySelectHotels establishments leave little to be desired – where else could you snowboard, ski, cross-country ski, relax in a top-class spa complex, pet baby animals in a petting zoo, or visit the animals on a farm? The 21 specially selected hotels in Austria and Italy guarantee that the whole family will have a perfect holiday.
