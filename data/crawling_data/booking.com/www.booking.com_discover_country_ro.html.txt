Featuring free WiFi and a restaurant, Pensiunea Cazanele Dunarii offers accommodations in Dubova. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free private parking is available on site.

“Location, view over the river.”

Featuring free WiFi and a barbecue, Pensiunea Lac Colibita offers accommodations in Colibiţa. The guesthouse has a hot tub and sauna, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

“Very nice and exceptionally well built new pension. Food was excellent (Romanian traditional) and the staff super nice. Great view of the lake and mountain. We had a very pleasant stay.”







Pensiunea Lac Colibita


Average Review Score: 9.4








Situated near the forest in Baile Felix, Hotel Termal offers free access to 4 outdoor swimming pools and an indoor pool with thermal water, free sauna, free internet access and free air conditioning.

“Everything is excellent. Staff is extremelly friendly and professional.The facilities are great and it is very close to the main attractions in Felix :-) .”







Hotel Termal


Average Review Score: 8.5








Club Vila Bran is a mountain resort situated 1km from Bran city centre, and 4 km from Zanoaga Ski Centre.

“Everything EXCEPTIONAL! 
Very good position, extremely nice staff, very good food, acceptable prices, very nice rooms with superb mountain views, very clean and the list could go on endlessly.
A lot of great daily activities for people of all ages! The best choice in Romania undoubtedly! It's the 13th time i come here and i still do not get bored!”







Club Vila Bran


Average Review Score: 8.8








Offering a seasonal outdoor pool and children's playground, Mera Resort is located in Venus, just 100 metres from the sandy beach.

A big thank you to all mentioned above.”







Mera Resort
