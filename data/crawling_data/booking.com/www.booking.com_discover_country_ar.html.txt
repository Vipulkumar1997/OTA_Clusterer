Top Hotels








Located in Villa la Angostura, Correntoso Lake & River Hotel offers peaceful accommodation at the mouth of the Correntoso River. It features stylish rooms. Wi-Fi and parking are free.

“The Correntoso is a beautiful hotel in a beautiful location.  Makes it hard to want to leave and go explore anywhere else.  Staff is great, from front desk to restaurant and SPA - Very relaxing with a homey feeling and beautiful views.”







Correntoso Lake & River Hotel


Average Review Score: 9.2








Featuring an outdoor pool, a thermal hot tub, a spa and fitness center, Los Pinos Resort & Spa Termal is an all-inclusive resort in Termas de Rio Hondo that has rooms with free Wi-Fi and plasma TVs.

“Lovely pool....great food...very good attention...”







Los Pinos Resort & Spa Termal All Inclusive


Average Review Score: 9.1








Located 165 ft from San Martin square and 215 yards from the financial area, the Amerian Hotel Casino Carlos V has free Wi-Fi and serves a buffet breakfast in Termas de Río Hondo.

“The facilities in the bedroom were good. the termal bath is excellent. the spa was very good.”







Amérian Hotel Casino Carlos V


Average Review Score: 9.2








Featuring an exclusive and relaxing spa and wellness centre, Termas Cacheuta is located in Cacheuta, Luján de Cuyo. The property is surrounded by the Andes mountains and the Mendoza River.

“Incredibly delicious food.  Gorgeous scenery.  Nice spa.  Kind staff (front desk staff not so prompt or courteous, but all other staff, especially in restaurant, absolutely amazing)”







Hotel & Resort Termas Cacheuta


Average Review Score: 8.9








Located only 150 metres from Florida Street, Hotel Waldorf offers rooms with air conditioning. There are lots of pubs and restaurants in the area.

“The hotel is just fine, a good value for money. I was lucky to have Sergio (sorry if misspelled) as the receptionist. He solved all my issues with a smile and helped a lot. The room was nice and clean, I was happy to stay by myself there. The bed was good, the A/C worked perfectly, and it was quiet there. The location is good. The minimum you need for a calm short-term stay.”







Hotel Waldorf


Average Review Score: 7.9
