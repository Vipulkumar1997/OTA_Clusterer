Top Hotels








Located at the base of Kazbek Mountain in Stepantsminda Village, 7 km from the 14th-century Gergeti Trinity Church, Hotel Kazbegi features a lobby bar with a library and free Wi-Fi.

“This hotel was beyond fabulous . It was perfect for somewhere to stay in that region. Great outdoors Area surrounding so the hotel is like a haven . Wonderful food. Super design. Probably the best hotel I stayed in my life.”







Rooms Hotel Kazbegi


Average Review Score: 9.1








Featuring free Wi-Fi and free private parking, this hotel is located at the car market area in Rustavi town. It offers rooms with air conditioning and a restaurant.

“nice stuff,clean rooms, best prices. for shore recommended! ✅”







Autopapa


Average Review Score: 8








Featuring a tennis court, Lopota Lake Resort & Spa is located on the private lakeside, 26 km from Telavi town. It offers 3 swimming pools and air-conditioned rooms with a fridge.

“There is no place that remotely compares to Lopota Lake in Georgia in the similar price range. We are extremely pleased and enjoyed our stay. Russian spa Banya was a great! We will definitely be back and would highly recommend it to anyone.”







Lopota Lake Resort & Spa


Average Review Score: 8.8








Offering free WiFi and a terrace, Rooms Hotel Tbilisi is located a 5-minute walk from Rustaveli Metro Station. The rooms feature air conditioning, a minibar and a flat-screen TV.

“I really like the service, the food quality and cost benefit is also excellent, all rooms and suites are very large with plenty of natural light and comfortable beds/overall facilities.”







Rooms Hotel Tbilisi


Average Review Score: 9.1








Showcasing a spa center and a private beach area, Euphoria Batumi Hotel is located in Batumi. The hotel has a hot tub and sauna, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

“New hotel super clean rooms n big size with sea view n breakfast was multiple for all kind of digestive also i recommended it 8/10”







Euphoria Batumi Hotel


Average Review Score: 8.4








Located 0.4 miles from Argo Cable Car in Batumi, Leogrand Hotel & Casino Batumi features a restaurant, bar and free WiFi.
