Sofia is highly rated by travelers for food, culture and shopping.

Sofia has plenty of natural sites too, including the renowned thermal and mineral springs, the primary reason Thracians established the city here before the 7th-century BC. Enjoy a walk in the most popular parks, the Borisova gradina or the impressive Vitosha Nature Park with more tan 6,500 acres within the city limits.
From hiking to skiing on the Vitosha and from sightseeing to relaxing in mineral springs, Sofia has  fun for everyone! On Booking.com you can find nearly 190 properties in the city, from 5-star accommodations downtown to convenient hotels near Sofia Airport.

Do not miss the SPA, they have discounts for treatment packages and you'll spend the most pleasant hours of your stay!”
