About our Serviced Apartments in Manchester
Citybase Apartments have carefully hand selected a range of serviced apartments in Manchester, so you can have enjoy a comfortable home away from home. Situated in the North West of England, Manchester is widely regarded as a fashionable, cosmopolitan city, with something for everyone.
Families can enjoy the many interactive museums in the city and friends can get together over a few drinks in the trendy Northern Quarter, all within easy reach of many of our Manchester apartments. We also have apartments further out of the city centre, towards the busy Mediacity and a short drive from the famous Trafford Centre.
With self-catering facilities, a number of bedroom options, including studio apartments, and great city centre locations, our Manchester apartments are ideal for your next trip. Let our team of city break experts find you the best accommodation for your visit, no matter how long or short you're planning to stay.
New to serviced apartments?
If you enjoy the freedom of self-catered accommodation, you will love our serviced apartments in Manchester. Marrying the benefits of hotel service with the convenience of a self-catering holiday, our apartments offer differing levels of maid and linen services and all come equipped with a private kitchen. Save money from having to eat out in expensive restaurants for the duration of your stay, and opt for a home cooked meal in your personal serviced apartment. Feel like a local rather than a passing visitor, with your home from home in the city.
