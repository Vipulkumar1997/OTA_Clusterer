Undiscovered World




It’s that time of the year again – everyone is releasing their top trend destinations for next year (yes, us included) but with great titles come great responsibil-… crowds. That’s why we’d like to introduce you to places that are lesser known, less crowded and less touristy. Our alternative best in travel 2018, so to speak.

So, while you have the benefit of discovering a destination before the crowds, you’re also doing a lot of good with it. You travelling to
Djibouti
(yep, that’s a country),
Bhutan
or
Madagascar
supports these countries and their locals directly; especially when you travel with us (
read why here
).

And because we really believe in spreading the travel love evenly across this beautiful world, we’re taking
20% off
selected trips and we're donating another
20%
to

The Intrepid Foundation
– a win-win situation for everyone involved!
