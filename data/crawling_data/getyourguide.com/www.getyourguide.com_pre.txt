About GetYourGuide
As the leading booking platform for travel activities, GetYourGuide powers access to more than 31,000 attractions, tours and activities in upwards of 7,300 destinations around the world. From skip-the-line tickets to the Eiffel Tower to shark diving in Cape Town, GetYourGuide offers things to do for every traveller, both on its own site and in partnership with premier travel brands worldwide. Headquartered in Berlin, GetYourGuide employs more than 400 people across its offices in 11 countries. For additional information about GetYourGuide, please visit the company’s website at
