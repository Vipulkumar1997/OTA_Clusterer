Luxury Villa and Holiday Home Information

Australian holiday home and apartment rental: Renting a luxury holiday home or apartment guarantees the ultimate self catering accommodation option. Easy, comfortable and private they're like a home away from home in an amazing location and destination.

About Australia

Wide-open skies, dramatic landscapes, countless activities, fine wining and dining, and friendly locals, Australia is one of the largest countries of the world and never ceases to amaze. One of the most cosmopolitan and dynamic societies in the world being home to people from more than 200 countries the diversity of its culture and cuisine are as extreme as its landscape. A huge welcome comes to all and you will see and experience things simply not available anywhere else in the world.

Australia’s People and Culture

Total Population – 21, 457, 000; More than 20 per cent of Australians are foreign born and more than 40 per cent are of mixed cultural origin. The defining feature of Australia’s culture is its diversity. The original inhabitants are the Aboriginal people who still practice some of the worlds oldest cultural traditions.

Australia - Getting There

About 25 international airlines fly to Australia. The national airline is Qantas.

Australia - Getting Around


Air:
Australia is vast and air travel may be necessary. Qantas is the national carrier but there are also smaller budget airlines that operate between most major towns and cities.
Bus:
Extensive and cheap although having so much area to cover, travel times can be long.
Car & Motorcycle:
A popular choice is to hire or buy your own vehicle. There are both major international and smaller hire places.


Train:
No cheaper or faster than other modes of transport. However many of the journeys are some of the best rail journey’s in the world.

Australia - Popular Destinations


Brisbane
:
Australia’s third largest city and a hugely popular tourist destination. Cityscapes, glorious beaches and an abundance of fine eateries ‘Brissie’ is just getting better and better.
Carins
:
It is not hard to see why Cairns is such a popular tourist town. The lush tropical beaches combined with the Great Barrier Reef make Cairns a must stop destination.
Melbourne
:
Filled with vibrancy and character Melbourne is multi cultural and cutting edge.
Sydney
:
A stunning city with beautiful beaches, a glamorous city and diversity aplenty.  With something for everyone Sydney has everything you need for the perfect break.







Weather and climate

Australia is in the southern hemisphere so has seasons opposite to those in Europe and North America. Spring to summer (October to March) is warm or hot everywhere, tropical in the north, and warm to hot in the south. Autumn to winter (April to September) Northern and central Australia have clear warm days and cool nights; the south has cool days with occasional rain but still plenty of sun. Drought is prevalent with southeast Queensland, Victoria and South Australia all badly affected.
