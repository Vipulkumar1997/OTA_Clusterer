About the city of San Diego
San Diego is the eighth-largest city in the state of California. MoreHotels4Less provides an excellent variety of cheap hotels in San Diego. Located in Southern California, San Diego is known for its beaches, Balboa Park, the famed San Diego Zoo, Old Town and SeaWorld San Diego. One of the most visited cities in the state, San Diego offers a variety of culture, cuisine and landmarks. With hundreds of days of sunshine, you’ll have plenty of time to enjoy the outdoor activities that make San Diego such a great place to visit.
Getting around San Diego
The easiest way to get to San Diego is to fly into the San Diego International Airport. It is recommended that you rent a car while in San Diego. The airport provides a great selection of major car rental companies.
Things to do in San Diego
Take a drive to Torrey Pines State Reserve as well as to Sunset Cliffs Natural Park. You can visit San Diego’s numerous art galleries, artist studios, museums and gardens. San Diego is known for the USS Midway Museum. This is a real naval aircraft carrier that offers a self-guided audio tour featuring 60 exhibits highlighting this “city at sea” and 25 restored aircraft. Above all, take time to visit the famed San Diego Zoo.
