About the city of Orlando
Orlando is located in central Florida and MoreHotels4Less has an excellent variety of cheap Orlando hotels. Known for Disney World, Universal Studios and SeaWorld Orlando, the city is one of the most visited cities in the United States. Orlando offers a variety of entertainment venues. Stay as long as you can because there are so many things to see and do in this fabulous city!
Getting around Orlando, FL
The easiest way to get to Orlando is to fly into Orlando’s International Airport. A rental car is one of the best ways to get around Orlando. Walt Disney World has its own transportation system that can easily and efficiently get you around the park. If you stay on International Drive near Universal Studios, you can walk to many restaurants, or simply get a taxi to take you wherever you need to go.
Things to do in Orlando, Florida
You’ll have no shortage of things to do in the city.  The trip isn't complete until you visit Disney World, Universal Studios Orlando, or SeaWorld. The city has an excellent variety of affordable hotels. If you’re a sports enthusiast, take in an Orlando Magic Basketball game, or during baseball season, watch the Houston Astros and Atlanta Braves conduct their Spring Training in nearby Kissimmee and Lake Buena Vista respectively. Numerous golf courses can be found in the city, with the most famous being Bay Hill Club and Lodge, home to the Arnold Palmer Invitational.
