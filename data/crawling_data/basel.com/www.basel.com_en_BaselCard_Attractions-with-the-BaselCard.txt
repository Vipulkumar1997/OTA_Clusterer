Attractions















Ferries

Basel Zentrum







Take a trip across the Rhine in style and comfort on one of the four ferries that connect Kleinbasel with Grossbasel.

Details
















Fondation Beyeler

Basel Zentrum







By building Renzo Piano’s museum in 1997, the Fondation Beyeler made its collection accessible to the public.

Details
















Kunsthalle Basel

Basel Zentrum







Kunsthalle Basel is dedicated to exhibiting, discussing, and reflecting on the art of the present, and it has been so ever since its founding in 1872.

Details
















Kunstmuseum Basel | Gegenwart

Basel Zentrum







Kunstmuseum Basel | Contemporary is a joint undertaking by the Emanuel Hoffmann Foundation, the Christoph Merian Foundation and the Canton of Basel-Stadt.

Details
















Kunstmuseum Basel | Hauptbau

Basel Zentrum







The main building of Kunstmuseum Basel, dating from 1936, was designed by Basel-based architect Rudolf Christ together with master builder Paul Bonatz from Stuttgart, both advocates of conservative modernism.

Details
















Museum Tinguely

Basel Zentrum







Situated directly on the Rhine, the Museum Tinguely, built after plans by the Ticinese architect Mario Botta, houses the greatest collection of works by Jean Tinguely (1925–1991), one of the most innovative and important Swiss artists of the 20th century.

Details
















Vitra Design Museum

Deutschland







The Vitra Design Museum numbers among the world’s most prominent museums of design.

Details
















Anatomisches Museum der Universität Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Anatomical Museum of the University of Basel was founded by Prof. Carl Gustav Jung in 1824. Original preparations of human body parts, organs and tissue are displayed in the museum.

Details
















Antikenmuseum Basel und Sammlung Ludwig

Basel Zentrum







The Basel Museum of Ancient Art is the only Swiss museum devoted exclusively to the ancient civilizations of the Mediterranean region.

Details
















Augusta Raurica, Augst

Basel Region







Back to Roman times! The largest archaeological park in Switzerland offers more than thirty surprising, unique and exciting sights including the most impressive Roman theatre north of the Alps, the atmospheric Roman house and, on display, in the museum the largest collection of late antique silver.

Details
















Basel Zoo

Basel Zentrum







The Zoological Gardens, known affectionately as “Zolli” by Basel's inhabitants, opened in Basel in 1874 and is home to a large number of indigenous and exotic animals.

Details
















Basler Papiermühle

Basel Zentrum







Within the walls of a fascinating medieval mill, in an authentic setting, the visitor will learn about many things, from hand-made paper to finished books.

Details
















Cartoonmuseum Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Cartoonmuseum Basel is the only museum of its kind devoted exclusively to satirical art – from the caricature to the comic.

Details
















HMB - Museum für Geschichte / Barfüsserkirche

Basel Zentrum







The Historisches Museum Basel (HMB) is housed in three separate buildings and ranks among the most important museums of cultural history on the Upper Rhine.

Details
















HMB - Museum für Musik / Im Lohnhof

Basel Zentrum







Switzerland’s largest collection of musical instruments is on display in the historic Lohnhof building.

Details
















HMB - Museum für Wohnkultur

Basel Zentrum







The magnificent Kirschgarten House – built between 1775 and 1780 as the home and office of a Basel silk ribbon manufacturer – is one of Switzerland’s principal museums of domestic life.

Details
















Museum der Kulturen Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Museum der Kulturen Basel ranks among the leading ethnographic museums in Europe. The impressive collections (320,000 objects) including artefacts of world renown reflect the vast and varied scope of human ways of life.

Details
















Naturhistorisches Museum Basel

Basel Zentrum







Discover the archives of life. Lose yourself in the variety of our exhibitions. Learn exciting facts about exotic animals. Go back to the past, to the age of dinosaurs, even to the formation of the Earth.

Details
















Pharmazie-Historisches Museum der Universität Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Pharmazie-Historische Museum of the University of Basel currently contains one of the largest and most significant collections relating to the history of pharmacy.

Details
















Rhine cruises

Basel Zentrum







Whether a cosy family excursion, a school trip or a romantic day with your loved one, a cruise on the Rhine is a very special experience!

Details
















S AM Schweizerisches Architekturmuseum

Basel Zentrum







S AM – the Swiss Architecture Museum – seeks to enlarge our perception of architecture and urbanism.

Details
















Skulpturhalle Basel

Basel Zentrum







This is one of the largest collections of plaster casts of ancient sculptures.

Details
















Spielzeug Welten Museum Basel

Basel Zentrum







With over 6000 teddy bears, dolls, toy grocers’ shops, doll houses and carousels, the Toy Worlds Museum Basel is unparalleled in Europe. The collection of over 2500 teddies is the world’s largest of its kind.

Details
















Spielzeugmuseum, Dorf- und Rebbaumuseum Riehen

Basel Region







The three departments of the Museum of Riehen are housed in the 17th-century Wettsteinhaus. The historic building provides an idyllic setting for one of the leading collections of European toys of every kind.

Details
















Theater Basel

Basel Zentrum







The largest mixed-programme theatre company in Switzerland offers a broadly diverse repertoire, including dance, opera and theatre.

Details
