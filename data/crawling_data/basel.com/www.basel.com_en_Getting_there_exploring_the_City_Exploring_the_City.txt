Exploring the City


You are sure to enjoy the fact that nothing is far away in our city. Here you can find out more about getting from A to B in Basel – whether on foot, by tram or bus, or with a taxi.


















Public Transport in Basel


The city of Basel and the surrounding area has an excellent public transport network that is known for its punctuality, short waiting times and modern vehicles.

Details

















Exploring Basel on foot


Thanks to its manageable size, the city of Basel is easy to explore on foot. You can find your way around using the grey information boards at all the key locations of the city centre.

Details

















Taxis in Basel


There are taxi ranks dotted throughout the city centre. There are a number of taxi firms to choose from and as a visitor you are free to choose whichever you prefer.

Details

















Facilities for the Disabled


Pro Infirmis, the organisation for disabled persons, has issued a Basel city map for wheelchair users, containing comprehensive information on mobility in Basel.

Details
















Tourist Information SBB train station

Basel Hauptbahnhof







If you arrive in Basel by train, you can obtain tips and information at the railway station.

Details
















Tourist Information in the Stadtcasino at Barfüsserplatz

Basel Zentrum







Welcome to Basel! We want you to enjoy your stay in Basel. We are here to offer advice and assistance during your stay in our city.

Details

















Mobility Ticket


Every guest who stays in Basel receives a free Mobility Ticket when they check in, provided that their accommodation is officially registered.
