Top 10 works of art


Top must-see works of art in Basel.









For art lovers, Basel is definitely worth a visit. Our selection of the top 10 gives you a tiny preview of the masterpieces that await you here. Lose yourselves in the stories that these artworks and their creators tell, and you will begin to understand why Basel is so passionate about art.







Le bassin aux nymphéas

Claude Monet, 1917-1920, Fondation Beyeler











Seated harlequin

Pablo Picasso, 1923, Kunstmuseum Basel











Méta-Maxi-Maxi-Utopia

Jean Tinguely, 1987, Museum Tinguely











The Burghers of Calais

Auguste Rodin, 1884-1889, Kunstmuseum Basel











The Isle of the Dead

Arnold Böcklin, 1880, Kunstmuseum Basel











The Hearth

Joseph Beuys, 1968-1974, Kunstmuseum Basel | Gegenwart











The Body of the Dead Christ in the Tomb

Hans Holbein the Younger, 1522, Kunstmuseum Basel











Self-portrait with a Japanese print

Vincent van Gogh, 1887, Kunstmuseum Basel











Ta matete

Paul Gauguin, 1892, Kunstmuseum Basel











Madame Cézanne à la chaise jaune

Paul Cézanne, 1888-1890, Fondation Beyeler
