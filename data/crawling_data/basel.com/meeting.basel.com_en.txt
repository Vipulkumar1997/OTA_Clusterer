Meetings & conferences in Basel

Its central location, first-class transport connections and modern infrastructure make Basel one of the most important trade fair and congress cities in Europe. Event organisers and visitors alike also appreciate the compactness of the city. The Convention Bureau at Basel Tourism would be happy to help you organise all the aspects of your event or congress as a capable, unbiased and free consultant. We hope to be able to welcome you to Basel very soon.
