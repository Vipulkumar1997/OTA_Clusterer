In summer 2018, the Basel Tattoo takes place for the thirteenth time already. This top-quality musical event features bagpipes, brass bands and traditional folk dancing with top dance troupes from around the world and delights both young and old in equal measure. The Basel Tattoo guarantees a sensational and perfect show with the best formations, which will thrill the audience into standing ovations. Be there - with our attractive all-inclusive package including overnight accommodation and tickets.
