Attractions















Fondation Beyeler

Basel Zentrum







By building Renzo Piano’s museum in 1997, the Fondation Beyeler made its collection accessible to the public.

Details
















Kunstmuseum Basel | Gegenwart

Basel Zentrum







Kunstmuseum Basel | Contemporary is a joint undertaking by the Emanuel Hoffmann Foundation, the Christoph Merian Foundation and the Canton of Basel-Stadt.

Details
















Kunstmuseum Basel | Hauptbau

Basel Zentrum







The main building of Kunstmuseum Basel, dating from 1936, was designed by Basel-based architect Rudolf Christ together with master builder Paul Bonatz from Stuttgart, both advocates of conservative modernism.

Details
















Kunstmuseum Basel | Neubau

Basel Zentrum







The new extension located opposite the main building and joined to it by an underground walkway is a top-quality exhibition venue for contemporary art presentation and a further architectural highlight in the City of Basel.

Details
















Museum Tinguely

Basel Zentrum







Situated directly on the Rhine, the Museum Tinguely, built after plans by the Ticinese architect Mario Botta, houses the greatest collection of works by Jean Tinguely (1925–1991), one of the most innovative and important Swiss artists of the 20th century.

Details
















Vitra Design Museum

Deutschland







The Vitra Design Museum numbers among the world’s most prominent museums of design.

Details
















Anne Mosseri-Marlio Galerie

Basel Zentrum







The gallery's programme includes nonobjective art and conceptual art, as well as some works from the minimalist scene.

Details
















Artstübli

Basel Zentrum







Since 2004, Artstübli has been promoting the urban art scene at various alternative locations in Basel, thus ensuring a lively exchange.

Details
















Cahn Jean-David

Basel Zentrum







Jean-David Cahn specialises in Ancient Greek, Roman and Egyptian art.

Details
















Daniel Blaise Thorens Fine Art Gallery

Basel Zentrum







Daniel Blaise Thorens founded his gallery in 1976.

Details
















Galerie Carzaniga Basel

Basel Zentrum







Galerie Carzaniga brings together classic and contemporary art from Switzerland, Germany and Italy.

Details
















Galerie Eulenspiegel

Basel Zentrum







Galerie Eulenspiegel was established by Gregor Muntwiler in 2001 and is located at the heart of Basel's old town.

Details
















Galerie Ficher Rohr

Basel Zentrum







This gallery specialises mainly in exhibitions of works by renowned artists from South America. It also promotes young talent.

Details
















Galerie Gisèle Linder

Basel Zentrum







Galerie Gisèle Linder focuses on concrete art.

Details
















Galerie Guillaume Daeppen

Basel Zentrum







With six exhibitions each year, this gallery features a large variety of installations, pictures and graphic art.

Details
















Galerie Henze & Ketterer & Triebold

Basel Region







This gallery specialises in artists from the German expressionism movement, post-1945 abstract art, the New Figuration movement and contemporary painters and sculptors from Italy, Spain and Germany.

Details
















Galerie Hilt

Basel Zentrum







At this gallery you will find Swiss art, international graphic art with an emphasis on classic modernism and African tribal art.

Details
















Galerie Katapult

Basel Zentrum







Works of both established and less well-known contemporary artists from Switzerland and abroad are displayed in changing group and one-person shows across the 200 m2 exhibition space. One of the focal themes will be contemporary art from Latin America.

Details
















Galerie Knoell

Basel Zentrum







The gallery’s ongoing programme of exhibitions showcases a wide range of styles, positions and currents, extending from shows of old masters to engagements with contemporary art.

Details
















Galerie Mollwo

Basel Region







Galerie Mollwo concentrates mainly on contemporary art.

Details
















Galerie Stampa

Basel Zentrum







The focus at Stampa is on Swiss and international contemporary art.

Details
















Galerie Zangbieri

Basel Zentrum







Galerie Zangbieri features naive Brazilian painting, contemporary art from Switzerland and increasingly also international art.

Details
















Galerie von Bartha

Basel Zentrum







Since opening in 1970, von Bartha has exhibited modern and contemporary artists across historically significant movements such as Zero, Arte Concreto and Arte Madí.

Details
















Graf & Schelble Galerie

Basel Zentrum







Admire contemporary art from Switzerland and around the world at Galerie Graf & Schelble.

Details
















JanKossen Contemporary Galerie

Basel Zentrum







The Jan Kossen Contemporary Galerie is committed to fostering international cultural exchange by curating shows particularly from outside Europe.

Details
















Klingental exhibition space

Basel Zentrum







The Klingental exhibition space introduces the public to art created here and now in the Basel region.

Details
















Kunst Raum Riehen

Basel Region







Kunst Raum Riehen, next door to the Fondation Beyeler, shows temporary exhibitions of all genres of contemporary art.

Details
















Kunsthalle Palazzo Liestal

Basel Region







The naming of an exhibition hall “Kunsthalle” (art gallery) in the small town of Liestal only became possible once people first began to understand this term not as an institutional tradition, but rather as a cultural programme.

Details
















Kunsthaus Baselland

Basel Region







Kunsthaus Baselland is the Basel region's leading exhibition venue for contemporary art.

Details
















Laleh June Galerie

Basel Zentrum







The Laleh June Galerie was founded in 2008 and is located in the city center, in Picassoplatz, between Rhine and the historic old town, steps away from the Kunstmuseum Basel.

Details
















Mitart Galerie

Basel Zentrum







The mitart gallery shows contemporary works by national and international artists with an emphasis on painting, sculpture, room installations and photography.

Details
















Nicolas Krupp Galerie

Basel Zentrum







The focus at the Nicolas Krupp Galerie is firmly on international contemporary avant-garde art.

Details
















Projektraum M54

Basel Zentrum







The venue puts on three exhibitions each year featuring individual or group works.

Details
















Rusterholtz Galerie + Auktionen

Basel Zentrum







Rusterholtz Galerie is well known for works on paper and prints by internationally renowned artists.

Details
















Schaulager

Basel Region







Neither traditional museum nor warehouse, Schaulager creates a new type of art institution. It is the storage facility for the collection of the Emanuel Hoffmann Foundation.

Details
















Tony Wuethrich Galerie

Basel Zentrum







Since its opening the gallery has concentrated on contemporary paintings and sculpture.

Details
















balzer projects

Basel Zentrum







In addition to paintings, graphic art, sculpture and photography, balzer projects also presents video installations and new media art of largely emerging, international artists.

Details
