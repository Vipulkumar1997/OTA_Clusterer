Attractions















Treasure hunts with Basil

Basel Zentrum







An exciting voyage of discovery through Basel – specially for the juniors.

Details
















Aerial tramway Reigoldswil-Wasserfallen

Basel Region







Discover Wasserfallen and discover fun and recreation at one of the best-loved excursion destinations in the region.

Details
















Augusta Raurica, Augst

Basel Region







Back to Roman times! The largest archaeological park in Switzerland offers more than thirty surprising, unique and exciting sights including the most impressive Roman theatre north of the Alps, the atmospheric Roman house and, on display, in the museum the largest collection of late antique silver.

Details
















Basel Zoo

Basel Zentrum







The Zoological Gardens, known affectionately as “Zolli” by Basel's inhabitants, opened in Basel in 1874 and is home to a large number of indigenous and exotic animals.

Details
















Basler Papiermühle

Basel Zentrum







Within the walls of a fascinating medieval mill, in an authentic setting, the visitor will learn about many things, from hand-made paper to finished books.

Details
















Bottmingen Castle

Basel Region







The moated castle of Bottmingen, a community to the south­west of Basel, is a highlight for all romantics. Dating from the 13th century, it is one of the few such buildings in Switzerland that are still intact.

Details
















Bürgeln Castle

Deutschland







The castle of Bürgeln, a manor house in Germany's Markgräflerland, is well worth a visit. Begun in 1762 and completed two years later, it is now a popular venue for seminars and other events.

Details
















Cartoonmuseum Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Cartoonmuseum Basel is the only museum of its kind devoted exclusively to satirical art – from the caricature to the comic.

Details
















Château Haut-Koenigsbourg

Frankreich







The Château Haut-Koenigsbourg is located in the heart of the Alsace wine-growing region. Entering through the doors of the impressive château is like taking a trip back to the Middle Ages.

Details
















Europa-Park

Deutschland







Europa-Park in Rust, Germany, is probably the most spectacular leisure park in the region. With over a hundred attractions and shows in 14 European themed areas, the park is a delight for both young and older visitors.

Details
















Haus der elektronischen Künste Basel, Münchenstein

Basel Region







The House of Electronic Arts (HeK) is the national competence centre that deals with art forms that address and reflect upon new media and technologies.

Details
















Lange Erlen Animal Park

Basel Region







Founded in 1871, Lange Erlen Animal Park is a popular destination for families.

Details
















Läckerli Huus «World of Indulgence»

Basel Region







Look forward to an interactive experience for all the senses.

Details
















Monkey Mountain – Montagne des Singes

Frankreich







Discover Barbary macaques in the wild at the foot of the Château du Haut-Kœnigsburg. A thrilling excursion for all the family!

Details
















Museum der Kulturen Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Museum der Kulturen Basel ranks among the leading ethnographic museums in Europe. The impressive collections (320,000 objects) including artefacts of world renown reflect the vast and varied scope of human ways of life.

Details
















Museum für Musikautomaten

Basel Region







A world-famous collection of music and movement. A delightful hour-long guided tour wins visitors over to the universe of mechanical music.

Details
















Myclimate Audio Adventure

Basel Zentrum







Myclimate Audio Adventure brings you exciting audio stories on the topics of climate protection and sustainability, combined with local history and amusing anecdotes through which you'll get to know some new aspects of Basel.

Details
















Mühlemuseum

Basel Region







The mill from the 16th century is situated at the heart of the Merian Gardens. Even though flour is no longer produced here, the water-powered wheel is still in use.

Details
















Naturhistorisches Museum Basel

Basel Zentrum







Discover the archives of life. Lose yourself in the variety of our exhibitions. Learn exciting facts about exotic animals. Go back to the past, to the age of dinosaurs, even to the formation of the Earth.

Details
















Pharmazie-Historisches Museum der Universität Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Pharmazie-Historische Museum of the University of Basel currently contains one of the largest and most significant collections relating to the history of pharmacy.

Details
















Rötteln Castle

Deutschland







Rötteln Castle, near the city of Lörrach, is built on a mountain spur on the steep eastern slopes of the Wiesental valley in southern Germany.

Details
















Schloss Binningen

Basel Region







Historic Schloss Binningen is located at the centre of the municipality of Binningen, near Basel.

Details
















Schweizerisches Feuerwehrmuseum Basel

Basel Zentrum







Fire-fighting equipment, ranging from medieval hand-operated spraying devices to modern oxygen inhalers.

Details
















Skulpturhalle Basel

Basel Zentrum







This is one of the largest collections of plaster casts of ancient sculptures.

Details
















Spielzeug Welten Museum Basel

Basel Zentrum







With over 6000 teddy bears, dolls, toy grocers’ shops, doll houses and carousels, the Toy Worlds Museum Basel is unparalleled in Europe. The collection of over 2500 teddies is the world’s largest of its kind.

Details
















Spielzeugmuseum, Dorf- und Rebbaumuseum Riehen

Basel Region







The three departments of the Museum of Riehen are housed in the 17th-century Wettsteinhaus. The historic building provides an idyllic setting for one of the leading collections of European toys of every kind.

Details
















Steinwasen Park

Deutschland







With its breathtaking mountain landscape and various attractions, Steinwasen Park is an outdoor experience for all the family.

Details
















The Kandertal railway

Deutschland







The Kandertal railway is an exciting excursion for families, hikers and cyclists alike.

Details
















Verkehrsdrehscheibe Schweiz und unser Weg zum Meer

Basel Zentrum







A shipping museum and an exhibition on current means of transport, all rolled into one: water, rail, road and air, with the focus on shipping.

Details
