Information




You can enjoy culinary delicacies from around the world at around 20 stands along Rheingasse. Throughout the Advent period a host of different events –  both large and small – are guaranteed to get you in the Christmas mood.

Connoisseurs and fans of Christmas specialities shouldn't miss the offerings on the Adväntsgass ("Advent alley"). The many stands along the street invite you to indulge.
