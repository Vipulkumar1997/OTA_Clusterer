BaselCard


“More of the city for less.” Visit the beautiful city of Basel at the heart of Europe and discover 100% culture with the BaselCard.  On the banks of the Rhine, at the border triangle of France, Germany and Switzerland is a fantastic city just waiting to be discovered by you.



Discover the sights of the city with a BaselCard (valid for either 24 or 48 hours) and take advantage of numerous discounts on cultural offers and attractions. Regardless of whether you visit Basel's famous zoo, go on a walking tour of the Old Town, take a boat trip on the Rhine, visit the theatre, cross the river by ferry or visit one of the first-rate museums in Basel and the surrounding area: with the BaselCard you’ll save 50% on selected tourist activities in the city.
