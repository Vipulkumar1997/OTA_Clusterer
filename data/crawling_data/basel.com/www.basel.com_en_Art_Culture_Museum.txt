Museums


Nearly 40 museums with spectacular collections and special exhibitions – unique in Switzerland and at the top of the international league too. If you want to understand why Basel’s residents are so keen on art, you should visit at least one of them.

Attractions















Fondation Beyeler

Basel Zentrum







By building Renzo Piano’s museum in 1997, the Fondation Beyeler made its collection accessible to the public.

Details
















Kunstmuseum Basel | Gegenwart

Basel Zentrum







Kunstmuseum Basel | Contemporary is a joint undertaking by the Emanuel Hoffmann Foundation, the Christoph Merian Foundation and the Canton of Basel-Stadt.

Details
















Kunstmuseum Basel | Hauptbau

Basel Zentrum







The main building of Kunstmuseum Basel, dating from 1936, was designed by Basel-based architect Rudolf Christ together with master builder Paul Bonatz from Stuttgart, both advocates of conservative modernism.

Details
















Kunstmuseum Basel | Neubau

Basel Zentrum







The new extension located opposite the main building and joined to it by an underground walkway is a top-quality exhibition venue for contemporary art presentation and a further architectural highlight in the City of Basel.

Details
















Museum Tinguely

Basel Zentrum







Situated directly on the Rhine, the Museum Tinguely, built after plans by the Ticinese architect Mario Botta, houses the greatest collection of works by Jean Tinguely (1925–1991), one of the most innovative and important Swiss artists of the 20th century.

Details
















Vitra Design Museum

Deutschland







The Vitra Design Museum numbers among the world’s most prominent museums of design.

Details
















Anatomisches Museum der Universität Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Anatomical Museum of the University of Basel was founded by Prof. Carl Gustav Jung in 1824. Original preparations of human body parts, organs and tissue are displayed in the museum.

Details
















Antikenmuseum Basel und Sammlung Ludwig

Basel Zentrum







The Basel Museum of Ancient Art is the only Swiss museum devoted exclusively to the ancient civilizations of the Mediterranean region.

Details
















Augusta Raurica, Augst

Basel Region







Back to Roman times! The largest archaeological park in Switzerland offers more than thirty surprising, unique and exciting sights including the most impressive Roman theatre north of the Alps, the atmospheric Roman house and, on display, in the museum the largest collection of late antique silver.

Details
















Basler Papiermühle

Basel Zentrum







Within the walls of a fascinating medieval mill, in an authentic setting, the visitor will learn about many things, from hand-made paper to finished books.

Details
















Cartoonmuseum Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Cartoonmuseum Basel is the only museum of its kind devoted exclusively to satirical art – from the caricature to the comic.

Details
















Dreiländermuseum

Deutschland







The permanent exhibition is the first comprehensive depiction of the eventful history of the border region where Germany, Switzerland and France meet. Interactive terminals make this trinational show a unique experience.

Details
















HMB - Museum für Geschichte / Barfüsserkirche

Basel Zentrum







The Historisches Museum Basel (HMB) is housed in three separate buildings and ranks among the most important museums of cultural history on the Upper Rhine.

Details
















HMB - Museum für Musik / Im Lohnhof

Basel Zentrum







Switzerland’s largest collection of musical instruments is on display in the historic Lohnhof building.

Details
















HMB - Museum für Wohnkultur

Basel Zentrum







The magnificent Kirschgarten House – built between 1775 and 1780 as the home and office of a Basel silk ribbon manufacturer – is one of Switzerland’s principal museums of domestic life.

Details
















Haus der elektronischen Künste Basel, Münchenstein

Basel Region







The House of Electronic Arts (HeK) is the national competence centre that deals with art forms that address and reflect upon new media and technologies.

Details
















Jüdisches Museum der Schweiz

Basel Zentrum







The Jewish Museum of Switzerland shows one of the best collections of Judaica in central Europe.

Details
















Klingental exhibition space

Basel Zentrum







The Klingental exhibition space introduces the public to art created here and now in the Basel region.

Details
















Kunst Raum Riehen

Basel Region







Kunst Raum Riehen, next door to the Fondation Beyeler, shows temporary exhibitions of all genres of contemporary art.

Details
















Kunsthaus Baselland

Basel Region







Kunsthaus Baselland is the Basel region's leading exhibition venue for contemporary art.

Details
















Kupferstichkabinett und Bibliothek (Kunstmuseum)

Basel Zentrum







With 60,000 drawings and watercolours, 250,000 prints and engravings, the Kupferstichkabinett of the Kunstmuseum Basel is not only the biggest in Switzerland, but also houses one of the world's most important collections of its kind.

Details
















Läckerli Huus «World of Indulgence»

Basel Region







Look forward to an interactive experience for all the senses.

Details
















Museum Kleines Klingental

Basel Zentrum







This museum is housed in the historical rooms of the former Klingental Dominican convent and features original medieval sculptures from Basel Cathedral, a 17th-century model of the city and the history of the convent itself.

Details
















Museum der Kulturen Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Museum der Kulturen Basel ranks among the leading ethnographic museums in Europe. The impressive collections (320,000 objects) including artefacts of world renown reflect the vast and varied scope of human ways of life.

Details
















Museum für Musikautomaten

Basel Region







A world-famous collection of music and movement. A delightful hour-long guided tour wins visitors over to the universe of mechanical music.

Details
















Museum.BL

Basel Region







The Museum.BL is an open and lively thematic museum for all children and grown-ups. It invites its visitors, especially families, to explore issues relating to the environment, the past and present-day life.

Details
















Mühlemuseum

Basel Region







The mill from the 16th century is situated at the heart of the Merian Gardens. Even though flour is no longer produced here, the water-powered wheel is still in use.

Details
















Naturhistorisches Museum Basel

Basel Zentrum







Discover the archives of life. Lose yourself in the variety of our exhibitions. Learn exciting facts about exotic animals. Go back to the past, to the age of dinosaurs, even to the formation of the Earth.

Details
















Pantheon - Museum für Oldtimer

Basel Region







The Pantheon is both an attractive event venue and a classic car workshop, as well as a classic car museum. During your visit, you will learn all about the history of the motor car.

Details
















Pharmazie-Historisches Museum der Universität Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Pharmazie-Historische Museum of the University of Basel currently contains one of the largest and most significant collections relating to the history of pharmacy.

Details
















S AM Schweizerisches Architekturmuseum

Basel Zentrum







S AM – the Swiss Architecture Museum – seeks to enlarge our perception of architecture and urbanism.

Details
















Sammlung Friedhof Hörnli

Basel Region







This unique collection of items from funerary culture shows that everyone is not the same, even in death.

Details
















Schaulager

Basel Region







Neither traditional museum nor warehouse, Schaulager creates a new type of art institution. It is the storage facility for the collection of the Emanuel Hoffmann Foundation.

Details
















Schweizerisches Feuerwehrmuseum Basel

Basel Zentrum







Fire-fighting equipment, ranging from medieval hand-operated spraying devices to modern oxygen inhalers.

Details
















Skulpturhalle Basel

Basel Zentrum







This is one of the largest collections of plaster casts of ancient sculptures.

Details
















Spielzeug Welten Museum Basel

Basel Zentrum







With over 6000 teddy bears, dolls, toy grocers’ shops, doll houses and carousels, the Toy Worlds Museum Basel is unparalleled in Europe. The collection of over 2500 teddies is the world’s largest of its kind.

Details
















Spielzeugmuseum, Dorf- und Rebbaumuseum Riehen

Basel Region







The three departments of the Museum of Riehen are housed in the 17th-century Wettsteinhaus. The historic building provides an idyllic setting for one of the leading collections of European toys of every kind.

Details
















Sportmuseum Schweiz

Basel Region







This museum conserves the cultural heritage of Swiss sports.

Details
















Verkehrsdrehscheibe Schweiz und unser Weg zum Meer

Basel Zentrum







A shipping museum and an exhibition on current means of transport, all rolled into one: water, rail, road and air, with the focus on shipping.

Details
