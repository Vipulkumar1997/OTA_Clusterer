Basler Kindertheater

Basel Zentrum







The Basel Children's Theatre offers a glimpse of another world. Here the next generation take to the boards and stand in the spotlight for the world to see.

Details
















Basler Marionetten Theater

Basel Zentrum

Details

Gare du Nord

Details
















Häbse Theater

Basel Zentrum

Details
















Junges Theater Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Junges Theater Basel (lit: Young Theatre Basel) perfects and performs its chosen pieces in Basel before taking them on tour around Switzerland and increasingly to international festivals.

Details
















Kaserne Basel

Basel Zentrum







The Kaserne Basel is the largest centre in north-west Switzerland for the free contemporary theatre, dance and performance scene and for innovative popular music.

Musical Theater Basel

Theater Fauteuil

Theater Semi-Circle
