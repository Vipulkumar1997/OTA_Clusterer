The inside story

La Scala Theatre (Teatro alla Scala) is perhaps the world's most famous Opera House and Ballet Theatre, founded in 1778.
In central Milan, La Scala has been the stage of many famous composers of the past (such as Giuseppe Verdi, Vincenzo Bellini and Arturo Toscanini), and today it’s the home of quality Operas, Classical Music Concerts and Ballets.
