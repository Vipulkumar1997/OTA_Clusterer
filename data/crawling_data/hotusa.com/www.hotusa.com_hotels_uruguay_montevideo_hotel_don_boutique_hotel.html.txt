Located in the emblematic Mercado del Puerto, Don Boutique Hotel is perfect for those who appreciate the exclusiveness. The exquisite design details combine with the fabulous personal service. The accommodation, built by R. Pose and sons in 1938, maintains in its façade its nautical art-decó style. DON is smoke free, except in the outside areas. It has 21 rooms: 14 standard rooms, 6 superior rooms and one luxury room. From their terraces, guests can enjoy of wonderful views to the bay and to the Montevideo harbor. *Non smoker hotel.

How to get there
From the international airport of Carrasco: take Las Americas Avenue, Italia Avenue and from there on, turn left into Rambla Costanera. Border the city in direction to the historical center of Montevideo (Ciudad Vieja) and continue until Fco. Maciel street (just before the Harbour Market). Once here, turn right, cross one block and at the left you'll find the Piedras street.
