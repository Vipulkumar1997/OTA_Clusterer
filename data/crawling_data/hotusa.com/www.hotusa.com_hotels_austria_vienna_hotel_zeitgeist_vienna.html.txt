Since May 2013, this modern hotel is situated right on the new Vienna Central Station. The Stephansplatz is a half hour walk or a 5-minute metro ride from the hotel. Hotel Zeitgeist Vienna Hauptbahnhof is perfect for business and leisure trips. The 254 bright and modern rooms at the hotel feature air conditioning, 42'' LED TV with international TV and radio, free high speed wireless and wired internet TV, telephone, desk, walk-in shower with Raindance shower and hairdryer. Almost all rooms have a separate toilet from the bathroom. The hotel also offers barrier-free rooms, allergy-free bedding and rooms with connecting doors (on request). The hotel Zeitgeist Vienna Hauptbahnhof also has a modern Technogym, a sanarium style sauna on the seventh floor, roof terrace included. In the hotel there is also parking, refreshment vending machines with candy, snacks and drinks, and two meeting rooms with up to 26 people. Enjoy tasty meals and refreshing drinks in a relaxing atmosphere in the Bar-Restaurant Lokal Pergola.
