The Flag Zurich is an apartment concept geared towards the needs of business people and city travellers. Our hotel is located in Altstetten quarter and its motto is "Home away from home". The Flag Zurich offers the convenience and amenities that modern-day nomads appreciate. The Welcome Desk is open from 6AM to 12:30AM during weekdays. On weekends, the reception is occupied from 8AM to 4PM. In case of a late arrival, you can always check in via our Check in station located in front of the lobby. The Flag Zurich counts 101 rooms. Every one of them is equipped with a Smart TV system, which can be used to download over 200 movies free of charge. The fitness centre is only 300 meters away. Those who prefer to exercise outdoor can explore the Uetliberg area. Jogging routes from our hotel in Altstetten can be found on the panel of the virtual concierge. Free WLAN and USB connections and network cables provided, add convenience for working professionals. In the lobby, a tasty breakfast is available upon request. A modern coffee maker is also at your disposal. In the evening, you can eat at a restaurant in Altstetten, near our hotel.
