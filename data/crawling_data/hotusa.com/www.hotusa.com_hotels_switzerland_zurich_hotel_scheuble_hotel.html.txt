Your hotel in downtown Zurich - Hotel Scheuble. This small, precious gem of a hotel is located in the heart of Zurich's Old City, in Niederdorf. And here with us, you are in the center of all that we do. This is our conviction, and how we understand the whole concept of hospitality. Our team dedicates itself one hundred percent to ensuring your stay becomes an unforgettable experience. Hotel Scheuble features 64 rooms spread over 5 floors. The special design elements are particularly charming and individually tailored to each room and conceived by internationally renowned architect, Pia Schmid. The carefully renovated interior unites Euro-Asiatic details with the valuable and original architectural structure of the house. Hotel Scheuble has a long tradition, yet boasts all the modern comforts, including free WiFi and TV.
