Rome is the capital of Italy and offers the visitor a wide range of tourist attractions and services. Rome is the meeting point of artistic styles representing every period of history. Its streets and neighbourhoods are the result of this fusion, a blend that is manifested in its ancient, medieval, Renaissance and modern buildings.
