The hotel Portón de la 10 has 21 spacious and comfortable rooms, equipped with the most complete services for your business or pleasure trip, with air conditioning, television, Security Box, and Wi-Fi completely free in all areas of the hotel. The "Portón de la 10", is located in an exclusive area, close to Olaya Herrera Airport and to the south transport terminal, five minutes from the subway station of the village next to the commercial and financial area of Medellín, surrounded by Shopping centers, restaurants, cinemas and easy access to the main roads of the City. Likewise, it has an excellent service of private transport that is transferred to any sector of the City.
