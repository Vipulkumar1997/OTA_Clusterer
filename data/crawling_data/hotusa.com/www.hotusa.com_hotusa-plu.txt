What is Hotusa Plus?

Hotusa Plus is a travellers' community that rewards you for doing what you enjoy most: travelling!
With Hotusa Plus, every time you make a reservation at any of the 2,500+ hotels available on the website, we will return 3% of the cost of the booking. This balance will accumulate in your account, so you can use it for future reservations.



No more points

No need for complicated point counting, count Euros instead!
For each booking, we will add money to your account that you can use to pay for your hotel reservations.



When is the money added to my account?

After your date of departure from the hotel, the money will be automatically added to your user account.



How can I check my balance?

You can check your movements in your customer account.  From there, you can manage the money accumulated and monitor your booking history in real-time.



Use the money when you want

You decide when to use the amount you have accumulated. You can use your balance when you pay for your next booking or keep on accumulating it, as long as you don't go past the expiry date.



How can I be Plus?

Sign up right now using our registration form.  You will immediately receive a confirmation e-mail with your access codes so that you can start making reservations and accumulating euros.



How can I book my hotel?

You can book your hotel through es.hotusa.com, identifying yourself with your Plus codes.



How can I invite my friends to be part of Hotusa Plus?

You can send invitations to your friends and acquaintances from your customer account so that they can enjoy the advantages of being Plus.Five euros will be added to your account the first time that each of your invitees makes a reservation.
