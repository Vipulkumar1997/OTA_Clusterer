Hotel Schani Wien is situated at the Belvedere Quartier, right next to the central railway station 'Hauptbahnhof Wien'. The hotel is a genuine Viennese property in its own way and we want our guests to experience the modern Viennese spirit. Hotel Schani Wien bridges the gap between contemporary local culture and guests from all corners of the globe. Our guests can expect ideal surroundings for business and leisure purposes, starting with cutting-edge features of the rooms and property. The neighbourhood includes the new and moderns railway station as well as the historic buildings of Belvedere Castle. New features like mobile check-in, door locks that open with your mobile device, your own room selection and coworking spaces with 20 coworking places, are some of the highlights of this "future hotel" (in research with "Institut Fraunhofer"). *Breakfast not included: 12 EUR / person / day.

How to get there
Take an Ice direct train to the central railway station 'Hauptbahnhof Wien' (18 minutes). Hotel Schani is right next to the railway station. Take de City Airport Train to the station 'Wien Mitte' and change there to S1 or S3, going to the central railway station.
