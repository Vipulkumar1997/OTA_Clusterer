A warm welcome and a friendly atmosphere awaits you to ensure that your stay will be pleasureable and memorable. The hotel Ilbertz is located on the right site of the Rhine. Ideally situated within a few minutes walking distance to Kölner Dom, KölnMesse, Köln Arena and the train station. You can leave your car in the garage. It offers 26 comfortable guestrooms equipped with shower/toilet, air conditioning, hairdryer, telephone, TV and minibar. A rich and well-balanced breakfast buffet gives a good start for the day. Furthermore, you will find some modern conference facilities. You can also relax and finish your day in the steam sauna, Finnish sauna or solarium. Breakfast can be booked directly at the hotel for an additional supplement.
