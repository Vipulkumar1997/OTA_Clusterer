The Hotel is located in the north of the city, just a few steps from Plaza Foch, the tourist area of Quito, an interesting mix of houses, shops, restaurants, bars, art galleries, tour operators and travel agencies. Hotel Bonaventure offers you carpeted rooms with spacious beds, double and triple accommodations, fully equipped, with excellent service and comfort for your stay either for work and / or pleasure. The property is located 6 blocks from El Ejido park, 3 km from the Historic center of Quito and 4 km from the Mariscal Sucre airport.
