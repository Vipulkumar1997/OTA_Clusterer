Contact Our Offices

Would you like to learn more about our adventures and other services? Do you have a question about a particular destination? Are you ready to book? Our friendly reservations teams in London, Toronto and Sydney are happy to take your email, phone call or live chat request.



Employment

Live the adventure! Join our award-winning team

If you've always dreamed of a career in the travel industry, or are looking for your next job in adventure tourism, Tucan Travel could have the perfect position for you. We're always on the look out for talented individuals to join our on-road staff as tour leaders and in our worldwide destinations, as well as office-based positions in our reservations and operations teams.



Travel Shows

Join us at a travel show, expo or travel talk

Meet the Tucan Travel team at a travel show, travel expo, travel event or free presentation and ask our friendly, well-travelled team for personal travel advice. We often have stalls at major travel shows, and host smaller presentations for specific destinations. We're the travel experts and we're here to help!



Press Room

Press releases, contact details and media archive

How to Book

Online, direct through our offices or through an agent
It's easy to book your adventure with us. Contact our friendly, experienced reservations team for assistance or if you prefer, you can book online or via a travel agent. We accept several forms of payment including credit cards, debit cards, cheques, transfers and cash.
