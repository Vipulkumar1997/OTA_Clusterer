Why Choose Tucan Travel?



Responsible & Sustainable

Working from a strict
Responsible Travel Policy
, our tours are sustainably run by giving back to local communities and preventing environmental damage.



Professional Tour Leaders & Local Guides

Our Tour Leaders are resilient, passionate, experienced and energetic. They are often supported by Local Guides who provide specialised knowledge.



Over 2000 Guaranteed Departures

We clearly display which tours are guaranteed and frequently update our website with new guaranteed departures, so you can book with confidence.



Experienced & Award-Winning

Our 30 years' of experience means we have won various awards. This includes winning the Best Escorted Experience Tour Operator in the British Travel Awards and South America's Leading Tour Operator in the World Travel Awards.



Small Group Adventures with Friends

We specialise in small group tours. Smaller groups allow you to have a more personal experience meeting new, likeminded friends along the way.



Flexibility to Travel for Longer

Most of our tours are short sections of longer trips and can be combined to form an extended adventure through the region of your choice.
