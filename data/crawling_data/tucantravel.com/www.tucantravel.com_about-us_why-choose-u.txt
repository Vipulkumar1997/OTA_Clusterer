Why Choose Us?








Why Choose Us?

There are at least 10 good reasons to travel with us

When you're taking the holiday of a lifetime you need to choose an established, bonded tour operator you can trust. We are proud to offer excellent quality and value for money in our tours, which give you the chance to experience your chosen destination in an authentic and exciting way.



Guaranteed Departures

Book your group tour with confidence

Tucan Travel's guaranteed departures take the guesswork out of planning your adventure and give you the peace of mind to book the tour that suits you.



Your Financial Protection

Book your adventure holiday with confidence

Safeguarding your adventure holiday is important to us. We adhere to strict industry standards so you can be sure your adventure holiday is secure and fully protected.



Industry Memberships

Top international tour-operator organisations to which we belong

Tucan Travel is a member of the world's top tour operator organisations that are committed to a professionalism of service within the adventure tour industry and responsible policies in the countries and regions in wich members operate.




Overland Travel

What to expect on an Overland Tour

Everything you need to know about overland tours, from a history and overview of the first overland tours, to how to select the best overland tour for you. Advice on how to choose your ideal overland tour and information on what to expect while you're travelling including what the vehicles are like and what participation is required.




History of Tucan Travel

Online, direct through our offices or through an agent

It's easy to book your adventure with us. Contact our friendly, experienced reservations team for assistance or if you prefer, you can book online or via a travel agent. We accept several forms of payment including credit cards, debit cards, cheques, transfers and cash.




Our Prices

Great value for money

We are often asked why our tours offer such great value for money compared to our competitors. The answer is simple. Our prices take into account full itineraries, high quality accommodation and transport, plus logistical support from our offices worldwide.
