Luxury Holidays in Nha Trang, Vietnam


Nha Trang in Vietnam is widely known for its amazing exotic environment and tropical beaches while it is also considered as one of the most beautiful bays in the world with amazing snorkelling and diving sites !
However, Nha Trang offers more than just luxury hotels, resorts, and villas right on stunning beaches! Elegant restaurants and bars, as well as shopping and entertainment choices await you in the town's southern areas.



Luxury Travel Tips



Vinh Luong and Dam are two excellent street markets where you can observe the daily life of Nha Trang's locals as well as buy clothes, jewelry and handmade goods at low prices.




Nha Trang Centre is the first huge mall in Nha Trang with four stores that house over 120 boutiques, restaurants, cafes & entertainment options like a Karaoke bar, a cinema, and a bowling center.
