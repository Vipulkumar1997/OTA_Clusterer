Barcelona



Spain




Barcelona
is
one of the most vibrant cities in Europe
. It owes its success to the fact that it is
    suitable for all audiences. Whether you are 7 or 97 years old, Barcelona has all you need : tapas bars, wine bars,
beaches, sunshine
, incredible architecture,
one of the best shopping destinations in the world
; Barcelona has everything to please you.



Discovering this city during a cruise is an incredible experience but if you want to avoid the traditional circuits we advise you to choose among
original activities during
    your stay in Barcelona
. There is the traditional  guided tour of the
Sagrada Familia
and
Park Güell
as well as a
Day trip to Montserrat
; that
    monastery was built in the mountains of Catalonia.



Enjoy your stay in Barcelona and
book your hotel at the best prices
.

in Lisbon.
