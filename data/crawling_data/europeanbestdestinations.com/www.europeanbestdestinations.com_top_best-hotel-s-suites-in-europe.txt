The Suite



Shangri-La-Paris





































Perched on the top floor of the hotel, which was added to the building in 1966,
The Suite Shangri-La
’s modern architecture is balanced with classic French furnishings.
    Pierre-Yves Rochon blended the Directoire (1795-1799) and Empire (1803-1821) styles, complemented with elegant Asian touches throughout. With a total of 225-square-metres (2,420-square-feet) of
    living space, the suite’s spacious 100-square-metre (1,076-square-foot) terrace offers a sweeping
panoramic view over the city
.



The suite looks down on to
Gustave Eiffel
’s former home and directly on to the first level of the
Eiffel Tower
. This unobstructed view is thanks to the visionary
    engineer’s efforts to prohibit high-rise construction around his tower.

The Royal Penthouse



Corinthia Hotel London





































At 5,000 sq ft,
The Royal Penthouse
takes its place as the largest two-bedroom luxury hotel residence in London. Overlooking the River Thames, it offers an uninterrupted 180
    degree view all the way from the soaring towers of the City to the heritage sights in Westminster from its sweeping terrace complete with loungers and fire pit.



The ultimate London vantage point offers a majestic array of features set over two storeys. Highlights include an imposing entrance hall, an elegant panoramic living room and a high tech study. A
    private butler’s kitchen and private wine cellar service the unique oval dining room which is clad in facetted and bevelled mirrors.

The Yeatman Porto





































Named after the Roman god of wine,
The Bacchus suite
is the jewel in
The Yeatman
’s crown, with its spacious, open plan design providing the most exclusive and
    private accommodation in the hotel. Boasting approximately 150m² in total, and a high vaulted wooden ceiling,



A round copper
Jacuzzi
features a freestanding fireplace to one side and city views to the other, whilst the large rotating bed can be positioned to view one of two large plasma
    TV screens or to face the expansive views offered by 6 metres of floor to ceiling sliding doors.

The Penthouse



Rome Cavalieri Waldorf Astoria





































The treasure chest that is the
Rome Cavalieri
boasts a priceless new jewel:
THE PENTHOUSE
. Located on the exclusive Executive Floor, this 250 square-meter suite
    offers the ultimate in amenities and luxury as well as its own private 200 square-meter panoramic roof garden.



In the great Rome Cavalieri tradition of recherché style and refinement, the decor of the new suite is an exclusive blend of antique and modern: boiseries; important works of art, such as a

Majestic Barrière Cannes

Hotel Majestic Barriere
experience encompassing luxury, elegance, charm and style. Overlooking the mythical Croisette, the palace, recently renovated and refurbished,
    welcomes you in a luxurious universe combining tradition, modernity and refinement. From its Fouquet’s brasserie to its private beach, passing through its 2,000 square meters of luxurious French

Palacio Deluxe Suite



Palacio Estoril












































Built in 1930 and completely renovated in 2007, the
Palácio Estoril
, is one of the most prestigious hotels in Portugal. Situated in the heart of
Estoril
- a
    cosmopolitan resort - just
25 Km away from Lisbon
Airport, the Palácio is a five-star deluxe hotel, enjoying beautifully landscaped gardens and sea views.



Elegantly decorated the
Palácio Deluxe Suite
measures 65 square metres. Large bedroom with exquisitely embroidered king size bed. Furnished with elegant luxury fabrics. Large
    sitting room with sofa, armchairs and coffee table. Marble bathroom with separate bath and shower. Fine views over the gardens of
Estoril
.

The Blaton Suite



Rocco Forte Hotel Amigo Brussels





































The
most unique and elegant suite
, providing the highest level of comfort in the heart of
Brussels
, the Royal Suite is named after Armand Blaton, the founder of
    the
Hotel Amigo
. The Blaton Suite measures 180 sq m/ 2000 sq ft and is situated on the top floor of the hotel. It has an extensive outside terrace (60 square metres) with views
    over the rooftops of Brussels and the picturesque town hall. It is extremely spacious with a large hall, one bedroom which features a king bed with en suite bathroom, a large sitting room, a
    dining room hung with many paintings from the Amigos original collection, a private kitchen and an open fireplace.

Imperiale Suite



Le Bristol Paris





































Spanning 320 m², the Imperial Suite is now the hotel's largest suite. Its decor has been overseen by Mrs Maja Oetker, working with the famous architect Pierre-Yves Rochon.



The dining room, with its impressive Versailles parquet, overlooking the hotel’s magnificent French-style garden can host up to twelve guests. The adjacent lounge, the furniture for which was
    obtained from the famous French establishment Taillardat, is decorated with high quality fabrics featuring gentle shades from the house of Canovas, creating a friendly atmosphere brimming with
    pure Parisian style. On the walls, among other things, guests can admire a magnificent painting dating from the 18th century by the French painter Jean-Baptiste Pater, portraying a country
    festival.

nterContinental Amstel Amsterdam has been celebrated as the most beautiful and luxurious hotel in the Netherlands

The Royal Suite

    private bar.

Sumahan on The Water






European Best Destinations
































“
Sumahan on the water
” a restored nineteenth century Ottoman distillery is a rare example of thoughtful, historical transformation in Istanbul. “Suma” is the word for
    unadulterated spirit. The hotel was the industrial factory that distilled the high proof ingredient for “Raki” the famous Ottoman tipple.



Spacious large suite approximately, 70 square meters, with four large windows over looking the Bosphorus. The fire place in the middle of the room divides the room in to two parts. Living room
    and bedroom with large marble bathroom. It also has a connection with a deluxe room.
