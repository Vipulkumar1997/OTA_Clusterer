1. Val Thorens



Val Thorens
is the best! The ski resort has been standing out for years thanks to high quality infrastructure and teams.
    Val Thorens offers everyone the unique, unforgettable, perfect experience. The station never stops improving and invests large budgets every year.



The famous ski resort has been rewarded best ski resort  in Europe for the 4th time and is also the best ski resort in the world. You have also ranked Val Thorens first in the "
Best ski resorts for guaranteed snow
" and among the "
Best family ski resorts
".



Congratulations to the ones who have been working for years to make Val Thorens the best ski resort in Europe. If you want to enjoy a great ski holiday now you know the name of  the 2018
    best ski resort. Hurry up and book a great winter holiday in the best ski resort in Europe!



Book your stay in val thorens

2. Alpe d'Huez



Alpe d'Huez
is one of the best ski resorts in the world ; it was elected "Best ski resort in Europe" in 2017. Alpe
    d'Huez offers  high quality infrastructure for young and old ; it was also ranked among the
Best family
    ski resorts in Europe
. The atmosphere  in Alpe d'Huez is unique. The ski resort  offers a great experience sprinkled with fun, relaxation, laughter, beautiful days and
    evenings with friends, family or lovers. Visit the page dedicated to
Alpe d'Huez
; you will be convinced to choose it!

Do not wait any longer and book your winter holiday in Montgenèvre ; it is one of the best ski resorts in Europe.

6. Saalbach Hinterglemm



Saalbach-Hinterglemm
is a dream ski resort. It is elected  among the best ski resorts in Europe each year
    ; it is perfect for sports lovers, beginners or expert skiers, and for families, both in summer and winter. Saalbach-Hinterglemm is one of the most famous resorts in Austria, it is also one of
    the ski resorts with one of the largest ski area with almost 200 km of slopes.



As each year, you have chosen Saalbach Hinterglemm among your best ski resorts in Europe. Book now and you will soon be amazed by its natural beauty.



Book your stay in Saalbach




















































































7. La Plagne


Every year
La Plagne
is ranked among the best ski resorts in Europe. This ski resort is exceptional and was elected the
    second best European ski resort in 2017 ; it has always been in the ranking of the best ski resorts in the world for the quality of its slopes, its infrastructure and activities for young and
    old, beginners or professional skiers and its excellent quality / price ratio.



Book your stay in La Plagne

8. Valloire


It was
Valloire
’s first participation to the competition this year and it has already been elected one of the best ski
    resorts in Europe! Valloire is a welcoming family ski resort. Enjoy a unique authentic mountain experience alongside great skiing on the 150km of slopes up to 2600m and activities for everyone.



Simplicity and sincerity are the main ingredient to provide a human experience of ski holidays in the streets, on the slopes or your favorite restaurant in Valloire. Valloire is a beautiful and
    welcoming family ski resort. Book your stay in Valloire today! It  is one of the best ski resorts in Europe!

9. Crans Montana


You ranked
Crans-Montana
as one of the best ski resorts in Europe. This very famous ski resort is a paradise for
    skiers from all over the world. Crans-Montana combines all the benefits of  urban lifestyle in a mountain setting with a wide selection of restaurants ranging from mountain huts to
    award-winning restaurants, numerous boutiques and shops, a wellness centre, entertainment and a vibrant nightlife.



Be sure that your expectations will be met in Crans-Montana !



Book your stay in Crans Montana

















































































10. Les Contamines-Montjoie



Les Contamines-Montjoie
is a 5-star ski resort for a winter holiday in Europe. Many quality experiences
    await you in this ski resort. Dominated by the summits of the Mont-Blanc, the picturesque village of Les Contamines-Montjoie is one of the best family ski resorts in the French Alps.



The variety of the ski area meets all winter sports lovers’ expectations. A rich and preserved heritage delights the most curious ones and a preserved nature the most contemplative ones while
    delicious culinary specialties meet the needs of the finest gourmets.



Les Contamines-Montjoie is all about families looking for great skiing and outdoor activities.



Book your stay in Contamines









































































11. Val Gardena



Val Gardena
is probably one of the most beautiful ski resorts in Europe. With breathtaking landscapes, this UNESCO
    classified resort is also ranked among the
most beautiful landscapes in Europe
. For its first
    participation in this competition you have decided to classify this ski resort among the best ski resorts in Europe.



Book your stay in Val Gardena

































































12. SkiWelt Wilder Kaiser – Brixental


Every year travellers from all over the world rate
SkiWelt Wilder Kaizer Brixental
as one of the
    top ski resorts in Europe. When it comes to reliable snow, the conditions are also unbeatable: SkiWelt Wilder Kaiser - Brixental is lucky with its natural snow.  Moreover, 225 km of slopes
    out of 284 can be completely covered with manmade snow thanks to its 1,700 snowmaking machines! Some slopes  require a good ski level, others are perfect for families. The views are
    stunning, the snow guaranteed!



Book your stay in Skiwelt


































































13. Kitzbühel



Kitzbühel
is a real legend ; it should be visited at least once in a lifetime !



Kitzbühel is ranked among the best ski resorts in Europe every year. It is one of travellers’ favourite ski resorts in Austria and one of the most popular one in the world. It deserves, like many
    other stations, the first place of this ranking for the quality of its slopes and its offers.



Today Kitzbühel remains a place of contrasts, where rustic Tyrolean guesthouses rub shoulders with international designer boutiques. Visitors can experience Kitzbühel in many ways, from the
    alleyways in the medieval old town and the breathtaking pistes to the town's elegant hotels and scenic hiking trails.



Book your winter holidays in Kitzbühel and you will be sure to make the most of it.



Book your stay in Kitzbühel

14. Saint Sorlin d'Arves


We are glad to find another family ski resort in this ranking of the best ski resorts in Europe.
Saint-Sorlin
    d'Arves
is a village in the French Alps ; the snow is good and the setting exceptional. Facing the Aiguilles d'Arves, at the foot of the Iron Cross and the Etendard Glacier,
    Saint-Sorlin d'Arves offers a different scenery.



Enjoy its traditional architecture, the shops and services of a ski resort and the highest slopes of the the Sybelles ski area.



Book your stay in St. Sorlin

Chamonix Mont-Blanc
is one of the pioneering ski resorts in Europe, one of the first one to offer its slopes to
    skiers from all over the world and certainly one of the most recognized and renowned ski resorts in Europe.



Book your stay in Chamonix
