Best Family Ski Resorts













Europe is  the number one destination for skiers looking for the
best ski resorts
in the world. Some resorts are ideal
    for small budgets: we have selected the
cheapest ski resorts in Europe
for you. Others are ideal
    for
very large budgets
.



Some are perfect for beginners, others for
snowboard
enthusiasts. Each resort has its own
    specificity and it is not easy for travellers to know which resorts are perfect for a family winter holiday.



We propose a large choice of ski resorts offering a very good infrastructure for both children and parents. Whether you are a beginner, intermediate or professional skier or you just want to rest
    in the wellness centre, do some shopping or enjoy a good restaurant, these resorts offer the best for young and old, at affordable prices for a winter holiday in Europe. Discover the best ski
    resorts for families and
book your stay at the best price guaranteed
.

Avoriaz



France




Avoriaz
is a privileged destination for families. The resort has already been awarded at the World Snow Awards for its
    constant concern to meet the needs of families. Avoriaz is home to "The Children's Village" which has been welcoming children since 1975 and teaching them skiing.



Avoriaz also offers free ski passes for children under 5 and quality facilities such as the "Kids Parkway" specially designed for children. This resort has been investing in well-being and
    hospitality for years and travellers appreciate it.
Book  your stay at the best
    price guaranteed in Avoriaz
and treat your family to an unforgettable winter holiday.



hotels in avoriaz






















Copyright Gaspar Janos










Alpe d'Huez



France




Alpe d'Huez
is a happy, dynamic family ski resort that will make you happy. The teams do an amazing job there: they offer
    a superb infrastructure, quality accommodations,  fun activities for young and old. They also plan exceptional events throughout the year. This ski resort is one of travellers’ favourites.
    Come enjoy 250 kilometres of slopes, 63 lifts and guaranteed snow (altitude : 3,300 m).
Book your stay in Alpe d'Huez
today for a successful
    family skiing holiday.





hotels in alpe d'huez

Val Thorens



France




Val Thorens
is probably the best ski resort in Europe. The Ski Resort "Les 3 Vallées" offers more than 600 km of slopes
    and more than 140 lifts and one of the largest ski areas in the world.
Val Thorens
offers a wide variety of activities
    for children and adults. Ski lessons, entertainment for children, but also shopping, good quality restaurants, relaxation and leisure but also thrills for the more adventurous ones.




Val Thorens
has all the ingredients for an ideal ski resort: it is perfect for all levels, from beginners to advanced
    professionals, it also offers accommodation ranging from small, affordable apartments to superb hotels like the
Pashmina le Refuge
probably one of the best ski
    resort hotels in Europe. This hotel offers direct access to the slopes, a superb wellness centre and a very good restaurant. This challenge was overcome by  a native from Val Thorens.



Book your stay today at the
best price guaranteed
. Val Thorens is  one of
    the best ski resorts for families.

Switzerland



Going on a winter holiday to
Saas-Fee
ski resort means taking care of oneself and
    family. Teens will love the hundreds of kilometres of slopes, the youngest ones will enjoy dozens of indoor and outdoor activities : ski lessons for all ages, theme nights…




Saas-Fee
is labeled "Family Destinations" ; that label
    rewards all the professionals for the quality of their accommodation.
Book your stay at the best price now
in one of the best
    family ski resorts .

Mayrhofen-Ahorn



Austria



Children, teenagers and parents love this world famous Austrian ski resort for the quality of its 130 km of slopes.
Mayrhofen-Ahorn
is certainly the place where to
    book your stay if you want to keep your children busy with quality activities such as an Eagle’s stage with birds of prey shows, adventurous escapades along Ahorn Sinnespfad Trail... Come
    discover the Fun-Zone Mayrhofen, a recreation park for young and old with indoor and outdoor pools.




Book your stay at the best price guaranteed in
    Mayrhofen-Ahorn
for a dream holiday in one of the best family ski resorts in Europe.

Montgenèvre



France




Montgenèvre
is a historic and one of the oldest ski resorts in France; it is located just a few kilometres from the
    Italian border. More than 400 kilometres of slopes are awaiting you in one of all  families’ favourite ski resorts.  This enthusiasm for
Montgenèvre
is probably due to the many activities for children, the quality of its ski school but also the very affordable prices.



Book your stay today at the
best price guaranteed in Montgenèvre
, one of
    the best family ski resorts.

cheapest ski resorts

Serre-Chevalier-Briançon

    resort. It offers a personalized welcome for families but also attractive prices for children, many activities and ski lessons for the little ones.



Serre-Chevalier-Briançon therefore combines two important aspects for families: affordable prices and quality activities for children.

    but also for their parents and even seniors. This ski resort enjoys a very good reputation for the quality of its slopes and infrastructures. You can have great intergenerational holidays there!

Come enjoy moments of family fun on the 110 kilometres of slopes in
Obergurgl-Hochgurgl
