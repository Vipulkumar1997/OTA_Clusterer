Porto



Portugal




Porto
is definitely one of the
most beautiful welcoming and tolerant cities in
    Europe
. It enjoys a gentle climate throughout the year, although mist sometimes gets as far as the Dom Luis I bridge (created by one of Gustave Eiffel's students). The city looks a bit
    like
San Francisco
. In fact, many startups as well as large companies come to settle there. Porto is also known for its gastronomy and wine
    but if nostalgia grips you, you will find
one of the finest Mac Donald's
in the former Imperial Café. The Portuguese are smart and
    friendly and you will find English spoken in many places. Ready to go?
Book your hotel in Porto
at the best price and discover our selection of the
best activities in
    Porto
.

Vienna



Austria




Open-minded, welcoming, charming
, breathtaking, words fail to describe
Vienna
, one of the most beautiful
    European cities. Pack your bags and discover an incredible city which combines past and modernity, city and green spaces, culture and gastronomy. Vienna is a city visited each year by millions of
    travelers who especially appreciate its
tolerance and quality of life
.



Bring your family and discover the
Prater, a sublime amusement park
in the heart of the city, but also
the Schönbrunn
    Palace
. Come and forget the dreariness of the past few months in the capital of Austria and
get a new life in Vienna
.

Amsterdam



The Netherlands



There is not a town in Europe more open-minded and welcoming.
Amsterdam
is a beautiful cosmopolitan city. Both capital of
    the Netherlands and one of the most visited cities in Europe, Amsterdam also has the soul of a city on a human scale.
Move to Europe with your bike and give yourself a new life in
    Amsterdam
. Work is easy to find and in no time at all you can develop your own business and live a great new adventure.

Croatia



Located in Croatia,
one of the most beautiful countries in Europe

Zagreb

and in winter you can even ski in the hills of Zagreb. At Christmas, the city becomes magical and delights young and old. If you want a change of life,
    discover the capital of Croatia and leave your luggage in one of the most beautiful cities in Europe.

Portugal



Is election fatigue dragging you down? You probably could do with some peace and quiet, some fresh air and change? Come to
Madeira

Belgium



The Capital of Europe has many assets to help clear your mind.
Brussels
is truly welcoming and cosmopolitan with more than
    130 nationalities living side by side. You could say that parts of it with its many institutions are a bit of Washington in Europe, but Brussels is livelier with its many markets, typical
    districts, old traditional cafés and flea markets. Real estate remains more affordable than London and Paris and you benefit from connections with the whole world. You will soon feel at home in
    Brussels, capital of Europe and Belgium because everyone or almost everyone speaks and understands English.

France

. Culturally speaking, It is most certainly one of the most interesting in France and Europe even. Perfect for shopping but also rich in

a vibrant and young metropolis
renowned for its quality of life. Discover the city where Jules Vernes
    was born and forget all your problems in
Nantes
, the visionary and creative city.
