Most beautiful ski resorts in Europe













They are just as you imagined them when you were a child : authentic mountain villages, traditional chalets, firewood  cooking in an indescribable atmosphere... These real mountain villages
    have a soul. Now that you have selected your most beautiful villages in European ski resorts, we invite you to choose your favourite one and from now on
book your stay at the best price guaranteed
for an unforgettable holiday in one of the most beautiful ski resorts in Europe.

Kitzbühel



Austria



If you are familiar with this ski resort you probably know that the symbol of
Kitzbühel
is a pretty little mountain goat.
    A symbol that you will find everywhere on the spot. This symbol is not a coincidence since Kitzbuhel means "The mountain goat hill".



Kitzbühel is one of the most beautiful ski resorts in Europe but also one of the
best ski resorts
with most important sporting events such as the Alpine Ski World Cup. Come and enjoy the long track (158 kilometres) and treat yourself to a
stay at the best price guaranteed
in one of the
    most beautiful ski villages in Europe.

Garmisch-Partenkirchen



Germany



In addition to being a village of exceptional beauty, Garmisch Partenkirchen is one of the best ski resorts in Germany. You can stroll through the historic heart of this mountain village, 
    go shopping or discover its many tracks including the famous Olympic descent of Kandhar.



This family-friendly ski resort is perfect for professional and amateur skiers. Moreover it offers great rates.
Book your stay at the best price now
in one of
    the most beautiful mountain villages and enjoy great holidays!

Saalbach Hinterglemm



Austria



Saalbach-Hinterglemm is a dream ski resort. Elected each year among the best ski resorts in Europe, this ski resort is perfect, both in summer and winter for sports lovers, families, beginners or
    expert skiers. Saalbach-Hinterglemm is one of the most famous resorts in Austria, it is also one of the ski resorts with one of the largest ski area with almost 200 km of slopes.
Book your stay in Saalbach-Hinterglemm
for an
    unforgettable holiday in one of the most beautiful ski resorts in Europe.

St Anton am Arlberg



Austria



You will fall in love with this charming ski resort ! Alpine skiing was born in this authentic village.

St Anton has since then become a reference for ski enthusiasts from all over the world and a cosmopolitan village.



After or before a long day skiing, enjoy the pedestrian zone.
Book your hotel at the best price guaranteed in St Anton am
    Arlberg
for a 5 star holiday in Austria.

Murren



Switzerland




Murren
is an authentic mountain village located in
    Switzerland. This small village of 450 inhabitants offers a breathtaking panorama of the three most famous summits of the region: Eiger-Mönch-Jungfrau.



There are only 2000 beds available in this beautiful mountain village so hurry up and
book your stay now in one of the most beautiful ski resorts in
    Europe
. We particularly recommend the "
Eiger Swiss Quality
" hotel; it has been managed by the same
    family since 1886,  combining both modernity and tradition with its superb modern spa. Discover all the accommodation available in Murren and treat yourself to a dream holiday in one of the
    most beautiful ski villages in Europe.

Zermatt



Austria




Zermatt
is a world famous ski resort known for
    the quality and extent of the ski area but also the beauty of its historic heart. It is ideal for relaxing after a day of skiing. The city has kept an authentic aspect thanks to drastic urban
    planning rules and a pedestrian historic centre. It is nevertheless within easy reach thanks to shuttles and public transports.



We invite to to
book your stay at the best price guaranteed
in
    one of the most beautiful ski resorts in Europe.

Ellmau



Austria



A frail Christmas tree, in the middle of a snowy field can be so cute! This is not a picture postcard it is real real! The whole village of
Ellmau
is authentic. It is located in
    Austria, at the foot of
SkiWelt Wilder Kaiser ski resort
, one of the best ski resorts in Europe.



Ellmau is a charming Tyrolean village. Discover one of the most beautiful mountain villages by booking

Ischgl



Austria




Ischgl
is one of the
best ski resorts
in Europe ;
    it attracts skiers from all over the world every year. This ski resort, in partnership with Samnaun, offers one of the most beautiful ski areas in Austria called "Silvretta Arena Ischgl-Samnaun".
    If you're lucky you may meet Rihanna or Katy Perry. The city hosts concerts of the famous stars and many prestigious events.



Hundreds of kilometres of tracks, superb hotels, excellent infrastructure, Ischgl is a dream ski resort.
Book your winter holiday today at the best price
    guaranteed
in one of the most beautiful ski resorts in Europe.

Saint Jean d'Arves



France




Saint Jean d'Arves
is the perfect ski
    resort if you are looking for authenticity, friendliness and access to one of the largest ski areas: Les Sybelles.



You will be seduced by the charm of this mountain village and its privileged access to more than 135 tracks for all levels, from beginners to professionals.
Saint Jean d'Arves
is also a family
    friendly ski resort with many activities for the little ones, playgrounds and day care for children.



Treat yourself to a paragliding baptism or a dog sled ride and
book your stay at the best price guaranteed in Saint
    Jean d'Arves
, one of the best ski resorts in Europe

Megève



France




Megève
is certainly one of the ten most
    famous ski resorts in the world. Its reputation is first  due to its story. A century ago, the Rothschild family decided to make this small mountain village their own holiday resort. Since
    then the greatest personalities, crowned heads, movie stars and singers use to spend their winter holidays in the chic and friendly
Megève
ski resort.



We recommend  the "
Alpaga
" hotel for gourmets, Enjoy their Michelin-starred restaurant,
    relax while having a Scandinavian bath .You can even book your own chalet and your chef at home! Check our offer of hotels, B & B, apartments and guesthouses and book, at the
best price guaranteed
, your stay today in
    Megève, one of the most beautiful ski resorts in Europe.

Wengen



Switzerland




Wengen
is a beautiful Swiss mountain
    village. Wengen is also the starting point for the discovery of the entire region of Jungfrau, rich in ski slopes. You can also enjoy beautiful walks in the village of Wengen and its surroundings
    or sled rides with your family.



It’s time to
book your stay at the best price guaranteed in
    Wengen
. Spas, lodges, beautiful hotels or cozy apartments and chalets await you for an exotic holiday in one of the most beautiful mountain villages.

Tarvisio



Italy



This international winter sports resort is located in Northern Italy, on the border of Austria and Italy; it is probably one of the most beautiful mountain villages. Influenced by its Austrian
    and Slovenian border neighbours,
Tarvisio
is different because of this
    triple identity.



You will be charmed by the authenticity of this ski resort which was one of the most famous  favourite ski resorts in the 30s.
Book your stay at the best price guaranteed in
    Tarvisio
, one of the most beautiful ski resorts in Europe nowadays
