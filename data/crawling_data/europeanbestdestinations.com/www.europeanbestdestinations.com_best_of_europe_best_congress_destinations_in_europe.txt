Best Congress and Conference Destinations in Europe













A
Congress
is a milestone in the history of your company, in the positioning of your brand. One should not take the choice of its location lightly because
the choice of
    the destination may depend on the success of your event
.



We have selected for you the
best European destinations for your congress
, your event. These destinations are
easy to reach
. They are
well
    connected
by airlines, highways and trains. Their
hotels
are luxurious at affordable prices and their conference
    rooms are state-of-the-art.

Brussels



Belgium




Brussels
is the
first destination for business tourism in Europe
. No wonder that this city has the best
    Congress halls such as the
SQUARE
as well as the
best Congress hotels
.



Located in the
center of Europe
, this city is within easy reach and has many facilities to host your event. For its
central location
and the quality of its
    services we recommend the
Hotel Le Plaza
which has large conference rooms and the prestige of
    a high-end hotel. We also recommend "
The Hotel
" which is more modern, has
modular
    conference rooms
and a large capacity of reception on the chic Avenue de la Toison d’Or.
Barack Obama

himself stayed there
! The security service is
    obviously in a position to fulfill your commitments and ambitions.



Discover all the
hotels at the best prices in Brussels
as well as
    the
best
    activities
to suggest to your participants like a
beer tasting
or a guided tour of
    the city.
