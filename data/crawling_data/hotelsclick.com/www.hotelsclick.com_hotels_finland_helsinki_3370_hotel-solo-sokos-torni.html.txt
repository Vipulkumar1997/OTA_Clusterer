Towering the Helsinki centre, the Solo Sokos Hotel Torni offers high-class accommodation and services since 1931, maintaining unaltered its Art Deco style. From the top of the building, guests can enjoy one of the best views of Helsinki, drinking a cocktail at the famous Ateljee Bar. The common spaces of the hotel are wide and bright, the pastel colours and the Art Deco decorations keep alive the classy and elegant atmosphere of the '30s. The hotel is served by well-restored, perfectly working, original lifts. Rooms all have high ceiling and en-suite bathroom with shower. Standard equipment includes cable-channels flat TV, well furnished mini bar, safe box, electric kettle with nice selection of tea, coffee and cocoa, fluffy toiletries, high quality cosmetics by Thierry Mugler. The hotel is compliant with the Allergy Label from the Allergy and Asthma Federation, indoor air, cleaning methods, interiors decoration and the furnishing fabrics are hypoallergenic, as well as mattress and pillows that are available in three different sizes. The queen rooms are provided with two complimentary soft drinks, a coffee maker and have a stunning view of the Helsinki landscape. The wider suites are provided with living area, bathtub and panoramic view or a balcony overlooking Yrjönkatu Street. The rich breakfast presents hot and cold dishes; scrambled eggs, meat and fish based specialities, vegetables, fresh bread, sweets, pies and fresh fruits. The Torni Restaurant is one of the most famous in Helsinki; its menu introduces high quality dishes, sometime mixing the local traditional recipes with the most exquisite European and International ones. The hotel is famous for the impeccable organization that deploys in support of successfully conferences, meetings and events. In the hotel, besides the Ateljee Bar there are the American Bar, a lounge bar specialized on sophisticated cocktails and the Irish Pub O'Malley's, that offers good music and a rich selection of beers. Located in the real centre of Helsinki, the Solo Sokos Hotel Torni is just a short walk from the train station and from many of the most famous attractions. In the nearby there are some very good restaurants, nice shops and shopping malls like Forum Shopping Centre, the Kamppi Centre and the Stockmann department Store. Within 5 minutes by walk there is the Explanadi Park, the old church “Vanha Kirkko” and the Old Market Hall that overlooks the Eteläsatama, the charming south harbour of Helsinki.

Hotel Details


General
Solo Sokos Hotel Torni is the perfect place to stay in Helsinki, whether it's a pleasure trip or business. Located in the heart of the city offers to its guests various services, such as parking (for a fee), breakfast served from 6:30 to 11:00 and the restaurant Torni which offers a seasonal menu of traditional Finnish dishes with an international touch and contemporary.

Rooms
Solo Sokos Hotel Torni has many rooms, from standard to suites, in order to meet the needs of every customer. Each room is tastefully furnished and equipped with modern facilities such as: private bathroom, hairdryer, bath products, TV, minibar, telephone and internet connection.

Location
This beautiful hotel in Helsinki dates back to the 20's and is located 5 minutes' walk from the Esplanadi shopping streets and the main railway station. The hotel is located 21 km from Helsinki-Vantaa Airport and a 7-minute walk from Kamppi Metro Station.
