Hotel Opera is a four-star hotel located in the centre of Gothenburg, on the square Drottningtorget, near the railway station and well-known Nordstan shopping centre. The distance to the airport is 10 km (6.2 mi). It takes two minutes' walk to get to the tram stop. The city centre has lots of shopping malls, bars and restaurants, where tourists can have a wonderful time. There are 198 modern rooms which were fully renovated in 2008. The facilities of each room are: air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, safe box, balcony. Bathrooms are equipped with hair dryer, hot tube, shower and have a heated floor. Facilities are available for people with reduced mobility. Pets are not allowed. Guests would appreciate hotel’s fitness centre and sauna. Breakfasts are served in a buffet style. Guests can enjoy traditional Swedish foods at the hotels’ restaurant. At lobby bar sandwiches, salads and snacks are served. Indoor swimming pool and spa are available at an additional charge. Hotel Opera in Gothenburg has a great and comfortable conference hall for business meetings.

Hotel Details


General
Opera is the ideal place to spend a pleasant stay in the heart of Gothenburg, in the central square of Drottningtorget. The facilities available to guests include: 24 hour reception, air conditioning, luggage storage, bar, sauna, gym, jacuzzi, meeting rooms, internet point, restaurant.

Rooms
The hotel provides for its guests 198 rooms including Standard, Superior and Suites furnished in elegant style. The services offered include: hair dryer, room service, safe, desk, TV, wifi. The property is also equipped for disabled people.

Location
The hotel is located in the central square of Drottningtorget and is 28 km from the Gothenburg-Landvetter airport and 300 meters from the Centralstation. During their stay, guests can easily visit: the Museum of World Cultures, Gothenburg cathedral, bridge Alvsborg, the Avenyen and the numerous canals of Gothenburg.
