Conveniently located in the centre of Mestre, the Hotel Centrale is a modern 3-star hotel that offers good value for the money. The hotel is within 15 minutes by bus from the centre of Venice, the bus stop is in front of it. This hotel is very appreciated by business travellers, by who travels on budget and by young people. The Hotel Centrale is easy to reach from the Venice Marco Polo Airport and from the Treviso Airport San Giuseppe; Mestre Train Station is just 10 minutes by walk or 3 minutes by tram. The hotel is contemporary styled, offers light en-suite non-smoking rooms equipped with Wi-Fi Internet connection, air conditioning, flat TV, minibar, safe box and hairdryer. Complimentary toiletries and other in-room amenities are provided. The rich complimentary buffet breakfast is offered both in Italian and in English style. The hotel offers meeting facilities and support for events, parking facilities are available next to the hotel. In the hotel’s nearby there are some restaurants and bars, the main square of Mestre, Piazza Ferretto, is a short walk away. The Hotel Centrale is fully accessible, elevators serve floors, family rooms are available on request, pets are welcome.

Hotel Details


General
The Hotel also has a bar service, an Internet point and a Wi-Fi connection for those who wish to connect to the Internet from their own laptop. Guests may also request maps and tourist information from our personnel who are well prepared and who will be more than willing to provide useful advice and information! Those arriving by car may avail of a nearby car-park which reserves special discounts for residents of our Hotel.

Lobby
The lobby is medium in size and is connected to the breakfast room and the bar.
Exterior
Hotel Centrale is located in Mestre, just a 2 minute walk from the old town and from the central square, it is 8 km from Venice. Venice can also be easily reached via the public bus service (n° 7 and “Venezia”), the buses stop opposite the hotel every 10 minutes and on a 24-hour basis. The journey lasts 15 minutes.
Rooms
Hotel Centrale offers its guests 100 rooms of various types, distributed over 5 floors. The finely furnished rooms are all fitted with every comfort, including: air conditioning, heating, private bathroom with bath or shower, satellite TV, safe, hairdryer and telephone.
Restaurant
There is a cosy breakfast room on the ground floor where a buffet breakfast is served.

Location
In the heart of Mestre's old town, just a stone's throw from Venice, Hotel Centrale is proposed as a modern structure, it is comfortable and practical and capable of meeting the needs of the numerous tourists headed for Venice as well as an incentive type clientele. Hotel Centrale, which has 100 rooms distributed over 5 floors, is a young, modern structure, it has all the necessary facilities, it is located in Mestre's old town, from which you can easily reach Venice thanks to public buses which pass by every 10 minutes and stop right opposite the Hotel. The well presented, cosmopolitan setting, the family-managed services and the impeccable courtesy that singles out our group, will contribute to making your stay in Venice an unforgettable one!
