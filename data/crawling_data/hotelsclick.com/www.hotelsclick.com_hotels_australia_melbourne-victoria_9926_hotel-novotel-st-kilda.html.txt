Located in St Kilda, Novotel Melbourne St Kilda is ideal for both business travellers and tourists. This 4-star hotel overlooking the beach is situated just 6 kms from Melbourne city and 28 km from Melbourne Airport. The bright rooms of Novotel Melbourne St Kilda are equipped with satellite TV and air conditioning. Take a dip in the heated outdoor swimming pool and enjoy the fitness centre. At the Grill 3182 Restaurant & Bar you can taste delicious dishes prepared with fresh ingredients. Booking at the Novotel Melbourne St Kilda you can take advantage of services such as on-site parking, laundry service, Wi-Fi. Many shops, clubs and restaurants are located in the surrounding of the hotel. Novotel Melbourne St Kilda features rooms available for meetings and events.

Hotel Details


General
A relaxed property on the beach yet 15 minutes from the city centre (a pleasant tram ride). Ideal for the leisure traveller, with plenty of great eating nearby and shopping. This hotel has a really nice feel about it.

Lobby
Medium sized with light colourings and a relaxed casual atmosphere.
Exterior
6 floor cream coloured, attractive modern building.
Rooms
Large rooms, renovated early 2000, with fabric inspired by the marine life and beach colours. Very pleasant resort style rooms with bright outlook. Good bathrooms.
Restaurant
The Bayside Brasserie is below the lobby level facing the beach, with an outdoor area. Very light and airy, also carrying through nautical theme.

Location
7.5 km from Melbourne. Overlooks Port Phillip Bay and St KIlda Beach, 15 minutes drive from the centre of Melbourne. One block from Acland Street which is famous for its restaurants and coffee shops. 100 metres from St Kilda pier and Luna Park.
