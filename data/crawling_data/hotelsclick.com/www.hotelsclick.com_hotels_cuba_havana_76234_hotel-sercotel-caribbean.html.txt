Sercotel Caribbean  is located in Havana, more precisely on the Paseo El Prado, one of the most important avenues of the city, connecting the heart of Havana with the sea. Thanks to its favorable location, this 2-star hotel is an excellent point of departure for visiting the main attractions of the historic center of Havana, such as the National Art Museum, the Music Museum and the Grand Theatre. At a short distance you can also find the famous Central Park and the Malecon, the famous promenade. All the comfortable rooms of the Sercotel Caribbean are simple, clean and equipped with air conditioning and a private bathroom. At the on-site restaurant, guests can savor the best dishes of local and international cuisine, while at the bar they can enjoy special cocktails of various kinds. The José Martí International Airport is about 15 kilometers from Sercotel Caribbean.
