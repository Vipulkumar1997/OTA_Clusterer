Nomad Paris Roissy CDG hotel in France is the best hotel that is situated at about 5 minutes’ walk from Charles De Gaulle Airport. It has a central fitness centre that all guests can access. The rooms are decorated in eco-friendly designs with an air conditioner for the maximum comfort. There are a bar lounge and a self-service area where visitors can serve themselves whatever they might like. Also, guest of Nomad Paris Roissy CDG has access to a free shuttle from and to the airport. The staffs here have knowledge of various major languages in the world such as Spanish, English German and French among others. Therefore, guests do not have to worry about language barrier. There are family rooms for those who might like to travel here with their entire family. Safe, private parking is also available for a guest with private vehicles. In addition, they have special facilities for the physically challenged guest.

Hotel Details


General
The hotel has facilities which are large and spacious modern lobby, airport shuttle, bar, gym, wireless Internet access and business area. Inside there is also a terminal area, for do the check-in.

Rooms
It has 205 environmentally friendly modern rooms, equipped with wireless Internet access, projector and air conditioning. There are also light control, organic toiletries, a hairdryer and a shower with lateral jets.

Location
Nomad Paris Roissy CDG is located adjacent to the airport Charles de Gaulle.
