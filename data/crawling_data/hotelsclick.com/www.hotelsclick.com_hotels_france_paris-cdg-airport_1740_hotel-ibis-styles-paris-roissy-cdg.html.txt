Visitors travelling to Paris by plane will find the Hotel ibis Styles Paris Roissy CDG a convenient option. Located very close to Charles de Gaulle Airport, the hotel provides guests with a free shuttle service. From the airport, a rapid train line runs into central Paris, where visitors can enjoy all that the magical City of Lights has to offer. Disneyland Paris is also readily accessible. Plane spotters will enjoy the hotel rooms' views over the Charles de Gaulle Airport runways, while gamers can indulge in Xbox Kinect games. All rooms are fitted with bathtubs and showers, air conditioning and 110/120 V AC, 220/240 V AC electrical outlets. Hotel ibis Styles Paris Roissy CDG offers its guests complimentary breakfast, free unlimited Wi-Fi, restaurant, fitness centre and bar. There are 26 family rooms and five rooms specially adapted for guests with disabilities. Parking is available for those travelling by car.

Hotel Details


General
It is a 3-star hotel that has WIFI access, breakfast room, bar, restaurant, gym, private parking (for a fee) and shuttle service to the airport.

Rooms
The 309 rooms are modern and renovated. They are equipped with comfortable bed, LCD TV, private bathroom, air conditioning, telephone, radio, wireless Internet access (surcharge), desk and alarm clock.

Location
Ibis Styles Paris Roissy CDG is 3 km from the international airport Charles De Gaulle. Nearby there is also the Villepinte Exhibition Park.
