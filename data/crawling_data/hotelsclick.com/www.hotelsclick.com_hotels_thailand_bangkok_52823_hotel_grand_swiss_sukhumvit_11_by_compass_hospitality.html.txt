The Hotel Grand Swiss Sukhumvit 11 By Compass Hospitality is located in the Watthana District, the heart of Bangkok, where you can find lots of shops, bars and restaurants. Bangkok’s Sky Train network is only several metres from the hotel. The hotel has 144 rooms of five types suitable for any tourist. In each room there is a satellite TV, air conditioning, hair dryer, safe, free Wi-Fi, direct dial phone. Pets are not allowed. The parking is available for free. The main restaurant of the hotel serves delicious Swiss and Thai food and you can taste the traditional pad Thai. Guests can enjoy a sauna and different kinds of massage. The outdoor pool is found on the hotel’s roof where you can enjoy amazing city views while swimming. The reception is available 24 hours a day. The Hotel Grand Swiss Sukhumvit 11 By Compass Hospitality is a perfect place for families with kids because it offers babysitting services. Guests can benefit from the use of shuttle bus to or from the airport. Fitness centre is available for those who want to keep their shape.

Hotel Details


General
Grand Swiss Sukhumvit 11 by Compass Hospitality offers excellent services perfect for both business and leisure travelers. It boasts 144 luxuriously designed guestrooms, a comfortable lounge with an amazing view, a well equipped meeting room and a cosy bar with a wide selection of drinks and snacks. The hotel provides leisure facilities such as outdoor swimming pool and fitness center. Grand Swiss Sukhumvit 11 offers room service, concierge service, babysitting service, laundry service and airport transfer.

Rooms
The rooms at the hotel are elegant, modern and spacious. Each room is equipped with flatscreen TV with satellite channels, working desk, private bathroom with shower, complimentary toiletries, hairdryer, direct dial telephone and electronic safety deposit box. Wi-Fi internet connection is available in all rooms.

Location
The hotel is located just at the beginning of Sukhumvit Soi 11, just a few minutes away from Terminal 21 shopping center. It is just 2 minutes walk away from the public transport which allows a great access to all the main attractions of the city. Shops, restaurants and bars are nearby.
