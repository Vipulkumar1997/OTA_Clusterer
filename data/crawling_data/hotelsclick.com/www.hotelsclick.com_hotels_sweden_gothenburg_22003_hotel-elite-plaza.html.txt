Placed in the heart of Gothenburg and only 500 metres from Nordstan Shopping Mall, the renowned Elite Plaza Hotel is the ideal solution for a pleasant trip with the whole family or couples and for business travellers.  By making a reservation at this 5-star hotel in Gothenburg, you can stay in spacious and well-furnished bedrooms equipped with cable TV, free WiFi, safe, minibar and private bathroom with toiletries. The Elite Plaza Hotel offers a modern relaxation area with sauna and fitness facilities. On site there is also a renowned restaurant, the Swea Hof, where you can taste the best dishes of traditional cuisine, and two pubs. Every morning at the Hotel Elite Plaza a rich breakfast buffer with fresh and genuine products is served. The Elite Plaza Hotel is located at just a 8-minute drive from the Congress Centre, the Swedish Exhibition and Liseberg Amusement Park. The Landvetter Airport - Gothenborg is located at about 20 kilometres from the hotel.

Hotel Details


General
Elite Plaza is located in a sumptuous building dating from 1889, where everything has been carefully preserved by the venerable stucco ceilings to the mosaic floors. The hotel addition to being beautiful, is equipped with many modern amenities, such as: bar, restaurant, sauna, gym, laundry, baby sitting (on request and for an additional fee), parking (for a fee) and 8 fully equipped halls used for meetings and conferences.

Rooms
All rooms from Standard to Suite are tastefully decorated in a fusion of classic style and modern needs. Each room has a spacious environment to live in and many comforts, including satellite TV, wifi, safe, minibar and private bathroom.

Location
The hotel is located less than a kilometer from the city center and 800 meters from Gothenburg Central Railway Station. The hotel offers to its guests a transfer service to the airport ( on request and for a fee ), situated 26 km from the hotel.
