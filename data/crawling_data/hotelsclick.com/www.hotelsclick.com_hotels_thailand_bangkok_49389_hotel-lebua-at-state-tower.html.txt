Lebua At State Tower is a luxury five-star hotel located in Silom, the Bangkok’s financial district. It has spectacular views to the Chao Phraya River, the major river in Thailand. The distance from the hotel to the Suvarnabhumi Airport is 35 km. The hotel offers free transfer until the Saphan Taksin BTS Station. The hotel has 357 rooms. Pets are not allowed and parking is available for free. From the windows of the modern suites you can view the cityscape and the Chao Phraya River. The elegant suites are equipped with TV with DVD player, free Wi-Fi, large bathroom with a set of luxury amenities, mini kitchen and a lounge area. Guests can swim in the outdoor swimming pool resembling a lagoon, enjoy a massage session, relax in sauna and attend the fitness centre. Families with children can benefit from the babysitting service. Lebua At State Tower has a stunning rooftop bar called Sky and a rooftop restaurant serving Mediterranean foods. Every day a buffet-style breakfast is served at the Mozu café behind the swimming pool. In addition, the hotel houses the Breeze bar and Mezzaluna and Distil restaurants. The hotel has a luxury business centre for business meetings and conferences.

Hotel Details


General
Lebua at State Tower is a luxury hotel that features modern suites and extensive facilities. Guests can enjoy services such as: baby sitting, laundry, shuttle to the station, parking, gym, spa and pool. The property also has many fully-equipped rooms used for events, weddings, meetings and conferences.

Rooms
The hotel offers various types of suites, in order to meet the needs of every type of customer. Each room is furnished with extreme taste and equipped with the most modern comforts, such as: private bathroom, hairdryer, iron, desk, TV, DVD player, minibar, safe, desk, telephone and wifi.
Restaurant
Guests can relax and spend pleasant evenings in the many restaurants available inside the hotel. Each restaurant (Breeze, Cafe Mozu, Mezzaluna) offers high level fine dining. Guests can enjoy a breathtaking view from the terrace restaurant Sirocco. The property also features 3 lounge bar, the perfect places to end the day with a pleasant drink.

Location
The hotel is located 7 kilometers from the center of Bangkok and 32 kilometers from Suvarnabhumi International Airport; in the Silom area. The property overlooks the Chao Phraya River and is just over one kilometer from Saphan Taksin BTS Station.
