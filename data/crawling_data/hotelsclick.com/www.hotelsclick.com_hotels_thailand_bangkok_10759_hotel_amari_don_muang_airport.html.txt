The Hotel Amari Don Muang Airport is located in Bangkok, near the Don Muang International airport and is the perfect location for holding business meetings. Guests also can have easy access to the train station. The distance to Suvarnabhumi Airport is 50 km (31 miles). The hotel has 429 rooms. Each room has a TV, hairdryer, direct dial telephone, mini bar, radio and alarm clock. Families with children can take advantage of the babysitter service. In the Hotel Amari Don Muang Airport are working two excellent restaurants where you can enjoy Asian cuisine. An outdoor swimming pool is situated in the garden. Pets are not allowed. Parking is free for the guests. There are facilities for disabled people. Pool and sauna will help you to relax at the end of the day while the fitness centre will help you to maintain your shape. Golfers will appreciate the golf fields in the nearby area. Take advantage of the hotel’s meeting halls to organise your business conferences. A professional team will help you with the organisation of your business events.

Hotel Details


General
Amari Don Muang Airport Hotel offers to its guests a modern atmosphere and equipped with every comfort, guests will benefit from facilities such as swimming pool, gym, business center, bar, restaurant, lounge area, parking and shuttle service to the station and the airport. The property features 10 fully equipped halls used for meetings and events.

Rooms
The hotel has 347 rooms and suites, in order to meet the needs of every type of customer. All rooms are decorated in contemporary Thai style and equipped with many amenities, such as soundproof windows, wifi, satellite TV, telephone, desk, air conditioning, minibar, bathroom and hairdryer.

Location
This hotel is the perfect place to stay whether it's a pleasure trip or business. The hotel is located just 3 km from the international airport in Bangkok - Don Mueang and 35 km from the international airport Suvarnabhumi.
