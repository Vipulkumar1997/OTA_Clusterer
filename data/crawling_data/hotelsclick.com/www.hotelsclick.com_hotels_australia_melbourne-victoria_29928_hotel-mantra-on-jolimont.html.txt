Set in the central business district, near Melbourne city centre, the Mantra on Jolimont offers accommodation in studio rooms or apartments. Two kinds of apartments are available: with one or two bedrooms. A living room, fully equipped kitchen, bathroom and bedrooms compose them. The following features are provided: complimentary Wi-Fi Internet access, fridge, tea and coffee maker, microwave oven, toaster, hair dryer, ironing equipment and full laundry facilities. The more basic en-suite studios rooms are provided with kitchenette, fridge, sink, kettle, toaster, and microwave oven. Within the common facilities there are a fitness centre, a heated outdoor pool, a lounge bar and some dining areas. Limited parking area is available. The Mantra on Jolimont is surrounded by some of the most relevant sporting and entertainment facilities of the city: Melbourne Cricket Ground, Rod Laver Arena, and Olympic Park Stadium. There are also other interesting attractions like the Royal Botanic Gardens, and the Victorian Arts Centre.

Hotel Details


General
Mantra on Jolimont hotel is the perfect place to stay whether it be a business trip or leisure, thanks to its central location. The hotel offers to its guests all the services of a 4-star hotel with the privacy of a private accommodation (appartameto or studio). The hotel offers to its guests a paid parking, an outdoor pool, gym, spa, bar and restaurant.

Rooms
The hotel has 133 apartments and studios, which can accommodate groups of friends and large families. Each room is elegantly furnished and equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, minibar, kitchen or kitchenette, microwave, telephone, wifi, TV, iron, bathroom, hair dryer and laundry service.

Location
Mantra on Jolimont is located in a privileged position in the heart of Melbourne, close to the shopping area, restaurants and many tourist attractions of this cosmopolitan city. It is less than a two minute walk from Chinatown and its many excellent restaurants and a few blocks from Victorian Markets and Bourke Street Mall, a paradise for shopaholics.
