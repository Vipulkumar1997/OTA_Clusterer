The Scandic Hotel Grand Marina enjoys an prime seafront location in the Katajanokka area. This elegant 4-star hotel in Helsinki is ideal for both business travellers and tourists interested in exploring the city. The hotel is close to points of interest such as the Cathedral, the Presidential Palace and the city centre of Helsinki. Booking at the Scandic Grand Marina of Helsinki you can take advantage of a sauna and a gym. Take a seat at the Makasiini restaurant to taste the best dishes prepared by the chef. Certified as an environmentally friendly hotel, all rooms at Hotel Scandic Grand Marina have wooden floors and are equipped with TV, free Wi-Fi and tea and coffee facilities. In the surroundings you can find many bars and restaurants.

Hotel Details


General
Set by the harbour, the Scandic Grand Marina hotel offers a central Helsinki location. Keep fit in the gym, relax in the sauna, or do business in 18 meeting rooms for up to 1500 people and wireless internet access. Dine in Makasiini Restaurant and take in the harbour views from Key West Pub.

Lobby
The lobby is a very large and impressive area. Immediately to the left on entering, there is a seating area next to the gift shop. The reception is spacious, and at the other end of the lobby there is a long breakfast room.
Exterior
The hotel is an unusual building, having been converted from the old customs house. It has an attractive brick facade.
Rooms
The bedrooms throughout the hotel follow a modern design with nautical touches as used in the rest of the hotel. Although they vary in size, they are  all spacious and in very good condition.
Restaurant
The hotel has an assortment of restaurants found principally on the ground and lower ground floors, and cater for all culinary tastes.

Location
The hotel is situated in a waterfront location opposite the main harbour, by the main terminals for the two principal ferry companies serving Helsinki. The main shopping area is just a few minutes on foot.
