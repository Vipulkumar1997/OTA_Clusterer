Set amid the main Helsinki’s attractions, the Scandic Park Hotel is in the centre of the city, in between of the Seurasaarenselkä bay and the Töölö lake. This very quiet 4-star hotel overlooks the Töölö park, one of the largest, well-kept and popular green areas of Helsinki.  The hotel is well linked by public transport, the Train Central Station is just 10 minutes by walk. The bus stop to the International Airport Helsiki-Vaanta is just on the other side of the street. The transfer from the hotel to the airport takes 15 minutes by car and 40 minutes by bus. Free parking is available. The hotel is very appreciated by who travels for business or leisure. Families in vacation will enjoy the nice playground for children at the top floor. Pets are welcomed.  The en-suite hotel rooms are bright and spacious, furniture is modern styled, air conditioning, free WiFi internet connection, flat TV are provided, as well as mini bar, safe and ironing set. Bathroom has shower, hairdryer, bathrobes and toiletries. On the ground floor, Restaurant Famu’s menu offers a mix of Finnish and International dishes, the buffet breakfast is an impressive array of 101 energetic foods! The Scandic Park Hotel features excellent meeting and conference facilities, providing up-to-date multimedia equipment and high quality catering service. At the top floor there is also the health and beauty centre, with sauna and swimming pool. Within a short walk there is the Finnish National Opera, the famous Finlandia Hall designed by Alvar Aalto, the Helsinki Music Centre and the Olympic Stadium. To the Linnanmäki Amusement Park it takes about 20 minutes by tram.

Hotel Details


General
Scandic Park Hotel is the place to stay whether it be for a journey of pleasure or a business trip. The hotel offers to its guests many amenities, such as indoor pool, gym, sauna, parking (for a fee), 22 meeting and conference rooms , all fully equipped. Inside the hotel there is the Bar Famu, to enjoy a drink and relax and a restaurant that offers dishes of Finnish and international cuisine, all prepared with fresh and organic products.

Rooms
The hotel offers to its guests 523 roomsare available non-smoking rooms and rooms for people with reduced mobility. Each room is elegantly furnished and equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, desk, wifi, hairdryer, minibar, safe, TV and private bathroom.

Location
The hotel is situated opposite parkland and some famous city  landmarks including the Finlandia congress and the concert hall. The central station is approximately 10 minutes walk away. The international airport is just a 15 minute drive.
