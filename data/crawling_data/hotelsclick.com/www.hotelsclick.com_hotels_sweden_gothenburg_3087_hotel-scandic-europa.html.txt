The Hotel Scandic Europa is located in the centre of Gothenburg and this area is famous for the numerous shopping centres and cafes. The advantage of the hotel is that it is 250 metres (820 ft) from the railway station. There is a tram stop nearby where you can take a tram and go to visit the city’s most important sights, including Museum of World Culture, Liseberg amusement park, Universum science centre. The hotel has 456 rooms designed in a Scandinavian style with bright colours. Each room has a working desk and free Wi-Fi, and some rooms have a mini bar. There are rooms with a flat-screen TV. The hotel provides facilities for disabled. Pets are allowed on request. Parking is not included in the room price. Every day in the restaurant guests are served in a buffet style. HAK restaurant and bar are the places where guests can enjoy food and art in one place, because here often various art exhibitions and live concerts are organised. Recreational facilities such as indoor swimming pool and sauna are available to restore your physical and mental well-being. The fitness centre will help you to maintain the shape. For business meetings the Hotel Scandic Europa has 12 comfortable conference halls.

Hotel Details


General
Scandic Europa is the perfect place to stay whether it be a journey of pleasure or business. The hotel offers to its guests a variety of services, such as: cozy guest rooms, gym, pool, sauna, on-site parking, lobby area, bar and 12 fully equipped halls used for meetings and conferences. The property also has the Hak restaurant, guests can enjoy delicious lunches and dinners. Are also available 17 rooms for persons with reduced mobility.

Rooms
The property features standard room, superior room, and suites, in order to meet the needs of every type of customer. Each room is tastefully furnished and equipped with wifi, TV, bath, body care products, hairdryer and safe (not available in all rooms).

Location
The hotel is located in the city center just 2 minutes from the Gothenburg Railway Station. Opposite the hotel, there is a shuttle service to the airport, located 28 km from the property.
