The Aetas Lumpini is a classy 5 star hotel situated in Bangkok City. This magnificent hotel was established in 2011. For the short time that it has been in operation, it has received positive reviews from its customers due to its excellent services and ambience. This luxury hotel is also strategically located a few minutes away from Patpong and Lumpini station. This proximity makes it convenient for clients who have travelled to Bangkok for business reasons. It has a spectacular view of the city as well. The 28 storey hotel is spacious and elegant. Its rooms and lobbies have modern decoration and are in excellent condition. They are sure to make visitors to relax and have privacy. The food served is also of good quality and your will definitely not be disappointed. Patrons can also have a good time in the swimming pool where they can swim and lounge after a long busy day. They can also enjoy refreshing drink in the hotel’s wine bar. Indeed, Aetas Lumpini in Bangkok hotel offers customers with value for money with its exceptional services and luxurious feel.

Hotel Details


General
The hotel has a fitness center, a sauna and a pool bar. At guests disposal: a restaurant, the Hourglass, which serves international and Thai specialities. Free car park.

Rooms
The 203 rooms of the Aetas Lumpini are modern and spacious and equipped with flat-screen TVs and a seating area. Free wifi internet access.

Location
It is located at about 1 Km from Gaysorn Plaza with easy access to the Lumpini MRT subway.
