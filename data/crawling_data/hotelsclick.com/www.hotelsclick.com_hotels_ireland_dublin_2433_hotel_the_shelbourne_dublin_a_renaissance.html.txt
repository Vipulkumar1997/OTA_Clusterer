Overlooking the beautiful St. Stephen’s Green in the heart of Dublin, The Shelbourne hotel is an historic landmark 5 star rated hotel located just a 5-minute walk from Trinity College and the National Gallery. Other points of interests easily reachable from this hotel in Dublin City Centre are National Library, Grafton Street and National Museum. The hotel features a 19-metre (57 ft) swimming pool, an exclusive Health Club, a manicure and pedicure area, luxurious spa offering 6 treatment rooms, dance studio and a state-of-the-art fitness gym. There is an on-site elegant restaurant that serves steak and seafood cuisine. Guests wishing to book a stay at the elegant Shelbourne Hotel can choose from one of the 265 guest rooms. The guest rooms are spacious and feature free Wi-Fi connection, luxury bathrobes and slippers, digital flat-screen TV, and luxurious bedding to make your stay here an unforgettable one.

Hotel Details


General
Boasting a picture-perfect location overlooking St. Stephen's Green, just moments from some of Dublin's most famous historic landmarks, The Shelbourne Dublin, A Renaissance Hotel successfully marries traditional splendour and contemporary convenience. Timber floors and Georgian ceilings provide the setting for the lively No. 27 Bar & Lounge. The Lord Mayor's lounge provides the perfect setting to two of Dublin's oldest and finest traditions, morning coffee and afternoon tea. This is an exceptional hotel, created over three floors, The Spa is the largest in Dublin City Centre and the most exclusive. The Spa features six treatment rooms including a suite for two people to share their spa experience together, while an opulent relaxation room is the perfect place to unwind. An elegant manicure and pedicure area complete the pampering facilities. The Spa offers the ultimate in luxurious products and partners with three excellent and inspirational brands, Pevonia, Elemis and Pañpuri. In addition, this oasis of calm features a swimming pool, thermal facilities, a dance studio and state-of-the-art gymnasium. Wifi is now complimentary in all areas of the hotel.

Lobby
The lobby is medium and elegant, with the reception area set to one side. It is decorated with ornate plaster on the walls and ceiling.
Exterior
This is an historic hotel that has a traditional appearance. It is large and grand, and very distinguished with flags flying outside.
Rooms
All 265 rooms are furnished to the highest standard using traditional fabrics and furnishings. They are particularly eye catching, with bright decor and large windows, offering elegance and comfort combined with all the modern amenities required.
Restaurant
Fine cuisine with an international flair, can be found at the restaurant. It is extremely formal, with decor to match the interior of the hotel.

Location
The hotel is located in the centre of Dublin, alongside St Stephen's Green and minutes away from the Temple Bar area.
