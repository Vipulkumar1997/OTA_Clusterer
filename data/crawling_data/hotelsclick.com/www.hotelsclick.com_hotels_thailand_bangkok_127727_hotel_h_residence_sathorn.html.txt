The stylish H-Residence Sathorn hotel is located in the Sathorn district of Bangkok. Its design combines traditional Thai elements along with modern style. Sathorn is a perfect place for those who are interested in Thai culture, sights and temples. Built in 2011, this hotel has 113 rooms. Guests can take benefit from free transfer service to and from the airport. It takes 30 minutes to drive to beautiful Lumpini park and night clubs in Silom district. The distance from the hotel to Bangkok’s Suvarnabhumi Airport is 23 km (14 miles). The rooms have soft lighting and wood floor. Each room has air conditioning, 32" LCD television, mini bar, safe, free Wi-Fi, hair dryer. Parking is available for free. Smoking is prohibited on the H-Residence Sathorn area. Pets are not allowed. The hotel offers swimming pool, fitness centre, massage service and sauna. Breakfasts in the H-Residence Sathorn are served in a buffet style. In the HEW restaurant guests can taste delicious Asian and European foods. Business people will appreciate the business centre facilities.

Hotel Details


General
H-Residence Sathorn offers to its guests many amenities to make their stay as pleasant as possible. The hotel has 24/24 reception, business center, restaurant, rooftop swimming pool, bar, laundry service, luggage storage, cleaning and parking (on request - limited places).

Rooms
The hotel has 113 rooms located on 9 floors, the different types of rooms available for guests are: superior, deluxe and suites. Each room is furnished in modern style and equipped with many amenities, such as air conditioning, wifi, safe, satellite TV, telephone, bathroom and hairdryer.

Location
The hotel is located in the Sathorn business district, is a 30-minute drive from Lumpini Park and nightlife district of Silom and about 35 km from Suvarnabhumi Airport. The Hualampong railway station is located just 4 km from the hotel and the Surasak BTS station is just one kilometer.
