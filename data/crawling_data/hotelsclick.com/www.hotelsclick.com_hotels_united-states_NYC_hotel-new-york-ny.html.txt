Discover New York (Ny)
New York is the most populated of cities in the whole of the
USA
(closely followed by
Los Angeles
,
Chicago
and
Houston TX
). New york city is divided into 5 administrative regions, each of which is characterized by a specific identity, and represents almost a city in itself: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, Staten Island. Manhattan is the famous island situated between the Hudson River and the East River, where you will find many of the most famous tourist sights in New York such as Times Square, Central Park, some fashionable neighborhoods like Soho and Greenwich Village, as well as the famous Wall Street, the financial centre where you can find the famous stock exchange.
Brooklyn is the most populous area of the city, famous primarily for artists, musical trends, and Coney Island beaches.
Queens is home to two airports, John f. Kennedy and La Guardia Airport; This area is characterized by the more than 170 languages spoken, and this is one of the most diverse regions of the United States from an ethnic point of view. The Bronx is home to many interesting tourist attractions, including the Zoo and botanical gardens. This area also houses the professional baseball team much loved by New Yorkers, the NY  Yankees.
Finally, Staten Island is an island located near the port of New York, to the South and near the Strait known as the Kill Van Kull, which combines with Bayonne, New Jersey. New York city is in many ways a unique city, cosmopolitan flavour that offers many attractions and places to visit, like Ellis Island, where immigrants have arrived at the turn of the century.
New York is a city that offers so much from a point of view of culture and art, and is extremely interesting due to its lively nightlife. If you intend to visit, consider also that the city has a very diverse range of accommodations, from the family-run hotels to luxury 5 stars ones. You can then take advantage of any special offers via hotelsclick.com and book a hotel a midrange 3 or 4 star hotel, possibly in a central location, so you can visit and get to know in depth this extraordinary city.

museums
