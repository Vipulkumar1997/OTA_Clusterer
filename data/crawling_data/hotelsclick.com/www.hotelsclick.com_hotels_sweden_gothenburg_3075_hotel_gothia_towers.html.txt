Located in the heart of Gothenburg, the renowned Hotel Gothia Towers is located next to the Swedish Exhibition & Congress Centre and the Liseberg Amusement Park. Booking a room at this 4-star hotel in Gothenburg you will enjoy modern rooms equipped with free WiFi Internet access, tea and coffee making facilities and a private bathroom. The Gothia Towers offers its guests free use of an equipped fitness area. On site there is the Gothia's Heaven 23 Restaurant, where you can taste the best local cuisine while at Restaurant Incontro are served traditional Italian dishes. Do not miss the restaurant West Coast and Twentyfourseven for your quick meals. Trams, local buses and airport shuttles stop in front of the Gothia Towers.

Hotel Details


General
Gothia Towers Hotel is the perfect place to spend a pleasant stay a few steps from the famous Liseberg amusement park. The services available to guests include: several restaurants among which an Italian restaurant, a bistro with local produce, Twentyfourseven open 24 hours on 24 and the Heaven 23 famous for its sandwiches, louge bar, large breakfast room, spa and gym at the Upper House hotel, 24 hour room service, parking, rooms for meetings and events.

Rooms
The hotel, located just minutes from the convention center Svenska Mässan, provides for its guests modern rooms, most with views of the city. The hotel's amenities include: TV, wifi, telephone, minibar, hair dryer, iron and ironing board and safe.

Location
The hotel, located 2 km from Gothenburg Central Station, is 23 km from the Gothenburg-Landvetter Airport. During their stay, guests can easily visit: Museum of World Cultures, Gothenburg Cathedral, Älvsborg Bridge Avenyen and numerous channels of Gothenburg.
