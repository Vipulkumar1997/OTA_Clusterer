Zermatt – Holidays in Switzerland
Welcome to Zermatt, the picturesque mountain village at the foot of the world-famous
Matterhorn
. This holiday resort on the Italian border lures guests from around the world to the Swiss Alps every year. Zermatt is a shrine for winter sports enthusiasts and active holidaymakers boasting 38 peaks over 4,000 meters. And still, this
car-free resort
has retained its original Valais mountain village character and offers a combination of modern holiday location and traditional Alpine atmosphere.

Zermatt inside stories










Your Dream Swiss Holiday
Throughout the year, Zermatt and the surrounding Mattertal offer exciting leisure activities resulting in an unforgettable
holiday in the mountains
. More that 400 kilometers of
hiking trails
and thrilling mountain tours make the hearts of hikers and mountain climbers race. The peaks of
Gornergrat
and
Rothorn
can be reached by train or boat and offer first-time visitors and families with children suitable excursion destinations. In winter there are three ski regions with 360 km of pistes for
skiing and snowboarding
. In addition, the largest and
highest summer ski area
in Europe can be found here at
Matterhorn glacier paradise
.
With a varied offering of activities, Zermatt guests truly get their money’s worth. Besides winter sports and the surrounding idyllic nature, there are also inviting
restaurants
to discover with their regional cuisine. Trekking tours, open-air theatre and further
activities
ensure a rich variety of leisure activities during any stay.
