Plan Your Trip from Ontario to Newark
Airports in Ontario
The province of Ontario is primarily served by Toronto Pearson International Airport. Travelers can use Orbitz.com to search for low cost flights from Ontario through a variety of airlines flying out of the airport, including Air Canada, Air France, Air India, American Eagle, American Airlines, British Airways, Emirates, Lufthansa and United Airlines. The airport is situated approximately 14 miles northwest of the city of Toronto.
Transportation at Toronto Pearson International Airport
Toronto Pearson International Airport offers a variety of transportation options. Taxi service is available in the Greater Toronto Area. Rates are based on distance and time from your departure area. The Downtown Express bus also offers affordable transportation to the airport. Car rental services are available at the airport and can be arranged in advance through Orbitz.com. The LINK train offers complimentary shuttle service between various areas in the airport, including from the parking garages to the terminal areas.
Parking at Ontario Airports
Several parking options are available for travelers departing from the Toronto Pearson International Airport. The parking lot is available at a daily maximum rate of $15.00. The parking garage offers covered parking for just $3.00 more per day. The daily parking lot is only steps from the departure area and is available at a rate of $28.00 per day.
Restaurants at Ontario Airports
Travelers departing from the Toronto Pearson International Airport can easily grab breakfast or a quick snack at one of the airport's many eating establishments, including Starbucks, Subway, Thai Express, Pizza Pizza and A&W.; Travelers interested in flights from Ontario may also wish to look for sales when searching for low cost flights on Orbitz.com.
Newark Liberty International Airport
Newark Liberty International Airport was the first major airport in the United States. It is also the busiest airport in the New Jersey area. Finding flights to Newark is simple when you use Orbitz.com. The site searches many different airlines at once to find discount rates for budget travelers. The airport is located just three miles from downtown Newark on Brewster Road. Over 30 domestic and international airlines operate out of the Newark Liberty Airport, including American, Delta, JetBlue, SAS and Virgin Atlantic. Orbitz.com offers bookings on most of these airlines.
Transportation From the Airport
This airport provides a variety of ground travel options. Car rentals are available through Orbitz.com at discounted rates. Taxi services are available at metered rates, and Amtrak runs throughout the city. There is a flat ticket fee for most locations within Newark. Many hotels also offer free shuttle services to and from the airport. Travel time from the airport to downtown generally takes no more than ten minutes regardless of the method of travel.
Weather in Newark
Newark, New Jersey, has cold winters with lows in the mid-20s and warm, humid summers with highs in the mid-80s. Temperatures can get down below freezing from December to March, and the city experiences several snowfalls throughout winter.
Newark Highlights and Attractions
The city of Newark offers a wide variety of things to do. The Newark Museum is the city's largest museum and is located in the heart of downtown. It features a variety of galleries and exhibits throughout the year. The Prudential Center is host to many music and sporting events. The Branch Brook Park is one of Newark's most beautiful parks. Summer months are the best time to visit Newark when the weather is warm and sunny. Orbitz.com offers discount travel services and flights to Newark for your next trip.
