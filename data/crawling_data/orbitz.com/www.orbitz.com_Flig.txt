Flights and Cheap Airline Tickets

Want discount airline tickets? We’ll send you free alerts

Whether you’re looking for cheap airfare for a next year’s big vacation or just cheap flights for a weekend
            getaway, we’ll find you plenty of cheap airfare and flights to choose from. Finding cheap airlines and sales for
            our customers is our specialty.

Get airline miles and points on flights

Are you a member of an airline frequent flier program? Add your account info to your Orbitz profile and get
            credit on applicable Orbitz flights. That’s right: Our discount airline tickets and cheap flights will also help
            you save in the long run. The airlines will credit your account with the points you earn. Pretty cool, huh?

Get real-time updates on flights sent to you

Stay on top of gate changes, delays on flights, and other things that come up with free Orbitz Care Alerts. You
            can have flight notifications sent by phone, email or text to up to 6 other people, too.
