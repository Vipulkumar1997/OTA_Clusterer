Empower yourself with Baptiste yoga
Founded in the 1940’s by Walt Baptiste, Baptiste yoga is inspired by Hatha yoga and the teachings of Krishnamacharya and his famous students, Desikachar and Iyengar. This style of yoga is all about empowering you to achieve your dreams and goals. If you need this is your life, then you should definitely try it! Go on, book yourself a spot on a Baptiste yoga retreat and get ready to hit refresh on your life!
