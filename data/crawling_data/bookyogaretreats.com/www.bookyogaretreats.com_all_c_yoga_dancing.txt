Feel the rhythm of a yoga and dance retreat
Ever wanted another activity to complement your yoga sessions with a bit of pizzazz? Well then, a yoga dance vacation might just be your cup of tea! Yoga helps awaken your body’s natural flow and refresh your mind. In turn, dancing strengthens your body and allows your mind to fuel creativity. The dances during the retreats can vary from contemporary and spiritual dances, to salsa and belly dancing. It doesn’t matter if you’re a beginner or an advanced yogi. Everyone is more than welcome to book a rhythmic yoga and dance holiday!
