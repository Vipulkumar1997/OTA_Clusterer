Thai yoga massage is a healing practice that incorporates yoga movements into an incredibly soothing massage. It is meant to relax your body and mind as well as restore your energy. On a Thai massage retreat, the gentle pressure applied on certain energy points in the body will improve your general state of well-being. Whether you’re interested in becoming a proficient Thai yoga massage therapist or you’re here just for a relaxing Thai massage retreat, one thing is certain. You’re in for a life-changing, transformative experience!
