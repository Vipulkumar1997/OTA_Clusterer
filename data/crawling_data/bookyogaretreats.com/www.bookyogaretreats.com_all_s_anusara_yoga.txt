Anusara yoga retreats, for a soothing yet active experience
Anusara yoga was developed by yogi John Friend in 1997. Since then, this yoga style has spread all around the world, and it’s easy to see why. Anusara yoga will help you stretch every muscle in your body, improving your overall posture. Not only that, but the breath-work and meditation included in the practice will allow you to explore your inner self on a whole new level. Book your Anusara yoga retreat today and prepare to find inner joy!
