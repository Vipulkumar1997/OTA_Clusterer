Let Bhakti yoga cultivate love and devotion in you
Bhakti is the yoga of devotion. Called the sweetest of the yoga approaches, Bhakti yoga focuses on loving devotion towards a personal god. Be it Krishna, Radha, Vishnu, Lakshmi, Ganesha, Rama, or another god, Bhakti yoga will guide you on a spiritual path for salvation.
