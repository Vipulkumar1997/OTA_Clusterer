Push yourself on a Dynamic yoga retreat
A dynamic person needs a dynamic style of yoga! Dynamic yoga is an athletic and modern style of Vinyasa yoga. It combines elements of Power, Forrest, Baptiste, and Jivamukti. Just a heads-up that you will be challenged and you will sweat more than you'd expect! If that sounds like your own personal heaven, then go ahead and book a spot on a Dynamic yoga retreat and enjoy yourself!
