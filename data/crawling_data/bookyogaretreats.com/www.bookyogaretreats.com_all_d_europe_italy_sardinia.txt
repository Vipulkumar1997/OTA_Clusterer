Discover charming Sardinia on a yoga holiday
Sardinia is the second-largest island in the Mediterranean Sea, full of places and facets to discover. The coastline is never ending and has plenty of sandy beaches. The mountains are perfect for hiking, and medieval castles and palaces will allure you. Between yoga classes, walk through all the narrow streets and cathedrals. A Sardinia yoga retreat is definitely a vacation to remember.
