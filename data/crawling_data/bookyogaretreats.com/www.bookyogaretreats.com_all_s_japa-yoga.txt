Feed your soul on an illuminating Japa yoga retreat
Japa stands for mindful repetition of a certain mantra. Reciting mantras throughout your yoga practice is known to silence the mind and dissolve negative emotions. It is like a divine potion that will open your soul up and help you find enlightenment. On a Japa yoga retreat, you will go beyond the practice of asanas and breath-work. You will discover ways to get closer to Divinity and find inner peace. Book your Japa yoga holiday today and have a life-changing experience!
