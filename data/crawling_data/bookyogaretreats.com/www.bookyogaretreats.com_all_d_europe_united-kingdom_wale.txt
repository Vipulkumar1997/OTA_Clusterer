Wales is culturally Celtic, unique to the rest of the United Kingdom. It is known for its beautiful landscapes, from majestic mountains to fabulous coastlines, with stunning national parks and age-old castles. Cardiff, the capital city of Wales, is also a worthwhile destination. Whether you pick a city or a picturesque, remote area, one thing is certain.
