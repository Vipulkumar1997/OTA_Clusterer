Indulge in healthy yet tasty food with vegetarian yoga holidays
Many yoga packages also offer vegetarian or vegan yoga retreats. On Ayurveda yoga holidays, for instance, you can even get a personalized diet, tailored just for you. On many vegetarian or vegan retreats, you may not be allowed to consume alcohol at all. You will thank yourself for trying out a yogic diet though. Meals are totally delicious and nourishing, sometimes even prepared gourmet style! So, dare to try out some tasty, yet 100% healthy vegetarian food on your best yoga retreat so far!
