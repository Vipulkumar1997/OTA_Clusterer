Yoga Nidra, for a deeply relaxed state of mind
Yoga Nidra is rooted in Tantric traditions and it is an easy practice that guarantees to bring you complete relaxation. On a Nidra yoga retreat, you will invigorate your body while freeing your mind from tensions and blockages. You will learn to listen to your subconscious and you will evolve spiritually. Book a Nidra yoga retreat today and learn to rest your body and mind, by letting go of all negativity.
