Unlock your full potential on a Satyananda yoga holiday
Satyananda yoga or Bihar yoga was founded by yoga teacher and guru Swami Satyananda Saraswati. His teachings integrate a unique blend of several yoga styles, such as Hatha, Nidra, Jnana, Karma, and Tantra. On a Satyananda yoga retreat, yogis have the chance to address all sides of their practice. Asanas will help you balance, strengthen, and stretch your body. Meditation and breath-work will silence your mind and help you embrace your inner self. Book a Satyananda yoga retreat today and prepare to completely reset your life!
