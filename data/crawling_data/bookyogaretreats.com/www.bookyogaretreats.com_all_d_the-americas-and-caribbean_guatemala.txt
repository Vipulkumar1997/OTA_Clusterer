Get inspired on a Guatemala yoga holiday
Located in Central America, Guatemala is a country with rich culture. During your yoga vacation here, you’ll be able to explore more of its history and natural beauty. Want a close look of ancient Mayan sites? There’s Tikal, Yaxha, Aguateca, El Mirador – the list goes on! How about attractions featuring nature’s finest? We couldn’t recommend Lake Atitlan and Semuc Champey enough. Book a Guatemala yoga retreat and find inspiration in one of the most wondrous countries in the world!
