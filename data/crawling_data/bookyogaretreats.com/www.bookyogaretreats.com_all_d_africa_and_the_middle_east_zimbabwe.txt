Create the best African adventure on your Zimbabwe yoga retreat
Are you a nature lover and an adrenaline junkie too? Then, we bet a yoga holiday in Zimbabwe is the perfect choice for your next escape. Bungee jumping, trekking, hiking, freshwater fishing, safaris; these are only a few of the many exhilarating activities available here! Practicing yoga in Zimbabwe also means being surrounded by incredible nature. Don't forget to catch a glimpse of the famous Victoria Falls and Malindidzimu Hill, also known as World’s View! Later on, you can even go shopping in Elephant’s Walk Shopping & Artist Village for quality African crafts.
