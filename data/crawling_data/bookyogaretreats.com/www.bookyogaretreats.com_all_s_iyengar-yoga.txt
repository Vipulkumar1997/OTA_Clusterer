Have a surprising, yet nourishing Iyengar yoga holiday
Iyengar yoga teaches about the right alignment during your practice. Props and extensive breath-work are also used throughout the practice, to make sure your yoga poses turn out as perfect as they can be. This yoga style is nourishing yet refreshing, on both physical and spiritual levels. Book an Iyengar yoga retreat now and prepare to learn about the perfect body alignment!
