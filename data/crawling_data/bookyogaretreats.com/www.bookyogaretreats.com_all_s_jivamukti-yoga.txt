Deepen your yoga practice on a Jivamukti yoga retreat
Jivamukti yoga was founded in 1984 in New York by Sharon Gannon and her partner, David Life. A Jivamuki yoga retreat will do wonders on your body, mind, and soul. How? Through the five principles of Jivamukti yoga: scripture, devotion, kindness, music, and meditation. At the moment, Jivamukti yoga is very hot among celebrities, so why don’t you give it a try?
