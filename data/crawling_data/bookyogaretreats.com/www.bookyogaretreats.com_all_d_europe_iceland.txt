Immerse yourself in sensational nature on an Iceland yoga retreat
Located between Europe and North America, Iceland is a mountainous island that abounds in glaciers and geothermal hot spots. Nature reigns here, making this country a stunningly beautiful place. So, prepare to soak in hot pools and explore the Icelandic wilderness. Or, if you’re lucky, you could even marvel at aurora borealis! Most Iceland yoga retreats combine the yoga practice with hiking or walking. This way, you get to experience Iceland’s natural scenery in all its glory! So, book a yoga retreat in Iceland today and get ready to chase those northern lights!
