As the country with the world's largest population, China is one of Asia's great tourism jewels. It has a rich history that spans over roughly four thousand years. Modern yet historic, China attracts millions of tourists each year. While yoga has only recently arrived here, a yoga holiday or course in China will give you the chance to experience the best of what this country has to offer.
