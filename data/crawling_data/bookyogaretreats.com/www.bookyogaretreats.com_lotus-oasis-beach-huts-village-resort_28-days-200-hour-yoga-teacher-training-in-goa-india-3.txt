Accommodation



Featuring a beachfront, Lotus Oasis Beach Huts Resort is located in Patnem. The accommodation is of tropical bamboo beach huts. Staying in these huts on the beach with the sound of the ocean and a few dogs will get you really close to nature and can really calm you down.
Free Wi-Fi access is available at this resort. The accommodation will provide you with a patio and a seating area. Featuring a shower, the private bathrooms also come with free toiletries. Extras include a desk, an outdoor seating area, and bed linen. During the retreat, you will be staying in single occupancy or twin occupancy room.
At Lotus Oasis Beach Huts Resort, you will find a 24-hour front desk, a bar, and a snack bar. This property also is one of the top-rated locations in Patnem. Guests are happier about it compared to other properties in the area. This property is also rated for the best value in Patnem. Guests are getting more for their money when compared to other properties in this city.


Lotus Oasis Beach Huts Village Resort Yoga Teacher Training in India Program



Yoga is a way of life, a life-changing action, a process of transformation. In this training, you will have a welcome ceremony followed by havan on the first day, and will have a closing ceremony and graduation day on the day of departure.

Location



This training will take place at Patnem Beach, Goa, India. Patnem Beach is part of Canacona, which is a district in South Goa, India. Patnem is one of the very few undiscovered beaches in Goa and is still not commercialized, although it is very close to Palolem Beach, which is totally the opposite.
So, staying in Patnem, you can relax and enjoy your time and when you want to shop or want to see busy beaches of Goa, Palolem, Agonda, and other beaches are easily accessible.

Nearby places


Canacona bus station, 2.5 kilometers

Canacona railway station, 3.1 kilometers

Goa International Airport (GOI), 59 kilometers

Palolem Beach, 2.5 kilometers




Food



You will be provided with vegetarian buffet meals daily except on Sundays. Vegetarianism is part of yoga philosophy, so all students are suggested to eat vegetarian food during the course. You will also be provided with herbal tea and drinking water during your training.


Things to do (optional)



Lotus Oasis Beach Huts Village Resort will offer cooking classes, Reiki sessions, Ayurvedic massages, opportunities for swimming enthusiasts, and some small beach activities. You will also be able to easily get bikes on rent during your stay.

How to get there



Arrival by airplane

Please book your flight to arrive at Goa International Airport (GOI). Transfer from and to the airport is included. Lotus Oasis Beach Huts Village Resort will pick you up from the airport.

Arrival by bus

If you reach Goa by bus, you need to get down at Margao KTC bus stand, in which you can be picked up at at an extra cost.

Arrival by train

The nearest train stations are Margao railway station and Canacona railway station. The resort can also arrange a pickup from these stations.
