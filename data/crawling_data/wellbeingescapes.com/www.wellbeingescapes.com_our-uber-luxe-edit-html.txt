Ultra Luxury Spa Holidays


If you are looking for a uber luxury spa holiday then you've come to the right place. We've hand-picked a stunning range of exclusive escapes where you'll be cosseted and looked after to the highest degree with luxurious surroundings, top notch treatments and impeccable service.




Country

Hotel

Overview





France



Evian Resort Hotel Ermitage

This 4* hotel is gloriously perched overlooking the historic town of Evian-les-Bains and Lake Geneva. It's an much-loved resort boasting an award-winning spa and golf course;  offering a winning combination of elegance, authenticity and relaxation.





France




Evian Resort Hotel Royal

Situated between Lake Geneva and the beautiful French Alps, the 5* hotel is surrounded by stunning views. Nestled in the centre of 47 acres of parkland, the historic and newly refurbished Hotel Royal has hosted a wealth of celebrities over the years and boasts an award-winning spa and golf course.





France



Le Lodge Park

Tucked amongst the glorious Rhone-Alpes, in the heart of the chic mountain resort of Megève is Le Lodge Park. Along with fabulously designed interior this newly refurbished hotel hosts the renowned Pure Altitude Spa and offers a short drive to the spectacular Mont Blanc. The area has plenty of activities and is a spa and fitness-lovers paradise.





France



Les Fermes de Marie

Situated within five acres in the chic mountainous resort of Megève is Les Fermes de Marie. The hotel is both timeless and luxurious and boasts the stunning and effective Pure Altitude Spa. Revitalise in the fresh Alpine air as you walk through the private gardens or unwind in the spa.





France



Terre Blanche

Terre Blanche is located in one of the most beautiful regions in the south of France. Renowned for top notch service and the feeling of space, this uber luxurious resort is a gem. Boasting 14 spa treatments rooms and an 18-hole golf course it is a perfect escape for singles, couples, families or any ultimate wellbeing seeker!





Italy




Capri Palace Hotel & Spa

Looking out across the amazing Gulf of Naples on the Italian island of Capri sits an ancient palace built in the 1700's which is now the home of Capri Palace Hotel & Spa. With 30 spa professionals, a 5* beauty centre and two restaurants which boast three Michelin stars between them, Capri is home to ultimate relaxation.





Italy




Novessentia

The stylish and historic hotel is perched on the edge of Lake Maggiore in the heart of a charming medieval village. Offering peronalised programmes with a mix of wellbeing and preventative medicine, plus world class service and a friendly atmosphere - results and relaxation ensue!




Spain



Six Senses Puente Romano Beach & Spa

This stunning, contemporary beachside hotel is part of the Marbella Golf Club Resort with a brand-new Six Senses spa, nine restaurants (including gluten-free and organic ones) plus a wide range of fitness facilities and weekly group classes. You are guaranteed to love every moment.





Vietnam



Six Senses Con Dao

Ranked as one of the top eco-lodges in the world by National Geographic Traveller, Con Dao is located in a breathtaking protected national marine park and has a great team of alternative medicine and fitness practitioners. This world diving hotspot is also home to many turtles that come ashore to lay their eggs from May to October.





Vietnam



Six Senses Ninh Van Bay

Ninh Van Bay is the ultimate luxury bolthole offering seclusion and relaxation. Only accessible by boat the resort is located in a stunning harbour with tremendous views of the East Vietnamese Sea. Discover traditional healing practices, delicious Vietnamese cuisine and possibly even catch glimpses of endangered monkeys and eagles.
