Your online resource for ferries and ferry crossing information and booking. No matter where you want to travel whether it is a ferry to France, Irish ferry or ferry crossing to most European destinations we can help. You can book online and get cheap ferry crossing rates to major European ports including Dover, Ostend, Belfast or Cherbourg.

Kasbah have developed a partnership with Direct Ferries who have for several years specialised in the provision of low cost ferries and travel solutions to the Travel Industry, to all major European Ferrry destinations, Direct Ferries have specialist Car, Motorbike, and Freight divisions.

We are proud of our reputation and the client base we have built up and we are now able to offer the same standards of low prices combined with fast customer service to the general public. With over 36 routes to and from the UK giving us a combined total of over 200 crossings per day, no need for you to call several different ferry operators and compare prices and schedules, you can do it all here with one click.
