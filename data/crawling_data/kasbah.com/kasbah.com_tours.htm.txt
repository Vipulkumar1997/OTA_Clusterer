Featured Destination: Ayres Rock Adventure Tour Australia
In central Australia, Ayres Rock is the world's largest monolith at 3.6 kilometers long, two kilometeres wide, 348 metres high and 9.4 kilometres round. Uluru, which belongs to the Anangu Aboriginal people: holds a significant place in the aboroginal people's creation stories and laws, known as Tjukurpa. Many stories relate to how ancestral beings formed the rock. Aboriginal guides share these ancient tales with visitors on tours around Uluru's base. More >>
