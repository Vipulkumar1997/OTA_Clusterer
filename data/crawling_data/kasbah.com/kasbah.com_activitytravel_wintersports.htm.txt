Winter Sports


We think we have picked the best of the bunch here. There are some very comprehensive sites listed below covering ski and winter sports travel to every corner of the planet. Let me know if your favorite site should be here.
e-mail

Jump down to:
Weather









Tactics Boardshop
- Looking for the killer deal on snow gear? The new stuff is starting to arrive and the rest is marked down, so you cant miss the prices. Check it out!








ski.com
- British based ski travel company offering some good looking deals, especially in the late availability section. Mostly European destinations plus a few in Colorado.








Absolute Best Ski Buys
- Daman-Nelson Ski Travel - These people know about ski travel, and it shows. A great site, easy to use with some great looking deals. Sections on charter flights, special weeks, late availability and much more.








Southwest Airlines Ski Vacations
- Customized Packages - Ski the south west with South West Airlines. Direct flights from many US cities to resorts in Utah, Nevada, New Mexico, Idaho and Colorado.








Ski-Search
- Searchable directory of ski resorts and accommodation. Most information relates to European resorts, with a little US info thrown in for good measure. Also has useful sections on Ski equipment and publications.








Free Ski Package Quote Request
- Just as the title suggests. Select a ski resort , and receive the best price on your own customized ski vacation package. Covers resorts in the USA and Europe, with a section on summer skiing.








SkiIN
- More than 2300 resorts at your fingertips from this site. All the ski deals you could wish for here. Strong on European destinations. This is also one of the best ski information sites we found during our recent survey.








Ski and Snowboard Vacation and Travel Packages
- Tours De Sport - Vacation packages for skiers and snowboarders. Destinations in the United States and Canada at some really good inclusive prices. Online trip customization and quote form.








SkiCentral - Travel Companies
- This is a very useful directory of 270, at last count, ski travel companies worldwide. If you can't find what your looking for here it probably doesn't exist. Use the left hand column of the site to take you to much more very useful ski info.








SkiCentral - Accommodations
- From the same people as above (SkiCentral), but this time a directory for global ski accommodation. Some good deals when we checked.








Moguls Ski & Snowboard Tours
- A ski trip, ski tour, snowboard trip, ski vacation or just plain skiing or snowboarding, whatever you want to call it, when you're planning a winter vacation, you want to make sure it's done right. Since 1982 Moguls has been planning the very best in ski vacations and snowboard vacations. They arrange trips to all major Western resorts in the US and Canada, as well as New England and South America. They will also organize an outstanding ski trip for parties from 1 to 1000, taking care of all the details.








RSN Fast Travel Resort Index
- This is a general ski and snowboard site but it appears to offer some good looking travel deals. The main site information is excellent. They seem to major in "ski cams" for some reason.








Travel Time Vacations
specialize in discounted summer and ski vacations to Vail, Breckenridge, Winter Park, Keystone and Steamboat, all resorts in Colorado. Their travel experts live and play in these beautiful resort destinations, consequently they can let you in on the "secrets" to enjoying the most memorable travel experience of your life. And in the process, you'll save a bunch of money! So they say!








SkiNet.com
- Browse the top 60 North American Ski Resorts. See which areas scored tops in categories such as snow quality, apres ski, and terrain. Find just what you want and make a booking. A great site that takes you straight to the action.








SkiMaps.com
- Although this site offers some interesting ski travel deals, the major reason for the sites inclusion here is the amazingly comprehensive ski map archive. Hundreds of resort and run maps from just about every resort on the planet. A good site to check out if you are planning to visit a resort for the first time.







Weather








SnowReport.com
- Live from the SnowPhones. Real Audio weather reports for dozens of ski and snowboard resorts across the United States and Canada. Text reports are available for resorts in Europe and other areas. This site also has an in-depth listing of snowCAMS.








SKIcast!
- An infectious site, packed with much useful and some not so useful information for winter sports enthusiasts worldwide. Take your pick from satellite views, ski reports, ski forecasts, dewpoints, ski imagery, radar and even the weather up there on the slopes. A great site to know about if you break your leg on the first day!
