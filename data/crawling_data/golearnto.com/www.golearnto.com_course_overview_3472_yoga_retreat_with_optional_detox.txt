Holiday overview

This fantastic all inclusive 7 night yoga retreat is perfect for those looking to relax, restore balance and possibly detox; twice daily yoga sessions provide the core to this holiday, with all meals & juices prepared on site using the freshest ingredients, you can opt for a juice detox too

Yoga programme over 7 nights / 8 days - there are usually two yoga sessions offered per day


Additional activities programme offered, for example, walks, meditation, rebounding, aqua aerobics (exact schedule to be confirmed on arrival)


All breakfasts, lunches and dinners (you can choose between juice or food at all meals and how long you stay on the juice based diet is your choice)


Unlimited water and herbal teas


7 nights / 8 days accommodation in a twin room in a shared studio apartment


Please note: Prices are per person and based on 2 people sharing a room in an apartment


If you are a solo traveller, you will be placed with another solo traveller of the same sex or you can book a private twin room for a single supplement


Check in any time after 2pm on day 1 and check out at 10am on day 8


Please note that arrivals after 10pm (9pm 1 November to 31 March) will not be accepted at the retreat


Return airport transfer for flights arriving and departing from Alicante airport between 9am and 8pm (7pm 1 November to 31 March)

Location overview

This wonderful retreat on the sunny Costa Blanca of Spain is situated in peaceful surroundings with lush gardens, two pools and plenty of space to relax and rejuvenate. Accommodation is available to share or you have the option to reserve a room to yourself and with fresh healthy meals provided daily, you really can be assured that you will return home relaxed, reenergised and ready to continue your new healthy lifestyle.
