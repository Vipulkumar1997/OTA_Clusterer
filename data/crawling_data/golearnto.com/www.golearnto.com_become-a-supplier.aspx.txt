Become a holiday partner with GoLearnTo.com

We are always looking for new, high quality and exciting holidays and love to hear from the locals who run them.

If this sounds like something you can offer and you would like to learn more about distributing your holidays through GoLearnTo.com, please consider the following before registering your interest.

What do we look for?

- We only sell holidays with a
learning or activity focus

- We look for
packages including accommodation
(we do not take on accommodation only)
- We
work directly with the local sellers
of holidays (e.g. chefs in Tuscany, yoga instructors in Turkey)

Sorry but we don't work with...

- holidays with one unique date (your holiday must run multiple times throughout the year)
- DMC's or tour operators

Sounds like something you do?

Please contact us by email at
product@golearnto.com
with the following information so that your application can be considered:
- Your company name
- Website address
- Your contact details (phone, email, name)
- A short description of the activity and accommodation you offer
- Highlight the learning / actvity element of your holiday
- The dates you run the holidays
- Please tell us how long you have been running the holidays
Once we’ve received your application, we’ll be in touch with more details about the benefits of partnering with GoLearnTo.com and how we work.
Please don't live chat us or call our reservations line if you are looking to list your holidays, our team cannot deal with these requests. We promise if you complete the information we've requested above, one of the product team will get back to you.
Thanks and we look forward to hearing about your holidays.
