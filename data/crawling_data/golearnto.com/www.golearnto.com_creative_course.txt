Find your muse
Leave your number-crunching left brain at the office and get your creative juices flowing in settings meant to inspire and energise your mind.
Our imaginative creative courses put you in the heart of the action -- here, professional photographers help you frame the perfect shot, passionate Flamenco dancers teach you fiery steps and local artists share how they express gorgeous vistas on canvas.
For added flair, you can even combine creative and art courses with local activities.
Hold your frame while salsa dancing in Spain, then learn the steps in Spanish. Trade your paint brush for a spatula on a multi-activity cooking and painting holiday in Greece.
Surf in the morning and photograph the changing lights over the rainforest in Costa Rica. Design exquisite jewellery and tasty dishes on cookery and art holidays in France.
Whatever your style, find creative courses that free your artistic side and indulge your inner photographer, painter, creator or dancer alongside other passionate artists. Never settle for ordinary.
Who are our creative holidays guests?
Expressive souls, beginners who’ve never set up an easel, lifelong artists – everyone is welcome in the studios of our creative hosts.
You’ll meet people of all ages and nationalities, from solo travellers to couples to friends. Some have devoted their lives to creativity, others are keen to escape the 9-to-5 and inspire their minds.
All that’s required is a passion for creativity and a refusal to be boxed in.
Is this you? It looks like you.
Thousands of people have already discovered that our eclectic mix of art courses and creative holidays are as diverse as the people who go on them. Come and join them.
