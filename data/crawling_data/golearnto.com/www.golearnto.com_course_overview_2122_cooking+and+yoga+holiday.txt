Holiday overview

Combine a passion for the delights of Thai cookery with a love of yoga on this unique 7 night yoga and cookery holiday; take advantage of the wonderful natural beauty of
Koh Yao Noi Island
, an hour from Phuket where you’ll stay in a boutique hotel/spa, blend daily yoga and 3 cooking classes
more details...


20

18








Included in the price

Please note that holidays
do not
include flights, please see the 'getting there' tab for more details.


7 Yoga classes (1.5 hours per session)


Yoga classes start at 8.30am each morning


1 private yoga class


3 days of Thai cookery classes (2 hours per day)


Cookery sessions start around 1pm and the exact cookery schedule will be confirmed on arrival#


Please note that cookery lessons run with a minimum of 2 people, if you are a solo traveller, it may be possible for you to join an existing group but availability but be confirmed first


4 x 90 minute body treatments of your choice during the week (appointments will be confirmed on arrival)


Accommodation at the resort in a Superior Twin/Double Studio with en-suite for 7 nights


Please note: Prices are based on two sharing a twin or double room with en-suite bathroom


Single travellers can book a twin/double room with en-suite bathroom for sole occupancy for a compulsory single room supplement (subject to availability)


Please note that shared rooms for solo travellers are not offered on this holiday and singles must pay a compulsory single supplement for their own room


You can check in anytime after 2pm on day 1 and check-out by 12pm on day 8


Full buffet breakfast each day


Unlimited use of the herbal steam room and waterfall plunge pool bath


Fresh local mineral water in your room


Return airport transfers from Phuket Airport to the retreat (please note transfers will run at fixed times due to the ferry schedule - departure from the ferry terminal will be at 11am or 4pm; if you are unable to make these times, you will need to book overnight accommodation in Phuket)


Location overview

Stay in the magnificent
Phang Nga bay
, a stunning island retreat and spa bathed in warm tropical sun with secluded beaches. You will be in the expert hands of the senior instructor, a knowledgeable and welcoming guide with a commitment to improving your wellbeing through the practice of yoga for an unrivalled wellbeing holiday.
