Holiday overview

This all inclusive 6 night luxury boot camp and detox retreat, set in a beautiful coastal resort complex on the Costa Blanca near Alicante, will leave you relaxed, invigorated & pampered with daily yoga, Pilates, core, HIIT, circuits at the beach, guided hikes plus delicious nutritious food

6 nights luxury accommodation in your own spacious and well-equipped suite with full bathroom and private terrace or balcony


Prices are based on 1 person in their own private suite with ensuite bathroom (no single supplement for a private room)


If two people travel together, it is possible to share a suite with 2 double bedrooms
Private 1-2-1 health & wellness check upon arrival
Arrival day welcome dinner
5 Day full light detox board with breakfast, lunch & dinner plus juice shots, fresh fruit and healthy snacks too, all prepared freshly each day by your dedicated Chef and his team
5 Day targeted fitness program including: daily yoga, Pilates, circuit training at the beach, HIIT, core strength training, swiss ball, BOX FIT, stunning guided walks/hikes along the coast and up into the mountains
Meditation class for stress reduction
Healthy snacks freshly prepared by our chef
Unlimited mineral water, herbal infusions and organic teas
Complimentary use of the on-site Wellness Spa with heated indoor wave pool, sauna, Jacuzzi & spacious treatment rooms
Complimentary use of spa gym facilities
Optional private 1-2-1 sessions
Optional spa therapies, holistic therapies and massages
Free WiFi access inside the suites and outdoor terraces
A pre-arrival pack to help you prepare for the holiday and receive maximum results
Post retreat pack including nutritional information, recipes plus a free 1-2-1 taster session via Skype with the head personal trainer or yoga teachers according to guest preference
A scheduled return airport transfer - you will be collected from Alicante airport at 11/11:30am on Day 1 and you will depart from the retreat at 12pm on Day 7
If you have booked flights outside of the transfer times or scheduled arrival/departure dates, the school can normally accommodate this, but it may be that an additional supplement will be charged depending on the time of your arrival/departure
Check in after 12pm on day 1 and check out by 10am on day 7
Location overview
Stay in a stunning private gated complex of luxury suites set around a large lagoon pool in the heart of the beautiful green triangle of the Costa Blanca, just 10 minutes from the beach, and enjoy the unique experience of a luxury holistic wellbeing retreat with a difference. Nestled between the mountains and the sea and only an hour from Alicante, this location offers the perfect combination of fitness, relaxation, detoxing, healthy eating, pampering and wellness ensuring you return relaxed.
