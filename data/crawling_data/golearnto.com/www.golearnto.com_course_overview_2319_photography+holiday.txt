Holiday overview

Unleash the creative within on this fantastic 7 night / 8 day photography holiday set in the Dordogne and allow your photography skills to develop and flourish through small group and individual tuition during fun, organised excursions to fantastic local sites in this stunning region of France

6 photography excursions into the surrounding villages and countryside


All transportation for photography excursions


One-to-one photographic tuition and advice from cameras and lenses to the fundamentals of successful composition and getting the best from state-of-the art software


24/7 access to your own dedicated Apple Mac workstation with Photoshop CS6 for the duration of your stay


A full studio set up for you to link your camera into and use at your leisure of with tuition


Access to Epson R3000 A3+ printer


All meals are included


Daily choice of breakfast and a 4 course evening meal


7 nights’ accommodation in twin or double en-suite room with Wi-Fi and a flat screen television


Prices are based on 2 people sharing a twin/double room


If you are travelling solo, you can choose to share a room with a guest of the same gender for no additional cost, or you can have your own private room for no extra charge


Arrive on day 1 from 3pm and check out on day 8 at 10am


Location overview

This charming photography school in the Dordogne region of France provides top quality facilities, accommodation and cuisine. Set in a pretty and traditional village just 6 miles from Brantôme, the photography school provides
peaceful countryside photoshoot locations
as well as exciting and equally photogenic excursions to nearby sites of interest.
