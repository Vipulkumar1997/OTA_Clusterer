Holiday overview

Stay in a beautiful luxury hotel with spa and learn to cook in the Chef's 19th century country kitchen in the Provencal hilltop village of
La Cadière d’Azur
set in the Bandol wine region between Nice and Marseille and discover the art of fine Provencal cuisine with a Michelin star Chef

Location overview
Perched upon an ancient hill top in a village in the heart of the Bandol vineyards in Provence, this family run hotel is the setting for your French cookery holiday. Perfectly positioned to discover Provence, this hotel is made up of a cluster of ancient 11th century buildings set around a beautiful pool in pretty gardens with stunning views over the Provencal hills.
