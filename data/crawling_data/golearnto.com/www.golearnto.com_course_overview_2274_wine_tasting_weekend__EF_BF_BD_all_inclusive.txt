Holiday overview

Enjoy free-flowing wine and fine local food on this relaxed, all-inclusive 3 night wine tasting weekend
near Avignon
and discover the secrets of wine tasting in Rhône, one of France's most famous wine regions, and stay in a beautifully restored 18th century Provençal farmhouse
more details...

6








Included in the price

do not
include flights, please see the 'getting there' tab for more details.


*All meals and accommodation included*


Accommodation at the farmhouse in a twin/double room for 3 nights


You can check in any time after 2pm on day 1 and check-out by 12 noon on day 4


2 wine tasting teaching sessions of 2 hours each including tasting, assessing and discussing different wine style and quality


All transport to and from the restaurants, wine villages, producers and vineyards if you don’t have your own car


Private visits and tastings at 3-5 local wine producers


Visit to the village of Châteauneuf-du-Pâpe


Visit to the wine villages of Gigondas and Beaumes-de-Venise


All wine tasting materials


Continental breakfast each morning


Lunch on day 2 and 3


A 3 course dinner each evening


Evening wine tasting sessions on days 2 and 3 at the farmhouse before dinner


Please note: prices are per person and based on 3 nights/4 days accommodation in a shared twin room with a shared bathroom


Solo travellers can share a twin room with a shared bathroom with another guest (of the same sex) for no single supplement


Solo travellers who prefer to have their own private single room can pay a compulsory single supplement for their own private room with either a shared bathroom or a private bathroom, subject to availability
Location overview
Stay in a beautiful 18th century farmhouse surrounded by vineyards, olive and cherry trees with magnificent views of the majestic Mont Ventoux in scenic Provence and discover the secrets of the Rhône valley’s famous wines, learn about wine production, and how to taste wine whilst sampling delicious local produce.
