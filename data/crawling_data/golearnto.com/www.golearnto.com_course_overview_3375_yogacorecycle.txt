Holiday overview

Stay 7 nights in beautiful, beachside accommodation on the stunning island of Koh Samui at this tranquil and secluded wellness retreat; this fitness and wellbeing programme combines cardio and strength training as indulge in daily yoga lessons, core sessions and cycle classes

7 nights accommodation in a shared twin room with private bathroom


Please note: prices are per person and based on two sharing a twin or double room with private ensuite bathroom


Solo travellers can share a room with another solo traveller of the same sex for no extra cost or can choose to upgrade and book a twin/double room for sole occupancy for an extra supplement
Daily morning and evening yoga sessions
Please note: Saturdays are a rest day from the Ashtanga, there will be a class devoted to breath work and conditioning and restorative asana starting at 8am and a one hour restorative asana class at 5pm
Herbal tea is served at 7am before your first yoga lesson of the day
Morning breath work starts at 7.30am and asana begins at 8am and lasts for 1.5 hours
Evening restorative yoga and breath work starts at 5pm and lasts for 1 hour
Daily 30 min breath work class
A healthy and delicious brunch is served between 10am-12 noon each day
Core sessions to work all muscle groups
Cycle classes in a high intensity sea-view work out
Open programme
Dinner buffet each evening served around 6pm
Unlimited fresh mineral water
Optional evening activities which might include meditation, videos, social nights
Check in anytime after 4pm on day 1 and check out by 12noon on day 8
Note: yoga mats are not provided so please do bring your own, you can buy a mat from the yoga retreat’s shop if you prefer
Location overview
Stay in this serene eco friendly yoga retreat on the tranquil southern coast of
Koh Samui
and enjoy a relaxing holiday that is dedicated to wellness. Just a stone’s throw from Laem Sor beach on Koh Samui, a remote island paradise, 20 minutes from the nearest town and 45 minutes from Koh Samui airport your location will ensure a calming retreat far from the crowds for the perfect combination of nature, holistic wellness and relaxation.
