About FlipKey


FlipKey is part of TripAdvisor Rentals, a leading vacation rentals marketplace with more than 830,000 properties in 190 countries. Whether you’re looking for a city-center studio, a far-flung private island, a cozy ski chalet, or a sprawling beach house, you’ll find the perfect home with TripAdvisor Rentals.
TripAdvisor Rentals connects the insider knowledge of our homeowners with the collective wisdom of 465 million TripAdvisor traveler reviews so you can stay the smarter way. With TripAdvisor, you’ll discover not only the best rental, but also the best restaurants, attractions and experiences—no matter where you’re headed.
FlipKey was founded in 2007 and joined the TripAdvisor Rentals family in 2008. The TripAdvisor Rentals family also includes Holiday Lettings, Niumba, Vacation Home Rentals and HouseTrip. To see all 830,000+ TripAdvisor Rentals listings, visit www.TripadvisorRentals.com
Are you a property manager or homeowner with a home to rent? List it on TripAdvisor, the world’s largest community, reaching 390 million average unique monthly visitors.
