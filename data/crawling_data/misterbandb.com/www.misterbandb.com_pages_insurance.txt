Rental Properties Outside of the European Union


This policy may be used by any User (a) who is not a resident of the European Union and (b) that is travelling to a Rental Property outside of the European Union.

If a Guest refuses to occupy or commence a booking for a Rental Property upon first arriving at the Rental Property, due to the Rental Property not existing or conforming to the description on the Site, Services and/or App, misterb&b’s insurance policy may apply. Such policy coverage requires that Guest reports the issue to both misterb&b and EUROP ASSISTANCE within twenty-four (24) hours of first arriving at the Rental Property. misterb&b’s policy with EUROP ASSISTANCE may cover certain costs for any damage caused by the Guest (such as if the Guest causes bodily injury and damage to property in the Rental Property belonging to the Host). Such policy coverage requires that the Host or Guest, as applicable, report the loss promptly and in strict accordance with EUROP ASSISTANCE'S general terms and conditions.

EXCLUDED COUNTRIES

The following countries are excluded from the above-listed policies: North Korea, Cuba, Iran, Syria, Sudan, Russia, China and the Crimea region, as well as countries engaged in civil or foreign wars; experiencing acknowledged political instability; reprisals; restriction in the freedom of circulation of goods and persons, irrespective of the cause, in particular for medical, security and meteorological reasons; experiencing acts of terrorism; experiencing natural catastrophes or release of nuclear radiation.

In addition to the exclusions set forth above, there may be other countries excluded from these insurance policies.  Please refer to the specific policies for actual coverage provided and/or contact misterb&b for additional information.
