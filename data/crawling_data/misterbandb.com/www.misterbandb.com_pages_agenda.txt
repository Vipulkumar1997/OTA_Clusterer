SEPTEMBER 2017



1st - 11th

Sitges

International Bears Week

Bears Sitges it consists of friends with common goals such as solidarity, make new friends, have fun, organize events, outings, games, discussions, etc ... And actively promote the cultural, architectural and social heritage of Sitges



3rd - 13th

Amsterdam

Atlantis Amsterdam-Barcelona Cruise

Join over 2800 guests for ten nights of fun and exploration in four different countries onboard the Celebrity Silhouette. Take a stroll along the canals of Amsterdam, sip on a glass of wine in Bordeaux, relax on the beaches in Malaga or simply unwind onboard on the real grass lawn and take in the sea air.



4th - 10th

Benidorm

Benidorm Gay Pride

Benidorm Pride 2017 will take place from September 4th to 10th with the Parade on Saturday the 9th. This is only the 7th big pride outing for the high octane resort and is set to be the biggest and most wow-factor event on the Costa Blanca coast in 2017!



6th - 10th

Berlin

Folsom Europe

The whole fetish community is represented and everyone enjoys the possibility to show his or her fetish, meet old friends or make new ones. Some people discover their kinkyness by visiting the street fair. Listen to the Djs as you get a musical preview of the Pig Party. Also take a look around at all the fetish lovers and decide who will be your companion for the night.



16th - 17th

Dallas

Dallas Pride

Dallas Pride Weekend kicks off Saturday, September 16, 2017 with the Festival in the park! Parade day is Sunday, September 17, 2017 at 2pm.



18th - 25th

Lisbon

The Cruise by La Demence

This 7th edition of the European gay cruise offers you a unique itinerary from Lisbon to Madeira and the Canary islands, on board of Monarch. Offering a perfect mix of culture, relaxation and the best open air parties, this is your best holiday option



26th - 1st

Orlando

International Bears Week

International bears gathering at the amusement Park Center of the World, Orlando



27th - 1st

Curaçao

Curaçao Gay Pride

Curaçao Pride will be a 7-days long event filled with plenty of parties and activities for LGBT's and their friends.

Black & Blue Festival provides financial support to groups providing direct aid to people living with HIV/AIDS and to the gay and lesbian community action in Canada.

Gay Days Anaheim

The original Disneyland at Anaheim, near LA, is the venue of the unofficial Gay Days Anaheim.

Gran Canaria

Maspalomas Fetish Week

The Maspalomas Gay Fetish Week is a yearly event by several bars and clubs, with parties and events in and around the Yumbo centrum

Berlin

Hustlaball Berlin

HustlaBall is an Event concept by Jeffrey Davids and  saw its beginnings in New York City’s legendary hustler club Stella’s (March 1998).



20th - 22nd

Las Vegas

Las Vegas Pride

To educate the community by invoking, promoting, and celebrating lesbian / gay / bisexual / transgender pride



21st

Austin

Austin Gay Pride

The mission of the Austin Gay and Lesbian Pride Foundation is to educate, resource, and connect the lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer (LGBTQ) community in Austin, Texas



21st

Hawaii

Honolulu Gay Pride

The Hawai’i LGBT Legacy Foundation was formed in 2008 to connect and act as a facilitator with all other LGBT organizations, businesses and services throughout the state of Hawaii



26th - 29th

Amsterdam

Amsterdam Leather Pride

Amsterdam Leather Pride is the largest main party in the center of Amsterdam. Leather men from all over Europe come to Amsterdam for various parties and events.



31st

Los Angeles

Gay Halloween Streetparty

The largest Halloween street party in the world happens in West Hollywood! Every year on October 31st, 500,000 people take to the streets of Santa Monica Boulevard to enjoy the wild costumes and uninhibited crowds at the West Hollywood Halloween Carnaval.

Palm Springs Pride organization (pspride.org) is a non-profit organization founded to promote the public education and public awareness of individual rights and civil liberties of the lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender community and to promote the history, diversity and future prosperity of the Greater Palm Springs LGBT community.
