About Chandigarh



About the city:

Located near the foothills of Shivalik range of the great Himalayas, Chandigarh is one of the major tourist destinations in Punjab. Known for its fabulous colonial buildings, beautiful gardens, parks, tranquil lake, bustling markets and vibrant culture, Chandigarh city is undoubtedly, one of the major tourist hubs in whole of North India. The city beautiful Chandigarh serves as common capital to two Indian states, namely, Punjab and Haryana and is a union territory of India.

About Mumbai



About the city:

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay is a thriving commercial centre and a major touristic hotspot. Flying into Mumbai is easy as the Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport has connecting
flights to and from Mumbai

Amidst it all stands a wealth of grand colonial relics, museums, galleries, and old bazaars. Mumbai boasts of some of the finest restaurants in the country alongside its amazing street food. When it comes to shopping it offers some of the best picks and features grand shopping malls and well-known shops.
