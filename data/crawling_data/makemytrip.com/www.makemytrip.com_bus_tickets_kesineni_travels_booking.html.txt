About Kesineni Travels

Kesineni Travels was found by Srinivas Kesineni in 1992, under the guidance of his grandfather Mr. Kesineni Venkaiah, who had started bus operations in Andhra in 1928. Initially it was the family business where it was mainly into transportation. Currently they have expanded with bus operations for passenger transport, Cargo transport and Hotel Business. Kesineni has organized teams to manage its bus and cargo operations independently, which are lead by Chairman Kesineni Srinivas and Group President H T Ramakrishna. Kesineni travel is well known for its safe and modern fleet of Buses and includes all types of buses such as Volvo, AC and sleeper.
