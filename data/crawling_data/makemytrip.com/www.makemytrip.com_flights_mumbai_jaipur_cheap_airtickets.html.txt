About Jaipur



About the city: Cradled by the mighty Aravalis, Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan state, lies on the edge of the Thar Desert in eastern Rajasthan. This city of the royals is famous for its distinct architecture and stately monuments. Owing to its popularity as a tourist destination, Jaipur is well connected to major cities in India and the Middle East with regular flights. Several domestic low cost carriers as well as full service airlines offer cheap tickets, making air travel convenient. The ideal time to visit Jaipur is from October to March when the weather during the day is mild and the sunshine feels divine. The nights can get chilly though!
Jaipur International Airport is located in Sanganer around 13 km from the Jaipur city. Air Arabia, Jet airways, Kingfisher airlines, IndiGo and Spice jet have regular flights to Sharjah, Chandigarh, Delhi, Jammu, Kolkata, Bangalore and Dubai from Jaipur. Rambagh Palace hotel and Jaipur Marriott Hotel are nearest to Jaipur airport. Check

About Mumbai



About the city:

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay is a thriving commercial centre and a major touristic hotspot. Flying into Mumbai is easy as the Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport has connecting
flights to and from Mumbai
to around the world. Mumbai isn’t just the financial capital of India, but a melting pot of cultures and with great deals on flights to Mumbai, one can experience this vibrant city in all its form. From its amazing skyscrapers and posh malls to Bollywood and much more it has something to please everyone. Amidst it all stands a wealth of grand colonial relics, museums, galleries, and old bazaars. Mumbai boasts of some of the finest restaurants in the country alongside its amazing street food. When it comes to shopping it offers some of the best picks and features grand shopping malls and well-known shops.

IndiGo, Kingfisher and many other Indian airlines connect major cities including Delhi, Kolkatta, Bengaluru and many others. Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport is around 32 Kilometers far from the main city centre.
