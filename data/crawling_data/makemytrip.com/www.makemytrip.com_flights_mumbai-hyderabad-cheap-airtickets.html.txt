About Hyderabad



About the city:

Hyderabad, the capital city of Andhra Pradesh is a fascinating blend of history and culture. Situated on the banks of the Musi River its renowned as the City of Pearls and Land of the Nizams.
This modern, cosmopolitan city has a lot to offer the discerning traveler. With flights to and from Hyderabad, one can visit this historical city that is home to splendid monuments, forts and fine museums. When here, you can see a distinct Indo-Islamic architectural elegance in this culturally rich city. Its beautiful lakes and parks are a favorite amongst locals and tourists alike.
Hyderabadi cuisine that comprises of Mughal influences is rich and varied; its famous for its delicious briryanis and sweets. When it comes to shopping, do visit the citys old bazaars for an array of traditional arts and crafts, exquisite saris and fine pearls.
Lying in the Deccan Plateau, it has a moderate climate and with
cheap flight tickets to and from Hyderabad
its perfect for a short adventure. Hyderabad is easily accessible; The Rajiv Gandhi International Airport has connecting flights to and from Hyderabad.







Hyderabad is served by major leading domestic as well as international airlines. IndiGo, Emirates, Oman Air, Sri Lankan Airlines, Druk Air, Kingfisher and many other domestic as well as international airlines serve Hyderabad. Major cities like Delhi, Kolkata, Bengaluru, Mumbai and many others are some major cites which are well linked with the city via these airlines. Novotel, Marriott hotel, Leo Continental, Golkonda Resort & Spa and many others are famous hotels in Hyderabad. Rajiv Gandhi International Airport is almost 22 kilometers from main city. Check

About Mumbai



About the city:

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay is a thriving commercial centre and a major touristic hotspot. Flying into Mumbai is easy as the Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport has connecting
flights to and from Mumbai
to around the world. Mumbai isn’t just the financial capital of India, but a melting pot of cultures and with great deals on flights to Mumbai, one can experience this vibrant city in all its form. From its amazing skyscrapers and posh malls to Bollywood and much more it has something to please everyone. Amidst it all stands a wealth of grand colonial relics, museums, galleries, and old bazaars. Mumbai boasts of some of the finest restaurants in the country alongside its amazing street food. When it comes to shopping it offers some of the best picks and features grand shopping malls and well-known shops.
