About Vishakhapatnam



About the city:

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a city in the Indian state Andhra Pradesh. Primarily an Industrial city, Visakhapatnam also attracts tourists who love unspoilt sandy beaches, maintained parks and scenic surroundings, like Araku Valley.
Often referred to as Steel City, Jewel on the Coramandel Coast, and the City of Destiny, Visakhapatnam has beautiful sandy beaches, stunning landcapes, smooth roads and laterite hillocks. Healt tourism is developing fast here.
People here mainly speak Telugu. However, other languages like Oriya, Punjabi, Tamil and others are also widely spoken here due to migration of workers from different parts of the country. Though mainly known for its beaches, Visakhapatnam houses other tourist attractions too. Some fof the must-sees in Visakhapatnam are Kailasagiri, Simhachalam, Indira Gandhi Zoological Park, The Araku Valley, ISKCON temple and many others.
The city Visakhapatnam can be reached by rail, road and air. The fastest way to reach the city is of course by air. Visakhapatnam Airport is one of the busiest international airports in Coastal Andhra. There are daily
flights to Visakhapatnam
from cities like Hyderabad, Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi, Vijayawada, Bengaluru, Kolkata, Tirupati, Pune, Kolkata, Patna, Kochi, Indore and Raipur. Book your Visakhapatnam air tickets with makemytrip.com and avail fabulous deals and discounts.







Vishakhapatnam airport is the second busiest airport in Andhra Pradesh. Situated at a distance of approximately 7 kilometers from the main city, the airport has regular flights from major Indian cities such as New Delhi, Bhubaneswar, Chennai, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Mumbai, Raipur and Tirupati. IndiGo, Air India, Jet Lite, Spice Jet and Jet Airways are some of the well known airline operators having regular flights to and from Vishakhapatnam airport. About Hyderabad



About the city:

Hyderabad, the capital city of Andhra Pradesh is a fascinating blend of history and culture. Situated on the banks of the Musi River its renowned as the City of Pearls andLand of the Nizams.
This modern, cosmopolitan city has a lot to offer the discerning traveler. With flights to and from Hyderabad, one can visit this historical city that is home to splendid monuments, forts and fine museums. When here, you can see a distinct Indo-Islamic architectural elegance in this culturally rich city. Its beautiful lakes and parks are a favorite amongst locals and tourists alike.
Hyderabadi cuisine that comprises of Mughal influences is rich and varied; its famous for its delicious briryanis and sweets. When it comes to shopping, do visit the citys old bazaars for an array of traditional arts and crafts, exquisite saris and fine pearls.
Lying in the Deccan Plateau, it has a moderate climate and with
cheap flight tickets to and from Hyderabad
its perfect for a short adventure. Hyderabad is easily accessible; The Rajiv Gandhi International Airport has connecting flights to and from Hyderabad.







Hyderabad is served by major leading domestic as well as international airlines. IndiGo, Emirates, Oman Air, Sri Lankan Airlines, Druk Air, Kingfisher and many other domestic as well as international airlines serve Hyderabad. Major cities like Delhi, Kolkata, Bengaluru, Mumbai and many others are some major cites which are well linked with the city via these airlines. Novotel, Marriott hotel, Leo Continental, Golkonda Resort & Spa and many others are famous hotels in Hyderabad. Rajiv Gandhi International Airport is almost 22 kilometers from main city. Check
