A stay at Knights Inn Atlanta Near Six Flags places you in the heart of Atlanta, convenient to Six Flags Over Georgia.

This hotel is within the region of Greenbriar Mall and Point University.

Make yourself at home in one of the 150 air-conditioned rooms featuring refrigerators.

Make use of convenient amenities, which include complimentary wireless Internet access and gift shops/newsstands.

Featured amenities include express check-in, a 24-hour front desk, and laundry facilities. Planning an event in Atlanta? This hotel has facilities measuring 6500 square feet (604 square meters), including meeting rooms. Free self parking is available onsite.
