Located in Orlando, Baymont Inn and Suites Florida Mall is close to Florida Mall and Orlando Kart Center.

This hotel is within the vicinity of
SeaWorld
® Orlando and
Universal Studios Florida
™.

Make yourself at home in one of the 162 air-conditioned rooms featuring refrigerators and microwaves. Wired and wireless Internet access is complimentary, and flat-screen televisions with cable programming provide entertainment. Conveniences include desks and coffee/tea makers, and housekeeping is provided daily.

Take advantage of recreation opportunities such as an outdoor pool, or other amenities including complimentary wireless Internet access and a television in a common area.

A complimentary continental breakfast is served on weekdays from 6 AM to 9 AM and on weekends from 7 AM to 10 AM.

Featured amenities include complimentary wired Internet access, a business center, and a 24-hour front desk. Planning an event in Orlando? This hotel has facilities measuring 300 square feet (28 square meters), including a meeting room. Free self parking is available onsite.

Hotel Amenities


Hotel Amenities




Baymont Inn and Suites Florida Mall features an outdoor pool and a children's pool. A computer station is located on site and wired and wireless Internet access is complimentary. Business-related amenities consist of a business center and a meeting room. Guests can enjoy a complimentary breakfast on weekdays. Event facilities measure 300 square feet (28 square meters). This business-friendly hotel also offers multilingual staff, a safe deposit box at the front desk, and ATM/banking services. Complimentary self parking is available on site.
