When you stay at Excellence Playa Mujeres - Adults Only - All Inclusive in Playa Mujeres, you'll be next to a golf course and convenient to Punta Sam Ferry Terminal and Playa Mujeres Golf Club.

This 5-star resort is within the vicinity of El Meco Archaeological Site and Norte Beach.

Make yourself at home in one of the 450 air-conditioned rooms featuring free minibar items and DVD players. Rooms have private furnished balconies or patios. 42-inch LED televisions with satellite programming provide entertainment, while complimentary wireless Internet access keeps you connected. Private bathrooms with separate bathtubs and showers feature jetted bathtubs and complimentary toiletries.

Pamper yourself with a visit to the spa, which offers massages, body treatments, and facials. After dipping into one of the 7 outdoor swimming pools, you can spend some time at the private beach. Additional amenities at this resort include complimentary wireless Internet access, concierge services, and an arcade/game room.

This resort is all inclusive. Rates include meals and beverages at onsite dining establishments. Charges may be applied for dining at some restaurants, special dinners and dishes, some beverages, and other amenities.
Enjoy a meal at one of the resort's dining establishments, which include 9 restaurants and a coffee shop/café. From your room, you can also access 24-hour room service. When you are in need of a refreshing drink, visit a beach bar or one of 11 bars/lounges, or 7 poolside bars. A complimentary buffet breakfast is included.

Featured amenities include complimentary newspapers in the lobby, dry cleaning/laundry services, and a 24-hour front desk. Planning an event in Playa Mujeres? This resort has 182986 square feet (17000 square meters) of space consisting of conference space and a meeting room. A roundtrip airport shuttle is provided for a surcharge at scheduled times, and free valet parking is available onsite.

Resort Amenities




Excellence Playa Mujeres - Adults Only - All Inclusive is nestled on 2 miles (3.2 kilometer) of white-sand Caribbean beach in a resort community with inland marina, spa, and golf course. The resort features 9 restaurants, 11 bars, and 24-hour room service. Amenities include a nightclub, 4 freestanding spa tubs, 5 spa tubs adjoined to pools, and 7 outdoor pools, 4 of which are heated in the winter.

Other recreational opportunities include scuba diving, deep sea fishing, private yacht rentals, tennis courts, and the Playa Mujeres Golf Club, a 7,260-yard, 18-hole course designed by Greg Norman. A health club, which includes a sauna and a steam room, is also on site. The full-service day spa, Miile Spa, offers massage and beauty treatments for a charge.

The property offers 6 meeting rooms for large and small groups, banquet facilities, and complimentary wireless Internet access. Concierge services are available at desks throughout the property.

Nearby Things to Do




The resort has seven outdoor pools with five adjoining spa tubs. Four other spa tubs are freestanding. A health club with sauna and steam room is also on site.

Non-motorized water sports equipment includes kayaks, sail boats, snorkeling gear, and windsurfers. Land sports include two tennis courts and a basketball court. Lessons such as salsa dancing, cooking, yoga, aerobics, and rifle shooting are also included in the room rate.

Other recreational opportunities include scuba diving, deep sea fishing, and private yacht rentals (surcharge). Golfers will appreciate the Playa Mujeres Golf Club, a 7,260-yard, 18-hole course designed by Greg Norman (surcharge).
