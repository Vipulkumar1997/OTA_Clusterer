With a stay at Stratosphere Hotel, Casino & Tower, BW Premier Collection in Las Vegas, you'll be on the Strip and minutes from Chapel of the Flowers and close to Las Vegas Convention Center.

This casino resort is within close proximity of Little White Wedding Chapel and Arts Factory.

Make yourself at home in one of the 2427 air-conditioned rooms featuring plasma televisions. Complimentary wireless Internet access keeps you connected, and cable programming is available for your entertainment. Conveniences include desks and irons/ironing boards, and housekeeping is provided daily.

Pamper yourself with a visit to the spa, which offers massages, body treatments, and facials. After enjoying recreational amenities such as an outdoor pool and a spa tub, a lucky night at the casino is the perfect end to the day. This resort also features concierge services, an arcade/game room, and gift shops/newsstands.

Enjoy a meal at one of the resort's dining establishments, which include 6 restaurants and a coffee shop/café. From your room, you can also access 24-hour room service. Relax with a refreshing drink from a poolside bar or one of the 4 bars/lounges.

Featured amenities include limo/town car service, a computer station, and express check-out. Event facilities at this resort consist of conference space and a meeting room. Free valet parking is available onsite.

Hotel Amenities


Hotel Amenities




Guests take in views of the Las Vegas Strip from the 1,149-foot Stratosphere Tower observation deck. Admission to the tower is $5 per person (up to four guests staying in the same room). Rising 112 stories, the tower is one of the tallest free-standing structures west of the Mississippi River. Elevators whisk guests to indoor and outdoor observation decks with 360-degree views of the mountain-ringed desert valley.

An outdoor pool complex includes a freeform pool with spa tub, water volleyball, and rental cabanas. A fitness center in the Tower Shops has free weights, cardio, and strength-training machines available to all guests. The Roni Josef Salon and Spa includes a sauna and offers massages and beauty treatments.

The 80,000-square-foot casino provides table games, slot and video poker machines, a poker room, a race and sports book, and a center bar.

The revolving Top of the World restaurant serves global cuisine, Fellini's Ristorante offers courtyard dining, and Crafted Buffet has daily brunch and dinner buffets. Other options include Roxy's 24-hour 1950s-style coffee shop, and 24-hour room service.

The resort's Tower Shops mall includes fast-food choices and retail shops offering such items as jewelry, artwork, resort wear, flowers, souvenirs, sunglasses, and perfume. Valet parking and self parking are both complimentary in a secured garage with in/out privileges. Other amenities include a computer station, concierge services, luggage storage, business services, limo service and a dry cleaning/laundry service.

Where to Eat




Top of the World
- Located more than 800 feet above Las Vegas, this fine dining restaurant features culinary delights served in an elegant atmosphere with unparalleled views of the city as the restaurant revolves 360 degrees every 80 minutes. Lunch daily from 11:00 AM – 4:00PM and dinner nightly from 4:00 PM – 11:00 PM.


McCall’s Heartland Grill
– Experience the thrill of the grill! We’ve taken the best of the Midwest and added a touch of the coast. Open Daily from 11:00 AM – 11:00 PM.


Fellini’s Ristorante
- For intimate ambiance and authentic Italian cuisine, there’s no place like Fellini’s Ristorante Italiano. Open Sunday –Thursday 5:00 PM– 11:00 PM and Saturday 5:00 PM – 12:00 AM.


Crafted Buffet
- Try plate after plate of global cuisine, including Pan-Asian, Southwestern, American comfort foods, regional European flavors, the carving station, a deli display, omelets made to order and freshly baked pastries. Open Mon-Thurs 7:00 AM – 2:00PM, Friday 7:00 PM – 4:00 PM, Champagne Brunch Saturday and Sunday 7:00 AM – 4:00 PM and Dinner is nightly from 4:00 PM – 10:00 PM.


Roxy’s Diner
– Take a trip back in time at Roxy’s, where a singing wait staff and all-American food add up to a rocking and rolling good time. This Las Vegas diner with a twist serves a wide selection of mouthwatering burgers, sandwiches and shakes. Open 24 hours.

El Nopal
- This independently operated Mexican restaurant is open for lunch and dinner.


Level 8 Pool Bar (seasonal)
- Savor the Southwest as you cool off at the Level 8 Pool Bar. Treat yourself to Baja style dining, including wraps, salads and featured drink specials poolside.


Tower Pizzeria
- This restaurant features pizza, fresh salads, and sandwiches on warm, fresh-baked bread.


Fast Food
- Fast-food options in the hotel’s shopping promenade include McDonald’s, Chicago Hot Dog Construction Company , Starbucks, Mookies Ice Cream, Tower Creamery, WOK Asian & BBQ , and Fat Tuesday’s frozen-drink bar.


107 Sky Lounge
– Level 107 Lounge serves up an extensive appetizer, dessert, and cocktail menu in a Las Vegas hotel lounge atmosphere. Open 4:00 PM to 4 AM daily.

Entertainment




AirBar
- Guests can enjoy cocktails or beer at the highest bar in Las Vegas offering spectacular views of Sin City - Day or Night. Features the signature drink Jet Fuel. Air Bar is Open Sun - Thurs from 10:00 AM - 1:00 AM, Fri - Sat from 11:00 AM - 2:00 AM

C Bar
- The circular bar in the heart of our Las Vegas casino keeps the action flowing with drink specials, music videos, and video poker at the bar. C Bar operates 24/7

Images Lounge
- Sit back, relax, and get to know someone while taking in the game on one of the HD Televisions. The lounge is open nightly on the casino level. Live entertainment is Thursday- Monday, 10:00 PM - 3:00 AM

107 Sky Lounge
- 107 Sky Lounge serves up an extensive appetizer, dessert, and cocktail menu in a Las Vegas hotel lounge atmosphere. It is located on the 107th floor of the Stratosphere Tower. Open Daily from 4:00 PM - 4:00 AM.

The World's Greatest Rock Show
- Celebrating some of the most beloved classic rock bands of all time. Las Vegas' most talented tribute artists deliver breathtaking performances.
Relive classic anthems from The Rolling Stones, KISS, Bon Jovi, Bruce Springsteen, Journey, Guns N' Roses, AC/DC, Aerosmith, and more.

MJ Live
- Showcasing the pure joy of this legendary superstar and his music including "BAD," "Billie Jean," "Beat It," "Dangerous," "Smooth Criminal," "Black & White," "Thriller," "Don't Stop Till You Get Enough," and many more. Features live band and dancers.

L.A. Comedy Club
- L.A Comedy Club has been serving Vegas tourists and locals since 2007, and will showcase its newest home at the Stratosphere's 160 seat Dragon Room. The Comedy club won several awards and earned reputation of being "the young rebel brother of improv."

X-Scream
- X-Scream propels you and several other riders headfirst, 27 feet over the edge of the Stratosphere Casino, Hotel & Tower. After being shot over the edge, you'll dangle weightlessly above the Las Vegas Strip before being pulled back and propelled over again for more.

Big Shot
- Strap into the Big Shot and prepare to be shot 160 feet in the air at 45 miles per hour as you overlook the majestic Las Vegas Valley. In a matter of seconds, the Big Shot thrill ride catapults 16 riders from the 921-foot high platform up the Tower's mast to a height of 1,081 feet and down again.

SkyJump
- For your inner adrenaline junkie wanting the ultimate Las Vegas free fall, sky dive or base jump, SkyJump is the attraction to choose. It provides the highest controlled free fall in the world, all from the Stratosphere Casino, Hotel and Tower.

Insanity
- A massive mechanical arm extending out 64 feet over the edge of the Stratosphere Tower at a height of over 900 feet, this Vegas ride will spin you and several other passengers in the open air at speeds of up to three Gs.

Elation Pool (Seasonal)
- Located on the 8th floor of the Stratosphere, our newly remodeled resort style pool offers incredible views and bustling pool activity. Experience casual, open - air dining & drinking and spectacular cityscapes from the rooftop at Elation Poolside Bar & Cafe. Pool is open daily 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Radius Pool (Seasonal)
- Radius rises 25 stories above the world-famous Las Vegas Strip. Experience breathtaking, panoramic views of the valley and skyline while you enjoy the poolside scenery. Must be 21 or above in order to enter. Open Daily 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM

Nearby Things to Do




The outdoor eighth-floor pool complex includes a 155-foot-long freeform pool with a rock waterfall, an oversized spa tub, volleyball nets, and rental cabanas with TVs, ceiling fans, and padded lounges.

A daily resort fee includes use of an indoor/outdoor fitness center with free weights and cardio and strength-training machines.

The South Seas-themed Bali Hai Golf Club is 7.5 miles away, and the 18-hole courses at Desert Pines Golf Club and Royal Links Golf Club are both within 7 miles.

Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area, 26 miles away, provides rock climbing, biking and hiking trails, and scenic drives. The Lake Mead National Recreational Area, 30 miles away, offers fishing, boating, swimming, and hiking. Mount Charleston, 40 miles away, has biking and hiking trails, horseback riding, skiing, and snowboarding.

The recreational activities listed below are available either on site or near the hotel; fees may apply.
