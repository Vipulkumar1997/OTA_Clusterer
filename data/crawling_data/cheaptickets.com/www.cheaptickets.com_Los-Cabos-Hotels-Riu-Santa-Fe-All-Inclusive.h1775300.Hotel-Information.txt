With a stay at Riu Santa Fe All Inclusive in Cabo San Lucas, you'll be on the beach and convenient to Plaza San Lucas and Land's End.

This all-inclusive resort is within close proximity of Monuments Beach and Cabo San Lucas Country Club.

Make yourself at home in one of the 902 air-conditioned rooms featuring minibars. Rooms have private balconies or patios. Complimentary wireless Internet access keeps you connected, and satellite programming is available for your entertainment. Private bathrooms with showers feature complimentary toiletries and hair dryers.

Pamper yourself with a visit to the spa, which offers massages, body treatments, and facials. You're sure to appreciate the recreational amenities, which include 5 outdoor swimming pools, a nightclub, and a health club. Additional amenities at this resort include complimentary wireless Internet access, babysitting/childcare (surcharge), and gift shops/newsstands.

This resort is all inclusive. Rates include meals and beverages at onsite dining establishments. Charges may be applied for dining at some restaurants, special dinners and dishes, some beverages, and other amenities.
Satisfy your appetite at one of the resort's 5 restaurants. Thirsty? Quench your thirst at one of the 7 bars/lounges or 3 poolside bars.

Featured amenities include a business center, express check-in, and express check-out. Planning an event in Cabo San Lucas? This resort has 28594 square feet (2656.46 square meters) of space consisting of a conference center and meeting rooms. Free self parking is available onsite.

Resort Amenities




Riu Santa Fe All Inclusive sits on the beach at the southern end of the Baja California peninsula, in front of the famous Cabo San Lucas stone arches. Downtown Cabo San Lucas is 4.5 km (2.75 mi) away.

This Cabo San Lucas hotel features four restaurants serving Asian, Italian, and American cuisine. Both buffet and à la carte options are available, as well as complimentary breakfast buffet. Snacks and drinks are available 24 hours a day. A discotheque and a club with daily activities are also on site.

In addition to 5 outdoor pools, one designed especially for children, the Cabo San Lucas Resort also has an outdoor tennis court. The swim-up pool bars serve drinks to swimmers and sun-worshippers.

Saunas, massages and beauty services are available on site. Guests can take advantage of wireless Internet access throughout the hotel. The hotel also has three fully equipped meeting rooms (covering an area of 2656.46 square meters), banqueting facilities and a kids' club with supervised activities for children.
