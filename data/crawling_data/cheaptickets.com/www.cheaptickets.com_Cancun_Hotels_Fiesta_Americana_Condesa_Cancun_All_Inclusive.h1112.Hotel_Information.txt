With a stay at Fiesta Americana Condesa Cancun All Inclusive in Cancun (Avenida Kukulkan), you'll be minutes from Aquaworld and close to Delfines Beach.

This 4-star resort is within close proximity of Maya Cancun Museum and Ballenas Beach.

Make yourself at home in one of the 502 guestrooms featuring minibars and LCD televisions. Rooms have private balconies. Complimentary wireless Internet access keeps you connected, and satellite programming is available for your entertainment. Private bathrooms with showers feature complimentary toiletries and hair dryers.

Pamper yourself with a visit to the spa, which offers body treatments and facials. You can take advantage of recreational amenities such as a health club, an outdoor pool, and a spa tub. Additional amenities at this resort include complimentary wireless Internet access, concierge services, and babysitting/childcare (surcharge).

This resort is all inclusive. Rates include meals and beverages at onsite dining establishments. Charges may be applied for dining at some restaurants, special dinners and dishes, some beverages, and other amenities.
Enjoy a meal at one of the resort's dining establishments, which include 4 restaurants and a coffee shop/café. From your room, you can also access 24-hour room service. Thirsty? Quench your thirst at a bar/lounge, a beach bar, or a poolside bar.

Featured amenities include a business center, complimentary newspapers in the lobby, and dry cleaning/laundry services. Event facilities at this resort consist of conference space and a meeting room. Free valet parking is available onsite.

Resort Amenities




Fiesta Americana Condesa Cancun All Inclusive is centrally located in Cancun, where guests can enjoy snorkeling and diving at the largest barrier reef in the Western hemisphere, or day trips exploring the nearby Mayan ruins.

Amenities at this beachfront resort include a pro shop, car-rental service, shopping arcade, beauty salon, jewelry store, luggage storage, babysitting services (surcharge), tours/ticket assistance, creche (nursery) and medical assistance (surcharges may apply). Like all major Cancun hotels, the Fiesta Americana Condesa Cancun is equipped with a water-purification system.

The Fiesta Americana Condesa offers 3,144 square meters (34,000 square feet) of event space, including a conference center and 10 meeting rooms ideal for business and social functions, and also offers a technology support staff. Event catering and banquet facilities are available.

The hotel, inspired by the grand architecture and gracious hospitality of Mexico, offers 24-hour Mexican and International restaurants offering a variety of menus and culinary options.

Fiesta Americana Condesa Cancun All Inclusive also offers an arcade/game room, billiards/pool table and snack bar/deli, beach umbrellas, and a garden. Families will have fun during the day participating in sand castle competitions, beach games, volleyball, water polo, and basketball. Other activities include kite flying, boat building, cooking courses, cocktail-mixing classes, yoga lessons, and Mayan language courses. At night, guests can experience tequila tasting, dance to live music or learn Mayan astrology, while children can spend unforgettable moments in the Club Fiesta Kids (for children 4 to 12 years of age) or the Fiesta Teens Club, which offers video games, pool tables, ping pong and hockey, as well as music, film and non-alcoholic drinks and snacks.

Parking during the guest's stay is complimentary, and there is also valet parking available.

Fiesta Americana Condesa Cancun All Inclusive is a smoke-free property.

Where to Eat




Restaurante Kambù
- Family-friendly buffet restaurant serving American breakfasts, and international cuisine for lunch and dinner. Guests enjoy Italian, Asian, Mexican, and American cuisine. Located next to the pool, in a Mayan open-air palapa overlooking the Caribbean shore. Casual attire is recommended.


Kalmia Restaurant
- Restaurant with a casual ambience serving regional and international cuisine for breakfast, lunch, and dinner daily. Large windows offer panoramic views of the Caribbean. Service hours are from 6:30 AM to 10:30 PM. Casual clothing is suggested.


Rosato Restaurant
- Serves Italian cuisine, including antipasti, salads, soups, pastas, pizzas, and desserts. Open for dinner. Service begins at 5:30 PM and children aged 5 to 12 years old are welcome until 7 PM. From 7 PM to 9 PM, children under 13 are not permitted. Casually elegant attire is recommended.


Mexicano Restaurant
- Serves traditional Mexican dishes, and a variety of wine and tequilas. Contemporary decor includes amber-colored stained-glass windows and glass cases filled with regional art, blown glass, and traditional talavera pottery. Open daily for breakfast and dinner. Casually elegant attire is recommended.

Cevichería
- Every afternoon, guests will find fresh ceviches (raw fish marinated in lime and spices), snacks and other dishes accompanied by the beat of salsa and merengue music. Open from noon until late afternoon.

Tapas Bar
- Offers hot and cold tapas. Casual and modern Spanish ambience, and international music. Open nightly from 5:30 PM until 2 AM. Casual attire.


Café Los Naranjos

Nearby Things to Do




A pool complex features a 50-meter-long (165-ft) freeform upper pool cascading into a freeform lower pool that meanders 122 meters (400 ft) and includes a shallow children's section. Adjacent to the upper pool, a spa tub accommodates 10. Fiesta Americana Condesa Cancun's beach frontage comprises 274 meters (300 yards) of Cancún's powdery white sand.

An independent water sports center, AquaWorld, near the beach rents boogie boards, arranges parasailing flights, and conducts a beginners' scuba-diving class (surcharge) in the resort's pool. AquaWorld's main center is a marina located across the street from the resort, 200 meters (219 yards) away on Nichupté Lagoon, where a variety of water sports are available, including kayaking, personal watercraft, sailing, and windsurfing. AquaWorld also books fishing trips and scuba diving and snorkeling excursions.

The 18-hole Hilton golf course is 1 km (0.75 mi) from the resort, and the 18-hole Club de Golf Cancún is 9 km (5.75 mi).

The recreational activities listed below are available either on site or near the resort; fees may apply.
