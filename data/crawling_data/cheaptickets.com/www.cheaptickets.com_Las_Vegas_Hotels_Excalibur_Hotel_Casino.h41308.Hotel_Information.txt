Located in Las Vegas (Paradise), Excalibur Hotel Casino is minutes from Park Theater and close to T-Mobile Arena.

This casino resort is within close proximity of Las Vegas Convention Center.

Make yourself at home in one of the air-conditioned guestrooms. Your room comes with a pillowtop bed. Wireless Internet access (surcharge) is available to keep you connected. Private bathrooms with showers feature complimentary toiletries and hair dryers.

Relax at the full-service spa, where you can enjoy massages, body treatments, and facials. Before a night out at the casino, the 2 outdoor swimming pools are a perfect way to relax and recharge. This resort also features wireless Internet access (surcharge), concierge services, and an arcade/game room.

Grab a bite to eat at one of the resort's 15 restaurants and 2 coffee shops/cafés, or stay in and take advantage of room service (during limited hours). Relax with a refreshing drink at one of the 3 bars/lounges.

Featured amenities include a business center, dry cleaning/laundry services, and a 24-hour front desk. Self parking (subject to charges) is available onsite.

Hotel Amenities




In addition to the renowned Tournament of Kings dinner, Excalibur Hotel Casino is home to The Steakhouse at Camelot, Dick's Last Resort, Buca di Beppo, Baja Fresh, and Drenched, the poolside restaurant. It makes a great place to eat well and relax as well as get in some gaming action at the casino.

The Royal Treatment Spa includes relaxation lounges, saunas, steam rooms, and spa tubs. Treatments include the Loofah Scrub, Eucalyptus Steam Wrap, Aveda Refresher Facial, and Aveda Therapy Massage. The spa's fitness center overlooks the pools. Use of spa facilities is available for a daily charge or complimentary with a treatment.

Where to Eat




The Steakhouse at Camelot
- This contemporary steakhouse serves prime-grade beef and fresh seafood for dinner. Features include subdued lighting, a glassed-in show kitchen, wine/cigar room, piano lounge.


Dick's Last Resort
- Burgers, barbecue, seafood, and more are served in buckets and accompanied with bibs and hats. There is also live music. It is open daily for lunch and dinner.

Drenched With
- Casual poolside dining featuring American fare and a full-service bar. Open seasonally for breakfast and lunch. Satisfy your hunger and indulge in your favorite foods including juicy hamburgers, cheesy pizza, and crisp waffle fries.

Castle Walk Food Court
- Dining includes Krispy Kreme, McDonalds, Pick Up Stix, Popeyes Chicken, Pizza Hut Express, Schlotzsky's Deli, Starbucks, Auntie Anne's Pretzels, Cinnabon, Hot Dog on a Stick, Tropical Smoothie Cafe, and Big Chill. It is open 24 hours.


Buca di Beppo
- Delicious entrees, pastas, salads, pizzas and desserts are all served family style with portions that each feed 2 to 6 people.

Octane Lounge
- Located outside the Thunder from Down Under Theatre, offers drinks and music. Must be 21 or older.

The Lounge
- Located on the casino level, offers drinks and live entertainment and music. Must be 21 or older.

Entertainment




The medieval-style
Tournament of Kings
dinner show (no dining utensils) includes jousting, horseback-riding skill demonstrations, hand-to-hand combat, beasts, fire, and pageantry.

Six or seven nights weekly, Australia's all-male strip revue
Thunder from Down Under
performs in its own theater. (Guests must be 18 or older to attend. Those 18–20 must be accompanied by an adult).

The Australian Bee Gees Show
, A Tribute to the Bee Gees, has become the world's leading Bee Gees show. The group’s unbelievable resemblance and sound of the legendary Bee Gees has garnered international kudos from media and showgoers alike. The 75-minute multi-media concert event is presented from a contemporary perspective with state-of-the-art sound, superb lighting, and giant screens featuring video clips, live camera images, and stunning graphics.

Fun Dungeon
- The world-famous Excalibur arcade, where guests can save the day on one of the latest arcade games. The Fun Dungeon offers over 150 games, including 16 Carnival Midways, 19 prize redemption games, 65 arcade games, 10 sports games and 83 redemption games!

The 100,000-square-foot
casino
includes a poker room and a race-and-sports book.

Union Pay

Vehicles that are self-parked for more than one hour are subject to a parking fee. The self-parking fee is USD 5 for vehicles parked 1-2 hours, USD 8 for vehicles parked 2-4 hours, and USD 10 for vehicles parked 4-24 hours. The credit or debit card used to place the reservation will be required upon arrival to register. The hotel will place an authorization against this account to cover applicable charges for room, tax, and resort fees, as well as an incidental amount per day to allow access to room charging privileges. Additional authorizations may be placed should the guest's charges exceed the originally approved amount. For debit cards, unused funds are released upon check-out and made available after up to 7 days for domestic banks and up to 30 days for international banks. For details, please contact the office using the contact information found on the confirmation received after booking.
