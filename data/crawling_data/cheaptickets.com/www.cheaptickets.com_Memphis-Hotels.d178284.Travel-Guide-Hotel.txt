Memphis hotels
The home of the famous Memphis Rock 'n' Soul Museum, the city of Memphis has a strong blues music culture that visitors will have a chance to experience. Memphis also has a number of lodging options for tourists to consider. Memphis hotels consist of locally owned inns, four-star hotels, and comfortable budget inns. Travelers will also have the option of making room reservations at a comfortable bed and breakfast lodge. Moreover, Memphis has many entertainment options to choose from.
Memphis Quick Picks and Tips
Conventions
Visitors can attend major events held at the West Memphis Convention Center and Memphis Cook Convention Center. Notable Memphis Cook Convention Center attractions include the Dub Magazine Car Show & Concert and the Memphis Gift and Jewelry Show.
Dining
Memphis is known for its BBQ and tasty Southern dishes. Visitors will not have to worry about a lack of dining options. Memphis has restaurants throughout the area that serve everything from French dishes to Thai food. Quality restaurants in the city are Texas De Brazil, The Arcade, and Huey's.
Major Point of Interest
Memphis hotel guests should not leave the city without checking out the famous Graceland home, which was once the residence of the late Elvis Presley. A trip to this classic landmark will provide a memorable experience.
Shopping
Tourists can get their shop on at either the Collierville Town Center or Overton Square.
Sports
Sports enthusiasts with hotel reservations in Memphis will enjoy experiencing a University of Memphis or Memphis Grizzlies basketball game.
