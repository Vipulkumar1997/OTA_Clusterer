A stay at Monte Carlo Resort and Casino places you in the heart of Las Vegas, steps from Park Theater and close to T-Mobile Arena.

This casino resort is within close proximity of Bellagio Conservatory & Botanical Gardens and Las Vegas Convention Center.

Make yourself at home in one of the air-conditioned rooms featuring minibars and LCD televisions. Your room comes with a pillowtop bed. Wireless Internet access (surcharge) keeps you connected, and cable programming is available for your entertainment. Private bathrooms with showers feature complimentary toiletries and hair dryers.

Head straight for the casino, or wait for that lucky feeling while you enjoy one of the other recreational opportunities, such as a 24-hour fitness center. Additional features at this resort include wireless Internet access (surcharge), concierge services, and gift shops/newsstands.

Grab a bite to eat at one of the resort's 3 restaurants and 2 coffee shops/cafés, or stay in and take advantage of room service (during limited hours).

Featured amenities include a business center, limo/town car service, and express check-in. Planning an event in Las Vegas? This resort has 28,000 square feet (3 square meters) of space consisting of conference space and meeting rooms. Self parking (subject to charges) is available onsite.

Hotel Amenities




Monte Carlo Resort and Casino is located on the famous Las Vegas Strip, a hub for fine dining, shopping, and unparalleled entertainment.

The 102,000-square-foot casino is open 24 hours. It features high-limit slots and a table games lounge,
race and sports book, and over 1,500 slot machines.

Other amenities include WiFi, luggage storage, and concierge services. Business services are available at the Business Center. Room service is also available during limited hours.

Please note that all guestrooms are non-smoking, and guests found to be smoking in rooms are subject to a fee.

Where to Eat




Double Barrel
- A new take on the American pub experience can be found at Double Barrel Roadhouse. Serving up pub fare like the Chicken & Waffle Sandwich and Hangover Burger to re-imagined favorites like Pig in a Blanket, Double Barrel redefines traditional favorites. Enjoy live entertainment on the weekends and strip-side views while enjoying the selection of draft beers and hand-crafted cocktails. Whether you are meeting up with friends to watch sports, grabbing a bite to eat, or just hanging out, Double Barrel has something for everyone

Primrose
- Evoking the atmosphere of a country home in southern France, Primrose serves garden-inspired dishes and cocktails in its drawing room or dining room, or in its leafy outdoor terrace and garden.

Bavette’s Steakhouse & Bar
- An acclaimed Chicago classic has arrived in Las Vegas. A stylish departure from a traditional steakhouse, Bavette’s embraces French flair without the formality, blending fine dining with unabashed fun. Indulge in timeless steakhouse fare, including richly flavorful ribeyes, fresh seafood towers, traditional side dishes, and house made desserts. Strong cocktails and a well-curated international wine list enhance the gilded space which features red leather banquettes, glowing chandeliers, and lively jazz.

Bed type is based upon availability for the Deluxe Room and Deluxe Room (Strip View) category and will be assigned at check-in. For more details, please contact the office using the information on the reservation confirmation received after booking.

Vehicles that are self-parked for more than one hour are subject to a parking fee. The self-parking fee is USD 7 for vehicles parked 1-2 hours, USD 10 for 2-4 hours, and USD 12 for 4-24 hours. The valet parking fee is USD 15 for 0-4 hours and USD 20 for 4-24 hours. All parking fees repeat each 24-hour period. The parking fee provides registered guests with in-out privileges within the same parking type (self or valet) across all MGM Resorts destinations. For details, please contact the office using the contact information found on the confirmation received after booking.

The credit or debit card used to place the reservation will be required upon arrival to register. The hotel will place an authorization against this account to cover applicable charges for room, tax, and resort fees, as well as an incidental amount per day to allow access to room charging privileges. Additional authorizations may be placed should the guest's charges exceed the originally approved amount. For debit cards, unused funds are released upon check-out and made available after up to 7 days for domestic banks and up to 30 days for international banks. For details, please contact the office using the contact information found on the confirmation received after booking.
