Miami hotels
Miami is renowned for "having a hotel for everyone," so what better way to enjoy this vibrant city than booking a stay in one of the many hotels available. Here you will find all manner of accommodations to suit your preferences, including discount, airport, and beach hotels in Miami. It is relatively easy to find a suitable hotel, whether on a business trip, a vacation or just passing through the city. It only takes a little effort to locate the best rooms available for a particular budget.
Aspen Quick Picks and Tips
Conventions
Surrounded by many quality Miami hotels, the Miami Convention Center is conveniently located in the center of downtown Miami. The auditorium seats up to 5,000 people, and is a pillar of sophistication and technology.
Dining
Miami is well known for its ethnic diversity, and the dining choices available reflect this. Here you can sample delicious steaks at Capital Grille in Downtown Miami, or sample some Italian delights at the award-winning Cafe Prima Pasta on Miami Beach.
Major Point of Interest
Miami has many interesting activities available for visitors, but it is undoubtedly more famed for its beautiful beaches. Soak up some sun on Virginia Key Beach or South Beach plus many others that are easy to reach from Miami hotels.
Shopping
The City of Miami is a shopping hotspot with many malls such as the Dolphin Mall near the airport and CocoWalk.
Sports
Sports are very popular in Miami. The city has several well-known professional sports teams, including the Miami Dolphins football team and Miami Heat basketball team.
