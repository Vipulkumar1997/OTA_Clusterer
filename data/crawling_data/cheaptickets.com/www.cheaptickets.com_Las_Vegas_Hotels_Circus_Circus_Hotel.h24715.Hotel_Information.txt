Located in Las Vegas, Circus Circus Hotel, Casino & Theme Park is on the Strip and convenient to Las Vegas Convention Center and Fashion Show Mall.

This casino resort is within close proximity of Las Vegas Premium Outlets and T-Mobile Arena.

Make yourself at home in one of the 3773 air-conditioned guestrooms. Wireless Internet access (surcharge) keeps you connected, and cable programming is available for your entertainment. Private bathrooms with shower/tub combinations feature complimentary toiletries and hair dryers. Conveniences include safes and irons/ironing boards, and housekeeping is provided daily.

Try your luck at the casino or dip into one of the 2 outdoor swimming pools. This resort also features wireless Internet access (surcharge), concierge services, and an arcade/game room.

Enjoy a meal at one of the resort's dining establishments, which include 5 restaurants and a coffee shop/café. From your room, you can also access room service (during limited hours). Relax with a refreshing drink from a poolside bar or one of the 6 bars/lounges.

Featured amenities include a 24-hour business center, express check-in, and express check-out. Planning an event in Las Vegas? This resort has 21001 square feet (1951 square meters) of space consisting of a conference center and meeting rooms. Free self parking is available onsite.

Hotel Amenities


Hotel Amenities




Circus Circus Hotel, Casino & Theme Park features 3,773 rooms with unique entertainment options for all ages including: complimentary world-class circus acts performing daily, carnival and video games on the Midway, and The Adventuredome, a large indoor theme park.

A 101,000-square-foot (9,383-square-meter) casino offers slots and video machines, table games, poker room, and newly remodeled Race & Sports Book. Casino patrons must be at least 21 years of age.

Ten on-site dining options range from an award-winning steakhouse, THE Steak House, to casual dining, buffets, and on-the-go eats. All-you-can-eat specials are available throughout the property.

Meeting and event space accommodates 600 guests, and a wedding chapel is on site.

The hotel also offers 2 outdoor pools, 2 spa tubs, and a fitness center.

Please note that all guestrooms are non-smoking, and guests who are found to be smoking inside the rooms are subject to a fee. Accessibility hearing equipment is available.

Where to Eat





Vince Neil's Eat*Drink*Party
- The mouth-watering menu offers up Vince Neil's signature Tatuado burgers, burritos and more to tantalize your taste buds. The dining room bar allows you to pair your meal with a cold beer, a lavish cocktail or one of the rocker's very own premium brands. The party bus entertains with some of the world's best flair bartenders.


THE Steakhouse
- The award-winning restaurant features mesquite-grilled steaks, prime rib, lobster and crab legs in a traditional steakhouse setting.


Pizzeria
- Piping hot, freshly baked pizzas made to order with your favorite toppings, to calzones, salads and sandwiches. The Pizzeria offers dine-in, take-out, and in-room delivery.


Westside Deli
- Open 24 hours a day, this spot serves fresh made-to-order sandwiches and other deli favorites.


McDonald's
- Fast food is served for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.


Krispy Kreme Doughnuts
- Coffee and doughnuts are served 24 hours a day.


Blue Iguana Mexican Express
- Blue Iguana offers quick, fresh, authentic Mexican cuisine. From tacos and burritos to enchiladas and nachos. There is something for everyone!

Entertainment




Experience one of the world's largest permanent circuses, showcasing free world-class acts. Aerialists, trapeze artists, acrobats, and jugglers perform daily on the Midway level. There is no cover charge.


The Adventuredome
features 25 rides and attractions including the Canyon Blaster, a double-loop roller coaster; Sling Shot; Inverter; and Rim Runner water ride. Junior and family rides include a balloon Ferris wheel, carousel, train ride, and bumper cars. The 4D theater shows "Ice Age: No Time For Nuts 4-D" and "Sponge Bob Square Pants 4D." Admission into the park is complimentary. Ride tickets and all day ride passes are available. Clown shows are complimentary.


Circus Circus Midway
has 200 carnival and classic games including an arcade. Tickets may be redeemed for premium prizes.


Casino
features include 2,200 slot and video machines, and 88 table games including the Rockin' Party Pit that pays double on Blackjack. A newly remodeled Race & Sports Book with 32 high-definition TVs and modern furnishings is on site. Casino patrons must be at least 21 years old.


Vince Neil's Eat*Drink*Party
offers free nightly entertainment and features flair bartending shows.

Nearby Things to Do




The hotel features 2 outdoor pools and 2 spa tubs. A 160-foot (49-meter), arc-shaped outdoor pool and oversized spa tub are open year-round. A 75-foot (23-meter), kidney-shaped pool and spa tub are open seasonally. Guests can also take advantage of a 24-hour fitness facility.

The South Seas-themed Bali Hai Golf Club is 7 miles (11.3 kilometers) away, and the 18-hole courses at Desert Pines Golf Club and Royal Links Golf Club are both within 8 miles (13 kilometers).

Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area, 25 miles (40 kilometers) away, provides rock climbing and scenic drives. Mount Charleston, 40 miles (64 kilometers) away, has horseback riding, skiing, and snowboarding.

The recreational activities listed below are available either on site or near the hotel; fees may apply.
