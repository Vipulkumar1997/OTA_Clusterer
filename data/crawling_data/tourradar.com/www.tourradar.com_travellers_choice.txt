Travellers' Choice
The world's best tour operators as chosen by travellers.
The Travellers' Choice recognition badge is awarded to tour operators with the best overall feedback from the TourRadar community. These prestigious statuses recognise operators that are leading the way in creating outstanding travel experiences.
Badges are awarded acrosss three categories
The best-of-the-best tour operators; consistent ratings above 4.5 stars and reviews present on majority of tours listed.
Extremely high-performing tour operators; consistent ratings above 4 stars and reviews present on at least 20% of tours listed.
The tour operators that consistently receive excellent feedback for a specific festival; operator has received overall highest rated and most consistent feedback.
