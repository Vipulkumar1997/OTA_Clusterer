Highlights
Experience vibrant street life and observe the rainbow culture of Johannesburg’s sprawling Soweto Township.
Spy on elephants drinking at waterholes, lions lazing under trees and cheetahs frolicking across the hot savanna at the Kruger National Park.
Keep an eye out for snapping crocodiles in the Chobe River, and watch herds of marching elephants in the wild and carefree environs of the Chobe National Park, in Botswana.
Drift along the intricate waterways of the Okavango Delta, explore the surrounding ecosystem and listen to the raw symphony of the African bush, in Botswana.
Take survival lessons from San Bushmen in the harsh and inhospitable terrains of Botswana’s Kalahari Desert.
Test your nerves as you bungee jump to the resounding roar of the mighty Victoria Falls, in Zambia.
Observe intriguing formations of gigantic granite boulders in the weathered rock art galleries of Bulawayo, Zimbabwe.
Listen to the gentle sounds of rolling sand dunes and come face to face with hyenas, oryxes and gemsbok in the red desert landscape of the Namib Naukluft National Park.
Tips
Relax by the sea and taste German inspired cuisine in the restaurants of Swakopmund, Namibia.
Sample high quality South African wines, from silky pinots and creamy chardonnays, to chenin blancs and crisp sauvignons.
Buy colourful Zulu necklaces and bracelets in South Africa.
Drink the refreshing red hued Rooibos tea available in all southern African countries.
Purchase paper products such as envelopes and handmade cards made from elephant dung in Botswana.
Observe rare white rhinos at the Matopos National Park in Zimbabwe.
Enjoy unforgettable views of Victoria Falls from inside a helicopter.
Taste Zimbabwean Nhedzi soup (mushroom soup) and wash it down with some locally brewed Zambezi beer.
