Itinerary

Introduction
Expand all
Day 1: Arrive Seville
Day 2: Using the network of cycle track head north-east to reach the historic walled 
town of Carmona
Day 3: Enjoy a flat day of cycling through small traditional towns towards 
Montellano
Day 4: Pedal along quiet country roads and the 'Via Verde' cycle lane with the 
mountains of Grazalema as your backdrop
Day 5: Follow the river Guadalete through Mediterranean woodland and agricultural 
land, on undulating terrain
Day 6: Reach the sea today and the ancient city of Cadiz, thought to be the oldest 
city in Western Europe
Day 7: End Cadiz
