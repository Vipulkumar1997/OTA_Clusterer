Charming French Canada featuring Montréal, Quebec City, Charlevoix and Montebello (Montréal, QC to Montebello, QC)
San Vino (2 nights)
Baltic March 2018 tours
Highlights
Pedal from one romantic chateaux to another, step inside medieval castles, and explore rustic markets in the gorgeous Loire Valley, in France.
Explore maze-like alleyways, stroll in the shade of beautiful gardens and hit seaside tapas bars at night during your stay in sophisticated Barcelona.
Breathe in crisp Alpine air, stay in charming mountain villages surrounded by pine forests, and feast on chocolate fondue at Switzerland’s Jungfrau alpine region.
Be transported to an ancient era of chariots and gladiators in the historic city centre of Rome.
Enjoy a languid cruise along the Rhine River, with stops at fairytale castles, picturesque vineyards and charming Gothic villages.
Discover the cosiness of Amsterdam’s coffee shops, admire paintings of Renaissance masters and explore the notorious passageways of the Red Light District.
Enjoy the mouth-watering aroma of Belgian waffles, moules frites and Belgian beer in chic Brussels.
Explore magical Tyrolean villages and go skiing on the freshly powdered pistes of the Austrian Alps.
Tips
A guided tour will help you explore the baroque exquisiteness of Vienna’s grand palaces and cathedrals.
You can shop for discounted diamonds and designer and vintage fashion in Brussels and Antwerp.
The Dutch landscape is a photographer’s paradise, as it is sprinkled with colourful tulip fields, windmills and dairy farms in villages such as Edam, Leiden and Haarlem.
Learn a few German phrases such as ‘Bitte’ for ‘Please’, and ‘Danke’ for ‘Thank you’.
Try some regional Italian cuisine such as Bolognese pasta, Neapolitan pizza and Roman offal.
Look for embroidered linen, Swiss army knives, wood carvings and handmade clocks in Switzerland.
Follow the popular Camino de Santiago walking trail, which leads to the resting place of St. James in Spain.
Experience Parisian café culture and indulge in the popular activity of people watching.
