India



Embrace Ayurveda, the traditional Indian medicine, and find your natural balance. Discover how to balance your mind, body and soul in India on an Ayurvedic retreat. Therapies and programmes will be based on your dosha. Treatments use herbs and oils to treat your dosha’s and combined with breathing techniques, yoga and meditation you will come away feeling better and being able to achieve and maintain good health. Spas in India also offer pamering treatments and accommodations vary from basic to luxurious.

Please note:
India now has an e-visa facility for UK nationals, enabling visitors to gain entry to the country via 16 airports including: Ahmedabad, Amritsar, Gaya, Jaipur, Lucknow, Tirchy, Varanasi, Bangalore, Chennai, Cochin, Delhi, Goa, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Mumbai and Trivandrum.
Important to know
: this is not visa on arrival, it is an e-visa and visitors need to apply online before their travel to India. Please refer to this
