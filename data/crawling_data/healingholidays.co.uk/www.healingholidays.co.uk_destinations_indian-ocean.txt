Regions of  Indian Ocean



If you seek the ultimate island escape, somewhere you can go to forget your cares and simply put all your focus into relaxing, a spa holiday to one of the Indian Ocean islands is a must. Relaxation and pampering are the name of the game here, you will find all manner of spas ready and waiting to spoil you with indulgent treatments that will see any stress instantly melt away and have you looking and feeling much healthier and happier in no time at all.

The destination has to be one of the biggest attractions of any Indian Ocean spa. Think pristine white sandy shores, sparkling turquoise waters, and glorious year-round sunshine and you will begin to get an idea as to why so many of the world’s rich and famous choose to frequent these incredible spas. Indulge in the ultimate relaxing massage in a treatment room that looks out across the stunning horizon, attend a yoga session on the beach, and enjoy countless other unique experiences that are sure to make this your most relaxing spa holiday yet.

Perfect for those who might be heading on their honeymoon or even for a family holiday, many of the top spas in the Indian Ocean Islands tend to be set within lavish resorts that offer plenty of facilities and activities that will keep everyone entertained. These spas truly offer something for everyone, the combination of the fantastic spa treatments, luxurious resorts, and of course the beautiful location will make you feel as if you have drifted away into paradise. So whether you are heading solely for a spa break or as part of a more general holiday, the spas here will not disappoint.
