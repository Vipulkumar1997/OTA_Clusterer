Portugal



Recognised for its expansive sandy beaches and cliffs, stunning views along the coast, friendly hospitality, and historical monuments, Portugal is an endlessly fascinating country. The perfect destination to relax, rejuvenate and to be warmed from the inside out. Portugal offers a range of spa experiences from pampering retreats where you can relax with massages and a glass of wine by the pool, or alternatively opt for stricter weight-loss, detox or stress management programmes. Expect to find the top fitness retreats that will enable you to enjoy the outdoors in the sunshine, hiking around and exploring the countryside and beach activities. Then to recover immerse yourself in our serious medical spas recognised for the best thalassotherapy treatments utilising the natural healing powers of sea water.

Our offer of wellness holidays in Portugal will help you to adapt and lead a healthier lifestyle.
Speak
to our wellness consultants, they will advise on the best retreats that are right for you.
