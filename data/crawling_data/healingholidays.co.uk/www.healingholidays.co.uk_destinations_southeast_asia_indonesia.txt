Indonesia



The Indonesian archipelago contains over 17,000 islands with only 922 that are officially inhabited. They boast beautiful beaches and a warm climate and are the perfect backdrop for a spa holiday. For spas Bali is extremely popular, there's a large array of resort and destination spas to choose from. Spa treatments and programmes in Bali range from relaxation and pampering to cleansing detoxes, stress management, yoga and fitness. Be renewed and balanced in the beautiful surroundings as you learn about the Balinese way of life and learn to incorporate these philosophies into your life back home.
