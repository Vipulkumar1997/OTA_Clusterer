Cyprus



The warm Mediterranean climate and glistening sea will do wonders for your wellbeing as soon as you step off the plane, but Cyprus also offers an array of great resort spas for a perfectly relaxing retreat. The spas offer luxurious pampering treatments and Thalassotherapy, with a holistic philosophy. Cyprus is a great family destination with the child-friendly resorts offering great kids clubs and activities allowing parents to take advantage of some time out to unwind with an indulgent spa treatment. However, Cyprus is also a great destination for couples and singles and you need not worry about being overrun by kids. For fitness fanatics there are sport activities and facilities readily available at many of the resorts




Spas in Cyprus









Almyra


Nestled on the coastline of beautiful Paphos in Cyrpus sits the sleek, chic and supremely modern Almyra.
