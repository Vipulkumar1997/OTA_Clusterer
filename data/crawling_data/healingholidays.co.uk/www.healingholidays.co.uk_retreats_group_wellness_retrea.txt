Group Wellness Retreats



Relax, Unwind, Find Peace of Mind and enjoy a wellness retreat.

Group Retreats are holidays specialised on a specific wellness theme such as yoga, detox, ayurveda, meditation, weight-loss or fitness. The suggested health holidays take place in our selected luxurious spas on set dates during the year and can be booked by single travellers or by groups. You will also find wellness specialist retreats that are led by unique visiting specialists who embody their highest skills, talent or wisdom in their area of expertise and share it with the retreat participants.

Whatever you prefer a group retreat or are looking for a perfect wellness retreat, contact us for more information on
020 7843 3597
or email
