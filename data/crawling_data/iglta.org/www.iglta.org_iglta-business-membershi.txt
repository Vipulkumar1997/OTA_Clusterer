IGLTA Membership Application



On a global scale, LGBT travelers are using IGLTA as a resource to find businesses that welcome gays and lesbians into the world of travel. Make it easy. Join us to be part of a global gay friendly tourism community that includes major corporations, as well as many localized businesses and organizations.

Membership is open to any individual, association, corporation or partnership directly or indirectly involved with the LGBT travel industry. It's a big market and this is a big world. Together we can help LGBT travelers see more of it.




Benefits of IGLTA Membership





Annual Global Convention

Discounted attendance to our Annual Global Convention, the premier global educational and networking event for the LGBT tourism industry.






Buyer/Supplier Marketplace

Pre-qualified buyers will meet with participating suppliers at an appointment-driven marketplace during the Annual Global Convention.






Post Events and Tours

Ability to post events and tours to the IGLTA website.








Trade Shows

Access to booth share and brochure distribution opportunities at tourism and consumer trade shows worldwide.






Market to Thousands

Ability to market deals, discounts, and giveaways to thousands of LGBT travelers through our website.






Online Profile

Dynamic profile on the IGLTA website with business descriptions, photos and social media links.








Discounted Distribution Service

Discounts on TravelOutNewsWire, a news release distribution service that reaches more than 315 global journalists and media outlets covering LGBT travel.






Marketing Tools

Direct marketing tools to target travel professionals and/or consumers, such as e-blasts, weekly enewsletter sponsorships and web banners.






Discounted Advertising Rates

Discounted advertising rates with select IGLTA Media Partners








Global Support

Global Network of IGLTA ambassadors offering support.






TripAdvisor Traveler Reviews

IGLTA is now featuring traveler reviews from TripAdvisor. Opt-in to display reviews on member profile and link to TripAdvisor profile.






FAM Program Discount

Press FAM consulting program for destinations at a discounted rate.








Membership Subscriptions

Complimentary subscriptions to LGBT publications (based on region).






Use of IGLTA Logo

Use of the IGLTA logo in your marketing, recognized globally by LGBT travelers.







General Business Membership

Includes full voting privileges and the right to run for the board.

Who is Eligible?

Business membership is open to travel agents, tour operators and wholesalers, hoteliers, travel suppliers like airlines, car rentals, publishing firms, attractions, booking services, concierge services, cruise lines, CVB/tourism offices, gaming/casinos, ground transportation, meeting planners, associations, insurance, internet services, and marketing companies.

Register Online Now



*A one-time admin fee will be added to each new membership registration. (Fee is 100 USD, but discounted for travel agents and African, Asian, and Latin American businesses to 50 USD. No fee for Associate Members.)


Member dues are valid from the date of joining for 12 months. Upon the year's end, you must renew to stay a member of IGLTA. Please note that IGLTA, Inc. and its Board of Directors do not endorse or accept responsibility for any individual members, product or service.
