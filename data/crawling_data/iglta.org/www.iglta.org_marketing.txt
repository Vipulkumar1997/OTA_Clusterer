Advertise with IGLTA

IGLTA members have the ability to reach our growing number of LGBTQ  travel and tourism members and business members around the world, gay, lesbian and gay friendly. We currently have over 5700+ businesses contacts in over 75+ countries around the world.

Questions?  Contact our Membership Department at
membership@iglta.org
today!

IGLTA reserves the right to refuse any advertising piece(s).

Advertising Services



Email Blasts


We offer three different types of email blasts:

IGLTA Global Business Membership Email Blast:
These are sent to our business members who consist of buyers and suppliers.  We have 7000+ tourism business contacts in 80+ countries.

IGLTA Global Buyer Membership Email Blast:
These are sent to our travel agent and tour operator members in 80+ countries. Please email us for our current reach

Consumer Email Blast:
These are sent to our database of LGBTQ travelers and allies and given additional promotion via our Facebook and Twitter accounts. Please email us for our current reach.

- US $995/blast

US $550/blast

SPECIFICATIONS:
All creative material should be received at least two (2) weeks before distribution date for proper review. IGLTA only accepts HTML files that have all necessary coding within the <body> tag, is 600 pixels wide or less and houses all images on a public server (we do not host images/graphics).  We do not provide design services as part of our fees.

Payment is required in advance. To inquire about available dates, please contact
membership@iglta.org

Please note that nudity, sexually explicit content, adult material is prohibited.  IGLTA reserves the right to refuse any advertising piece.

Brochure Distribution


IGLTA staff and volunteers travel to events all over the world every week. We provide the opportunity for you to send your business' brochures to these events so we can display them whether you are able to attend or not. Some of these events are business-oriented such as ITB Berlin while others are consumer-oriented like pride festivals.

A listing of events that offer brochure distribution and ability to download forms is available on our
IGLTA Booth Events
page.

Pricing:
Pricing depends upon each event. Some events can be as low as USD $100. Pricing also depends on how many brochures you would like to send and how large your business is.

Specifications:
Any promotional brochure you would like to send is eligible, subject to IGLTA approval. We do not return left over brochures after the event.
