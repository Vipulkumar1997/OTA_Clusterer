An Evening Benefiting the IGLTA Foundation




​
Our annual IGLTA Foundation fundraising event soars to new heights in 2018 as
Voyage
, a cocktail reception and networking dinner at Toronto’s Fairmont Royal York. Join IGLTAF, global tourism professionals, and other special guests for an inspiring journey to support LGBTQ tourism education, research and the next generation of industry leaders.


Ticket price includes open bar, buffet dinner and the
IGLTA Honors
awards ceremony, presented with the support of Visit Philadelphia, to celebrate those who have improved the global landscape of LGBTQ tourism.

Voyage takes place during the IGLTA 35th Anniversary Annual Global Convention in Toronto, an event that brings leaders in LGBTQ tourism worldwide
TOGETHER
for four days of education and networking.

Date:
Friday, 11 May 2018
Time:
6 pm - 10 pm
Location:
Fairmont Royal York,
100 Front St. W., Toronto M5J 1E3

Ticket Prices

$200 USD / $243 CAD*

Voyage Premier Ticket:
$500 USD / $607 CAD*
Includes General Admission, plus a VIP gift bag, special photo opportunity, and VIP contributor listing in the
IGLTA Annual Global Convention Journal
