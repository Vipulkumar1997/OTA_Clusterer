IGLTA Gay Pavilion at ITB BERLIN





One of the World’s Largest LGBTQ Travel-Trade Pavilions










7-11 MARCH 2018




Join
IGLTA
in the
Gay Pavilion
at ITB Berlin and be a part of one of the
World’s Leading Travel Trade Show®
. More than
11,000 exhibitors
from
180+ countries
come together at ITB every March to do business in Berlin.

Approximately
170,000 visitors
are expected to pass through the doors of the
26 exhibition halls
that comprise the Messe Berlin fairgrounds. With more than 40% of buyers coming from abroad, ITB Berlin is one of the industry’s most
balanced and international events
in terms of exhibitors and visitors. Business conducted at ITB Berlin is estimated at a value of
six billion Euros
.

The Gay & Lesbian Travel Pavilion has grown to more than
400m2
of dedicated exhibition space!
The
Gay Pavilion
is the cornerstone for
LGBT business
at ITB. IGLTA is proud to offer
Booth Sharing
and
Brochure Distribution
opportunities to its members.

The IGLTA all-inclusive booth share package includes WIFI
and
electrical connections

plus

refreshments
and access to the exclusive
private café area
in the Gay Pavilion for conducting your business meetings. Enjoy unlimited coffee, tea and sodas for you and your guests at
no additional charge
.

As an IGLTA booth share partner, you will also receive up to
two entrance badges
to ITB Berlin, a listing in the
ITB Berlin Catalogue
and on the
ITB Berlin Virtual Marketplace
and invitations to the
IGLTA Member Networking Receptions
.
Brochure Distribution
includes distribution of up to
500 pieces of collateral material
from within the IGLTA Stand in the Gay & Lesbian Travel Pavilion.

IGLTA has partnered with ITB Berlin for the sales and management of its stand in the Gay & Lesbian Travel Pavilion.
Be sure to check option #3 or #4 on the ITB Stand Registration Form
to be included in the IGLTA Stand of the Gay & Lesbian Travel Pavilion.
