Ski-touring

Whether you are after glistening powder snow or firm spring snow - the Lötschental outdoor sports region is a paradise for ski-tourers. Ski-tourers will find many opportunities to discover a magnificent winter landscape at their own pace in the Lötschental. First the ascent, then the reward - a feeling of limitless freedom.

Plan your tour carefully

Ensure that you have a full set of appropriate equipment

Never embark on a tour alone

Inform friends, relatives, the mountain hut supervisor or the hotel porter about your proposed tour. Report back when you arrive at your destination.

Keep an eye on weather developments

Adhere to the principle: if in doubt turn back!
