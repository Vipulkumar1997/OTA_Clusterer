Wintersports school Lötschental




Whether you are embarking on your first winter sports experience or want to perfect your skills on snow - our local ski school "COOL SCHOOL" is the expert. The ski school office is located on the Lauchernalp by the mountain station. Whether you are a beginner or expert, youngster or young-at-heart, whether you want to ski or snowboard, COOL SCHOOL offers a tailored programme of expert tuition to meet your needs. And even ski-jumpers are well looked after on the Lauchernalp - under COOL SCHOOL's careful tuition, you can hone your jumping skills and technique.


Information and reservations:
Wintersports School Lötschental - COOL SCHOOL, Tel. +41 (0)27 939 16 11,
www.coolschool.ch

Snowli Land is open from 10.00am on Saturdays and Sundays.

Would you like to start with cross-country skiing or to improve your technique? You can learn cross-country skiing in different groups depending on skill and technique (classical or skating). The team of the cross-country ski centre attachs great importance to  respond individually to your needs and your level of proficiency in the classroom.

Offer:


Private lessons


1 person 70.00 CHF per lesson / person
2 people 40.00 CHF per lesson / person
3 people 30.00 CHF per lesson / person

A lesson takes 60 minutes.




Group lessons (minimum 4 people)


Adults 50.00 CHF per lesson / person
Children 40.00 CHF per lesson / person

One lesson takes 120 minutes.


The participants are in possession of a valid cross-country skipass. Who does not have his/her own equipment, can rent the equipment with Handlebar - skis and more. The participant is responsible for his/her own accident insurance. The course management disclaims any liability.



For further information and reservations contact:
Cross-country ski centre, Tel. +41 (0) 27 939 13 20,
info@langlaufzentrum-loetschental.ch
,
www.langlaufzentrum-loetschental.ch
