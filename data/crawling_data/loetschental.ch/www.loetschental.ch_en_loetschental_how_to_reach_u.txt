How to reach us




Lötschental – it’s closer than you think. This section includes information that will help you to plan your journey by road to the Lötschental.



Travelling from the North (D) (CH) - Basle, Berne, Zurich


Take the autobahn following the signs for Berne and Interlaken as far as Spiez

Follow the main road to Kandersteg following the signs for Autoverlad Lötschberg (BLS Lötschberg Tunnel Car Transport Train)

Goppenstein, Ferden, Kippel, Wiler, Blatten





Travelling from the West (F) - Geneva, Lausanne, Chamonix


Take the autobahn passing Geneva and Lausanne to Siders (Sierre)

Follow the main "A" road to Steg following the signs for Autoverlad Lötschberg (BLS Lötschberg Tunnel Car Transport Train)

Goppenstein, Ferden, Kippel, Wiler, Blatten





Travelling from the Southwest (I) - Turin, Aosta region


From Aosta head towards the Great St. Bernard Pass

Either cross the pass (2,469 m above sea level) or go via the tunnel

Take the autobahn to Martigny, heading for Siders (Sierre)

Follow the main road to Steg following the signs for Autoverlad Lötschberg (BLS Lötschberg Tunnel Car Transport Train)

Goppenstein, Ferden, Kippel, Wiler, Blatten





Travelling from the South (I) - Milan, Lake Maggiore


Take the Milan autostrada towards Sempione (Domodossola)

At the end of the autostrada, continue to the border at Iselle/Gondo

Cross the Simplon Pass (2,004 m above sea level) and head for Brig

Follow the main road towards Visp and Sion as far as Steg following the signs for Autoverlad Lötschberg (BLS Lötschberg Tunnel Car Transport Train)

Goppenstein, Ferden, Kippel, Wiler, Blatten





Travelling from the Southeast (CH) - Ticino


Take the Lugano autobahn towards Airolo (St. Bernard)

Cross the Nufenen Pass (2,478 m above sea level) towards Brig

Follow the main "A" road towards Visp and Sion as far as Steg following the signs for Autoverlad Lötschberg (BLS Lötschberg Tunnel Car Transport Train)

Goppenstein, Ferden, Kippel, Wiler, Blatten





Travelling from the East (CH) (A)- Vorarlberg, Grisons


Take the Bregenz autobahn towards Chur as far as Reichenau

Head towards the Vorderrheintal through the Oberalp Pass (2,044 m above sea level) as far as Andenmatt

Continue towards Hospental over the Furka Pass (2,431 m above sea level) or take the car transport train

Follow the main road towards Brig and Sion as far as Steg following the signs for Autoverlad Lötschberg (BLS Lötschberg Tunnel Car Transport Train)

Goppenstein, Ferden, Kippel, Wiler, Blatten





Information on the car transport Lötschberg
The Car Transport Lötschberg runs every 30 mins and every 15 mins at weekends between Kandersteg and Goppenstein. For more information about the Car Transport Lötschberg call telephone +41(0) 900 55 33 33 (CHF 1.50/min from the fixed network) or visit
www.bls.ch/autoverlad

So far and yet so near. The Lötschental is much closer than many people believe and visitors can reach it easily and conveniently by public transport. This section provides information that will help you to plan your journey to the Lötschental by public transport.

The Lötschberger departs from Berne and Brig directly to Goppenstein, where passengers swap over to the Post Bus.Trains depart to and from Berne in the morning and evening and throughout the day on Sundays.

The journey time from Berne is approx. 1 ½ hours and from Zurich and Basle approx. 3 hours.
Visit www.loetschberger.ch for train times and interesting excursions.
Plan your own individual journey – including journeys within the Lötschental - using the
SBB CFF FFS Rail/Bus Timetable
.

This section provides information to help you plan your journey by air.

International airports in Berne, Zurich, Geneva, Basle and Malpensa (Milan).
There are airport taxis and air buses from Berne-Belp Airport to the main rail network.

Alternatively, fly into Sion Airport and continue your journey by rail/bus, taxi or helicopter.
