Tobogganing

Fancy the exhilaration of a fast toboggan run? A toboggan run from the Lauchernalp, the Fafleralp or from Weissenried is a quite unique experience.




Starting point - Lauchernalp, 1,968 m above sea level
Descents are possible to Wiler or Kippel. Take care - this route is also used by skiers!

Toboggan to Blatten
Start at the Fafleralp or Weissenried.
Combine the toboggan run with a wonderful winter hike to the Fafleralp or Weissenried and then enjoy the descent.



Toboggan hire from
Sporthaus Lauchernalp
