Contemplation Trail
"The rushing of the river - unceasing, troubled. Immersing me into this flow. In the constant rushing, hear the message: we are all one."
This is only one message out of the 10 stations situated along the Contemplation Trail from Blatten to Kühmatt. Take your time and enjoy the peace and tranquillity along the route to Kühmatt and be inspired by the messages to pause and reflect.
