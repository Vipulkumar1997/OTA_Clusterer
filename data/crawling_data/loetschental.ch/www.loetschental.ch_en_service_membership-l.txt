About us







Become a member!

Lötschental Tourism is an assoiciation that advocates the promotion of tourism in the Lötschental. Support tourism in the Lötschental and become a member of Lötschental tourism. Our members get attractive member benefits:



Always be informed
As a member of Lötschental tourism you receive our eNewsletter with interesting news and 4 times a year the magazine "Lötschental infomiert" with current topics of our region. Please note: The eNewsletter as well as the magazine are currently only available in German.



Have a say
As a member, you will not only get informed but also have a say in the touristic development of the valley at the the annual general assembly.



Marketing to join
Tourism service providers will benefit as a member of Lötschental Tourism of numerous marketing opportunities. You can list your business / property / restaurant at the Lötschental on our website for free and put a link to your own website. Once a year, you receive a list of "Marketing to join"-opportunities from Lötschental tourism.



Direct Booking TOMAS
Accomodation facilities, that are member of Lötschental tourism, can register for free in our reservation system TOMAS. Accomodation facilities, that sign a contract for direct booking with us, can be booked online via www.loetschental.ch and via the local tourist office.



Membership fee


Private membership: CHF 30.00 per year

Business/Shop: CHF 150.00 per year

Restaurant: CHF 50.00 per year

Holiday flat: CHF 20.00 per year (+ CHF 5.00 pro Bett)

Group accomodation: CHF 50.00 per year (+ CHF 2.50 pro Bett)

Hotel/Restaurant: CHF 70.00 per year (+ CHF 5.00 pro Bett)
