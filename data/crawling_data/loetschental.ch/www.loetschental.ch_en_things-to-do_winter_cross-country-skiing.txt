Cross-Country Skiing

Is cross-country skiing your thing? Then discover our perfectly prepared network of cross-country ski trails that run along past the picturesque Lötschental villages to the Fafleralp. There are 24 km of classic or skating style cross-country trails to choose from to challenge you. Irrespective of whether you are an ambitious athlete or a leisure-skier, you will find a wide variety of trails to choose from in the Lötschental.


Our tip for guests!
Every Tuesday, Thursday and Friday you can ski the circuits in Kippel at night from 7.00pm - 9.00pm.

All-inclusive cross-country active day

Interested in trying cross-country skiing but don't have the kit? No problem - for
CHF 42.00
you can have a day's cross-country skiing in the Lötschental, kitted out with top equipment.
The offer includes:


Hire of cross-country ski equipment (classic)

A one-day cross-country day pass for the Lötschental

A hot non-alcoholic drink in a restaurant of your choice


The cross-country active day is also available with skating style equipment for CHF 52.00.



Cross-country under the full moon

Romantic cross-country skiing in the moonlight on the night trail in Kippel and then rest your weary legs over a hot drink in a restaurant of your choice.
Our
CHF 9.00 special offer
includes:


A one-day cross-country ski pass for the Lötschental

A hot drink in a restaurant of your choice in Kippel


This offer is available from the Lötschental Tourist Information Office or Kily Sport in Wiler. Pop in and experience magical cross-country moments in the Lötschental.
