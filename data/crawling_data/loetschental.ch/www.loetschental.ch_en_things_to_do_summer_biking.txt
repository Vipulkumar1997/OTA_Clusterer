Biking

The wide range of exhilarating bike trails also sets mountainbikers’ and freeriders’ hearts racing.


Please follow the following rules:


Only use marked routes and roads and follow the signs.

Look out for walkers and hikers and respect their need for peace and quiet and give them priority at all times.

Be respectful of plants and animals - be responsible for Nature and the natural environment.

Respect the needs of agriculture - close gates and do not bike through fields


Please note that you ride these routes at your own risk and that the owners of the land are not liable in the event of accidents.
