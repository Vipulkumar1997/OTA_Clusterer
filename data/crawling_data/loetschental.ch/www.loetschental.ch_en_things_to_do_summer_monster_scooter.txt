Monster Scooters

An exciting and funny experience for everyone: From the mountain station of the cable-car Wiler-Lauchernalp you can enjoy an exhilarating ride on a monster scooter to Wiler. The monster scooters are specifically designed for the Alps and can be driven by anyone.






Hire a "monster scooter" at the valley station in Wiler or the mountain station at the Lauchernalp and enjoy an exhilarating or, if you prefer, gentle descent on your "monster scooter" to Wiler. 100% fun for all the family!
