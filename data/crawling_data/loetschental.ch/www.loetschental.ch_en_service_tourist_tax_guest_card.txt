Tourist Tax and Guest Cards

Tourist tax

Pay the tourist tax on your first day.
All visitors to the Lötschental are obliged to pay the tourist tax on time. The tourist tax is generally collected by your host; otherwise please pay it at the Lötschental Tourist Information Office in Wiler.

Guest Card

Whether you are staying in a holiday flat, hotel, chalet or Alpine hut: it is best to pay your tourist tax to your host on the first day of your holiday. You are then entitled to your
Guest Card
