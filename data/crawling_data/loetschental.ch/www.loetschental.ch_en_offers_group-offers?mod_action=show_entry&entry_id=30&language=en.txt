Mask Carving










2017-12-07T08:38:57
Follow the footsteps of the Tschäggättä and create your own Lötschental mask! Group mask carving with professional instruction is available upon request from 5 people or more in our mask carving workshop in Ferden. Duration 2-3 hours.
Location

Single inscription possible on the following dates:

16.12.2017 / 09:30 am /
It takes place

27.12.2017 / 3:00 pm

03.01.2018 / 3:00 pm

07.02.2018 / 3:00 pm

10.02.2018 / 10:00 am

14.02.2018 / 3:00 pm

21.02.2018 / 3:00 pm

If five inscriptions the mask carving will take place.



Online-Inscription



Price: CHF 45.00/Adult and CHF 40.00/Child.
For more information and to register, contact the Lötschental Tourist Information Office in Wiler Tel. +41 (0)27 938 88 88.

Please book a minimum of 2 days before the date you require.
