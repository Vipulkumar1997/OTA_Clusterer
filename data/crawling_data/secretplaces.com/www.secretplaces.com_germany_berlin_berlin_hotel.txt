Berlin's perfect combination of glamour and authenticity is bound to mesmerize anyone keen to explore its vibrant culture, cutting-edge architecture, fabulous food, intense parties and tangible history. If you want to experience Berlin from "within" and feel like a local, just choose one of our trendy, elegant and hip Berlin hotels and guesthouses. Catch the atmosphere of the neighborhood in one of the stylish hotels in Prenzlauer Berg, Friedrichshain or Berlin Mitte or a cosy bed and breakfast or more luxurious hotel on Kurfüstendamm.
