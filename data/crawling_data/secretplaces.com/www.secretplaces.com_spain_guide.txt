Spain hotels, Fincas and country inns

The choice of hotels, fincas and guesthouses is wide. Be it secluded
guesthouses in Extremadura
,
beautiful
romantic countryside hotels in Costa Brava
with cosy bedrooms where dishes cooked with local produce are served as well as
boutique hotels in Barcelona
with elegantly designed rooms and famous cuisine. In between, there are friendly hotels for families, the perfect spot for your honeymoon or just one of the
best hotels in Majorca

and
Ibiza
with splendid views over the mountains or the sea inviting you to relax. All are places to stay that value local flair and traditions, good taste and design, as well as good food. Beautiful surroundings and a touch of history are other important attributes.




Spain - where to go

Travelling to Spain means not only discovering sun, excellent gastronomy and genuine hospitality but also uncovering dazzling architectural masterpieces, past and present, and incredible natural environments. In short, it a fascinating country with fire in its blood - where on top of all this all people know how to enjoy the good things in life.
The wonderful variety of countryside in the Iberian Peninsula is matched by the many charming towns and monumental cities throughout the land. From UNESCO heritage sites to border towns, dramatic hilltop settlements and sophisticated cities - all with their own unique Iberian character and charm. In short, they are a testament to the region’s wealth of architecture, culture and history.
