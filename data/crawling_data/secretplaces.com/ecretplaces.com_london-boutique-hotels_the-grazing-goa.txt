Located on a quiet street in Marylebone, you will find the
very charming Grazing Goat. This Public House and Hotel offers will for sure seduce
you with its warm, rustic and cosy atmosphere.
The Grazing Goat offers eight beautiful rooms set over three
floors. Each room boasts modern country house styled interiors, en-suite
bathrooms, complimentary Wi-FI and free toiletry products. From your room, you
will also be able to enjoy gorgeous views on the neighbourhood, allowing you to
relax and escape the busy London lifestyle.
On the first floor, you will find the bright and charming
dining room. Here, you will be able to enjoy a British menu featuring local
seasonal products as well as a wine list of aromatic whites and full-bodied
reds.
And on the ground floor, you will discover the authentic pub
of the property. Treat yourself and enjoy a local ale, a nice cocktail or the
daily market fish special while being surrounded by open fireplaces, wooden
panelling and large French doors.
The Grazing Goat Public House and Hotel has it all:
beautiful luxury accommodations as well as a warm and welcoming traditional English
pub and restaurant. The perfect combination for you to enjoy your stay in
London at its fullest. When leaving the place, you will for sure have an idea
of what the British spirit is all about.
