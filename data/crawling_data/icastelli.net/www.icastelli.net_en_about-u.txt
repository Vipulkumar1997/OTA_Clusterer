About us


A journey from luxury to history

icastelli.net offers you a unique collection of historical hotels all over the world, providing top service to its clientele,  not simply a booking website but a Dedicated Club, with benefits and exclusive rates. A journey  through the most beautiful destinations in the world including luxurious boutique hotels, design hotels or exclusive riads, for customers with particular needsthat are looking not only for the usual hotel, but something more, a unique travel experience.

THE IDEA

icastelli.net was born in
2001
, thanks to an idea of a young entrepreneur with a passion for luxury tourism, history and castles:
. Marco is fascinated by, a different kind of tourism, that surpasses usual hotels and destinations. Indeed, the idea was to offer hotels with distinguisehd haracteristics, due to a higher sense of luxury or historical significance, or that combine together a perfect match the two.
Our team works every day to give life to this project, based in Bristol, UK, icastelli.net now boasts a unique selection of more than 3000 hotels around the world , with thousands of customers daily, is now a recognized leader in the field of online hotel reservation.

THE HOTELS AND DESTINATIONS OF OUR COLLECTION

Unlike other sites, every hotel or accommodation that we select must absolutely correspond to our criteria of uniqueness and quality:
luxury and history
. Every day our team is in search of the most unique and uncommon destinations to be offered to our customers. We offer you the opportunity of an unforgettable journey: from a resort in the heart of the Scottish
Highlands
, to a medieval
Castle
in the French
Loire
valley, to an ancient village nestled in the green hills of
Tuscany
, to an exclusive
Riad
in a charming
Moroccan
city, and much more...A UNIQUE JOURNEY in the luxurious modernity of the past.

THE DEDICATED CLUB

All of our customers can subscribe to our free program dedicated to privileged customers:
TOWER CLUB
, thus having access to services and benefits, discounted offers, private sales and previews of everything is new at icastelli.net. Registration is free, and allows you to book any hotel at the lowest rates on the web.
Why book anywhere else ?
From the first bookingit is possible to use the loyalty discount, thereby accumulating the bonus that can be used for future reservations. Depending on the volume of annual bookings the TOWER CLUB level can be increasedin order to obtain higher benefits and bonuses.

DEDICATED CUSTOMER SERVICE AND SUPPORT

icastelli.net thanks to a modern interface and cutting edge technology offers a reservation service quick and immediate. All our customers can easily see countless hotels with images, multilingual descriptions and complete information.
We also offer a reservation center and dedicated customer support, with multi-lingual staff 24/7, whatever your question or search for a hotel or special destination might be, we will accommodate you.
