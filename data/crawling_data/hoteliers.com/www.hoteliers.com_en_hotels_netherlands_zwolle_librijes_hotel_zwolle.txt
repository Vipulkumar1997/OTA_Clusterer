Librije’s, in Zwolle’s historic centre, offers elegant rooms and a three Michelin-star restaurant in the unusual setting of a renovated 18th-century women’s prison. It features free use of an iPad with Wi-Fi, free mini-bar and 24-hour butler service.
All of the luxurious air-conditioned rooms and suites at Librije's Hotel come with a flat-screen TV and unique décor.
Each room includes modern facilities and a luxurious bathroom with a large bath or spacious shower.

Librije is less than a 10-minute walk from the Zwolle City Museum. Zwolle Railway Station is less than a 20-minute walk away.
The centre of Apeldoorn is just over 30 minutes’ drive from the hotel.
A range of cookery lessons and wine courses are available at Librije's Atelier.
Restaurant De Librije serves contemporary gourmet cuisine in the dining room.
There is also a cosy wine bar in the cellar and a courtyard terrace where guests can enjoy a drink.
In Librije's Shop you can buy wines and delicacies.
