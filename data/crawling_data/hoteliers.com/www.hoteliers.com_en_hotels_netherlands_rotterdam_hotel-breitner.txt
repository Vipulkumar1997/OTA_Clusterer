Hotel Breitner has been a haven of peace in the heart of Rotterdam since 1977. Comfortable accommodation, where service is all important.
The hotel has 36 different rooms over 3 floors, accessible by stairs and lift. The rooms are equipped with all modern conveniences, such as flat-screen TV, a private bathroom and there is free access to wireless internet throughout the hotel.
Every morning an extensive breakfast buffet is served in the attractive conservatory, with a smile! In the lounge of the hotel, where there is always a log fire going during winter months, you can read the paper or use the internet corner whilst enjoying a drink. The bar of the hotel is open every night.

Within walking distance from the Central Station, picturesque Delfshaven, Witte de With streat, Markt Hall, the Euromast, museums and Spido for a tour through the harbour of Rotterdam. Comfortably furnished accommodations from single rooms to rooms for four persons are spread out across three floors connected by lift. All rooms have double-glass windows, a bathtub or shower, toilet, hairdryer, cable television, Laptop size Safe, telephone and a wireless internet connection.
