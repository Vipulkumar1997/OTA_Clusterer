Information about
You are about to book a hotel in London. We would therefore like to provide you with some extra information. London, politically and culturally seen as one of the most important cities of the world, is the capital of both England and the United Kingdom. The city counts about 7 million inhabitants and has loads of historic monuments to offer.

Getting there
London is very easily reachable. You could do this by car, train, boat or plane. With
Waterloo Station
and
King’s cross
, the city has several big train stations. Public transport in the city itself is very well-organized as well. It has for example a very extensive bus- and metro network. In addition, the city has five major airports (
