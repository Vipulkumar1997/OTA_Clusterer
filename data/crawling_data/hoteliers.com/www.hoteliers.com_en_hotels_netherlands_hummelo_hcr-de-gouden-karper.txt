Surroundings cities Hummelo


The village Hummelo is just a stone’s throw away from Doetinchem, a city with many shops and entertainment possibilities. You will also find the old Hanze cities Doesburg and Zutphen not far from Hummelo. The visitor finds here hundreds of historical buildings, courtyards and many vendors.
Between these cities you will find yourself back in the countryside of the Achterhoek with many castles, beautiful estates and surprisingly museums.

HCR De Gouden Karper is one of the oldest and most renowed of hotels in Holland, located in Hummelo. The hotel is situated in a woody area. Across the hotel, you will find the village church of Hummelo. HCR De Gouden Karper is an ideal start point for many bike trips through the beautiful region of the Achterhoek.
Children under 4 years free
Children from 4 years to 12 years € 25, - including breakfast
Rates are per room, per night, including breakfast and excluding city tax
Your dog is welcome. We charge € 5, - per night for extra cleaning.
Bikes are available for guests to rent for € 8, - per day.

On the terrace of the hotel you can enjoy a cup of coffee or a delicious dinner served by our restaurant. The Achterhoekse café has a friendly atmosphere were everyone is welcome. All the decorations like the genealogical table made of marble, the reading table with pluche and the wooden furniture makes this café the meeting place of the Achterhoek. There are many people from the district. In the café, the hotel serves various dishes from the small and the big menu. When the weather is nice, then you can go to the deck or the terrace under the old oak trees. Next to the café you find two ‘living rooms’ for a (family)diner or a small meeting. This rooms is equipped with internet and other facilities for a business occasion.
The twelve hotel rooms are divided over the first floor of the main building and the dependance, which has a wonderful view over the forest and the lands. Every room is provided with a bath or shower with toilet, telephone and television.

Cancellation- and additional conditions HCR De Gouden Karper

Your credit card details are required to guarantee your booking up to your arrival at the hotel. Payment will be made exclusively to the hotel. If you cannot take up your booking, you need to cancel no later than 1 day before your date of arrival. Please take into account that the date of arrival starts at 0.00 am (CET.). Bookings not cancelled 1 day before arrival will be billed at a cost equivalent to the price of the first night. In case of no-show, the total price of the booking will be charged. A different cancellation policy may apply for group bookings. The hotel will contact you within 24 hrs. if this is the case.
