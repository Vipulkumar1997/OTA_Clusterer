Van der Valk Hotel Emmen is situated on the edge of the largest city in the Province of Drenthe, near the hamlets Erica and Nieuw Amsterdam.
The hotel has 93 double rooms, 1 double room for disabled, 2 triple rooms, 6 family rooms and 4 very luxurious bridal suites.
All rooms are fitted with a luxury bathroom with whirlpool.
Guests can enjoy the 2-hrs free wireless internet for laptop or phone.
The hotel has a own swimming pool and fitnesscentre, with free entrance for the hotelguests.
It is the perfect location within a stone's throw (5 km)of the famous zoo Noorder Dierenpark.

The van der Valk hotel Group is a chain of 77 comfortable hotels with 53 branches in the Netherlands. The group operates hotels in the United States, Germany, France, Belgium, Spain and the Dutch Carribean islands of Curacao and Bonaire.
Power of the van der Valk formula is the excellent service and quality offered at a 'Dutch' budget.

Additional information about Van der Valk Hotel Emmen

Van der Valk Hotel Emmen also has 7 multifunctional rooms which are fit for weddings, receptions, parties, meetings and conferences etc. On the ground floor of the hotel are the hotel bar and the à la carte restaurant, which seats up to 250 people.








Cancellation- and additional conditions Van der Valk Hotel Emmen

Your credit card details are required to guarantee your reservation. The final payment will be held by the hotel at the end of your stay, no fee will be charged on your credit card. The reservation can be cancelled with no penalty up to 10.00 on the day of arrival.  Reservations not cancelled before 10:00 on the day of arrival will be subject to charges for one night's stay. A different cancellation policy can be applied for group bookings. In that case the hotel will contact you.
In case you booked a NON REFUNDABLE room or package, the following conditions are applicable:
Your credit card details are necessary to qualify for this promotion. No change or cancellation can be made after booking. Upon booking, you accept to be charged for the full price of the stay, this amount is non refundable in case of cancellation or change of dates of stay.
