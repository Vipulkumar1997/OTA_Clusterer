Club Zonne


Hotel Zonne is situated in the beautiful and quiet dunes of Noordwijk. Hotel Zonne has multiple sunny terraces with comfortable lounge-chairs, a flower garden and two tennis courts. It is the perfect place to relax.
It is our pleasure to suggest some possibilities that we can offer.
- A barbeque with a salad and dessert buffet
- High tea with several sweet refreshments and spiced sandwiches
- Cocktail time with tasty tapas
- Garden brunch with a palette of bread types, fresh salads and fresh fruit
- A tennis tournament
- Business lunches, in combination with meeting rooms

We are happy to send you an overview of the possibilities so you can plan a friendship lunch, a family barbeque or a cosy cocktail complete tailor made to your wishes.

Hotel Zonne is a Family Hotel in the beautiful and quiet South Dunes of Noordwijk aan Zee. It has 27 hotel rooms, 4 conference rooms, a restaurant, bar, garden, sun terraces, play area for children, 2 tennis courts, parking facilities, free Wifi and friendly staff.
A perfect place to relax and enjoy the surroundings of Noordwijk.

Hotel Zonne is uniquely located:
Sandy Beach - 150 meters
City centre - 10 minutes walking
Leiden - 15 minutes by car
Amsterdam - 20 minutes by car
Schiphol Airport - 20 minutes by car
The Hague - 20 minutes by car

Cancellation- and additional conditions Hotel Zonne

Your credit card details are required to guarantee your reservation. The final payment will be held by the hotel at the end of your stay, no fee will be charged on your credit card. The reservation can be cancelled with no penalty up to two days prior to arrival. Please be informed that the date of arrival starts at 0.00 p.m. Reservations not cancelled 2 days prior to the scheduled arrival date will be subject to charges for one night's stay. A different cancellation policy can be applied for group bookings. In that case the hotel will contact you. Please note: € 5.00 reservation costs will be charged to your account.
In case you booked a NON REFUNDABLE room or package, the following conditions are applicable:
Your credit card details are necessary to qualify for this promotion. No change or cancellation can be made after booking. Upon booking, you accept to be charged for the full price of the stay, this amount is non refundable in case of cancellation or change of dates of stay.
