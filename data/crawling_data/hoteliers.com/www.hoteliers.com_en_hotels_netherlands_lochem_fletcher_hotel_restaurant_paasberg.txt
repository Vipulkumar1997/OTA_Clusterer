Location













Surrounding

Hotel-Restaurant Paasberg is located on the edge of the woods on the hill 'De Paasberg'. The surrounding area is perfect for a walk or bike ride, and the nature of 'De Veluwe' can also be easily reached from Lochem. You can also enjoy the different areas of outstanding natural beauty : 'Het Grote Veld', 'Lochemse Berg' or 'Kale Berg'. Lovers of culture can pay a visit to Royal palace 'Paleis Het Loo', Museum 'De Zwiepse Molen' or the Kröller-Müller Museum of modern art.

The hotel is situated on the outskirts of the charming municipality of Lochem and is surrounded by magnificent woodlands and natural beauty. The "Gelderse Achterhoek" is the Netherlands at its most beautiful. The area is covered with woodlands and traditional farms and the expanses of fields are dotted with delightful villages and small communities. The region is perfect for long cycling tours and walks. Hotel-Restaurant Paasberg is located in the Paasberg nature reserve in Lochem and is the ideal home base for a relaxed weekend away or mid-week break.

Hotel-Restaurant Paasberg offers you every comfort and all the amenities necessary for a pleasant and relaxing stay. From the patio, the view of the rustic grounds and seemingly endless fields is breathtaking. The view of the surrounding countryside is equally beautiful from the spacious restaurant. The delightful restaurant offers an excellent menu with a wide range of delicious dishes; there is something to suit every taste. In the evenings, you can visit the bar or lounge for a final drink or a game of billiards or chess. Dogs are allowed in deluxury rooms.
