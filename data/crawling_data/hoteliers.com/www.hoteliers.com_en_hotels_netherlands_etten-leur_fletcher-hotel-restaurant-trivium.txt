Etten-Leur


Etten-Leur has much to offer. Situated centrally in the Netherlands, cities as Breda, Oudenbosch, Rotterdam and Antwerp(B) are closeby. The renewned town centre now has great opportunities for shopping, enjoying a nice drink or lunch. Brabant has extensive possibilities for cycling especially with the in 2005 opened cycle system network. What how about a visit to the Efteling, Ouwehandsdierenpark (Zoo), Nature Park De Biesbosch...? Wether you are here on business or for leisure, Etten-Leur can offer it all. Why don't you come and see for yourself?

Fletcher Hotel Restaurant Trivium is a very warm decorated hotel situated in a building with special architectual features. The hotel has spacious rooms & suites with a view over the meadows & the sports fields. The hotel also offers you a Wellness & Beauty Center with 2 types of sauna's, turkish steambath, infrared beamers, cool down pool, relax area and a Beauty Treatment Salon. A cosy brasserie with bar, lounge area and fireplace and a non smoking restaurant who are both climatised. It also has extensive banquet- and conference facilities, including an auditorium. Excellent situated in the Netherlands (Rotterdam-Breda-Roosendaal-Antwerpen) nearby the highway and a large, free parking.
