Just across the border, 8 kilometres from Maastricht, atop a green hill in the picturesque Hoge Kempen region, stands Hostellerie La Butte aux Bois, a place of pure indulgence where you will feel at home from the moment you are warmly welcomed.
The hotel that has been created in this simply marvellous manoir house is distinctive for its unique, stylish yet informal atmosphere. The Manoir was built in 1924 for Ridder Lagasse de Locht and was converted by the Bullens family in the 1980s into the splendid Hostellerie you see today.

Hostellerie La Butte aux Bois is situated amid the peace and quiet of natural surroundings.
This fairy tale estate has majestic gardens complete with lake, meandering lane ways and idyllic little spots where you can wander in a world without care.
The 5 Cs of the Relais & Châteaux network (Courtesy, Charm, Character, Calm and Cuisine) are an integral part of the gastronomic philosophy of personal hospitality you will receive at La Butte aux Bois.

Additional information about La Butte Aux Bois

Your lunch or dinner is served in the elegant restaurant or out on the sheltered terrace that has stunning views over the grounds.
After an after-dinner drink or final cup of mocha in the charming bar or by the open fire, you can retire to one of the 40 gloriously comfortable hotel rooms dotted throughout the Manoir and stately home.
The luxury rooms have been designed in the French country style and feature every modern comfort and a magnificent bathroom.
The central location of Hostellerie La Butte aux Bois in relation to the Burgundian town of Maastricht (8 km) and the cities of Liège (30 km) and Aachen (40 km) offers you a wide variety of cultural and recreational attractions. La Butte aux Bois is also an easy bike ride to the village voted the loveliest in Flanders, Oud-Rekem. La Butte aux Bois is easy accesable via the E314 or A2 from the Netherlands.
‘Aquamarijn’ is the name of the wellness and fitness centre at La Butte aux Bois, where you will enjoy being deliciously pampered by the qualified beauty specialists. The swimming pool, sauna, whirlpool and various treatment areas are all designed to relax and revitalise you.
As a guest at La Butte aux Bois you can park your car free of charge. We also offer you a free WIFI connection in our public areas.








Cancellation- and additional conditions La Butte Aux Bois

Your credit card details are required to guarantee your booking up to your arrival at the hotel. Payment will be made exclusively to the hotel. If you cannot take up your booking, you need to cancel no later than 3 day(s) before your date of arrival. Please take into account that the date of arrival starts at 0.00 am (CET.) Any booking not taken up and not cancelled 3 day(s) before arrival will be billed at a cost equivalent to the price of the first night. A different cancellation policy may apply for group bookings. The hotel will contact you within 24 hrs. if this is the case.
