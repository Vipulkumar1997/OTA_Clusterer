Because of its central location, international travelers from around the world meet at the Old Nickel for vacation or simple rest and relaxation. A multilingual staff stands ready to assist them with information about what to do during their stay in town.

Additional information about Hotel Old Nickel

The cozy restaurant-bar is decorated with wood-carvings and paneling from the captain's quarters of famed 17th century ship of Admiral Michiel de Ruyter.
A hearty ham and eggs breakfast is served each morning and is included in the price of the room.








Cancellation- and additional conditions Hotel Old Nickel

Your credit card details are required to guarantee your reservation. The full amount must be paid at check in, no fee will be charged on your credit card. The reservation can be cancelled with no penalty up to three days prior to arrival. Please be informed that the date of arrival starts at 0.00 p.m. Reservations not cancelled 3 days prior to scheduled arrival date will be subject to charges for the whole booked stay. A different cancellation policy can be applied for group bookings. In that case the hotel will contact you. If you will arrive after 18:00, please contact the hotel directly to arrange a late check in.
