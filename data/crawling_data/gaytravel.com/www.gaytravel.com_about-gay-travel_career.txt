About Us
The Gay Travel brand is widely considered to be the equivalent of the "Good Housekeeping Seal of Approval" among LGBTA travelers as well as those travelers who appreciate the discerning taste of our community experts, editors and influencers.

Since 1999 millions of site visitors have relied on Gay Travel for the best places to stay, the best tours and cruises, the latest events and the hottest dining and nightlife trends. Gaytravel.com maintains the #1 organic search result on Google, Yahoo! and Bing for "gay travel." The US Department of State has sought Gay Travel's feedback in developing safe protocols for LGBT travelers abroad. Gay Travel has been featured in the New York Times, The Wall Street Journal and Smart Money Magazine.
