The H10 Rubicon Palace is an emblematic seafront resort located in Playa Blanca, in the south of the island of Lanzarote. Discover its new Chill-Out terrace with sea views, its spectacular pools, wide selection of cuisine and full programme of activities for adults and children of all ages. The hotel also has a Despacio Thalasso Centre with spa and personalised treatments and the Daisy Adventure, where children can have a ball with an incredible pirate ship and fun water games.

Make yourself at home in one of the 584 air-conditioned rooms featuring mini bars. Rooms have private balconies where you can take in ocean and garden views. Satellite television is provided for your entertainment as well as bathrooms with shower/tub combinations boasting complimentary toiletries and bidets. Satisfy your appetite at one of the hotel's 3 restaurants and why not relax with your favourite drink at a bar/lounge or a poolside bar.
