Facilities

Hotel Rooms: 90

































































































The Londonears Hostel has a large range of bedrooms, which consist of dormitory rooms and private rooms to suit every traveller. Dormitory rooms vary in size and are the ideal place for guests to meet and interact with fellow travellers.

For those guests who prefer some privacy, there are also private rooms with double and twin beds. WiFi is available throughout the hostel, and there is also a computer lounge.

There is also a small cafe, where guests can fill up on snacks as well as meals for breakfast, lunch and dinner. All rooms are modern with basic amenities.

Locations near
The Londonears Hostel

The Londonears Hostel is set in Earls Court with excellent transport links that enable guests to be in the Centre of London within 20 minutes via the Piccadilly and District Underground Lines. Top attractions and landmarks that are within easy reach include Buckingham Palace, Westminster Abbey, the Houses of Parliament, Hyde Park, the West End and Covent Garden.
