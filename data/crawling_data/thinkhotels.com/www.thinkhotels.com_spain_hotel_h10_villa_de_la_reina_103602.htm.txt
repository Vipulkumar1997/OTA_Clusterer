Located in the middle of Madrid, the H10 Villa de la Reina hotel occupies a building featuring the architectural style associated with the early twentieth century, though now fully renovated and restored. It has two halls for holding all kinds of events, whether for professional or for private purposes. Its comfortable rooms and central location make it the perfect hotel for your visits to Madrid.
