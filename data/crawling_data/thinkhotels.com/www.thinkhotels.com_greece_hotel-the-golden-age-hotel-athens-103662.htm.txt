The Golden Age Hotel is a unique 4 star boutique hotel, situated in Megaro Mousikis area, in one of the safest and most accessible areas of Athens offering visitors a sense of freedom and ease to reach the business center and the cultural heritage of the city.

The hotel is close to the metro station, just 200 meters walking distance and just 2 stops from Syntagma Square. A selection of comfortable rooms and suites, elegantly decorated and fully equipped together with  pleasant well trained staff will make your stay enjoyable and relaxing.

The Olive Tree Spa at The Golden Age Hotel combines ancient lore and skilled treatment. Let the treatments and healing rituals reveal the wisdom of time-honored Greek medicine, combined with the mystical lore of Thailand, providing a truly unique spa experience.

Locations near
The Golden Age Hotel Athens

The Hotel is located on Michalakopoulou Avenue in one of the safest areas of Athens, near the Music Hall (Megaro Mousikis). The Acropolis and the famous New Acropolis Museum is a ten minute Metro ride from Megaro Mousikis station while Athens central shopping area and the main square of Athens (Syntagma Square) are a 25 minute walk away. You can also use the public bus from Illisia bus stop, Bus no. 8, or the Metro from Megaro Musikis station to Syntagma station.(Both bus stop and Metro are a 2 mins walk from the Golden Age of Athens.
