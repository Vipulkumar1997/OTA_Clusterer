M by Montcalm Shoreditch London Tech City is more than just a luxury hotel, it is a symbol of the pioneering spirit of London. Located near the Silicon Roundabout, close to Old Street, Moorgate and Liverpool Street, this hotel offers luxury in the midst of techno-savvy business district of London.

A new landmark in London's skyline, the hotel boasts an iconic architectural design, breathtaking interiors and stunning views of London. Along with opulent rooms with awe-inspiring bathrooms, this magnificent destination offers you the finest service and facilities throughout.

Facilitated with an exquisite bar, restaurants and brasserie, along with state-of-the-art meeting and event spaces, M by Montcalm Shoreditch London Tech City will soon arise into the glistening skyline of vibrant London.
