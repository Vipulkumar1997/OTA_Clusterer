Facilities

Hotel Rooms: 115

















































































































































Perched on the idyllic setting of Tonle Sap River, banks of the Chaktomok River, be immersed by the essence of serenity at Himawari Hotel Apartments; a five-star establishment. Enjoy splendid views of Mekong Delta in a tranquil resort haven. Be pampered in the Himawari Suites and Apartments where you can enjoy the privacy and comfort of home.

Himawari Hotel Apartmentsâ restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner. A bar/lounge is open for drinks. Room service is available. Recreational amenities include microbrewery, an outdoor pool, a children's pool, an outdoor tennis court, a sauna, and a fitness facility. Spa amenities include massage/treatment rooms, body treatments, beauty services, and a hair salon.

This 4.5-star property has a business centre and offers small meeting rooms, a meeting/conference room, and secretarial services. Complimentary wireless Internet access is available in public areas and rooms. This Phnom Penh property has 120 square feet of event space consisting of a conference centre and banquet facilities. For a surcharge, the property offers a roundtrip airport shuttle (available on request). Business services, wedding services, and tour/ticket assistance are available.

115 air-conditioned guestrooms at Himawari Hotel Apartments feature fireplaces and iPod docking stations. Balconies offer city, river, or pool views. Beds come with pillow top mattresses and premium bedding. At this 4.5-star hotel, accommodations include kitchens with microwaves, refrigerators, coffee/tea makers, and kitchen linens (dishtowels, sponges, etc). Bathrooms feature shower/tub combinations, bathrobes, slippers, and complimentary toiletries. Wired high-speed Internet access is complimentary. In addition to desks and fax machines, guestrooms offer phones. 32-inch LCD televisions have cable channels and DVD players. Also included are laptop-compatible safes and washers/dryers. Housekeeping is available daily. Guestrooms are all non-smoking.
