The Sumahan on the Water offers a tranquil waterside retreat in the vibrant city of Istanbul.  Located along the Bosporus Strait, each guest room offers panoramic views of the water from spacious and contemporary settings.  Visitors enjoy the freedom to walk along the waterâs edge or explore the region by ferry.

The hotel features a fully equipped spa and fitness centre on the property, offering massage and traditional Moroccan Hammam treatments.  Guests can also use the weight room for some exercise.

The surrounding region is rich with local culture and unique restaurant options.  Guests are encouraged to sample fresh local seafood.

Locations near
Sumahan on the Water

Positioned on the edge of Europe and Asia, the Sumahan on the Water hotel enjoys a splendid location along the Bosporus Strait.  The hotel rests in an eclectic section of Istanbul, surrounded by small wooden house, fishing piers, and delicious restaurants.Conveniently located, the hotel is just a few steps from the ferry pier, which offers visitors an easy transportation option between both the European and Asian sides of the strait.
