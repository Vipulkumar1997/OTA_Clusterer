Located near the legendary palm grove of Marrakech, and the new big football stadium. The Marrakech Ryads Parc & Spa has a privileged location in a quiet location offering guests a fantastic view at the foot of the wonderful Atlas Mountains. This true four star luxury replaces a haven with beautiful gardens  1200 square meters outdoor pool, tennis courts, mini football field, beach volleyball court, basketball court,  and a sublime wellness centre built on 1400 square metes. including ( treatment rooms, massages, sauna, steam rooms, hair salon , pool Jacuzzi, fitness room ).

The Marrakech Ryads Park & Spa features 260 rooms spread over 13 Ryads and the harmony of colours attracts you and makes you enjoy your stay. The location of this hotel and the reception you will find there will make your transition an ideal place to stay in, your business trip, your meeting or for your family weekend.
