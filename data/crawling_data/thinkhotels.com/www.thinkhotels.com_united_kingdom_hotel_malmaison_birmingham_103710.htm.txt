Hotel Rooms: 189









































































The Malmaison Birmingham is the gloriously converted Royal Mail Sorting Office.  There are exclusive shops, restaurants and bars on the hotel doorstep and Birmingham New Street Rail Station is just a 10-minute walk away.

All 189 bedrooms and suites at Malmaison Birmingham offers you style and luxury. This former Sorting Office has been well and truly sorted, offering the most first class Birmingham rooms and suites you'll find anywhere.  All rooms have the complete set of Mal ingredients - great beds for sleepy heads, moody lighting, en-suite bathrooms with baths and power showers, digital TV, serious wines and naughty nibbles. Other little luxuries include same-day laundry, exclusive toiletries that you're encouraged to take with you, 'vroom' 24 hour room service, fast and free Wi-Fi, hairdryers, ironing board and iron and complimentary tea and coffee making facilities.

You'll also find classic and contemporary cocktails with a twist in MALBAR, plus the best burgers in the black country in the Mal Birmingham Brasserie.

The luxurious Le Petit Spa has a range of health and beauty treatments, using the latest Elemis products. Guests also have free use of the weights and machines in the modern gym.
