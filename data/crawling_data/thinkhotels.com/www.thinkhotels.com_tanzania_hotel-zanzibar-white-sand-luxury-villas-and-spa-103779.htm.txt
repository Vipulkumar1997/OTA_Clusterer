The Zanzibar White Sand Luxury Villas & Spa offers private and serene luxury spaces along a secluded beach.  Guests lavish in spacious, air-conditioned villas, admiring incredible ocean views and palm groves.

A fully equipped spa is onsite, offering guests the opportunity to refresh and revive the senses through massage and body treatments.  Visitors may also elect to take a relaxing swim in the private pool or walk through the hotel garden.

The hotel offers traditional local cuisine and international fare in the hotel restaurant, and a variety of beverages to sample at the beach front bar.

Locations near
Zanzibar White Sand Luxury Villas and SPA

Situated on a pristine coastline, far from the closest town, the Zanzibar White Sand Luxury Villas & Spa enjoy a secluded and relaxed environment.  The collection of villas offers guests one of the most beautiful beach views in the world, far from the clutter of tourism.Two small villages are close to the hotel.  Guests enjoy the serenity of the space, yet can reach the city and airport in just under an hour of driving.
