Facilities

Hotel Rooms: 781








The Renaissance Orlando at SeaWorld provides an ideal setting for a luxury holiday in Orlando.  Surrounded by theme parks, the hotel is perfect for families.  Guests enjoy large and contemporary guest rooms and suites, each of which is fully equipped with modern comforts and amenities.  The private rooms feature spacious bathrooms, air-conditioning, flat panel HDTV, and plush bedding.

The hotel features a fully equipped fitness centre, perfect for guests who desire some exercise during their stay.  The fitness room offers plenty of cardio and resistance machines, as well as free weights.

The Renaissance Orlando at SeaWorld offers plenty of spacious luxury guest rooms and suites.  Each guest space is designed to deliver optimal comfort and convenience.  Visitors enjoy luxury amenities such as spacious private bathrooms, complimentary Wi-Fi, and working spaces.  Each king and queen bed features the Renaissance Revive bedding system.

Locations near
Renaissance Orlando At Seaworld

Located directly in front of SeaWorld, the Renaissance Orlando at SeaWorld is the perfect base for families or vacationers visiting the popular theme park.  Within walking distance, guests will find the gates to SeaWorld Orlando or Aquatica water park.  The hotel is also a short drive from other Orlando theme parks, including Universal Studios and Walt Disney World.This area also features plenty of restaurants and shopping destinations, offering travellers ample opportunity to make the most of their vacation.
