Locations near
The Athenaeum

Located in Mayfair, The Athenaeum Hotel is positioned near Park Lane, where Hyde Park and Green Park meet.  From here, visitors can walk to explore some of Londons most incredible sites, including Buckingham Palace and Westminster Abbey.  The Mayfair area is also known for its many upscale boutique shops and fine restaurants, providing plenty of opportunities for fashionable London shopping and fine dining.The hotel is positioned minutes from the Green Park and Hyde Park Corner Underground stations, making it a convenient choice for guests who need to use the London Underground.
