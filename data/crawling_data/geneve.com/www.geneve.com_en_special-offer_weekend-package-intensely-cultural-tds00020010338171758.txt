/ General Terms




Duration of the offer
Valid for weekend nights (Friday, Saturday and Sunday) until 31 Dezember 2018. Please make your booking (subject to availability) through Geneva Tourism.

Payment
Prices are in Swiss francs (CHF), per room; VAT and service included. These weekend prices are not valid for groups. Free for a child up to 6 years in the room of the parents. The payment is to be made by credit card, directly at Geneva Tourism. Accepted credit cards: Visa, Eurocard/Mastercard, American Express.

Policy
Any change or cancellation must be announced exclusively to Geneva Tourism.
A CHF 50.- charge will be made for any change or cancellation made from the day of the reservation up to 3 working days preceding your arrival. At the end of this period, and according to the hotel's conditions, the total amount of the package could be forfeited or partially refunded.
