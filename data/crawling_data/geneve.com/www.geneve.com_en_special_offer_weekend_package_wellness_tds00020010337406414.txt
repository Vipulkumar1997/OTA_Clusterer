/ General Terms




Duration of the offer
Valid for weekend nights (Friday, Saturday and Sunday) up to 1 January 2018. Please make your booking (subject to availability) through Geneva Tourism 7 days before your arrival at the latest.

Payment
Prices are in Swiss francs (CHF), per room; breakfast, taxes, VAT and service included. These weekend prices are not valid for groups. The payment is to be made by credit card, directly at Geneva Tourism. Accepted credit cards: Visa, Eurocard/Mastercard, American Express.

Policy
Any change or cancellation must be announced exclusively to Geneva Tourism. 
A CHF 50.- charge will be made for any change or cancellation made from the day of the reservation up to 3 working days preceding your arrival. At the end of this period, and according to the hotel’s conditions, the total amount of the package could be forfeited or partially refunded.
