Why Book Your Flights with G Adventures?
Competitive Rates
We work with more than 80 airlines worldwide so we can find you the right flight for the right price. There are no extra fees (we’re all friends here!) and don’t forget to ask about our special fares throughout the year. Want an upgrade? We can help with that too.
Convenience
Trip, flights and insurance. Book it all with G Adventures and save yourself time and the hassle. Our dedicated Air Team is always ready to lend a hand. Oh, and if you need extra nights booked at hotels, we can help.
Collect Points? No Problem.
Just give us your frequent flyer information when you book and the points are yours.

Upgrades
Don’t be shy. Need that extra legroom? Really want to treat yourself to premium or business class? Just ask.
