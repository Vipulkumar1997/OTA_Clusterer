A Maharaja Palace in the Himalayan foothills

Ananda in the Himalayas, member of TemptingPlaces boutique hotels collection, is like an ideal India of the Maharajas' era. The hotel was built on a 40 ha property surrounding the Maharaja palace of Tehri-Garwal near Rishikesh, one of the hindu holy cities, at the source of the Ganges. The 78 rooms and suites cover splendour and luxury of yesteryear with an elegance that echoes the harmony of the landscape and the mind-blowing view on the mountain and the Ganges that snakes in the valley. More than a retreat place, Ananda is a perfect boutique-hotel for travellers who seek a holistic, quiet and spiritual lifestyle.

The Destination

The holly region of Uttarakhand

Ananda offers treks and safaris in full nature to discover the region’s richness. Ananda is close to the two holy cities Rishikesh and Haridwar, that overlook the Sal National Forest. In Rishikesh you can stroll by the pedestrian district of Swargashram and Laksham Jhula an ideal atmosphere for an healthy soul. Haridwar is the starting point of the holy pilgrimage to the sources of Ganges. It is also a city of numerous temples. The spiritual atmosphere ofthe temples of Dksha Mahadev and Mansa Devi, to name but a few, is magic.

The hotel experience


A hotel for the harmony of the body, the spirit and the soul

Past by the dazzling palace facade and  the hotel’s reception opens her doors with a traditional welcoming ceremony, a magic experience out of time called the aarti, along with a homemade infusion enjoyable and regenerating after the trip.
The owners, Ashok Khanna and his wife Neelam wanted their hotel to be a place of relaxation, a youth therapy includes in a custom program organized by the Ananda team to meet the needs and objectives of each.

The wonderful view of the balconies in each suite on the valley of the Ganges and the heights of the hills of the Himalaya brings a unique energy to the place.
The Palace View Room, as its name indicates, has a view on the Viceregal Palace. For those who prefer a view on the valley of the Ganges, choose the Valley Viw Room. Take great delight of the best views and panoramas on the entire region, from the Premium Valley Rooms.

For additional romantism, the Viceregal suite has a private jacuzzi with a bird’s eye view on the gardens and the valley of the Ganges for a most harmonious bath.





The Tempting Experience:

Beauty and well being at the source of the Ganges


This region, birthplace of yoga, of ayurveda and of vedanta, is ideal for a wellness cure. The traditional indian treatments are personalized and enriched with international techniques which, combined to the benefits of a flavourful and adapted cuisine, allow to find tranquility and energy forf the body and soul.

The Services


Garden
All the organic vegetables served at the restaurant are from the organic community farms and the hotel have an Ayurvedic herb garden on the property.
Restaurant
The main restaurant was built according to the harmonious principle of tantra. The healthy menu is made of organic vegetables cultivated in the garden of the hotel. 
The restaurant The Pavillon proposes snacks for a maximum nutritional intake. You can also taste traditional indian meals in the Winter Garden.
Spa & Wellness
The spa offers 80 treatments of face and body combining modern and traditional hindu methods. The Ayurveda is the basis of the wellness program implemented by the Ananda. It meets a personal desire of recovering, purification of the body and soul. You can for instance test the Pizhichil, a method involving in applying ayurvedic oil, with cotton tissue, by regular and rhythmic movements on the entire body starting by the head and shoulders. A warm bath is given for a ultimate relaxation.
Swimming pool
Nested on the hillside with a panoramic view on the valley, surrounded by the luxurious forests on the one side and the splendid building of the spa on the other side. It is the supreme place of relaxation.
Tea parlour
The tea house in the royal palace is a haven that makes you travel at the Raj era. A romantic and warm break. You can taste a fresh ginger and lemon tea or a mango lassi!




The Activities


Classes
Sessions of indian cooking and traditional music are available in the hotel.
Discovery
For an holistic experience, discover Ganga Aarti, a ceremony happening at the edge of the Ganges in Rishikesh. Children of the ashram Parmath, studying Veda, sing priers, candles and flowers are dropped in the river for offering. A devotion and joy moment, a wonderful souvenir to bring back from India !
Golf
A six holes golf course onsite
Sports
Fitness and yoga courses are planed throughout the stay for a personalized keep-fit program.
