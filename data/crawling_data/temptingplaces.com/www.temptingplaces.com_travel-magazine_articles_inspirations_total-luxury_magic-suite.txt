Magic Suites

The greatest suites of our boutique hotels

















Magic, gleaming, sumptuous... Here are some of the ingredients for a great stay in one of the finest boutique hotel suites! Colourful like a candy in Crete, urban et stylish in Taiwan, cocooning in Courchevel, relax in Spain, stylish in Fez, with a view all over Athens or to live like king & queen in Marrakech...
We selected the greatest new suites all around the world. To travel is to dream!

Elegance and luxury of the Suite Royale

Tigmiza Marrakech – Marrakech, Morocco

Tigmiza Marrakech is a sumptuous “kazbah” with 5-star hotel services, nestled in the Palmeraie of Marrakech. The suites and rooms have each their own theme and colour sheme. With its lush green trees, bright colour mosaics, precious fabrics and works of art, Tigmiza Marrakech is sure to mesmerise any traveller.

The brand new Royal Suite is set on a 4000m2 garden. It is composed of 2 luxury suites : Suite du Roi and the Suite du Prince. The precious decor is contemporay and very Moroccan as well made of designers signatures, vintage find and tailor-made furniture. It features a private spa, a private garden with an heated pool and a terrace. For total luxury, a butler is at disposal 24/24.

TemptingPlaces Advantages:
VIP welcome in the room, complimentary afternoon snack each afternoon, welcome tea and moroccan sweets, late check-out available upon availabilities.

Colourful Candy Suites in Crete

AVLI Lounge Apartments – Rethymnon, Crete

Avli Lounge spacious and enchanting suites are housed in two 15th century Cretan mansions. The boutique hotel is part of a world of its own, featuring an organic store, a renowned Cretan restaurant and an impressive enoteca.

A total contrast with the 15th century walls, the new Candy Suites are colourful and with a décor inspired from candy, jellies, small cakes, lollipops and cookies. Like in a child dream!

TemptingPlaces Advantages:
Complimentary soft drinks, local spirits, a bottle of wine and a fruit basket.

Intimate and trendy suites in Taipei

VVGB+ B – Taipei, Taiwan

VVG BB + B is a charming and surprising boutique hotel located in the heart of Taipei. With only 3 suites it is one of those addresses we like to preciously keep to ourselves. It is a whole lifestyle concept: one hôtel, three restaurants (Bistro, Action and Thinking), a bookstore (Something) and a Arty and DIY boutique (Chiffon).

The brand new June Suite (66m²) is designed as a London Library filled with over 1000 books!

TemptingPlaces Advantages
: Pastries and candies offered.

A chalet of luxury

La Sivolière – Courchevel, France

La Sivolière is a prestigious 5 star hotel, situated in the heart of the mythic resort village of 1850 Courchevel. Rooms and suites decorated by designer Tristian Auer, a spa, Chef’s cuisine… Perfect for your return from the slopes! With a view of either the forest and slopes or mountains and village, the 11 suites, all decorated by Tristian Auer, are highlighted by a play of soft and enveloping light. The materials were chosen for their noblesse: mirror bedside table lamps, silk lampshade, black lacquered tables, aged hardwood floors, velours drapes…
Defined as “the most guarded secret of Courchevel,” La Sivolière offers you a cocooning stay under the snow.

Magic view on the Acropolis from the penthouse

New Hotel Athens – Athens, Greece
New Hotel Athens is the first hotel project from Brazilian designers Fernando and Humberto Campana. The large picture windows, the bamboo floors and furniture and the touches of folklore give a certain bohemian soul to this new design hotel situated in the heart of Athens.

The Penthouse Suite offers a 360° view of all the monuments of the city: the Acropolis, the Mount Lycabettus, the Syntagma Square and the Parliament building are all at your fingertip!

TemptingPlaces Advantage:
– 15% discount on treatments at New Sense Spa.

The very soul of Fez

Karawan Riad – Fez, Morocco

The luxurious boutique hotel Karawan Riad can be found in a quiet alley of the Medina of Fez, a World Heritage Site listed by UNESCO. Karawan Riad provides the very best of contemporary comfort: air conditioning, heating and Wi-Fi, combined with the elegance of a customized service and remarkable decor. The 7 suites are spread around the open roof patio. The renovation work was entrusted to local craftsmen so that Moroccan techniques could be respected whether for the details of a zellige or the particular colour of a coating. A particular focus was brought to the lights, staging them with care to highlight the hotel natural beauty. a style that could be described as “elegant nomad life”. Its elegant, bohemian atmosphere will prove particularly soothing and relaxing.

TemptingPlaces Advantages
: Complimentary traditional Hammam for 2 and Moroccan tea and pastries offered all along your stay.

The keys of the Palazzo Ricasoli

Leone Blue – Florence, Italy
Neither a palace nor a house, Leone Blu is a boutique hotel focusing on emotions: here, culture and architecture complement each other, hospitality bridging past and present beautifully. The historic residence, now an intimate boutique hotel, is ideally located near the Carraia Bridge in the city centre. The imposing staircase then leads to 9 suites, all name after the illustrious family ancestors. The decoration, signed by Pirotecnico Studio, brings a balance between the past and the present, thus elegantly combining the family antiques with contemporary details. Lovers of 18th century art will appreciate with no doubt the Gisla or Pandolfo suites and their vaulted ceiling decorated with frescoes and stucco. The Bindaccio Suite, more contemporary, is highlighted with beautiful pieces inspired by works of art of Sol Le Witt.

TemptingPlaces Advantage
: Wine tasting with 2 types of wine per evening (1 glass per type of wine/each guest).
