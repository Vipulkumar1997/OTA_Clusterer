In the intimacy of a Bordeaux castle

Château Pape Clément, member of TemptingPlaces boutique hotels collection, is not only a rare wine, Grand Cru Classé de Graves, but also a property with the luxury services and intimacy of a "Maison d'Hôtes", offering 6 suites and rooms in the heart of the Bernard Magrez vineyard of Pessac. Just outside Bordeaux, the estate extends over 150 acres of parkland with ancient trees and vines. Far from the urban hustle and bustle, it offers a tailor-made trip for lovers of French wine heritage and the art of winemaking that this facility offers. A unique Bordeaux getaway...

The Destination

Pessac

Pessac is the cradle of viticulture in the "bordelais". Pape Clément vineyard is one of the treasures of the area. Whilst wine makes up a significant part of the culture, the local architectural heritage is just as important. Such as, the Cité Fruges deisgned by renowned French architect Le Corbusier. These 50 houses built in 1929 were out of step with the regional habitat. Pay a visit to the Maison Municipale Le Corbusier at Pessac to complete your architectural journey.
For a quiet stroll we suggest you to walk past the coulée verte du Peugue, an idyllic space of more than 700 acres along the river.

The hotel experience


A stay in the heart of a Grand Cru Classé vineyard

The ancient olive trees and imposing Lebanon cedars keep watch over Chateau Pape Clément, sheltered under a glass roof designed by Gustave Eiffel. The mansion takes its name from Pope Clement V, who was the owner in the 14th century. Today it is one of forties and so properties across the world owned by Bernard Magrez, lover of excellence, fine wines and an avid art collector.

Lacquered wood furniture, a harp, harpsichord, portrait galleries and paintings from the private collection, refined amber-fragranced candles... the property shines with elegance. A wooden staircase leads to characteristic rooms and suites poetically named after dreams: Le Clos des Songes, Mon Seul Rêve, La Tempérance, La Sérénité, L'Égrégore - names borrowed from the nearby vineyards. They are all luxuriously decorated but each is distinguished by its unique style: tapestry fabric taut, marble bathrooms, velvet and satin, Hungarian parquet, canopy beds, bronze chandeliers, antique Louis XV furniture...

Enjoy baroque style in L'Égrégore with Baccarat crystal chandeliers and sumptuous bathrooms in black marble or the ladylike look of Le Clos des Songes with its gold-embroidered canopy bed and white marble fireplace.





The Tempting Experience:

Fly over the vineyards by helicopter, take a great escape in a Rolls Royce or take the available private jet to the major European capitals. Everything is possible to experience the real Château life!

The Services


Boutique
The vineyard tour ends with a wine tasting in the boutique, which takes its furniture and shelving from an ancient pharmacy. It includes a hundred or so of the owner’s wines in various vintages.
Park
The property rests in a park with Mediterranean and exotic themes in which palms, olives, eucalyptus and magnolias blossom, culminating in the month of May, with the flowering of lemon and orange trees.
Table d'hôtes
To round off this gourmet adventure is the Table d'Hôtes of the Château Pape Clément hotel. Expect a fine cuisine of the region that reinvests French gastronomy. The menus will be accompanied by advice from the sommelier. *On demand only. A special request for Table d’Hôtes must be done on the day of booking, minimum 2 days before arrival. Upon availability.




The Activities


Golf

Vineyard & Cellar
Tours of the vineyard and cellar are organized and adapted to different profiles: amateurs, connoisseurs, tasters or demanding collectors. In the spectacular winery, a short video recalls the great stages of the development of this great wine. The cellar holds hundreds of barrels where the wine is aged for 18 months before bottling.
