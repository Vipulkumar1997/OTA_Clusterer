Online Travel – Director of Product Marketing


COMPANY OVERVIEW

AdventureLink www.adventurelink.com,
                                    located in Pasadena California is at the forefront of developing the
                                    leading system
                                    for researching and booking
                                    adventure travel worldwide. We are looking for a talented individual
                                    with experience in online
                                    marketing to join our team.

JOB OVERVIEW

As the Director of Marketing, you
                                    will be responsible for all aspects of the AdventureLink consumer direct
                                    and affiliate
                                    marketing programs. You will be
                                    responsible for developing the overall program, execution and adjusting
                                    the plan
                                    accordingly to achieve site traffic
                                    and booking objectives.

RESPONSIBILITES


Plan, manage and adjust the monthly marketing objectives for attaining traffic and overall booking objectives

Develop the strategy, budget
                                        forecasting and ongoing report for all online channels including SEM,
                                        SEO, Affiliate &
                                        online display advertising
                                        programs

Drive the overall search strategy for AdventureLink for SEM & SEO

Identify and recruit affiliates that can help drive content and revenue for AdventureLink

Work with the technical team to develop and prioritize the engineering plan to achieve sales objectives

Proactively propose new strategies and aggressively test different tactics in order to optimize campaigns

Drive quantitative analysis for determine the most effective distribution channels and marketing related programs

Budget management and detailed forecasting


EXPERIENCE


A minimum of 5 years in online
                                        marketing with a proven track record of achieving SEM plan attainment of
                                        $100k or
                                        more annually

A minimum of 5+ years hands-on
                                        experience managing large-scale PPC campaigns with top and secondary
                                        search
                                        engines (Google, Yahoo!, Bing,
                                        Ask.com) using geo-targeting and retargeting techniques

Experience with multi-variable
                                        analysis and tracking & analytics tools like KissMetrics, MixPanel,
                                        CrazyEgg,
                                        MouseFlow


SKILLS


Expert with ad copy and A/B landing page testing design, implementation and analysis (multivariate testing is a plus)

Data driven with strong analytical skills. Proficient with MS Excel

Detail-oriented with strong project management skills

Ability to thrive in a demanding, fast-paced, startup environment

Proven ability to deliver
                                        valuable business insights based on multiple data sources available in
                                        the organization and
                                        search traffic tools

Ability to plan, organize and establish priorities with significant analytical, logical and strategic thinking skills

Excellent communication (written
                                        and verbal) skills and interpersonal skills to effectively drive the
                                        overall marketing
                                        strategy for AdventureLink


COMPENSATION


Market competitive salary with significant stock options for the right candidate


For further details, please submit your resume directly to
jobs@adventurelink.com
.

                                    ASP.NET developer that has a background in front-end GUI and back-end
                                    service components. If you are a hard working, passionate developer
                                    looking for a great long-term career opportunity then we would like to
                                    talk to you!


Requirements:
We are
                                    looking for someone who is experienced with ASP.NET MVC, JavasScript,
                                    and HTML. Knowledge of MySQL, Solr, and MemCached preferred. You will be
                                    responsible for building various parts of our consumer website and
                                    backend services.

Prerequisites:

C# with .NET 2.0/3.0/3.5/4.0 (mandatory)

Knowledge of MySQL

XML / Web Services (.NET 3.0/3.5)

Expert HTML/CSS 3, JavaScript & responsive web design

You are just as comfortable with front-end as back-end development.


Additional Requirements:


AMySQL 5.1+

Memcached / Apache Solr

Git for version control

Amazon EC2 cloud management

Multi threaded application development

SEO optimization

Data analytics, reporting systems
