Flights To Baltimore
Are you searching for fun and adventure in the American north-east? Flights to Baltimore, Maryland will transport you to a city known for its history, character, and vibrant cultural attractions overlooking the waterfront. While "Charm City" is surrounded by numerous states, it's also located on the scenic shores of the Atlantic Ocean. Explore cheap flights to Baltimore via the Baltimore-Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport (BWI), and get ready to experience over 70 historic districts in the "city of neighborhoods." Immerse yourself in this charismatic city by visiting the Inner Harbor, Baltimore Symphony Orchestra, Camden Yards, John Hopkins University, the National Aquarium, and the Baltimore Museum of Art, or take a tour of the National Brewing Company, a monument to Baltimore's once thriving brewery industry.
Transportation from BWI
Airport hotels near BWI
, situated in Baltimore's business district, are within proximity to entertainment and amenities, including Arundel Mills Mall, the National Electronics Museum, and Andover Park. Many hotels are conveniently located less than 2 miles from BWI, and they are easy to reach via a rental vehicle at the airport or a taxi. Keep in mind some airport hotels also offer complimentary airport shuttles and scheduled train station pick-ups, so be sure to ask what services are available when you book tickets to Baltimore. If you take I-95 from your hotel, you'll find downtown Baltimore is only 17 minutes away by car. The city center is also accessible via public transit, such as Baltimore Light Rail, Amtrak Train at BWI Rail Station, and the 017 Patapsco Station-Parkway Center bus.
